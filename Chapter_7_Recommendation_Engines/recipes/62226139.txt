Title:
Inside Out Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb extra lean ground beef
2 cups coleslaw mix or shredded cabbage
1 cup Minute rice
2-16 oz cans stewed tomatoes
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp basil
1/4 tsp garlic powder
2 Tbs Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain well. Add cabbage and continue cooking over medium heat until cabbage is crisp tender. Stir in rice, tomatoes, spices, and cheese.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and cook until rice is tender.
Can vary by using Italian or Mexican spiced tomatoes.

Submitter: 
Leanne Kramer Chesshir
Description: 
Quick, easy recipe for a balanced meal. Just add a fruit salad and whole wheat rolls.
