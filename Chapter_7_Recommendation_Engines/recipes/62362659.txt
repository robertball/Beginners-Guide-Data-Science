Title:
Ice Cream Strawberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/443028.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ice cream sandwiches
Whipped topping or whipped cream (8 0z?)
1 pint ice cream.
Chocolate fudge topping, room temperature (optional)
Strawberry or raspberry glaze
One strawberry cake (baked in 9x12 pan)

Directions:
Slice baked strawberry cake into two pieces and slice each piece horizontally. Place two thin cake pieces on bottom of 9x12 baking dish (cover bottom).
Whip together ice cream and whipped cream for filling and topping.
Spread the cake layer with ice cream/whipped cream.
Place 10-12 ice cream sandwiches side by side on top of the cake and ice cream filling in baking dish.
Spread evenly with hot fudge topping, then the raspberry glaze, then drop on some of the whipped topping.
Then cover everything with the rest of the strawberry cake.
Ice cake with ice cream/whipped cream topping.
Cover and freeze for at least 1 hour before serving. Garnish with strawberries or raspberries on top of cake.
