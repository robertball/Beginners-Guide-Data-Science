Title:
Easy Fruit Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992894.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup boiling water
1 (3 ounce) package Jell-O® mix, any red flavor
1 cup cold water
1 (15.25 ounce) can DOLE Tropical Fruit Salad, drained
Thawed whipped topping

Directions:
Stir boiling water into gelatin in medium bowl 2 minutes until completely dissolved. Stir in cold water. Pour gelatin into 4 tall dessert or parfait glasses, filling about half full.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm. To serve, layer with whipped topping and tropical fruit salad.

Submitter: 
Dole Food Company
Description: 
Fruit Parfaits are a quick and easy, no-cook dessert that are a refreshing treat any time of year. Simply chill your favorite flavor gelatin in a pretty glass, top with DOLE® Tropical Fruit Salad and whipped topping, and elegance is served!
