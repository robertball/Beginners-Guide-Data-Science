Title:
Peanut Butter Brownies Paleo Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup natural nut butter (I used half cashew, half almond)
1/2 cup Raw Honey,or dark agave nectar
1 egg
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup (scant) chopped 85% cacao  Optional
1/4 cup (scant)chopped unsweentened banana chips) optional
Directions
Mix all ingredients with a handmixer until smooth
pour into 8X8 brownie pan
bake at 350 for 22 to 25 minutes

Directions:
Mix first 4 ingredients together until well combined. Gently stir in chips and banana if using.
Spread into an 8 by 8 pan, bake at 350 22 to 25 mins.
Cool on rack and then refrigerate.
