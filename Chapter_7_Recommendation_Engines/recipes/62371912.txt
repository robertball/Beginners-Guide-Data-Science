Title:
White Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans crescent rolls
2 8oz pkg cream cheese
1 envelope Hidden Valley Ranch mix
8 oz mayo
1 c chopped fresh broccoli
1 c chopped fresh cauliflower
chopped onions
1/2 c carrots
1/2 c cheddar cheese

Directions:
Place rolls falt on  cookie sheet & bake as directed
Mix cream cheese, dressing mix and mayo. Spread over cooled crust.
Mix remaining ingredients together & pat on top of crust.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott Provost
Description: 
Lashawn had at her mom's house - says it's good! Hers was not on crescent dough; more like flatbread.
