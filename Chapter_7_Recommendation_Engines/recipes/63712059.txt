Title:
Big Ray's Caramelized Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031497.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken
1/2 cup soy sauce
3/4 cup  ketchup
3/4 cup honey
3-4 clove garlic, minced
3 Tbl Minced onion
dash of hot sauce
2 Tbl cornstarch

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Mix ketchup, honey soy sauce, garlic, hot sauce, and onions in a saucepan and heat to boil.
Mix cornstarch with water and add to sauce to thicken.
Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour sauce over chicken.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 min covered then 15 min uncovered basting every 15 min.
