Title:
Ultimate 7 - layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (16 oz) refried beans
1 Tbsp. taco seasoning mix
1 c. Sour Cream
1 c. Salsa
1 c shredded lettuce
1 c. Mexican style shredded cheese
1/2 c. chopped green onions
2 Tbsp. Sliced pitted ripe olives

Directions:
1. Mix beans & taco seasoning mix. Spread onto bottom of 9 in. pie plate.
2. Layer remaining ingredients over bean mixture; cover. Refrigerate several hours or until chilled.
3. Serve with tortilla chips or assorted  crackers.
