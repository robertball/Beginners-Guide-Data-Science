Title:
Overnight Caramel Pull-Aparts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup finely chopped pecans or walnuts
18 frozen Parkerhouse-style rolls
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/4 cup granulated sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 box (3 5/8 oz.) butterscotch pudding mix
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
6 tbs. butter or margarine
1 1/2 tbs. grated orange peel (optional)

Directions:
Grease 12-cup Bundt pan. Sprinkle nuts in bottom of pan. Set aside.
Coat each frozen roll in 1/4 cup melted butter, then in mixture of sugar and cinnamon. Arrange single layer in pan. Sprinkle pudding mix over rolls.
In small saucepan, heat brown sugar, 6 tbs. butter and orange peel until boiling. Pour over rolls
Cover pan with towel. Let stand at room temperature 8-10 hours or overnight until rolls have tripled in size.
Remove towel. Bake in preheated 325 degree oven 35-40 minutes. Invert onto serving dish. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Frozen rolls make this convenient.  A box of butterscotch pudding mix and letting it rise overnight also adds to the convenience. This came from a grocery store recipe magazine.
