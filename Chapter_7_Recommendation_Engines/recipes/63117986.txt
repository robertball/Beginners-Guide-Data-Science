Title:
Crock Pot Sunday Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 pounds beef roast
2 tsps minced garlic
salt and pepper, to taste
2 whole potatoes, cubed
2 whole carrots, sliced
1 whole onion, sliced
2 whole bay leaves
1 tsp dried rosemary
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1 cup water
1 packet au jus gravy mix

Directions:
Cut beef into bit-sized pieces, season with salt and pepper and brown in frypan.
Place potato, carrot, onion and bay leaves in crock pot and sprinkle with garlic, rosemary and thyme.  Arrange beef on top.
Mix water with au jus mix and pour over contents of slow cooker.
Cover slow cooker and cook on low heat 8-9 hours or until beef is tender.
Make gravy from slow cooker juices if desired.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
An easy, flavorful, juicy beef roast.  I love the flavor that seeps into the potatoes and carrots that cook with the roast.
