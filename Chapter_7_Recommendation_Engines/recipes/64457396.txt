Title:
Low-Fat Tandoori Chicken with Sauteed Veggie Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2408107.jpg
Ingredients:
Tandoori Chicken:
2 chicken drumsticks
15 ml Indian Tandoori Spice
250 ml 1% milk
30 ml cornstarch
Veggie Couscous:
250 ml life smart couscous
1 medium carrots, sliced thinly
1 medium stalks celery, sliced thinly
1 onion,cut Julienne
15 ml canola oil
30 ml curry powder
500 ml water

Directions:
Tandoori Chicken: take the spices, milk and cornstarch and combine them in a bowl, mix well, microwave 2 minutes stirring every 30 seconds until thickened
place chicken in the sauce and marinade about 1 hour
Place chicken on a rack in a pan to elevate chicken bake until well done
Veggie Couscous: saute all the vegetables, in the oil, until carrots and celery are tender
then add the water curry powder, when water boils pour in couscous then stir and cover for 5 mins. on low
