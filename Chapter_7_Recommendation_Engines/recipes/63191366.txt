Title:
Coconut Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/822934.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c Jose Cuervo Gold Tequila
2 c Bone Daddy Sweet & Sour
1/2 c Triple Sec orange liquor
1/2 c coconut milk (can be found in the Asian aisle of the grocery store)
Juice of 2 small limes

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a pitcher.  Stir vigorously.  Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
Kat R
Description: 
A creamy, slightly sweet margarita approved by all my friends.  I learned how to make margaritas from my husband's Mexican family, so I tend to prefer them stronger than some people, though I've never had complaints.  Enjoy!
