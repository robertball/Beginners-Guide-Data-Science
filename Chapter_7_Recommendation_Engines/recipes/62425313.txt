Title:
Dona Ana's Chilie Relleno's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/880738.jpg
Ingredients:
SAUCE:
2 chopped tomatoes
1 chopped, roasted,seeded jalapeno
1/2 chopped onion
small handful chopped cilantro
1 Tbs. flour
Salt,Pepper,Garlic powder
2 Tbls Lard or veggie oil
1 C water
RELLENOS:
6 Pablano chilies roasted and seeded
1 LB of Queso Fresco (A soft Mexican Cheese)sliced
5 eggs separated
1Tsp.flour + flour for dredging
Oil/lard for frying
Salt,Pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute the tomato,onion & jalapeno with the oil/lard. Season with salt,pepper,garlic powder.Add the cilantro at the end and then add the flour. Cook for 2 or 3 mins (this removes the Raw taste of the flour)
Add the water and bring to a boil, simmer a few mins to thicken the sauce. Set to the side and keep warm.
Whip the egg whites till stiff,season with salt and pepper and add the flour.Add the yolks beat till just combined,set aside.
Stuff the prepared chilies with the cheese and dredge in flour then dip in the egg mixture, fry until golden brown. keep warm while you fry the rest.
Serve Rellenos with the sauce and Enjoy!!
