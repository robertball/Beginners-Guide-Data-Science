Title:
Rhubarb and strawberry jello freezer jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups chopped rhubarb
1/4 cup water
2 1/4 to 3 cups sugar
1 small pk strawberry jello

Directions:
On med-low heat bring water and fruit to boil, mash well.
Add sugar and bring to a boil again.
Remove from heat and add jello.
Put in mason jars and refrigerate of freeze.
