Title:
Souper Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds hamburger, browned
10 large Tbs. flour
2 cans French Onion Soup
Hamburger buns
Butter
Dill pickles

Directions:
Brown the hamburger until done.  Do Not drain drippings.  Stir flour onto hamburger and drippings until hamburger is well coated.  Add two cans of soup and stir until thick.  Serve on buttered hamburger buns and top with dill pickles.
