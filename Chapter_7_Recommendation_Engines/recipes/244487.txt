Title:
Yogurt Cumin Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2562888.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup plain yogurt
1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Whisk yogurt, mustard, garlic, cumin, and black pepper together in a bowl. Slowly pour olive oil into mixture while continually whisking until dressing is smooth.
