Title:
Gingerbread Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white rice flour
3/4 cup mix brown rice flour and oat flour
1 or 2 Tbsp coconut flour (optional)
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1/4 tsp or more ginger
3 Tbsp molasses
2 eggs
1 or 2 Tbsp light oil
1 1/2 cup yogurt and milk mixture

Directions:
Whisk eggs until light and fluffy. Whisk in oil and yogurt mix.
Whisk together dry ingredients. Mix together with wet ingredients.
Spray oil onto griddle before cooking.
