Title:
Buttermilk Coffee Cake quick bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 cups sugar
3 eggs lightly beaten
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 cup yellow raisins
4 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
3 teaspoons baking powder
2 cups buttermilk
Crumbs
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 and 1/4 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Beat eggs, sugar, butter and vanilla. Slowly add flour baking soda, baking powder and buttermilk. Slowly stir in the raisins. Lightly grease the pan and bake for 45-60 min.
Crumbs
combine ingredients and sprinkle on top of the baked coffee cake and allow to cool for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Danielle Kroll
Description: 
Here is an excellent coffee cake that is made with no yeast.My grandmother always made this at every holiday.
