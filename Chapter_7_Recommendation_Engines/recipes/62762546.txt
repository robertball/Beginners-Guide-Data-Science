Title:
Mushroom Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c butter
1 T parsley
1 T grated onion
1 T prepared mustard
1/8 t cayenne
1/8 t nutmeg
1.5 T flour
1 lb fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 c heavy cream

Directions:
Blend all ingredients EXCEPT mushrooms and cream.
Layer butter blend with mushrooms in greased casserole, ending with butter on top.
Pour cream over all.
Bake uncovered at 375F for 1 hour.
