Title:
Lazy Cabbage Rolls....... Thanks Jolene Winder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp butter or marg
2 large onions
1 lb bacon
3 cups cooked rice
28 ounce tomato soup with 1/4 cup water
salt and pepper (to taste)
1/4 tsp seasoning salt
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 head chopped cabbage

Directions:
Cook bacon to crispy break into pieces, add onions to pan and cook with marg for 3 minutes. Add to cooked rice and mix.  Layer enough chopped cabbage in bottom of sprayed crock pot (med size) then spread a layer of rice over top, then cabbage then rice ending with rice on top. Pour soup over top and cook on med setting for 4 to 5  hours or high for 4 hours....

Description: 
Easy way to make cabbage roll taste in a crock pot.....yuppie!!!
