Title:
Chicken Vessuvio Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Large Chicken Breasts skin off cut in half
4 to 6 Italian Sausages sweet or hot
Salt & Pepper
2 Cans Cannellini beans Drained
1 Medium Onion
3 Cloves Garlic Chopped
1/2 cup White Wine
2 Stalks Celery Chopped
10 Ripe Plum Tomatoes,cored,Seeded,Chopped
1/4 cup Chopped Fresh Parsley
4 Tablespoons Fresh Basil
Red Pepper Flakes (OPTIONAL)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F. In a heavy frying pan,heat olive oil, brownchicken and sausages well on all sides. Put in ovenproof cassero;e dish large enough to hold all meat in one layer.Bake 20 minutes.
While meat is roasting,add onions & garlic to pan meat was cooked in cook till onions are translucent. Add the wine 7 scarpe up the browned bits from bottem of pan. Add tomatoes cook 10 minutes till soft & are becoming a chunky sauce. Add drained beans & season with salt & pepper add hot pepper if using.Cook 5 minutes. Add Parsley & basil, mix well.Remove meat from oven add bean mixture to dish containing meat, laying meat on top of beans & stirring the meat juices into the sauce. Bake 10 to 15 minutes or till chicken juices run clear. To serve place bean mixture on plate then place chicken and sausage on top, spoon sauce over top:)
