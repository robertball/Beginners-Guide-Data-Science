Title:
Southwest Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (2 cups) Black beans
1/2 16 oz. bag frozen corn
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1/2 pound chicken breast
Corn tortillas
2 red bell peppers, seeded and diced
3 small cans diced green chiles
Salt, pepper, garlic, onion powder, and cumin to taste

Directions:
Dice and season chicken with spices.
Saut√© the chicken over medium heat.
In a bowl mix cream of chicken, green chillies, and red bell peppers.
Take a casserole dish and spread a portion in the bottom. Add corn tortillas.
Mix beans, corn, chicken, and cheese and add sprinkle over the tortillas. Spread remaining soup mixture. Layer with corn tortillas. Sprinkle with cheese.
Cover with tin foil and Bake 375 F until bubbly. Check in half hour, and then 10 minute intervals.
Garnish with green onions and serve.
