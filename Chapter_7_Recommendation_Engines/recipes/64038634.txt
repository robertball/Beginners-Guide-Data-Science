Title:
French Chocolate Custard  (Pot de Creme) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound high quality sweet chocolate
2 cups warm milk
6 egg yolks

Directions:
Melt chocolate and milk together over low heat until they reach the boiling point.  Remove from heat.
Stir them into the egg yolks, whisking until thickened and smooth.
Strain the custard and pour it into custard cups.
Chill well.

Submitter: 
Diana B. Jeck
Description: 
From the 1946 version of Joy of Cooking.  Dense, rich and smooth - a little goes a long way.
