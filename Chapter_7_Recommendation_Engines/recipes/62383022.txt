Title:
Big Daddy's Mila, Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana, thickly sliced, frozen
2 cups chopped kale
1 scoop Mila
1/4 cup milk
1/3 cup orange juice

Directions:
Place the banana, kale, and Mila into a blender, pour in the milk and orange juice. Cover, and blend until smooth; serve.
