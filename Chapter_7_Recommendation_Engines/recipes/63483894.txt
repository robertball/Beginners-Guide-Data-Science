Title:
Pork StirFry and Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pork sliced
1 tbs oil
1 cup water
1/4 cup teriyaki marinade and sauce
1 tbs ketchup
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp fennel
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp ground ginger
3 green onions chopped
1 tbs corn starch
2 cups rice, cooked

Directions:
stirfry pork in oil until brown.  add garlic and cook 1 min.  add sauce ingred and simmer covered 15mins.  stir in green onions and cook 5 mins.  blend 1 tbs water and cornstarch and stir into pan.  cook until thickened. serve over rice
