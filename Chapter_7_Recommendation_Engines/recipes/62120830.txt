Title:
Cheesy, Spicy Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz can of artichoke hearts
1 - 2 fresh or pickled jalapenos
2 tsp minced garlic
1 pkg (8 oz) cream cheese
3/4 cup freshley shredded parmesan cheese
16 oz chredded mozzarella cheese
jalapeno juice - to desired taste

Directions:
Drain artichoke hearts. Combine hearts,jalepenos, and garlic in food processor and corsley chop. (If you deisire, you can add 1 cup of lump crab meat before chopping)
Add cream cheese, paremsan cheese, and mozzarella to food processor and continue to blend until it is a somewhat smooth mixture.  Add jalapeno juice to desired heat.
Transfer dip to a baking dish and put in 350 degree oven for about 30 mintues.
Serve warm with an assortment of crackers or thin sliced baguette.

Submitter: 
Kathy Valdez-Rabenold
Description: 
While looking for a unique artichoke recipe, I decided to take a few items from each recipe and came up with my own.  You can add lump crab to this too, to make a spicy, cheesy, artichoke crab dip.  Yummy!!
