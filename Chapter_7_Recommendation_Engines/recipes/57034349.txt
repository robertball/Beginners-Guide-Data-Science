Title:
Chili Colorado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 1/2 pounds boneless pork, cut into 1 inch cubes
2 tablespoons  canola oil
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 heaping tablespons tomato paste
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 Tablespoon chopped flatleaf parsly
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and black pepper to taste
3 cups water (more or less)  depending
on how thick you want it,

Directions:
Place 3 tablespoons flour in a plastic bag. Add pork and shake to lightly coat with flour. Set aside.
Heat  oil in a Dutch oven over medium high heat. Add pork and cook until meat is well browned, about 5 to 8 minutes. Stir in 1 tablespoon flour, and cook 3 minutes. Stir in the onion, tomato paste, chile powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, pepper and water.  mixture will be lumpy but will  become the right consistency after about 5 minutes of stirring. Bring to a boil, then reduce eat to medium low and simmer until pork is just falling apart, about 1-1/2 to 2 hours.
