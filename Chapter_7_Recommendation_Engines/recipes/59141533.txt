Title:
Applesauce Noodle Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package wide egg noodles
1/2  cup butter plus 2 Tbsp.
3/4 cup fat free sour cream
4 eggs beaten
1-1/4 cups white sugar
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 (16 ounce) jar applesauce
1/4 cup raisins
3/4 cup graham cracker crumbs (optional)
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente. Drain.
In a large bowl, mix together butter, sour cream, eggs, sugar, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and applesauce. Stir in noodles and raisins.
Combine graham crackers w/cinnamon & 4 Tbsp. melted butter.  Pour the noodle mixture into the prepared dish. Sprinkle top with graham cracker mixture.
Bake 50 minutes in the preheated oven, or until set.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Tender noodles are stirred with a comforting blend of margarine, sour cream, egg substitute, sugar, lemon juice, vanilla extract, chunky applesauce, and raisins. Bake over a light sprinkling of graham cracker crumbs and top with cinnamon for a sweet treat.Modified from: Applesauce Noodle Kugel
