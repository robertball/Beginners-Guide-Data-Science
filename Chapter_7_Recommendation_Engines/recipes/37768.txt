Title:
Coconut Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds sweet potatoes , cooked, peeled and cut into 1-inch chunks (about 4 cups)
2 apples, peeled, thinly sliced
2/3 cup maple-flavored syrup
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon salt
2/3 cup BAKER'S ANGEL FLAKE Coconut

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place sweet potatoes in greased 13x9-inch baking dish; top with apples. Mix syrup, butter and salt; pour over apples. Sprinkle with coconut; cover.
Bake 30 minutes. Uncover. Bake 20 to 30 minutes or until apples are tender and coconut is lightly browned.
