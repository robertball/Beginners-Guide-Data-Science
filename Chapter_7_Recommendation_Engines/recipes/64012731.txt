Title:
Sole Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 6-oz. skinless fillets grey or Dover sole or any flaky white fish like turbot or tilapia
Kosher salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste
1/2 C flour
1/4 C canola oil
4 T unsalted butter
1 large shallot, minced
1/2 lemon, thinly sliced
14 C dry white wine
2 t. capers

Directions:
Pat fillets dry w/paper towels and season w/s&p; dredge in flour
Heat 2 T oil and 1 T butter in a 12" skillet over high heat. Cook 2 fillets, flipping once, until golden and cooked through, 3-4 min.; transfer to 2 plates. Wipe skillet clean and repeat w/remaining oil, 1 T butter, and fillets.
Add 1 T butter, the shallot, and lemon to skillet; cook until shallot is soft, 1-2 min. Add wine; cook, scraping up browned bits from pan. Add remaining butter, the capers, salt, and pepper. Cook until sauce is slightly thick; spoon over fish.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Pan-fried fillet, finished with a white-wine butter sauce laced w/lemon, capes and shallots.
