Title:
Crock Pot Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless pork chops
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 tsp ground ginger
2 cloves crushed garlic
salt and pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Place the pork chops in the slow cooker
Mix the remaining ingredients together and add this marinade to the slow cooker
Set your slow cooker to low and let it work its magic for 6 hours
Pat yourself on the back for making an incredible meal
