Title:
Vegan Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained
1 (8 ounce) package/tub Vegan cream cheese
1 cup Vegan parmesan cheese
1/2 cup Vegan mayonnaise, or olive oil
1 clove garlic, peeled
3/4 teaspoon dried dill weed. cilantro, basil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Place the artichoke hearts in a food processor and process until smooth. Process the vegan cream cheese, vegan parmesan cheese, mayonnaise, garlic and dill with the artichoke hearts until the mixture is smooth and well blended. Transfer to a 9 inch pie pan.
Bake in the preheated oven 10 to 15 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly brown.
