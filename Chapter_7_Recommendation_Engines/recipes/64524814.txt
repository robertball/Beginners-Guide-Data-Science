Title:
Chocolate Chip Peppermint Frappuccino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Tb of chocolate chips
2 Tb of torani peppermint syrup
3 Tb of chocolate syrup like the stuff u put in milk
2 cup of ice
1/2 of milk
1/4 of coffee if you want decaf do 1/4 of milk
whip cream (optional)

Directions:
In blender ad all item except chocolate chips.
Blend it till ice is blended.
Then add chocolate chip.
Blend it till it mixed up.
