Title:
Doughty's Sweet Potato Casserole with Pecan Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of sweet potatoes (about 4 medium)
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs beaten
1 tsp. vanilla
1/3 cup milk
Topping:
1/3 cup melted butter
1 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup flour
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
1. Boil and drain potatoes (can put 1/2 cup ginger ale in the water)
2. Beat potatoes
3. Mix in sugar, butter, eggs, vanilla, and milk
4. Grease a 13" x 9" baking dish
5. Spread potatoes evenly in dish
6. Mix topping ingredients together and sprinkle over potatoes
7. Bake for 25 minutes or more at 350 degrees (top will be brown)
It may take up to one hour to cooke

Submitter: 
Megan Fellows
Description: 
Sweet and delicious sweet potato and pecan casserole for every day or holiday meals.
