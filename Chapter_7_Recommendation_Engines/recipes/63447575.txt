Title:
Fred’s Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sausage
Basil leaves
Chicken broth
Potatoes
Salt & Pepper
Diced parsley (optional)
Dough
2eggs
2 tab water
1 cup flour

Directions:
Gently mix eggs and water add flour
a little at a time to form a ball, flour counter
Sprinkle the ball of dough with flour roll out into a
6 to 8 inch circle roll thin…thinner then pie crust
Cut into strips of 2 inch wide squares, let dry a little
Using a 5qt stock pot add chicken broth or water half way
Add 6 to 8 potatoes cut into chunks
Slice sausage into thin slices
Bring to boil then turn heat to med low with lid on
Simmer until just about done then add
dough squares last 10 minutes, add parsley if using
