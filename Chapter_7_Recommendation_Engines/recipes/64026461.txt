Title:
chili rubbed pork, coconut milk sweet potatoes, pineapple pesto red onion salsa Recipe

Ingredients:
pork
sweet potatoes
coconut milk
pineapple
red onions
pesto

Directions:
Peel and cut sweet potatoes, boil, stir in coconut milk and mash
chop pineapples and red onions. Mix with pesto
Rub pork with chili powder and grill
