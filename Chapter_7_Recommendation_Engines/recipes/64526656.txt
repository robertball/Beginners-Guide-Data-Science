Title:
Jim's Best Slow Cooker Pork Loin Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pound pork loin roast
3 T Worcester sauce
1 t soy sauce
1 t El Yukatan hot sauce ( I like the flavor!)
3 T balsamic vinegar
2 t minced garlic
1 Pkg Lipton onion soup mix
1 cup water
1 tsp corn starch for thickening

Directions:
Put the pork in a slow cooker. Put in the water and cornstarch. Take the other ingredients and pour over the pork loin roast. Turn on slow cooker on low for 7-8 hours

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
This pork loin is so tender and the sauce it creates is so tasty!I had guests spooning the sauce out of the slow cooker! It's my favorite pork recipe! You can also use pork chops
