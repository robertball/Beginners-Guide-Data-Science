Title:
"I have no food in the house" Vegetarian Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 oz. can of vegetarian baked beans
8 small russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/4 cup melted margarine
1 tbsp dehydrated minced onions
2 tbsp. BBQ sauce
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/8 tsp. black pepper
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Arrange cubed potatoes in a 9x13 baking dish coated with no-stick spray.
In a medium bowl, mix baked beans, margarine, dehydrated onion, BBQ sauce, garlic powder and pepper. Pour mixture over potatoes.
Bake in the preheated oven for 60 minutes. Then, top with shredded cheese and bake for 10 minutes more or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Sola
Description: 
I made this when the cupboards were looking rather bare. Baked beans and potatoes will always fill you up though, and it's all in what you add.
