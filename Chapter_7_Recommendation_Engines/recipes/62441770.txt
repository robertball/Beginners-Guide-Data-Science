Title:
Italian Wedding Soup Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 egg
Â¼ cup breadcrumbs
2 Tbsp. parmesan cheese
1 tsp. basil
1/3 cup onion, grated
6 cups chicken broth
2 cups water
2-3 cups chopped kale/spinach
1 cup orzo pasta
1 shredded carrot

Directions:
Combine meat, egg, breadcrumbs, cheese, basil and onion. Shape into mini sized meatballs and fully cook in frying pan.
In large pot, bring broth to a boil and combine remaining ingredients. Add cooked meatballs and ENJOY.
