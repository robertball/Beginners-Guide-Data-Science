Title:
Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup apple cider
2.3 cup plain low-fat yogurt
1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
2 cups chopped apples (granny smith or gala)
2 cups chopped pears (bosc)
1 cup seedless grapes-halved
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup golden raisins
1/3 cup coarsely chopped pecans (toasted)
6 boston lettuce leaves

Directions:
1. Bring cider to a boil over high heat in a small suacepan. Cook until reduced to about 3 tbl., 15-20 minutes. Cool completely.
2. Mix cider, yogurt, and cinnamon until blended. Set aside.
3. Mix aples, pears, grapes, celery, raisins and pecans in bowl. Add yogurt mixture, tossing to coat. Serve salad on lettuce-lined plates.
TIP: You can either peel the apples and pears--or not.
