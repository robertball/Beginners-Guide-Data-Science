Title:
Cranberry Fizz Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32 ounce bottle cranberry cocktail juice
1 2-litre bottle 7-up

Directions:
Mix juice and 7-up together and serve!
You can substitute ginger ale for the 7-up. Add some orange slices on top or a few scoops of sherbert to dress it up.

Submitter: 
MW
Description: 
This is a super simple way to make a yummy punch. Add fruit slices to dress it up or alcohol to punch it up a notch!
