Title:
Quick Chicken Tortilla Soup - Ole! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. diced, or shredded, cooked chicken
1 tsp. EVOO
2 med. zucchini, sliced 1/2"
1 med. red onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 bell pepper, diced
1 28 oz. can stewed Mexican-seasoned tomatoes
1 15 oz. can corn
3 cans low-sodium chicken broth
1 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 TB chili powder
4 6" corn tortillas
2 TB lime juice
1 c. tightly-packed, finely-chopped cilantro

Directions:
Lightly-saute onion, garlic, bell pepper in EVOO. Add zucchini til semi-tender. Remove from pan.
Meanwhile, in same pan, heat another 1 TB EVOO, and fry corn tortillas in batches, flipping to brown. Drain on paper towel, and cut into 1" strips.
In lg. pot, stir remaining ingredients and seasoning, bring to a boil, add sauteed veggies, and simmer 30 min. 5-10 min. b4 serving stir in tortilla strips, lime juice, and cilantro. Serve w/ grated cheese, a lime wedge, and sour cream, if desired.
