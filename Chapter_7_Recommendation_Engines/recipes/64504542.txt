Title:
Dirt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg chocolate cake mix
1 pkg (large) Jell-o Chocolate Instant pudding
3 C cold milk
1 tub (8 oz) Cool Whip Topping, thawed
6 worm-shaped chewy fruit snacks

Directions:
Prepare cake batter and bake in 13 x 9 pan as directed on package.  Cool completely.
Beat pudding mix and milk in large bowl with phisk 2 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes.  Stir in cool whip.  Cut 3/4 of the cake into 1/2 inch cubes.  Layer half each of the cake cubes and pudding mixture in large glass bowl; repeat layers.
Crumble remaining cake; sprinkle over dessert.  Refrigerate 15 min.
Top with fruit snacks just before serving.  Refrigerate any leftovers.
