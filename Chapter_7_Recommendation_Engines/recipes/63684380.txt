Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef (cooked)
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped
1/2 head lettuce (largely chopped)
1/2 cup sliced black olives
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 bag Taco Flavored Doritos (or Nacho Cheese flavored Doritos), crushed
1/2 - 1 cup French Dressing

Directions:
In a skillet, brown ground beef and onion, set aside and let it cool
Prepare and chop vegetables
Mix lettuce, tomato, olives, beef and cheese in a bowl
Just before serving, add Doritos and dressing
