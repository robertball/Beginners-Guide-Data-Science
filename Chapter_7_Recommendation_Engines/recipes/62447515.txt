Title:
Adobo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/462323.jpg
Ingredients:
Equal amounts of:
onion powder
garlic powder
dried oregano
Half amounts of:
black pepper
sea salt
(For example, 1 Tablespoon of the first set of ingredients & 1/2 Tablespoon of the second set.)

Directions:
Put everything together in an empty seasoning shaker, close lid tightly &shake like crazy before each use! Fun right?   :-)
