Title:
Strawberry Banana Sandwich Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bread
20 g butter, softened
2 Tbsp strawberry jam
1 pc banana

Directions:
Preheat the sandwich maker.
Spread butter on one side of each bread.
Spread strawberry jam on the non-buttered side of 2 breads.
Slice the banana and arrange it on top of the strawberry jam.
Top with the other slice of bread with the buttered side facing up. Put the sandwiches on the sandwich maker then toast for about 5 minutes or until it is brown and crispy.
Slice and serve.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
This is a very simple sandwich toast recipe. A little bit sweet but delicious. I've used strawberry jam but you can also try other types of jam like blueberry, orange, mango, or whatever you have at home.
