Title:
Sour Cream Raisin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519655.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1 c. sour cream
1 tbls. flour
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 cup raisins, chopped
3 eggs (2 yolks and 1 whole egg)

Directions:
Boil until thick. Cool.  Pour into baked pie crust.  Top with meringue.  Beat 2 egg whites until stiff and gradually add 4 tablespoons sugar.  Spread on pie and brown.  Bake in moderate oven at 350 degrees for 12-15 minutes.
