Title:
Bowl of Crap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4518470.jpg
Ingredients:
1) Two (2) Tbsp of peanut butter; 2) Two (2) Tbsp of cottage cheese; 3) One (1) Tbsp of honey; 4) Handful of blueberries; 5) Packet of your favorite uncooked oatmeal (mine is apple & maple)

Directions:
Combine ingredients until it looks a crazy night of drinking.

Submitter: 
Michelle Hoffman-Zubatch
Description: 
Seriously, probably the best snack I have ever had. Son named it because it contains whatever is available. This is the original recipe. Hits the spot after a serious workout. This is not for the faint of heart. You will need a nap after eating.
