Title:
Cinnamon Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Granny Smith apples, peeled and sliced
1/2 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
1. Toss together first 4 ingredients in a large zip-top plastic bag, tossing to coat apples.
2. Cook apple mixture, 2 Tbsp. water, and 1 Tbsp. butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally, 8 to 10 minutes or until apples are tender.

Submitter: 
Jessica Tackitt
Description: 
My kids say that these cinnamon apples are even better than the ones found in restaurants. Very yummy and so EASY to make! These are great for breakfast, lunch or dinner and even dessert if served with ice cream or cool whip.
