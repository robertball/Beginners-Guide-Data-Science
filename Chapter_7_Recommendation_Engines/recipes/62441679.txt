Title:
Butternut Squash Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. macaroni (or mini penne/tubatini)
Salt
1 Tbsp. olive oil
2 Tbsp. butter
½ medium onion (grated)
1 tsp. thyme
3 Tbsp. flour
2 cups chicken stock
10 oz. cooked butternut squash, diced
1 cup half-and-half
2 cups sharp cheddar, grated
½ cup grated Parmigiano Reggiano
¼ tsp. nutmeg
Black pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta
Heat olive oil in frying pan. Add thyme and grate onion directly into frying pan. Cook onion in oil for 1-2 minutes and whisk in chicken stock.
Puree onion mixture with cooked butternut squash.
Make rue with flour and butter in frying pan.
Pour pureed mixture into frying pan and warm through.
Stir in half-and-half and bring sauce to a bubble. Stir in cheeses and season with salt, nutmeg and pepper.
