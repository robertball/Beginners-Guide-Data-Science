Title:
Retro hamburger on a Bun Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/8211683.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb ground hamburger lean
1 lg onion diced smalL
I / 2 cup diced peppers ( could be small colorful ones or just 1 lg green one)
1 talb minced garlic
4 talb steak sauce
4 talb ketchup
2 talb  worchestire sauce
1 talb balsamic vinegar
1 tea salt and pepper
1 tea cumin
1 tea garlic lovers spice
1 tea basil
1 tea jamician jerk spice
Do not fret if you do not have these spices ... improvise
8-10 Nice large rolls / hamburger buns like keiser

Directions:
Lg frying pan put 1/2 cup water and place onion  garlic peppers...Cover and cook till slight soft add ground beef and rest of ingredients... simmer 20 min
Serves 6-8

Gina Lee Lafreniere
