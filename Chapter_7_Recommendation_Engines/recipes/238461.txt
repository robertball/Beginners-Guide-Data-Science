Title:
Coconut Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1959063.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vanilla-flavored coconut milk beverage (such as SilkÂ®)
2 tablespoons cocoa powder
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Stir coconut milk beverage, cocoa powder, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon together in a saucepan over medium heat until cocoa and sugar are completely dissolved and mixture is heated through, 5 to 10 minutes. Pour hot cocoa into a mug.

 Cocoa leads to a bittersweet chocolate taste and the coconut milk adds a hint of richness.
