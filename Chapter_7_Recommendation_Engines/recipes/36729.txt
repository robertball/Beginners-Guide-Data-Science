Title:
Pasta Salad with Shrimp and Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977614.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound medium shrimp, cooked and peeled
4 cups cooked medium seashell pasta
2 cups chopped plum tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup chopped shallots
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large bowl. Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Cooking Light magazine
Description: 
Ripe plum tomatoes and aromatic basil accent fresh shrimp. Farfalle, radiatore, rotini, or any other short, small pasta works well in place of seashells.
