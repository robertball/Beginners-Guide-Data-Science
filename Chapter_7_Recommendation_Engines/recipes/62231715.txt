Title:
Seafood Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/321550.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Onion, chopped
1 c. carrots, chopped
1 c. Celery, chopped
2 tab butter
1 tab bacon fat
4 or 5 strips cooked bacon
Can of cream of shrimp
Can of cream of potatoes
1 can half and half
1 can milk or less depending on how thick you want it.
4 oz. cream cheese
1 c. Green onion, chopped
½ pound shrimp
½ pound scallops
1 c. Spinach
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Cook bacon until crisp, drain reserving 1 tablespoon, crumble & set aside
2. Add bacon grease & butter to stock pot, melt
3. Add chopped onion, carrots & celery sauté 5 minutes or until soft
4. Add soups, milk, cream & cream cheese to sauté. Mix until blended & simmering.
5. Add scallops & simmer 3 minutes
6. Add shrimp, green onion & spinach. Simmer 3 to 5 minutes longer until shrimp is no longer pink
7.Salt & pepper to taste.
