Title:
Strawberry jello salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box of strawberry jello
2 cups of boiling water
1 cup of frozen or fresh strawberries, sliced
3 medium size ripe bananas, mashed
1 20oz can of crushed pineapple, undrained
1 8oz of sour cream
1 3oz cream cheese, softened
2TBLS of sugar

Directions:
Boil 2 cups of water. In a large bowl combine the powdered jello and the boiling water. Stir til dissolved. Add sliced strawberries, crushed pineapple and mashed bananas and stir well. Pour  jello mixture into a casserole dish and set in fridge til firm. In a seperate bowl stir sour cream, cream cheese and sugar well. When jello has firmed pour the creamy mixture over the jello and spread. Then return to fridge for an hour or til ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Cheryl's Cooking Now!
Description: 
This is a cool dish to serve when it's hot. Can eat it as a side dish or dessert.
