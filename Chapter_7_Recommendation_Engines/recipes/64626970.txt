Title:
Pizza Crust - low carb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4468074.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Italian Style blend shredded cheese
2 cups Pizza blend shredded cheese
4 eggs
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp basil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Mix all ingredients together.  Place about a tablespoon of the mixture on no stick foil on cookie sheets, flatten out and bake until golden brown.....about 15-20 min. I didn't time it, just watched.
