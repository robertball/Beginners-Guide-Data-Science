Title:
Ham and Butternut Squash Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg. Whole Wheat Spaghetti
2 Tbls. Olive oil
4 oz. Smoked Ham (cut into thin matchsticks)
3-4 Cloves of Garlic(thinly sliced)
Red Pepper Flakes(to taste)
1 1/2-2 Cups Chicken Broth
3 Cups Butternut Squash(diced to 1")
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 Cup Marscarpone Cheese
1-2 Tbls. Italian Parsley(chopped)
Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese(freshly grated)

Directions:
Bring water to boil to cook pasta.
Saute ham in olive oil till it starts to just brown on edges. Add garlic and red pepper and cook until garlic edges start to turn. Add chicken broth and squash along with salt and pepper. Cover and bring to solid simmer and cook 3-4 min. until squash is just tender.(Add pasta to water when you cover squash0.Lower heat to LOW after squash has cooked and add Marscarpone cheese;stir until cheese melts. Add parsley and squash mix to already cooked and drained pasta(do not rinse) and mix gently as to not break up squash. Let sit covered as to absorb sauce. Serve with Parm-Reg. cheese grated on top.
