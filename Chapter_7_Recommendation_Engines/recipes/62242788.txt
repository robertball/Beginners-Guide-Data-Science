Title:
Steak house rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/328163.jpg
Ingredients:
* 1 3/4 cups warm milk
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 3 tablespoon sugar
* 2 tablespoons oil
* 4 1/2 teaspoons yeast (same as 2 packages yeast)
* 4 cups flour

Directions:
Mix all and knead on a floured surface or in mixer with kneading hook for about 10 minutes.
Let rise 20 minutes and knead again.
Make into rolls, by spraying hands with cooking spray, and then rolling dough into a ball shape. Place on a lg. greased pizza stone or cookie sheet .
Let rise 20 minutes or until double in size.
Bake at 350, for 15-20 minutes. Spread butter on top immediately out of the oven.

Submitter: 
tigershoes
Description: 
These are soooo good.   They remind me of the rolls you get at steak houses. They are Super soft. I don't sub whole wheat flour in these ever because for some reason they just turn out bad (dry).  So I don't suggest altering the dough very much.  These are by far one of my favorite recipes!
