Title:
Marilyn's Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c chopped celery
1/2 c chopped onion
2 chicken bouillon cubes
4c water
1c frozen carrots
2.5 c potatoes
10 oz frozen vegetables
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1lb Velveeta

Directions:
Combine water, celery, onion, and bouillon and simmer 20 minutes. (CP low 2 hours)
Add all vegetables and simmer 40 minutes. (CP low 2-3 hours)
Add soup and Velveeta and simmer until heated through. (CP low 1 hour)
