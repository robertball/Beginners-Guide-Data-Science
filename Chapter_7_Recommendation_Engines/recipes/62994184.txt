Title:
Julie's Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs. Butter
1 Onion, chopped
2 cloves Garlic, minced
1/2 lb. Chicken, cut up (I use 1 or 2 frozen chicken breasts)
1/2 lb. cured Ham, cut in cubes
6 Pork Sausages, cut up (or 10 oz. andouille sausage casing removed)
2 stalks Celery, chopped
1 Bay Leaf
1/2 tsp. Dried Parsley Flakes
1/4 tsp. Dried Thyme
1/8 tsp. Ground Cloves
1 cup uncooked Rice (regular, not minute)
1 can Diced Tomatoes, DON'T DRAIN
1-1/2 quarts Chicken Broth (1 big can)
2 tsp. Beef Boullion
1/2 tsp. Tabasco
3/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Melt butter in a heavy pot or dutch oven. Add onion, garlic, chicken, sausage and ham. Brown slowly, stirring frequently.
Add celery, bay leaf, parsley, thyme, cloves, and rice; cook about 5 minutes.
Add tomatoes, broth, boullion, Tabasco and salt; bring to boil. Cover and simmer gently a half hour or until rice is tender. Stir often to mix well. Season to taste.

Submitter: 
Julie Kimball Fitzgerald
Description: 
Don't let the ingredient list intimidate you. It's very quick to put together. Just allow time for it to simmer. Serve with a warm loaf of crusty bread. ENJOY!!
