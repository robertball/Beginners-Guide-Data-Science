Title:
Shrimp Chowder- with Shrimp Bisque flavor! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sliced celery
1/2 cup finely diced onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup butter
1 tablespoon sweet paprika
2 tablespoons tomato paste
3 dashes hot pepper sauce (to taste)
3 dashes Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning TM
3 fluid ounces brandy
1 1/2 cups cubed potatoes, cooked to desired tenderness
1 lb. frozen raw shrimp, peeled and deveined, thawed, drained and cut into 1/3 pieces
3.25 oz frozen corn
1/4 cup dry sherry or white wine
1/4 cup chicken or seafood broth
salt and pepper to taste
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, diced
1 cup milk

Directions:
In a large stock pot saute celery, onions and garlic until tender in butter.
Add paprika, tomato paste,hot pepper sauce, Worcestershire,Old Bay and stir to combine.
Add brandy, and cook for 1 minute.  Add potatoes, shrimp, corn, sherry/wine, broth and salt and pepper.  Cook until brandy/wine reduces and Shrimp are opaque and cooked through.
Add cream cheese and milk; and stir over low heat until cream cheese is completely melted, stirring occasionally and then serve.

Submitter: 
~Gina~
Description: 
A cross between a flavorful shrimp bisque and a hearty chowder this version uses shrimp bisque seasonings,brandy and sherry, along with cream cheese and milk to create this shrimp and potato (and corn!) chowder.Modified from: Shrimp Chowder
