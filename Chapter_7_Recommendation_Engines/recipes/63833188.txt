Title:
Spinach Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065348.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz Baby Spinach, shredded
1 cup Craisins (dried cranberries or dried pomegranates)
1/4 cup white sugar
1/8 cup rice vinegar
1/8 cup cider vinegar
1/4 cup vegetable or olive oil
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
2 Tablespoons finely chopped sweet onion
1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
1/4 cup finely shredded Parmesan or Feta cheese

Directions:
In a small bowl whisk together sugar, rice vinegar, cider vinegar, oil, lemon juice and Italian seasoning until well-blended and sugar is dissolved. Stir onions and Craisins into the sauce and allow to sit for 30 minutes.
In a large bowl toss spinach, cheese, bell pepper and sauce until evenly coated.
Serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
This salad idea isn't unique. There are lots of them out there using craisins, but my light bulb moment was when I saw the Craisins on the shelf in the store and I immediately thought about my spinach in the fridge that needed to be used.   This was a big hit at our table.
