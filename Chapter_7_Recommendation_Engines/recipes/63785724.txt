Title:
Gail Lee's Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb mushrooms
1/2 large onion
1 chicken oxo cube
1 cup water
3 Tbsp margarine
1/4 cup flour
2 cups of milk
White pepper
Paprika
1 egg

Directions:
Slice mushrooms and chop onion.  Fry in large teflon pan.  Add chicken oxo cube and 1 cup water.  Simmer 20 minutes.  Pour into 4 cup measuring cup and add water to 3 cup level.
In large teflon pan melt margarine, add flour and mix well.  Slowly add milk.  Add pepper and egg.  When mixture is very thick, add mushroom/water mixture slowly to the thickened rue.
