Title:
Layered Tex Mex Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb russet potatoes, peeled and sliced 1/4 inch thick
3 tbsp olive oil, divided
1 pkg taco seasoning, divided (approx 1 oz)
Salt and pepper to taste
2 12 oz jars pickled jalapenos
1 16 oz package sour cream or plain Greek yogurt
1/2 cup salsa verde
6 avocados
2 tbsp minced garlic
2 tbsp fresh lime juice
2 cups thinly sliced lettuce
1 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 cup chopped scallions
2 3.8 oz cans sliced black olives
16 oz pico de gallo
2 cups crushed corn chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 with a baking sheet inside.
Toss potatoes with 2 tbsp oil, 2 tbsp taco seasoning, salt, and pepper.  Transfer potatoes to hot baking sheet and roast until fork tender, 20 minutes.
Drain jalapenos, reserving 1/4 cup juice.  Pour juice over hot potatoes and chill until cool.
Combine sour cream, salsa verde, and 2 tbsp taco seasoning.
Mash avocados with garlic, then lightly season with salt.
Layer potatoes, avocado mixture, jalapenos, and sour cream mixture in a large glass bowl.  Cover and chill until ready to serve.
Whisk together lime juice, remaining oil, and taco seasoning; toss with lettuce, cilantro, scallions, and olives.
Top salad with lettuce mixture, pico de gallo, and corn chips.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
For the most impressive presentation, use a large, clear, and straight-sided bowl.
