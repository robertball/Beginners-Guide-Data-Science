Title:
Roasted Sweet Potato with Cinnamon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium sweet potatoes
2 tbs cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 degrees
Place rinsed whole sweet potatos into the oven.
Cook for 50 minutes or until tender.
Remove and cool.
Remove skins. Slice or mash them & sprinkle with cinnamon.
Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Adelma Lilliston
Description: 
Great simple snack instead of sweets. Super easy and satisfying.
