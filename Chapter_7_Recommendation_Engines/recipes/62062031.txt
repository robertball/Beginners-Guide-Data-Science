Title:
Heirloom Blueberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup buttermilk
3 cups flour
3 tsp. baking powder
2 cups blueberries

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350F.
2.  Grease 13" x 9" baking pan (or use cooking spray)
3.  Combine ingredients in the order listed, stirring after each addition.
4.  Pour into prepared pan
5.  Bake 35-45 minutes.
Frost if desired.  I use vanilla frosting and dust with cinnamon-sugar.
