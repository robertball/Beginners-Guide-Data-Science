Title:
Dreamy Drowned Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med strawberries quartered
1/2 blood orange sectioned (or any orange)
1/2 banana sliced
6 blueberries
6 rasberries
4 SunSweetÂ© Cherry Essence prunes (optional)
3 tbsp heavy whipping cream
4 tbsp half and half
4 tbsp skim milk
1/4 cup fat free cottage cheese
1/8 tsp pure vanilla extract
1 1/2 tsps splenda sweetner
dash ground cloves
dash allspice
dash cinnamon

Directions:
In a small single-serving salad bowl combine fruit, and set aside.
In a second small bowl, combine dairy ingredients, splenda, vanilla, and spices.
Pour the dairy mixture over the fruit, being sure to work cottage cheese down into fruit.
Add a dash of any or all of the above spices, and enjoy immediately!

Submitter: 
Lady Lerandia
Description: 
Berries & oranges are drowned in heavy cream, spices, and cottage cheese with a hint of vanilla to make it an especially dreamy brunch, lunch, or snack. And surprise, fruit is good for you too.
