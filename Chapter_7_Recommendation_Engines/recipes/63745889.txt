Title:
warm strawberry teacup cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz/ 1/2 c butter softened plus extra for greasing
4 tbsp strawberry conserve
4 oz/ 1/2 c superfine sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
4 oz/ 1/2 c self rising white- flour
1 lb strawberries
confectioners sugar for dusting

Directions:
greasy 6 teacups and spoon 2 tsps of conserve in each one.
beat butter and sugar together until lightly fluffy. gradually add eggs then vanilla extract. sift in flour then berries.
stand the c in a roasting pan, then pour in hot water 2/3 of the way full. bake for 40 mins or until well risen in a 350 oven. let cool for 5 mins and top with confectioners sugar
