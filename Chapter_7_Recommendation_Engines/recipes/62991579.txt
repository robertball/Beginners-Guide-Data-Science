Title:
Meringues Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
pinch of cream or tartar
3/4 cup superfine granulated sugar, divided
1/4 cup bittersweet chocolate, melted
disposable pastry bag with star tip
parchment paper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
Beat egg white with vanilla and a pinch each of cream of tartar and salt with an electric mixer at med high speed until in just holds soft peaks. Add 2 tbsp super fine sugar, a little at a time, beating. Then increase speed to high and beat until meringue holds stiff, glossy peaks. Fold in 2 tbsp superfine sugar gently but throroughly.
Transfer meringue to a pastry bag fitted with tip. Place a dab of meringue mixture from bowl under corner so parchment paper to make it stick to the pan. Pipe 1 inch wide stars onto paper about 1 inch apart. Bake until firm. about one to tow hours. Remove and let cool on paper.
Dip coole dcookies into melted chocolate. Let cool until chocolate is firm
