Title:
Chocolate Milk Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 parts Vodka
2 parts Kahlua
2 parts Creme de Cacao
Hefty splash of milk

Directions:
Pour all ingredients in a shaker filled with ice cubes & shake.  Pour & enjoy!
