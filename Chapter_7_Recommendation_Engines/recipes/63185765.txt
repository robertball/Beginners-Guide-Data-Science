Title:
Rich Chocolate Peanut Butter Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups butter (no substitutes), softened
1 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
9 cups confectioners' sugar
2 1/2 cups baking cocoa
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup milk

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and peanut butter. Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar, cocoa and vanilla. Add enough milk until frosting reaches spreading consistency.
