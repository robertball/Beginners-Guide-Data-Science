Title:
Delicious Vegan Hot Chocolate (Almond Milk + Water) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Almond Milk
1 cup water
1/3 cup agave (to taste)
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions:
Bring the almond milk, sugar, cocoa powder, salt, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper to a simmer in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Remove from the heat and whisk until frothy. Serve immediately.
