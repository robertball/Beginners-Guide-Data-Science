Title:
Crockpot Chicken, Rice and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breast cooked and diced
2 boxes uncle bens herb flavor white and wild rice w/ seasoning packet
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can celery soup
1 can water
1 can veg all mixed vegetables drained

Directions:
Boil chicken breast, let cool and dice into bite size pieces
Add Chicken, soups, water, and veggies to crockpot. Mix well. stir in seasoning packets from rice, add rice stir well. cook on low-med for approx 5 hours.
