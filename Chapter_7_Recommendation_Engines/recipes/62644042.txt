Title:
Mexican Standoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 tbs. chili powder
2 tsp. garlic powder
pinch red pepper flakes (opt.)
1 medium chopped onion
1 can tomato sauce
1 can niblets corn, drained
1 small box corn bread mix

Directions:
Brown ground beef. Add onion while meat is browning. Drain. Remove from heat.  Stir in spices, corn and tomato sauce. Pour into 8"x8" pan. Prepare corn bread mix according to directions.  Pour over meat.  Bake at temperature on package directions until corn bread is done.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cindy D In TN
Description: 
This was a dish that I invented long ago.  It can be varied many ways. Example, add cheese to the cornbread mix or even jalapeno peppers. Quick, easy and tasty.
