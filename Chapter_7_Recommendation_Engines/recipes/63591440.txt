Title:
Sweet & Sour Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken cubed
2 Tbs olive oil
1 C green & red pepper
1 C carrot strips
1 garlic clove, minced
1 Tbs cornstarch
1/4 C lite soy sauce
1 can (8 oz) pineapple chunks in juice
3 Tbs vinegar
3 Tbs brown sugar
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1 1/2 C minute rice

Directions:
Cook & stir chicken and garlic in hot oil until well browned.
Add peppers and carrots. Cook & stir 1-2 minutes.
Mix cornstarch with soy sauce and add to pan with pineapple and juice, vinegar, sugar and ginger. Bring to a full boil.
Meanwhile prepare rice as directed. Serve chicken over rice.
