Title:
Santa Fe Bean and Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 oz) can black beans, drained
1 (14 oz) can kidney beans, drained
1 (14 oz) can great northern beans, drained
1 (14 oz) can pinto beans, drained
1 (14 oz) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes
1 (14 oz or thereabouts) jar salsa
3 (14 oz) cans chicken broth
2 t. cumin
1 t. red pepper flakes
2 c. cooked diced chicken
Garnishes:
Chopped onion
Sour Cream
Shredded Cheese
Corn Chips

Directions:
Dump all the ingredients except for the garnishes in a large pot.  Heat on medium high until all is heated through, about 10 minutes.  Serve with whatever garnishes you would like.

Submitter: 
Lisa Meece
Description: 
Like a fajita in a pot!  This recipe is so versatile--you can choose any type of bean you want, skip the chicken if you want a vegetarian dish, add less broth to make it more a chili or a stew, and add whatever garnishes you want!
