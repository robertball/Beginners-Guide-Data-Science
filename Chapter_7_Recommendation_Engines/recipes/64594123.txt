Title:
Blueberry Blackberry Crumb Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups blueberries
1 cup blackberries
1 cup sugar
3 tablespoons tapioca (2-3 T)
2 tablespoons margarine
1/2 cup flour (1/2 to 3/4)
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 1/2 tablespoons margarine
1 pie crust

Directions:
Heat the oven to 400 degrees F.
Combine berries with 1 cup sugar and tapioca and set aside for 10 minutes or so.
Turn filling into crust and dot surface with butter.
Mix flour, remaining sugar, salt, baking powder, cinnamon and nutmeg in bowl.
Add margarine and mix with fork or pie crust cutter till crumbly.
Cover pie with topping.
Bake about 40 minutes until filling is bubbling and crust is nicely browned.
