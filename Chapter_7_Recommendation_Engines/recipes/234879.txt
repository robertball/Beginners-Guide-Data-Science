Title:
Mango Tahini Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2519462.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen mango chunks, slightly thawed
1 cup water
2 tablespoons tahini
2 tablespoons lime juice

Directions:
Blend mango, water, tahini, and lime juice together in a blender until smooth.
