Title:
Honey Mustard Chicken sheet pan dinner with roasted potatoes and corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 thin sliced chicken breasts
1/4 C mayonnaise
1 TBS mustard
1 TBS honey
1 C Seasoned bread crumbs
4 Golden potatoes cut into wedges
2 TBS olive oil
1 tsp seasoned salt
1 10 ounce bag of frozen corn
1 small onion chopped
2 TBS butter melted.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
In a plastic bag, add potatoes, oil and seasoned salt. Toss to coat.
Add potatoes to 1/3 of the sheet pan.
In a bowl, mix onions, frozen corn and melted butter.  Mix well so butter is incorporated all the way through. Spray the other side of the sheet pan with oil add corn. Leaving space for the chicken. Bake potatoes and corn for 15 minutes.
Mean while prepare chicken. Sprinkle both sides with salt and pepper.
Mix mayonnaise, honey and Mustard together. Place bread crumbs in a separate bowl. . And Dip chicken into wet mix and then dredge in the bread crumbs.
Remove pan from oven, toss corn mixture and flip potatoes.
Spray the center of the pan with cooking oil and add chicken.
Bake for 20 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink.

Description: 
Easy one pan dinner that pairs chicken with corn and potatoes. If you use a thick pieces of chicken you’ll need to cook the chicken along with the potatoes and corn. If you use a thinner cut you’ll have browned potatoes and corn with just perfect chicken.
