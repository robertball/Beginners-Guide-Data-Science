Title:
Everyday Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/833574.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
1 mango, peeled and cubed
1 cup frozen blueberries
2 Tablespoons Hi-Protein Hemp Powder
2 Teaspoons Flax Seed Oil
1 Tablespoon nut butter
1 1/2 Tablespoons Chia Seeds
1 Cup Water
5 Ice Cubes

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a blender and puree until smooth (about 1 minute).

Submitter: 
squawk93
Description: 
Our search for a more nutrient rich breakfast led us to this smoothie formula. It is a tasty way to get more fruits, fiber, and protein into our diet. We also found that we weren't as hungry halfway to lunch as we normally were.
