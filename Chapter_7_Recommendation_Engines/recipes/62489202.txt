Title:
Creamed Hashbrowns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (30 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes
1 pint half & half
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup diced onion
salt & pepper to taste (may also add some garlic salt)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a large saucepan and heat together over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until hash browns are thawed and all ingredients are mixed well.
Pour contents into casserole dish sprayed with cooking oil.
Bake at 350 degrees for approximately one hour or until golden brown on top.

Submitter: 
Marie
Description: 
The most delectable 'mashed potatoes.' A must-have at our Thanksgiving table.
