Title:
Simple Bechamel Style White Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30g/1oz unsalted Butter
30g/1oz Flour
500ml Whole Milk
pinch Nutmeg

Directions:
Melt butter in saucepan, over low/medium heat;
Gradually sprinkle in the flour, stirring constantly and cook 1 to 2 minutes;
Remove from heat and SLOWLY add milk, whisking to avoid lumps ;
Return to medium heat, bring to boil, stirring constantly;
Cook for 3 to 4 minutes or until sauce thickens and can coat the back of a spoon;
Season with salt, black pepper and nutmeg;
Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
An easy roux to ease the way for recipes requiring a white style sauce.
