Title:
Sweet and Spicey Chicken Barbecue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  3lb chicken cut up into parts trimmed of fat and skin removed.
1/4 cup water
3/4 cup ketchup
3 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp vinegar
1 tbsp lemon juice
2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp salt
1 tsp dry mustard powder
1 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp minced garlic

Directions:
Stir all together to combine ( except chicken ).
Bring to boil in saucepan over medium heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer  10 min  stiring oaccaisionally. Cool slightly.
Arrange chicken in a 9 x 13 inch baking dish and pour sauce over chicken making sure it is well coated on top and underneath chicken pieces.Bake @ 375F for one hour basting occaisionally .

Submitter: 
BramptonMommyof2
Description: 
A deliscious sticky sweet and spicey sauce for chicken pieces or chicken breast. Whipped up for something different.
