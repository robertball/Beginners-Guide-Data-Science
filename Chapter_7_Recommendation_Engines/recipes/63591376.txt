Title:
food.com low cal low fat pizza crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup warm water
1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive oil
1/4 tsp salt
2 garlic cloves minced
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried oregano
2 cups bread flour
2 tsp fast rising active dry yeast

Directions:
1.Add all the ingredients in the bread pan of bread machine. Process according to manufacturer's instructions for a dough setting.
2. When bread machine has completed, remove it to a lightly oiled surface and knead the dough several times and form into an oval and cover with a towel and let rest for 10 minutes.
3.Roll and stretch the dough into a 14-inch circle. Place the dough on the prepared peel. Brush the dough with some olive oil and layer your ingredients. Bake for approximately 15 minutes or until golden brown on the bottom and the top is bubbly.
Makes one large pizza or 16 servings.
