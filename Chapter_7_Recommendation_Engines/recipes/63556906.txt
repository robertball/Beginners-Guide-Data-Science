Title:
grannys beef stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds cubed beef stew meat
1/4 cup flour
3 tablespoons butter
4 cubes beef bouillon, crumbled
4 cups water
1 cup dry red wine
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
1 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 Tablespoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
4 carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
4 stalks celery, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 large onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic
12 oz frozen peas

Directions:
mix herbs, salt, pepper, and flour.  Dredge meat in flour mixture In a large pot or dutch oven, cook beef in butter (can use olive oil or other oil) over medium heat until brown. Do in batches if necessary so the meat browns.  Deglaze pan with red wine (meat can be removed for this step but it isn't necessary.  Add water, bullion, bay leaves, vegetables and chopped garlic. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer or bake in 300 degree oven for a couple of hours.  Stir peas in right before serving

Submitter: 
Anisa
Description: 
Beef, carrots, potatoes and celery are seasoned with rosemary and parsley in this simple, stovetop stew.Modified from: Beef Stew VI
