Title:
Skillet Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fat
1 med onion chopped
1/2 med green pepper chopped
1/2 lb ground beef
1 cup raw rice
2 cans tomato sauce
1 3/4 cups hot water
1 tsp prepared mustard
1 tsp salt
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt fat in skillet Add onion, green pepper, beef, and rice. Stir over high heat until lightly browned. Add tomato sauce and remaining ingreedients. Mix well. Bring quickly to a boil. Cover tightly and simmer 25 minutes.
