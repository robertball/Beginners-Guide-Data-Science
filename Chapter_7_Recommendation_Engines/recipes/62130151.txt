Title:
Whole Wheat Banana-Lemon Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups whole-wheat flour
5 tsp baking powder
1.5 tsp salt
1.5 very ripe bananas
.5 cup butter or margarine
1.5 cups sugar
1.5 cup milk
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 tsp lemon extract
1 cup lemon juice, divided
.5 cup icing sugar, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and grease 2 9-by-5 loaf pans.
In a large bowl, combine flours, baking powder, and salt.  Set aside.
In a medium-sized bowl, mash the bananas.  Add the butter and sugar and cream together until smooth.  Stir in milk, vanilla, and lemon extracts.
Stir together the wet and dry ingredients and divide evenly between the two loaf pans.  Sprinkle the tops with lemon juice and icing sugar.
Bake for 45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.  During baking, remove the cakes every ten minutes and sprinkle with lemon juice and icing sugar.
