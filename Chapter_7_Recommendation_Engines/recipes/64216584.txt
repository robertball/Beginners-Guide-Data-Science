Title:
Almond Butter Vegan Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup almond butter
2 tablespoons flaxseed + 3 tablespoons water
½ teaspoon sea salt
½ teaspoon baking soda
½ cup coconut palm sugar
½ teaspoon vanilla
½ cup pecans, crushed
½ cup of “Enjoy Life” mega chocolate
chunks

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Line a small dish (8×8 or 8×6) with parchment paper.
Mix all ingredients except pecans and chocolate chunks until smooth.
Fold in pecans and chocolate chunks and pour batter into pan.
Bake brownies for about 25 minutes or until golden dark brown.
Cool brownies for at least 10 minutes before cutting

Easy to make vegan no oil brownies. Nuts make it rich.
