Title:
Avocado Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/388489.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Chicken Breast- sliced in half
1 7oz Pkg Macaroni Noodles
8 oz Mexican Sour Cream
2 Tablespoons Miracle Whip
2 Tablespoon Real Mayo
3-4 Cups Mixed Vegetables
3 Roma tomatoes diced
2 Shallot minced
1 Avocado
1/2 Cup Cilantro
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
3-4 Tablespoons Water
Tostados
Hot Sauce
Chicken Seasoning:
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1 Teaspoon Ground Chili Ancho Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Ground Chili de Arbol Powder
1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Cumin
1 Teaspoon Dried Cilantro

Directions:
Mix the Chicken Seasonings together.  Pre-heat broiler.  Place sliced chicken breasts on greased broiling pan.  Spread seasoning mixture on one side and flip and do the other side.  Place under broiler flipping once, until golden brown (about 20 minutes). Remove and set aside.
Prepare macaroni noodles as package directs.  Drain them and rinse with cold water.  Place noodles into bowl with mixed vegetables, diced tomatoes and diced shallot.
Place avocado, sour cream, mayo, miracle whip, water, cilantro and salt into blender and blend until smooth. Add to bowl with vegetables and noodles.
Dice Chicken and add to bowl with vegetables and noodles and mix everything together. Refridgerate for an hour or until cold.
Serve with tostados and hot sauce.
