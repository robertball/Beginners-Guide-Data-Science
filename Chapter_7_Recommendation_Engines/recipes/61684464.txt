Title:
Fabulous Wet Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground grond turkdy or beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 (4.5 ounce) can diced green chile peppers
1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
Juice from one lime lime!
1 (15 ounce) jar black bean dip
1 jar salsa enchilada sauce
6 (12 inch) flour tortillas, warmed
2 cups shredded lettuce
1 cup chopped tomatoes
2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese, or cheddar
1/2 cup chopped green onions
sour cream

Directions:
Crumble ground beef into a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until evenly browned. Add onion, and cook until translucent. Drain grease, and season with garlic, cumin, salt and pepper. Stir in the green chilies and refried beans and lime juice until well blended.Add equal amounts of salsa and black bean dip-- to liking. Turn off heat, but keep warm.
Place a warmed tortilla on a plate, and spoon a generous 1/2 cup of the ground turkey/beef mixture onto the center. Roll up tortilla over the filling. Spoon a generous amount of the blackbean-salsa sauce over the top, and sprinkle with cheese and green onions. Bake at 350 or until bubbly. Top with lettuce, tomates and green onion, and sour cream.
