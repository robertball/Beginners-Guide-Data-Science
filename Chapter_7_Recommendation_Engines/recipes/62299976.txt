Title:
S'more Pudding Please Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364058.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chocolate pudding Cups
3/4 cup frozen whipped topping
6 graham crackers crushed
1 cup mini marshmallows
mini semi sweet chocolate chips for garnish
sliced strawberries (optional)

Directions:
Take 4 ramekins and sprinkles graham cracker crumbs on the bottom. Next a layer of the moussed pudding, marshmallows, graham, and chocolate pudding. Finish with marshmallows, crushed grahams, and chocolate chips, Topped with a dab of cool whip, garnish with strawberries.

Submitter: 
Dancing Queen
Description: 
I had 4 chocolate pudding cups that I wanted to use up in a light dessert treat to take to a get together. This is what I came up with.
