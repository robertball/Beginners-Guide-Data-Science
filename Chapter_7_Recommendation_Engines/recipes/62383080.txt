Title:
Big Daddy's Yam Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium yams (prefer can yams)
3 cups vanilla yogurt
1 cup milk
4 scoops Mila
2 cups ice cubes
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 ripe banana, sliced

Directions:
Prick yams with a fork, and place on a plate. Cook in the microwave for 8 to 10 minutes, turning once, until tender. Cool, peel and dice.
Combine the yams, yogurt, milk, Mila, ice cubes, sugar and banana in the container of a blender. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
This smoothie is quick and easy to make. You could also use a large can of yams in place of cooking the fresh ones.Modified from: Yam Smoothie
