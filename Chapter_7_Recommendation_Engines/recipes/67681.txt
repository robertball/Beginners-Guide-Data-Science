Title:
Spinach Mandarin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985286.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package DOLEÂ® Spinach
1 (15 ounce) can DOLE Mandarin Oranges, drained
1 (8 ounce) can DOLE Pineapple Chunks, drained
1/2 red onion, sliced, separated into rings
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette

Directions:
Combine spinach, mandarin oranges, pineapple chunks, and red onion in large serving bowl.
Pour dressing over salad, toss to evenly coat and serve.

Submitter: 
Dole Food Company
Description: 
Fresh spinach, mandarin oranges, pineapple, and red onions are all tossed together with a simple balsamic vinaigrette for sensational flavor and colorful presentation.
