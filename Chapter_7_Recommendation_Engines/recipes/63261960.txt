Title:
Burst O'lemon muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4c flour
3/4c sugar
1t baking powder
3/4 t baking soda
1/4t salt
1c lemon yogurt
1 egg
6T butter, melted
2T lemon zest
1 T lemon juice
1/2c flaked coconut
Topping
1/3c lemon juice
1/4c sugar
1/4c flaked coconut

Directions:
Mix all muffin items, stir in coconut. Bake 400* 18 min.
In a sauce pan cook lemon juice and sugar until dissolved, add coconut.
Poke holes in the muffins and pour on topping.
