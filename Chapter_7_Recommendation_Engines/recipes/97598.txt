Title:
Tanimura & Antle Sweet Gem™ BBQ Lettuce Cup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979520.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked shredded chicken
1/2 cup diced sharp Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped red onion
3 tablespoons barbeque sauce
1 head Tanimura and Antle Sweet Gem™ Gourmet Lettuce
Salt to taste

Directions:
Combine chicken, cheese, onions and BBQ sauce in a small bowl.  Season to taste with salt. Remove leaves from Tanimura & Antle  Sweet Gem(tm) lettuce and chill for at least 1 hour. Fill center of  Tanimura & Antle Sweet Gem(tm) lettuce leaves with mixture.

Submitter: 
Tanimura & Antle®
Description: 
Adding a small amount of your favorite barbeque sauce to this chilled salad made with shredded chicken and sharp Cheddar gives a hint of what is to come.
