Title:
Compromise Cream Cheese Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz bittersweet chocolate
12 oz unsweetened chocolate
16 oz butter
10 eggs
2 1/2 cups white sugar
3 cups brown sugar
6 oz molasses
3 cups flour
16 oz nuts
24 oz cream cheese
3 cups of white sugar
vanilla to taste

Directions:
melt chocolate and butter together in the microwave on low setting, until both are melted
transfer chocolate to mixing bowl, add sugars and eggs and mix until smooth
slowly add flour to mixture; mix until incoroporated
add nuts if using and mix through
fill each mini muffin cup about 2/3 full, using a pastry bag (do not try this if you have added nuts)
beat cream cheese and sugar until medium soft consistency;  may add a little milk or cream
add cream cheese mixture to brownies, piping about 1/2 tsp to each well
bake at 350 for about 15 minutes;  better to under than overbake; if using convection, then 325 for 13 mins

Submitter: 
millegrazie
Description: 
A hybrid between Nick Maligieri's Supernatural and Maida Heatter's Santa Fe Brownies.  This particular recipe is for a big crowd, either 16 dozen mini-muffin size or a half-sheet pan.
