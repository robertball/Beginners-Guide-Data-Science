Title:
Virgin Pina Colada Sunrise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups pineapple juice
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1 can coconut milk
2 T granulated sugar
1 1/4 cups ice
Garnish: favorite strawberry drink syrup

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender and process until smooth. Drizzle Strawberry syrup on the bottom of glass, then add in colada. Garnish with strawberry syrup and a cherry.
