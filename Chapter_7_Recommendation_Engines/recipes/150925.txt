Title:
Strawberry Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979013.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Cuervo Especial® Gold
3 ounces lime margarita mix
Handful of fresh or frozen strawberries
4 ounces crushed ice
1 teaspoon sugar
Strawberry, for garnish

Directions:
Rub rim of a margarita glass with half a lime and dip into sugar.
Add Cuervo Especial Gold, margarita mix, fresh or frozen strawberries, and ice. Mix in blender until smooth.
Pour into glass and garnish with a strawberry.

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
