Title:
Watermelon Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980772.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups peeled, seeded, cubed watermelon
1/2 cup light rum
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1/4 cup orange liqueur
Ice cubes

Directions:
FREEZE watermelon in a shallow pan at least 6 hours.
PROCESS frozen watermelon and next 3 ingredients in a blender until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides. Add enough ice to bring mixture to 5-cup level; process until smooth. Repeat procedure, if necessary, until mixture measures 5 cups.

Submitter: 
Southern Living magazine
