Title:
Ultimate Spiced Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/902020.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup packed light brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 cups rolled oats
1 cup Craisins
1 cup walnut pieces
1 cup dark chocolate chips
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup unsweetened coconut flakes
3/4 cup chopped,candied ginger
1/2 cup sunflower seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).!
In a large bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and white sugar. Add eggs and vanilla. Beat  until smooth. Combine the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, cloves, and salt; stir into the sugar mixture. Stir in the oats and remaining ingredients. (you may have to use your hands to mix at this point) Drop by rounded tablespoonsful onto parchment lined cookie sheets.
Bake 9 to 10 minutes until light and golden. Do not overbake. (They will look underdone in the middle.)  Let them cool for 2 minutes before removing from cookie sheets to cool completely. Store in airtight container. These are ULTIMATE crispy, chewy, buttery, spicy goodness!
