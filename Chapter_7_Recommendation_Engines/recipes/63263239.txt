Title:
Beef 'n' Cheese Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 LBS pound ground beef drained
20 LBS package elbow macaroni
2 #10 Cans Cheese

Directions:
In a skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink. DRAIN and rinse
Meanwhile, in a saucepan, cook macaroni according to package directions; drain and set aside. In a hotel pan cook mix the beef and mac and cheese together. Stir beef and macaroni into cheese sauce; heat through.PUT IN SMALL PANS!! ONLY HEAT ONE PAN AT A TIME BATCH COOK NOODLES WILL GET SOFT AND MUSHY!
