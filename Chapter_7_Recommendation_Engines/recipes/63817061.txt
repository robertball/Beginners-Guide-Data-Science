Title:
2Deep Dish Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup bread flour
2 cup all purpose flour
2/3 cup water
1/3 cup sugar
1 packet yeast
2 tsp salt
1/4 cup corn oil
6 Tbsp vegetable oil
1/2 cup extra all purpose flour for working with dough

Directions:
Proof the yeast with water and sugar
Mix bread and all purpose flour, salt, and corn oil
knead together the and yeast flour mixture
Let rise for 2 hours until doublet in size
Preheat the oven to 400Â°
Punch down dough and spread into 16" pan
Top with sliced cheese, then sauce and then toppings
Bake for 20-25 minutes
