Title:
Southern Broccoli and Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad
6 cups (1 large head) cauliflower
6 cups (3 heads) broccoli
1 medium red onion finely chopped
2 stalks celery finely chopped
1 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese
1 12 oz. package bacon, finely chopped
8 oz. almond slivers or chopped pecans
8 oz golden raisens
Dressing
1 cup mayonnaise (Dukes)
1 cup sour cream (Daisy)
8 oz cream cheese (Philadelphia)
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup lemon juice

Directions:
1.  Combine first 8 ingredients in a very large bowl.
2.  Whisk together 5 ingredients for dressing in a large bowl.
3.  Combine, stir, and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Bob Butler
Description: 
Creamy salad with a few extra ingredients.  Delicious.  Fantastic for a potluck.
