Title:
Baked Falafel with Tahini Yogurt and Tahini Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132309.jpg
Ingredients:
Falafel
1 can 15 oz garbanzo beans
4 garlic cloves
1/2 onion (could try green onion)
1 T cumin
1/2 T paprika or 1 tsp??
1/2 T or 1 tsp coriander
1/2 c fresh parsley
1/2 c fresh oregano (or use cilantro)
Juice 1 lemon
1/4 c plus 1/2 to 3/4 c garbanzo bean flour
Tahini Yogurt Sauce
1/4 c tahini
1/4 c Greek yogurt
1/4 c parsley fresh chopped
1/2 lemon, juice
2 garlic cloves
1/3 c or more water
Salt
1/4 c Tahini
1/2 c chopped fresh dill
1/4 c chopped fresh parsley
1-2 garlic cloves
1/2 lemon, juice
1/3 c water
Salt

Directions:
preheat oven to 400
Falafel:
Combine all ingredients except flour in blender; do not over blend (want a little chunky)
Add flour to desired consistency to form patties (be careful -- firms up when chilled)
Chill 30 min
Add more flour if needed
Form into 2 inch patties or balls on parchment lined cookie sheet
Bake at 400 on parchment lined cookie sheet for 25 min flip once at 10-15 min
Tahini Yogurt
combine all in blender (thickens as chills)
salt to taste
Tahini Dill
combine all in blender (thickens as chills)
salt to taste

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
Baked falafel with Tahini sauces; sauces great as salad dressing too!
