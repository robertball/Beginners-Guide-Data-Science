Title:
Herb-Roasted Chicken with Root Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, about 3 lbs.
2 Tbsp. rosemary, thyme, sage, & marjoram
(reduce to 1 Tbsp. if using fresh herbs)
1/2 lemon, juiced
or
3 cloves of garlic, crushed
Butter-flavored nonstick cooking spray
Dash of salt & pepper
2 med. carrots, peeled & halved
2 small beets, peeled & quartered
2 small sweet potatoes, peeled & quartered
1 small turnip, peeled & quartered

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Clean chicken and place breast side up on rack in roasting pan.
Squeeze lemon juice inside chicken or rub inside of chicken with garlic.
Use 10 spritzes of nonstick cooking spray to coat outside of chicken.
Sprinkle evenly with herbs, salt, & pepper.
Spritz vegetables evenly with cooking spray and place in pan around chicken.
Cover chicken with foil and bake for 55 mins.
Bake uncovered for additional 15 mins. or until juices run clear.
