Title:
Loaded Baked Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 red skin potatos
1 cup sour cream
1 cup Miracle Whip
3 cups Cheddar Cheese
1 cup crumbled bacon
1/2 cup green onions, diced

Directions:
Microwave(15 minutes)potatoes until soft.
Peel (leave skin on half) and dice potatoes into 1 inch cubes.
In a large bowl mix remaining ingredients reserving 1 cup of cheese.
Place mixture into casserole dish and sprinkle with remaing cheese.
Bake at 350*F for 20-25 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden.

Submitter: 
Angela T.
Description: 
A warm comforting potato salad that tastes like a steak house loaded baked potato.
