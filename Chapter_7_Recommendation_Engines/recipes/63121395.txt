Title:
Oatmeal Carrot Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c butter
1 c all purpose flour
3/4 c whole wheat flour
3/4 c packed brown sugar
1 egg
1 t baking powder
1/2 t cinnamon
1/4 t baking soda
1/4 t ground cloves
1 t vanilla
2 c oatmeal
1 c shredded carrots
1/2 c raisins
1/4 c chopped nuts
coarse sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Beat butter and sugar with electric mixer until creamy.
Add egg and vanilla, beat till combined.
In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients.  Add to wet mix.
Add oatmeal, carrots, raisins, and nuts.  Mix well.  Drop large mounds onto parchment paper lined baking sheet.
Sprinkle with coarse sugar.
Bake 10 - 12 minutes.
