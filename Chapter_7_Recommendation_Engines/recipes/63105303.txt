Title:
St. Patrick Shamrock Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
6 tablespoons sugar
6 tablespoons brown sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup natural peanut butter
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup flour
Green M'n'Ms- about 72

Directions:
Cream butter with both sugars in large bowl.
Add egg, vanilla, and peanut butter, mixing well after each one.
Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
Spoon 2" balls onto greased cookie sheet. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Bake for 8 minutes. Take out and top each cookie with three M'n'Ms in the shape of a shape of a shamrock immediately after taking cookies out.

Submitter: 
QueenCook
Description: 
We call these St. Patrick Shamrock Cookies because the three green M'n'Ms on top resemble a shamrock. We bake them every St. Patrick's Day and usually take them to St. Patrick's Day parties. Everyone loves them!
