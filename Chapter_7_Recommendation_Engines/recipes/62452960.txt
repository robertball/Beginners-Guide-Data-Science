Title:
Eagle Brand Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup boiling water
1/4 cups margarine
2 cups white sugar
4 cups powdered milk

Directions:
dissolve margarine and sugar in water, gradually add powdered milk.  Beat as you add
continue beating for 5 minutes or until thick.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
this tastes just like the canned eagle brand milk
