Title:
Smoky Chicken Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351817.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups finely chopped cooked chicken
1/2 cup finely chopped celery
1/2 cup coarsely chopped smoked almonds
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
Crackers

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first eight ingredients; mix well. Cover and chill at least 2 hours. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Mary Beth  Wagner
Description: 
The unique 'smoky' flavor in this spread comes from smoked almonds. It makes a hearty snack on you favorite crackers. Don't expect many leftovers!
