Title:
South Carolina Mustard Based BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Prepared Mustard
10 oz. Ketchup
1/3 cup of Brown Sugar
2 tbls. of Onion Powder
4 tbls. of Apple Cider Vinegar
2 tbls. of Worchestrshire sauce
2 tbls. of soy sauce
1 tsp. of Hot Sauce
1 cup Water
Ground Pepper
Salt/Celery Salt/Cajun Seasoning to taste.

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a sauce pot and simmer on low heat for 1 1/2 hours, stirring generously throughout. Take off of heat and let cool. Store in refridgerator until ready to use.

Submitter: 
Tyler Melnick
Description: 
There's no recipe on here like it. Only state in the nation with this delicious BBQ sauce...great for pork or chicken.
