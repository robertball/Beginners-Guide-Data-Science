Title:
Oven Roasted Ranch Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497414.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ears fresh corn on the cob in husks
1/2 cup butter
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Place corn directly on the oven rack and roast for 20 minutes or until corn is soft.
Peel down husks and use as a handle. Remove corn silks.
In a small bowl, combine melted butter and Hidden Valley(R)Original Ranch(R)Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix.
Generously brush Ranch butter over corn.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
Oven-roasted right in their husks, this tender corn on the cob is brushed with a blend of melted butter and Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix.
