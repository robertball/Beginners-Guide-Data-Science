Title:
Greek Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Grilled Chicken breasts
Cherry tomatoes
Cucumber
Kalmata Olives
Feta
Olive Oil
Lemon juice
Oregano
Garlic
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Grill or bake the chicken breasts until done.
Cut the chicken into 2in pieces.
Halve the cherry tomates and kalmata olives. Cut the cucumber.
Add the chicken, tomatoes, olives, cucumber and crumbled feta to a bowl.
Mix the olive oil, lemon juice, crushed garlic, and crushed oregano. Toss the dressing with the other ingredients until mixed.

Submitter: 
Marney Ayers Freeland
Description: 
Quick and easy greek salad. Great for leftovers too.
