Title:
Better than EVER Brownines Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 tbsp unsalted butter
1 1/4 cups white sugar
3/4 cups cocoa powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/2 cup flour
For Oreo layer
1 package of Oreos
For the cookie dough layer
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
1 egg
1 1/4tsp vanilla extract
1 1/4 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
For the brownie layer:
In a medium sauce pan melt the butter over medium high heat. Add the sugar and coca powder once the butter is melted. Whisk to combine and and remove from heat. Add the salt, vanilla and eggs and contiuoulsy whisk until the eggs are comnbined.Add the flour and cont to mix. Set aside.
For COOKIE LAYER
Cream together the butter and sugars in a mixer. Add the eggs and vanilla, making sure to scrape down the sides of the mixing bowl. Add the flour, salt, baking powder and mix on low until everything is incorporated. Fold in choco. chips. Set aside.
Assembly:
Preheat over 350
Line bottom of 9x9 baking pan with tim foil and then spray with baking spray.
Layer the cookie dough on the bottom of a 9x9 pan, pressing down to form the bottom of the brownies.
Layer as many oreos that will fit on top of the cookie dough. One layer will do dont overlap
Pour the brownie batter on top of oreos make sure to cover it evenly.
Bake for 30-35 minutes. Test with knive to see if center comes out clean.
Serve with ICE CREAM YUMMMMMMMYYYYYY
