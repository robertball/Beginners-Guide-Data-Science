Title:
Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chuck Roast 5 lbs.
1 Large Yellow Sweet Onion
2 Beef Bouillon Cubes
Jar of Peppercorns (grinder shaker)
Bottle of Italian Salad Dressing
Provolone Cheese
Deli Rolls
Jar of Banana Peppers
Can or 2 of Beer

Directions:
Brown roast in frying pan. Put roast in roaster pan and pour lots of Italian Dressing over roast. Grind up 1/4 jar of peppercorns. Pour enough beer over roast to cover well over 1/2 the roast. Add a few dashes of pepper. I added 2/3 jar of banana peppers. Put in over on 275 degrees for 8-10 hours. At 8 hours check and see if it will fall apart on its own.
