Title:
Jeff's Turkey Brine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/928954.jpg
Ingredients:
1 qt. Vegetable Broth
1 qt. Water
1/2 cup Salt
1/2 cup Pepper
1/2 cup Sugar
1/2 cup Garlic Powder
1/2 cup Thyme
1/2 cup Rosemary
1 bag of Ice

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat combine the vegetable broth, water, and spices (salt, pepper, sugar, garlic powder, thyme, rosemary). Mix and whisk together.  Bring to a bowl and ensure the salt and sugar is dissolved.
After boiling, let cool.
In a large stew pot or other large container (ice chest, etc) place the thawed turkey in the center of the pot.  Combine with the ice and cooled broth.  Ensure the turkey is fully submerged.  Let the brine sit for approximately 12-24 hours in the refrigerator.  After 12-24 hours, remove the turkey from the brine, pat dry, then sprikle lightly rub the turkey with pepper (the brine is salty, do not add anymore).  Cook appropriately (preferrably deep fried).

Submitter:

Description: 
This is an experimental turkey brine.  Normally brine for 12-24 hours, then season (simple dry rub) for another 12-24 hours. Then cook your turkey the best you know how (deep fried is preferred).
