Title:
Garlicy sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup sour cream
1-2 cloves of fresh garlic (this is key)
1 tbsp lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Throw everything together in a blender or majic bullet and wah-lah your done.

Submitter: 
Toni Losada
Description: 
I didn't have cucumber or yogurt to make a tzaki sauce so I had to concoct something to serve with my falafel and this turned out way better than I could have imagined. I'll be using this more often
