Title:
Elegant Parmesean Cauliflower (Webbers Lodge) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large head of cauliflower
1/4 Cup lite mayo
1/4 Cup lite parmesean

Directions:
WASH and break apart cauliflower into large pcs.
PLACE in oven proof dish and microwave for 3-4 minutes (until tender crisp)
MIX mayo and parmesean together and spread over cauliflower
BAKE @ 350F for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
Super easy, fast and tastey!!  From the recipe book of the Webbers Hunting Lodge.
