Title:
Poor Man's Ceviche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of Imitation Crab/flake it
1/2 cup Celantro, chopped
3 Plum tomatoes diced
2 Serrano Chiles, chopped
1 stalk of green onion, chopped
5 to 6 limes, squeeze to make about 3/4 to 1 cup of juice
salt and pepper to your taste

Directions:
Flake the Imitation crab in a large bowl.  Add the chopped celantro, diced tomatoes, chopped serrano chiles, chopped green onions.  Toss until well mixed.  Add the lime juice.  Add salt and pepper to your taste.
Use tortilla chips or tostadas.

Submitter: 
Sandie Marie Leal
Description: 
This is a quick and delicious appetizer/dip dish.  I promise most guests will enjoy it.
