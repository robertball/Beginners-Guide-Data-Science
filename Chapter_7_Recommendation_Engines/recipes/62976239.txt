Title:
Sour Cream Lemon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
3 1/2 Tbsp.cornstarch
1 Tbsp.grated lemon rind
1/2 cup fresh squeezed lemon juice[2-3 lemons]
3 egg yolks,lightly beaten
1 cup whole milk
1/4 cup butter
1 cup sour cream
1 baked and cooled pie shell
whipping cream or Never-Fail Meringue

Directions:
I n heavy pot combine sugar,cornstarch,lemon rind,lemon juice,egg yolks and milk.Cook over medium heat,stirring often,untill thickened,about 8 minutes.
Remove from heat,stir in butter untill melted,cool to room temperature.
Add sour cream and pour into pie shell.
Store in fridge.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
A classic lemon curd with sour cream thats creamy,tangy and sweet!
