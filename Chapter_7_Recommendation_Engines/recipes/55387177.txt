Title:
Kettle Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup unpopped popcorn kernels

Directions:
Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
Add 3 kernels and wait for them to pop.
Once the kernels have  popped, stir in the sugar and stir with a wooden spoon until sugar melts.
Add the popcorn and put on the lid.
Shake 3 seconds and let the saucepan sit on the eye 3 seconds. Keep cooking this way until all the kernels are popped.
Pour popped corn into a large bowl, add salt, stir and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Kat G
Description: 
Take a trip to the county fair with a bowl of old-fashioned Kettle Corn. Your family will never want plain popcorn again! If you use white sugar, it will taste like popcorn balls and if you use brown sugar, it will taste like caramel corn.Modified from: Kettle Corn
