Title:
Sweet &  Sour sauce for meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1/3 cup ketchup
1/3 cup vinegar
2 tsp soy sauce
2 tbs corn starch

Directions:
Mix corn starch in a little of the water.
Put everything else in a sauce pan, slowly mix in corn starch mixture.
Bring to a boil until thickened.
Pour over meatballs and bake for 30-40 minutes

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
This recipe was used at a Mom & Pop restaurant for many, many years.  We were asked not to share but since it’s been over 10 years since the place closed I think that it’s time to share, it’s too good not to.
