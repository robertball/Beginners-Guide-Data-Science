Title:
Roasted Root Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1-inch chunks
2 medium carrots, washed (or peeled) and cut into 1-inch chunks
2 medium beets, washed (or peeled) and cut into 1-inch chunks
1 medium sweet onion, peeled and cut into large wedges
3 tablespoons olive oil
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Combine sweet potato, carrot, beet, onion, oil and salt. Mix well.
Arrange vegetables in a single layer on a baking sheet. Bake for 40-50 minutes or until tender, turning once or twice.

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
The earthy, natural flavors of these root vegetables shine through without being bogged down with too many spices or other seasonings. Any root vegetable can be substituted for the veggies called for.
