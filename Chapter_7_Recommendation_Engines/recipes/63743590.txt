Title:
No Bake Cracker Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg jello instant pudding
4 cups milk
1 lage box of non salted crackers
1 tub cool whip
1 can cherry pie filling

Directions:
Combine milk, pudding, and cool whip together
Spread a small amount of mixture on the bottom of pie/cake plate
Layer with crackers
Add layer of cool whip mixture
Add another layer of crackers
Make as many layers as you like but top must be crackers
Spread pie filling over crackers
Refrigerate for a few hours before serving
