Title:
Mild Cheesy Chicken Enchiladas  Lo Carb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed, cooked chicken meat
1/4 cup chopped onion
4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
Sour cream
cumin, chile powder, oregano
salt
1 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes
1/2 cup black olives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking dish.
In a medium bowl, mix the chicken, onion, 1 cup Cheddar cheese, and 3/4 cup sour cream. Season with cumin, chile powder, oregano and salt to taste. Top with the remaining Cheddar cheese and sour cream. Then top with the tomatoes and olives.
Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 30 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.
