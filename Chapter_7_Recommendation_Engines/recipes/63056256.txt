Title:
Firehouse Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Can Sliced Peaches
1 Stick Butter
1 Cup Sugar
1 Cup Self Rising Flour
2/3 Cup Milk
1 Tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In 9x13 pan put stick of butter in over until melted while oven is preheating.  While butter is melting mix flour, sugar, milk and vanilla.  Pour peaches in melted butter.  Pour flour mixture evenly over peaches and butter.  Cook at 350 for 1 hour or until brown and bubbly.
