Title:
Easy But Deelish Slow Cooker Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1479702.jpg
Ingredients:
1 30oz. bag of frozen, diced hash browns
1 32oz. box of chicken broth
1 can of cream of chicken soup (10 oz)
1 pkg. cream cheese (8oz., NOT fat free)
3 oz. bacon bits
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Put frozen potatoes in slow cooker.  Add in the chicken broth, cream of chicken soup and half of the bacon bits.  Add a pinch of salt and pepper.  Stir gently.
Cook on low for 8 hours or until potatoes are tender.
An hour before serving, cut the cream cheese into small cubes.  Place the cubes into the slow cooker.  Stir gently a few times throughout the hour.
Once the cream cheese is completely melted in, it's ready to serve.
Top each serving with the cheddar cheese and some additional bacon bits.
Be prepared for complements!

Submitter: 
Steven Kelly
Description: 
This is so ridiculously easy that it is cheating.  My family loves this delicious, wholesome dish....best in cold weather, but I'd still lap it up during a heatwave!
