Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pretzel sticks
Small Marshmallows
White Candy Coating
1 Tablespoon Coconut oil
Sprinkles of your choice
Cookie sheet lined with parchment paper

Directions:
Melt Candy coating with Coconut oil in double boiler, set aside
Line your cookie sheet with parchment paper
Stick Marshmallow at one end of the pretzel stick, dip into candy coating and place on lined cookie sheet. Sprinkle with sprinkles of your choice and let candy coating harden.

Submitter: 
Regina Salmans-Hall
Description: 
This is a fun recipe, quick and easy. Kids enjoy helping and eating :)
