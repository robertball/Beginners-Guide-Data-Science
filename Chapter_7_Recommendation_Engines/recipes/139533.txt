Title:
Easy Party Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 (0.13 ounce) packages unsweetened strawberry-flavored drink mix powder
2 (46 fluid ounce) cans pineapple juice
3 cups SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
4 quarts water
2 liters ginger ale

Directions:
In a large punch bowl combine drink mix, pineapple juice, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and water. Stir until dissolved. Stir in the ginger ale.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Pineapple juice and powdered KOOL-AID® are the main ingredients in this reduced-sugar punch for a crowd. Ginger ale adds a bit of carbonation.
