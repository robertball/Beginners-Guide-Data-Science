Title:
Herbal rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup long grain rice
2 cups water
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 tablespoon Mrs. Dash
1 tablespoon dried parsley
2 teaspoons seasoned salt

Directions:
bring water to boil in a 2 quart saucepan
add all ingredients
return to boiling
stir ONCE to mix all ingredients
cover with lid and reduce fire to low
simmer for 20 minutes until liquid is gone and rice is fluffy
