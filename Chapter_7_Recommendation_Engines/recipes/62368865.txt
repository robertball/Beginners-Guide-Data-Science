Title:
Gelatine Ice Cubes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/409690.jpg
Ingredients:
silicone baking pan
2 envelopes (Knox) unflavored powdered Gelatine
1 cup cold water
1 1/2 cups boiling water

Directions:
In a 2 cup Pyrex glass, Sprinkle gelatine into cold water, allow to bloom.
Add boiling water and stir until gelatine is dissolved.
Place baking pan on a cookie sheet. Pour gelatine into the squares.
Refrigerate at least 3 hours.
Gently remove cubes from pan, even if they break they look just like ice.

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
This recipe makes the perfect ice cubes to use in cake decorating. Simple, cystal clear gelatine cubes look just like the real thing. You'll need a silicone baking pan for 24 mini squares to make these easily, available at craft supply stores.
