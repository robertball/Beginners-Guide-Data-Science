Title:
Green Chili Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Jar Cookwell 2 Step Green Chili Stew
2 lbs Cubed Chicken
2 Cups Chicken Broth
1 Tbs Adam's Reserve Rub
1 Can Refigerated uncooked butter milk biscuites
2 Cups Heavy cream or wholemilk

Directions:
Remove Biscuits from package and tear into small pieces
Combine Chicken and remaining ingredients except biscuits to a stock pot. Warm on medium high.
When mixture boils add the biscuit pieces and stir every two minutes, for a total of 8 mins
Reduce heat to low and simmer for an additional 5 mins
