Title:
Best Ever Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 large eggs, beaten
1 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla
3 cups finely chopped apples
1/2 cup apple juice
1 cup chopped walnuts
Glaze topping for cake:
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup butter
1/3 cup whipping cream

Directions:
1. In a large bowl combine flour, sugar, soda and salt.
2. Make a well in the center and set aside.
3. In a medium bowl combine eggs, oil, apple juice and vanilla.
4. Stir in apples and nuts.
5. Add the egg mixture to dry ingredients, just until moistened.
6. Spread batter in a greased and floured 9 x 13-inch pan.
7. Bake at 350Â°F for 45-50 minutes.
8. In a small saucepan combine the brown sugar, butter and cream.
9. Cook and stir till bubbly and all of the sugar is dissolved.
10. Cool slightly.
11. Drizzle warm sauce over cake, when it has cooled for 5 minutes, so it can seep into the cake, keeping it moist.
