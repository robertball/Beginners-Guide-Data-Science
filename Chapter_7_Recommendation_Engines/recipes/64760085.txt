Title:
Hungry Dad's Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7081578.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground beef
1/4 cup of diced yellow onion
1 ten ounce can of diced tomatoes and green chilis
1 cup of picante sauce
1 packet of taco seasoning
8 ounces of canned black beans, drained
3 cups of crushed tortilla chips
4 medium sized flour tortillas
1 1/2 cups of Monterey/Colby Jack cheese
1 1/2 cups of Pepper Jack cheese
1/2 cup of chopped green onion
1/2 cup of cilantro
Sour cream to taste

Directions:
Brown hamburger meat and onion in a skillet over medium high heat.
Drain and return to the pan.
Reduce heat to low.
Stir in the tomatoes and green chilis, black beans, taco seasoning, and picante sauce.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Simmer for 20 minutes or until most of the liquid is evaporated.
While simmering, spray a 9x13 glass baking dish with non stick cooking oil.
Spread the flour tortillas, covering the inside of the dish.
Spread the beef mixture over the tortillas.
Sprinkle half of the cheese over the beef mixture.
Sprinkle half of the crushed tortilla chips on top.
Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes at 350 degrees.
Remove from oven and remove aluminum foil.
Sprinkle the remaining cheese and crushed chips on top.
Place back into the oven uncovered and bake until cheese is melted and bubbly.
Remove from the oven.
Sprinkle with chopped green onion and cilantro.
Add sour cream to taste.
