Title:
J-Lo's Asian Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/414388.jpg
Ingredients:
5 fresh mint leaves
3 lime wedges
1 fluid ounce sour apple schnapps
1 1/2 fluid ounces limon flavored rum
1/2 fluid ounce pineapple juice
6 cubes ice
1 fluid ounce club soda

Directions:
Muddle (crush) together the mint leaves, lime wedges and sour apple schnapps in a glass using the handle of a wooden spoon. Add lemon rum and pineapple juice; shake well and pour over ice. Top with club soda.

Submitter: 
janice
Description: 
This mojito is not too sweet, but is very flavorful.
