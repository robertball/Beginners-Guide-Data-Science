Title:
Potato casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1can cream of chicken soup
2lbs frozen hash browns
3/4C shorening-melted
1/4tsp pepper
1/2C onion chopped
1 pint sour cream
2C grated cheddar cheese
2C crushed cornflakes

Directions:
Defrost the potatoes. Combine in large bowl, potatoes, 1/2C shortening,salt, pepper, onion,soup, sour cream and cheese. Pour into 3qt casserole dish. Mix remaing shortening and cornflakes and sprinke over the potato mixture. Bake at 350 for 45min
