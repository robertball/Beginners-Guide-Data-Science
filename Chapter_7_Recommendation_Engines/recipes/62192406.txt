Title:
Baked Samosas II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C flour
2 T margarine
1 t salt
water
2 large potatoes
2 carrots
1 C peas
1 large onion
1/2 C beans
1/2 t garam masala
1 t cumin
1 t oil

Directions:
Take flour, salt and 1/2 T of margarine, add water and make a dough of it.  Melt the remaining margarine.
Roll out into a square.  Grease with the melted margarine on the inner surface, fold in half and grease again, repeat once more.  Fold in half again and roll out into a sheet.
Cool all veggies except onion in microwave.  Saute onion in cumin and oil.  Add veggies, season with salt and garam masala, using a little water to mix together.
Cut the rolled out dough into 3" squares.  Roll out into cones, fill with stuffing and seal the edges using a fork.
Bake at 350 for 20-30 min. until done, rotating the sides of the samosas.
