Title:
Peanut Butter Cup Fat Bombs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6347225.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups melted coconut oil
1 1/2 cups unsalted natural peanut butter
6 tablespoons cocoa powder
4 tablespoons maple syrup, or more to taste
1 pinch sea salt (optional)

Directions:
Line an 8- to 9-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
Mix coconut oil, peanut butter, cocoa powder, and maple syrup together in a bowl. Season with salt and mix well.
Pour mixture into the prepared pan and refrigerate until set, at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
IK
Description: 
These peanut-butter-cup-flavored, keto-friendly fat bombs are made of simple, all-natural ingredients including coconut oil, cocoa powder, and maple syrup.
