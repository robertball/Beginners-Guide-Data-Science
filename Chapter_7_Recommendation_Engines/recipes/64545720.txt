Title:
Blackened catfish Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 fresh catfish filets
Old bay seasoning
2tbls. veg oil
Fresh spinach
Sour cream
Shredded cheddar cheese
Flour tortillas
Salsa
Lime

Directions:
Wrap tortillas in foil.
Heat tortillas in oven or on grill on low setting (till just warmed thru).
Heat a cast iron skillet very hot. (I do this outside on the grill as the smoke is overwhelming)
Sprinkle Old Bay seasoning on catfish till coated well
Add veg oil to hot cast iron pan (carefully)
Add cat fish fillets to pan. Do not disturb for 3 minutes
Turn catfish and allow to sear for 3 mins. or until it starts to flake easily
Squeeze juice from one lime over fish
Remove fish and assemble burritos
Add 1/2 filet of fish,  Handful of spinach, sour cream, cheese and salsa to warm tortilla!
Enjoy
