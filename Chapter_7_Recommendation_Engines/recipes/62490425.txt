Title:
Grammys Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups Flour
1/2 stick of butter softened
1/4-1/2 tsp cinnamon (I like to use 1/2 tsp)
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
4 med Bananas..mashed
8oz apple sauce
8oz Raspberry yogurt(I use non-fat,but use whatever you like and whatever flavor)
dash of nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Grease 9x5 loaf pan...or largest you have
I use a Mix master for entire recipe
Stir together softened butter and sugar.Add eggs,vanilla and apple sauce,mix well.Combine Flour,baking soda,baking powder and salt together,stir into butter mixture until smooth.
Fold in Yogurt and bananas.
Spread evenly into pan.Bake 1 hour 15 minutes till a toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Kellie Smith
Description: 
I made this bread with a basic recipe I had but changed it up to a more delicious,moister out of this world Banana Bread!!!
