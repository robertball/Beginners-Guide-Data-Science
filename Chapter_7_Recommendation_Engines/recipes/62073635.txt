Title:
Curry Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cubed cooked chicken
2 cups seedless red grapes, halved
1 cup diced celery
1 bunch chopped green onions
1 cup slivered almonds
1 can sliced water chestnuts, very well drained
1 16 oz. can pineapple tidbits, very well drained
1 lg. can mandarin oranges, very well drained
1 3 oz. can La Choy noodles
DRESSING:
2 cups mayo (not miracle whip)
1 TB. soy sauce
1 tsp. curry powder
1 TB. lemon juice
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
1.  At least 3 hours prior to serving, combine dressing ingredients and mix well.  Best if refridgerated over night.
2.  Combine remaining ingredients, except la choy noodles, pour dressing over all, and toss.  Just prior to serving, add noodles.
3. Serve on lettuce leaf or croissant.

Submitter: 
Penny Allen
Description: 
I got this recipe from my aunt after she made it for my sister's bridal shower.  Delicious and different. Best if dressing chills over night.
