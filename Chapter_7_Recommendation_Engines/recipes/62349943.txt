Title:
Easy Chili con Carne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz uncooked carne asada from Trader Joes
2 cans fire roasted tomatoes with jalapenos
2 can black beans drained
1 onion chopped
2 cloves garlic crushed
salt

Directions:
place all indredients into a slow cooker. Add salt to taste.  Slow cook on low approx 4-6 hours depending on the make and model of your slow cooker.
