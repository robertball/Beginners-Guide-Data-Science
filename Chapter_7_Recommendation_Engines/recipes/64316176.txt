Title:
White Peach Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 750ml bottle dry white wine
3/4 cup peach flavored vodka
6 tbsp thawed frozen lemonade concentrate
1/4 cup white sugar
1 lb white peaches, pitted and sliced
3/4 cup red grapes, halved
3/4 cup green grapes, halved

Directions:
In a large pitcher, combine wine, vodka, lemonade, and sugar. Stir until sugar is dissolved.
Add sliced peaches and grapes.
Refrigerate until well chilled (at least 2 hours, or overnight to blend flavors).
Serve over ice, including some fruit in each glass.

Submitter: 
Beth Mower Dodge
Description: 
An enjoyable white sangria (for those of us that prefer white to red).
