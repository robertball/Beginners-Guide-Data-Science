Title:
Dutch Oven Strawberry Short Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636213.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boxes pound cake mix
1 stick butter, melted
1 cup milk
2 eggs
2 cans strawberry pie filling

Directions:
Line Dutch oven with foil or parchment paper insert. Spray with cooking spray.
Combine pound cake mix, melted butter, milk, and eggs.
Pour mixture into Dutch Oven.
Pour strawberry pie filling on top.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 45 minutes.
