Title:
Dallas Mexican Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/626010.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 (16 ounce) cans refried beans
4 (10 inch) flour tortillas
3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 green onions, chopped
2 roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
1 can enchilada sauce
1 can rotel tomatoes with green chilis
a pinch of salt to your taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
In large skillet over medium heat, cook beef until browned.  Drain.  Combine with taco seasoning and refried beans and can of tomatoes.  Spread half of mixture in 9x13 inch baking dish.  Top with two tortillas, trimming if necessary. Pour enchilada sauce and spread evenly.  Spread the rest of the meat and last 2 tortillas. Cover with cheese Bake 35 to 45 minutes until heated through and cheese is bubbly.  Top with green onions and tomatoes.  Let cool 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Dallas Massey
Description: 
Seasoned ground beef and refried beans layered with cheese, tomatoes and tortillas and baked until hot and bubbly.  Top with your favorites, sourcream, green onions, cilantro and/or slices avacados!Modified from: Mexican Lasagna II
