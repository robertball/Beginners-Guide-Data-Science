Title:
Shanda's White Vegetarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5534776.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 C Olive oil
1 Large onion, diced
3 Stalks chopped celery
6 oz Baby portobello mushrooms, sliced (optional)
2 Carrots, Thinly sliced
3 T. McKay's chicken style seasoning
4 T. Chopped Garlic
1 4oz. Can of Green Chile's
1/2 tsp. Cumin
1 tsp. Dried oregano
1/2 tsp. Cayenne
1/4 tsp. Black pepper
2 C. Broth (see below)
1 12oz. Container sour cream
2 15.5 oz Cans of Great Northern Beans
2 15.5 oz cans of Worthington FriChick, chopped into medium pieces, including the juice in the can

Directions:
In a large saucepan, sauté onions and celery in olive oil until translucent, then add garlic for a few minutes of sautéing.
Add mushrooms and sauté until tender adding spices, seasonings and chili's mixing into the sautéed mixture.
Then add great northern beans and diced fri-chik.
Save juices from fri-chick and beans to make the 2 cups of broth and add.
Add carrots and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 30 minutes.
Finally cool enough that its hot but not boiling and add sour cream allowing to get warm but not boil and mix well to remove any lumps.
Enjoy!
