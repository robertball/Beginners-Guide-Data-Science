Title:
SouthWest Hot Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029834.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Roma Tomatoes
3 Jalapenos
1 bunch of fresh Cilantro
3 green onion bunches
1tsp. Ground Cumin
2 garlic cloves
3 dried Chile De Arbol
1/2 tsp. of salt

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender. Blend until you see the consistency you like.
Squeeze fresh lime on finished salsa.
Place salsa in fridge to chill and flavors to set at least 1 hr.
Then enjoy some of the best tasty spicy salsa you have ever had!

Submitter: 
Diona Thompson
Description: 
This is recipe I learned from watching my mexican friend's grandma in the kitchen making fresh salsa years ago. It is the best!
