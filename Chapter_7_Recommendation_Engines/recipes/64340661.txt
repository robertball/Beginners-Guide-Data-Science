Title:
Nancy's Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-9 cored and sliced apples (Macintosh, Golden Delicious or Granny Smith)
1 c. white sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1-2 T cinnamon
1 t. nutmeg
1/4 t. salt
1/2-1/3 c. flour
1-2 T. orange juice
5-6 T. butter
1 egg with a little water, mixed with a fork
crust for double crust pie

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Line pie pan with crust
Mix all ingredients but the butter.
Pour into crust and dot with butter
Top with upper crust and crimp edges.
Cut slits in pie to release steam, and brush with egg mixture.
Bake in the oven for 40-45 minutes, checking pie after 30 minutes to be sure it's not browning too much. If it's getting too brown, cover edges with aluminum foil.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
A delicious pie from our friend Nancy, guaranteed to please everyone.  Add cranberries for some variety.
