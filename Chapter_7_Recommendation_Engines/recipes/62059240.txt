Title:
Grilled Ham & Cheese Hashbrowns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Frozen hashbrown patties (Great Value Brand - Walmart sells a 12 pack
6 slices of Ham or any deli meat
1 cup grated cheddar

Directions:
Bake hashbrowns according to package directions or at 425 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
Top with a slice of ham and sprinkle with cheese.
Put back in warm oven until cheese melts.
Enjoy!
