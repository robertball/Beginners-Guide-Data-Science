Title:
Carolina Style Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup prepared yellow mustard
3/4 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cub white vinegar
1/4 cup of water
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp white pepper
1 tblsp chili powder
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp soy sauce
2 tblsp salted butter
1 tsp liquid smoke

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan over medium-low heat, mix all ingredients except for the butter and liquid smoke.  Cook for 20 minutes.
Add butter and liquid smoke, and simmer for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Craig Carlson
Description: 
This sauce is thinner, more tangy and less rich than your typical southwestern style sauce.  There is no molasses or tomato-based products in this sauce.  This sauce works great on ribs, pulled pork and chicken.
