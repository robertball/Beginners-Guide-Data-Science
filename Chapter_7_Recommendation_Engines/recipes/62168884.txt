Title:
Cheesy Chicken Macaroni Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole rotisserie chicken (or can use 4 large boneless chicken breasts, cooked and diced)
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 15-oz. can chicken broth
1 c. milk
1 7 oz. package uncooked macaroni
1 lb. Velveeta, cut into small cubes

Directions:
Pull all the meat off of the rotisserie chicken and shred.
Mix the chicken, mushroom soup, celery soup, chicken broth, milk, macaroni and half of the Velveeta together in a large bowl.
Pour into a 13" x 9" baking dish.
Top with the remaining Velveeta cubes.
Refrigerate overnight.
Remove from the fridge 30 minutes prior to baking.
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Bake for one hour until bubbly and browned on top.

Description: 
A yummy, cheesy casserole that you put together the day ahead, let sit overnight and bake to perfection.
