Title:
Apple Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. shortening
2 large eggs
1 t. vanilla
1 t. baking powder
1 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
1 t. cinnamon
2 c. flour
2 c. chopped apples, peeled and cored
1/2 c. chopped nuts
1 T. sugar
1/4 t. cinnamon

Directions:
Cream the first four ingredients.
Add dry ingredients; blend well. Fold in apples and nuts.
Pour into greased loaf pan. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar over the batter.
Bake at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes.
