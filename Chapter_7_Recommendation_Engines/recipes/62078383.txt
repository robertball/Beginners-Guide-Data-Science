Title:
Smokey Cheddar Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2/3 cup real chopped bacon
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/3 cup chopped black olives
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup real mayonnaise
crackers, party rye squares

Directions:
Combine the cheese, bacon, onion, olives, and parsley in a bowl and mix well.  Stir in mayonnaise.
Can be spread cold on crackers or warm spread onto party rye squares and baked at 400 degrees for 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
A delicous spread served cold on crackers or warm baked on party rye squares.  Enjoy!
