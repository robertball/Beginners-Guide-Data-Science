Title:
Chocolate Pecan Doughnut Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pudding
10 Glazed or Chocolate Doughnuts, cut into chunks
1 14oz Can of Condensed Milk
1/2 Cup Semi Sweet Mini Chocolate Chips, Divided
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
2 Tablespoons Pecans, Chopped
Icing
1/4 Cup Semi Sweet Mini Chocolate Chips
2 Tablespoons Butter
1 Cup Powdered Sugar
1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla
2 Tablespoons Hot Water

Directions:
Cut doughnuts into chunks.
In Microwave melt 1/4 cup of chocolate chips along with condensed milk.  Add Vanilla and Salt.
Pour mixture over doughnuts and toss to coat well.  Let sit for a few minutes to cool.
Beat egg and toss in doughnut mixture.  Allow to sit for a few minutes to absorb some of the egg/chocolate mixture.
Pour into 9x13 greased baking dish.
Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup of chocolate chips and pecans.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 30 - 40 minutes or until set.
Meanwhile make your icing.  Mix butter and chocolate chips into bowl.  Microwave for 30 seconds and stir until melted.  Add powdered sugar, vanilla and hot water.  Pour over pudding.
Serve warm.

Description:
