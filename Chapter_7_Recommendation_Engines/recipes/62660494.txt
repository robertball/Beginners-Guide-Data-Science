Title:
Palichinkas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-Crepes-
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup unsalted butter; softened
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs; beaten
3 cups milk
-Filling-
2 lbs. large curd cottage cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup sugar
2 tbsp butter
1 egg beaten
1 tbsp vanilla

Directions:
For Crepes - Combine all ingredients until mixed well.  Use about 3 tbsp of batter per crepe.  Grease small pan with crisco and cook crepe until edges slightly turn up, then flip.
For Filling - Combine all ingredients.  Use about 2 tbsp mixture for each crepe.  Fill in center, roll up, place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Put the remaining filling on top of crepes.
Bake 325 for 45 minutes

Description:
