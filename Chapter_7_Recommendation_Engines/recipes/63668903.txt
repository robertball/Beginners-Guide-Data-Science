Title:
Prosciutto Wrapped Chicken with sage pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. packed fresh baby spinach
1/3 C. packed fresh sage leaves
1/3 C. grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 C. EVOO
2 TBS. toasted pine nuts
2 cloves garlic
1 TBS. fresh lemon juice
Salt & pepper to taste
4 boneless skinless chicken breast fillets
8 slices prosciutto

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
For the pesto: puree the all but the last two ingredients.
Spread 2 TBS pesto on one side of chicken fillet, then fold each in half (taco style) Roll two slices of prosciutto around each fillet and secure with toothpicks.
Arrange in a baking dish; roast for 20-25 minutes or until 165 degrees registered.
