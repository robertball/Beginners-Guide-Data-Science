Title:
Cathie's Crunchy Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages Angel hair Coleslaw Mix (4 cups)
1 package beef flavored "Ramen Noodles"
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
1/4 cup pecan pieces or almond
2 Tbl butter
1/4 cup oil
1/4 cup sugar (or Splenda)
3 Tbl white vinegar
1/4 tsp garlic salt

Directions:
Break ramen noodles into small pieces.
In sauce pan, melt butter, brown noodles, sunflower seeds and pecans, set aside to cool.
In a small bowl mix oil, sugar, vinegar, garlic salt and beef flavor mix, set aside, this is your dressing.
When you are ready to serve, combine coleslaw, noodles, sunflower seeds and the pecan pieces, pour dressing over the mixture and toss well.
Do not mix ahead of time as the noodles will become soggy.

Submitter: 
Cathie
Description: 
Lite, crisp and crunchy. This is a great alternative to the traditional slaw.
