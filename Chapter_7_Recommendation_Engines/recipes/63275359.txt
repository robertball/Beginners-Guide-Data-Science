Title:
Easy Leg of Lamb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- boneless leg of lamb 5-6 pounds
20 + whole cloves of garlic
Garlic powder to taste
10 + sprigs of fresh rosemary

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Peel garlic. Make several indentations in leg with a knife. Stuff a clove of garlic in each indentation. Make sure garlic is stuffed all over leg! Rub leg with garlic powder to taste. Lace leg netting with rosemary sprigs.
Ace meat thermometer in leg. Roast 2-2 1/2 hours for medium rare ( final roasting temp I'll be 135 degrees, and final doneness temp will be 145 degrees ). Roast 2 1/2-3 hours for medium (final roasting temp will be 150 degrees, and final doneness temp will be 160 degrees). Cover in foil after roasting until final doneness temp is reached.
Carve to desired thickness.
