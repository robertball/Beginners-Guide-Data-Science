Title:
Homemade Kahlua Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups sugar
6 cups water
20 tsp instant coffee
1/2 gallon vodka
12 tsp pure vanilla

Directions:
In a large saucepan, mix sugar, water and coffee together. Bring to a slow boil. Turn heat down and simmer one hour. Remove from heat and cover surface with plastic wrap. Allow to sit for 12 hours. Remove wrap, with film from top of liquid. Add vodka and vanilla and stir well. Bottle in dark bottles.  Ready to drink, immediately.
