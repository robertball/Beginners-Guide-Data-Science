Title:
Chicken Satay Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-4 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
-1/2 cup soy sauce&
-1/2 cup lemon or lime juice
-1 tablespoon brown sugar
-2 tablespoons curry powder
-6 cloves garlic, chopped
-1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
-1/2 Cup Chopped Green Onion (Optional)
-6 (6-8oz) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves  Pounded Thin or cut in Chunks

Directions:
-In a mixing bowl, combine peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, brown sugar, curry powder, garlic and hot pepper sauce.
-Place the chicken breasts in the marinade and refrigerate. Let the chicken marinate at least 2 hours, overnight is best.
-*Grill;**Broiler&amp;;***Oven
-*Preheat a grill to high heat Weave the chicken onto skewers, then grill for 5 minutes per side.
-**Preheat Broiler to high heat, place chicken on appropriate foil lined sheet pan or oven rack, cook turning once until juices run clear, about 10 minutes.
-***To bake, Heat oven to 400F,put chicken on baking dish or foil lined cookie sheet, bake approximately 15 minutes until juices run clear.
