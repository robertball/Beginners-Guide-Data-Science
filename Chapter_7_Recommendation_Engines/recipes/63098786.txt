Title:
Make Ahead Lunch Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cups uncooked Quinoa or rice
1 teaspoon Cumin
1 Tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
1 (15 ounce) cans black beans rinsed
1 (15.5 ounce) cans pinto beans rinsed
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles
1 bag frozen Southwestern vegetable mix (corn, black beans, onions, peppers)
OR
1/2 bag frozen Meal Basics Southwest blend (onions, red and green peppers)
and 1 11 oz can sweet white corn
1 small can sliced black olives - drained (optional)
8 oz pepper jack cheese, shredded
1/2 of 12 oz can white chicken in water drained
10 (10 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
Cook Quinoa or rice per package directions. Remove from heat, and cool.
While grain is cooking put beans, corn, onions, and peppers in colander to drain.
When grain has cooled place it in a large bowl and mix with cumin and cilantro.
Add vegetable/bean mixture, chicken, cheese and optional black olives to grain and gently mix.
Put about 1 cup of mixture on tortilla and roll burrito style.
Wrap individually in plastic wrap, place into a large freezer bag, and freeze.
Heat at 50% power about 3 minutes in the microwave. Makes about 10 rolls

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
Easy to make, easy to take.
I make a smaller batch so that I can fit everything in my colander to drain.Modified from: Make Ahead Lunch Wraps
