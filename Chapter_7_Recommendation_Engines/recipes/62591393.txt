Title:
Sausage Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871684.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces ground sausage
2 tsp garlic, minced
3 (14 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 cup water
2 packages refrigerated cheese tortellini
2 Tbsp parmesan cheese - finely shredded
Italian Bread

Directions:
Lightly coat an unheated Dutch oven with nonstick cooking spray. Heat over medium heat. Add sausage and garlic to hot Dutch oven; cook until sausage is browned and onion is tender. Drain off fat.
Add chicken broth and the water. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Add tortellini. Return to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, for 5 to 6 minutes or until tortellini are tender.
Serve at once with a side of Italian Bread. If desired, pass Parmesan cheese.
