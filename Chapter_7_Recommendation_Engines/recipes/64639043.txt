Title:
Jerry's Rhubarb Strawberry Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
2 cups flour
2 tsp. baking powder
6 TBSP powdered sugar
2/3 cup butter
2 eggs, beaten
8 tsp milk
FILLING:
6 cups diced rhubarb
2 (3oz) packages strawberry gelatin
CRUMBLE TOPPING:
1 cup flour
2 cups sugar
2/3 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
CRUST: In medium bowl mix flour, baking powder and powdered sugar. Cut in butter with hand pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add eggs and milk then stir until ball forms. pat into greased 9x13 in. baking pan. Top crust with diced rhubarb and sprinkle gelatin powder over rhubarb.
TOPPING: Combined flour and sugar in small dish. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. sprinkle over rhubarb-gelatin layer.
BAKE: 45 to 50 minutes. allow to cool until firm.
SERVE: Warm with Ice Cream or Room Temperature
STORE: Covered will keep at room temperature for 2-3 days, maybe longer but eaten up so fast it never last a full 3 days.
