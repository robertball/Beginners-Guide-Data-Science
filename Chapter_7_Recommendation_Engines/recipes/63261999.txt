Title:
Granny's Brown Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Syrup
1c brown sugar
2T butter
2 1/2c water
Paste
2T baking powder
1/2c raisins
1/4t cloves
1c sugar
1c flour
3/4c milk
1t cinnamon

Directions:
Boil sauce ingredients until sugar is dissolved and slightly thickened.
Combine remaining and pour into a dutch oven. Top with syrup, do not stir.
Bake 350* for 1 hour.
