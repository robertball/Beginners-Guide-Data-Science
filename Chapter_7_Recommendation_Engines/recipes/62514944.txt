Title:
Betty Dugan's RAISIN DRESSING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521809.jpg
Ingredients:
8 C. Bread cubes
1/2 C. minced Onion
1/2 C. chopped Celery
1 C. Raisins
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Pepper
1 - 1 1/2 C. warm Milk (Heat just enough to melt the Butter)
1/2 C. Butter

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients and add milk and melted butter. Add additional warm milk to achieve desired consistency.
Dressing should be on the "slightly wet" side.
Place in greased casserole and Bake at 350F until well set and crispy - 45 min to 1 hr.
