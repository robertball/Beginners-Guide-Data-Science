Title:
Peanut Butter & Chocolate...Sandwich! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices white toast
1 heaping tablespoon chocolate hazelnut spread
1 heaping tablespoon peanut butter

Directions:
Lightly toast the bread in the toaster (or don't!).
Spread chocolate spread on one piece of toast and peanut butter on the other and put together.
