Title:
Scalloped Potatoes with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBS butter
1 tsp. dried parsley
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 Cup flour
3 Cups 2% milk
6 Cups thinly-sliced russet potatoes (about 3 large)
1/3 pound low sodium ham steak, chopped
1/2 small onion, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat.
Add parsley, thyme, salt, pepper, flour, and whisk together.
Cook for 2 minutes, whisking occasionally.
Slowly add milk, whisking continuously, until smooth.
Turn heat to high and bring mixture to boil, whisking continuously.
Decrease heat to medium and continue whisking for 2 minutes.
Combine potatoes, ham, and onion in large bowl.
Spray 9 X 13 pan and add half the potato mixture. Top with half the sauce, then repeat the layers.
Cover with foil and bake 65 minutes.
Remove foil and bake an additional 15-20 minutes, until potatoes are tender and top is golden
