Title:
Healthy Banana Peanut Butter Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup soy flour
1/4 cup wheat gluten
1/4 cup wheat germ
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1/3 cup applesauce
1/3 cup plain non-fat yoghurt
1/2 cup splenda
3 small bananas mashed
1/2 cup peanut butter chips
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1.5 tsp cinammon
1/4 cup milk
4 egg whites

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients.
Mix wet ingredients.
Fold ingredients together.
Place in prepared muffin tins (either sprayed with non-stick spray, or muffin cups)
Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
Cool completely.
Store in air tight containers.

Submitter: 
LYNNIE1979
Description: 
I tweaked a few banana muffin recipes to come up with a much healthier, yet very delicious version!  They are best eaten after being stored for a few hours in air tight containers.  They are moist, sweet, and a great snack!
