Title:
Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Cream cheese
1/2-1 pkg. Ranch dip mix
1 C. cheddar cheese, grated
1 T sour cream or ranch dressing (how "ranchy" do you like it?)

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, and ranch dip mix w/ mixer
Add grated cheese and sour cream. Chill.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A classic party favorite. Serve with crackers or cut up chunks of sour dough bread.
