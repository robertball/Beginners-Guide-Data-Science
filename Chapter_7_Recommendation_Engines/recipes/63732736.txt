Title:
Lemon Garlic Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 Cup Olive Oil
1 1/2 Lemons, peeled and halved
1/2 Tsp hot sauce
3 Garlic Cloves, peeled
1/2 Tsp Paprika
1/4 Tsp Cumin
1 Tsp Salt

Directions:
Place all ingredients in machine in order listed.
Select low speed.
Turn on machine and switch to high.
Blend 1 minute.
