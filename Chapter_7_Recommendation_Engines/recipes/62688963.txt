Title:
Chili Blanco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut in small pieces
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 small onion
2 teaspoon chopped garlic
1 (7 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 to 2 tablespoons diced jalapenos
2 teaspoons cumin
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
2 (15 ounce) cans white (or pinto) beans, rinsed and drained
3/4 cup chicken broth
1 1/2 cups shredded jack cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
optional - cilantro, tomatoes, green onions, corn

Directions:
In a 4 quart pot over medium heat, saute chicken in 2 teaspoons of vegetable oil for 5 to 6 minutes or until chicken is cooked through. Remove chicken from pot and set aside. In the same pot over medium-high heat, saute onions and garlic in remaining oil for 3 minutes. Add chiles, jalpenos, cumin, and oregano; cook for 3 minutes more. Add white beans, chicken broth, and reserved chicken. Heat to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Add cheese and sour cream; cook and stir until cheese melts. Add 1/4 cup corn if desired. Optional: serve topped with additional sour cream, green onions, cilantro, and tomatoes.
