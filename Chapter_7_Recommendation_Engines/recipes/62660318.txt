Title:
Nonnie's French Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Wesson Oil
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup ketchup
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
generous shaking paprica
2-3 tbs minced onion

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a jar bigger than a pint jar. Cap and shake vigorously to blend. (An old pickle jar thoroughly cleansed is perfect).

Submitter: 
RedHotTarheel
Description: 
Very easy to make and is perfect over a Bibb lettuce, tomato and avocado salad. My mom would serve this at Ky Derby dinner parties and it was always a hit. Simpler than other recipes on this web site.
