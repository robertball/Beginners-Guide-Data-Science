Title:
Refrigerator Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1467805.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C Shortening (butter flavor)
1 C Brown Sugar
1 Egg
1/2 tsp Vanilla
1 3/4 C Flour
1/2 tsp Baking Soda
1/4 tsp Salt

Directions:
Mix together shortening, egg and vanilla. In seperate bowl blend together flour, soda and salt. Stir dry ingredients into shortening mixture.
Form dough into a roll about 2 1/2" across and wrap in waxed paper, twisting off the ends. Chill dough until firm.
Heat oven to 400 degrees.
With a thin sharp knife, slice 1/8" thick slices. Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake 8 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Alicia
Description: 
These refrigerator cookies are super easy and can be kept in the freezer for whenever a craving arises. The date-nut variation in the notes is a perfect holiday cookie and a tradition in my husband's family.
