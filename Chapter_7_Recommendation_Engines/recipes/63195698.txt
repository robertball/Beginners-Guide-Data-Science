Title:
Easy Cranberry Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/825007.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups peeled, chopped apples
2 cups fresh cranberries (1-12 oz bag)
3 Tablespoons all purpose flour
1 cup sugar (I use brown, but white is fine!)
3 packets of Cinnamon Spice Instant Oatmeal
3/4 cup chopped pecans or walnuts
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Combine apples, cranberries & 3 Tablespoons of flour.
Toss to coat. Add 1 cup sugar and mix well.
Place in 2-quart casserole dish.
For the topping, combine oatmeal, nuts, 1/2 cup flour, brown sugar, add butter & stir well. Spoon over fruit mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Maeraj
Description: 
With my husband prepping the apples and cranberries and me working on the topping, this recipe was done in a snap! It tastes great warm as it is or with a scoop of ice cream.
