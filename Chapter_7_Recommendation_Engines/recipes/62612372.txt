Title:
Darn Tootin Good Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/567061.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground beef
1 Medium Green Bell Pepper (diced)
1 Medium Onion (diced)
5 Garlic Cloves (diced)
2 cans (15oz each) red kidney beans (undrained)
1 6oz can tomato paste
1 14.5oz can diced tomatos
1 15.25oz can whole kernel corn(drained)
4 slices of diced bacon
1 Tbsp Chili Powder
Generous sprinkling of black pepper, cumin, oregano, and italian seasoning.

Directions:
1. Cook beef and bacon seperately, strain grease.
2. Lightly stiry fry onion, bell pepper, and garlic in a touch of olive oil.
3. Combine all ingrediants in a large pot and bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes.
