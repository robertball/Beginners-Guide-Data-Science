Title:
Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages vanilla instant pudding
1 cup sour cream
1 can sweetened condensed milk
3 cups milk
1 large tub cool whip
1 package (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
4 bananas
1 box vanilla wafers

Directions:
Mix pudding and milk with mixer. Set aside.
Mix sweetened condensed milk, cream cheese and sour cream with mixer. Combine with pudding/milk mixture
Fold in cool whip.
Layer with wafers and bananas.
