Title:
Thai Spicy Basil Chicken Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked Jasmine rice
1 to 1 ½ lbs chicken breast (cut into small strips)
6 big cloves garlic (crushed)
2 to 4 Thai red and green chili  peppers or 1 to 2 Serrano peppers (crushed)
2 cups sweet basil leaves (Torn)
3 tablespoons oyster sauce
2 tablespoons fish sauce
1 teaspoon sugar
1 medium size red bell pepper (thinly sliced)
1 medium size onion (thinly sliced)
Garnish (optional)
1 cucumber (cut into bite sizes)
½ cup cilantro sprigs

Directions:
Heat the oil in a deep pan or wok over medium / high heat & wait until the oil starts to smoke.
Add crushed garlic & chili peppers, stir quickly (don’t let them burn)
Immediatley add sliced chicken & stir
Add bell pepper & onion, stir
Add oyster sauce, fish sauce, sugar and stir until the chicken is cooked through
Turn heat to high and add cooked rice
Stir quickly until sauces are blended with rice (a couple of minutes)
Add basil leaves, turn the heat off and stir.
Serve Immediately
*Garnish with sliced cucumbers and cilantro sprigs if desired.

Submitter: 
ErinInVegas
Description: 
A staple in Thai cooking. Adjust the spices to your own tastes. A really great use for leftover rice!! I get the basil from a local Asian market...it's different then regular basil and makes all the difference in this recipe. Fast & pretty easy to make but requires constant stirring.
