Title:
Chewy Peanut Butter Cranberry Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2144063.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C light corn syrup
1/2 C light brown sugar, packed
3/4 C crunchy peanut butter
1 t vanilla
1 1/2 C rice crispy type cereal
1 C quick oats
1/2 C dried cranberries
1/2 C shredded coconut

Directions:
In a large saucepan bring to boil brown sugar and corn syrup.  Boil for 1 minute and remove from heat.
Add peanut butter and vanilla and stir well.  Add cereal, oats, raisins, and peanuts.
Work quickly because it will become stiff and hard to spread.  Press into an 8 or 9 inch square pan.  Allow to cool and cut into 10 bars.
