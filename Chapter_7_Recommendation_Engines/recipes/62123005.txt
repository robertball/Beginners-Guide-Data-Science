Title:
Curry and Coriander Carrot Concoction Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 carrots, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 carton vegetable or chicken broth
salt and pepper to taste
curry powder to taste
ground coriander to taste
mustard seed to taste

Directions:
Cook all ingredients in a stock pot over medium heat until tender.
In batches, puree in food processor and return to pot.
If too watery, add a pinch of flour and stir.
