Title:
Bacon 'N' Egg Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1/4 cup crumbled cooked bacon
2 tablespoons butter
3 slices American cheese, dived
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
6 flour tortillas
Salsa (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl, beat the eggs; add bacon. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add egg mixture; cook and stir until the eggs are completely set. Stir in the cheese, salt and pepper. Warm the tortillas. Spoon 1/4 cup down the center of each tortilla; fold sides over filling. Serve with salsa if desired.
