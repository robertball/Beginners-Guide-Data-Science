Title:
Vegetable and feta baked frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp olive oil
8 cremini mushrooms
1/4 cup thinly sliced shallots
4 asparagus spears, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 scallion, thinly sliced
1/4 cup peas
1 tbsp fresh chopped dill
4 egg whites, lightly beaten
1 oz reduced-fat feta, crumbled
4 slices grilled canadian turkey bacon

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F
In a med oven-safe frying pan, heat oil over med heat.  Cook mushrooms and shallots until browned, 5-7 mins.
Season with salt and pepper. Mix in asparagus, scallion, peas and dill.
Pour egg whites on top of mixture; sprinkle on cheese; shake pan to incorporate. Bake until egg whites are set, 15-20mins
Serve immediately with turkey bacon on the side
