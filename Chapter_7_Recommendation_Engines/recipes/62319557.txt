Title:
Pizza Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sm. zucchini and/or yellow squash
1 sm. onion
1 clove garlic
1 T. olive oil or cooking oil
1/2 tsp. dried Italian seasoning, crushed
1 (14 1/2 oz.) can Italian style stewed tomatoes
1/2 c. mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/4 c. Parmesan cheese, shredded or grated

Directions:
Wash squash.  Slice zucchini &/or squash into 1/2 inch thick slices. You should have approx 2 2/3 cups. Slice the onion into 1/4 inch thick slices and separate into rings.  Mince garlic.
In an 8-inch skillet heat the oil over med-high heat 1 to 2 minutes or just until hot.  Add zucchini &/or squash, onion, garlic, & Italian seasoning.  Cook, uncovered, over med-high heat for 5 minutes.  While cooking, use a wooden spoon to stir vegetables occasionally.  Add undrained stewed tomatoes.  Cover and cook over med-low heat 3 minutes or until squash is crisp tender.  Spoon vegetable mixture onto serving plate and sprinkle with mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
