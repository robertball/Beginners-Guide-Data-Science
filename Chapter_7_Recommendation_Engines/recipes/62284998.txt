Title:
Sweet chicken and Red pepper curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
1 red onion, chopped
2 cloves Garlic, crushed
3 breasts of chicken cubed
1 red pepper, roughly chopped
2 bay leaves
1 tin chopped tomatoes
1 tin coconut milk
2 tbsp curry paste (cumin and coriander based)
1 squeeze fresh lemon juice
1 tbsp sugar

Directions:
1. Sautee onion and garlic in olive oil for 5 minutes. Add chicken, red pepper and curry paste and cook for a further 5 minutes.
2. Add all other ingredients and cook on a low heat for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Serve.

Submitter: 
Melanie Booth
Description: 
A beautiful creamy, aromatic chicken curry, beautiful served with fluffy hot pilau rice.
