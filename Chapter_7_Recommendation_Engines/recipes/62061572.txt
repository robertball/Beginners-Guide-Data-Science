Title:
Basil, Tomato and Mozzarella Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 fresh basil leaves chopped
2 tomatoes sliced
1 ball of fresh mozzarella
1 loaf of Italian bread
1/8 tsp. of crushed red pepper flakes
Balsamic vinegar for dipping

Directions:
~Cut the Italian bread length wise.
~Layer tomatoes, mozzarella and basil between the 2 halves of bread.
~Cut into four sandwiches and serve.
~Add red pepper flakes to balsamic vinegar to use as a dipping sauce for the sandwich.

Submitter: 
Melissa Rapoza Fragoza
Description: 
This is a quick and refreshing no cook vegetarian meal.  Great for those hot summer evenings when you don't feel like cooking.
