Title:
Easy mushroom soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound frozen mushrooms (or ½ pound fresh ones)
2 spring onions, chopped
½ cup fresh dill, chopped
½ cup medium grain rice (or any other starchy rice)
4 tablespoons of virgin olive oil
juice of 1 lemon
1 egg yolk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil in a saucepan with the mushrooms and the chopped onions.
Stir for a while until the mushrooms are soft.
Add the fresh dill and 4 glasses of water, salt and pepper.
Bring to a boil and stir in the rice.
Simmer for about 20 minutes or until rice becomes sticky.
When the soup is ready, prepare the egg and lemon sauce (avgolemono).
In a bowl whisk the egg yolk with the lemon juice and add from time to time tablespoons from the soup.
Pour the sauce in the saucepan.

Submitter: 
constantina
Description: 
This easy to make and even more easy to eat mushroom soup is the best dish for a light dinner in a cold winter night or for an entrée of a multi-course meal.
