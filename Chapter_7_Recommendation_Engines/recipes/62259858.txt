Title:
Murtz's Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans cream of mushroom soup/cream chicken
1 pkg enchilada mix
2 cans diced green chilis
4-5 cups cut up chicken or turkey
12 corn or flour tortillas
3/4 lb mex blend shredded cheese

Directions:
mix soup, enchilada mix, and chilies in large bowl.. add meat, roll totillas
put in 9x12 greased baking dish. pour remaining filling over the tops of tortillas, spread cheese over top...cook 350 35-40 min until cheese is melted
