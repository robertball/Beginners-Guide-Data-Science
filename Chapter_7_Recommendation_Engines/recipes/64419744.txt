Title:
Dilly Zucchini Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c biscuit/ baking mix
1/2 c grated parmesan cheese
1 Tbsp dill weed
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
4 eggs, beaten
1/2 c oil
3 c zucchini, chopped
1 large onion, chopped

Directions:
In a bowl, combine biscuit mix, parmesan cheese, dill, salt and pepper.  Add eggs and oil; mix well.  Sir in zucchini and onion until blended.  Pour into a greased 1 1/2 quart baking dish.  Bake, uncovered, at 375 for 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Noel Keen
Description: 
A great way to use that zucchini harvest!  This has been a family favorite for years.
