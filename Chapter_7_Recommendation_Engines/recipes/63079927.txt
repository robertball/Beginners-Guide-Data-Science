Title:
Hashbrown Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs frozen hashbrowns
1 box Pacific brand condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 box Pacific brand condensed cream of celery soup (or chicken)
3/4 tub of sour cream (mixed with chopped dill)
1/3 lb (5-6 oz) cheddar cheese - grated
onion powder
garlic powder
pepper
1 C breadcrumbs
1/2 stick butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Mix soup boxes and sour cream together in saucepot and heat to barely boiling.
Pour into large bowl over the frozen hashbrowns and mix thoroughly, adding onion powder, garlic powder, fresh ground pepper to taste.  Add about 1/2-2/3 of the grated cheese.  Spread hashbrown/soup mixture into 9X13 pan.  Add remaining cheddar cheese spread over the top.  Cook in oven for about 20 minutes.  While baking, melt butter in a small container.  Mix melted butter with breadcrumbs thoroughly.  After first 20 minutes or so of casserole in oven, take out and spread the buttery breadcrumbs all over the top of the casserole.  Place casserole back in oven and continue cooking for 40-50 minutes.
