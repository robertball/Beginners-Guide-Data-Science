Title:
Soft Dinner Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup warm water
1/4 cups sugar
2 Tbs butter, softened
1 tsp salt
1 large egg
3 1/4 cup bread flour
3 tsp active dry yeast

Directions:
Add ingredients to bread maker and select dough cycle.
Grease a 9x13 baking dish. When dough is done, gently push fist into dough to deflate. Divide dough into 15 equal pieces and shape into balls, place in baking dish. Brush with butter. Cover loosely with plastic wrap. Let rise for 30 minutes or until doubled in volume.
Bake in preheated 375 degree oven for 12-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
beth
Description: 
These rolls are soft and delicious and mixing them up in a bread machine makes them easy.
