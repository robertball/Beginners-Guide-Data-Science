Title:
jicama, avocado, and bean salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. black beans or kidney beans
2 c. red kidney beans (1 can, drained)2 avocados, diced
2 tomatoes, diced
1 c. peeled, diced jicama
2 scallions, chopped
1 red or green bell pepper, diced
2 large cloves garlic, minced
juice of 1 large lime
2 T. chopped cilantro
2 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. cumin
1/2 each salt and pepper

Directions:
Combine ingredients and chill.  Serve on lettuce
