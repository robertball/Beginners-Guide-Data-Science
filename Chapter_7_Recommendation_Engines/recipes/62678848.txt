Title:
French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large sweet white onions
3 TBLS oil (canola)
3 cans beef broth
sliced sour dough bread (toasted)
8 oz shredded mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
thinly slice onions into rings
Saute over medium heat in oil until tender and carmelized.  Add broth.  Put in bowls, top with toasted bread and mozzarella cheese.  Broil  in oven until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
Quick and easy, goes well with french dip sandwiches.
