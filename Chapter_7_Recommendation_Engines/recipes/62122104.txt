Title:
Semi-healthy Chocolate Chip Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Earth Balance buttery spread
3/4 cup packed organic brown sugar
1/4 cup evaporated cane juice (natural sugar)
3/4 of a package (3.8 oz) organic vanilla or coconut pudding mix
2 eggs
1 teaspoon pure vanilla
1-2 teaspoons of coconut rum (optional)
2 1/4 cups whole-wheat pastry flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup shredded unsweetened coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
In a large bowl cream the butter spread with both sugars until smoothe.
Add eggs, pudding mix, vanilla, and rum.
Combine flour and baking soda, then pour into the wet ingredient mixture. Mix well.
Fold in chocolate chips and coconut until well mixed.
Drop by small spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake for 9 to 12 minutes.
