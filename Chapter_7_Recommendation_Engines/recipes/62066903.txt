Title:
World's Easiest Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops
6-12 tablespoons of whole-grain mustard (depending on how much you like mustard)

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to broil.
2. Spread one side of each pork chop with half the mustard and lay them in the broiler pan.
3. Broil for 7 minutes.
4. Flip each pork chop over and smear the other side. Continue broiling for 5 more minutes. Voil√©! You're done!
