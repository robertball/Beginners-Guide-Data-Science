Title:
Cowboy Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983930.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2.1 ounce) package HORMEL速 Fully Cooked Bacon, finely chopped
2 (14.3 ounce) packages HORMEL速 Chili With Beans
4 large eggs
4 scallions, roughly chopped

Directions:
Place chili in a large skillet over medium heat. Place bacon pieces on a paper towel and microwave for 30 seconds to crisp up. Fold bacon into the chili. Flatten mixture with the back of a spoon. Make 4 indentations big enough to fit an egg. Break eggs into indentations. Cover and continue to cook until eggs are done as desired (about 12 minutes). To serve, use a large spoon to scoop up an egg with a portion of chili and bacon for each serving. Sprinkle with chopped scallions.
