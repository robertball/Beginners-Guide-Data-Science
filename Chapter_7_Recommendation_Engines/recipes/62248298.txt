Title:
polenta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 c broth
1 1/2 t salt
1 c corn meal
2 T Butter
1/2 c parmasean

Directions:
Boil broth and salt. Add corn meal a little at a time. Simmer stirring often, 45 min.
Remove from heat and add butter and cheese.
