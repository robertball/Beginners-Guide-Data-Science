Title:
Grand Champion Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pumpkin (preferably fresh)
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar (packed)
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp salt
1 Tbsp flour
3 eggs (beaten)
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 cup milk
1 unbaked 9" pie shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together - except pie shell :)  Pour mixture into pie shell.  Place on center rack in oven.
Bake for 10 minutes at 425 degrees.
Reduce heat to 375 degrees and bake an additional 45 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
