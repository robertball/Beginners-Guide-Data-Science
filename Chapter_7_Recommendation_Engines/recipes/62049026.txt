Title:
Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (14 oz.) sweetened condensed milk
(NOT evaporated milk!!)
1 1/2 cups cold water
1 pkg. (4-serving size) INSTANT vanilla-flavored pudding mix
2 cups (1 pint) whipped cream
1 box (approximately 36) vanilla wafers
6 bananas, dipped in lemon juice
(this helps keep them from browning)

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk & water.  Add pudding mix; beat well.  Chill 5 minutes.  Fold in whipped cream.  Spoon 1 cup pudding mixture into a 2 1/2-quart glass serving bowl.  Top with 1/3 EACH of the wafers, bananas & pudding.  repeat layering twice, ending with pudding.  Chill thoroughly.  Ganish as desired.
Refrigerate any leftovers.
