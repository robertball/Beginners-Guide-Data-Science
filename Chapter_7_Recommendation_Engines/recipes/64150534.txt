Title:
Whole Grain Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1396738.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1/2 C. Butter
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Brown sugar
2 Eggs, beaten
1 C. Quick oats
1/2 C. Walnuts, chopped finely
1/2 C. Chocolate chips
1/2 C. Pumpkin flax granola (other varieties may be used also)
1/2 C. Roasted sunflower seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl. Set aside
Mix butter and sugars together to form a smooth paste
Add beaten eggs to butter/sugar mixture and blend ingredients together well.
Mix in oats, granola, chocolate chips, sunflower seeds and chopped walnuts.
Stir mixture well and add dry ingredients to form a smooth cookie dough
Take small amounts of dough and place 2 inches apart on parchment paper
Press mounds with a fork to flatten out and bake in preheated oven for 12 minutes or until lightly browned on top.
Remove from oven and place on wire rack to cool completely before storing away in a container

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
An old family favourite with added granola and sunflower seeds  to make a chewy cookie with lots of chocolate chips to enjoy in every bite.
