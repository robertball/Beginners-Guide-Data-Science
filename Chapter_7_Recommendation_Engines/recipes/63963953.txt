Title:
Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 ounces pork and beans
bacon, cooked, optional
hamburger, cooked, optional
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup catsup
1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon mustard
1 onion, chopped

Directions:
Bake in greased casserole dish, covered at 350 degrees for 1/2 hour.  Uncover and cook for 1/2 hour longer.
