Title:
Cranberry Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter - softened
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tbsp. lemon zest
8 eggs
1 1/2 cups flour
1/8 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/1/2 cups cranberries - fresh or thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Spray loaf pan with cooking spray.  Cream together butter and sugar.  Add lemon zest then eggs.  Slowly add dry ingredients then carefully fold in cranberries.
Pour batter into pan and bake 10 minutes.  Cover with foil and reduce heat to 325.  Bake until set - about 1 hour and 10 minutes.  Cake is done when a toothpick comes out clean.
