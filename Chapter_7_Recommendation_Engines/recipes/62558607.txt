Title:
No Salt Garlic Herb Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/541456.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons granulated garlic
2 tablespoons onion powder
2 tablespoons dried parsley
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried marjoram
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried ground lemon peel
1/2 teaspoon dried crushed thyme leaves
1/2 teaspoon dried crushed rosemary leaves
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground coriander seeds
1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground celery seeds
1/8 teaspoon cumin
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Mix all together and store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Alberta Rose
Description: 
I use sodium free all purpose type seasonings almost daily but have never found a blend I love. This is my custom version. It can be used for just about anything.
