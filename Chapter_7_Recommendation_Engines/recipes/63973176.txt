Title:
Aunt April's Heart Attack Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 8 oz. carton sour cream
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
4 cans green beans, drained
1/4 lb. grated cheddar
1 c. buttery crackers, crushed
1/2 stick butter
French fried onions (optional)

Directions:
Combine soup, sour cream, salt, and garlic powder.
Add green beans and mix gently.
Pour into 2 quart casserole.
Cover with shredded cheese.
Combine cracker crumbs and French fried onions (if desired) and add melted butter to coat.
Sprinkle cracker mixture over cheese.
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.

Karen Martin

It's terribly unhealthy but it tastes so good.  Fattening ingredients can be scaled back if desired.
