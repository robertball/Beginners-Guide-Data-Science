Title:
Sweet & Savory Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (16 oz.) cans pork and beans
1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1 small onion, chopped
2 tablespoons yellow mustard
4 slices bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In an 8x8 baking dish, mix beans, brown sugar, corn syrup, onion, and mustard.
Layer bacon on top of bean mixture.
Bake uncovered in preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, till bacon is browned.
