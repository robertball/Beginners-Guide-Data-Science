Title:
Watermelon Smoothie Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of ice
1/2 sugar or more if needed
1 Watermelon, broken into cubes or pieces , without seeds and
2-3 leaves Spearmint ( Pudina )

Directions:
1. In a blender put the sugar and ice and blend it for 1-2min.
2.Add watermelon and blend it again.
3.Pour into tall glasses.
4.Serve chill and garnish it with the spearmint leaves.

Submitter: 
Rajvi
Description: 
This is one of my favourite recipes and it is also very easy to make and helps alot in summer and prevents dehydration. 
Have a happy summer!
