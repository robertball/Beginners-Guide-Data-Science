Title:
Hot Ham and Cheese Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. sliced ham
3/4 lb. swiss cheese, sliced
1 stick butter, melted
1 TB poppy seeds
1 tsp. mustard
1 tsp. onion flakes
20 hamburger buns

Directions:
Place ham and swiss cheese on top of half of bun.  Mix poppy seeds, mustard and onion flakes in with the butter and spread on the other half of the bun.  Place on cookie sheet and bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.

Description: 
These are easy to fix and great for a large party.
