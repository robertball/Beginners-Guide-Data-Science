Title:
Spicy Udon Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I lb. Linguine pasta
2 cups Chicken or Vegetable stock
1 Serano pepper stemmed, seeded,and de-veined
2 links Kielbasa, preferably a non-spicy one
or
1 package firm tofu
1 head  Napa cabbage
or
3 leeks
1 medium onion (if using cabbage)
1 pkg. Cremini mushrooms washed
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
Salt and White pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring 1 cup water and 2 cups Chicken or Vegetable stock to boil
Add linguine and serano pepper and boil until al dente
Add the mushrooms, cabbage and onion, or the leeks along with tofu and/or Kielbasa and cook until they have somewhat lost their pigment.
Serve in bowls and garnish with the cilantro (this also adds alot of flavour)
Use individual salt and white pepper to taste

Submitter: 
Anna
Description: 
If you know what I mean by clean flavours, this is defiently one of them! Very healthy with lots of veggies and also a hearty bowl you can warm up to!! A family staple in my home!! If you are looking for something very hearty, you can add both kielbasa and tofu!
