Title:
Crawfish Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb crawfish tails
1 large onion
1/2 cup shallots
1/2 bell pepper
1/c butter
2 celery ribs
1 can creamof celery soup
1 beaten egg
1-1/4 c minced parsley
1/2 c bread crumps
1 tsp salt
Optional: Taste mixture before adding 1/2 tsp red pepper and /2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Chop onion, garlic, pepper and celery, then saute in butter.  Add soup, tomato sauce crawfish and parsley.  Cook slowly for ten (10) minutes.  Turn off heat, add milk, egg and bread crumbs.  Mix well, pour into pie crusts.
Bake for 35 - 40 minutes @ 350 degrees F.
