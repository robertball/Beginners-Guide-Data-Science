Title:
Kicked Up Salmon with White BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Salmon Filet
2 Tbsp Adams Reserve Kicked up Chicken Ru
3 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Sauce:
1 Jar Cookwell & Company White BBQ Sauce
1 8oz Heavy Whipping Cream

Directions:
Coat fish with olive oil then season with Adams Rub. Combine BQ sauce and cream. Heat in sauce pan
Preheat oven to 400* Cook salmon for 15mins
Serve fish over White BBQ sauce
