Title:
Cinnamon Sugar Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/761149.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
4 teaspoons packed brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Beat all ingredients until creamy. Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Donna
Description: 
A cinnamon sugar butter that is great on any kind of toast, especially multigrain toast
