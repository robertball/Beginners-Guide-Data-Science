Title:
Chocolate Banana Buttermilk Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups mashed ripe bananas ( about 3 large)
2 tsp lemon juice
2 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup cocoa
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup vegetable oil or applesauce
2 cups lightly packed brown sugar
3 eggs
1 tbsp vanilla
1 1/2 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
GLAZE
1/4 cup dark chocolate Chips
3 tbsp milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F or 190 C. Grease and flour or spray bunt pan. In a small bowl mix together banana and lemon juice. Set aside. In a medium bowl, mix together flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt. Set aside. In a large bowl, beat oil or applesauce, brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs, one at a time and add vanilla. Blend in half the flour mixture and half the buttermilk, then mix in remaining flour and buttermilk. Fold in bananas and 1/2 cup of the chocolate chips. Spread into bunt pan.  Bake for 55 min or until toothpick comes out clean. Cool in pan for 30 minutes before turning onto cake plate.
To make glaze:
melt 1/4 cup chocolate chips with milk on high for 1 minute, then stir together with fork until smooth. Drizzle over cake.
