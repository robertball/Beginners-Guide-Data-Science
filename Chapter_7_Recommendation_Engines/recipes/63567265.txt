Title:
Groundmeat 'N gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# hamburger
1 C. Onion,chopped
1 C. Green pepper, chopped
1/2 pkg. fresh sliced mushrooms(or 1 15-oz can, drained)
tsp. minced garlic (optional)
1 TBS Worchestershire Sauce
1 Pkg. Microwaveable Brown or Brown/Wild Rice
salt & pepper to taste
2 Packets of herb gravy mix
1-1/2 C. water.

Directions:
Chop onion and green pepper and put in a large bowl with fresh or drained canned 'shrooms.
In a large skillet, brown hamburger and set aside, without draining.
While meat is browning, microwave rice according to package directions (90 seconds) and make gravy (below).
Put gravy mix and 1-1/2 C. water in a small saucepan on medium heat, stirring often.  When gravy begins to bubble, turn heat down to low and simmer for 1 minute.
Add chopped veggies and garlic (if used) to the pan and cook, stirring occasionally, till tender, about 7 minutes.
Add the meat back to the pan, add the gravy and rice. Stir to mix well.
Simmer, covered, for 30 minutes stir again and serve.

Submitter: 
Cindy Sherry-Cassel
Description: 
This is my husband's favorite meal.  It's so simple I'm surprised I haven't seen the recipe anywhere else. Follow the cooking order above and this hearty dish will be on the table in less than an hour.
