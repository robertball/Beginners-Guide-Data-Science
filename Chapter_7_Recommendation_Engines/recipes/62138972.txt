Title:
Cream Cheese Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 oz) cans crescent rolls
2 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 Tbsp milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Grease a 10x13" pan and spread 1 can of crescent rolls across the bottom.
Beat together the sugar and cream cheese until creamy.
Spread over the crescent rolls in pan.
Spread the other can of crescent rolls on top of the cream cheese mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the rolls on top are golden brown and crispy.
Meanwhile, mix together the powdered sugar, vanilla, and milk.
Spread frosting over the top of rolls when done and refrigerate until hard, about 3-5 hours.
