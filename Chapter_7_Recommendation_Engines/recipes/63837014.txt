Title:
Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2c finely crushed creme filled chocolate sandwich cookies about 18
1/4 c butter, melted
3-8 oz. packages of cream cheese, softened.
1-14 oz. can Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk
3 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 c mini chocolate chips
1 tsp. flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees
Combine crumbs, and butter.
Pat firmly on bot of 9 in. springform pan.
In large mixer bowl, beat cheese until fluffy.
Add eggs and vanilla, mix well.
Toss together 1/2 c. chips with flour to coat.
Stir in to cheese mixture.
Pour into prepared pan.
Sprinkle remaining chips evenly over top.
Bake 1 hour or until cake srings back when touched.
Cool to room temperatur.
Chill.
Remove side of pan.
Refrigerate leftovers.
