Title:
Green Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds seedless green grapes
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese or Neufatchel
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream or Greek yogurt
1/2 cup white sugar or Splenda
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 ounces chopped pecans
2 tablespoons brown sugar or Splenda brown sugar

Directions:
Wash and dry grapes.  In a large bowl, mix together the cream cheese, sour cream, sugar and vanilla.  Add grapes and mix until evenly incorporated.  Sprinkle with brown sugar and pecans, mix again and refrigerate until serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
Green grapes are folded into a sweetened cream cheese and sour cream mixture freckled with pecan bits in this fruit salad.Modified from: Green Grape Salad
