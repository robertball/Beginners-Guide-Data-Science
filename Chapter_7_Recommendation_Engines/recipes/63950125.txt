Title:
pumpkin bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 c. whole wheat flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. each: allspice, cinnamon, nutmeg
1/2 tsp. cloves
3/4 tsp. salt
1 1/3 c. brown sugar, packed
3/4 c. milk
1/3 c. oil
2 tsp. vanilla extract
2 eggs
1 (15-oz.) can pumpkin
1/2 c. walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Combine flour through salt in a large bowl; make a well in the center.
Combine remaining ingredients and stir well with a whisk.
Add to flour mixture, stirring just until moist.
Spoon batter between two bread pans and sprinkle walnuts on top.  Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 1 hour.
Cool loaves in pan 10 minutes on a wire rack, then remove.
