Title:
Low-Fat Cheddar Cheese Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/2 cups (125 ml) Veg. or canola oil
1/2 cups (125 ml) granulated sugar
2 tbsp (30 ml) butter milk powder
1 1/2 cups (375 ml) Low-Fat Cheddar or marble cheese
2 cups (500 ml) all-purpose flour
1 cup (250 ml) self-rising flour
2 tsp (10 ml) baking soda
5 canola cooking spray

Directions:
1) Pre-heat oven to 350
2) in a medium-large bowl combine eggs sugar oil water milk powder and cheese, stir until well mixed
3) in a medium-large sieve sift together, the 2 flours and baking soda
4) Gradually stir in the flour mixture
5) Spray the muffin pan with canola spray (i used butter flavor), then fill each cup until full for large muffins
6) Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown, cool 10 minutes before removing from pan then spray 4 cups for large filling them to
