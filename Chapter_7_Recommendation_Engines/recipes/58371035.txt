Title:
Refreshing Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/295357.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Roma Tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1 Cucumber, seeded and chopped
1/2 Bell Pepper, chopped
2 Green Onions, sliced
1/8 cup Colby Cheese, cubed
2 TBL Cilantro, chopped (or to taste)
1/2 tsp Sugar
Salt
Pepper
Balsamic Vinaigrette Spray

Directions:
Prepare vegetables and cheese as described and put in bowl.
Add Cilantro and Sugar.
Add Salt and Pepper to taste.
When ready to serve, pour off any accumulated juice and spray with Balsamic Vinaigrette Spray.

Submitter: 
CGG
Description: 
Summer time....the days are hot and you have plenty of tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers and other produce.  Use what you have in this salad and it will only get better.  Add pepperoni or ham and make it a refreshing and light main dish.  This is very refreshing on a hot day.
