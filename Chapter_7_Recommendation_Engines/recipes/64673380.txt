Title:
Ranch Dressing/Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4589809.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp of Ranch Seasoning Mix
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1 1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Mix Ranch Seasoning Mix and mustard into sour cream.
Add buttermilk and blend well.
Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
Serve chilled.
For a thicker dip, change buttermilk amount to 1/4 cup.

Submitter: 
Chef DJ
Description:
