Title:
Pasta with Lamb Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797685.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1 small onion, minced
2 cloves garlic
1 carrot, peeled and minced
1/2 green bell pepper minced
1 celery stalk, minced
1/3 cup red wine
1 lb. ground lamb
1 packet beef concentrate in 1/2 c water
1 t cinnamon
2 t dried oregano
2 T tomato paste
1 14.5-ounce can diced tomatoes
1/2 pkg rice pasta
1/4 cup grated parmesan for serving
salt/pepper to taste
red pepper flakes for serving

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion, carrot and celery. Cook until vegetables have softened, about 4 min. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds
Add lamb, salt, pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally to crumble the meat.
Add wine and cook until reduced by half.
Stir in cinnamon, oregano, tomato paste and diced tomatoes, beef broth and return to a simmer. Reduce the heat to medium and cook until the sauce has thickened, about 12-15 minutes. Season to taste with additional salt, pepper and cinnamon.
Toss the pasta and meat sauce. Serve with parmesan and red pepper flakes.
