Title:
Jalapeno Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-12 Pickled Jalapeno Rings
1/2 cup Popcorn Kernels
1/4 -1/3 cup Butter flavored cooking oil
dash of salt

Directions:
Start heating oil in heavy bottomed pan with a lid to med/high.
Add Jalapeno rings 2-3 for light flavor, keep adding for more heat. Pick your own pain.
Let the rings sizzle for 30-45 seconds.
Add 3 popcorn kernels place the lid on pan and cook until the three kernels pop.
Add the half  cup of kernels and cover the pan shake lightly to keep the corn moving in the pan.
When popping slows pour into serving bowl.
Drizzle with extra oil if wanted and salt to taste.
Enjoy
