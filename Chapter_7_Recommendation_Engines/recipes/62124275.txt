Title:
Chicken Avocado Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 or 4 slices of chicken breast lunch meat
1/2 avocado, mashed
mustard
mayonnaise
2 slices of bread

Directions:
Toast both slices of bread if desired.
Spread on miracle whip and mustard
Add the slices of chicken breast
Top it with the mashed avocado

Submitter: 
Char A.
Description: 
A delight to the taste buds, this chicken avocado sandwich can have as little or as many condiments added to it.  I didn't know mustard and avocado blended so well together for a great taste.
