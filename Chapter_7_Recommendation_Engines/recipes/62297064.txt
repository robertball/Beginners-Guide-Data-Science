Title:
Maple Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 can pumpkin
1 can evaporated milk
1/2c sugar
1/4c maple syrup
1 tsp cinn
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp maple flavoring (?)
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 unbaked pie shell
additional pastry for decorations

Directions:
mix first 10 ingredients and pour into pie shell. cover edges with tin foil. Bake at 400 for 10 min and then reduce to 350 for 40 - 45 min.  cool.  While pie is cooling  make maple leafs for decorations bake at 350 for 10 to 12 min. cool
