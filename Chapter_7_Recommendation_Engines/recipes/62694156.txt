Title:
Garlic Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/607585.jpg
Ingredients:
Olive oil or Vegetable Oil
Garlic Powder
1 loaf of bread

Directions:
Cut bread into cube size pieces
Place in a large bowl and lightly spray oil till coated
Sprinkle garlic powder and mix well
Place in a preheated oven of 350 degrees F
Bake only 10 minutes or till toasted
