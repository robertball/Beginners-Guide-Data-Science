Title:
Asian Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons of sugar
3 tablespoons of soy sauce
3 tablespoons of rice vinegar
1/2 cup slivered almonds
2 tablespoons of sesame seeds
1 7-10 oz bag of spinach
1 bunch of green onions, chopped
2 Teaspoons of Emeril's Asian Spice mix

Directions:
Whisk together the (6 tbsps) of olive oil, the sugar, rice vinegar and soy sauce. Add salt annd pepper to taste.
Toast the almonds and seeds with the remaining 2 tbsps of olive oil in a skillet with medium to low heat.  Add 2 tsps of Emeril's Asian spice while toasting (and add the optional dried noodles at this point) Toast the almonds and seeds for about 6 minutes.
Toss the dressing, nuts and seeds and spinach togther.

Submitter: 
tomn
Description: 
Healthy Spinach Salad with an Asian kick. Could use the noodles from an asian noodle soup mix such as Ramen, but it's not necessary.  I used Emeril's Asian Spice to add a kick to the recipe while toasting almonds.
