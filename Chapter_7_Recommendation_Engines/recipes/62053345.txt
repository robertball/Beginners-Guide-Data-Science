Title:
Mexican Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans bean dip,
8 ounces sour cream,
1/2 cup mayo,
1 pkg taco seasoning mix,
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese,
3 tomatoes, diced,
1/2 cup chopped green onions,
1 can black olives.

Directions:
Mix bean dip, sour cream, mayo, and taco seasoning.  Place in a 9x9 pan.  Top with cheese, tomatoes, onions, balck olives.  May also add jalapeno peppers if desired.  Decorate with sour cream and green onion.  Serve with corn chips or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
Served this at a party, and it was gone before the party time actually arrived, so I had to go to the store and buy more ingredients to make more.
