Title:
Corned Beef Dip in Rye Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1 (8 ounce) container mayonnaise
1 tablespoon dill pickle juice, or to taste
1 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
.
12 ounces cooked corned beef at room temperature
1 bunch scallions including 1/2" of green tails, finely chopped
2 small dill pickles, finely chopped
4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
.
1 (2 pound) cannonball round loaf of rye (or sourdough) bread, unsliced
1 package Club or Ritz Crackers

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine the cream cheese, sour cream, mayonnaise, pickle juice and garlic powder.
Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, just until mixture is smooth and creamy.
Cut the corned beef into bite-sized pieces.
In a medium bowl, toss together the corned beef, scallions, pickles and cheese.
Add the corned beef mixture to the cream cheese mixture and stir until well combined.
Transfer to a plastic or glass covered dish and refrigerate for at least two hours.
When ready to serve, cut the top off of the bread; set top aside.
Pull large shreds of bread out of the loaf, leaving a 1/4‚Äù layer of bread within the crust; reserve shreds.
Spoon the corned beef mixture into the loaf and place the top on it.
On a large serving tray, place the loaf with bread shreds and crackers around it.
