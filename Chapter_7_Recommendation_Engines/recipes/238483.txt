Title:
Watermelon, Strawberry, and Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1341165.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 watermelon, cut into bite-size pieces
4 fresh strawberries, hulled and quartered, or more to taste
1 sprig fresh mint, leaves removed and stem discarded
1/2 sprig fresh lavender, flowers removed and stem discarded
1/2 lemon

Directions:
Mix watermelon and strawberries together in a bowl; add mint leaves and lavender flowers. Squeeze lemon over watermelon mixture and stir.

Submitter: 
Christine Alexandra Deer
Description: 
I am always looking for ways to add herbs from my small garden to my foods. I was so delighted to find that lavender and mint go well together in this mix.
 Happy eating!
