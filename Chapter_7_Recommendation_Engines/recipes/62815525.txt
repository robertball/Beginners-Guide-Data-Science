Title:
Latin Style Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boneless pork loin roast, about 3 lbs, trimmed & cut in 1/2 lengthways
2 large yellow peppers, seeded & sliced 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
1 tablespoon capers, drained
1 tablespoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
3 garlic cloves, sliced (1 tablespoon pickled sliced jalapenos
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place pork, peppers, onion, tomatoes, capers, cumin, cinnamon, garlic & jalapenos in slow cooker pot, stir to combine.
Cover & cook on low for 6 hrs, high for 3 hrs.
Remove pork from slow cooker & shred, discard any excess fat. Add pork back to slow cooker & add salt. (I left mine for another 30 mins at this point).
Serve with brown rice.
