Title:
World Famous Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup tequila
3/4 cup lime juice
1 oz Patron Citronge (orange liqueur)
1 cup powdered sugar
2 cups ice

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender (ice last).
Blend until smooth.
