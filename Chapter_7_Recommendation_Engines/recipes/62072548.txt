Title:
Grandma Dean's Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 medium onion- chopped small
1 medium carrot-grated
1 celery rib-chopped fine
1 tbl. oil
1 can beef or chicken broth
2 tbl. water
1 inch round angel hair pasta

Directions:
SAUTE onion, carrot and celery in oil over medium heat until soft.
ADD water, turn down heat and put lid on for about 3 minutes.
ADD broth and bring to a low boil.
ADD angel hair pasta, broken in half.
COOK until pasta is tender.
TIP: Add 1 can of shredded chicken if using chicken or vegetable broth when adding angel hair pasta.

Submitter: 
CHEF17411
Description: 
Easy to make like soup, but more filling because of the angel hair pasta.
