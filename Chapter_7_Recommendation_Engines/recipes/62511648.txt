Title:
Carmelized Ambrosia and Maple Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large eggs
1 L half and half
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup pure maple syrup
1 tsp. vanilla
1 large baguette, cubed or torn into chunks
1 Tbsp. canola oil
1 Tbsp. butter
2-3 large apples, cored and sliced
2-3 Tbsp. sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, cream, brown sugar, maple syrup and vanilla. Put the baguette into a large bowl and pour the egg mixture overtop. Stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate for an hour or overnight.
In a large heavy skillet, heat the oil and butter over medium heat. When the foam subsides, saute the apples for 5 minutes, or until soft and starting to turn golden. Sprinkle with sugar and cook for another minute or two, until carmelized, Set aside to cool slightly. Preheat the over to 375F.
Butter a 9 x 13 inch pan or baking dish or spray it with non-stick spray. Pour in the bread mixture and spread the apples overtop, stirring them into the bread mixture slightly. Bake for 40 minutes, until puffed and golden. Serve warm with ice cream, at room temperature or cold, plain or drizzled with caramel sauce.
