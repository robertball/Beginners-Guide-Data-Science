Title:
Pickled Eggs & Beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4565366.jpg
Ingredients:
1 dozen hard boiled eggs - shelled
3 medium beets boiled with liquid or 2 cans whole beets with liquid
1 medium onion sliced in rings
2 C white vinegar
3 Tbsp corse salt
Ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut beets into bite size pieces
Add 1/3  beets, onions and eggs in a 1 gallon glass jar repeat until all are in the jar
Measure beet liquid and add water to make 2 cups
Combine beet water, vinegar and salt in a large sauce pan and bring to a boil
Add boiling liquid to glass jar covering all of the eggs and put a lid on it loosely
Leave on counter until cooled to room temperature
Tighten lid and refrigerate for 1 week
