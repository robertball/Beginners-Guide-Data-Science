Title:
Easy Tangy Asian Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/863280.jpg
Ingredients:
2 seedless English cucumbers, thinly sliced
1 large clove garlic diced small
1 Tablespoonful toasted sesame oil
4 tablespoons Rice Wine Vinegar
1 level teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
Grind of fresh pepper
Toasted sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Place cucumbers in a large bowl. Whisk vinegar, sugar, oil, garlic, and salt and pepper together in a separate bowl; pour over cucumbers. Toss to coat. Refrigerate at least 1 hour to allow flavors to blend. Garnish with sesame seeds

Submitter: 
Tom Sobieski
Description: 
Crunchy cucumbers are tossed in a tangy and simple dressing for a light side dish to any meal. This is a recipe from a few Chinese restaurants in New York's China Town. Liked it so much I made them give it to meModified from: Easy Tangy Cucumber Salad
