Title:
GF Buckwheat Stuffed Bull Horn Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1116937.jpg
Ingredients:
8 medium to large sized Italian sweet red peppers (long and thin)
300 grams lean ground beef or pork or a combo of those
1/2 cup (60 grams) partially cooked buckwheat (boiled in 1 cup of water for 8 minutes and drained)
50 grams gluten free bread cubes, stale
2 Tbsp chopped white onion
125 mozzarella diced
1 egg
40 grams shredded parmigiano reggiano ™
400 grams tomato sauce
1 fresh hot pepper, seeded and minced (optional)
1/2 TBSP thyme
1/2 TBSP oregano
1/2 tsp ground pepper
8 large fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200° C.
Line a shallow baking dish with either parchment paper or tin foil.
Wash the peppers and cut off the top putting them aside.
Seed the peppers being careful not to split them.
In a large bowl, mix together all the other ingredients except for the basil leaves.
Place one basil leaf inside one pepper and fill with the meat mixture being careful not to break the pepper.
Place the pepper in the baking dish.
Repeat this with all the peppers.
Bake in the oven until cooked (approx. 60 minutes).

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Not your mama's stuffed bell pepper.  These horn shaped peppers make this dish fantastic because they are sweeter than green bell peppers. The combination of meat, buckwheat, mozzarella and basil give a unique flavour that makes this dish a Kids love to help stuff these; their long thing finger reach better to ensure the whole pepper gets filled.  Use gloves to fill the mixture if you use the fresh, hot pepper.
