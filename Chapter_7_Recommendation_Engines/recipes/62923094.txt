Title:
** Lemoncello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 ripe lemons
4 c. vodka
2 1/2 c. sugar
2 c. water

Directions:
Wash lemons well & peel away zest. Place zest in a quart glass jar or bottle. Add vodka. Cover & let stand in the sun for 8 days.
Prepare sugar syrup with sugar & water. Bring to bolil in heavy saucepan, stirring to dissolve sugar. Simmer 10 min., then set aside to cool.
Filter vodka & lemon by pouring through cheesecloth or paper coffee filters. Pour lemon liquid back into jar & stir in sugar syrup.
Let stand for at least 10-15 days before drinking. Can be held for months before drinking.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Lemon liqueur. Sip from shot glasses.
