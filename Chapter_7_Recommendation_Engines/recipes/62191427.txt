Title:
Mini Cranberry Orange Breads Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter or margarine softened
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
3 eggs
3/4 cup milk
3 cups all purpose flour
1 Tablespoon baking powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries, coursely chopped
2 1/2 teaspoons grated orange peel
3/4 cup chopped pecans, optional

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
spray pan cavities lightly with pam
In large bowl beat butter and sugar with mixer until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time; add milk . Combine flour, baking powder and salt. Add to butter mixture stir until just moistened. Fold cranberries, orange peel and pecans if desired into batter. divide batter amoung 4 mini loaf pan cavities.
