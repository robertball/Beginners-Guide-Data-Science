Title:
Cheesy Mostaccioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. package mostaccioli, cooked and drained
1 1/2 lb. ground beef, browned and drained
1 11 oz. can cheddar cheese soup
1 48 oz. jar spaghetti sauce
1 t pepper
1 t Italian seasoning
4 C mozzarella cheese, shredded

Directions:
Combine pasta, ground beef, soup and spaghetti sauce.
Add pepper, seasoning and 3 cups of cheese.
Mix well.
Place in a 9x13 pan.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake, covered at 400 degrees for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
FoodFan
Description: 
A quick, satisfying casserole.  Serve with a salad and garlic bread.
