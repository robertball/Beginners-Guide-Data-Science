Title:
Easy Meat Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz jars basil spaghetti sauce
1 14.5 oz can of diced tomatoes
2 large garlic cloves, minced
1/3 cup onion, finely chopped
1 tsp poultry seasoning
1 pound ground beef
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Pour spaghetti sauce into a crockpot, on high.
Grab a fork and crush the diced tomatoes in the can well, drain juices from tomatoes, add in with the spaghetti sauce.
Add garlic, onion, and poultry seasoning in with the sauce.
Lightly salt and pepper the beef on one side. Cook in a medium skillet until no more pink remains. Drain very well.
Add meat in with sauce, and stir.
Cook on high in the crockpot for about 30 minutes, or until sauce is heated through.
Serve over about 1 1/4 pound of cooked spaghetti.
