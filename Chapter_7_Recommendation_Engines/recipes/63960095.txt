Title:
Garlic Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1098836.jpg
Ingredients:
10 fresh asparagus
1Tablespoon soy sauce
1 Tablespoon cooking oil
1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
1/2 teaspoon white sugar
4 cloves garlic, choped

Directions:
Add the cold water in a medium pot, until the water boiling . Add asparagus into the boiling water, cook 1 minute. Take the asparagus out of  the boiling water,  immediately rinse the asparagus with cold water. Cut each asparagus to half, place it on a plate.
In a medium pan over medium-high heat, add cooking oil, fry garlic for a second( do not let garlic change to brown). Place the oil and garlic in a small  bowl, add soy sauce, sesame oil and sugar mix well. Pour the mixture on the top of the asparagus.
