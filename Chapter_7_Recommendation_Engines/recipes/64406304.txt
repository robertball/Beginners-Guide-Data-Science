Title:
Beef NoStroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2280823.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. 85% Lean Ground Beef
1 cup  White Onion (Minced)
2/3 cup Red Onion (Minced)
3 Cloves Garlic (Minced)
1 tsp Black Pepper (Coarse Ground)
1 tsp Salt
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup (Campbell's)
3 tbsp Butter
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup skim
1/2 tsp lemon juice
1-1/2 tbsp Flour
Pre-Cooked or Fresh Egg Noodles

Directions:
Prepare Egg Noodles according to package instructions and set aside.
In a sauce pan, melt butter, add Onions, salt, pepper, garlic.  Stir and allow to cook till onions are tender and translucent.
In a separate 10" pan, brown ground beef.
To sauce pan, add soup and stir to combine. Allow a few minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
Add Cream and milk and lemon juice and stir to blend ingredients.
Once Beef is no longer "red", Drain fat and add to sauce, stir to combine.
Add sauce to Beef and stir together.
Add flour and carefully mix to thicken.
Add about 2cps prepared egg noodles and fold into beef and sauce.

Submitter: 
Dave Niezabitowski Sr.
Description: 
Was originally going to be a Ground Beef Stroganoff but was missing a few ingredients, so I designed this one as a replacement.
