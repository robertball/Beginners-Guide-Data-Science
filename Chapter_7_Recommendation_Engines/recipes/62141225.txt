Title:
Lychee Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb canned lychee (in syrup)
1/2 cup dried goji berries (aka wolfberries)
3 cup water
3.5 oz agar-agar
4 oz yellow rock sugar

Directions:
Drain lychee while reserving syrup, cut lychee in halves.
Soak goji berries in hot water for at least 5 minutes until softened.  Drain.
Bring water to boil, add agar-agar and stirring occacionally, bring to boil again until all agar-agar has dissolved.
Add rock sugar and cook until dissolved.
Remove from heat, add reserved lychee syrup.
Pour into ring mold and add lychee and goji berries.
Refrigerate until firm (at least 3 hours).
Cut into wedges to serve.

Submitter: 
moosie
Description: 
Goji berry is held to be a super health food especially good for eyes.
All ingredients are readily available in Asian Supermarkets. (Agar-based dessert = halal & vegan-friendly!)
