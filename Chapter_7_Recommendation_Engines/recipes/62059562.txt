Title:
Bleu Cheese & Walnut Wafers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (do not substitute!)
4 oz bleu cheese
1 1/4 cups flour
1/8 tsp salt
1/3 cup finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
With metal blade in a food processor,add all ingredients but the nuts.  Pulse til smooth then stir in walnuts. Cover and chill 5 minutes. Divide dough in half, shaping into 2 logs.Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate 1 hr. Slice into 1/4" rounds and place on an ungreased baking sheet. Bake for about 12 min in a 350 oven. Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
DECAR48
Description: 
A good make ahead for Christmas time. Keeps fresh for up to a week in a tin. Don't keep in a plastic bag tho'!
