Title:
Fresh Hot Garden Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540282.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups husky cherry tomatoes (I used this variety because of their intense flavor but any fresh garden tomato would work well to).
4 fresh garlic cloves, peeled
2 jalapenos  w/half of seeds removed (add more or less as desired)
1 habanero with seeds removed (add more or less as desired)
1 cup packed fresh cilantro
1/2 red onion peeled cut into 4 chunks
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a food processor or high speed blender.
Blend until desired consistency is achieved.

Submitter: 
Beccaham
Description: 
A delicious homemade salsa made with fresh garden ingredients with a great kick.  It Tastes amazing with your favorite tortilla chips.  A great thing to do with an extra abundance of tomatoes from your garden.  Enjoy!
