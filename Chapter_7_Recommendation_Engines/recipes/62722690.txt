Title:
Enhanced Quick Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/622718.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of water
1 packet of instant hot chocolate mix
1 teaspoon of instant coffee
Milk, to taste

Directions:
Set the water boiling in either a kettle or in the microwave.
While it heats, get your favorite cup and add the coffee and hot chocolate packet to the bottom.
When the water is done boiling, add to the cup and stir well. Then add a splash of cold milk to cool down and make a little more creamy. Enjoy! Do you taste a difference?

Submitter: 
fiestyturtle
Description: 
Often times the instant hot chocolate packets just don't satisfy your chocolate craving. With a bit of one secret ingredient, your quick hot chocolate will come alive.
