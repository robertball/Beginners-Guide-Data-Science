Title:
New Potatoes with Parsley and Saffron Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 T. extra-virgin olive oil
1/2 t. sea salt
1/2 t. freshly ground black pepper
2 garlic cloves, minced
dash of saffron threads
1 pound small red potatoes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat.  Bring mixture to a boil.  Cover and reduce heat to medium-low; cook 20  minutes or until potatoes are tender when pierced with a knife.
