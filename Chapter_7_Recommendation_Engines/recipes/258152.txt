Title:
Prawn and Mussel Soup with Rice Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces mussels, cleaned and debearded
1/2 (8 ounce) package dried rice noodles
1 quart water
1 tomato, diced
1/3 cup diced onion
2 tablespoons tomato puree
1 tablespoon sauerkraut
2 teaspoons chile oil
1 teaspoon sliced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 ounces prawns, peeled and deveined
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Soak mussels in a bowl of cold salted water for 20 minutes. Drain.
Bring a small pot of water to a boil. Add noodles; simmer until tender, 2 to 3 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
Bring 1 quart of water to a boil in the same pot. Add tomato, onion, tomato puree, sauerkraut, chile oil, garlic, and salt. Simmer until onion softens, about 10 minutes. Stir in mussels; simmer for 1 minute. Stir in prawns; simmer until opaque, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in rice noodles. Garnish with parsley.

Submitter: 
haixingzi
Description: 
In this easy Chinese noodle soup, mussels and prawns are simmered in a tomato-based broth with a little sourness from sauerkraut. A big hit with seafood fans.
