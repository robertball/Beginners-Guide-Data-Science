Title:
Crunchy Curry Cauliflower with Tahini and Pomegranate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads cauliflower, cut into florets
1/4 cup olive oil
1 to 2 tbsp. curry powder
1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
Tahini dressing:
1/4 cup tahini paste
juice of 1/2 lemon
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 tbsp. minced cilantro
1-1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Combine the cauliflower, olive oil and curry powder in a large bowl and toss to coat.  Transfer to a baking dish, cover with foil and bake about 20 minutes.  Remove the foil and bake another 20-30 minutes until golden and crisp.  Transfer to a serving dish.
Meanwhile, prepare tahini dressing.  Combine tahini paste, lemon juice, garlic, cilantro, salt, pepper and a 1/4 cup water in a jar with tight fitting lid.  Shake to thoroughly combine.  Drizzle over the cauliflower and sprinkle the pomegranate seeds over.

Description: 
This vegetable dish is a bit different and looks very pretty served in a nice bowl.  You can adjust the curry seasoning to your taste.
