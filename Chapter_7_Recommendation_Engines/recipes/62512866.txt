Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5280642.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/4 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. nutmeg
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
3 cups sugar
1 cup oil
4 eggs, beaten
2/3 cup water
2 cups zucchini, grated
3/4 cup chocolate chips
1 tsp. lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl. Add wet ingredients and mix well.
Grease 2 loaf pans and fill to 2/3 full.
Bake for 1 hour or until cooked through. Cool. Wrap well (freezes well).
