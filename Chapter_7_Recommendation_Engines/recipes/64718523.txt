Title:
Easy Beef & Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5840247.jpg
Ingredients:
1-3/4lb. Chuck roast
2- yellow onions
1-bag Essenhaus wide homestyle Amish noodles
Knorr Beef bouillon
5Tblsp. Parkay
4 Tblsp.Flour
3/4c.milk
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
First cut chuck roast into chunks, place into pressure cooker then salt and pepper generously. Cut both onions into chunks and place on top of meat. Fill pressure cooker to max mark on fill line with water and add 7/8 Tblsp. Knorr Beef Bouillon.
Pressure cook for 1-1/hours or until beef is easily shedable.
You can use a crockpot as well, use the same steps, but cook on high until tender.
When meat is finished. Drain liquid into a large Stockpot, bring to a boil, and add noodles.Shred beef and add to noodles while cooking.
In a skillet, you will make a roux. On medium heat, melt 5T. of butter then add flour. Continue to stir until flour browns a little (3-4mins) lower the heat to a simmer, then add 3/4c. milk until a paste is made. When noodles look aldente drain add the roux into the Stockport and stir. Place a lid on the stockpot and simmer for 15mins. Add salt & pepper to desired taste & Voila! ??
