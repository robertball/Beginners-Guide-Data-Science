Title:
Spicey Chicken Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounce package of spaghetti
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 medium onion, chopped
1 can tomatoes with green chile's
1 pound Velveeta cheese, cubed
2 cans chunk chicken or 2 chicken breasts, cooked and cubed

Directions:
Cook spaghetti according to package directions and drain.  Combine all other ingredients and mix into spaghetti.  Pour into greased casserole and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Graden
Description: 
The green chile's and cheese create a greate flavorful combination in this easy spaghetti recipe.  I always get requests for the recipe.
