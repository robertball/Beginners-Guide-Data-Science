Title:
Black Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs canned chopped green chilies
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 15 oz can back beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup plain non-fat yogurt
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Place chilies, onion, garlic and black beans in a blender. Blend until smooth. Hand stir in the yogurt and spices. Serve with chips or veggies.
