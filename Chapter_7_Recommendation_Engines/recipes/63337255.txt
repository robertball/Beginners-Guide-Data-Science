Title:
Couscous with Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/885480.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Medium Onion, chopped
1 Medium Tomato, chopped
1/2 Cup of Basil, chopped
1 Bay Leaf
1/2 Cup of shredded Monterey Jack or Parmesan cheese
1 Cup of Lentils
1 Cup of Couscous
12/3 Cups of Vegetable Stock

Directions:
In a medium-sized pot, saut√© onion in  a few tablespoons of chicken stock for 2-3 minutes or until translucent.
Stir in the rest of the stock , lentils and Bay leaf; bring to a boil.
Reduce to a simmer and cover for about 30-45 minutes until lentils are cooked through.
Remove the pot from heat and remove the Bay leaf. Stir in the couscous, 2/3 of the tomato, 2/3 of the basil and cheese. leave for about 5 minutes.
Plating:
In bowls spoon in the mixture and garnish with left over tomato, basil and shredded cheese.
