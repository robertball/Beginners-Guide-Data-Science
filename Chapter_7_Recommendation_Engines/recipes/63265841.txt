Title:
Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups assorted chopped vegetables, such as squashes, peppers, mushrooms, onions, potatoes or whatever else is in season.
1 Tbsp chopped fresh thyme
2 Tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
1/4 cup olive oil
2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
Kosher salt and fresh pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450. Combine thyme, rosemary, olive oil and balsamic vinegar in small bowl. Put cut vegetables in gallon ziploc bag and pour oil mixture over. Seal; gently turn and massage until all veggies are coated. Spread evenly on large baking sheet. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake 30-40 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes, or until lightly browned.
