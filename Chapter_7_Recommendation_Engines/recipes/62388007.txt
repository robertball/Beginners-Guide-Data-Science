Title:
PB frosting for the clueless Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422915.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter, softened
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup confectioners sugar, divided
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 1/2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Beat butter and peanut butter with electric mixer on medium speed until creamy. Add 1/2 cup confectioners sugar and beat again until creamy. stir. Add other 1/2 cup confectioners sugar, vanilla extract, and milk. Beat with electric mixer on medium speed about 1 minute or until light and fluffy. Stir again then put on cake or brownies.
