Title:
Spinach and roasted red pepper pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10-oz.) can refrigerated pizza crust
1 T. cornmeal
4 oz. shredded Asiago cheese, divided
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1 (10-oz) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 (7-oz) bottle rasted red bell peppers, drained and cut into 2-in. strips
1/2 tsp. dried thyme

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Pat dough into a 12-inch round pizza pan coated with cooking spray; sprinkle with cornmeal.
Combine 1/2 c. cheese, nutmeg, 1/8 tsp. pepper, and spinach in a bowl.  Arrange mixture over dough and top with peppers. Sprinkle with thyme and remaining cheese.
Bake for 15 minutes or until crust is browned and cheese melts.
