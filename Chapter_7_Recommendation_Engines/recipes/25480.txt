Title:
Travis's Turkey Burgers with a Bite Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/412026.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 (1.1 oz) package dry mesquite flavored seasoning mix
1 fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
In medium bowl, mix together ground turkey, dry mesquite flavored seasoning mix and jalapeno pepper. Form the mixture into 3 thin, flat patties.
Cook patties on the prepared grill 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.

Submitter: 
JENLLONG
Description: 
Yummy, moist, mesquite-flavored turkey burgers -- seasoned with a chopped jalapeno for that extra special kick.
