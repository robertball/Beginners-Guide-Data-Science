Title:
Tasty Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large potatoes, thinly sliced
4 Tablespoons flour
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 1/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
5 slices swiss cheese, finely chopped, divided
2 medium onions, sliced, and separated into rings
1 1/2 cups 2% milk
2 Tablespoons minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Place a third of the potatoes in a shallow baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray.  In a small bowl, combine the flour, salt and pepper; sprinkle half over the potatoes.  Sprinkle with 1/4 of each cheese and half the onion rings.  Repeat layers.  Top with the remaining potatoes.  Pour milk over all.
Cover and bake at 350Â°F for 50-60 minutes.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Bake, uncovered, 10 minutes longer or until cheese is melted and potatoes are tender.  Sprinkle with fresh parsley.

Description: 
A cheesy side dish will be a hit at any meal.  Potatoes are layered with Cheddar and Swiss cheese and baked until tender and delicious.
