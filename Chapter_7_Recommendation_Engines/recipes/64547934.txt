Title:
Perfect Steamed Rice in a Tagine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3255251.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup rice (rinse, if desired)
1 and 1/2 cups boiling water
1 tsp or so oil (I used olive oil or sesame oil)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 185°C (365°f)
Add rice to the tagine.
Pour water and oil over rice and give it a slight stir.
Place top on the tagine, put in oven and bake.
Long grain rice: about 40 minutes
Brown rice: about an hour
Basmati/Jasmine: 30-35 minutes

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Bake any type of rice to perfection in a tagine. This is perfect for steamed rice to be eaten immediately or to use in other recipes after being refrigerated or frozen.
