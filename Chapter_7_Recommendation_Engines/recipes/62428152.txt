Title:
Junes's Chicken Tetrazinni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg vermicelli pasta
5 tbps Chicken bouillon concentrate
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 small can evaporated milk
2 cups chopped chicken
1 cup mushrooms
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
5 tbsp butter
1/4 slice lemon

Directions:
Cook pasta with chicken bouillon in water. Combine soup mix, milk, chicken and mushrooms in medium sized microwavable bowl. Heat mixture until warm. Once pasta cooked pour into casserole dish and sprinkle lemon juice over top. Stir in. Pour soup mixture over pasta and then dollop the butter evenly over sauce. Sprinkle generously Parmesan over top.
Broil in over at 350 degrees for 3-5 minutes or until Parmesan is golden brown.
