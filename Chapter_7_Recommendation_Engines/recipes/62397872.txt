Title:
Classic Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (16 ounces) Lasagna Noodles
1 lb. ground beef
3 cups Pasta Sauce
16 Ounces Light Ricotta Cheese
2 cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
2 eggs

Directions:
Mix Eggs, Parmesan and Ricotta Cheese
Brown Ground Beef and mix with sauce
Layer from bottom up in 9x13 baking dish; noodle, cheese mixture, meat mixture, mozzarella cheese.
Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
Remove foil and bake an additional 10 minutes uncovered.
Let cool 10 minutes.
