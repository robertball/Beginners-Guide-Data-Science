Title:
Hidden Valley Ranch Smashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/714493.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes, with or without skins
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1/2 cup milk
3 tablespoons butter or margarine

Directions:
Pierce skin of potatoes with fork; microwave 8 to 10 minutes or until tender.  Mash with fork. Add Ranch mix, milk and butter to potatoes; stir well.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
The addition of Ranch dressing makes a smashing version of mashed potatoes.
