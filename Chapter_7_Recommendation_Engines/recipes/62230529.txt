Title:
Buffalo chicken meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ground chicken
one cup fresh bread crumbs
13 cup sliced scallions
1tsp psmoked paprika
1tsp minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients above, form into 32 balls, place on pan and bake at 475 degrees 10-12 minutes or until browned and cooked through. Remove from oven drizzle with hot pepper sauce or buffalo wing sauce.  serve with toothpicks and blue cheese dressing.
