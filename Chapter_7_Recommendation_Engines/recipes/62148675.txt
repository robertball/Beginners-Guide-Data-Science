Title:
Asti Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-64 ounce bottle of fruit punch
1 quart of orange juice
1 quart of pineapple juice
1 bottle asti spumante

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a punch bowl and serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Livie's Mommy
Description: 
We serve this punch every Christmas and is the one thing all of our family routinely request. The original recipe I received from a friend included strawberries but my family prefers the punch without.
