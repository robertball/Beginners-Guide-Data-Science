Title:
Bakers Grease Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup crisco
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup flour

Directions:
Mix all together and store in airtight container in fridge - will keep for ages

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Use instead of spray mix for easy removal of all baked goods.  May make in any size, just use equal portions.  I use either vegetable or canola oil - not sure about others.
