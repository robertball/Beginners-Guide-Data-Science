Title:
My Mom's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894888.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup dry bread crumbs
1 cup skim milk
1-1/2 lb ground beef
2 slightly beaten eggs
1/4 cup grated onion OR 1 Tbsp onion powder
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp sage
Sauce:
3 Tbsp brown sugar
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp dry mustard

Directions:
Mix all ingredients thoroughly.
Place into large loaf pan, patting top flat.
Bake 350 degrees for 60 minutes.
Mix sauce ingredients.
Spread on loaf and bake 15-30 minutes longer.
