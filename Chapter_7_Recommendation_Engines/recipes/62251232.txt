Title:
Mom's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 eggs
bread crumbs - three cheese or italian
ketchup
1-2 cans of stewed tomatoes
2-3 cans of potatoes
1 lb. ground turket breast

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except potatoes and tomatoes.
Put in a loaf pan - add tomatoes and potatoes.  Sprinkle with extra bread crumbs.
Cook at 425 for 1hr.
