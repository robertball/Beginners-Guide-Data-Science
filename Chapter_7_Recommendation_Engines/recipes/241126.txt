Title:
Home-Opener Pasta Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2279011.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle WhipÂ®)
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 large cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons spicy Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon chopped fresh basil
1/4 teaspoon chopped fresh oregano
1/4 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
1/4 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
2 tablespoons pickle juice, or more to taste

Directions:
Beat salad dressing, olive oil, vinegar, garlic, Dijon mustard, sugar, pepper, salt, basil, oregano, thyme, and parsley together in a bowl with a whisk until smooth; add pickle juice and stir to thin the dressing to your desired consistency.
