Title:
Shrimp Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1   med Onion (chopped)
2   T. Olive Oil
3/4 lb Med Shrimp (deveined and shelled)
4   oz chopped green chilies
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp pepper
8   oz cream cheese
8   Flour Tortillas
1.5 C Salsa
1.5 C Monterey Jack (shredded)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350
Cook first 8 ingredients in skillet then add cram cheese until melted.
Fill tortillas with the mix and roll up and place in greased baking pan.  Smother with salsa and cheese.  Bake uncovered 20-25 min.
