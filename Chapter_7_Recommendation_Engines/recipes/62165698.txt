Title:
New Orleans Shrimp Po'Boys Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/335595.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound raw shrimp, shelled, cleaned, deveined
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 egg
3/4 cup cornmeal
3 teaspoons cajun seasoning (Tony Chachere's), divided
oil for frying
1 cup mayo
1 tablespoon cider vinegar
hot pepper sauce, optional
4 soft French rolls or hotdog buns
lettuce
tomato, sliced then halved (moon shape)

Directions:
Stir vinegar and several dashes of the hot pepper sauce (if using) into mayo; cover and refridgerate until ready to use.
Season cornmeal with 2 teaspoons cajun seasoning and season the cleaned shrimp with the other teaspoon of seasoning.
Working in batches, dredge shrimp in flour, egg then cornmeal and fry 2-3 minutes per side until the cornmeal begins to color slightly. Drain on paper towels.
Add a generous amount of the spicy mayo into each bun, add lettuce, tomato, shrimp and several dashes of hotsauce (if desired).

Submitter: 
JasLak
Description: 
A traditional New Orleans lunch - this po'boy has crunchy fried shrimp served in a soft bun with spicy mayo and fresh lettuce & tomato.
