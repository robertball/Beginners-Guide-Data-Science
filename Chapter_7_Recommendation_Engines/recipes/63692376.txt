Title:
BLT PASTA SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (7 oz.) package elbow macaroni
1 lb. bacon, diced
1 large tomato
1 large avocado
1/2 cup scallions
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup Miracle Whip
1/3 cup chili sauce
1/4 cup + 2 Tbsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. chicken bouillon granules
2 tsp. white sugar
6 cups lettuce, shredded

Directions:
Cook elbow macaroni according to package directions, rinse with cold water and drain. Set aside.
In a large skillet, fry bacon until browned and crisp, remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Refrigerate when cool.
Core and seed the tomato, dice. Peel and pit the avocado, dice and sprinkle with 2 Tbsp. lemon juice. Slice the scallions, green tops only.
In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, salad dressing, chili sauce, 1/4 cup lemon juice, bouillon and sugar; stir in the cooked macaroni, diced tomato, diced avocado and sliced onions. Cover tightly and refrigerate at least 6 hours or until well chilled.
Just before serving, mix in shredded lettuce and reserved bacon.
