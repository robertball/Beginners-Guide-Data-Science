Title:
cinnamon apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons butter
2 whole Gala apples, diced
½ cup dry white wine
2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
¾ cups maple syrup

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt butter.
Add apples and stir to combine them with the butter.
Pour in wine and vinegar and stir. Cook until liquid is reduced by half, about 5 minutes.
Pour in the maple syrup. Cover the pan and simmer on low for 20 minutes.
