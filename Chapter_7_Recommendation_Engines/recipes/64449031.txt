Title:
Tortellini Antipasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of cheese tortellini (no meat)
10 oz chopped salami
10 oz chopped pepperoni
10 oz chopped ham
8 oz chopped provolone
8 oz chopped Parmesan
8 oz Greek olives
2 red onions
2 red bell peppers
2 yellow bell peppers
2 green bell peppers
1 bottle Greek dressing

Directions:
Cook the tortellini al dente and plunge into ice water to cool. Chop all meats, cheeses and peppers into bite size cubes.  Dice onions.  Toss lightly and store in a jumbo zip-lock bag or large Tupperware - pour dressing over salad and mix well.  Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, turning over to distribute dressing every few hours.
