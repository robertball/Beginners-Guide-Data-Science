Title:
Baba Ghanoush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large eggplant
4 tablespoons tahini(sesame paste)
4 tablespoons lemon juice
3 cloves garlic
1 tablespoon chopped parsley
1 teaspoon hot paprika
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Slice  eggplant lengthwise
With a fork, prick holes all over the skin side of the eggplant and place meat side down on a baking sheet.
Bake in preheated oven for 35-45 minutes or until eggplant is soft and skin is blistered.
Remove eggplant from oven and allow to cool slightly.
When cool enough to handle, use a large spoon to scrape the meat out of the skin.
Process in a food processor until smooth.
Meanwhile, crush garlic cloves using a mortar and pestle with 1 tsp of salt to form a paste.
In a large bowl, mix eggplant puree with the remaining ingredients.  Stir.  Check to ensure there is enough salt adding more if necessary.
Cover and cool in refrigerator for 4 hours.
Transfer to a serving dish and sprinkle with more chopped fresh parsly and paprika.
Remove

Submitter: 
Maryam
Description: 
A traditional eggplant dip enjoyed in much of the arab world. Raw crushed garlic an paprika give this version a nice kick.
