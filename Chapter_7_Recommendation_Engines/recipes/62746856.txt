Title:
Blueberry Poppy Seed Brunch Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2/3 c sugar
1 1/2c all purpose flour
1/2 c butter or margarine, softened
2 T poppy seed
2 t grated lemon peel
1/2 t baking soda
1 egg
1/4 t salt
1/2 c sour cream
Filling:
2 c fresh or frozen blueberries, thawed, drained on paper towels
1/4 c sugar
1 T flour
1/4 t nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease and flour 9" square pan.  In large bowl, beat 2/3 c sugar and butter until light and fluffy.  Add lemon peel and egg, beat 2 min at med speed.  In med. bowl combine 1 1/2 c flour, poppy seed, baking soda and salt, add to butter mixture, alternatively with sour cream.  Spread butter over bottom and 1 inch up sides of greased and floured pan, making sure batter on sides is 1/4" thick.  In med bowl, combine all filling ingredients, spoon over batter.  Bake 35-45 min or until crust is golden brown.  Cool slightly.
