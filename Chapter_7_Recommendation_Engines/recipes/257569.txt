Title:
Paleo Cocktail Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4499712.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1/4 cup spicy tomato-vegetable juice cocktail (such as V8Â®)
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon lemon juice
3/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 lemon, cut into wedges

Directions:
Mix tomato paste, vegetable juice, horseradish, lemon juice, hot sauce, and salt together in a bowl. Cover and refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour. Serve with lemon wedges for garnish.

Submitter:

Description: 
A nice, sugar-free cocktail sauce that works perfectly on shrimp cocktails.
