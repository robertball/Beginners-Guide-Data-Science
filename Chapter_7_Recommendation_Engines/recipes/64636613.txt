Title:
Spicy Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion or ½ of a large onion
2 cloves of garlic (or 1 teaspoon of garlic powder)
1 tablespoon of brown sugar
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon dry mustard
½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 cups ketchup
2-3 tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce (or to taste)
A splash of Tabasco or Hot Sauce (optional)
Buns

Directions:
Dice the onion and garlic and brown with ground beef, salt and pepper to taste.
Drain meat well ( or rinse in hot water to remove the most grease)
Return to pan and add rest of ingredients (except buns) Stir until well combined and bring to a boil.
Simmer for 15-30 minutes stirring occasionally.
Serve on toasted bread or buns of your choice.
