Title:
Courgette (Zucchini) Ribbons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large but firm courgettes (Zucchini), cut lengthwise into slices 1/8 inch (3mm) thick.
1/2 garlic clove - or more if you want.
8 fresh basil leaves, roughly torn.
4 Tablespoons extra virgin Olive Oil
6 pinches of Salt and 4 of freshly milled black pepper

Directions:
Mix everything together.
Cover and refrigerate for 6 hours while it marinates.
When required, transfer everything, including juices to a large saucepan.
Cover and cook for 2 minutes over a high heat, until just tender. Remove with a slotted spoon.
Serve as an accompaniment, particularly to my BBQ Chicken Breasts
