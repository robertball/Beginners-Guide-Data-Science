Title:
Tropical Delite Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5211375.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk
1 (15.25 ounce) can mango slices with juice
1/2 cup orange juice
1/4 cup white sugar
1 cup shaved ice
1/4 cup whipped cream, divided (optional)
1 tablespoon shaved milk chocolate, divided (optional)

Directions:
Place coconut milk, mango slices with their juice, orange juice, and sugar into a blender, and blend until smooth; add the shaved ice, and blend again. Pour into 4 glasses, and top each serving with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkling of shaved milk chocolate.

Submitter: 
LindaC
Description: 
With homemade coconut milk made with our Florida friends, my son and I created this yummy smoothie using ingredients we had at home.
