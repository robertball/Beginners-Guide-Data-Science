Title:
Hawaiian Beef Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1334048.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground chuck
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 13 oz. can pineapple tidbits, drained
6 slices thick-cut (pepper) bacon
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 tablespoons ketchup
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Directions:
Combine beef, salt and pepper in a large bowl; mix well. Shape into patties.
Press 5 or 6 pineapple tidbits into each patty. Wrap each with a slice of bacon.  Secure with a wooden toothpick.  Place patties in a glass baking dish.
Combine garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, oil, ketchup and pepper flakes in a small bowl.  pour over patties.  Refrigerate, covered until ready to cook, at least 15 minutes (up to 8 hours ahead).
Preheat grill.  Drain patties.  Grill patties for 8 minutes on each side.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
Even my brother-in-law (who's not known for his acceptance of new food) loved these when I made them for the family at a recent reunion.  One tip? Don't forget to remove the toothpicks after cooking!
