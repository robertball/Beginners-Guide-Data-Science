Title:
Chicken Skewer Bento Box Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 ounce boneless, skinless chicken breast, marinated in Italian dressing.
1 bamboo skewer
8 ounces prepared salad mix
2 Tbsp salad dressing, any flavor
2 ounces swiss cheese chunks
1 mandarin orange, peeled and separated

Directions:
Cut chicken into bite sized pieces, place on skewer, and grill for about 7 minutes, or until done. Cool.
Add ingredients to bento style lunch box and refrigerate until ready to eat.
