Title:
Microwave Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ear corn, pull husk 3/4 down and thoroughly rinse

Directions:
Place on a dinner plate. Cook in the microwave for 5 minutes for 2 ears of corn. Carefully remove husk and silk, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kristi Dockter Watkins
Description: 
This is a no-nonsense recipe for corn on the cob. Perfect for when you run out of stove or grill space.Modified from: Microwave Corn on the Cob
