Title:
Homemade Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 1/2 oz) can Del Monte Stewed Tomatoes, Mexican flavored
1 lb hamburger
1 (15 oz) can Hunt's chili flavored tomato sauce
1 (16 oz) can pintos, drained
Onion, chopped (as much or as little as desired)
2 tablespoons chili powder

Directions:
1. Brown hamburger in large skillet over medium high heat.  Drain and return to skillet.  Turn down to medium heat and add all ingredients together.  Simmer for 1 hour covered on medium heat, stirring occasionally

Submitter: 
April Butler
Description: 
This is a great homemade chili that was passed down to me.  It's great for those cold nights and makes plenty!  Great with crackers.  But you must get the ingredients listed as they are for that certain flavor.  Can also be prepared in slow cooker while you are at work.
