Title:
Easy Hollandaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg yolks
1/2 cup butter or margarine
2 Tablespoons lemon juice
Dash of Cayenne
White pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat butter until melted and almost bioling. Place egg yolks, lemon juice, cayenne in blender and mix 2 times for about 3 seconds on low. Butter should be ready by now. Pour in 1/2 of the butter blend on high speed for 5 seconds then add the rest and blend for about 30 seconds more. It should be fluffy and thick.
Now just heat in a pan on a low temp stiring constantly. Make sure it is heated throughout warm not hot. Pepper to taste. Serve right away!

Description: 
Wow, talk about easy. No double boiler required!! Great classic taste, in no time at all. This recipe does not use water. Just mix in a blender and heat, it's that easy.
