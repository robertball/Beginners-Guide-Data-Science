Title:
Banana-Nut Broil Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c butter or margarine
2 1/4 c sugar
3 eggs
1 1/2 c mashed bananas
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
6 tbsp buttermilk
2 1/4 flour
1 1/2 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup chopped pecans
TOPPING:
1/3 c butter or margarine
2/3 c. brown sugar
1/4 c. rich cream
1/2 c chopped pecans
Mix all topping ingredients until well blended.  Set aside.

Directions:
CAKE:
Mix butter, sugar, eggs, bananas, buttermilk and vanilla.
Sift together flour, soda, salt and add to wet ingredients.
Add pecans.
Beat 4 minutes with electric mixer.
Pour into 9x13" pan.
Bake for 45 minutes in preheated 350*F oven.
Remove from oven and immediately spread with topping you had set aside.
Broil until bubbly & lightly brown.
Serve warm with a dollup of whip cream (or cook whip).

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
This is a good alternative way to use ripe bananas (instead of banana bread).  It's an easy dessert for family or even to top off a meal for guests.
