Title:
Helen's Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1067346.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices bread – toasted, then cubed
2c chedder cheese, grated
1lb sausage
4 eggs
2.5c ½ and ½  (1.25c cream 1.25c milk)
¼ cup butter, melted
1tsp dry mustard (optional)

Directions:
Cook sausage and strain,
Add to bread and cheese in buttered casserole dish
Mix milk, eggs, mustard, butter and pour into casserole dish
Cook 350F for 45m to 1h

Classic southern breakfast casserole my mother makes
