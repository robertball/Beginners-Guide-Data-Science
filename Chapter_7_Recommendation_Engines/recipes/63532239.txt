Title:
Chinese Lemon  Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3tbsp Sunflower Oil
180g/6oz Chicken Breast - shredded into strips
1 clove Garlic - thinly sliced
1½in piece fresh Ginger - peeled & thinly sliced
4 Spring Onions - sliced
½ Red Pepper - chopped
1 Italian Green Pepper - chopped
100g/3½oz Button Mushrooms - quartered
60g/2oz Mangetout(Snow Peas) or Green Beans - roughly chopped
½ Lemon - juice only
Splashes Soy Sauce - to taste
2tsp Sesame Oil
1Pkt Straight to Wok Noodles

Directions:
Heat the wok until hot, add 1tbsp oil and, over a high heat, add the chicken and stir fry for some 3 minutes or so until just cooked through;
Remove the chicken, drain on kitchen towel and set aside;
Return wok to the heat and add the remaining oil;
Add the peppers, onion, mangetout and mushrooms, stir and cook for some 3 to 4 minutes or so until just tender with the mushrooms still with bite;
Add the garlic, ginger, lemon juice and soy sauce and, stirring continuously, cook for a minute or so;
Tip in the noodles and the chicken and stir fry until both are heated through;
Drizzle on the sesame oil, mix thoroughly and serve.
