Title:
Big Ray's Horseradish-Cottage Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2616748.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz. small curd  whole milk cottage cheese
1/4 cup (or more) preserved horseradish
1 teaspoon fresh chopped dill
1/2 teaspoon sugar
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
Wheat crackers (Triscuits)

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a medium bowl. Cover dip and chill for at least 2 hours and up to 2 days to let the flavors meld.  Serve with crackers.
