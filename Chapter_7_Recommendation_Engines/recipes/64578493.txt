Title:
Blueberry Buckle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
3/4 cup white sugar
1 egg
2 cups sifted flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup milk
2 cups fresh blueberries
Crumb topping:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup flour
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Cream butter, add sugar and beat until light.
Add egg and beat well.
Add dry ingredients alternately with milk.
Beat until smooth.
Fold in blueberries.
Pour into greased 9x9 pan.
Sprinkle with crumb topping.
Bake in preheated 375 oven for around 45 minutes.
Check at 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Brenda
Description: 
Dessert or coffee cake filled with plump blueberries.
