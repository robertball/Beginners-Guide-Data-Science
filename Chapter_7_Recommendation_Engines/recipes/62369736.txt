Title:
Cornmeal Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup yellow cornmeal or polenta
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoons olive oilt

Directions:
Place the polenta, flour, baking powder and 1/2 teaspoon salt onto your work surface. You could also combine the ingredients in a food processor or a bowl.
Make a well in the center and add the water and olive oil.
Combine and knead for about 8 minutes until the dough is elastic and shiny.
