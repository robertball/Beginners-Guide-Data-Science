Title:
Sweet & Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635179.jpg
Ingredients:
Meat:
1 pkg. dry onion soup mix
1/2 cup water
2 eggs
2 lbs. ground beef
Sauce:
1 (8 oz.) can cranberry sauce
3/4 cup chili sauce
3/4 cup water
1/3 cup brown sugar

Directions:
1) In large mixing bowl, combine soup mix, and water (1/2 cup); beat in eggs with fork. Add ground beef; mix well. Shape into meatballs. Brown in a skillet.
2) Combine cranberry sauce, chili sauce, water (3/4 cup), and brown sugar. Pour half into 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange meatballs on top. Pour remaining mixture over meatballs. Cover with foil.
3) Bake for 20 minutes at 325 degrees; uncover and bake an additional 15-20 minutes.
