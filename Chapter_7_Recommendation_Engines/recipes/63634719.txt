Title:
Stir-Fry Pork with Ginger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 inch piece fresh ginger root, thinly sliced
1/4 pound thinly sliced pork shoulder chops
1/4 pound frozen stir-fry vegetables,thawed
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon dark soy sauce
1/3 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1 green onion, chopped
1 tablespoon Chinese rice wine

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Fry ginger in hot oil until fragrant, then add veggies, pork, soy sauce, dark soy sauce and sugar. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes.
Stir in the sesame oil, green onion, and rice wine. Simmer until the pork is tender.

Submitter: 
Dale
Description: 
A simple Chinese dish. The wine and ginger gives the dish its fragrant smell. I added stir-fry veggies to the ingredients. I like to serve this with peanut butter noodles on the side, posted on this site.Modified from: Stir-Fry Pork with Ginger
