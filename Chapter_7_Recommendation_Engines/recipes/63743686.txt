Title:
Greek Wedding Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 32 oz containers chicken broth
3/4 lb lean ground beef or lamb
2/3 cup finely chopped flat leaf parsley
1/3 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup feta cheese crumbled
1 large egg
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 sprigs oregano, finely chopped
salt and pepper
1 cup orzo pasta
grated peel and juice of 1 lemon

Directions:
In a skillet or dutch oven, bring broth to a boil,
Lower heat to simmer
Meanwhile, combine meat, hlf parsley, breadcrumbs, feta, egg, garlic and oregano
Season with salt and pepper
Form into 1 " balls
Add to the simmering broth along with the orzo and cook for 10 minutes
Stir in lemon juice and peel and the remaining parsley
