Title:
Easy Cheesy Au Gratin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. frozen hash browns (thawed)
1/2 stick butter (4 Tbsp.)
1/2 cup chopped onions
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 - 16 oz. carton sour cream
1 can cream of _____ soup
2+ cups shredded sharp cheddar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Saute onion in butter.
Mix together all ingredients.
Spread in a 9x13 greased casserole dish.
Bake at 375 for 1 1/2 hours.
May add more cheese on top the last 15 minutes.

Great anytime, but especially good with Easter ham.
