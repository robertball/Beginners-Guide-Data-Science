Title:
BEEF STROGANOFF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/717877.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large sirloin,cut in strips
3 Tbsp.oil
10-15 fresh mushrooms thinly sliced
1 med.onion,thinly sliced
3 beef bouillon cubes
1/2 tsp.salt
2 Tbsp.ketchup
1 tsp.dry mustard
2 Tbsp.flour
1/2 cup cold water
2-4 Tbsp.dry red wine
1 cup sour cream
4-5 cups cups cooked broad noodles

Directions:
Saute mushrooms and onions in hot oiled pan,remove to a bowl.Brown steak in hot pan,then reduce heat.
Add salt,ketchup,mustard,mushrooms and onions.
Dissolve bouillon cubes in 1 cup hot water and add to pan.Simmer for 10 min.Dissolve flour in 1/2 cup cold water and add to pan.Add wine,stirring untill thickened.
Immediately before serving stir in sour cream,DO NOT LET BOIL.
Pour over hot noodles and serve.
