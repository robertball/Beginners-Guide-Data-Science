Title:
Butterscotch Rice Krispie Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup karo syrup
1 cup peanut butter
6 cups rice krispies
2 bags butterscotch or chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt karo syrup and peanut butter in a pan. Add 6 cups rice krispies and stir together.  Put in 9x13 pan. Melt butterscotch chips and pour over squares.
