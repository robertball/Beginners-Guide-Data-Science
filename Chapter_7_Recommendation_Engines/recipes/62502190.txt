Title:
Low's Funnel Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 cup water
1/2 tsp. vanilla
3 cups flour
1/4 cup sugar
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
Canola oil
Icing sugar and fresh strawberries.

Directions:
Mix batter with a spoon.
Pour canola oil into frying pan and heat to bubbling.
Using a funnel to pour out the batter, make a cool design in the frying pan.
Remove hot funnel cake once golden brown and sprinkle with icing sugar and fresh strawberries.
