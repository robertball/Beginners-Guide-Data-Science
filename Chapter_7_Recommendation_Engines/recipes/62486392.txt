Title:
Mother Earth's Baked beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/489266.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. thick-cut bacon (fried and corsely cut into squares)
1 8oz. bag of chopped onion (sauteed in bacon grease)
1 lb. ground beef (browned)
1 1/3 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. ketchup
1/2 c. barbeque sauce (sweet baby ray's honey)
1 Tbsp. molasses
1 tsp. liquid smoke
2 Tbsp. prepared mustard (deli)
1 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. chili powder
1 (28oz) can of Bush's original baked beans (undrained)
2 (16 oz) cans Kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
2 (16 oz) cans Great Northern beans (drained and rinsed)
2 (16 oz) cans Pinto beans (drained and rinsed)

Directions:
In a large skillet, fry the bacon until desired crisp. set aside on paper towels.
Sautee the onion and ground beef in 2tbsp of the bacon grease and put into crock pot
Crumble up the bacon and add to the crock pot
Combine the brown sugar, ketchup, barbeque sauce, mustard, pepper, chili powder, molasses and liquid smoke into a small bowl and mix well.
Pour over meats in crockpot and stir up well.
Add the baked beans, kidney beans, great northern beans and pinto beans to the crock pot and mix well.
Cook in crock pot on low setting for 6 hours the day before you want to serve them. Refrigerate overnight and heat up in crock pot before serving.
