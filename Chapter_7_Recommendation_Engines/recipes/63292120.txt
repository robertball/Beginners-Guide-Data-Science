Title:
Blueberry muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/867807.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1 C. Granulated sugar
1 C. Whipping cream
2 Tbsp. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs
1 C. Fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F (180 C)
Combine flour, Baking Powder, Baking Soda and sugar in a medium size bowl and set aside.
In another bowl put whipping cream and granulated sugar together and whip until nice and thick
Add eggs to whipped cream and mix until nice and smooth
Mix dry ingredients with whipped cream mixture until all the flour has been absorbed
Mix in blueberries until well coated and then put into greased muffin tin
Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until lightly browned on top
Wait for 10 minutes before removing from cups and let cool on a serving plate

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very juicy muffin with blueberries bursting with flavour and goodness.  The addition of whipping cream makes them very light and soft textured.
