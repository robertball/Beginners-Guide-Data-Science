Title:
Prom Night Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/877059.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 fluid ounce coconut rum
2 fluid ounces cranberry-raspberry juice

Directions:
Measure the vodka, rum, and cranberry-raspberry juice into a cocktail shaker. Add a generous scoop of ice, cover and shake until frosty, about 30 seconds. Strain into a martini glass to serve.

Submitter: 
Heather
Description: 
This cocktail is pretty in pink and will remind you of a spiked fruit punch.  Hence the name Prom Night, as the punch at our prom in high school was spiked.
