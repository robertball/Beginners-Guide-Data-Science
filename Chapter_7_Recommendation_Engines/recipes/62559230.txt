Title:
Yummy Hot White Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c white chocolate
1 c heavy cream
4 c milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
whip cream for topping (optional)
white chocolate shavings for topping (optional)

Directions:
Combine white chocolate & heavy cream in saucepan over medium heat. Stir continuously until chocolate melts.
Add milk & vanilla & keep stirring until heated.
Pour into mugs & top with whip cream and chocolate shaving if desired.
