Title:
Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. boneless chicken breasts
1 3/4 cups chicken broth
2 chicken bouillon cubes
2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. poultry seasoning
6 small carrots, cut into 1" chunks
2 cups buttermilk biscuit mix
1/2 cup plus 1 tbsp. milk
1 tsp. parsley

Directions:
Arrange chicken in slow cooker.
Dissolve bouillon in broth in a bowl.  Stir in salt, pepper, and poultry seasoning.
Pour over chicken.
Spread carrots over top.
Cover.  Cook on low 6-8 hours or high 3-3 1/2 hours or until chicken is tender but not dry.
Combine biscuit ingredients (last 3 on list) until just moistened.  Drop by spoonful's over steaming chicken.
Cover.  Cook on high 35 minutes.  Do not remove cover while dumplings are cooking.
Serve immediately.
