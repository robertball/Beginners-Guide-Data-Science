Title:
Hawaiian Hot Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 hot dogs
5 hoagie rolls
1 can crushed pineapple, or tidbits, drained
1/4 sweet onion, diced
1/4 cup mayo
1 1/2 Tbls. mustard (dijon if you prefer)
garlic powder
butter spread
shredded Jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Mix pineapple, mayo, mustard and diced onion in small bowl
Cut hoagie rolls in half and spread butter on each half
Sprinkle garlic powder on buttered hoagie rolls
Toast hoagie rolls in oven 5-10 mins until slightly crispy
Cook hot dogs throughout on grill or in broiler
Put cooked hot dogs on toasted hoagie rolls
Top with pineapple mixture
Finish with shredded jack cheese
Put in broiler just long enough to heat pineapple mix and melt the cheese
Serve with corn on the cob

Submitter: 
BEARNESTA
Description: 
Pineapple, sweet onions, mayo, mustard and melted Jack cheese make these hot dogs a hawaiian treat.  Simple, quick, and inexpensive - the kids will love them!
