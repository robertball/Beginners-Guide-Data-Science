Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pkgs frozen broccoli flowerettes thawed
11/2 C Hellmans mayo
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
2 cups sharp cheddar cheese
2 eggs slightly beaten

Directions:
Beat eggs and blend rest of ingredients in with wire wisk. Preheat oven to 350. Grease 13x9 pyrex dish. Layer Broccoli/sauce/broc/sauce. Bake casserole for 40-45 mins. Add 1 cup crushed cheese it cracker to top and bake an additional 15 minutes.
