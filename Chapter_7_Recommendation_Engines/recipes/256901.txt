Title:
Fuzzy Navel Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4461306.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup fresh or frozen sliced peaches
1/2 medium banana
1/4 cup plain fat-free yogurt
1 packet Classic French Vanilla Flavor CARNATION BREAKFAST ESSENTIALS® LIGHT START™ Complete Nutritional Drink

Directions:
Place juice, peaches, banana, yogurt and Carnation Breakfast Essentials Drink in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Carnation Breakfast Essentials
Description: 
This shake is a refreshing no-alcohol alternative with all the great taste. Try the Fuzzy Navel recipe from Carnation Breakfast Essentials today!
