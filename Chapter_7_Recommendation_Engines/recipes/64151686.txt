Title:
Orange Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oil
1-3/4 cups sugar
3 eggs
2 tsp almond flavoring
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup orange juice
1 Tbsp grated orange rind
2 cups grated zucchini
1/2 c nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  Mix all dry ingredients and set aside.  Then mix the rest of the ingredients and cream well.  Add the dry ingredients and mix well.
Pour into 2 greased bread pans and bake for 50-60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
An orange and almond twist to zucchini bread.
