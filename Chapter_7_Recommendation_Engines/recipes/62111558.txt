Title:
Peanutty Chicken Ramen Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package ramen noodles (beef or chicken flavor)
1 tablespoon peanut sauce
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1/4 c cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
1/2 c cooked chicken, diced

Directions:
Bring 1 1/2 cups water to a boil. Add ramen noodles ONLY and cook until soft, about 3 minutes. Drain, reserving about a teaspoon liquid.
Return to low heat. Add 1/2 tsp of the seasoning packet, as well as the peanut sauce, soy sauce, chicken, and cucumber. Stir until warmed and combined. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Hugatunity
Description: 
This dish is easy, economically friendly, and a great way to use up leftover chicken. If you don't have peanut sauce, a tablespoon of peanut butter with about 1/2 tsp vineager, some ground ginger and pepper will do.
