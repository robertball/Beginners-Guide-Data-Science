Title:
Gorgonzola and Pear Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf ciabatta bread
1 pear, cored and very thinly sliced
1 red onion, very thinly sliced
1 T. olive oil
1 cup Gorgonzola, crumbled
1/4 cup chopped, toasted walnuts

Directions:
Slice bread into one inch slices and place on ungreased baking sheet.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Place on slices in this order: pear, onion, drizzle of oil, gorgonzola.
Bake for 10 minutes, then sprinkle with walnuts.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Cathy H.
Description: 
Very elegant and delicious appetizer - and easy!
