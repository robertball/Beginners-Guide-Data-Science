Title:
Jalapeno & Cilantro Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup mayonnaise
1 tbsp lime juice
3 cups shredded cooked chicken
1 celery rib, diced fine
½ small red onion, diced fine
½ red bell pepper, seeded and diced fine
2 tbsps chopped fresh cilantro
¼ cup sliced pickled jalapenos, chopped fine
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise and lime juice in a small bowl until combined.  Toss chicken, celery, onion, pepper, cilantro, and jalapenos in a large bowl.  Add mayonnaise mixture and toss to coat evenly.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.  Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Glenn
Description: 
This is a decepeptively simple but delicious salad.  Although there are only 8 ingredients, the flavor is decidely rich and satisfying.  Be sure to let this salad chill for at least 2 hours so the flavors can blend properly. Try this with tuna too!
