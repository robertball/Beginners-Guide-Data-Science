Title:
Trishie's Gang-Up Breakfast Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507415.jpg
Ingredients:
I small potato thinly sliced
2 T flour
2 T butter divided
1/2 small apple chopped
2/3 C ham chopped
1/8 t cinnamon
1 T onion finely chopped
1 large egg
2 t milk
1/4 C hollandaise sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200*C
Dip the slices of potato in flour to coat fully.
Melt 1T butter in a hot pan and fry the potatoes on both side till golden and crispy and remove to a plate to cool.
Melt 1T butter in a pan and add the ham and apples and sprinkle over the cinnamon. Cook till apples are soft.
Line the bottom of a small loaf tin (or ramekin)with a layer of the crispy potatoes.
Next add a layer of half of the ham & apple mix. 7. Repeat with another layer of potatoes then the remaining apple & ham mix.
Cover with the chopped onion.
Finish off with a final layer of potatoes.
In a small bowl whisk up the egg with the milk and pour into the loaf tin (or ramekin)
Place in the oven on a baking tray (in case it spills over the top!) and bake for 25 minutes.
Serve with a 1/4 C of Hollandaise Sauce!
