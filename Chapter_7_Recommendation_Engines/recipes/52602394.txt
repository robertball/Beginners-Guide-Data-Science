Title:
Mexican Rice II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 cup uncooked long-grain rice
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup finely chopped green and/or red pepper
1/2 cup tomato sauce
2 cups chicken broth
Optional: 1-1/2 tsp taco seasoning

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat and add rice.  Cook, stirring constantly, until puffed and golden.  While rice is cooking, sprinkle with cumin.
Stir in onions, minced peppers and garlic and cook until tender.  (Optional: Add taco seasoning) Stir in tomato sauce and chicken broth; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.  Fluff with a fork.
