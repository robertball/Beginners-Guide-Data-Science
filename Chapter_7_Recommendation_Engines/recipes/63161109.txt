Title:
Easy but Delicious Homemade Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 pkg. (3.4 ozs.) instant cheesecake, vanilla or other flavor instant pudding mix
4 cups heavy whipping cream
2 cups milk
2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together all ingredients. Chill well. Freeze in ice cream freezer according to manufacturers instructions. Refrigerate remaining mixture until ready to freeze. Whisk before pouring into ice cream freezer, mixture will have some lumps. Freeze.

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
This is a simple, but delicious ice cream. I like it made with cheesecake flavored instant pudding.
