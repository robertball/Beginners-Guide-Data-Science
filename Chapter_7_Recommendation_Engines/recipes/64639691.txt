Title:
On Luck's Chicken Curry Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4510906.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breast, cut into small cubes
1 Green Pepper, sliced into strips
1 Medium Onion, sliced into strips
2 tablespoon Sesame Oil
1 tablespoon minced Ginger Root
1 teaspoon minced Garlic
1 tablespoon of Curry Powder
½ cup Chicken Broth, divided

Directions:
Heat large skillet over medium-high heat, and add ½ oil. Saute garlic and ginger root for about 5 seconds, then add vegetables, and cook until they are lightly tender, and then set aside.
Heat skillet over medium-high heat, and add the other ½ oil. Add chicken and begun to brown and cook until they are lightly tender, and then add vegetables back into skillet.
Add ¼ Chicken Broth and cover.
Mix Curry Powder and ¼ Chicken Broth, and add corn starch to thicken. Then add it to the skillet and stir the ingredients together and serve on a bed of rice.

Description: 
This dish is typically what are the ingredients you get from a restaurant. I've been know to add shiitake mushrooms, but you have to be careful to not overuse them. Chicken, curry, and onions go together and add more curry if you like it stronger.
