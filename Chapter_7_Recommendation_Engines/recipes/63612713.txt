Title:
Asparagus Ricotta Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz Ricotta Cheese
4 Large Eggs
1/4 Cup Freshly Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 Cup Milk
3 Tbsp. Snipped Chives
1/4 Tsp Salt
1/4 Tsp Pepper
1 Bunch Asparagus

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a medium bowl, (except the Aparagus) put into a 10 inch non-stick oven-safe skillet, top with pencil-thin asparagus spears, trimmed to fit and bake in 375F oven for 40 min.
