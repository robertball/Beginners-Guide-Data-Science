Title:
Chocolate Pudding Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. White sugar
3 TBLS. Corn Starch
1/3 C. Unsweetened cocoa powder
2-1/2 C. Milk
1 tsp. Vanilla extract

Directions:
Put dry ingredients in saucepan. Turn heat to medium and add milk, stirring to combine with dry ingredients. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until pudding begins to thicken. Remove from heat and add vanilla. Let pudding cool, refrigerate before serving.

Submitter: 
Lisa Garnier
Description: 
Mix up dry ingredients only to use in place of instant pudding when called for in a recipe.
