Title:
Rice Porridge with Orange, Pistachio & Mint Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup short-grain rice (such as arborio)
1 cup coconut or almond milk
1 1/2 cups water
Juice and zest of 1 orange
1 Tbsp. almond butter
1 Tbsp. pistachios, shelled
1 Tbsp. fresh mint leaves, finely chopped

Directions:
Rinse rice well in cold water before placing it in a large saucepan.
Add milk, water, orange juice and zest and cook over medium-low heat, stirring often until liquid is absorbed and rice is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
Remove from heat and stir in almond butter.
Serve topped with the pistachios, mint leaves, and extra coconut milk, if desired.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This porridge keeps well for a few days in the fridge.
