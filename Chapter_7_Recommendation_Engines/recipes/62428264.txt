Title:
Secret Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Juice from 1 fresh lime
1/2 teaspoon tequila
1/4 cup fresh parsley
1/4 cup fresh cilantro
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 teaspoons salt
6 large tomatoes, chopped
1 small onion, diced
1 bell pepper (green), diced

Directions:
In a medium size bowl mix lime juice, tequila, fresh herbs, salt, and spices.
Set aside while preparing vegetables.
Add vegetables and toss to coat.
Refrigerate until ready to serve or use in recipes calling for salsa.

Description: 
Sweet and spicy pico de gallo style salsa with my secret ingredient !
