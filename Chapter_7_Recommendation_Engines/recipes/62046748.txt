Title:
Apple Pie Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon seltzer water
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 medium red apple, chopped
1/4 cup pecans, chopped

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and seltzer water.  Stir in cinnamon and brown sugar. Fold in apples and pecans.  Serve with graham crackers or Ritz crackers.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
If you like apple pie, you will enjoy this spread.  It is great on graham crackers or ritz crackers.  I make this every New Years Eve.
