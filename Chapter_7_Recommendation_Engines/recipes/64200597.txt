Title:
Wild Rice with Fruit and Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups wild rice, rinsed
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup chopped dried apricots
1/2 cup almond slivers, toasted
5 cups chicken broth
1 cup orange juice
2 tbsp butter, melted
1 tsp cumin
2 green onions, thinly sliced
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine rice,dried fruit, and almonds in a slow cooker.
In a medium bowl, combine juice, butter, broth, and cumin.  Pour over rice mixture.
Cover and cook on low 7 hours or high 2 1/2 to 3 hours.  Stir once, adding more broth as necessary.
When rice is tender, stir in green onions and parsley.  Season with salt and pepper, and cook an additional 10 minutes.
