Title:
Chicago Style Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-5 pounds beef chuck roast
1 (1 ounce) package dry Italian salad dressing mix
1 package of AuJus mix
16oz jar of Banana pepper rings, drained
2 cups water

Directions:
Place the roast into a slow cooker. Sprinkle both packets of dry mixes over the meat. Spread the pepper rings next over the spice mixture. Pour water over all and cover. Slow cook for 6 hours. Remove meat from juices, shred and return to the gravy. Serve on crusty rolls with provolone cheese. Lovely!

Submitter: 
Mary McCarthy Maslowski
Description:
