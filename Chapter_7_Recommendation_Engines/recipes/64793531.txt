Title:
Rich's Rockin Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBL brown sugar
1 1/2 TBL  paprika
1 TBL salt
2 tea black pepper
1 tea garlic powder
1 tea onion powder
1 tea mustard powder
1 tea cayenne (more or less based on desired level of heat)

Directions:
1. Mix all spices together.  Rub at thick layer of rub into pork on all sides.  Optional:  You can rub pork with mustard before applying rub to ensure good adhesion.
Optional:  Rub can be applied early, wrap meat in saran wrap, and refrigerated to "marinate" (2 hours to overnight).

Great rub for ribs and pork butt
