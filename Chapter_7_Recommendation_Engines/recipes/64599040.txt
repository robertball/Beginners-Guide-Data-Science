Title:
Parmesan Crusted Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 4 oz tilapia filets
1/4 cup Parmesan
3 T butter
2 tsp lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Salt and pepper both sides of tilapia
Combine in a bowl cheese, butter, lemon juice, salt and pepper to taste, may need to put into microwave to soften butter.
put tilapia on cookie sheet sprayed with Pam, cover the top of tilapia with the cheese mixture and broil until it is golden brown. serve hot.
