Title:
Beachcomber Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978590.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
1/4 ounce Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ Coconut
1/2 ounce pineapple juice
1 tablespoon orange juice
2 slices orange

Directions:
Fill a glass with ice.
Add Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka, Captain Morgan Parrot Bay(TM) Coconut, pineapple juice, orange juice, and stir well.
Garnish with orange slices.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Clean and ultrasmooth Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka gives this cocktail a clear, pure taste unmuddied by impurities or harshness.
