Title:
Pretzel Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 Cup Crushed Pretzels
1/4 Cup Sugar
3/4 Cups Melted Butter
12 oz. Cream Cheese
1 1/2 Cup Powdered Sugar
1 Small Container Cool Whip
6 oz. Package Strawberry Jello
2 Cups Water
1 1/2 Cup Sliced Strawberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix pretzels, sugar, and butter
Spread in 9X13 pan
Bake 350 degrees for 10 minutes
Allow to cool
Mix cream cheese and powdered sugar well
Fold into cool whip
Spread over cooled crust
Prepare jello using 2 cups water
Chill until slightly syrupy
Pour jello over cream cheese layer
Add sliced strawberries to top
Chill

Submitter: 
Jenni Rose
Description: 
This is a favorite of my family for years.  Every time someone tries it they always take a 2nd or 3rd helping!  It's a perfect balance of sweet and salty.
