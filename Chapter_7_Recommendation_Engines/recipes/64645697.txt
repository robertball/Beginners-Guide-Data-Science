Title:
chicken n rice casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken breasts
2 cups rice
small pkg frozen peas
1/2onions
1 bulb garlic
1/2 c mushrooms
1 cup salsa mix
1/4 c up chicken boullion
2 cups water

Directions:
2 cups spread out in pan
8 chicken breasts
1/2 onion  cut up and spread around chicken
chicken boullion over top of chicken pieces
mushrooms on top of chicken
salsa over top of chicken
peas around the chicken
pour water in pan
cover with aluminum foil
cook at 350 degrees
may add additional water as needed until all is done
