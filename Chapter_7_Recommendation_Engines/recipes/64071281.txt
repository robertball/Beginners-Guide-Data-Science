Title:
Yorkshire Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced cooked turkey
2 eggs
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup milk
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
Leftover turkey gravy or canned gravy

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray shallow 1 1/2 quart baking dish with Pam.
Put turkey in dish and bake while preparing remaining ingredients.
Beat eggs, butter and milk together. Add next 3 ingredients and beat until smooth. Pour over turkey and bake.
