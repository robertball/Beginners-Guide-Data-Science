Title:
Tamale Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 (16 oz.) can of corn, drained
1-2 (16 oz.) cans tomato sauce
1 (16 oz.) can pitted black olives, drained
2-3 "XLNT" tamales, sliced
Optional - grated Cheddar cheese for top

Directions:
Brown ground beef and onion; drain.
In large casserole mix ground beef, onion and all other ingredients.
Top with grated cheese (optional).
Bake at 350 degrees for 20-30 minutes.
