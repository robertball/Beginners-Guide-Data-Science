Title:
Olive Garden Eggplant Parmigiana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggplants, peeled, sliced 1/4in circles
flour, oil and seasoning salt
1# jar meat-flavored Prego
1/4 C grape jelly
14oz can sliced stewed tomatoes,broken with fork
1/2 # shredder mazzarella cheese
1/4C grated parmesan chesse

Directions:
Moisten eggplant with milk and coat lightly with flour. Quickly brown slices in hot oil, dusting each side generously with seasoned salt. When fork tender and golden brown transfer to 9 x 13 pan. Cover loosely with foil and bake 375 x 20-25 min. or until tender.
SAUCE: Combine sauce, jelly and tomatoes that have been broken up with fork. Heat on medium until hot but do not boil. Spread mozzarella cheese over eggplant, then add sauce. Top with parmesan cheese and return to oven for 5-10 min to melt cheeses. Serve immed.
