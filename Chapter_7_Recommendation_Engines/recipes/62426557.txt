Title:
Pasta Pomodoro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages angel hair pasta
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 CUP SHALLOTS, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
2-1/2 cups roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/2 cuplow-sodium chicken broth
1/2 cup white wine
1 lb shrimp
crushed red pepper to taste
freshly ground black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8  minutes or until al dente; drain.
Pour olive oil in a large deep skillet over high-heat. Saute shallots and garlic until lightly browned. Reduce heat to medium-high and add tomatoes, vinegar wine, and chicken broth; simmer for about 8 minutes.
Stir in shrimp,red pepper, black pepper, basil and cooked pasta, tossing thoroughly with sauce. Simmer for about 5 more minutes, until shrimp is cooked and serve topped with grated cheese.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Easy and light pasta with tomatoes and garlic. Cooked chicken breast chunks or shrimp can also be added to sauce for a great main dish!Modified from: Pasta Pomodoro
