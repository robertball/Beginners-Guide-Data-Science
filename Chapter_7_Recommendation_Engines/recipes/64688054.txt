Title:
Salami, Mozzarella, Basil & tomato skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Bamboo Skewers
24 Cherry tomatoes
24 basil leaves
12 0z. salami, cut in 1" cube
12 ounces smoked mozzarella, cut in 1" cubes
EVO
Fresh cracked pepper

Directions:
Soak skewers in water for 30 minutes, Preheat grill or broiler on low heat
Wrap each in a basil leaf
On each skewer, thread a cube of salami, acube of mozzarella and a basil wrapped tomato. Repeat with additional salami,   mozzarella and tomato.
Brush with olive oil
Place the skewers on a baking sheet . Broil 4-5 inches from heat about  minute or until just warm. ( if grilling, place them on the grill over low heat) for 1-2 minutes, turning once. Watch skewers carefully so that the cheese softens a little but does not melt
