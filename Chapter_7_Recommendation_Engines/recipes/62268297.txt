Title:
Shawn's Favorite Chocolate Cherry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
2 teaspoons almond extract
2 eggs
1 stick butter
4 heaping tablespoons cocoa
6 Tablespoons milk
3 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 9 x 13 inch pan.
In a large bowl, combine cake mix, cherry pie filling, almond extract, and 2 eggs by hand; stir until well blended.  Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until it tests done.  Cool.
In a medium saucepan, combine cocoa, butter, and  milk. Cook until boiling, then remove from heat. Add powdered sugar, beating until smooth.  Immediately pour and spread over cake.
