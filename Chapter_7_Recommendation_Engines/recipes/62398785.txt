Title:
Mama B's Best Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bags of Pasta (12-16oz.) Fusilli, Bowties or a Mix
1/2 cup of diced pepperoni
1 pound of diced Extra Sharp Cheddar or other Hard Cheese
4-6 medium peppers (green, red or yellow)
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes halved
DRESSING:
2 cups of Oil
1-1/2 cups Lemon Juice
5 cloves of garlic minced
1/3 cup minced fresh basil or 2 Tbsp Dried Basil
1 Heaping Tbsp. Salt
1Tbsp fresh Oregano or 1tsp. dried Oregano
1/2 tsp Freshly ground Pepper
1/4 tsp Cayenne Pepper

Directions:
Dressing-
In a large jar with a tight lid combine all of the dressing ingredients and shake well (shake prior to each use) Pour 2/3 of the dressing over the pasta salad. Stir Well to coat. Prior to serving stir and add more dressing as needed. (dressing can be made up to 4 days ahead of time)
Prepare Pasta according to package directions; rinse in cold water and drain. In a very large bowl combine cooked pasta, diced peppers, tomatoes, cheese and pepperoni.

Submitter: 
Lauren Barkanic
Description: 
If you are looking for a flavorful Pasta Salad, and not just a box of pasta and bottle of Italian than this is for you. This salad is perfect for parties and special occasions. You will be proud to show up with this!
