Title:
Southwest Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C cooked rice, chilled
2 C cooked chicken, shredded or cubed
1 bunch green onions, sliced
1 lg. tomato, chopped
2 T cilantro, minced
1 avocado, diced
1 jalapeno, minced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 t salt
3 T lime juice
1 T lime zest
1/3 C canola or olive oil
1 t oregano
1/2 t cumin
1/2 t chili powder
1 bunch letuce, cut or torn up

Directions:
Combine rice, chicken, green onions, tomato, cilantro, and avocado.
Whisk jalapeno, garlic, salt, lime juice and zest, oil, and seasonings in small bowl.
Add dressing to salad and toss to coat. Chill 1 hour. Serve over lettuce.

Submitter: 
Heidi
Description: 
I got this wonderful recipe from a friend several years ago. It's a great main dish salad for hot weather. I usually double it for my family so we can eat the leftovers for lunch the next day.
