Title:
White Chicken Chili with Jalepenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs cooked chicken (bone in - skin on, rub with oil, salt pepper in the oven for 30 mins at 375, let cool, take off skin and shred)
4 - 15 oz cans Great Northern Beans (not drained) these are white beans
1Tablespoon olive oil
4 cloves garlic minced
1/2 cup dry sherry or dry white wine
1 medium onion chopped
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 teaspoon ground oregano
2 teaspoon ground cumin
2 4oz cans chopped mild green Chiles
4 jalepenos seeded and diced
4 cans chicken stock or broth
20 oz Monterey Jack Cheese
Sour cream
Chopped Jalepeno Peppers

Directions:
In a pot, heat oil and saute onion for 10 mins.  Add sherry and reduce liquid to half ( or close to half) , 10-15 mins on simmer.  Add garlic, chilies, cumin, cayenne, crushed red peppers, diced jalepenos and oregano. Saute for 3 mins.  Add chicken, beans stock and 12 oz of cheese.  Let simmer one hour.   Top with chopped jalepenos, sour cream, cheese and avacado if you have!
Crock Pot instructions....once you saute everything, add all ingredients to crock pot and cook on low for 6 hours.
