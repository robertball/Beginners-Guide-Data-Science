Title:
Non-Dairy Sweetened Condensed Milk (Substitute) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large eggs, beaten
1 c. brown sugar, packed
1 t. pure vanilla extract
2 T. All-Purpose flour
1/2 t. baking powder
1/4 t. salt

Directions:
Blend eggs and brown sugar.  Add vanilla, flour and beat one minute.  Add baking powder and salt and beat one minute. This is better if used immediately.

Submitter: 
Shearone
Description: 
Recipe is from Joanne Fluke's Chocolate Cake Murder. Use to replace Sweetened Condensed Milk.  Contains no dairy or milk.
