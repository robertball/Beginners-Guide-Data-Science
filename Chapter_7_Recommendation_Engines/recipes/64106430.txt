Title:
Curried chicken vegetable chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken
1 can chicken broth (10.5oz)
2 cups water
1/2 tsp curry powder
1/8 tsp thyme
1/8 tsp pepper
2 cups cubed peeled potatoes
2 cups cubed cooked chicken
1 cup broccoli florets
1 cup fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup sweet red pepper cut in strips

Directions:
Stir soup until smooth. Gradually stir in broth, water, curry, thyme and pepper. Over medium heat, heat to boiling. Add potatoes. Boil 10 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients. Reduce heat to low. Cover; simmer 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
