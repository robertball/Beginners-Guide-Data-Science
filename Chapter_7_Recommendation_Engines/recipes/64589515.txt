Title:
Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups cocoa powder, unsweetened, not processed with alkali
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. Prepare cookie sheet with a light coating of cooking spray.
Add rinsed and drained beans to blender or food processor. Add up to 1/2 cup water and blend until beans are liquefied.
In a medium mixing bowl, add the cocoa powder. Mix until all ingredients are wet. Do not overmix. Cookies will be tough.
Scoop tablespoonfuls of dough onto baking sheet. Bake for 8-9 minutes.
