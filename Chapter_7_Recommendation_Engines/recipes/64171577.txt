Title:
Thrive - Stir  Fry Veggies with Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Tbs Balsamic Vinegar
1 Tbs Stevia
1 Tbs Coconut Oil
1 Tbs fresh ginger, minced
3 Cloves Garlic
1 lb. Beef Round Steak, cut into thin strips
1 Cup Broccoli, chopped
1 Red Bell Pepper, chopped
1/2 Cup Mushrooms, chopped

Directions:
In large skillet, heat oil.
All all ingredients and cook for 8 - 10 minutes, until softened to desired tenderness.
Serve over quinoa.

Submitter: 
Cyndi Martin McAlpine
