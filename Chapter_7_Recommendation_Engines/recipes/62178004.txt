Title:
Cucumber Tea Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 package dry Italian salad dressing mix, or 1/4 tsp. each garlic powder, oregano, basil, parsley and dill weed, and 1/8 cayenne
1 English cucumber, sliced thin but with substance
Dill weed for garnish

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, mayonnaise and spices. Refrigerate for several hours, or preferably overnight.
Spread the cream cheese blend onto slices of French bread or cocktail rye. Top with a cucumber slice. Sprinkle lightly with some finely chopped dill weed.

Submitter: 
Carol
Description: 
Great appetizer. These are always requested for the Ladies Teas at church.  They are so easy to make and the recipe easily adjusts to serve large groups without much more preparation time.Modified from: Cucumber Sandwiches III
