Title:
Oatmeal Cream Cheese Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup uncooked rolled oats
1 tablespoon soy sauce (optional)
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a bowl, mix the cream cheese, walnuts, onion, eggs, oats, and soy sauce. Form into patties.
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat, and cook the patties 2 minutes on each side, just until firm.
Place the patties in a baking dish. Pour in the cream of mushroom soup.
Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven.
