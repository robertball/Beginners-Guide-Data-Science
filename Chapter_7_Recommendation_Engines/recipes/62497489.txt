Title:
Tex Mex Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cans	Bean Dip
1 Cup	Sour Cream
½ Cup	Mayo
1 Pkg	Taco Seasoning
¼ tsp	Garlic Powder
3-4	Avocadoes, mashed
2 TBS	Lemon Juice
¼ tsp 	Pepper
Green onions, tomatoes, lettuce, olives    and     shredded cheese

Directions:
Spread bean dip in an 8-9” pan.  Mix sour cream, mayo, taco seasoning and garlic powder in a bowl.  Spread over bean dip.  Mix avocadoes, lemon juice and pepper.  Spread over sour cream mix.  Add veggies on top and then cheese.

Description: 
I make this taco dip every year for our guests during the annual rock festival where I live.  They can't get enough!
