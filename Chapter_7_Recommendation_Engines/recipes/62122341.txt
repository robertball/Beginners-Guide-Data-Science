Title:
Chicken Nugget Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Chicken nuggets precooked
1 1/2 spaghetti sauce
1 cup mozzarella cheese
1 tube of 6 crescent rolls

Directions:
*Precook nuggets as suggesteed
* Preheat oven to 350 degrees
*lay out the crescent rolls separately into to individual triangles
*Spoon 1Tlbps spaghetti sauce onto center of crescent dough
* place 1 nugget on each roll
*Spoon 1 more Tlbps sauce onto nugget
* sprinkle with cheese
* stretch dough around nugget, sauce and cheese, and pinch closed
* cook for 18-20 mins. according to the rolls directions
Pack 1 or 2 packages of goodness in lunchbox , serve with some baby carrots and ranch dressing.

Submitter: 
Robin Lee Starnes
Description: 
A quick fix that kid love. With only 4 simple ingredients.
