Title:
Acorn Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6384112.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C Acorn Flour
1 C All Purpose Flour (or Gluten Free Flour, which could be all acorn flour)
1/2 t Baking Soda
1/2 t Salt
2 Eggs
1 T Vanilla
1 C Sugar
1 C Brown Sugar
1 C Butter Softened
1 C Peanut Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Mix Flours, salt and baking soda in a small bowl
In a large mixing bowl cream together the softened butter and peanut butter
Add the vanilla, sugars and eggs
Mix well making sure that mixture is smooth without any lumps
Stir the flour mixture into the butter mixture until smooth
Roll dough into small to medium balls, arrange on cookie sheet with parchment paper and press each ball down with a fork
Leave room for the cookies to spread wider
Bake for 10 minutes
Allow to cool
Serve
