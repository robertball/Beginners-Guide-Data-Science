Title:
Margherita Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 8 inch flatbreads or whole round pita breads
4 tsbp. Extra Virgin olive oil
2 garlic cloves, pressed
1/2 c chopped fresh basil
2 vine ripe tomatoes,  sliced 1/4 inch thick
12 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced 1/4 inch thick

Directions:
Preheat oven and pizza pan to 450 degrees.
While oven is preheating, add garlic to olive oil and stir.
Place flatbreads on the preheated pan and brush with garlic and olive oil.
Bake for 3-4 minutes, being careful to not burn the garlic.
Brush crust to redistribute olive oil if necessary.
Add basil, then tomatoes.  Top with cheese.
Return to oven for 4 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sara
Description: 
Top quality ingredients are important to a tasty result.  Serve with a spring green mix salad or alone as an appetizer.
