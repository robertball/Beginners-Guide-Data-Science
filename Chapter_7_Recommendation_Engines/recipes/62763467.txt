Title:
T Bird's Beef Jerky (Wendie's dehydrator recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound thin-sliced round steak, lean
3-4 grinds from mill black pepper
1 teaspoons garlic powder
3 shakes each basil and oregano
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup soy sauce
4 dashes Tabasco (red)
Canola oil spray

Directions:
Trim any fat or silver skin from sides of beef. Cut meat into 1" strips, no thicker than 1/4-1/2". In a large glass bowl, add all ingredients. Place meat slices into the bowl, and combine with marinade. Cover, and refrigerate 24 hours. Minimum 4 hours, 24 hours is preferred.
Using dehydrator: spray rack lightly with Canola oil.
Place meat strips on oiled rack in the middle of the dehydrator.
Suggestion: Place aluminum foil on bottom of dehydrator to catch any drips, splatters or meat pieces.
Only switch on the power knob after food is on prepared drying shelves and front panel has been replaced.
Adjust control knob to 150F for jerky,  per Gander instruction book
Dry to desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Wendie
Description: 
T Bird's Beef Jerky edited to our needs, using thinly sliced round steak. Round steak is often available on sale in small packages; desirable to a dehydrator beginner. T's is a fantastic oven recipe.Modified from: T Bird's Beef Jerky
