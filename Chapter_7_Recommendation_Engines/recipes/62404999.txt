Title:
Tomatoes, Basil, Garlic, and Brie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/490856.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large tomatoes, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 c. basil, cut into bite-sized pieces
5 oz. Brie Cheese, rind removed, torn into bite-size pieces
1 c. Olive oil
1 tbsp. salt
1/2 tbsp. pepper
24 oz. Pasta, Linguini #17 (1-1/2 packages)
1 c. Parmesan, Grated
Mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, brie, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Stir well and cover with plastic wrap.
Let sit at room temperature for at least two hours.
Serve over cooked linquini, also cooled to room temperature.
Sprinkle with freshly grated parmesan.

Directions:
Mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, brie, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Stir well and cover with plastic wrap.
Let sit at room temperature for at least two hours.
Serve over cooked, drained linguini.
Sprinkle with freshly grated parmesan.

Submitter: 
ellenmoriah
Description: 
A great summer dish, served at room temperature. Perfect for buffets or when you're not sure when your guests will arrive.

This is slightly modified from a recipe in the Silver Palate cookbook. The modification cut the brie in half (actually, more than half).
