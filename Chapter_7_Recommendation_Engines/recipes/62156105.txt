Title:
ham and cheese ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-8oz package of cream cheese
1/4cup mayo
1-8oz can flaked ham
2Tbsp dried parsley
1/2cup grated cheddar cheese(or havarti, montery Jack or mixture)
1/4cup chopped white onion
1/4tspdry mustard
1/4tsp tobasco sauce

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and mayo until smooth.
with a fork, mix in flaked ham.
Add parsley, cheese, onion, mustard, and tobasco sauce.
Mix well.
Line a bowl with plastic wrap, then add mixture, pressing down.
cover, and place in fridge til ready to eat.
when serving, flip bowl over onto serving tray, and remove plastic. serve with crackers.
