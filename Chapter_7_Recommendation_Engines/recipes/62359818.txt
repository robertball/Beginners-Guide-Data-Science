Title:
Tracy Ann's Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/592556.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups All Purpose flour
3 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp sugar
1/2 cup margarine
1 Tbsp shortening
2/3 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.  Cut butter and shortening into flour mixture.  Add buttermilk and mix.
Roll into 10 balls and place in a greased pie pan. Bake at 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
An easy buttermilk biscuit recipe. These are perfect with sausage gravy.Modified from: Buttered Biscuits
