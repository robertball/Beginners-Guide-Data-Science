Title:
Cheesy Ground Beef Comfort Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, diced
1 TBLSP oil
4 pounds ground beef (can substitute with ground turkey)
2 28 oz cans diced tomatoes, do not drain
1 29 oz can tomato sauce
3 15.25 oz cans of corn, drained
2 7 oz cans mushrooms, chopped if serving to small children
1 envelope beef onion soup
garlic, salt and pepper to taste
16 oz processed cheese, such as Velveeta
8 cups cooked rice of your choice (can use pasta instead)

Directions:
In large pot, heat oil, add diced onion and cook until soft.
Add ground meat, cooking until no longer pink. Drain if desired.
Add both cans of diced tomatoes, can of tomato sauce, cans of corn and mushrooms.
Stir in the envelope of beef onion soup.
Add garlic, salt and pepper to taste.
Cover and let simmer on medium for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
While soup is heating through, dice the processed cheese.
When soup is heated through, turn heat to low (off if an electric stove). Slowly add the cheese, stirring constantly until melted and the soup becomes creamy. You may add more cheese, if desired.
Serve in bowls over cooked rice or pasta.

Submitter: 
KelleyBee
Description: 
This is a recipe I've created for my large family to enjoy on cold winter nights. All my children (and hubby, too!) love it! It features ground beef, corn, cheese and rice. Enjoy!
