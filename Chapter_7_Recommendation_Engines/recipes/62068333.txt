Title:
Chicken Fried Steak with Cream Pork Sausage Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon white pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
3 whole eggs, beaten
1 clove garlic, chopped fine
1 tablespoon fresh chopped parsley
4 cups vegetable oil
2 4-oz pieces filet mignon, pounded thin
CREAM GRAVY INGREDIENTS
3 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons flour
2 1/2 cups heavy cream or milk
2 pork sausages fried in pan until almost burnt
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine in bowl, flour, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
In another bowl mix eggs, chopped garlic and parsley.  Heat oil in skillet.  Place each piece of meat in flour mixture, then in egg mixture, then back in flour mixture.  Place in skillet.
Cook about 3 minutes, then flip.  Cook about 3 more minutes until golden brown.  Place on paper towel to remove some of the oil.  Place each steak on plate and top with gravy.
CREAM & PORK SAUSAGE GRAVY DIRECTIONS:
In small skillet fry sausages until almost burnt. Rip or cut sausages into very tiny pieces and set aside.
Heat butter until it sizzles.  Add flour.  Cook stiring constantly about 1 minute.   Add cream, salt and pepper and bring to boil.  Add pork sausage pieces.  Simmer until thickened. Makes about 2 1/2 cups.

Submitter:

Description: 
My grandmother use to make this for my grandfather.  She passed the recipe down to my mom who use to make this for our family, but especially for my father who absolutely LOVED this!  I now make this for my husband!  I serve this with buttered corn and smashed garlic potatoes!!!
