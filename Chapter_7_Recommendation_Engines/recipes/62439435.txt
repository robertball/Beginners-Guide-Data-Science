Title:
Roasted Garlic Rosemary Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower, cut into 1-inch florets
2 1/2 teaspoons olive or canola oil
2 teaspoons minced fresh rosemary
2 chopped cloves of garlic

Directions:
In a bowl, toss cauliflower with oil, rosemary and garlic until well coated. Arrange in a single layer in an ungreased glass baking dish. Roast, uncovered, at 450 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until cauliflower is lightly browned and tender, stirring occasionally.
