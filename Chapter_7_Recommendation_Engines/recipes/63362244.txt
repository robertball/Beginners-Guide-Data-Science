Title:
Ham and Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packs of cream cheese, room temperature.
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese.
9 ounces of thin sliced ham, diced.
1.5 teaspoons of Worcestershire sauce.
1 teaspoon garlic powder.
Cajun seasoning to taste (such as Tony Chachere's).
1 teaspoon smoked paprika

Directions:
Place all ingredients into a bowl and blend fully. Shape mixture into a ball.
Sprinkle paprika on top.
Place in refrigerator until chilled through (about an hour or so).

Submitter: 
Samantha
Description: 
A flavorful cheese ball that is great for holiday entertaining. Serve it with buttery crackers.
