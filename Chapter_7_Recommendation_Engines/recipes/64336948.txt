Title:
Grandma's Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box elbow noodles
Amount of mayonnaise you desire - I usually do a cup
Amount of cheddar cheese you desire-cut in small cubes- 8 oz
Amount of sweet pickles you desire – cut in small pieces - 1 1/2 cups
1 small onion – chopped very fine

Directions:
Cook noodles and drain. Run cold water over noodles and drain again until room temperature. Add everything together and mix.
