Title:
Sour Cream and Horseradish Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch asparagus
1 1/2 cups sour cream
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 1/2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
1 1/2 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs

Directions:
Trim hard ends of asparagus and steam until asparagus is crisp tender. Place asparagus into a 13 x 9 casserole dish in a single layer, side by side. Mix together sour cream, horseradish and garlic salt and spread over the asparagus.  Melt butter in the microwave until liquid and mix with the bread crumbs.  Sprinkle the bread crumb mixture over the asparagus.  Broil about 3 inches from the top broiler on high until brown.

Submitter: 
SHARONAIR
Description: 
This is a great sidedish that serves well with beef entrees.  It has sour cream, horseradish and garlic salt which combine to make a creamy sauce.
