Title:
Hula's Rich Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5047068.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz semisweet choc chips
1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
1/4 cup powdered sugar
2 tsp Vanilla
9" chocolate or regular graham cracker prepared crust

Directions:
Place half the cream in the top of a double boiler and heat until warm.
Add choc chips to the cream and stir often until the chocolate melts and you have a smooth consistency.
Cool to room temperature.
Stir in sugar and vanilla.
In a separate bowl, beat the remaining  cream until it is the consistency of whipped cream.
Gradually add the chocolate mix to the cream 1/3 at a time until well blended.
Spoon filling into the pie crust.
Refrigerate for 4 hours to set, for 8 hours for best taste.

Submitter:

Description: 
Delicious, rich, and quick to make. Very difficult to mess this one up!
