Title:
Foaming Iced Mocha Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups crushed ice
2 cups skim milk
4 Tbs unsweetened cocoa
2 Tbs instant coffee granulates
8 individual packets of Equal

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a blender.
Blend on high speed for one minute or until all ice is blended into small pieces.
Drink, Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Angie
Description: 
This is a guilt free chocolate coffee drink. It is quick and simple. You can't mess it up.
