Title:
Fried Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 apples - peeled, cored and sliced
1/4 cup butter
3 tablespoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
2 tablespoons cinnamon
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium-sized cast iron pan over medium heat. Lay the apple slices in the butter. Cook slowly, turning slices as they start to break down.
When they are soft on both sides, add the sugar, nutmeg and cinnamon....season with a pinch of salt.

Submitter: 
COLETTECOOKIE
Description: 
Apple slices fried in a pan! These are a good side with fried sausages and mashed potatoes. They would also be excellent served with vanilla ice cream!Modified from: Fried Apples
