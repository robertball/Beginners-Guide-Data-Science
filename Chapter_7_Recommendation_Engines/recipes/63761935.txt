Title:
Pappaw's Brunswick Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hens
6 lbs ground beef
4 lbs ground pork
10 lbs potatoes
5 lbs onions
4 bottles ketchup
1 large bottle Worcestershire sauce
1 large bottle mustard
2 green bell peppers
1 bottle hot sauce
1 lb butter
4 lemons, sliced
4 15.5 oz can corn
8 15.5 oz can tomatoes
sugar, salt, pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook hens and grind meat, save 2 gallons of stock and broth stock for stew.  Grind all ingredients except corn and lemons.  Add water to thin down to cook.  Cook slowly for 4 hours, stirring constantly to avoid burning.  Add lemon slices and corn the last 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
PiliPili
Description: 
My grandfather cooked this over a fire in the back yard in a large cast iron pot.
