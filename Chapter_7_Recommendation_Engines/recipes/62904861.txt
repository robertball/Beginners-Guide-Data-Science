Title:
Alfredo sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
5 tablespoons of unsalted butter
1 1/2 cup of grated parmesan
Salt and freshly ground pepper
Freshly grated nutmeg

Directions:
In a large saucepan over high heat, bring the cream and butter to a boil
Add 1 1/4 cups of the grated parmesan to the cream and butter mixture
Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 - 2 minutes, whisking until smooth
Add a pinch of nutmeg, and salt and pepper to taste
Remove from heat and pour over pasta of choice
Sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan on top of pasta

Submitter: 
Craig Carlson
Description: 
This recipe is simple, but great Alfredo is not to be messed with! The fresh nutmeg makes a huge difference in this recipe, so if that is available, I would highly recommend that!
