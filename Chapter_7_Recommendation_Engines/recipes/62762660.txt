Title:
Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onions, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 c olive oil
1/2 t ground ginger
1/2 t ground coriander seeds
1/4 t ground cloves
1/4 t pepper
2 c rice
3 c pureed tomatoes
2 t salt
1.5 c boiling water

Directions:
Saute onions and garlic in oil.
Add spices and rice and saute until rice is colored.
Add tomatoes, salt, and water. Stir once. Cover and simmer 25-45 minutes, depending on the type of rice, until liquid is absorbed.

Submitter: 
meghanmacrae
Description: 
Not Tex-Mex, this is still comfort food that pairs well with black beans and cantaloupe.
