Title:
White Bean Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685521.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1 18-20 ounce jar salsa verde
1 (16 ounce) can RoTel original diced tomato and green chilies
1 (11 ounce) can corn with red and green peppers
1 can corn drained
2 (15 ounce) can white beans drained
3-4 cooked chicken breasts shredded
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1tbs dried parsley flakes
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
garlic salt to taste
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
Saute onions and garlic in vegetable oil until translucent. Add all ingredients to slow cooker on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours, serve over tortilla chips and cheese
