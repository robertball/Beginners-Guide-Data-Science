Title:
Cheese Scalloped Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/756030.jpg
Ingredients:
12 medium carrots sliced into coins
1 small onion diced
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
1 t salt
1/4 t dry mustard
2 cups milk
1/8 t pepper
1/4 t celery salt
1/2 lb. chedder cheese shredded
3 cups buttered fresh bread crumbs

Directions:
Cook carrots in boiling salted water, covered until barely tender.
drain
Meanwhile in saucepan gently cook onion in butter for 2-3 minutes.
Stir in flour, salt, mustard, and milk.
Cook, stir until smooth.
Add pepper and celery salt.
In 2 Qt. casserole, arrange 1/3 of the carrots, 1/2 of the cheese and repeat with a second layer of carrots and the rest of the cheese.
End with last 1/3 of carrots
pour sauce slowly over caserole
top with buttered bread crumbs.  Refrigerate until ready to bake.  45 minutes before serving bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes

Submitter: 
Karen Dean Covington
Description: 
This is an amazing side dish that my mother always made for special occasions as far back as I can remember.  It has made a carrot lover out of many of my friends who claim they don't like cooked carrots!
