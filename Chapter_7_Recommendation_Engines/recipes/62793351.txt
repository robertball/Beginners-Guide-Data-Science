Title:
Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Sticks Butter
1 3/4 C Light Brown Sugar
3 Eggs
2 tsp Vanilla
1 1/2 C Flour
1 tsp Baking Powder
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes.  Add one egg at a time, incorporating each fully. Add Vanilla.  Stir in flour, baking powder and salt, 1/3 at a time.  Bake in a 13X9 greased and floured pan for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
PiliPili
Description: 
Just like a brownie without the chocolate.  Soft and chewy.
