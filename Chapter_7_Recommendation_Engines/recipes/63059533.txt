Title:
Dave's Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
Can of green chilies (4oz.)
Tortilla shells
White cheddar cheese or white Mexican cheese
Can of green chilies enchilada sauce

Directions:
Combine chicken with the cream cheese and green chilies
Fill tortilla shells with the chicken mixture.
Put in baking dish and top with cheese and enchilada sauce.
Bake until cheese has melted, about 20 min at 350 degrees

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
This is James's favorite food along with pizza.  It's a simple, easy to prepare dish using white cheeses and green enchilada sauce
