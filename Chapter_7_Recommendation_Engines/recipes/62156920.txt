Title:
Italian Meatball and Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz. bag of frozen meatballs [or homemade]
1 16 oz. bag of frozen tortellni
1 46 oz. can of tomatoe juice
1 14.5 oz can of diced tomatoes
1 14.5 oz can of beef broth
1 16 oz can of garbanzos beans [drained and rinsed]
1 16 oz can of kidney beans [drained and rinsed]
1 packet of dried onion soup mix
1 TBLSP. dried oregeno
1 TBSP.dried basil
1 6 oz. bag of baby spinach
1 small onion chopped
1 carrot diced
1 small zucchini shredded
1 stalk of celery diced
1/2 cup green peppers diced
3 cloves of garlic chopped
1/3 cup parmesan chesse [more for garnish]
3 TBSP. olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a dutch oven saute your onion, celery, green pepper, carrot, oregeno, basil, garlic in olive till translucent.
Add can of beef broth, simmer 5 to 10 minutes.
Add your tomatoe soup, simmer 5 to 10 minutes.
Add your dried onion soup mix, diced tomatoes, garbonzo beans, kidney beans, zucchini, and spinach simmer 5 to 10 minutes.
Add meatballs and tortellini, bring to a boil.
Add parmesan cheese, stir.
Serve hot with crusty bread.

Submitter: 
Trish Beier
Description: 
A very easy and hearty Italian soup. Great with crusty bread.
