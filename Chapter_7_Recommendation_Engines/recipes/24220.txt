Title:
Sex on the Beach II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/668565.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vodka
1/2 fluid ounce melon liqueur
1/2 fluid ounce raspberry flavored liqueur
1 1/2 fluid ounces pineapple juice
1 1/2 fluid ounces cranberry juice

Directions:
In a cocktail mixer full of ice, combine vodka, melon liqueur, raspberry liqueur, pineapple juice and cranberry juice. Shake vigorously and strain into glass.
