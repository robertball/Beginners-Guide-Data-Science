Title:
Jerk Pork Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb pork loin cut into bite sized pieces
1/2 C vegetable oil
1/3 C fresh lime juice
2 TBS cider vinegar
2 tsp dried, crushed thyme
1 TBS brown sugar
1 TBS ground allspice
1 tsp ground nutmeg
2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/2 C chopped onions
2 minced scotch bonnets or jalapeno peppers
4 minced garlic cloves
6 10-inch tortillas

Directions:
Place pork in a 1 quart, self-sealing plastic bag.
Combine the remaining ingredients (except tortillas).
Mix until well blended and pour over pork.
Seal bag and marinate in refrigerator 8 to 12 hrs or overnight.
Drain pork, discard marinade, and place meat in a single layer in a shallow baking pan.
Roast at 350 for 35 to 45 minutes, until pork is done.
Divide pork pieces evenly in center of each tortilla.
Roll up tortillas.
