Title:
Roasted Parmesan Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Parmesan cheese,or 1/3 Cup Romano cheese, finely grated
1/2 Cup flour
1 Teaspoon salt
1/4 Teaspoon black pepper
8 large boiling potatoes, (about 4 lbs) peeled and quartered lenghtwise
1/2 Cup butter, melted (unsalted or reduce the salt in the recipe)or use 1/4 melted butter and 1/4 olive oil

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Spread the grated Parmesan cheese on parchment or wax paper and let dry for about 1 hour.
Place the dried Parmesan cheese, flour, salt & pepper in a plastic sealable bag and shake to blend.
Rinse the potatoes and drain in a colander.
In two batches, drop the potatoes in the bag and shake to coat. Make sure the potatoes are well coated.
Pour the melted butter into a shallow baking pan and arrange the potatoes in a single layer.
Bake in center or lower third of the oven for 1 1/2 hours. Turn the potatoes twice while baking. Bake until browned and crisp.
