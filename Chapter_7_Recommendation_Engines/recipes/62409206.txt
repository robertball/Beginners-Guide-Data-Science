Title:
Baked Pollack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. pollack
3/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup melted butter
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Place 1 1/2 lb. pollock fillets in greased baking pan.  A 9 x 13 pan works great.
In a bowl, mix sour cream, Parmesan cheese, melted butter, salt and pepper.
Spread mixture over the top of the fillets.
Bake uncovered 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Joan Gerland Crabtree
Description: 
An easy and delicious fish recipe I recieved from a friend.  Tastes like it should be served in a good seafood resturant.  My family loves it and it is so easy to prepare and great for company.
