Title:
Pam's Chicken Breast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 8oz chicken breasts, skinned and deboned
1 pkg smoked dried beef
4 - 8 slices bacon
1 c sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
4 oz can sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Line baking dish with dried beef.
Place chicken breasts on top.
Wrap chicken breast with bacon.
Mix soup and sour cream together and pour over everything.
Bake 2 1/2 hours at 300.
This part can be done ahead of time. Then continue for 45 minutes.
Do not cover.
