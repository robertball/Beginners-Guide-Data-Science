Title:
Tamale Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbls. shortening
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove minced garlic
1 can creamed corm
1 can tomatoes
3 tsp. salt
1 Tbl. chili powder
1 1/4 cup yellow cornmeal
2 cups tomato juice
1 can pitted olives and juice

Directions:
Using a large frying pan melt shortening. Add ground beef onions and garlic. Cook until slightly browned.
Add corn, tomatoes, salt and chili powder.
Simmer 20 minutes.
Add cornmeal, stir constantly until well blended.
Add tomato juice and olives with juice.
Pour into greased baking dish and bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Pamela Euless-Barker
Description: 
This is one of those recipes that everyone loves. Serve it with corn bread.
