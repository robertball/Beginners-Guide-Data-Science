Title:
Shrimp and Okra Gumbo - Modified by Al Redding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
1/2 C butter
1/2 C flour
2 pounds chopped okra
1 tablespoon tomato paste
1 14 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 Tbs Cajun seasoning
5 cups water
Sea salt & ground pepper to taste
1/2 cup chopped green onions (garnish)
White rice

Directions:
Season the shrimp with salt, pepper and cajun seasoning to taste and set aside. Heat the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add flour and make roux to a light brown color. Add the okra and saute for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the tomato paste, tomatoes, onion, garlic, celery and green bell pepper and saute for 10 more minutes.
Add the water and seasonings to taste. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer for 45 minutes. Add the shrimp and simmer for 5 minutes before serving over a scoop of rice in the bowl. Finally, add the green onion garnish before serving.

Submitter: 
Al Redding Sr.
Description: 
Okra, shrimp, and a variety of sauteed vegetables are used to create a thick stew, even without the dark roux found in traditional gumbo recipes.Modified from: Shrimp and Okra Gumbo
