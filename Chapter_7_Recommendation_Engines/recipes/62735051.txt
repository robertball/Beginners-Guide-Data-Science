Title:
OREO Blitz Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
1 1/3 cup oreo  baking crumbs
1/3 cup melted butter
FILLING:
8 squares semi-sweet chocolate
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1 Tbsp.hot coffee
2 eggs,separated
3/4 cup icing sugar
500 ml.oreo vanilla ice cream
slightly softened
TOPPING:
4 squares semi-sweet chocolate
3 Tbsp.butter
1 Tbsp.corn syrup

Directions:
For crust-mix together oreo baking crumbs with melted butter and press into 9 inch springform pan.Set aside.Melt chocolate squares with butter in a double boiler over simmering water-make sure bottom of bowl does not touch the water,stir till smooth.Remove from heat.In a medium bowl,beat egg yolks,with coffee and icing sugar until fluffy.Pour in melted chocolate,mix well.In a small bowl,beat egg whites until stiff and fold into chocolate mixture.Pour over crust and freeze[about 2 hours]Spread ice cream evenly over chocolate layer.Freeze until firm and unmold.For topping melt chocolate squares with butter and corn syrup,then pour over the top and down the sides of the torte.Freeze for several hours or overnight.Garnish with mini oreo cookies and whipping cream if desired.
