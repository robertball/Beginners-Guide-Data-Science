Title:
Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (4-6 pound) pork butt
2 (12 fluid ounce) cans or bottles root beer
1 (18 ounce) bottle your favorite barbecue sauce
8 hamburger buns, split and lightly toasted
French Fried onions

Directions:
Place the pork tenderloin in a slow cooker; pour the root beer over the meat to cover. Cover and cook on low until well cooked and the pork shreds easily,7-10 hours. Note: the actual length of time may vary according to individual slow cooker. Drain well. Stir in barbecue sauce and cook for 30 minutes more. Serve topped with fried onion rings on buns.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Using root beer to season slow cooked pork tenderloin makes a novel, yet tasty preparation method guaranteed to win applause.Modified from: Slow Cooker Pulled Pork
