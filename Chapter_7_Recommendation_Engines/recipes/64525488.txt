Title:
Creamy Caramels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup dark corn syrup
1 cup butter or margarine
1 14oz can sweetened condensed milk (Eagle Brand)
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
*Read thru recipe before starting!
Line a 8" x 8" pan with foil and spray foil with cooking spray.
Combine sugar, corn syrup and butter in a 3 qt. saucepan.
Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.
Boil slowly for 4 minutes without stirring.
Remove from heat and stir in milk.
Reduce heat to med-low and cook until candy thermometer reads 238 degrees (soft-ball stage), stirring constantly. (Med-low = large burner on approx. 2.5 x approx. 1/2 hr)
Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Pour into prepared pan.
Cool.
Remove from pan and cut into 1/2" - 1" squares.
Wrap individually in waxed paper; twist ends.
*Use a 3" x 4" paper pattern to cut waxed paper. Place 4" side of pattern along the long uncut edge of waxed paper roll. Snip paper to mark and make first fold from end. Repeat a few times, then cut section off. Cutting along folds. Then fold stack of strips into 1/4's and cut along fold lines so you end up with several pieces of 3" x 4" waxed paper! Repeat.
OR just figure out a way that works for you :)

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Delicious melt-in-your-mouth soft caramel candies. Made in a pan, cut and individually wrapped. *Make sure to read the recipe thru well before starting!
