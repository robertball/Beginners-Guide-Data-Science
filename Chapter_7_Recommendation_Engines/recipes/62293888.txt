Title:
Chocolate Sheetcake with Milk Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 c sugar
2 c flour
1 c (2 sticks) oleo or margarine
4T cocoa
1 c water
2 eggs
1/2 c buttermilk (or 1 1/2 tsp lemon juice with milk to equal 1/2 cup)
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
Frosting:
1 c sugar
5 T butter or margarine
1/3 c milk
6 oz milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Cake:
Combine 2 c sugar with 2 c flour in mixing bowl.
In a saucepan, combine margarine, cocoa, and water.  Stir and bring to a boil.  Pour over sugar/flour mixture.  Add 2 eggs, 1/2 c buttermilk, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp vanilla.  Mix well. Pour into cake pan.  Bake at 350F for approx 20 minutes.
Frosting:
In saucepan, combine sugar, butter, and milk.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, for one minutes.  Remove from heat.  Add 6 oz milk chocolate chips, stirring until melted.
