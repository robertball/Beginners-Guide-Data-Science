Title:
Roasted Caprese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 - 8 Roma Plum tomatoes
Olive oil
Balsamic Vinegar
salt and pepper
3 - 4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp sugar
Sliced fresh mozerella
Fresh Basil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275
Halve the tomatoes and remove seeds.  Place on parchment lined baking sheet cut side up.
Sprinkle the tomatoes with olive oil, balsamic, salt and pepper.
Mince garlic and sprinkle over the tomatoes
Sprinkle with sugar.
Roast at 275 for at least 2 hours.
Slice mozerella, alternate slices of tomatoes and cheese on a platter.
Cut basil and sprinkle on the tomatoes and cheese.  Add salt and pepper and a bit more olive oil.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
Slow roasted tomatoes bring an intense flavor to this amazing dish.  This recipe is adapted from Ina Garten's recipe - Roasted Tomato Caprese Salad
