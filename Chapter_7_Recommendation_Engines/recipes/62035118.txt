Title:
Pork Chop Goodie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops,3/4 t. salt, 1/8 t. pepper, 3/4 C. brown rice, 1 can mushroom soup, 1 14&1/2 oz. can chop suey vegetables, drained (save liquid)

Directions:
Brown chops slowly on both sides, seasoning with salt and pepper. Wash rice and combine it with drained chop suey vegetables, add chop suey liquid into measuring cup and add enough water to make 2&1/2 C.add to rice and soup mixture. Use 2 qt. casserole, Lay browned pork chops on top of rice, Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 20 minutes.
