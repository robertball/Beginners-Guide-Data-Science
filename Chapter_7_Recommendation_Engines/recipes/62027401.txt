Title:
ALL TIME FAVORITE LASAGNA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 CUPS RICOTTA CHEESE
3 CUPS SHREDDED MOZZARELLA CHEESE
2 EGGS
1 LB. GROUND BEEF
2 (28 OZ) JARS THICK SPAGHETTI SAUCE (I USE MEAT FLAVORED SAUCE)
12 LASAGNA NOODLES, COOKED AND DRAINED
1/2 CUP GRATED PARMESAN CHEESE

Directions:
IN A BOWL, MIX TOGETHER THE RICOTTA, MOZZARELLA, AND EGGS.  SET ASIDE.  IN A LARGE SKILLET, BROWN GROUND BEEF, STIRRING TO SEPARATE MEAT.  DRAIN.  STIR IN SPAGHETTI SAUCE.  IN EACH OF TWO 2-QT SHALLOW BAKING DISHES, LAYER 1 CUP SAUCE MIXTURE, 2 NOODLES, AND 1 CUP CHEESE MIXTURE.  REPEAT LAYERS.  TOP WITH REMAINING NOODLES, REMAINING SAUCE, AND PARMESAN.  BAKE AT 400 DEGREES FOR 30 MINUTES.  LET STAND FOR 10 MINUTES BEFORE SERVING.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell
Description: 
IT'S BECOME A TRADITION TO HAVE FRIENDS FROM WORK COME OVER TO ENJOY MY LASAGNA DINNER - THEY LEAVE WITH HAPPY TUMMIES!
INSTEAD OF THE TWO 2-QT CASSEROLES, YOU CAN MAKE IT IN A 9x13 PAN, BUT PAN WILL BE VERY FULL!
