Title:
Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs frozen hash browns
1 can low-fat cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup butter, melted
16 oz. sour cream
2 cups shredded cheese
1/2 lb bacon, chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Thaw hash browns
Chop bacon and fry until crispy. Drain.
In a large bowl, mix soup, butter, bacon and sour cream until combined.
Add cheese and hash browns and mix well.
Spread into a 9 x 13 pan that has been lightly coated with cooking spray.
Bake for 1 hour.
