Title:
Italian (Arborio) Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 cup arborio rice
1/4 tsp. salt
1 Tb. butter
3 cups half & half
1 cup sugar
1 cinnamon stick
1 to 2 - 1-1/2 inch pieces of lemon peel
ground cinnamon for garnish

Directions:
Bring water to a boil, add rice, salt, and butter.
Cover, lower heat to simmer and cook for 20 min.
Add half & half, sugar, cinnamon stick and lemon peel.
Bring to a boil.
Pour contents into top of double boiler and simmer for 45 min., stirring occasionally.
Remove cinnamon stick and lemon peel.
Pour into dish and let cool.
Sprinkle with cinnamon powder.

Submitter: 
Nick Van Laar
Description: 
A very rich, smooth and creamy rice pudding with a hint of lemon.
