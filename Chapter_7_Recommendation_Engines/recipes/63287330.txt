Title:
Judy's Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Self Rising Flour
2.5 Cups Sugar
2 TBSP Pumpkin Pie Spice
1 15oz Can Pumpkin
4 Eggs
2/3 Cup Cooking Oil
Topping:
2 TBSP Sugar
1 TBSP Cinnamon

Directions:
Mix together flour, sugar, and pumpkin pie spice in a medium sized bowl. Mix in pumpkin, eggs, and cooking oil. Sprinkle topping all over the muffins. Bake in 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Hope Clayton
Description: 
These muffins are so easy! One can of pumpkin makes 36 muffins. So inexpensive, and super yummy!
