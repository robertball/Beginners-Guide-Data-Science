Title:
Chicken, Stuffing, and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken breasts
1 cup on instant rice
1 box of  instant chicken flavored stuffing
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 package of dry onion soup mix
1 cup of milk
Butter or cooking spray to grease baking dish

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees. Lightly grease baking dish. Make Stuffing according to directions on box and set aside.In a seperate baking dish, pour 1 cup of instant rice to layer bottom of dish. Add cream of mushroom soup, dry onion soup mix, and milk to rice. Mix until creamy. Place chicken breasts on top of mixture. Cover each chicken breast with stuffing. Bake 1 hour.

Tender chicken breasts cooked over a creamy rice and mushroom sauce topped off with golden stuffing.
