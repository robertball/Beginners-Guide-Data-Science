Title:
Creamy  Old Fashion Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4554026.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2pounds round red or white potatoes (about 6 medium), peeled
1 1/2cups mayonnaise or salad dressing
1tablespoon white or cider vinegar
1tablespoon yellow mustard
1teaspoon salt
1/4teaspoon pepper
2medium stalks celery, chopped (1 cup)
1medium onion, chopped ( 1/2 cup)
4hard-cooked eggs, chopped
Paprika, if desired

Directions:
Cook potatoes till tender. When cool enough to handle, cube. Mix all other ingredients except egg and paprika and mix with potatoes. Add egg and sprinkle with paprika.
