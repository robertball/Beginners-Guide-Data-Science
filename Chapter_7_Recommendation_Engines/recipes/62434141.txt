Title:
Onion Soup Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dried minced onion
1 tsp. onion powder
4 cubes beef bouillon, crushed
1/4 tsp. celery seed

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and use as needed for recipes or dips. If not using right away, store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Anne Marie Sweden
Description: 
Ever needed a package of onion soup mix for a recipe and found you didn't have it? This is a great substitution recipe and makes about just about what you would get in one envelope of soup mix.
