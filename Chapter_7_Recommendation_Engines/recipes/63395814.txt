Title:
Classic Caramel Corn Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/912830.jpg
Ingredients:
4 quarts Of Popped Popcorn
1 cup Of Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup Of Corn Syrup
1/2 Cup Of butter
1/2 Teaspoon Of Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Of Baking soda
1 Teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
16 Oz Of Salted Spanish Peanuts
16 Oz Of Salted Almonds
Cooking Spray As Needed

Directions:
Mix Brown Sugar, Corn Syrup, Butter And Salt In A Heavy 2-quart Saucepan. Stirring Constantly, Bring To A Boil Over Medium Heat.
Boil 5 Minutes Without Stirring. Cook To A Hard Crack Stage (300 degrees F), If Sugar Crystals Form On The Sides Of The Pan, Wipe Them Off With A Damp Brush.
Remove From Heat, then Stir In Baking Soda And Vanilla; Mix Well.
Spray A Large Container With Cooking Spray. This Will Keep The Carmel From Sticking To The Container.
Pour The Syrup Mixture Over The warm popcorn, stirring to coat evenly.
Take Hot Popcorn Mixture, And Form Into Balls.

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
Sweet and crunchy, this caramel corn is easy to make.
