Title:
Rebecca's kitchen sink tuna salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 oz can solid white tuna- drained well
1 tbsp mayonnaise
1 1/2 tsp honey mustard
1 1/2 tsp sweet relish
1 tbsp chopped red or green peppers
1 tbsp chopped celery or cucumber (or both!)
1 tbsp chopped green onion
1 tbsp chopped white onion
1 tbsp dried cranberries or raisins
1 tbsp chopped dried apricots
several baby carrots chopped
several mixed nuts chopped
1 cup pasta (penne or macaroni works best)
salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Mash the tuna in a mixing bowl with the mayo, mustard, and relish until well blended.  Taste and adjust accordingly.  Add all the other ingredients and mix well.  Refrigerate overnight to let the flavors blend in.  Serve cold with crackers.

Submitter: 
FOODIE19
Description: 
An easy and tasty recipe for tuna salad using ingredients you already have in the cabinet/ fridge!  Feel free to add other veggies or spices to your taste.
