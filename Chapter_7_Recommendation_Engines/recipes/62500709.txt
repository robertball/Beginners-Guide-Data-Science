Title:
Mexican Corn & Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 19oz cans drained & rinsed black beans
3 cans low sodium corn
1/2 Cup red onion, minced
1/2 Cup green onions, chopped
1 fresh serrano chili chopped
2 Cups ripe tomatoes, chopped
1/3 Cup rice vinegar
1 tsp sugar
1/3 Cup lime juice
1/2 tsp pepper
1/3 Cup olive oil
1 bunch cilantro, chopped (add by taste/preference)
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
1.  Chop and mix all veggies in a large salad bowl.
2.  In seperate bowl, mix together vinegar, sugar, lime juice, pepper, olive oil, cilantro and salt.
3.  Pour dressing into veggies and mix well
4.  Let marinate at least 1 hour before serving.  Really good on day 2!
5.  Serve with slotted spoon.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
The perfect salad for a summer buffet or picnic!
