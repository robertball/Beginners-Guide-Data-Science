Title:
amazing easy mexican dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black beans rinsed & drained
1 10.5 oz jar of raspberry chipotle salsa
6oz cream cheese (3/4 box) thinly sliced
1 80z bag shredded mexican cheese

Directions:
heat oven to 350
add beans
add salsa
layer cream cheese over salsa evenly to cover
add cheese
cook for 30 minutes
serve with tortilla chips

Submitter: 
sarah
Description: 
this is always a huge crowd pleaser & it is the easiest thing in the world to make!
