Title:
Chessmen® Bananas Foster Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075379.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7.25 ounce) package Pepperidge Farm® Chessmen® Butter Cookies
2 medium bananas, sliced
1/2 cup caramel sauce
1/2 cup toasted pecans, chopped
2 tablespoons bourbon
1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding and pie filling mix
1 1/2 cups milk
1 tablespoon caramel sauce
1 cup whipped cream

Directions:
Arrange 12 cookies in the bottom of an 8x8x2-inch baking dish. Arrange the bananas over the cookies.
Heat 1/2 cup caramel sauce in a microwavable measuring cup on HIGH for 20 seconds. Stir in the pecans and bourbon. Pour the hot caramel mixture over the bananas.
Prepare the pudding according to the package directions using the milk.
Spread the pudding over the caramel mixture. Drizzle with the remaining caramel sauce. Spread the whipped cream over the pudding mixture. Top with the remaining cookies. Cover the baking dish and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Campbells Kitchen
Description: 
Try our version of an all-time favorite dessert. It doesn't get any better when you combine the delicious flavors of bananas, cookies, caramel, pudding and bourbon. Best of all, it takes just 15 minutes to put together and no stove-top cooking required! Perfect for the holidays, but so good you'll want to make it again and again.
