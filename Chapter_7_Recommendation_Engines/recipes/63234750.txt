Title:
BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/842568.jpg
Ingredients:
2 5 ounce casn of tomato sauce
1/2 cup Heinz ketchup
1 can of tomato paste
4 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons EVOO
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons dark corn syrup
1 tablespoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon sea salt
1 tablespoon granulated garlic
1 tablespoon whole dried oregano
4 cloves garlic, crushed
4 tablespoons onion, minced
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 cup warm water
2 tablespoons prepared dijon mustard
1 teaspoon cayenne
1 teaspoon chili powder
kosher salt to taste
fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Sautee onions and garlic with a pinch of sea salt until translucent. Next, combine ketchup, tomato sauce, paste, mustard, all spices, water and worcestershire sauce. Simmer for 45 minutes to an hour. Let cool. Store in a covered container for 24 hours in refrigerator to allow flavors to marry. Yuuummmyyy
