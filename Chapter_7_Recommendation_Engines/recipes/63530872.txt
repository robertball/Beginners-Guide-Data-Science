Title:
Creamy Tomato Basil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 oz. whole peeled tomatoes
46 oz V8
1 medium onion diced
4 cloves garlic minced
1/2 c butter
Salt
Pepper
2 tbsp dry basil
2 tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp season salt
1 c heavy cream

Directions:
Over medium heat in a 4 qt stock pot sauté onions in butter until translucent, season with salt and pepper, add garlic. Sauté two more minutes, add V8,  tomatoes, basil and sugar.  Break up the tomatoes with a potato masher. Bring almost to boil, reduce heat to low and simmer covered for 1 hour. Working in small batches, transfer to a blender and blend thoroughly. Return to stock pot. Add cayenne pepper, season salt and cream, simmer on low 10 min or until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Gweb8
Description: 
A hearty and satisfying tomato soup. Worth the effort and will go great with grilled cheese.
