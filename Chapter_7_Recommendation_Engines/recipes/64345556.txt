Title:
Al's Wholesome Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
1/2 C. light brown sugar
1/4 C. white sugar
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 tsp. real maple syrup
1/2 C. all purpose flour
1/4 C. oat flour
1/4 tsp. FRESHLY ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp. GOOD quality cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt
1 C. oatmeal
1 C. currants

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper
In a medium bowl combine the flours, the spices, and the salt
In a large bowl beat the butter with a mixer or by hand, until smooth
Add the sugars to the butter and mix until combined and fluffy
Add the egg and maple syrup to the sugar mixture until completely incorporated and smooth
Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture and stir until smooth and light
Add the currents and oatmeal and stir until incorporated
Cover and chill the dough for at least 15 minutes
Roll the dough into about 1 1/2" balls
Arrange the balls about 1" apart (or about a dozen per cookie sheet)
Bake @ 375 for 13 to 15 minutes, until lightly brown and slightly firm
Remove from oven and allow the cookies to rest 1 minute before moving them to a cooking rack

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
The fresh nutmeg and maple syrup elevate these cookies to a comfort-food and the addition of the oat flour with the oatmeal, creates a light, crispy texture as well as heart-healthy fiber.
