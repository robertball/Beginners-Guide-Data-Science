Title:
Zesty Fruit Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
5 kiwis, peeled and diced
1 quart strawberries, finely chopped
1 pint fresh blackberries, chopped
4 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and coarsely shredded
2 tablespoons any flavor fruit jelly
6 tablespoons SPLENDAÂ® Brown Sugar Blend
1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
3 tablespoons habanero hot sauce
1 (7 ounce) can green salsa
1/3 cup lime juice

Directions:
Place kiwis, strawberries, blackberries, and apples in a bowl. Stir in jelly, SPLENDA(R) Brown Sugar Blend, cayenne pepper, hot sauce, green salsa, and lime juice. Stir together.
