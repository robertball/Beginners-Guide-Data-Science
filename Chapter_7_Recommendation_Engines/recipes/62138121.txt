Title:
Salmon Fillet With Cilantro, Tomato, and Jalapeno Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salmon Fillet With Cilantro, Tomato, and Jalapeno
1 small clove of garlic
1 cup fresh cilantro leaves
5 tablespoons virgin olive oil
Juice from one lime
salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
2-2.5 pounds salmon fillet
1 large organic tomato on vine seeded and chopped
1/2 jalapeno pepper seeded and chopped

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Place the garlic, cilantro, 2 tablespoons of the olive oil, the lime juice, salt, and pepper in a blender.
Blend until creamy smooth.
Brush a baking pan or sheet with the remaining oil and place the salmon fillet on the oil.
Spread the cilantro mixture on the salmon, arrange the tomato and jalapeno over the salmon's entirety, and sprinkle with a dash more salt and pepper.
Bake, uncovered, until the salmon is done 15-20 minutes.
Serve.
