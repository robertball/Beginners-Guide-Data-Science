Title:
crockpot meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz cocktail sauce
10 oz grape jelly
1 cup water
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon herbs de procince
1 teaspoon pepper
1 large bag frozen prepared fully cooked meatballs (recipe will cover up to 6 lbs meatballs- get them at Sams club- smaller bags at local grocer freezer section)

Directions:
crockpot on high
add all ingredients except meatballs...mix together breaking up the jelly as much as possible - add meatballs and stir...cook 4-5 hrs and lower to low till serving ( you can cook longer if start at low and keep there)

a great crowd pleaser at potlucks etc
