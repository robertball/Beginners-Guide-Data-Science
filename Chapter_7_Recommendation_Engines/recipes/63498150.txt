Title:
Crazy Complex Vegan Italian Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (4 ounce) tomato paste
2 bay leaves
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 Small Orange or Yellow Pepper diced
1 Medium Onion diced
1 Cup of Mushrooms (Shiitake) diced
1/2 Cup of Red Wine
1/3 Cup of Butter
2 Tbs of Red Wine Vinegar
2 Tbs of Sherry
1/2 Cup of Brown Sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti

Directions:
1- In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, bay leaves, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, basil, oregano, salt, pepper and  mix well.
2-Cook in saute pan onion mixed diced onions, peppers and mushrooms. Mix saute with red wine, butter, red wine vinegar and sherry. After softened add brown sugar and melt together for about 5 minutes.
3-Add saute mixture to tomato sauce mix for 5 minutes on medium head.
4- blend mixture in batches in blender.
5- reheat in sauce pan and serve with spaghetti
Simmer over medium-low heat for at least one hour; it is best if simmered all day.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Mix sauce with hot pasta; serve.
