Title:
CHICKEN TAMAULIPECAN, Vegetarian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large 2 lb 10 oz. can Fri Chick (or equivalent of favorite chicken meet substitute)
4 Chayote's (you can substitute white potatoes, but the chayote's really do make it special)
4 Carrots
Â¼ lbs. fresh green beans
2 Onions
1 head of Garlic
2 Tablespoons Raisins
1 Small can sliced black olives
4 Cups Water
3 Tablespoons McKay's chicken style instant broth and seasoning
1 10 oz can tomato puree
1 bunch of cilantro
1 Tablespoon oil
1/4 teaspoon dried red pepper
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel and slice carrots
Peel and cube Chayotes (If using potatoes do not peel them)
Wash and cut ends off of fresh green beans, cut them into three pieces
Peel and dice onions
Place oil in pan and heat
Add onions and saute until translucent
Add peeled diced Garlic
Add carrots and chayotes cook 5 minutes
Add fresh green beans
Add Frichick and all liquid in can
Add salt, black pepper, red pepper
Add raisins
Add chicken broth mixed with tomato puree
Clean bunch of cilantro and mince finely add to the pot
Bring to a boil
Cover and cook on low to medium heat for 30 minutes over medium heat
Serve over rice.
Very hearty filling meal.

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
I love this dish.  I was introduced to it in Mexico but being vegetarian I needed to make substitutions, and then I altered it a little more to my tastes.  I serve it over rice.
