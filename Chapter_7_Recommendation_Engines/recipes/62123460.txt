Title:
Creamy Green Chili & Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 Cup cooked chicken, shredded
1 (4.5oz) can green chilies, undrained
1 Cup sour cream
1 (14.5oz) can Cream of Chicken or Mushroom soup
1 can red Enchilada sauce
8 (6”) flour tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven 375
Mix together 1-1/2 cups cheese, chicken, chilies, sour cream, soup and 2 Tbsp. of enchilada sauce
Soften tortillas according to package instructions
Fill each tortilla with ½ cup of mixture
Place each tortilla seam side DOWN in 13x9” baking dish which has been sprayed with non-stick cooking spray
Pour remaining enchilada sauce over tortillas and sprinkle remaining cheese
Bake for 20 minutes or until cheese is melted and filling is hot.
Serve with sour cream, guacamole, tomato, pico de gallo or salsa.

Submitter: 
Catherine McCann Shelton
Description: 
Creamy rich chicken enchiladas with green chilies.  Add pico de gallo or salsa for a taste explosion!
