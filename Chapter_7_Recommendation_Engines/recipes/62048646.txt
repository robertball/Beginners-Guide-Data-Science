Title:
Choco Peanut Graham Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Approximately 18 graham crackers whole
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 cup chocolate chips
1 1/2 cup crushed salted peanuts

Directions:
Line large cookie sheet with graham crackers. Make sure there are not any spaces, fill in with pieces of graham cracker.
Crush peanuts in food processor.
Meanwhile, bring 1 cup butter and 1 cup brown sugar to a boil.  Boil 1 minute, stirring.
Pour onto graham crackers and spread quickly with spatula.  Immediately sprinkle chocolate chips over hot caramel mixture.  The chips will begin to melt..spread with spatula evenly over graham crackers.  Sprinkle crushed peanuts over all.  Place in freezer for about 30 minutes.  Remove.  Break into pieces.

Submitter: 
CHRISPENTINA
Description: 
These are an easy, fast bar-like treat of chocolate chips and chopped peanuts on a graham cracker base! You always have the fixin's in your pantry. Make a double batch, they go fast!
