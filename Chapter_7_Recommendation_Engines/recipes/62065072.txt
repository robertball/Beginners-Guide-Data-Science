Title:
Best Ever Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb pot roast
seasoned flour
olive oil
1 can golden mushroom soup
1 package of beefy onion soup mix
potatoes, carrots, onions, garlic

Directions:
Dredge pot roast in seasoned flour & brown in olive oil in skillet.
Add chopped veggies to crock pot.  Top with seared pot roast and pan juices.  Add soups (no water).
Cook 8 hours or until you decide you're done!
It's wonderful -- enjoy.
PS -- leftovers make a great Shepherds Pie, topped with mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Susanna Joiner Boyd
Description: 
Easy and delicious -- left overs (if you get so lucky) make a great Shepherd's Pie.
