Title:
Jan's Chipotle Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2155210.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 30 ounce jar Kraft Fat Free Mayonnaise
1 - 7 ounce jar La Costena Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce
2 cloves garlic
1/2 tablespoon lime juice
1 tablespoon dry parsley flakes

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a food processor and blend until creamy and no lumps appear.
Store covered in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Spicy and flavorful spread for sandwiches that also makes a great dip, especially for french fries.  You can spread over chicken breasts the last 10 minutes of baking for added flavor. Our favorite is to serve with crab cake sandwiches.
