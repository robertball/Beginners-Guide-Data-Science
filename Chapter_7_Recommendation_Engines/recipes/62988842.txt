Title:
Dill Vegetable Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
4 tablespoons finely chopped onion
2 teaspoons dried parsley flakes
1 teaspoon dill weed
1 teaspoon seasoned salt
Assorted fresh vegetables

Directions:
Combine the first six ingredients; mix well. Cover and refrigerate. Serve with vegetables.

Submitter: 
WendyD
Description: 
A friend gave me this zesty dip recipe many years ago, and now I serve it every year at our holiday open house.Modified from: Dill Vegetable Dip
