Title:
pasta with sausage and peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
1 1/2 pounds italian sausage
1 red onion chopped
5 bell peppers mixture of red,green and yellow diced large
4 garlic cloves chopped
1 cup dry red wine(burgandy is good)
1 28oz. can crushed tomatoes
1 teaspoon dried oregano
salt and pepper
8 oz. cooked pasta (ziti, rigatoni)
crusty italian bread

Directions:
in olive oil brown sausage 5 minutes. Remove from pan. In same pan saute onion, peppers and garlic for 5 minutes. Return sausages to the pot. Pour in wine bring to a boil, boil for 1 minute.Add tomatoes, oregeno and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer covered for 50 minutes. Serve with pasta and bread.
