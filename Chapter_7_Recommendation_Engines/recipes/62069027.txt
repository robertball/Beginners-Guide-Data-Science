Title:
Yellow Squash Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter
1 large onion, chopped
2 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced
2 carrots, sliced
1 1/2 pounds yellow squash, sliced
3 tablespoons flour
1 1/2 quarts chicken stock
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 cup half-&-half
1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
2 teaspoons Hungarian sweet paprika

Directions:
In large pan melt bult. Add onion over medium and cook 5 minutes. Add potatoes, carrots and squash; toss to coat with butter. Add flour all at once; stir to combine and cook 2 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in stock and season with cayenne pepper. Cover and simmer 45 minutes. Let cool then puree soup in batches. Return to pot and stir in half-&-half. Heat through but do not boil. Stir in nutmeg and paprika.
