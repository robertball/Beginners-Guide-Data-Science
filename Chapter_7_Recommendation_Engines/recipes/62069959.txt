Title:
Caramel rice crispy bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups rice crispys
1/2 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup corn syrup
2 tsp. corn starch

Directions:
In large saucepan combine butter, brown sugar, corn syrup. stir for 2 minutes, then add corn starch.
When caramel is thickened, add 3 cups rice crispys and stir until combined. (If the mixture still seems watery add more rice crispys)
Pour into a 9-inch serving dish and chill for at least an hour.

Description:
