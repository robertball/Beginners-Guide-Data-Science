Title:
Purple Moose - Canadian Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1127469.jpg
Ingredients:
15 ice cubes
1 ripe banana
1/2 cup vanilla rice milk
1/4 cup fresh blueberries
2 tablespoons natural peanut butter
2 tablespoons maple syrup

Directions:
Combine ice cubes, banana, rice milk, blueberries, peanut butter, and maple syrup in a blender; blend on low speed. Increase speed to medium-high and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
The Jazz Chef
Description: 
The purple moose is an easy smoothie that tastes great to carnivores and vegans alike.
