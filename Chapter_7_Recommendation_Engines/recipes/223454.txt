Title:
Greek Garden Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982033.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sliced green onions
3 tablespoons low-fat plain Greek yogurt or low-fat plain yogurt
1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
3/4 cup garlic hummus
1 cup finely chopped cucumber
1 cup seeded and finely chopped tomato
3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/4 cup chopped, pitted kalamata olives
Keebler速 Town House速 Flipsides速 Original Crackers

Directions:
In small mixing bowl beat cream cheese, green onions, yogurt, Italian seasoning, pepper and garlic powder on medium speed of electric mixer until combined. Spread on 9-inch serving plate or in 9-inch pie plate.
Spoon hummus over cream cheese mixture.
Sprinkle cucumber, tomato, feta cheese and olives on top. Serve with KEEBLER TOWN HOUSE FLIPSIDES ORIGINAL CRACKERS.
