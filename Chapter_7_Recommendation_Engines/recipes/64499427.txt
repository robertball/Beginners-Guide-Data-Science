Title:
Big Ray's Sausage and Waffle Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2613456.jpg
Ingredients:
8 frozen waffle, cubed
16 oz breakfast pork sausage
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
6 eggs
1 1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup maple syrup
salt
pepper

Directions:
Brown sausage in a medium skillet , breaking it up with a spoon until no longer pink. Drain and set aside.
In a greased 11" x 7" pan, place half of the waffle cubes.  Top with half the sausage then  half of the cheese.  Repeat the layers until all are used up.
In a medium bowl whisk eggs, milk, syrup, salt and pepper.  Pour evenly over top of casserole, pressing down with your hands if needed.  Wrap with foil and refrigerated at least 1 hour or best overnight.
Remove from refrigerator at least 30 minutes before baking in a  350 degree oven for 30 minutes covered, then 10-15 minutes uncovered until nicely browned.  Serve with addition syrup if desired.
