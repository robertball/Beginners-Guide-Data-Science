Title:
Favorite Fall Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1495110.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. hamburger
1 small onion, chopped
1 can of original Rotel
1 can of original flavor stew tomatoes 14 or 16 oz.
1 8oz. can of tomato sauce
1 26 oz. can of ranch beans
1 pkg of taco seasoning
1 pkg of ranch dressing

Directions:
Brown meat in a pot. Add onion towards the end of browning. Add the rest of the ingredients.
Bring to a boil and then turn down to low and let simmer for about 1 hour.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
bakergirl
Description: 
This is a delicious chili recipe that is wonderful on fall nights! I got this from a lady that I know after she made this at a party. Very yummy, hearty, and easy! Make with bread or cornbread for a delicious meal!
