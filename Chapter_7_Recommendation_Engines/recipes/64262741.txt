Title:
Fina' denne' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup vinegar
1/4 white onion, diced (green onion or red onion optional)
4 hot peppers (2 jalapeno was about right)

Directions:
Mix ingredients in quart jar. You may crush the peppers, cut them or serve them whole. Crushing will intensify the spiciness. Can be spooned over meats, poultry or  fish. Or used as a dip. Let sit in fridge overnight.
