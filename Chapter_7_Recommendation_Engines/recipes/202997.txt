Title:
Strawberry Broco-Flower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/790939.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups chopped fresh broccoli
1 1/2 cups chopped fresh cauliflower
1/2 cup shredded carrot
5 fresh strawberries, sliced
1/4 cup slivered almonds
1/4 cup raspberry vinaigrette

Directions:
Combine the broccoli, cauliflower, carrot, strawberries, almonds, and vinaigrette in a large bowl; toss to coat evenly. Serve immediately.
