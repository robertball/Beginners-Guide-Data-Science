Title:
Aunt Betty's Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bar sharp cheddar cheese
1 bar extra sharp cheddar cheese
1 onion
1 green pepper
mayonaise

Directions:
Grate cheese, set aside. Grate onion and pepper onto paper towels, compress and drain out the water...this is important...be sure to get all the water out of the vegetables. Comine all the ingredients with just enough mayo to bind together to desired consistency.

Description: 
Great with breadsticks...rich and tasty.
