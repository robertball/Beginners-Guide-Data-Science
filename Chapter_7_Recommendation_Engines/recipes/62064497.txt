Title:
50,000 hamburger casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. hamburger
2 lrg cans tomatoe sauce
1 small package of egg noodles
1/2 pt cottege cheese
1 (8oz) Cream cheese
1 1/3 C Sour cream
6 stalks grn onion

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350. Brown hamburger and season.  Add tomatoe sauce.  Boil and drain noodles.  In a seperate bowl Mix cheese, cream and grn onions together. Grease a 13X9 inch pan.  Layer 1/2 noodles, all the cheese mixture and the other 1/2 of the noodles, then pour the meat sauce over top.  Bake at 350 for 30-60 minutes or until bubbly.  Enjoy!
