Title:
Wheatberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup   Soft Wheat Berries
1 cup   Corn Kernels, cooked
1 cup   Cucumber, chopped & seeded
1       Sweet Red Pepper, chopped
1/2 cup Fresh Parsley, chopped
4       Green Onions, chopped
Dressing:
2 tbsp  Lemon Juice
2 tbsp  Olive Oil
1/2 tsp Dijon Mustard
1/4 tsp each Salt & Pepper
dash    Hot Pepper sauce

Directions:
Rinse wheat berries; drain well. Transfer to saucepan.
Pour in enough water to come to 2" above wheat berries; bring to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 1-1/2 hours or until tender but not soft.
Drain; chill under cold running water. Drain again.
In large bowl, combine wheat berries, corn, cucumber, red pepper, parsley and onions.
DRESSING:
Whisk together all ingredients
Pour over salad and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Julie Holden
Description: 
This recipe came from Canadian Living magazine. Serve over bed of lettuce, or on its own.
Wheatberries (or Wheat Berries), are an excellent source of protein and may be found near the rice or barley in your grocery store.
