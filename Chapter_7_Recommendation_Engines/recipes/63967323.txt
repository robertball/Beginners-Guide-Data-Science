Title:
Marilyn's Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups hot tap water
2/3 cup honey
2/3 cup oil
2 tablespoons salt, scant
2 tablespoons yeast, in 1 cup warm water
wheat flour, 5-8 cups
white flour, 1-4 cups

Directions:
In mixer add water, honey, oil, salt, 5 cups wheat flour and beat for 20 minutes.  While mixture is mixing put yeast in 1 cup warm water and set it aside.  After mixture has mixed 20 minutes add yeast.  Add seeds (poppy, sesame, sunflower, in desired)  Gradually add more flour until the mixture has cleaned the side of the bowl.  Cut and put into greased loaf pans.  Let rise 1 1/2 hours (until doubled).  Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Put  bread in oven.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees.  Cook 30 minutes or until brown.
