Title:
Fall in Love (with) Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/994317.jpg
Ingredients:
3 avocados, peeled and pitted
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1/2 cup diced onion
2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
3 tablespoons chopped cilantro
1 pinch cayenne pepper, or more to taste (optional)

Directions:
Mash avocados, sea salt, garlic powder, and lime juice in a bowl using a fork.
Mix onion, tomatoes, and cilantro into avocado mixture; season with cayenne pepper.

Submitter: 
MissMissy
Description: 
I call this 'fall in love' guacamole because everyone I know who has tried it has fallen in love with guacamole. Quick, easy, and addictive! Add more salt, lime juice, or cilantro to your taste preference!
