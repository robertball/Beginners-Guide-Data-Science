Title:
Sweet Potato Chocolate-Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cup brown sugar
¼ cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
2 cups sweet potato
¼ cup water
1 ½ cups all-purpose flower
¾ teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon ground cinammon
1 teaspoon pumpkin-pie spice
¼ teaspoon salt
About ½ cup chocolate chips (any kind)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees (fahrenheit).
Grease muffin pan or use cupcake liners.
Mix together sugar, vegetable oil, eggs, sweet potato, and water in a mixer (or by hand).
In a separate bowl mix baking powder, baking soda, flour, cinnamon, pumpkin-pie spice, and salt.
Combine the two mixtures.
Add chocolate chips.
Fill the muffin tins about ½ of the way up.
Sprinkle a couple of chocolate chips in each tin.
Fill the muffin tins with remaining batter (until filled about ¾ of the way).
Cook in preheated oven for 25 minutes or until fully cooked.
