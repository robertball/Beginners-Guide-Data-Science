Title:
Baked Muffaletta Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. package cream cheese, softened
1 cup cooked ham, cubed
1 cup provolone or mozarella cheese, cubed
1/2 cup red onion, chopped
1/2 cup red bell peppers, chopped
1 6 oz. jar Spanish olives, chopped and drained
1 small can sliced black olives, drained
1 or 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbs. fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large mixing bowl.
Spoon into a 9 inch pie plate and bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 25 to 30 minutes.
Garnish with chopped parsley and serve with crackers or toasted baguettes.
