Title:
Yummy Crockpot Beef Short ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs beef bone in short ribs
1/2 lb bacon
1 T Italian Seasoning
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 T beef stock seasoning
1 large onion - sliced
3 large potatoes - large cubes
8 oz baby carrots
1 C dry red wine
1/2 C Sherry

Directions:
cut up bacon-cook
combine Italian seasoning, onion powder and garlic powder
rub short ribs, then sear in pan with bacon grease
line bottom of pot with onion, cooked bacon, potatoes, carrots and sprinkle any remaining rub on top.
add ribs, bone side up and sprinkle with beef stock seasoning
add wine and sherry
turn on high 30 min, then low 3-4 hours
