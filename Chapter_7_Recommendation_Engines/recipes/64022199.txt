Title:
Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Shredded Hash Browns
1 can cream of chicken
2 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
3/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
Salt, pepper, paprika
Optional Topping ingredients for non-crock pot use:
1 1/2 cups corn flakes, coarsely crushed
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
Spray 9x13 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
In large bowl combine all hash brown ingredients; mix well.
Spread into prepared baking dish.
Sprinkle with salt, pepper, & paprika
Bake 350 for 1 hour.
Transfer to crock pot on low.
Optional topping for non-crock pot service: In small bowl stir together topping ingredients and spread over hash browns prior to baking. Do not use crock pot.
