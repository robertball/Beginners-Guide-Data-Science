Title:
Sausage Pasta Michelle Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/403329.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 pound pasta
1 pound Country Sausage
1 onion, chopped
1 Tablespoon diced garlic
1 cup chicken boullion
1 Tablespoon fresh oregano, chopped
1 pint cherry tomatoes, cut in half
1/2 package fresh spinach, chopped

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and reserve.
In a large skillet, cook sausage, garlic and onion thru until sausage is no longer pink. After sausage has cooked, drain and place the tomatoes, spinach, oregano, bullion and meat back into skillet and cook until spinach is tender. (about 5 minutes) Mix meat mixture with pasta. Serve immediately.
