Title:
Joyce’s Crock Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2½ pound roast
1 large onion, sliced
3 whole garlic cloves
½ pkg. brown gravy mix
1 oz. pkg dried Italian salad dressing mix
¾ tsp black pepper
2 handfuls baby carrots
3 potatoes, chunked
½ cup water

Directions:
Season roast with salt and pepper. Brown in skillet with oil. Place onions and garlic in the bottom of the crock pot, then place the roast on top of them. Place the carrots and potatoes around the roast.
In a bowl whisk together water with both packages of seasonings, and black pepper (don't worry if there is lumps, they will work our during the cooking). Pour the mixture over the roast and veggies.
Cook on LOW for 8-12 hours or on HIGH for about 4½ hours. I cooked it on HIGH,
