Title:
BEEF BROCCOLI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/713929.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb.beef steak
2 Tbsp.cornstarch
4 Tbsp.soy sauce
1 tsp.sugar
2 tsp.minced ginger
1 clove minced garlic
1 lb.broccoli,trimmed
1 onion,cut in chunks

Directions:
Cut beef across the grain in thin slices.
Combine 1 Tbsp.each of cornstarch and soy sauce with sugar,ginger and garlic,stir into bowl with beef.
Let stand 15 mins.
Meanwhile remove flowerets from broccoli.If large cut in half lengthwise.Cut stalks into 1/8 in slices.
Combine remaining cornstarch and soy sauce with 1 cup of water,set aside.
Heat 1 Tbsp. oil in skillet on high.Add steak,fry 1 min.Remove from heat.
Heat remaining oil in same pan.Add broccoli and onion,fry 4 min.untill crisp.Return steak and soy sauce mixture,bring to fast boil,Serve immediately.
