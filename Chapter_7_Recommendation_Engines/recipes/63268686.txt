Title:
Shepards Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
salt
pepper
1 sm. can of pasta sauce
american cheese
1 can corn, drained
mashed potatoes

Directions:
Brown hamburger and drain. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Add sauce and line the bottom of a 9x13 dish.
Cover meat with cheese. Top with the can of corn.
Cover with mashed potatoes
Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes, covered.
