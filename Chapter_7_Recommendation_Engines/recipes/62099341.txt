Title:
Delicious pork and tomato sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz can crushed tomatoes
1 680ml can pasta sauce(or tomato sauce)
1 large onion
1 whole head garlic
1 zucchini
2 stalks celery
1 green pepper
1 tbsp sugar
2 tbsp dried oregano
salt
pepper
2 bay leaves
1 tbsp dried basil

Directions:
Brown meat.  Add onions and cook until tender.  Add the rest of the veggies except for garlic. Cook about 5 mins.  Add oregano and basil, bay leaves and a pinch each of salt and pepper. Add garlic and allow to cook for a minute( dont let the garlic burn!)
Add both cans of tomatoes.  If too thick add some water. Simmer for about 45 minutes.  Taste for seasoning.  Add sugar if too tomatoey.

Submitter: 
babybluangel
Description: 
Flavorful and hearty, this sauce will become a favorite of yours.  I use ground pork but you could use any kind of ground meat.  You can also substitute any kind of veggies you have on hand.
