Title:
Pumpkin Bread trial Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 can pumpkin puree (not pie mix)
3/4 c sugar
3/4 c packed brown sugar
1/3 c canola or veg oil
1 t pure vanilla
1.5 c flour
1 t baking soda
1/2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
3/4 t cinnamon
1/2 t ginger
1/4 t cloves
1/4 t allspice
1/4 t nutmeg
1/2 t pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Beat eggs; mix in pumpkin, sugars, oil, and vanilla. In separate bowl, mix dry ingredients thoroughly with whisk; fold gently into wet ingredients, just until combined.
Place in loaf pan which has been sprayed with cooking spray. Bake at 325 1-1.4 hours. Let cool in pan.
