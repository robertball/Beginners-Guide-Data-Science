Title:
Big Ray's Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1076193.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 8 oz. package cream cheese
1  pint half and half
1  Tbsp. minced garlic
1 lb. sliced mushrooms
salt
black pepper
2/3 cup  grated Parmesan cheese (preferably fresh)
1 lb fettuccine, prepared as directed

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, melt butter.  When butter is melted, add cream cheese.
When the cream cheese is softened, add heavy cream and mushrooms.
Season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Simmer for 15-20 minutes over low heat, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and stir in Parmesan cheese.
Serve over hot fettuccine noodles.
