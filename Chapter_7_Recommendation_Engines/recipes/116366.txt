Title:
Chicken Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/592519.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded cooked chicken
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
2 (8 inch) flour tortillas
Ranch salad dressing

Directions:
In a bowl, combine chicken, onion, mozzarella and cheddar; set aside. Spray one side of each tortilla with nonstick cooking spray. Place tortilla greased side down on hot griddle. Place chicken on half of each tortilla. Fold over and cook over low for 1-2 minutes on each side of until tortilla is golden brown. Cut into wedges. Serve with dressing.

Submitter: 
Carol  Hemker
Description: 
I've always loved going out for quesadillas. Then one day I came up with a recipe for making them in my oven. They cook in about 10 minutes.
