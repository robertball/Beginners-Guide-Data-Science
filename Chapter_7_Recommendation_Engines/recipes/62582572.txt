Title:
Guacamole with Feta Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados
couple dashes of table salt
1 T. lime juice
1-2 T. chopped onion
1-2 T. chopped green chiles (mild)
1 small tomato chopped
2-3 T. fat-free crumbled feta cheese
dash of ground cayenne red pepper

Directions:
Scoop insides from avocado and mash in a bowl. Add a couple dashes of salt to taste. Add lime juice. Add onions and chiles. Add tomatoes. Add cheese. Finally, add a few dashes of red pepper to your desired taste.

Submitter: 
arrion
Description: 
This is a yummy twist on your traditional guacamole. I recommend when making guacamole, you find out what you like best, more or less salt, more chiles, less red pepper, etc. Have fun experimenting with the ingredients.
