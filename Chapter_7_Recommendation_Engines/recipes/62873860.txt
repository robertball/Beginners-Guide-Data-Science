Title:
Whole Egg Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/2 tsp dijon mustard
1/4 tsp salt, heaping
1 Tbs lemon juice
dash of cayene pepper
1/2 Tbs sugar
1/2 cup light EVOO
1/2 cup canola oil

Directions:
Add all ingredients into quart jar
With immersion blender at bottom of jar, begin blending, drawing blender up as dressing thickens
Store up to 2 weeks in fridge
