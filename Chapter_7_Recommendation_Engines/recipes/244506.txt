Title:
Mike's One Pan Rotini with Sausage and Spicy Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2395737.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Barilla® Pronto™ Rotini
3 cups water
1 (26 ounce) jar Barilla® Spicy Marinara Sauce
1 link cooked sausage, sliced into 1/3-inch coins
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour whole box of pasta into a large skillet (approximately 12 inches in diameter). Pour 3 cups of cold water into the pan, ensuring that the water covers the pasta. Turn the burner to high, then set your timer for 10 minutes (optional: add a bit of salt to taste).
Cook on high, stirring occasionally, until almost all of the liquid has evaporated.
Remove the skillet from heat and add sauce; stir to combine. Add sausage and top with cheese, then serve.

Submitter: 
Barilla
Description: 
Sausage and spicy marinara sauce bring rich and zesty flavors to this quick and easy rotini pasta dish.
