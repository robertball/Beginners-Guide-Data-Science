Title:
Traditional Style Scottish Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4oz/120g White Pudding Rice - Spanish Paella rice would do
1Â½ pints/850ml Full Cream Milk
A piece of Lemon Peel - to taste
2oz/60g shredded Suet or Butter
3 Egg - lightly beaten
2 to 4oz/60 to 120g Sugar - depending on how sweet you like the pudding
A handful or so Raisins - to taste but can omit
Pinch Cinnamon or Nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 300f/160c/Gas 2
Place the rice, milk and the lemon peel in a pot and heat gently until simmering;
Cook for 10 minutes, stirring constantly to prevent sticking;
When the rice is soft, pour it into a baking dish, and mix in the shredded suet or butter;
Allow to cool to a warm temperature.
Add the beaten eggs, sugar, raisins and cinnamon or nutmeg (if using) to the pudding;
Place in oven and bake for half an hour or so until top is golden.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
An old fashioned Scottish comfort dessert. Brings back the best childhood memories. Sweet, savoury and delicious, lemon peel/raisins or not.
