Title:
Hot Spiced Cider with Orange Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1061226.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons packed SPLENDAÂ® Brown Sugar Blend
1/2 teaspoon whole allspice
1 teaspoon whole cloves
1 cinnamon stick
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground nutmeg
1 large orange, quartered with peel
2 quarts apple cider

Directions:
Place filter in coffee basket, and fill with SPLENDA(R) Brown Sugar Blend, allspice, cloves, cinnamon stick, salt, nutmeg, and orange wedges. Pour apple cider into coffee pot where the water usually goes. Brew, and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Great for a crowd! Try this novel technique for a delicious sugar-free cider spiced with cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg and allspice. If you don't own an automatic coffeemaker, heat the cider in a slow cooker or a saucepan over medium heat on the stovetop. (Clean your coffee maker according to the manufacturer's instructions before using for coffee again.)
