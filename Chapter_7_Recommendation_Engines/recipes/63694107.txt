Title:
Chicken Feta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lbs Split Chicken legs and Thighs
2 cans diced tomatoes with basil, garlig & Oregano
8 oz Reduced Fat Feta Cheese Crumbled
14 oz box Whole Grain Brown Rice (boil in bag)

Directions:
Combine chicken and tomatoes
let stand in refrigerator for a couple of hours
Put 4/oz of feta cheese on top
bake 45 minutes at 350 until done
add remaining feta cheese and serve with rice

Submitter: 
Laurie A Rockdashil Sutherland
Description: 
Quick and easy chicken dish for moms on the go! Make ahead in the a.m. pop in the oven when you get home.
