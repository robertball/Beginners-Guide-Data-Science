Title:
German Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head cabbage, finely shredded
3 green onions, sliced
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoons celery seed
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 cup canola oil

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cabbage and onions. In a saucepan, mix sugar, vinegar, celery seed and salt; bring to a boil. Add oil; return to boiling and cook until sugar dissolves. Pour over cabbage; toss gently. Chill.

Submitter: 
empebi
Description: 
This recipe has been handed down through generations of German families in northern Idaho. Everyone around here makes it. It's good served either warm or cold.Modified from: German Coleslaw
