Title:
RCC Chocolate Fudge Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Vanilla or Graham Cracker Pie Crust
1 cup soft butter
2 squares Baker's unsweetened chocolate (2 oz)- melted
2 cups powdered sugar
1 cup chopped pecans
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar
Add chocolate
Blend in eggs one at a time
Add vanilla and nuts
Put mixture in pie crust
Chill at least 24 hours.
