Title:
White Frost Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs white chocolate
3 cups rice cereal squares
3 cups Golden Grahams cereal
3 cups Honeycomb cereal
2 cups thin pretzel sticks
2 cups mixed nuts
2 Tablespoons oil

Directions:
Mix cereals, pretzels and nuts in large pan. Melt chocolate. Mix oil with chocolate. Combine all ingredients until evenly coated. Pour mixture onto wax paper to cool.
