Title:
Chocolate Chess Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
5 tablespoons cocoa powder
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup evaporated milk
3 large eggs, room temperature
1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 partially 9-inch baked pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Combine the sugar, cocoa, flour, and salt. Blend in milk. Beat in eggs 1 at a time.
Add butter and vanilla, beat until smooth.
Pour into a prebaked pie shell.
Bake in the oven at 325 degrees for 55-60 minutes. Cool the pie completely before topping with whipped cream.
