Title:
Overnight Chicken Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. cooked chicken; chopped or shredded
1 can Cream of Mushroom, or Cream of Chicken Soup
1 8 oz. Container Sour Cream
1 can Green Chillies, chopped
2 large cans Enchilada Sauce
10 6-inch CORN Tortillas; cut into quarters
2 cups Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Spray a 13x9 Baking Dish with Pam.
Saute chicken in skillet with butter. - Also add as you cook the chicken add several sprinkles of - season salt, garlic garlic, cumin, chili
Mix together Soup, Sour Cream, Chilies and chopped chicken.
Pour 1/4 cup of Enchilada sauce in bottom of baking dish.  Layer half of the tortilla pieces on top of sauce.  Pour 1-cup of enchilada sauce on top of shells.  Spread 1/2 of the chicken mix on top of sauce,and add 1 cup of cheese.  Repeat the layers starting with shells-enchilada sauce-chicken-cheese.
Spray a piece of foil paper with Pam and cover casserole.  Chill overnight or at least 8 hours.
Remove from Fridge and Bake COVERED for 30 min.  Remove foil and bake another 20 Minutes.  Remove from oven and let stand 15 minutes.  Serve Warm

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
This is so good.  You have to make it the night before and let it sit for the flavors to blend.  Don't be tempted to eat early.  All my friends love it.  Recipe calls for canned Green Chilies, but I sub for Jalapenos to add a little zip.
