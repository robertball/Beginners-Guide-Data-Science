Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1019214.jpg
Ingredients:
1	 pound ground turkey, or 2 to 3 cups cooked cubed chicken
2	tablespoons oive oil
1	medium onion, chopped
3      tablespoons M’s low sodium taco seasoning (recipe below)
1	can Cannollini  beans rinsed and drained
1	can black beans rinsed and drained
1	can whole kernel corn with liquid or, 1 cup frozen whole kernel corn thawed
32	ounces low sodium chicken broth
1	(15.25 oz.) diced tomatoes, undrained
1	(4.5 oz.) can Old El Paso chopped green chiles
2 	to 3 teaspoons diced jalapenos from a jar (along with about 2 teaspoons of the liquid)
Taco Seasoning
1	tablespoon chili powder (I use Gebhardt brand it’s very mild)
½	teaspoon garlic powder
½	teaspoon onion powder
Scant 1/8 teaspoon cayenne
2	teaspoons Mrs. Dash Southwest Chipotle Seasoning
1 ¼	teaspoons Mrs. Dash Fiesta Lime Seasoning
¾	teaspoon dried oregano
½	teaspoon paprika
1 ½	teaspoons ground cumin
¼	teaspoon Kosher or Sea Salt
¾	teaspoon black pepper
Toppings
Shredded Cheddar cheese or Mexican blend shredded cheese
Sour cream
Crushed tortilla chips or corn bread

Directions:
1.	In 5 to 6 quart pot, brown turkey or  ground beef until thoroughly cooked.  Drain fat.  Add onions and taco seasoning mix; cook 1 minute.
2.	Stir in tomatoes,  beans, corn, jalapenos and broth.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; simmer 10 minutes.
3.	To serve, ladle soup into serving bowls; top with cheese. Sour cream, and  tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Really easy and very good hearty soup.  freezes really well.
