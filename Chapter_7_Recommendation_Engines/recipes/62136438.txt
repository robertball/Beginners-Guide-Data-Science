Title:
Marvelous Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine, softened
3 cups sugar
2 cups mashed bananas(approx. 4)
4 eggs, beaten
3 3/4 cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1 cup buttermilk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 Tbsp. orange juice
1 cup chopped pecans
CUSTARD TOPPING
4 egg yolks
4 Tbsp. flour
1 cup sugar
2 cups milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream the margarine and sugar. Add mashed bananas and mix.  Stir in eggs.
In small bowl combine flour and baking soda.  Add this to the mixture alternating with buttermilk, beginning and ending with the flour. Mix well between additions.
Stir in vanilla, orange juice and pecans.  Pour into 3 greased round cake pans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes.
CUSTARD TOPPING
Mix the ingredients and cook in a double boiler until thick.  Remove from heat and add 2 tsp. vanilla.  When cooled, spread on cake and sprinkle with pecans.
