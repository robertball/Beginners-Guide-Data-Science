Title:
Basil-Red Pepper Tuna Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (6 oz) cans white chunk tuna packed in water, drained
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
2 Tbsp chopped fresh basil
2 Tbsp white wine vinegar
2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 Tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/4 tsp sea salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
8 slices whole wheat or multigrain bread
4 romaine or leaf lettuce leaves
4 tomatoes, thinly sliced

Directions:
Combine tuna, bell pepper, basil, vinegar, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
Place 4 bread slices on work surface. Arrange tuna mixture evenly on each bread slice. Top each with a lettuce leaf and a sliced tomato. Top each with remaining 4 slices of bread. Serve.

Submitter: 
KitchenWitch
Description: 
This sandwich has a fresh flavor and no mayonnaise. To juice the lemon, make sure it's at room temperature first, then roll it back and forth on the counter with your palm. This makes it much easier to juice!
