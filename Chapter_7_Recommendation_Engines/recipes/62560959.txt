Title:
Quick & Easy Crockpot Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536363.jpg
Ingredients:
two 15 oz cans beans of choice, drained and rinsed
6 oz lean Canadian bacon or smoked ham, chopped
15 oz can diced tomatoes
4 cups of vegetables Â– I used what I had in the fridge (celery, zucchini, summer squash, onion, bell pepper, green beans)
32 oz chicken or vegetable broth
1 teaspoon dried Italian Seasoning
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
6 oz whole wheat pasta
Sea Salt & Black Pepper to taste
Top with parsley and parmesan if desired

Directions:
Combine beans through crushed red pepper flakes in a 5 quart crock pot.  Cook on low all day long, about 8 hours.
Turn heat up to high, add pasta and continue cooking another 15 minutes or until pasta is cooked through.  Season to taste, top with parmesan, parsley and enjoy!
