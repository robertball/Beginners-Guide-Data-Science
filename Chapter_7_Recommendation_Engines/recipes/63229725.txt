Title:
Sweet Pepper Balsamic Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840141.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped yellow bell pepper
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 (7 ounce) jar roasted red peppers, drained
12 cherry tomatoes, halved
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon chopped fresh lemon basil
2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
1 teaspoon vegetarian Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon crushed garlic
1/2 head lettuce

Directions:
In large bowl combine yellow pepper, beans, roasted red peppers and tomatoes.
Then add parsley, balsamic vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, sugar, lemon basil and garlic; shake well. Pour over vegetables; toss gently.  Cover and let stand at room temperature for 30 minutes or refrigerate for 2 hours.
To serve, place leaves of lettuce on individual salad plates and top with bean mixture.

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
