Title:
Raspberry Bliss Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/667396.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh raspberries
1 cup reduced calorie vanilla yogurt
1 cup chipped ice

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend until smooth. If necessary, add a tablespoon or two of water as mixture will be very thick.
Top with reduced calorie whipped cream if desired.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
This simple and delicious smoothie combines fresh raspberries, yogurt, and ice for a refreshing and nutritious treat.
