Title:
Gruyere Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10.5 ounces pie crust
2 cups heavy cream
1/3 cup milk
1 Tbsp flour
2 egg yolks
4 eggs
9 ounces Gruyere cheese
salt to taste
pepper to taste
nutmeg to taste
butter to grease the pan

Directions:
Beat the cream with the egg yolks and the 4 whole eggs.
Mix the flour and milk and add to egg mixture; stir well.
Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
Roll out the dough 1/10 inch thick and place in the lightly greased pan. Prick several times with a fork.
Grate the cheese onto the dough.
Pour in the egg mixture and make at 390 degrees for 40 minutes.
The surface should be light brown.

Submitter: 
Cindy
Description: 
This is intended for a 9-inch pan. It is frequently served with a tossed salad.
