Title:
Roasted Red Pepper and Artichoke Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 Cup low fat mayonnaise
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1-2 cloves of garlic
1 (14oz) can artichoke hearts, drained and finely chopped (NOT marinated hearts)
1/3 Cup finely chopped roasted red peppers

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  In a food processor, combine cream cheese, mayonnaise, parmesan cheese and garlic.  Process until smooth.  Add finely chopped artichoke hearts and red peppers and process until well blended.  Spread into an ungreased 9 in. quiche dish or glass pie plate.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until bubbly.  Serve with crackers, bread slices or veggies.

Submitter: 
DoreenRN
Description: 
This appetizer is a favorite of mine that everyone asks me to bring to parties.  No one ingredient is overpowering and the ingredients compliment each other deliciously!
