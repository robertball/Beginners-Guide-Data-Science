Title:
Peanut Butter Oatmeal Muffins with Chocolate Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/708869.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T butter or margarine
1 c buttermilk
2 eggs
1 c chunky peanut butter
1 t vanilla
1 c quick cooking oatmeal
1 c all purpose flour
2 T brown sugar
1/2 c white sugar
1 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1 c mini chocolate chips
Turbinado sugar (optional)

Directions:
Melt 1 T butter or margarine in mixing bowl in microwave.  Add buttermilk, peanut butter, eggs and vanilla.  Stir to combine.  Add remaining ingredients except Turbinado sugar and fold gently to combine.  Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups 2/3 full.  Sprinkle lightly with Turbinado sugar.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Peanut butter lovers unite!  The smell will fill your house when these are baking.  Add more chips or regular sized chips if you like them heavier on the chocolate.
