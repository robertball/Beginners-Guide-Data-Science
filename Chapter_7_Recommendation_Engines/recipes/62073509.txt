Title:
No-Bake Chocolate Mint Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup chopped nuts, if desired
Frosting:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 Tbsp. milk
1 tsp peppermint extract
Few drops green food coloring
1/2 chocolate chips, for drizzling

Directions:
In a med. saucepan combine chocolate chips and milk.  Melt over low heat, remove from heat and add vanilla.  In a med. bowl combine graham cracker crumbs, powdered sugar and nuts, if desired. Pour in chocolate mixture, mix well.  Press into lightly greased 9x9 in. pan.  Cover with plastic wrap and chill for about 1 hour.  In a small bowl, combine all frosting ingredients except chocolate chips.  Beat wtih an electric mixer on med. speed until frosting is creamy and spreadable.  Spread over chilled choclate layer.   In a small bowl, microwave choclate chips on low until melted.  Drizzle chocolate over frosting layer.  Cover and chill again before serving.

Submitter: 
Ali
Description: 
These are really easy yummy squares that I usually make around Christmas.  They look very pretty on a plate of assorted cookies for the Holidays.
