Title:
Sara's Famous Cherry Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982983.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (21 ounce) cans LUCKY LEAF® Regular or Premium Cherry Fruit Filling
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups quick oats
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup finely chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place two cans of LUCKY LEAF Cherry Pie Filling in bottom of 9x13 inch pan. Blend softened butter, oatmeal, brown sugar and pecans until it is crumbly. Sprinkle all of mixture over top of the pie filling. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
LUCKY LEAF® Pie Filling
Description: 
A crunchy pecan topping covers this lovely cherry crisp.
