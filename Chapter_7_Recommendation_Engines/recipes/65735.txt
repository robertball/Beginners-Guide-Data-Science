Title:
Seafood Stuffed Pasta Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980189.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
1 egg
1/2 cup minced green onions and tops
4 tablespoons minced fresh parsley, divided
1 teaspoon grated fresh lemon peel
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 pound bay shrimp, rinsed and drained
1 cup Kikkoman Sweet & Sour Sauce
3 1/2 teaspoons sugar
3 tablespoons water
20 cooked jumbo shell macaroni, thoroughly drained

Directions:
Thoroughly combine cheese, egg, green onions, half of parsley, lemon peel and salt. Gently stir in shrimp; set aside. Blend sweet & sour sauce, sugar and water; spread 1/4 cup mixture over bottom of 13 x 9-inch non-metal baking dish.
Stuff shells with cheese mixture; place, filled side up, in single layer on sauce in pan. Pour remaining sweet & sour sauce mixture evenly over shells. Cover baking pan tightly with foil.
Bake in 350 degrees F oven 35 minutes, or until hot and bubbly. Just before serving, sprinkle remaining half of parsley over shells.

Submitter: 
Kikkoman
Description: 
Jumbo shell pasta is stuffed with a mixture of shrimp and ricotta cheese.
