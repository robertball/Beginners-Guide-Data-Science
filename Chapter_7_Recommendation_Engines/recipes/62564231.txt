Title:
Blueberry and/or Saskatoon Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups blueberries/Saskatoons
3 tablespoons white sugar
1/3 cup orange juice
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1 pinch salt
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Mix and simmer berries, 3 T. sugar and orange juicer for 10 minutes. Stir in lemon juice.
Pour into a greased 8 inch square baking dish. Set aside.
In a small bowl, thoroughly mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
In a medium bowl, cream butter and 1/2 cup sugar until light and fluffy.
Beat in egg and vanilla extract.
Gradually add flour mixture, stirring just until ingredients are combined.
Drop batter by rounded tablespoons over Saskatoon mixture trying to cover as much of filling as possible.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, until topping is golden brown and filling is bubbling.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
To make this judiciously sweet cobbler, blueberries/Saskatoons are simmered with sugar and orange juice, and then topped with a batter. And within 40 minutes, this cobbler bakes up bubbly and beautiful.Modified from: Blueberry /Saskatoon Cobbler
