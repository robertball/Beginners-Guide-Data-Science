Title:
Pink Frosting Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1cup brown sugar
1 tsp almond extract
2 Tbsp instant coffee (dry)
1/2 tsp baking powder
2 1/4 cup flour
6 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
Glaze
1 1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 tsp almond extract
Water- added a teaspoon at a time
3 drops red food coloring- mix and add water for desired glaze consistency

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Cream butter, sugar, extract and coffee.
Add in baking powder and mix
Add in flour and mix
Add in choc. chips
The dough will be hard and thick.
Press into a greased 9x13 inch pan.  Bake for 20-22 minutes and cool completely(i like to stick the bars in my chest freezer)
When completly cool frost with the above glaze. the glaze can be made thicker or thinner by changing the water amount added.
cut into small bars as these are very rich.

Submitter: 
rosburn
Description: 
These are my mom's Pink Frosting Bars.  The frosting is really only a light glaze.  They are really good and everyone on nightshift with me at the hospital loves it when I make these becuase they have coffee in them. Enjoy!
