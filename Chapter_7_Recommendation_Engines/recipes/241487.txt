Title:
Supercharged Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5334958.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup almond milk
1/2 cup fresh papaya
1 scoop protein powder
1 (1 inch) piece peeled and chopped fresh turmeric root
1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger root, peeled
3 cubes ice, or as desired

Directions:
Combine almond milk, papaya, protein powder, turmeric root, and ginger root in a blender; add ice and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
A great turmeric smoothie loaded with protein.
