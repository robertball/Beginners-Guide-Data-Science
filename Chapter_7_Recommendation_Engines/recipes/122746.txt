Title:
Lemon-Berry Pitcher Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982330.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sweetened lemonade powder
4 cups cold water
2/3 cup cranberry juice, chilled
1 1/2 cups lemon-lime soda, chilled

Directions:
In a pitcher, combine drink mix, water and cranberry juice.  Stir in soda.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Margaret O'Bryon
Description: 
'If you need to satisfy a large group, you can double or triple the recipe for this refreshing beverage,' advises Margaret O'Bryon from Bel Air, Maryland. The tangy combination of lemonade and cranberry juice is a real thirst-quencher on a warm day.
