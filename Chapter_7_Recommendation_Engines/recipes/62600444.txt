Title:
Beef Barley Soup with Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C roasted carrot, rutabaga, and turnip melange
2T veg. oil
1 large yellow onion, cut into 1/2-in dice or coarsely chopped
1 large clove garlic, minced
1/2# lean ground beef
2 ribs celery cut into 1/4-in dice
2 cans (14 1/2 oz) beef broth
1 can (14 1/2 oz) diced tomatoes w/juice
2 C milk
1 C pearl barley
1 sm. bay leaf
1 1/2t dried thyme
Freshly ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut roasted veggies into 1/2-in pieces and let sit at room temperature
In 4-qt heavy saucepan over med. heat, heat oil and saute onion and garlic until softened but not browned, about 5 min. Add ground beef and saute, breaking up lumps, until meat has lost its pink color. Add all remaining ingred. except the roasted veggies. Bring almost to a boil, reduce to simmer, cover and cook until barley is tender, about 30 min. Place room-temp. veggies in warmed soup bowls and lakle soup into bowls. Serve.
