Title:
Cranberry Pumpkin Bread Machine Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c dried cranberries
2 Tbsp Brandy
2/3 c water
1/3 c pumpkin puree
2 Tbsp nut oil
3 c bread flour
3 Tbsp light brown sugar
2 tbsp dry buttermilk powder
1 Tbsp gluten
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp pumpkin or apple pie spice
2 tsp SAF yeast or 2 1/2 tsp other brand of yeast

Directions:
Sprinkle dried cranberries with brandy in small bowl.
Cover with plastic wrap.
Let stand at room temperature for one hour to macerate.
Place ingredients, except the cranberries, into bread pan according to your bread machine's instructions.
Set crust on medium.
Program for sweet bread cycle. (This bread is not suitable for delayed timed baking).
When machine beeps or between knead 1 and knead 2, add the cranberries and any extra brandy in the bowl.
When baking cycle ends, immediately remove the bread from the pan.
Place on rack and let cool completely before slicing.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Tasty sweet pumpkin spice bread that is a big hit for the fall and winter holidays especially.
