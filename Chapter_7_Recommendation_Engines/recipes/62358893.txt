Title:
Beef Over Rice (or Mashed Potatoes) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds stew meat
2 cans each cream of mushroom & cream of celery soup
2 soup cans of water
2 packets onion soup mix
Cooked rice or mashed potatoes

Directions:
In a large, oven-proof pot, mix all ingredients except rice/potatoes.  Cover and bake at 350F for 2 hours or until meat is tender.  Serve over rice/potatoes.

Submitter: 
Susan T-O
Description: 
A home assembled meal that's easy, tasty & filling.  Mom originally served this over rice, however I like it better on mashed potatoes (I use instant).  Tastes great as leftovers.
