Title:
Very Best Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds ground beef
2 eggs
1 large onion, chopped
6 cloves of garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups milk
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 cups dried, seasoned bread crumbs
2 Tablespoons Italian seasoning
2 1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoon prepared mustard
1 1/3 cups ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Saute onion and garlic in 2 Tablespoons olive oil. In a large bowl, combine the beef, eggs, onion and garlic, milk, Worcestershire sauce, bread crumbs, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper. Mix well and place in a lightly greased baking dish forming into a loaf. In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
