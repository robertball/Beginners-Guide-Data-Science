Title:
Southern Addiction Mint Julep Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1042534.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons simple syrup
10 fresh mint leaves
1/2 cup crushed ice, or as needed
2 fluid ounces peach-flavored bourbon liqueur (such as Southern Comfort Â®)
1 1/2 fluid ounces peach schnapps
2 fluid ounces club soda
2 1/2 fluid ounces sweet tea, or as needed

Directions:
Muddle simple syrup and mint leaves together in a large glass; add ice. Pour in peach-flavored bourbon liqueur, peach schnapps, club soda, and sweet tea. Stir well.

Submitter: 
mimi52
Description: 
A mint julep with a twist. If you don't have enough room in your glass for 2.5 ounces of sweet tea, just top it off.
