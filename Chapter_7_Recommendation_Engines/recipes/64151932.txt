Title:
Jean's Mom's Lady Ross Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts cauliflower into flourets or broken
2 quarts chopped cucumber, no seeds or skins or 1 quart of small gerkins chopped
2 quarts chopped onions or 1 quart of silverskinds
6 red sweet peppers chopped finely
1 head celery chopped finely
brine
2 cups of pickling spice
20 cups of water
Pickling Mixture
3 pints of vinegar
8 cups white sugar
2 oz mustard seeds
1/2 oz celery seed
1-1/4 cups flour
8 oz mustard prefer keens
1/2 oz turmeric

Directions:
prepare all vegetables and put in brine overnight
drain and pat dry all vegetables
In large pot, heat all pickling mixtures,
add the vegetables
Keep hot and simmer for 1 hour
put in sterilized jars
