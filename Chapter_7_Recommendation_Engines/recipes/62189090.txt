Title:
Pumpkin & Cream Cheese Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz softened cream cheese
3 Tblsp. sugar
1 egg
Blend and set aside.
Streusel topping
5 Tblsp sugar
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup chopped pecans
3 Tblsp melted butter (not margarine)
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
Mix together and set aside
Muffins
2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups pumpkin
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine the sugar, flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda and cinnamon in a large bowl.  Make a well in the center and add: 2 eggs  & 1 1/2 cups pumpkin, 1/3 cup vegetable oil, 1/2 tsp. vanilla
Mix until just moistened (no beating).  Evenly divide the batter in the greased muffin cups or baking cups.  Place 2 teaspoons of cream cheese filling in the center of each cup and fill with the remaining batter.  Sprinkle some of the streusel topping over the top of each muffin.  Bake 20-25 minutes until tester comes out clean.
