Title:
Southern Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1100362.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup medium cheddar or longhorn cheese, grated
3/4 cup monterey jack cheese, grated
1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
2/3 cup real mayonnaise (not salad dressing-i use duke's)
4 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
4 Tablespoons diced pimentos, drained
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1/8 teaspoon onion powder (optional-i use a tiny dash)

Directions:
Mix the cheeses in a large bowl and add mayonnaise and cream cheese.  Mix by hand until thoroughly combined.
Add pimentos and seasonings.  Mix.  Add more mayonnaise if too dry.
Serve on bread as a sandwich or as a dip or snack on celery.  Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
Paula Todora (Paula T)
Description: 
Creamy pimento cheese with the addition of a little cream cheese to the mix.
