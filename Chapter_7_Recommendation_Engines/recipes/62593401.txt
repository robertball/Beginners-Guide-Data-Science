Title:
Burgundy Beef with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 boneless beef, cubed
2 Tbs. vegetable oil
8 oz. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 can cream of celery soup
a can cream of mushroom soup
3/4 C. dry red wine
1 env. dry onion soup mix

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat, brown meat, drain & set aside.
Combine mushrooms soups, soup mix & wine in 3 1/2-5 qt slow cooker.
Add beef, cover & cook on low for 8-10 hours or high for 4-5 hours.
Serve over hot cook noodles or rice.
