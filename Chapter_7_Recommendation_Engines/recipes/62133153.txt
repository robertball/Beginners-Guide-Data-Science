Title:
Sarah's Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups Tomatoes, diced
1/2 cup fresh Basil, chopped
1 tablespoon Salt
3/4 cup Olive Oil
1 tablespoon ground Black Pepper
1 cup fresh Garlic (from jar ok)
1 cup Onions, minced
1 tablespoon lemon juice
French baguette loaf, sliced 1/2 inch

Directions:
Drain excess liquid from tomatoes after dicing.  In large bowl combine all ingredients; mix well.
Process in canning jars or use on toasted bread:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place sliced bread on baking sheet.
Brush bread evenly with garlic olive oil.
Bake in preheated oven until lightly browned.
Serve bread slices topped with bruschetta mixture.  Garnish with shaved Paremsan (optional).

Description: 
Delicious bruschetta served on slices of toasted French bread.
