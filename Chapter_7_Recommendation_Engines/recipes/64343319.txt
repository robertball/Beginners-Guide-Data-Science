Title:
Eggplant Parmesan II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant, peeled and thinly sliced
1 eggs, beaten
2 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
3 cups spaghetti sauce, divided
8 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded and divided
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1/4 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
Peel and slice eggplant, salt slices and set aside for 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Rinse eggplant slices, dip in egg, then in bread crumbs.  Place in a single layer on a baking sheet.  Bake in preheated oven for 5 minutes on each side.
In a 8x10 inch baking dish spread spaghetti sauce to cover the bottom.  Place a layer of eggplant slices in the sauce.  Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.  Repeat with remaining ingredients, ending with a thick layer of sauce and the cheeses.  Sprinkle basil on top.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes, or until golden brown.

Description: 
Eggplant slices are dipped in egg and bread crumbs and then baked, instead of fried.  The slices are layered with spaghetti sauce, mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.Modified from: Eggplant Parmesan II
