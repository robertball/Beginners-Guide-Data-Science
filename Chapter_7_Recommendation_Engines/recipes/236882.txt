Description: 
Combine raspberry and orange vodkas with cranberry and pineapple juices. Give 'em a quick shake over ice, strain into a martini glass, and float some fizzy sparkling wine over the top. This beautiful cocktail is a signature drink at Nikki's Bar in Venice Beach, California.
