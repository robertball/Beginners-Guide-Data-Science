Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2446333.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup Margarine
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons Vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon baking powder
4 cups all purpose flour
1 cup shredded coconut
1/4 cup butter melted
1 cup milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.  Blend butter and sugar.  Add vanilla and ground cloves.  Mix flour and baking powder in a separate bowl.  Gradually add flour mixture to cream mixture.  Once dough is mixed, roll into 1" balls of dough.  Press 3-4 milk chocolate chips into the center of each dough ball and form into a ball. Roll dough balls in the melted butter, then roll them in the shredded coconut.  Place bites on a parchment paper covered cookie sheet.  Bake for 9-11 mintes.

Submitter: 
Emily Millar
Description: 
These are a great cookie to eat while recovering from a week of random acts.  Also a great cookie to bribe people who assist you in said tasks.
