Title:
Orange Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/891208.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup chopped walnuts or chopped pecans.
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup orange juice
2 eggs slightly beaten
2 tablespoon grated orange peel
1/3 cup melted butter
1 teaspoon vanilla flavouring, optional or 1/4 teaspoon almond extract, optional.
3/4 cup chopped oranges or drained canned mandarins, optional.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix dry ingredients together, flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Stir in nuts.
Mix remaining moist ingredients together.
Stir dry and moist ingredients together till just combined.
Pour into a 9x5x2" pan and bake.
Let stand 10 minutes before inverting onto rack.

Submitter: 
Loretta's Recipe Journal
Description: 
Golden coloured, and mild flavoured. It's great to have on hand to serve to guests with coffee or tea.
