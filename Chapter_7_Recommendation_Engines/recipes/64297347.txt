Title:
Amaranth Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup amaranth
1/2 cup water
1 cup garbanzo bean flour
1 cup rolled oats
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/3 cup sunflower seeds
1 T chia seeds
1/3 cup peanut butter
1/3 cup unsweetened appleasauce
1/4 cup honey
1 egg

Directions:
Heat oven to 375.  Spray 8" pie pan.
Combine amaranth and water in small saucepan.  Bring to boil, turn to simmer.  Heat until water is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
Combine peanut butter, applesauce, honey and egg.
Add cooked amaranth, garbanzo bean flour, rolled oats, cinnamon, sunflower seeds, and chia seeds.
Pour into pan, spread evenly.  Bake 20 minutes, until edges are golden brown.
Cut into wedges. Store covered in refrigerator.
