Title:
CHICKEN AND CHEESE ENCHILADAS */+ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/803255.jpg
Ingredients:
1 10-oz can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup picante sauce
2 tsp chili powder
2 cups chopped cooked chicken
1/2 cup shredded monterey jack cheese
6 flour tortillas (6") warmed
1 small firm tomato, chopped
1 or 2 green onions, chopped or sliced
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Stir soup, sour cream, picante sauce, and chili powder in medium bowl.
Stir ONE cup soup mixture, chicken, and cheese in another large bowl.
Divide chicken mixture among tortillas.  Roll up tortillas and place seam side down in 11x7x2 baking dish.
Pour the remaining soup mixture over the filled tortillas.
Cover baking dish with foil or lid, preferably foil.
Bake 40 minutes.
Pull out of oven and top with tomatoes, onions, and cheddar cheese.
Place back in the oven and broil for about 10 minutes or until the cheddar and other items on top of enchiladas are nice and toasty.
