Title:
Campbell's Kitchen Honey-Mustard Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/721397.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup or Campbell's® Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Chicken Soup
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon spicy brown mustard
Chopped toasted pecans
4 cups hot cooked rice

Directions:
Heat butter in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned.
Add soup, mayonnaise, honey and mustard. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 5 min. or until done. Sprinkle with pecans. Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Sauteed chicken breasts are topped with a sweet-spicy sauce, served over rice and garnished with toasted nuts.
