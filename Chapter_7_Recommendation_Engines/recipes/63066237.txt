Title:
Octodogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/772491.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Hotdogs

Directions:
1. Put a pot of water on high heat to boil.
2. Cut hotdogs in half.
3. To create the legs, leave about one-third of the hotdog at the top for the head.  Make two cuts on one side of the hotdog, then turn over and make two more cuts.
4.  Place octodogs into water and let legs go curly.  Boil for about a minute.
5. Remove from water and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
These cute little guys will be a hit at any kids party, fun get-together, or in a child's lunchbox!
