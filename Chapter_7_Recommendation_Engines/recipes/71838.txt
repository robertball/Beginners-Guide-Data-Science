Title:
Chocolate-Dipped Apricots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/30085.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 ounce bittersweet chocolate
6 dried apricots
1/4 tablespoon chopped pistachios

Directions:
Microwave the chocolate in a microwavable bowl on high for 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute. Stir until completely melted.
Dip the apricots halfway into the chocolate. Let the excess chocolate drip off. Place the apricots onto waxed paper. Sprinkle the pistachios over the chocolate-covered portions of the apricots. Place in the refrigerator until the chocolate is set.
