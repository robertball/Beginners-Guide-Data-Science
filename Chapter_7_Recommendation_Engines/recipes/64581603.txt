Title:
Tomato Chicken Gorgonzola Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3654851.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups mixed greens
1 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
1 tbsp crumbled gorgonzola
1/2 cup shredded pre-cooked chicken
3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 tsp dijon mustard
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tbsp crumbled gorgonzola

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir together the ingredients to make the dressing then set aside.
Assemble the salads by placing the mixed greens in a large bowl following by the shredded chicken, sliced tomatoes then crumbled gorgonzola. Drizzle a tablespoon of the dressing on top of the salad and devour!
Keep the dressing in an air tight container in the refrigerator up to two weeks.

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
A light & vibrant Tomato Chicken Gorgonzola Salad! An easy gluten free recipe you can prep ahead of time and assemble during the week on the go. Paired with a creamy gorgonzola dressing, this flavorful salad is one you can enjoy all summer long!
