Title:
Low-Fat Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
4 very ripe bananas
1/4 cup skim milk
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
Cooking spray

Directions:
In a large bowl, gently beat eggs.
Add bananas and mash together.  Don't worry about making this concoction smooth.  Stir in milk and vanilla.
Stir dry ingredients together in a medium bowl.
Add all at once to wet ingredients.
Mix only until moistened.
Fill greased muffin tins 1/2 full.
Bake in a 350F over 20-25 minutes or until wooden toothpick inserted in center of muffin comes out clean.
Cool in pan.

Submitter: 
Janet Raymond
Description: 
Light, moist and tasty, these muffins have little added fat. Mix-it up by added chopped nuts, dates or dried cranberries.
