Title:
Ambrosia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can fruit cocktail in juice, drained
1 can crushed pineapple in juice, drained
1 can mandarin oranges, drained
1 can peaches in juice, drained and chopped
1/2 lb. seedless grapes
1 can dark, sweet, pitted cherries, drained and halved
1 pint sour cream
1/2 bag flaked coconut
5-6 oz. mini-marshmellows (about half a bag)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together well, refrigerate and let sit for at least 4 hours.  Stir before serving.
FYI: You can use almost any fruit you want or add chopped walnuts if you want a bit of crunch.

Submitter: 
Valerie Kasper
Description: 
A delicious,light fruit dessert, not too sweet, that is always a big hit!
