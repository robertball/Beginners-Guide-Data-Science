Title:
Gluten free CHIAbanana Oat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5143970.jpg
Ingredients:
Muffins:
2 large eggs, room temperature
1 stick salted butter
2 bananas
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup truvia or sugar
3 T Rolled oats
2 cups Oat flour (gluten free)
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp xanthan gum
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 T Chia seeds
1/3 cup sour cream
Topping:
4 T cold butter
1 T Chia
3 T rolled oats
3 T brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F.
Prepare 2 muffin tins with liners.
In kitchen aide mixer (or hand mixer) cream together sugars and butter. Add one egg at a time, blending after each.  Add 2 bananas. Mix well so bananas are fully incorporated.  Add oat flour 1/2 cup at a time.  Add in remaining ingredients for muffins.  Mix for 2 minutes. Scrape the sides then mix one minute more.  Scoop into muffins tins.
In a separate bowl, cut butter into sugar, oats and chia seeds.  Spoon over the tops of the muffins.
Put filled tins into the oven and bake for 28-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean from the center.
