Title:
Cheryle's Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 1/2 cups macaroni
1 quart stewed tomatoes
2 teaspoons paprika
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
6 ounces tomato paste (optional)

Directions:
In large saucepan brown ground chuck with bell pepper, onion, garlic, salt and pepper.  Drain.  Add tomatoes, paprika, chili powder, macaroni and tomato paste (if using). Add water, a tablespoon at a time, if mixture seems too dry.  Simmer until macaroni is tender, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
trissann
Description: 
Macaroni is cooked in a spicy tomato sauce flavored with onions, garlic, paprika and chili powder. Add cooked ground beef to make a hearty, satisfying meal.Modified from: Goulash Supreme
