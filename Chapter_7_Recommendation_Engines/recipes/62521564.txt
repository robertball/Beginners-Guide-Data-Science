Title:
Sunday Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb roast
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 pkg onion soup mix
1 cup water
onion, potatoes and carrots optional

Directions:
place roast and vegies in crockpot
cover with sauce
cook until fork tender
in crockpot on low 6-8 hours or high 4-6 hours
