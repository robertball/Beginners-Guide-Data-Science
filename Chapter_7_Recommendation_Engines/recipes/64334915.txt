Title:
Healthy Macaroni Salad with Greek Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Â½ lb Elbow Macaroni
2 Hard-boiled Eggs
1 cup Non-fat Plain Greek Yogurt
1 Tbsp Mayonnaise
1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
4 Carrots, grated or thinly sliced
2 Celery Stalks, thinly sliced crosswise
2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Dill pickle
Salt and Pepper, to taste
2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Chives

Directions:
1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and return to pot.
2. In a small bowl, finely mash 1 egg yolk with a fork (reserve remaining yolk for another use). Add yogurt, mayonnaise, and lemon juice; stir until smooth. Add to pasta, then stir in egg whites, carrots, celery and pickle. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 3 days. Sprinkle with chives just before serving.
