Title:
Roquefort Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups mayonnaise
5 ounces Roquefort-Blue Cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 cup buttermilk
1 clove garlic, chopped
6 green onions, chopped fine
1 teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Thoroughly mix all ingredients except cheese. Crumble cheese over mixture and stir in.
Store in glass jars in the refrigerater.

Submitter: 
Yomama
Description: 
According to family legend, this recipe came from the El Gaucho restaurant in Seattle, about 50 years ago.  It has been a family favorite ever since! It's a wonderful salad dressing, and a top-notch dip as well.
