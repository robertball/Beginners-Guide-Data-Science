Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Teriyaki with Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1 cup soy sauce
3/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon pepper
9 teaspoons corn starch
9 teaspoons water
16oz bag of frozen stir fry vegetables
cooked rice or quinoa

Directions:
Place chicken in slow cooker.
In a bowl, whisk sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger, garlic, pepper. Once mixed well, pour over chicken.
Cover slow cooker and cook on high for about 1.5 hours.
Mix cornstarch and water in a small bowl, then add to slow cooker and stir. Add bag of stir fry vegetables and stir again.
Cover and cook for an additional 1 hour. Uncover and continue cooking for 1/2 hour or until sauce thickens.
Serve over cooked rice or quinoa.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
This is a great from-scratch teriyaki dish that you can make with common kitchen ingredients. It's very filling, and the chicken turns out very tender. It's a great way to get your three basics (meat, veggies, and starch) in a single dish.
