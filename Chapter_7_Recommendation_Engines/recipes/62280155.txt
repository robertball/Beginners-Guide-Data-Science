Title:
Libby's Walnut Crunch Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups coarsely chopped walnuts
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 15 oz. can Libby's 100% Pure Pumpkin
1 12 fl. oz. Nestle Carnation Evaporated Milk
3/4 cup sugar
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
1/2 tsp. salt
1 unbaked 9 inch deep dish pie shell
3 Tbsp. butter, melted

Directions:
Mix walnuts and brown sugar in small bowl. Combine pumpkin, milk, sugar, eggs, spice and salt in medium bowl; mix well. Place 3/4 cup of nut-sugar mixture on bottom of pie shell. Pour batter over nuts. Bake in preheated 425 degree oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350 degrees and bake for 40-50 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Cool on wire rack. Add butter to remaining nut-sugar mixture; stir until moistened. Sprinkle over cooled pie. Broil about 5 inches from heat for 2-3  minutes or until bubbly. Cool before serving. Makes 8 servings.
