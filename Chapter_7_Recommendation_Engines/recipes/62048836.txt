Title:
Wild Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. chicken breasts (cubed and cooked)
1 cup cooked wild rice
1 can water chestnuts (diced)
1/2 cup mayonaisse
4 green onions (chopped)
2 Tbl tarragon
juic of half a lemon. (about 1 tbl.)
1/2 cup seedless grapes (Halved)
1/2 cup cashews (chopped)
2Tbl. oil

Directions:
Wash and cube chicken. Cook in 2Tbls. oil until done.
Let cool.
In large bowl combine cooked rice, water chestnuts and green onions
Toss together. Combine cooled chicken.
Add tarragon and mayonaisse.
Squeeze juice of half a lemon over all ingredients,Blend well
Right before serving add grapes and cashews.
Toss to coat
I like this in honey wheat pita pockets
