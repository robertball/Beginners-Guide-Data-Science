Title:
Charleston Fried Dill Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4  large Claussen  dill pickles (pre sliced)
2 Boxes Panko bread crumbs
2 cups of milk
2 cups of flour
2 teaspoons of baking powder
2 eggs
2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon dill
Cayenne pepper to taste
Peanut or vegetable oil

Directions:
1.  Preheat oil to 365 degrees.
2.  Combine all batter ingredients and blend until smooth. Let sit for 5 minutes.
3. Dry pickle slices on a paper towel
4. Place one pack of Panko breadcrumbs into a medium sized bowl
5.  Dip pickle slices in the batter then into the Panko crumbs. Coat well
6.  Fry the pickles in small batches for 3-4 minutes or until brown and crispy
Note. Halfway through the process switch out the Panko breadcrumbs. They donâ€™t stick well once the get soggy
