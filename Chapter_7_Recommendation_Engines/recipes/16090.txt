Title:
Dressing for Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4643136.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
1 tablespoon ketchup
1 1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup diced onion (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix together the mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, onion and salt and pepper. Refrigerate until ready to toss with potatoes.
