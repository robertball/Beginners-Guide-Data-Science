Title:
Crockpot Potato Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*32 oz. Bag of Cubed Hashbrowns
*1 can Whole Kernal Sweet Corn
*1 can Creamed Corn
*1 can Evaporated Milk
*1 can Veggie or Chicken Broth
*1/2 Sweet Onion Chopped
*2 Stalks of Celery Chopped
*1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
*2 Tbsp. Dried Chives
*1 Tbsp.Garlic Powder
*Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crockpot and stir. Cook on low for around 6 hours or high for 3 to 4 hours. Stir occassionally.

Submitter: 
Holyflower93
Description: 
A thick filling chowder. Perfect for a cold day.
