Title:
Sweet Potato Ham Steak with Jelly Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tablespoon of margarine
1 large ham stake 1 inch thick
1/2 cup of grape jelly
2 tablespoon of lemon juice
1/8 teaspoon allspice
17oz. can sweet potatoes

Directions:
Melt the butter in a large skillet on Medium-high heat
when the butter is completely melted and bubbling  add the ham
while the ham is cooking mix the sauce
mix the jelly, allspice, lemon juice, and 3 tablespoon of the sweet potato juice.
when the ham is brown on one side flip and add the drained sweet potatoes an pour the jelly mixture on the ham sped and cover for three to five minutes then serve with sweet potatoes

Submitter: 
Little Chef
Description: 
A savory slice of ham with a jelly sauce and sweet potatoes
