Title:
CARROT CAKE OATMEAL Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636091.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
1 c steel cut oats
1 apple, pealed and chopped
.5 c carrots, shredded
.5 c raisins
1 tsp cinnamon
.5 tsp nutmeg
.5 tsp ginger
pinch of salt
1 tbsp butter
.75 c pecans
brown sugar
plain yogurt

Directions:
Boil 4 cups water
Add oats.
Blend spices and salt, add apples, carrots, raisins.
When oats start to thicken, add carrot mixture.
Cook for 30 minutes
Meanwhile, sautee pecans in butter, add 1 tbsp brown sugar.
Put the oats in a bowl.
Put some pecans up on there.
Top with yogurt.
Yum.
