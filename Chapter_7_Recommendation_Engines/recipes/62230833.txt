Title:
Perfect Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/376437.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c 1% buttermilk*
2 eggs
1/3 c liquid honey*
4 tbsp melted butter or canola oil
1 c flour
3/4 c cornmeal
1 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp salt

Directions:
In 2 cup measure, whisk together milk, eggs, honey and butter/oil. Set aside. Whisk together flour, cornmeal, baking powder and salt. Combine wet and dry until thoroughly mixed. Pour into greased 8" square pan and bake at 425 for 20 minutes.
