Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter
1 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Cream together butters and sugars.
Beat in eggs.
In separate bowl, sift together dry ingredients.
Stir into batter.
Refrigerate for 1 hour.
Roll into 1 inch balls, place on cookie sheet and flatten with a fork
Bake in preheated 375 degree oven for 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown.
