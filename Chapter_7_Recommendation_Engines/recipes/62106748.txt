Title:
Cheery Cherry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Red Velvet Cake mix
1 small jar manadrin oranges, juice reserved
1 small jar marachino cherries, juice reserved
3 eggs
1/3 cup veg. oil
Icing:
1 8-oz carton Cool Whip
1 large sized store bought cream cheese icing
1 Tablespoon cherry juice, reserved.
6-8 mar. cherries, reserved

Directions:
Make cake according to pkg. directions for 2 8-in. round pans EXCEPT instead of water, use 1/2 the juice of mar. cherries, and 1/2 of man. oranges to equal 1 1/4 cup of juice. Mix cake mix, juice, eggs, oil all on low speed with hand mixer (pkg. directions). Using a blender or magic bullet, chop 1/2 man. oranges and 1/2 of the cherries, fold into the mixture. Bake according to pkg. Let cool.
Mix cream cheese icing and Cool Whip together with a hand mixer. Add 1 Tablespoon of cherry juice to icing. Makes a nice, light pink color!
Ice cooled cake. Decorate with reserved cherries on top.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
This is a delightful cake for summer! Couldn't be easier with the use of a red velvet cake mix and pre-made icing! People will ask you for the recipe but shhhhh! Don't give away it uses a box mix!
