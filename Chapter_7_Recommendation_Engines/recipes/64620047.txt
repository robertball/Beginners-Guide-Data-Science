Title:
Insta Pot Pepper Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4406471.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Chicken Thighs
1/2 cup Soy sauce
Pepper to taste
3 garlic cloves
2 Green Peppers
1 large yellow Onion
2 Tb honey
1/4 cup Brown sugar

Directions:
First, add frozen(or fresh)  chicken thighs in insta Pot On manual for 10 mins if using fresh, 25 for frozen.
Let pot depressurize naturally
Second,  set pot to saute, add all ingredients and cook until most liquid is out of the pot and the vegetables are soft. about 15 mins.
Serve over rice

Submitter: 
Carla Lybarger
Description: 
Received an Instant pot for Christmas used the ingredients had on hand, turned out to be one of our favorite meals!
