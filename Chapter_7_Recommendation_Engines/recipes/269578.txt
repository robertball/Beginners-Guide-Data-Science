Title:
Refresh & Reboot Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6106330.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup spinach
1/2 pear, chopped
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 cup Almond Breeze Original almondmilk

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
A tasty green smoothie that is sure to please. Get your fruit and veggie servings before heading out the door.
