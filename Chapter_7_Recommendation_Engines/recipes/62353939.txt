Title:
Pulled Buffalo Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pound boneless roast (pork, beef, buffalo)
1 onion, thinly sliced
2 cups barbeque sauce
3/4 cup salsa
3 Tablespoons chili powder
1 Tablespoon fajita seasoning
flour tortillas

Directions:
Remove excess fat from roast.  Place roast in slow cooker.  Arrange onion on top.  Mix remaining ingredients (except tortillas) and pour over roast.
Cover and cook on low 8 - 10 hours until roast is tender.
Remove roast onto cutting board.  Using 2 forks, pull roast apart into shreds.  Put meat back into sauce and stir until well mixed.
Spoon into tortillas and serve.
