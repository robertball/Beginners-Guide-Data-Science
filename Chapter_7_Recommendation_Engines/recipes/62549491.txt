Title:
Billy & Johnny's Killer Potato Latkes (Pancakes) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of peeled grated potatos
2 eggs beaten
2 Heaping Tablespoons of flour
1/2 teaspoon of salt
2 Tablespoons of pureed onion
Vegetable oil

Directions:
Using a food processor grate potatos.
Measuring out two cups of hard packed grated potatos, and using a clean dish towel, squeeze out as much moisture as possible.
Add potatos to a mixing bowl, along with all other ingredients.  Mix well with a sloted spoon.
Place spoon fulls of the potato mixture into a large skillet with hot vegetable oil making 3-1/2" round pancakes.
Cook until both sides of the latkes are golden brown.
Drain latkes on cookie sheet with paper towels.
Serve immediately with apple sauce and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
My brother and I have sponsored an annual Hannukah party every year for the past 15 years.  We serve up braised brisket and these wonderful potato latkes.
