Title:
MD Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound crab meat
8 ounces cream cheese softened
1/2 c sour cream
2 TB Mayo
1 TB lemon juice
1 Tea Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tea dry mustard
1/2 tea garlic salt
1 TB milk
1 TB Old Bay Seasoning
1 c cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease 1 quart casserole dish
Mix all ingredients except crab and cheddar cheese
Fold in crab meat
Place in casserole dish and top with cheddar
Bake for about 30 minutes until bubbly

Submitter: 
Lynn Mauk
Description: 
Blending local spices using Chesapeake Blue Crab lump to create a creamy hot crab dip
