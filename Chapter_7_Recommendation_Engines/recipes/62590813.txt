Title:
Ginger Yogurt Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. freshly grated ginger
1 cup plain low-fat yogurt
3 tbsp. honey
1/4 cup sliced toasted almonds (optional)
fruit of choice (excellent on pineapple)

Directions:
Place ginger in fine mesh sieve set over small bowl.
Press to release juice - it should yield about 1-2 tsp., and discard pulp.
Add yogurt and honey to ginger juice and mix to combine.
Use to top fruit and garnish with almonds if desired.
