Title:
Pasta Fontina w/ Lobster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large South African Lobster tails (steamed or broiled and pulled into bitesize pieces)
1 pound fettucine (uncooked)
16oz. soft fontina cheese (Shredded)
16oz. Heavy Cream
2 sticks melted butter
Salt and pepper to taste
OptionalIngredients
pinch Ground ceyanne
Pinch Ground Sage

Directions:
Shred Fontina in food processor.
Cook Pasta al dente.  Strain and return to pot.  Add melted butter to pasta and stir pasta until thoroughly coated with butter.  Add Fontina a handful at a time allowing it to melt between handfuls.  Do this until cheese is all in pot.  Add part of cream and stir.  keep adding cream until desired creaminess is acheived.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Optional spices should be added at this point as well.  Once all of this has been acheived top with lobster or whatever add-on you want.
For garnish a lemon wheel and a celantro sprig.
Enjoy!  Everyone deserves to eat like a rockefeller once in a while!

Submitter: 
Cincinnati Designer
Description: 
This Pasta dish is velvety and creamy unlike any alfredo.  It is an excellent base for a plethera of ad-ons like Shrimp, grilled chicken, Lobster, et. al.  Easy to make and oh so impressive a dish.
