Title:
Apple envelopes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 thinly sliced apples
white sugar
ground cinnamon
1 sheet of puff pastry
milk

Directions:
Cut puff pastry into small squares(not too small though).
Mix the sugar and cinnamon.
Arrange a few thin slices of apple on each puff pastry square. Cover each layer with the sugar and cinnamon.
Seal pastry envelopes and brush the top with milk.
Place in over preheated to 230 degrees C (450F).Bake for 10 minutes and the reduce to 174 degrees C (350F).
Bake for 10-15 minutes longer. Serve warm or cold.
