Title:
Peanut Butter Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
1/3 cup milk
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla flavoring
1 box confectioners sugar

Directions:
Soften butter to the point where you can squeeze it and it collaspes. Add milk, peanut butter and vanilla, mix well. Add confectioners sugar and mix until desired consistency.

Submitter: 
J Mayo
Description: 
A good icing for cupcakes. If you like peanut butter you will like this icing.
