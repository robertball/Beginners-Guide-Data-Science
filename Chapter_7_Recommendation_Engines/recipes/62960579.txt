Title:
Crockpot Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2122238.jpg
Ingredients:
3 frozen boneless chicken breasts
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can baked beans
1 can corn, drained
1 can black beans, drained
1 can kidney beans, drained
1 pkg taco seasoning

Directions:
Place chicken in crock pot.
Drain corn, black beans and kidney beans.  Add to crock pot along with tomatoes, baked beans and taco seasoning.
Mix well.
Cook on low all day.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
This tasty chicken chili could not be any easier!  I usually substitute Penzy's Chili 3000 (1/4 cup) for the taco seasoning.  My family loves it!
