Title:
Jan's Quick-N-Easy Yakisoba Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3160432.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp oil
1 14 0z bag of coleslaw mix
1 small bunch of bok choy cut into large pieces. About 2 cups.
2 packets Chicken flavor Ramen Noodles (flavor packets set aside)
1 large onion cut into wedge slices
2 cups cooked chicken or pork cut into thin slices
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 tbsp low sodium Worcestershire sauce
1 tbsp minced garlic
1/3 cup ketchup
1 tbsp Siracha sauce (depending on heat preference you might use less)
1 tbsp sugar
1 to 3 tbsp cornstarch

Directions:
In a small bowl place the ginger, soy sauce, Worcestershire, garlic, ketchup, Siracha, sugar and the seasoning packets from the Ramen packages. Whisk to combine. Set aside.
Place a pot of water on to boil. When boils, add Ramen and cook for approximately 3 minutes. Remove from heat.
Place oil in wok or large pan. Add coleslaw and onion. Stir fry until veggies are cooked but still crisp. About 5 minutes.
Add the sauce and pre cooked meat bring to a simmer. Cook for a minute or two until heated through.
Add Ramen and toss to coat.
If you want to thicken the sauce add some cold water to 1 tbsp of cornstarch. Blend well then add to the wok. stir. Repeat until desired consistency.
Serve immediately.

Description: 
An easy and affordable, version of Yakisoba using prepared coleslaw and Ramen noodles. So much better than take out!
