Title:
Sparkling Strawberry Punch  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen strawberries, thawed
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, undiluted
1 (2 liter) bottle ginger ale, or to taste, chilled
1 liter club soda, chilled
2 trays ice cubes
1/4 cup white sugar, or to taste

Directions:
Place a 6-quart punch bowl in the freezer to chill thoroughly while preparing the punch, at least 20 minutes.
Combine strawberries and lemonade concentrate in a blender. Blend on high speed until strawberries are pureed. Pour into the chilled punch bowl.
Add ginger ale, club soda, ice cubes, and sugar. Stir until sugar is completely dissolved.

Submitter: 
WENDOCAM
Description: 
This wonderful strawberry lemonade and ginger ale punch has been a family favorite for years! Always a big hit with both adults and children at any gathering, and very easy to make. Garnish with orange slices.
