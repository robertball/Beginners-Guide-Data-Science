Title:
Vampire Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups  cold water
2 cups  red wine
1pkt.  (makes 2 qt. drink) CRYSTAL LIGHT Lemonade Flavor Drink Mix*
1 navel orange, sliced
1 apple, sliced
1 lime, sliced
2 cups  cold club soda or 7up

Directions:
ADD water and wine to drink mix in 2-qt. plastic or glass pitcher; stir until mix is completely dissolved.
STIR in all remaining ingredients except club soda. Refrigerate several hours or until chilled.
ADD club soda just before serving; stir. Serve over ice.
