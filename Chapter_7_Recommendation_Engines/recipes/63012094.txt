Title:
Oreo Truffle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 bag of Oreo cookies crushed
2. 1 8 0z pkg cream cheese
3. 1 12 oz chocolate chips milk choc or semi sweet

Directions:
in Med size bowl mix 1. and 2.
Form walnut size balls and place on wax paper on cookie sheet
melt 3. on stove or microwave
dip balls in 3......place back on wax paper and chill
store in  the fridge till eaten
