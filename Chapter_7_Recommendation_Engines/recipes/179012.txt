Title:
Souper Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/144420.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken parts
1 tablespoon margarine or butter, melted
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)

Directions:
Place chicken in 2 quart shallow baking dish.  Drizzle with margarine.  Bake at 375 degrees F for 30 minutes.
Spoon soup over chicken and bake 30 minutes more or until chicken is no longer pink.  Remove chicken.  Stir sauce.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Baked chicken topped with a creamy sauce is always a family favorite. Serve with stuffing and green beans.
