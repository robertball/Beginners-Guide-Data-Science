Title:
Ice cream in a bag Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Reduced Fat Milk
1/2 cup Heavy Cream
1/4 cup Sugar
3/4 tsp Vanilla Extract
1/2 cup Coarse Salt
Lots of Ice

Directions:
In a pint sized ziploc bag, pour in milk, cream, and vanilla. Add sugar. Close the bag, making sure all air is squeezed out. Secure with masking tape.
Fill a gallon sized ziploc bag about a third full with ice. Place the pint sized bag inside. Add some more ice. Then, the rest of the salt. Top with more ice until bag is full. Close bag.
Shake bag for a full 10 minutes. Cut a corner off the pint sized bag. Squeeze into two bowls & enjoy.
