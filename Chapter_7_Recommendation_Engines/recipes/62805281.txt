Title:
Roquefort Pear Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 (10 oz.) bags mixed greens
3 pears - peeled, cored and chopped, preferably Bosc
5 ounces Roquefort blue cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
1/2 cup white sugar
1 cup pecans
1-2 Tbsp. water
2/3 cup olive oil
6 tablespoons red wine vinegar
3 teaspoons white sugar
3 teaspoons prepared dijon mustard
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 teaspoon salt
fresh ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a skillet over medium heat, stir 1/2 cup of sugar & 1-2 Tbsp. water together with the pecans. Continue stirring gently until sugar has melted and caramelized the pecans. Carefully transfer nuts onto waxed paper. Allow to cool, and break into pieces.
For the dressing, blend oil, vinegar, 3 teaspoons sugar, mustard, chopped garlic, salt, and pepper.
In a large serving bowl, layer lettuce, pears, blue cheese, and green onions. Pour dressing over salad, sprinkle with pecans, and serve.
