Title:
Sweet Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 of a large sweet onion, finely chopped
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup Parmesan shreds
Pinch of cayenne (to personal taste)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix all ingredients in small bowl.  Put in oven-to-table heat proof casserole.  Top with a couple of extra tablespoons parmesan shreds.  Bake until bubbly hot.  Serve with baguette slices, apple slices, carrots.
Buen Provecho!

Submitter: 
BAJATHECAT
Description: 
This onion dip features sweet onions such as Vidalia, Maui or Walla Walla.  I have used this recipe for years as a quick, easy and always popular appetizer.  I often use non-fat mayonnaise and no one has ever noticed.
