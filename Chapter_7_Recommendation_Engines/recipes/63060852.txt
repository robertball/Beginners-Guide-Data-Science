Title:
Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123177.jpg
Ingredients:
1# ground beef, browned
1/4-1/2 # bacon, cooked
1/4 c. chopped onion
1 tsp. mustard
1 T. vinegar
3/4 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. ketchup
1# can pork & beans
1 can lima beans (baby) (drain)
1 can kidney beans (drain)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a casserole dish.
Bake @ 350 for 45 minutes.
