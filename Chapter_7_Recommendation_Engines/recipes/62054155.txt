Title:
Scallops  Linguini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound package linguini, cooked
1/4 teaspoon Rosemary
1/4 teaspoon oregano
1/4 teaspoon ground sage
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 teaspoon parsley
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 cup chicken stock
1 small onion
1 pound sea scallops
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook linguini according to package, or until al dente.  Heat oil and butter in pan over medium heat.  Add onion and sautee about 3 minutes, or until nearly translucent.  Add all spices, then the scallops.  Simmer for about 3 minutes, or until the scallops are seared on the outside.  Add lemon juice and chicken stock.  Let cook 10 minutes, or until the scallops are opaque.  Serve over linguini.

Submitter: 
Irlandes
Description: 
This is a very quick meal, the flavors and juices are sopped up by the linguini for a delightful sauce!
