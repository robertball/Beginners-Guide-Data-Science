Title:
split pea soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. dried split peas
6 cups water/broth
1/2 tsp. smoked paprika, thyme, and tarragon
1 tsp. dried chipotle pepper
1 onion, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
3 celery stalks,chopped
salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
Soak peas for several hours. Add herbs. Simmer, partially covered, till mostly done.
Meanwhile, saut√© onions, carrots and celery in oil till soft and tender, about 10 minutes. Add to peas, season with salt and pepper, and continue cooking till peas dissolve (use pressure cooker if needed).  Add more water/broth as needed to desired consistency.
