Title:
Slow Cooker Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/916437.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
salt and garlic pepper to taste
2 pounds 1 inch thick tougher cut steak
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
6 carrots
2 (14.5 ounce) cans Italian stewed tomatoes
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp beef base
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Salt, and pepper the steak pieces. Lightly coat the round with flour.
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat and brown the meat.  Saute onions if you want.
Place carrots in a slow cooker and top with steak and then remaining ingredients.
Cover, and cook 8 to 10 hours on Low, until the round steak is very tender.
