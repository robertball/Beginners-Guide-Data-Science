Title:
SALTED CARAMEL FRAPPUCCINO Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Silk Caramel Almond Creamer2 cups ice cubes1 cup dairy free vanilla ice cream or frozen yogurt3 tablespoons salted caramel flavored syrup teaspoon vanilla  Couponsoptional: whipping cream, caramel ice cream topping

Directions:
Combine creamer, cubes, ice cream or frozen yogurt, caramel syrup, and vanilla. Blend until smooth.Pour frappuccino into two glasses, top with whipping cream and caramel sauce and serve immediately.
