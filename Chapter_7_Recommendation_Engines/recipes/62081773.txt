Title:
Mexican Stuffed Shells with Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 jars salsa
shredded Mozerella or Cheddar cheese
sour cream
tortilla chip1 box Stuffing Shells
about 5 lb. Hamburger
16 oz. (2 packages) cream cheese
chili powder

Directions:
prepare noodles per box directions
while noodles are boiling brown hamburger and chili powder
add cream cheese to hamburger and mix well.
When noodles are ready stuff them with Hamburger mixture and put into cake pan in single layer any size pan but the smaller the pan the more pans you will need
when shells are stuffed cover with salsa and cheese
bake at 350 degrees until cheese is melted remove from oven
top with crused tortilla chips and sour cream serve
