Title:
Oma's soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 6 cups water
2 turkey thighs - cut up
2 bay leaves
2 TB barley - fine
salt to taste
2 stalks celery - chopped
1 medium onion - chopped
3 cloves garlic - chopped
2 carrots - shredded
2 small beets - shredded
1 bunch fresh dill - tied
1 small head of cabbage - shredded (not too fine)
3 small potatoes - peeled - quartered
1 can Italian tomato paste
1 small onion - chopped fine
olive oil
flour
1 26 oz. jar of tomato sauce - meat flavored
1 TB soy sauce
1 TB Worcestershire sauce
2 cans pork and beans

Directions:
1. In large stockpot boil water; add turkey, bay leaves, salt and barley.
Cook for about 45 minutes.
2. Add onion, celery and garlic. Bring back to a boil.
3. Add carrots, beets and dill. Bring back to boil.
4. Add cabbage and potatoes. Bring back to boil.  Simmer 30 minutes.  Taste and adjust salt if needed.
5. In small saucepan, make a roux with olive oil, finely chopped onion and flour.  Brown onions in olive oil before adding flour.  Add tomato paste to roux and cook another 5 minutes.  Thin with soup stock if too thick.
6. Take meat out of stockpot and cool. Remove dill from stockpot.
7. Add tomato paste and tomato sauce to stockpot.
8. Add soy sauce and worcestershire sauce.
9. Add 2 cans of pork and beans.
10. Remove meat from bones and cut into bite size pieces.
11.  Add meat back to pot, simmer and taste to adjust seasoning if needed.
12. Sit back and enjoy - preferrably with a slice of rye bread!

Submitter: 
njmom
Description: 
Kind of a borscht, with a lot of common ingredients you would have on hand.
Great for cold nights.  Tastes better the next day.
