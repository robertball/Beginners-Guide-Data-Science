Title:
Big Ray's Baked Chicken and Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3608384.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 tsp salt or to taste
1/2 tsp sugar
3 heavy dashes ground black pepper
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
6 medium-sized potatoes,  cut into 1/4 inch pieces
6 boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into cubes
4 Tbsp unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
1  cup shredded cheddar cheese
4 green onions, chopped

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a 7x11 baking pan with some oil or butter.
Add the salt, sugar, and pepper to the heavy whipping cream. Lightly stir to combine well.
Spread the potatoes, follow by the chicken, in one single layer. Sprinkle the  butter, and then top with half of the cheddar cheese and the green onions. Pour the heavy cream over top of casserole. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 1 hour. Uncover the pan and bake for another 30 minutes. In the last 10 minutes, sprinkle with the remaining cheddar cheese and bake until the cheese is slightly crusty. Remove from the oven and let sit 10 minutes before serving..
