Title:
Deconstructed S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package giant marshmallows
1 package milk chocolate chips
2 cups graham cracker crumbs

Directions:
Stick a skewer into each marshmallow
Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave for 1 minute or until melted. Check on it every 30 seconds
Put the chocolate, and graham cracker crumbs in 2 bowls
Dip each marshmallow into the chocolate, the coat it with the graham cracker.
Repeat.
Let the marshmallows set, and it can be served as is, or you can warm it over a fire like a s'more.

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
This is a treat that my whole family looks forward to during camping trips, or the fourth of July, or just any day.
