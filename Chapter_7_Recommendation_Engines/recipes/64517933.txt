Title:
Broccoli & Bacon Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Qt half & half
2 ea deep dish pie crusts
6 eggs
1 head broccoli
1/2lb white cheese (mozzarella, swiss, etc)
4 drops tobasco
1 t. onion powder
3/4lb bacon

Directions:
Dice bacon and fry until crispy
While bacon is frying cook pie crusts per package directions
Mix eggs, half & half, onion tobasco, onion powder in bowl
spread bacon, cheese, & broccoli in cooked pie crusts then cover with egg mixture
bake in over at 300 degrees for 45 minutes
