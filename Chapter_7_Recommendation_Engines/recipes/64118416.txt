Title:
Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1/2 cup peanut butter
* 3 Tablespoons margarine or butter, softened
* 1 cup sifted powdered sugar
* 8 ounces chocolate flavored confectioners coating.

Directions:
1)Stir together peanut butter and margarine. Gradually add sugar, stirring until combined. Shape into 1-inch balls; place on waxed paper. Let stand till dry (about 20 minutes). Melt confectioners coating. Dip balls into melted coating. Let excess drip off. Place on waxed paper; let stand till coating is dry.
