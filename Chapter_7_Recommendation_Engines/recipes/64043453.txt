Title:
Roasted Corn and Zuchinni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Ears fresh roasted or grilled corn
2 Zucchini
1 tablespoon cumin
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Heat butter over medium high heat in frying pan
Clean and chop zucchini lengthwise in half, then slice.
Cook zucchini stirring occasionally until there is some light browning on zucchini but it is still holding together
Add corn, cumin, chili powder and salt to taste
Serve hot

Submitter: 
Khatche
Description: 
The flavor of corn, zucchini, and cumin work well together and provide a healthy side.
