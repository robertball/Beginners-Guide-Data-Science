Title:
Epicure's Hot Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1 cup mayo
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
3 Tbps. Epicure seasoning for Spinach dip
1-10 oz. pkg. frozen spinach, thawed, chopped and drained
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Combine first 5 ingredients and spoon into dip baker. Sprinkle with parmesan.
Bake for 25 minutes, or until bubbling. Serve hot.
