Title:
Raspberry Lemon Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C. Lemon-Lime Soda
3/4 C. Sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
12 oz. Fresh Cranberries
1 C. Fresh Raspberries
Zest form 1 Lemon
Juice of 1/2 Lemon

Directions:
Bring soda, sugar and salt to a boil in saucepan over medium heat.
Add cranberries and simmer for about 5 minutes until slightly thickened and about 2/3 of cranberries have popped.
Transfer to serving bowl and add raspberries, zest and lemon juice.
Chill at least 1 hour & serve.
