Title:
Texas Barley Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup barley
3 cups water
2 Tbsp lime juice
2 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups black beans (or 1 15 oz can, drained)
1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
1 avocado, diced
1/2 cup corn kernals
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1/4 cup sliced green onions

Directions:
Bring water and 1/2 tsp salt to a boil in medium saucepan.  Add barley, cover and simmer until al dente (check at 20 minute).  Drain, if necessary and cool completely.
Meanwhile, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, cumin, and 1/4 tsp salt.  Toss in rest of ingredients, including barley.  Serve immediately.
