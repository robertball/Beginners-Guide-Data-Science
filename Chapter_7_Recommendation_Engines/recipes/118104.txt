Title:
Honey Butter Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/837635.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (no substitutes), softened
1/2 cup honey

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, beat the butter until light and fluffy. Add the honey; beat just until blended. Serve with bread, bagels or toast. Store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Ruth  Hastings
Description: 
Honey lovers will gobble up this spread in a snap! In addition to bread and bagels, try serving it alongside warm biscuits or pancakes.                                                                --Ruth Hastings
