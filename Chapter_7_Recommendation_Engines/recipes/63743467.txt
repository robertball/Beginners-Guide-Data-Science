Title:
Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups oil
3 jars baby food carrots
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
2 1/2 cups flour
Icing:
1 package cream cheese softened
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups icing sugar
1/2 cup marg or butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Mix all cake ingredients and pour into greased or sprayed baking pan (9 x 13)
Bake about 45-50 minutes until golden
Let cake cool and apply icing
