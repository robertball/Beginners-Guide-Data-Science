Title:
Jimmy Dean Hot Style Sausage Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976620.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package Jimmy Dean Hot Roll Sausage (cooked, crumbled, and drained)
3 whole eggs
3/4 cup milk
3 cups ricotta cheese
3 tablespoons basil, roughly chopped
2 cups mozzarella, shredded
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper, ground
16 halves lasagna noodles, precooked and cut in half
3 cups marinara sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Mix eggs, milk, ricotta cheese, basil, and mozzarella cheese in a bowl. Add salt and pepper to the bowl and mix very well.
Place1/2 Cup of the marinara sauce into the bottom of an 8 x 8 baking dish. Line the bottom of the baking dish with 4 lasagna noodles. Place 1/3 of the amount of ricotta filling across the first layer of noodles. Follow the cheese mixture with 1/3 of the amount of sausage, sprinkled evenly across the ricotta layer. Add 1/2 Cup of marinara sauce on top of the sausage layer; be sure to spread it evenly. Repeat the layering process until the remaining noodles are on the top. Place 1 Cup of marinara sauce across the top of the noodles.
Bake in oven for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jimmy Dean
Description: 
Hot-style sausage lends a spicy kick to this tasty lasagna.
