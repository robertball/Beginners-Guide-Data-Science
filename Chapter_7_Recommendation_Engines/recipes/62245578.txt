Title:
Mushroom Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
salt and pepper to taste
olive oil
1 clove garlic
1 onion, chopped
3/4 pound fresh mushrooms
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of golden mushroom soup
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp fresh tarragon

Directions:
Season pork chops with salt, pepper, and garlic salt to taste.
In a large skillet, brown the chops in olive oil over medium-high heat and remove to a plate. Saute the garlic, onion and mushrooms, and return pork chops to pan when done. Mix soup, milk and white wine and pour mixture over chops. Cover skillet, and reduce temperature to medium-low. Simmer 20 to 30 minutes, or until chops are cooked through.

Submitter: 
Crystal S
Description: 
Transform cream of mushroom soup into a delicious simmering sauce by tossing in some chopped onion and sliced fresh mushrooms. Add to seasoned and sauteed pork chops and you have a rich and creamy one-pan meal.Modified from: Mushroom Pork Chops
