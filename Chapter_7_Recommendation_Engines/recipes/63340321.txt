Title:
Buttercream Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup butter, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 cups confectioners' sugar
3 to 4 tablespoons milk
3 tablespoons coconut rum

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, and vanilla. Blend in the sugar, beating well after each addition. Beat in the milk, and continue mixing until light and fluffy. Keep icing covered until ready to decorate.

Submitter: 
Kelly
Description: 
An easy to make egg-free frosting that works great for decorating cookies for holidays, birthdays and parties.Modified from: Buttercream Icing
