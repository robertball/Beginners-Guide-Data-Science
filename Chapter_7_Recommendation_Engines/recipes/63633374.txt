Title:
Robert's Texas Sourdough Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs Instant Yeast
1 cup warm water
2/3 cup oil
2 cups buttermilk
5 cups self-rising flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 1/2 tsp sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together well.
Store in refrigerator at least 24 hours.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Take out of refrigerator and scoop needed amount into muffin tins.
Bake for 20-25 minutes.
The dough will keep in the refrigerator for up to six weeks.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
These rolls/biscuits were included in a hand-written cookbook that my wife's hairdresser gave us for a wedding gift! One of many treasured recipes from Robert!
