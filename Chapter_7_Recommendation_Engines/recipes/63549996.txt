Title:
Dianna's Slap Your Ma-ma Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969802.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons bacon fat
3 slices bacon, cut into pieces
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 Ham hock
2 cups chicken broth
pinch red pepper flakes
1 pound fresh collard greens, cut
2 cups water

Directions:
Heat bacon fat in a large pot over medium-high heat.
Add bacon, and cook until crisp.
Remove bacon from pan.
Add onion, and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
Add garlic, and cook until just fragrant, about 30 seconds.
Add collard greens, and fry until they start to wilt.
Pour in chicken broth, and season with salt, pepper, red pepper flakes and bacon.
Add water and cover; cook for 45 min -1 hours, or until greens are tender.

Collard greens full of flavor with a little kick.
