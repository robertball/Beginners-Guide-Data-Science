Title:
Green Curry Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 green chilies
3 T chopped shallots
1 T chopped garlic
1 t galangal
1 T chopped lemon grass
1/2 t chopped kaffir lime rind
1 t chopped coriander root
5 pepper corns
1 T coriander seeds
1 t cumin seeds
1 t salt
1 t shrimp paste

Directions:
Toast cumin seeds and coriander for 5 min. Then gind into a powder.
Put all ingredients except shrimp paste and blend to mix. Add shrimp until a fine paste.
Store in glass for 4 months.
