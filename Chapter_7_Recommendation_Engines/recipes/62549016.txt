Title:
Connie's Chicken Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken
1/2 stick butter
1/2 onion, chopped
1/3 package Velveeta
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can Rotel
1 can milk
12 oz. spaghetti
shredded cheddar

Directions:
Boil the chicken in a large stock pot and de-bone.
Use the water to boil the spaghetti.
Saute the onion in the butter and add the Velveeta.; stir until melted.
Add the soup, Rotel, and milk.
Heat thoroughly.
Combine all ingredients in a 9Ã—13 casserole dish.
Top with shredded cheddar and heat in 350 oven until hot and bubbly.
