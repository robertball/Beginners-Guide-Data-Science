Title:
Five Minute - Double Layer Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983045.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups cold milk
2 (3.9 ounce) packages chocolate flavor instant pudding & pie filling
1 (8 ounce) tub frozen non-dairy whipped topping, thawed, divided
1 Keebler速 Ready Crust速 Graham Pie Crust

Directions:
In large bowl beat milk and pudding mix with wire whisk for 1 minute. (Mixture will be very thick.) Whisk in half of whipped topping. Carefully spread in crust.
Spread remaining whipped topping over top.
Garnish as desired. Enjoy immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.

Description: 
A fluffy chocolate layer is topped with a layer of whipped topping in this fabulous chocolate pie that is ready almost instantly.
