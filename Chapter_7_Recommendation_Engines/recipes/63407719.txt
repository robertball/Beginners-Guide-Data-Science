Title:
Breakfast Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bacon, cooked to crispy
1 cup shredded cheese
2 cups milk
1 cup Bisquick mix
4 eggs
1/4 tsp. Salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 400.  Crumble bacon into bottom of greased deep dish pie plate.  Mix remaining ingredients until smooth and pour this mixture over the bacon.  Bake 35 to 40 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Karen Zastrow
Description: 
An easy breakfast pie that is hearty enough for brunch!
