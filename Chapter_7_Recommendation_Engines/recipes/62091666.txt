Title:
Fresh Apricot Compote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 fresh apricots
1 lemon
3/4 cup sugar
6 cups water
2 drops vanilla essence.

Directions:
Halve apricots and remove stones.
Use a potato peeler or knife to cut strips of lemon peel.
Place apricots, lemon peel strips, sugar, water and vanilla essence in saucepan.
Bring to boil and simmer very gently for 40-50 minutes.
Cool and refrigerate until served.

Submitter: 
Shelley
Description: 
An amazingly simple but delicious summer fruits dessert. It also freezes well so you can enjoy the taste of summer all year round. Serve with vanilla ice cream or sour cream. Also works well with plums.
