Title:
Garlic Spaghetti Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 spaghetti squash
10 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup olive or vegetable oil (or less)
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
optional-throw in chopped red peppers, mushrooms,onions, etc...

Directions:
Cook spaghetti squash and shred from shell. Meanwhile, in a skillet over low heat, cook garlic in oil until lightly browned and add squash,parsley, oregano, salt and pepper. Remove from heat add Parmesan cheese; toss to coat.
