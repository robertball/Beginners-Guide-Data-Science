Title:
The Simple Sauza® Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977703.jpg
Ingredients:
7 1/2 ounces Sauza ® Silver Tequila
4 1/2 ounces DeKuyper® Triple Sec
24 ounces Margarita Mix
GARNISH
Lime Wedge
Orange Wedge

Directions:
Combine the ingredients without ice and shake vigorously. Then just pour the mixture into your most festive margarita glass, garnish and enjoy a classic.

Submitter: 
Sauza
Description: 
The classic margarita, made with flowery silver tequila, triple sec, and margarita mix, is colorfully garnished with lime and orange wedges.
