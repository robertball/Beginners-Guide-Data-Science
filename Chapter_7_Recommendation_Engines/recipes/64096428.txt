Title:
Big Ray's Chicken Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1212448.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups elbow macaroni, cooked
3 cups shredded cooked chicken
1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1/2 cup cottage cheese
1 tsp granulated onion
1 tsp granulated garlic
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded Swiss cheese
1 package (10 ounces) frozen creamed spinach, thawed
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup loosely packed minced fresh basil
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
2 Tbl butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.
In a large bowl, combine chicken, soup, cheese, spinach, mayonnaise, basil, garlic, onion, thyme, cottage cheese and pepper.
Gently stir macaroni into chicken mixture. Transfer to an ungreased 2-1/2-qt. baking dish. Toss bread crumbs and butter; sprinkle over casserole.
Bake, uncovered, 40-45 minutes or until bubbly.
