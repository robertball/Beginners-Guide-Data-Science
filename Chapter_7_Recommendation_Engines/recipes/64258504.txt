Title:
Coconut Greek Yogurt Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1811120.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust
1 1/2 cup sweetened coconut
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup flour
1 egg white
Cake
2 cups Coconut flavored Greek yogurt
1/3 cup sugar
pinch of salt
2 eggs
2 teaspoon coconut extract
1 Tablespoon cornstarch

Directions:
for the crust preheat oven to 350 and grease 10 inch spring form pan
combine coconut, sugar, and flour in bowl
whisk egg white with fork until frothy and mix into dry ingredients
press into bottom of pan
Bake for 18 mins, and cool before adding filling
For the filling blend together the yogurt, sugar, eggs, and coconut extract till nice and smooth
add pinch of salt and cornstarch and blend again.
pour over cooled coconut crust, and return to 350 degree oven
bake for 35 mins till slightly jiggly in the middle. The edges should start pulling away from pan.
Chill for 3 hours in fridge and serve

Submitter:

Description: 
I LOVE cheesecakes, but sometimes feel guilty about eating it..or even thinking about it. So i decided to replace the cream cheese with Greek yogurt, went with Coconut because its one of my favorite flavors.This one doesn't make me feel as guilty to eat.
