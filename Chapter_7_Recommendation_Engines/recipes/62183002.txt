Title:
Pumpkin Angel Food Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Angel Food cake mix
1-15 oz. can Pumpkin
1 tsp. Pumpkin Pie Spice
1 cup Water

Directions:
Directions: Mix the dry angel food cake mix with the remaining ingredients. Pour into a greased 9" x13" pan. Bake at 350F for 25 minutes.
