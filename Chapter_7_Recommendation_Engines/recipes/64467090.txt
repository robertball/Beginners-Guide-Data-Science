Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2445553.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sheet puff pastry, thawed according to package directions and cut into 1.5 inch squares.
1 cut fresh kale, chopped fine
1 package frozen spinach thawed and squeezed of its water
1 clove garlic, crushed or minced
1 tablespoon butter
salt and pepper
1/2 cup feta, crumbled fine
1 egg, well beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Grease or spray mini muffin pan.
Sautee kale, spinach, and garlic in butter.
Season with salt and pepper.
Remove from heat and mix in bowl with egg and feta.
Gently stretch pastry square a bit.
Place 1/2 teaspoon kale mix in center.
Place a second square on top and fold up corners, seal edges.
Place in muffin tin.
Bake until golden brown.
