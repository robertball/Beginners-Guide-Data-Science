Title:
Creamy Slow Cooker Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
one 10-oz. can cream of chicken soup
one 8-oz. carton sour cream
2 lbs. potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 lg onion chopped
one 8-oz. pkg. cheddar-jack cheese, shredded
1 C. potato chips, crushed
Directions

Directions:
In a bowl, combine soup, sour cream, a pinch of salt and pepper, and 1/4 cup water. Combine potatoes and cheese in a 5-quart slow cooker.  Spoon soup-sour cream mixture over potatoes. Cover and cook on low for 8-9 hours.  When ready to serve, sprinkle crushed potato chips over potatoes.
