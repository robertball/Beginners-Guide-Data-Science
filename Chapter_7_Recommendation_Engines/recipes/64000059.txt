Title:
Jell-O Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 white cake mix
* 2 pkg's. Jell-O (1 red, 1 green)
* 1 container cool whip

Directions:
Using 2 round cake pans, bake cake as instructed on box. When done cool at room temperature. Use toothpick or fork to punch holes in cake. Make Jell-O as instructed on box. Pour liquid Jell-O into cake (there will be Jell-O left over, freeze the rest for a snack). One cake will be red and one will be green. Put in refrigerator for at least 1 hour. Take cake out of pans. Using cool whip cover one cake and layer the other cake on top. Garnish with red and green sprinkles.
