Title:
Wine and Lemon Ranch Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6412576.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Thick cut center pork chops
Hidden Valley Ranch Seasoning and Dressing Mix
Butter and olive oil (enough to brown the chops.
Juice from one Lemon or lime (about 3 TBLS.)
3 TBLS. Marsala wine (We prefer sweet Marsala)
1/4 - 1/2 C. Chicken Broth

Directions:
Sprinkle the pork chops with Hidden Valley Seasoning
Brown on medium high heat in butter and olive
Lower heat and finish cooking
Remove from pan to a warm platter
Spoon or pour off all but one TBLS. of the fat in the pan
Deglaze the pan using wine, juice and broth
Simmer the sauce to reduce it to desired thickness
Spoon over the chops and serve.
