Title:
Prejean's Crawfish and Crab Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter                                    1/2 tablespoon Granulated onion
1 1/2 cups diced onion
1/4 pound crawfish tail meat, loosely chopped
1/4 cup diced bell pepper
1/4 cup concentrated Crab Stock
1 tablespoon finely minced garlic
10 cups unseasoned bread crumbs
1 pinch cayenne pepper
1/4 pound crab claw meat, no shell
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 tablespoon granulated garlic

Directions:
In a 4 quart pot, heat butter until hot. Add onion, bell pepper, celery and minced garlic and saute until onion starts to caramelize. Add cayenne pepper, black pepper, granulated garlic, granulated onion and crawfish. Saute 5 min
Add stock and stir until incorporated. Reduce heat to low and simmer 5 min. Remove from heat. Fold in bread crumbs and crab & mix well.
Recipe makes 10 cups. Use this stuffing mushrooms, fish, steak or vegetables.
.

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
This is a great Cajun seafood stuffing for fish, mushrooms, shrimp or vegetables.  It just makes everything better.
