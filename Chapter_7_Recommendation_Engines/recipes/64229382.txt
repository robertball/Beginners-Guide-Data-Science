Title:
Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb Italian sausage, spicy hot links
1/2 lb ground beef
1 onion, small chopped
24 oz tomato sauce
6 oz tomato paste
1 tsp sugar
Black pepper to taste
Salt
Basil
Water
8 large manicotti shells (12-14 canelonni shells)
2 cups ricotta cheese (500 gm)
12 oz Mozerella cheese, grated (300 gm)
1 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (or 1 tsp parsley flakes)
Grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In large frypan, cook Italian sausage in 1/8 cup water in covered saucepan for 5 minutes.  Uncover, brown well, and drain on paper towels.  Cut into bite size pieces and set aside.
In large saucepan, brown ground beef and onion.  Stir in tomato puree, tomato paste, sugar, pepper, 1 tsp basil, 1/2 tsp salt and 1/2 cup water.  Simmer, covered, for 45 minutes.  Add sausage to mixture and cook 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Meanwhile, cook manicotti according to directions and drain.
In large bowl combine ricotta and mozerella cheeses, parsley, 1 tsp basil and 1/4 tsp salt.  Stuff into shells.
Spoon half of the meat sauce into baking dish.  Place shells in baking dish.  Top with remaining sauce.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.  (can use square casserole and do 2 layers of shells).
