Title:
Sunshine Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984922.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Red Cabbage, shredded
2 cups Green Cabbage, shredded
1/2 cup Carrots, shredded
1 Granny Smith Apple, cored and sliced (Do not Peel)
1 cup seedless Raisins, golden or dark, or combination
2/3 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing

Directions:
In mixing bowl, combine cabbage, carrots, apple and raisins.  Pour Marzetti(R) Slaw Dressing over and toss together lightly.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
You can use just red, just green or a combination of both cabbages for this tasty fruit salad.
