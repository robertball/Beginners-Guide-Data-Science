Title:
Arabic Rice with Almonds and Raisins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/526762.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup basmati rice
1 3/4 cup water
2 whole cardamon seeds
1 cinnamon stick
1 teaspoon Arabic seven spice blend
2 whole cloves
dash salt
2 teaspoons canola oil
1 small onion, thinly siced
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 teaspoon sugar
1 tablespoon salted butter
1/4 cup slivered almonds
1/4 cup golden raisins

Directions:
Combine rice, water, cardamon seeds, cinnamon stick, seven spice blend, cloves, salt and canola oil in heavy bottomed saucepan.
Bring rice to boil, cover, reduce heat to low and simmer 18 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Remove cardamon seeds, cinnamon stick and cloves.
While rice is cooking heat olive oil on medium heat.  Add onion and sugar and cook until onions are caramelized, about 18 minutes.  Remove onions and set aside.
In skillet melt butter over medium heat.  Add almonds and cook unti slightly toasted, stirring frequently to prevent burning.  Add raisins and heat through.
Add onions and raisin almond mixture to rice.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
Seasoned basmati rice sweetened with caramelized onions, plump raisins, and crunchy almonds.
