Title:
Cajun Garlic Jalapenos Poppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/860523.jpg
Ingredients:
20 Small jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese (Softened)
Sprinkle Cajun Seasoning (To Taste)
Sprinkle Garlic Powder (To Taste)
1 package of bacon, cut in half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
Stuff each jalapeno half with cream cheese.
Sprinkle each half with Cajun Seasoning and Garlic Powder. (To Taste - Pinch of each)
Wrap a half slice of bacon around each stuffed jalapeno half.
Arrange the jalapeno halves on large baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven 30-45 minutes, or until bacon is browned and/ or crispy.
