Title:
French Dip Crescents Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 count pkg crescent rolls
1/2 deli roast beef, thinly sliced
4 slices Swiss or provolone cheese, halved
Au Jus sauce
2 tbsp finely chopped red onion
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp minced garlic
2 tbsp white wine
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 c beef broth
1 tsp flour

Directions:
Prepare Au Jus first...
Caramelize onions in oil, add garlic and saut√© 1-2 minutes more.
Deglaze with wine, then Worcestershire sauce, and cook another minute, stirring constantly.
Add broth and bring to light boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
Strain broth, return to pot, add flour and simmer additional 5.minutes.
Prepare sandwiches
Unroll crescents, and place a slice of roast beef and a slice of cheese on each crescent.
Roll crescent back up staring with wide end.
Bake at 375 degrees for 11-13 minutes, until a golden brown color.
Serve with AuJus.
