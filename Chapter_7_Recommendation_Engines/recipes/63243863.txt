Title:
South of the Border Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Sour Cream
1/3 Cup Ricotta Cheese
1 package (3oz) Cream Cheese Softened
3 Cups coarsely chopped cooked chicken
3 Cups rice, cooked in Chicken Broth
11/2 cups shredded Monterrey Jack Cheese(Hot)
1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup(10 3/4 oz)
1 Can chopped Green Chiles, drained
1/8 tsp Garlic Powder
1 Cup Crushed Corn Chips

Directions:
Blend together sour cream, ricotta cheese and cream cheese until smooth.
Add remaining ingredients, except corn chips, to cheese mixture.
Pour into ungreased shallow 3 quart baking dish.
Sprinkle with corn chips.
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 25-30 minutes or until heated through.
