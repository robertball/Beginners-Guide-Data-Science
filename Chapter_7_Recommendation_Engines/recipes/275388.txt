Title:
Bacon Buffalo Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6980870.jpg
Ingredients:
1 frozen cauliflower pizza crust
1/2 cup ranch salad dressing (such as Hidden Valley® Original Ranch®)
2 cups shredded cooked chicken
1/2 cup Buffalo wing sauce (such as Frank's®)
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
3 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
3 green onions, chopped
1/3 cup crumbled blue cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Place cauliflower pizza crust onto a baking sheet or baking steel. Spread ranch dressing evenly over the crust.
Combine shredded chicken and Buffalo sauce in a small bowl; mix to combine, and place on top of dressing. Top with shredded Cheddar cheese, bacon, and green onions. Add blue cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown, 12 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
The ultimate party foods, pizza and Buffalo chicken wings, combine to make the best of both worlds. Bacon adds an extra level of flavor. Easier and less mess, this is always a welcomed addition to our dinner table.
