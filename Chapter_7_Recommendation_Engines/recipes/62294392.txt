Title:
Turkey Soft Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/360217.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 pound lean ground turkey
2 cloves minced garlic
1/2 cup frozen corn
2 T. Taco Seasoning (we prefer Taco Seasoning I from this site)
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup water
Tortillas
one bunch freshly chopped cilantro
1 small tomato, diced
crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add ground turkey and onion,cook until turkey is browned and onions are soft. Add garlic. Then stir in taco seasoning, corn and 1/4 cup of water. Continue cooking until corn is tender. Fill tortillas with meat mixture and top with cilantro, tomato and a sprinkle of feta cheese.

Submitter: 
Jillian Kuhlmann
Description: 
This is a healthy family favorite! Lean ground turkey meat with corn is seasoned with Taco Seasoning I,onion and garlic. Put in a tortilla and topped off with cilantro and feta cheese!
