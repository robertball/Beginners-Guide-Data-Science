Title:
Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 tsp. baking soda
1 egg
1/2 cup chocolate chips
1 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients till crumbly.
Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
Bake in greased muffin tin @ 375 x 15 - 20 minutes, or until tests done with toothpick.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
My daughter liked these! The buttermilk makes them extra delicious :)
