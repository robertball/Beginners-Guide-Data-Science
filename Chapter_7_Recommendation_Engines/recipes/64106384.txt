Title:
Classic green bean bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp soy sauce
generous dash pepper
4 cups frozen green beans
80g french fried onion

Directions:
In 1.5 quart casserole dish, combine soup, milk, soy sauce and pepper. Stir in beans and 1/2 the onions.
Bake at 350ยบ for 25 minutes or until hot and bubbling.
Stir bean mixture. Top with remaining onion.s Bake 5 minutes more.
