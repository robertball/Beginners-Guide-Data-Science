Title:
Vanilla Ice Cream Sandwiches vs 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
2   eggs
1   teaspoon vanilla extract
1   quart ice cream
Assorted toppings, including chopped nuts, jimmies, sprinkles etc.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325. Spray pan with vegetable spray
In a small bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt. In large microwave-safe bowl, melt butter. Whisk in sugars, eggs and vanilla: mix well. Add flour mixture; mix until blended. Spoon 2 tablespoons of batter into each pan cavity, spreading evenly.
Bake 8-10 minutes or until tooth pick inserted at an angle toward center comes out clean. Immediately unmold cookies onto cooling rack; cool completely. Repeat with remaining batter.
Scoop about 1/4 cup ice cream onto smooth side of half of cookies. Top with remaining cookies, pressing gently. If desired, roll edge of sandwiches in toppings. Wrap and freeze immediately.
Makes about 6 ice cream sandwiches.
Variations: Add flour to cookie batter by adding 1 tsp lemon extract, 1 tsp orange extract or 1 tsp ground cinnamon.
