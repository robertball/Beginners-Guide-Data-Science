Title:
Hot pepper sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. ketcup
1/2 C. vegetable oil
1/2 C. white vinegar
1/2 C. sugar (can use white or brown or combination)
2 quarts chopped hot peppers-Habenaro, jalapeno, serrano
Cumin, curry, salt and pepper optional

Directions:
Dice peppers, combine all ingredients, boild until heated through, jar, cook in canner 15 minutes past full boil or jar and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Janet Larsen
Description: 
This is a steamy hot pepper sauce.  It is great with crackers, use for bruscetta, or mix with soft cream cheese for a cheese spread on crackers!
