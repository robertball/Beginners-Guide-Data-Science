Title:
Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Manicotti Shells
Your favorite Sauce
3 cups Cottage Cheese (small curd)or Ricotta
1/2 Cup grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 Cup shredded Mozzerella
2 slightly beaten eggs
1/4 cup Parsley
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Cook Manicotti shells until softened but aldente
Mix Cottage Cheese, Parmesan, Mozzerella, Parsley, eggs, and salt
Spoon carefully into shells
Place in greased baking pan
Cover with sauce
Bake at 350 for 30-35 min
Remove from oven and let stand for 10 min before serving.
