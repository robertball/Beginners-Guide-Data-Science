Title:
Grandma's better than sex cake Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
18-1/2 package german chocolate cake
14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk
17 onnce jar Mrs. Richardson butterscotch camamel sauce
16 ounce carton fronzen whipped topping, thawed
3 heath candy bars, crushed

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour 9x13x2 inch pan. Prepare and bake cake mix as directed on package. When cake is still warm, but not hot, poke holsed in cake about one inch apart with the handle of a wooden spoon. Pour sweetend condensed milk over cake, then pour butterscotch carmal over cake. Refrigerate unitl completely cooled, about one hour. Top with whipped topping and crushed candy bars. Keep refrigerated.
