Title:
Busty Day Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077342.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb. beef stew
1 can tomato soup
1 small onion, sliced
1 can cream of celery soup
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients in crockpot. Cook on low 8-10 hours.
Mix the soups together to make wonderful gravy so you may want to serve over rice & cook biscuits for soppin'!

Submitter: 
laady_jr
Description: 
This is great over rice or noodles with a sprinkle of green onion!
