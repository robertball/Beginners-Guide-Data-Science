Title:
Tasty Baked Mac n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1027994.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box elbow Macaroni
1 tbsp cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 stick of (salted) butter - (softened)
1/2 stick or 4 tbsp butter (softened)
1 - 8oz package or 2 cups of sharp cheddar shredded cheese
1 - 8oz package or 2 cups of mild cheddar shredded cheese
2tbsp flour
1 egg yolk
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cayenne
1 cup milk

Directions:
Prepare macaroni according to box, drain and set aside.DO NOT OVER COOK.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F, line a 9 x 13 with parchment paper (or greased sheet of foil).
In a mixing bowl put in Cream Cheese, sour cream, 1/2 stick butter, sharp cheddar cheese, egg yolk. Stir lightly.
Next add flour, 1/2 tsp. salt, cayenne and milk. Blend on low until ingredients are well blended.
Sprinkle remaining 1/2 tsp salt on cooked macaroni and stir in remaining 1 stick butter in cooked macaroni.
Take a little or about 1/4 c. of liquid mixture and spread on bottom of pan. Then pour remaining mixture with cooked macaroni and stir. Pour macaroni in baking pan and spread evenly. Top macaroni with mild cheddar cheese.
Bake uncovered for 15 minutes.
