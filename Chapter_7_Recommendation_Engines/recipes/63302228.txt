Title:
Key Lime Mousse (and other flavors) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Haas Avacados'
1/2 Tsp Lime Zest
Juice of 1 Lime
1/4 cup crystal xylitol
1/2 tsp Stevia Crystals
2 Tsp pure vanilla extract
1 pinch sea salt
1 pinch ground cinnamon
1 pinch cardamom
2 Tbs Almond Butter
1/4 tsp other extract (cherry, maple, lemon, rasberry, coconut or any other flavor)
1/4 cup fresh sliced strawberries

Directions:
Put all in food processor or blender until well blended. Separate into four serving containers. Refridgerate for at least 20 minutes but the taste improves the longer it is refridgerated. Serve with sliced strawberry garnish

Submitter: 
Rosemary Vandello
Description: 
A mousse made from avacados but tastes like a desert. Only 4 grams Carbs
