Title:
Mary's Mexican Ranchero Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 red ripened tomatoes
1 whole onion diced
5 jalapenos
+ / - 1 c. water
salt to taste
3 tbs corn / vegetable oil

Directions:
Wash and dry the vagetables with exception of the onion. Heat a medium sized skillet. Add the oil and place the tomatoes and jalapenos in the pan. Turn every 2 minutes. Cook until all sides of the tomatoes and jalapenos are scalded. Take them out and place in a blender /  food processor. Puree the tomatoes and jalpenos. Set aside. To the same pan add the diced onions cook until transparent. Then add the tomatoe and jalapeno mixture to the pan. Add the water to it as well, let it simmer for 5 min. on medium / high heat. Serve it over scrambled eggs, or on anything you like. Serve warm. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mama Rivas aka Pablo's Hottie
Description: 
This is my favorite sauce. I got the recipe from my mother who has been making it since she was a young girl living with her grandparents on their ranch in Mexico.  You can adjust the hottness according to your preferrence.
