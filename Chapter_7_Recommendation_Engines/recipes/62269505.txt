Title:
AB's Lentil Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/344759.jpg
Ingredients:
4.75 ounces whole-wheat pastry flour (about 1 cups)
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
4 ounces sugar (approx 1/2 cup)
3 ounces unsalted butter, room temp
2 Tablespoons egg beaters (1/2 an egg)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 cup lentil puree
1 3/4 ounces rolled oats (approx 1/2 cup)
2 ounces dried fruit (approx 1/2 cup)
1 1/8 ounces shredded coconut (approx 1/2 cup)

Directions:
Lentil Puree:
2 oz lentils (approx 1/3 cup) & 1 cup water
Small pot over medium - combine, bring to a simmer, cover and simmer 30-40 minutes.
Puree in a blender/food processor.
Allow to cool if using immediately, will keep in fridge 3-4 days or 2-3 months in the freezer
Yields 3/4 cup puree.
Cookies:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and allspice.
In the bowl of a stand mixer, using the whisk attachment, cream sugar and butter on medium speed. Add the egg, mix until just incorporated. Add vanilla and lentil puree, mix til combined. Slowly add flour mixture, blend on low until just combined.
By hand, stir in oats, dried fruit, coconut.
Spoon onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Bake 15-17 minutes (internal temp = 195).
