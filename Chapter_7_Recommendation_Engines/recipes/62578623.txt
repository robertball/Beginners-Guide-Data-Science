Title:
Ham and Cheese Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (9 inch) pastry shells
2 cups diced fully cooked ham
2 cups shredded cheese (your choice of kinds)
1/2 cup minced onion
1/2 cup minced bell pepper (any combination)
8 eggs
2 cups half-and-half
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Divide ham, cheese, onion and peppers between the shells.
In a bowl, whisk eggs, cream, salt and pepper.
Pour into shells.
Cover and freeze for up to 3 months.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean (cover edges with foil if necessary).
Let stand for 5-10 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Katie Paulson
Description: 
Quick, simple quiche.  I scrounge through the fridge for veggies to include. Freezes well.
