Title:
Nacho Pizza with Beans and Olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 inch) prepared pizza crust
1 cup pizza sauce
3/4 cup ORTEGA速 Refried Beans
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup sliced ripe olives
1 tablespoon ORTEGA速 Diced Green Chiles
tortilla chips
chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
PREHEAT oven according to pizza crust package directions.
COMBINE pizza sauce and beans in small saucepan.  Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, for 1 to 2 minutes or until smooth.  Spread over crust to within 1 inch of edge; sprinkle with mozzarella cheese, cheddar cheese, olives and chiles.
BAKE according to pizza crust package directions or until crust is crisp and cheese is melted.  Garnish with tortilla chips and cilantro.
