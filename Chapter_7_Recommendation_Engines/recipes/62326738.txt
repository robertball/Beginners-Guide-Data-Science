Ingredients:
100 grams (about 3-1/2 ounces) feta cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup chopped cilantro leaves
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
2 cans (about 14 ounces each) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
Olive oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in food processor and process until roughly chopped.
Shape into walnut-size balls; flatten into patties.
Heat about 2 tablespoons olive oil in nonstick skillet over medium high heat. Working in batches, fry 4 or 5 patties at a time until golden brown and crisp on both sides (about 2 minutes per side). Between batches add additional olive oil to pan as necessary. Serve immediately.
