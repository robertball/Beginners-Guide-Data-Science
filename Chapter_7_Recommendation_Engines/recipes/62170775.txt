Title:
Chocolate Blueberry Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Milk Chocolate Chips
8oz Dried Blueberries

Directions:
Put milk chocolate chips in a glass or ceramic, microwave safe dish. Microwave for 1 minute, stir. Microwave for an additional 30 seconds, and stir again. Promptly add the blueberries to the chocolate, and mix well.
Spread evenly over a foil-lined cookie sheet. Place in the refrigerator for 30 minutes, or until hardened.
Peel off foil and break bark into pieces. This is stored best in an air-tight container in a cool dry place, or in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Jen
Description: 
If you love those gourmet chocolate covered blueberries, you will really loved this easy to make chocolate-blueberry bark! The easiest recipe you will ever make, and it's perfect for the holidays.
