Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123209.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. raisins
3/4 c. water
2 bunches of broccoli
1 small red onion
1/2 c. sunflower seeds
1 jar Hormel bacon bits
1 cup mayo
1/2 c. sugar
2 t. lemon juice

Directions:
Put the raisins in 3/4 c. water and bring to a boil.
Turn off heat and let set.
Chop broccoli into little pieces.
Dice onion.
Drain raisins and combine all ingredients.
Refrigerate.
