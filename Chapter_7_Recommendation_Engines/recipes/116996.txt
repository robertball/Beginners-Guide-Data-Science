Title:
Three-Cheese Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/762373.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) package uncooked elbow macaroni
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cheddar cheese soup, undiluted
2 slices processed American cheese
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, combine the soup, cheese, milk and butter. Cook and stir until cheese is melted. Drain macaroni; stir into cheese sauce.
