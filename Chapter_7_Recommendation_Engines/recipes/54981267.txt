Title:
Garlic Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ears  corn, husked and cleaned
12 tablespoons butter, divided
4 cloves minced garlic

Directions:
Preheat grill for med heat, or preheat your oven to 350 degrees F.  Parboil corn in sugar water.  Mix minced garlic and butter.
Place each ear of corn on a separate square of aluminum foil. Spread butter mix all over corn . Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.  Wrap ears tightly with the foil.
Place ears of corn on the grill or in the oven for 20 to 30 minutes, turning over occasionally.
