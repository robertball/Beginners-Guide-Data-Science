Title:
Oat Bread (Bread Machine) Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F6491168.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup lukewarm water
1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast
½ cup oat flour
1 ¼ cups bread flour
1 ¼ cups white whole wheat flour
½ teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons butter, melted
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon butter, melted

Directions:
Place lukewarm water, yeast, oat flour, bread flour, white whole wheat flour, salt, 2 tablespoons melted butter, and honey in the bread machine in the order recommended by manufacturer.
Cook on the setting for a 1 1/2-pound, whole wheat loaf setting. Brush top of bread with 1 tablespoon butter before slicing.

Submitter: 
Holly N.
Description: 
Our family does not care for whole wheat bread but we do care about our health! This bread is a good balance between taste and healthiness.
