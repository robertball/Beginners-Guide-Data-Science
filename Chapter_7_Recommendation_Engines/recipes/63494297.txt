Title:
Islander's Creamed Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 16 oz. bags Trader Joe's frozen chopped spinach, thawed and most of the liquid squeezed out
1 can coconut cream
1 can coconut milk
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 small onion, finely sliced or chopped
1 chili pepper and/or pinch of cayenne
Dash each of ground cumin & coriander seed
1 tsp oil
Salt to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil over medium-low heat.
Sauté the onion and garlic and chili until onions are soft.
Add spinach & salt, and cook until most of the water has burned off (or it’s cooked as much as you like it).
Add coconut milk/cream, cumin, coriander.  Stir and bring to simmer.  Taste and adjust seasoning.

Submitter: 
AREENA
Description: 
My family is Indian, from Fiji.  I don't know if this started as a Fijian dish or an Indian one, but I refer to it as the Island answer to creamed Spinach.  I grew up eating this & loving it. So far, others seem to agree.
