Title:
Easy Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked macaroni
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 can milk
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a 9x13-inch baking dish, combine the macaroni, tuna, soup, milk, salt, and pepper. Mix well, and then top with cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 30 minutes, or until bubbly. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Bonnie Cady
Description: 
Tuna, macaroni, creamy soup, cheese and fried onions are all you need to make this super easy tuna casserole that I learned from my roommate. It's great as leftovers, too.Modified from: Easy Tuna Casserole
