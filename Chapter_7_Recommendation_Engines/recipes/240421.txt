Title:
Camille's Crustless Ricotta Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1896837.jpg
Ingredients:
5 eggs
1 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 (16 ounce) containers ricotta cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Beat eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon together in a large bowl until smooth; add ricotta cheese and mix well. Pour mixture into the prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until set, about 1 hour. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
camille716
Description: 
I made up this recipe for my daughter who is gluten-free. She just loves it for breakfast.
 It's great with fruit for brunch or with whipped cream for a protein-packed dessert. Can be served warm or cold.
