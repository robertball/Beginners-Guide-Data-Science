Title:
Gomen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 lbs of collard greens
5 red onions
5 yellow onions
24 cloves of garlic and small piece of ginger ground
1 1/2 cups of oil
1 tsp cardamom
1 tsp fenugreek
1 tsp nigella seeds
1 tbsp of berbere
10 cups of water

Directions:
Rinse the greens in cold water. Pull out and discard some of the bigger stems and veins. Chop them into big pieces.
Heat vegetable oil in a pot add onions, garlic and ginger and sauté gently for 1–2 minutes.
Add cardamom, fenugreek, and nigella, berbere and cook, stirring often, until fragrant, 1–2 minutes.
Add collards, 10 cups water, and salt and pepper; cover and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and cook, stirring occasionally, until collards are tender, 50–55 minutes.
Stir in vinegar and serve collards hot.
