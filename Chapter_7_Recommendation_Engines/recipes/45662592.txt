Title:
Pacific Rim Blend Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Pappardelle's Pacific Rim Blend Pasta
2 grilled chicken breasts, cut into 1 inch cubes
1/4 cup green onions, diced
2 red bell peppers, sliced into bite size pieces
1 bunch cilantro, chopped
1/2 cup raw peanuts
SAUCE: 1/4 cup olive oil
2 Tbl white wine vinegar
1 Tbl honey
3 Tbl soy sauce
1 Tbl red chile flakes
1 Tbl garlic, minced
1 Tbl ginger root, minced

Directions:
Combine sauce ingredients in bowl, cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes
Cook pasta in 6-8 quarts of boiling water until al dente (about 8-10 minutes) then drain.
Roast peanuts until fragrant. Chop coarsly.
In a large serving bowl, add cooked/ cooled pasta, chicken, vegetables (except cilantro) and sauce.  Mix well.
Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro and peanuts.
