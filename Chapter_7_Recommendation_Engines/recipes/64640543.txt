Title:
Sweet and Fruity Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. Roasted or Grilled Chicken Breast, diced
2 stalks of Celery, finely chopped
14 fresh Red Cherries, pitted and cut into quarters
3 oz. Cherry flavored Dried Cranberries (like Ocean Spray Craisins)
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1 1/2 teaspoons Perfect Pinch Original Chicken Seasoning
3 tablespoons Lite Mayonnaise
1 tablespoon Jam (cherry or blackberry are best)
1/2 cup Unsalted Dry Toasted Slivered Almonds

Directions:
Place all the ingredients, except the Slivered Almonds, in a large bowl and stir thoroughly to combine all.  Place mixture in the refrigerator for 1 hour to allow the ingredients to meld.  Just before serving, add the Slivered Almonds, and stir thoroughly again.

Submitter: 
LonestarNR
Description: 
This Chicken Salad is perfect for the hot summer weather.  The fruit adds a nice level of sweetness to this Salad, which everyone will love, including the kids.  This is perfect for an afternoon picnic.
