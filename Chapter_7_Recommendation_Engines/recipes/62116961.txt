Title:
Slow Cooked Chicken Drumsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds of Chicken Drumsticks
2 tsp cajun seasoning
2 tsp poultry seasoning
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp flour
1 cup bbq sauce
Cooking spray
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees and grease pan.
Place chicken in pan and sprinkle seasonings, salt, and pepper over chicken
Sprinkle flour over chicken and spray cooking spray over chicken to lightly coat.
Place covered pan in preheated oven and cook for about 2 1/2 hrs.
Spread bbq sauce evenly over chicken.
Place back into oven without lid and continue cooing for about 1/2 hour.
Chicken is done when the meat falls off the bone.
Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
cheftini
Description: 
These delicious drumsticks are made in the oven and when they are done the meat falls right off the bone. Also, you can add more seasonings of your choice.
