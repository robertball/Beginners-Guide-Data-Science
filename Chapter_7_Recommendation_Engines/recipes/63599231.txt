Title:
Aunt Judi's Easter Pineapple Raisin Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup brown sugar
2 Tbsp flour
1/2 tsp mustard
2 Tbsp vinegar
3/4 cup raisins
1 1/2 cup hot water
1 cup crushed pineapple (one 8 oz. can)

Directions:
Whisk together flour and brown sugar in a medium saucepan. Add mustard and vinegar and mix well so there are no flour lumps. Add the rest of the ingredients. Simmer on medium heat, stirring often until thickened, about 10 minutes. Serve over ham.

Submitter: 
raesive
Description: 
One of my must-haves with ham, especially at Easter. This is the recipe I grew up with- my Aunt would make it every year. A nice sweet accent!
