Title:
Hot Buttered Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups water
3/4 cups sugar
1 1/2 tsp. grated lemon peel
3/4 cup lemon juice
2 tbsp. butter
6 cinnamon sticks

Directions:
Combine water, sugar, lemon peel, lemon juice and butter in slow cooker.
Cover, cook on high 2 1/2 hours or until heated through.
Serve very hot with a cinnamon stick in each mug.
