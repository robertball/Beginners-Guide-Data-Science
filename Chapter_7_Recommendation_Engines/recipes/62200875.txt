Title:
Joyce's Easy Cheesy Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp margarine, melted
3 Tbsp flour
1 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup of Velveeta, cubed

Directions:
Mix flour with melted margarine.  Stir in flour.  Add salt and pepper.  Stir in milk.  Microwave for 2 minutes.  Stir.  Microwave for 60 seconds; stir.  Repeat until think sauce is achieved.  stir in cubes of cheese until melted.  Keep warm in microwave until veggies are cooked.  Drain veggies.  Pour sauce over drained veggies and mix.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
This is a VERY easy cheese sauce that I use for Broccoli or Cauliflower.
