Title:
Coconut Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked pie shell
3 eggs
1/2 cup butter - melted
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla
3 1/2 oz flaked coconut
1/2 cup - 1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Beat eggs and add melted butte, lemon juice and vanilla. Beat together. Beat in sugar. Fold in coconut and pecans. Pour into unbaked pie shell. Bake for 45 - 50 minutes at 350'.

Submitter: 
LALYC
Description: 
This is a nice change from Pecan Pie. It is my husband's favorite. Very sweet and rich. If you use a deep dish pie crust, use 1 cup pecans. For a smaller pie crust use 1/2 cup.
