Title:
Roasted Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed, sliced in half
2 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp kosher salt
freshly ground black pepper
Maple Balsamic Drizzle (or Holiday variety):
3 Tbsp balsamic vinegar (or 2 Tbsp appe cider vinegar)
1 Tbsp maple syrup (plus 1 tsp Dijon mustard & 1/4 cup craisins)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.  Toss sprouts with olive oil, kosher salt, and pepper.  Spread out the sprouts in a single layer.  Roast sprouts until browned, about 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine the balsamic vinegar and mape syrup in a small saucepan.  Boil, strring constanty, about 3 minutes, until thickened.  (or combine apple cider vinegar and maple syrup with mustard.)  Drizzle over the sprouts.
