Title:
Mark's Big Batch Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 onion, diced
2 shallots, cut into slivers
2 medium green peppers, diced
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 pounds ground turkey or ground venison
2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, undrained
1 (15 ounce) can chili beans, undrained
1 (15 ounce) can black eyed peas, drained
2 (14.5 ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
3 tablespoons chili powder
2 tablespoons dried oregano
2 tablespoons dried basil leaves
1 tablespoon cumin
2 Â½ tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 teaspoon kosher salt, or to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large heavy pot over medium heat; cook onion, shallots and green pepper until onions are translucent. Add ground meat, ground black pepper, salt, and garlic and cook, stirring, until meat is brown. Stir in beans, tomatoes, chili powder, oregano, basil, cumin, crushed red pepper and vinegar. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 60 minutes or more, until flavors are well blended. Remove cover and reduce slightly for 30 minutes.

Description: 
Plain and simple, you'll love this fantastic chili! Serve with crusty bread and a dry red wine. Enjoy!
