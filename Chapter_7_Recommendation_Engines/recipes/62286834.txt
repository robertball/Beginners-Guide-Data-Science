Title:
Baked Sugar Soy Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T olive oil
Saly, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder to taste
6 pork chops
6 tablespoons brown sugar
3 tablespoons butter, cut in 1/2 T pieces
3 tablespoon soy sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Season chops with salt, pepper, onion powder and garlic powder.  Heat olive oil over high heat in pan. Brown chops on both sides, app. 4-5 min per side.
Place the chops in a 9x13 inch baking pan. Place 1 tablespoon of brown sugar on top of every chop. Place 1/2 tablespoon of butter on top of the brown sugar. Sprinkle chops with soy sauce.
Cover pan with foil and bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, until browned.
