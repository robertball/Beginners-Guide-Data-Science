Title:
Kay's Halva Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2   cups   water
1 1/3   cup   sugar
1/2   cup   butter (1 stick)
1 1/3   regular cream of wheat (not quick)
1/2-3/4   cup   chopped dates
1/2-2/3   cup   chopped pistachios

Directions:
In a small saucepan, bring water and sugar to just below boiling.
Meanwhile, melt butter with cream of wheat over medium heat.  Cook until cream of wheat is light brown, about 5-10 minutes.  Add the water/sugar mix slowly.  Stir out any lumps.  Add nuts and dates.  Stir until thickened.  Serve warm or room temperature.  Refrigerate any leftovers.
