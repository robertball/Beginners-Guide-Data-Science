Title:
Chicken & Veggie Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3798443.jpg
Ingredients:
4 potatoes
2 zucchini
1 1/2 cups baby carrots
1 341 ml can (or 1 1/2 cups frozen) kernel corn, drained
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
3 tbsp. olive oil
1 tbsp. chili powder
2 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Cut potatoes lengthwise into thin wedges and place in a large bowl.
Cut zucchini into 1" chunks; add to bowl along with carrots and corn.
Cut chicken into 1 1/2" chunks
Combine oil, chili powder, oregano and salt; pour half over veggies & toss to coat.
Spread veggies onto large rimmed baking sheet.
Combine remaining oil mixture with bread crumbs. Add chicken and toss to coat.
Arrange over top of veggies, leaving space between chicken pieces.
Roast in oven for about 40 minutes, or until chicken is done and veggies are tender.
Time-saving tip: cut chicken and zucchini the night before; cover & refrigerate separately for up to 1 day.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A complete one-pan meal featuring chicken, potatoes and veggies.
