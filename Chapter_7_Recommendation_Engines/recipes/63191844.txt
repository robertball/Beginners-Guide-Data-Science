Title:
CREAMY CHOCOLATE TARTLETS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
GREASE 4 INCH TART PANS
MELT 1/4 CUP PLUS 3 TBSP BUTTER IN A SAUCE PAN AND STIR IN THE GRAHAM CRACKERS UNTIL COMBINED.
PRESS THE MIXTURE INTO THE TART PANS & UP THE SIDES OF THE PANS
CHILL UNTIL FIRM ABOUT 15 MINUTES
MELT THE DARK CHOCOLATE, HONEY & REMAINING BUTTER IN A HEATPROOF BOWL AND SET ON TOP OF A SAUCEPAN OF SHIMMERING WATER. REMOVE THE BOWL FROM THE HEAT. POUR IN THE CREAM & STIR UNTIL SPOON
DIVIDE THE MIXTURE EVENLY AMONG THE TARTLET PANS. CHILL UNTIL SET, 3 HRS
MELT THE WHITE CHOCOLATE AND DROP A TSP OF THE MELTED WHITE CHOCOLATE ON TOP OF EACH TARTLET.  CHILL FOR 15 MINUTES. REMOVE FROM PANS & SERVE

Directions:
SEE ABOVE

Submitter: 
Colleen Boni
Description: 
1/2 CUP, PLUS TBSP UNSALTED BUTTER, DIVIDED
1 CUP GRAHAM CRACKERS CRUMBS
7 OZ DARK CHOCOLATE BROKEN INTO SQUARES
2 TBSP HONEY
1/2 CUP HEAVY CREAM
2 OZ WHITE CHOCOLATE BROKEN INTO SQUARES
