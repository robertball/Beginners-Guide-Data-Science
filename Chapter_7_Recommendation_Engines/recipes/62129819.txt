Title:
Minnesota Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. broccoli, cut in bite-size pieces
1/4 c. red onion, finely chopped
3 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
1 c. green olives, sliced
1 4-oz. jar diced pimientos, drained
3/4 c. mayonnaise
1 tsp. dry mustard powder
1/2 tsp. celery seed
1 Tbsp. snipped fresh dill

Directions:
Blanch broccoli until bright green and crisp-tender, about 2 minutes. Drain and chill in ice water bath until cool.
Mix remaining ingredients; stir in drained cooked broccoli. Refrigerate overnight for best flavor.

Submitter: 
Doughgirl8
Description: 
No bacon, no fruit, no seeds: this broccoli salad, adapted from a recipe in a newspaper clipping from 1980, combines potato salad flavors--hard-cooked eggs, dill, celery seed, mayo--with crisp fresh broccoli.
