Title:
Kung Pao Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into chunked
1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons white wine
4 tablespoons soy sauce
4 tablespoons sesame oil, divided
4 tablespoons cornstarch, dissolved in 4 tablespoons water
2 ounce hot chile paste
2 teaspoon distilled white vinegar
4 teaspoons brown sugar
4 green onions, chopped
2 tablespoons chopped garlic
1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and chopped
4 ounces finely chopped peanuts
veggies (carrots, broccoli, peppers, etc) optional
red pepper flakes to taste (optional)

Directions:
To Make Marinade: Combine 2 tablespoons wine, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 tablespoons oil and 2 tablespoon cornstarch/water mixture and mix together. Place chicken pieces in a glass dish or bowl and add marinade. Toss to coat. Cover dish and place in refrigerator for about 30 minutes.
To Make Sauce: In a small bowl combine 1/2 cup wine, 2 tablespoon soy sauce, 2 tablespoon oil, 2 tablespoon cornstarch/water mixture, chili paste, vinegar and sugar. Mix together and add chopped green onion, garlic, chopped water chestnuts and peanuts. In a medium skillet, heat sauce slowly until aromatic.
Meanwhile, saute veggies in wok till tender.  Remove chicken from marinade and saute with veggies until meat is white and juices run clear. When sauce is aromatic, add to chicken/veggies, along with red pepper flakes to taste (optional) and let simmer together until sauce thickens.
