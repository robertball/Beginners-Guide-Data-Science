Title:
Fiesta Lime Parmesan Halibut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(2) Halibut Steaks
Paste:
1/2 Cup Panko Japanese bread crumbs
1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese shredded
1/8 tsp Mrs. Dash FIESTA LIME seasoning
1/2 tsp Thyme
1/4 cup Olive oil
1/2 clove fresh garlish chopped fine
(Blend above use enough Olive oil to make the bread crumbs wet but NOT to wet)

Directions:
Season steaks with salt and pepper on both sides.
Sprinkle additional MRS. Dash Fiesta Lime seasoning on top of steaks.
Last step spoon paste onto the top of Halibut steaks
Bake at 450 or 400 till done, about 20 to 30 minutes depending on your taste.
