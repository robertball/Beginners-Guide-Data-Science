Title:
Pan Roasted Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved lengthwise
1 Tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbsp butter
10 sprigs fresh thyme, stemmed and  diced
1 large sprig fresh rosemary, stemmed and diced
1 tsp fennel seed
1 tsp kosher salt
1 Tbsp sherry

Directions:
In a large skillet cook the Brussels sprouts uncovered, in enough lightly salted boiling water to cover for 4 minutes, drain and pat dry with paper towels.
Place the skillet on high heat for 2 minutes.
Reduce heat to medium. Add oil and garlic, cook and stir for 2 minutes. Add the butter and increase the heat to medium-high. Arrange sprouts cut-sides down in the skillet. Top with the thyme, rosemary,fennel seeds, and salt.
Cook uncovered 3 to 4 minutes, or until well browned.
Sprinkle with sherry and quickly toss to distribute flavors.
