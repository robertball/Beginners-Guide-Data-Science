Title:
Sandy's Greek Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/940934.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12 oz box of rainbow rotini pasta
1 10 oz container of grape tomatoes, sliced in half
1 can of sliced olives, drained
1 15.5 oz can of garbanzo beans, drained
1 cucumber, peeled and diced
1/4 medium red onion, finely diced
2 garlic cloves, diced
4 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon fresh chopped basil
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
In a large bowl combine tomatoes, olives, garbanzo beans, cucumber, onion, garlic, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, lemon juice and basil.  Stir well.
Boil pasta according to directions on the box (9 to 11 minutes), drain, and combine with the above ingredients
Add salt and pepper to taste
Cool in refrigerator for 15-20 minutes so flavors meld
Mix in feta cheese and serve cold

Submitter: 
Sandy Williams
Description: 
I invented this fresh and easy recipe when I found myself continually invited to summer potlucks and needed a no-fuss dish to make.  You can even make this the day before.
