Title:
Mama's Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cornmeal
2 1/2 cups plain soymilk
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup canola oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Grease a 9 x 13 inch baking pan.
Combine cornmeal and soymilk, let soak for 5-10 minutes. Add eggs and oil to cornmeal mixture, blend well.
To cornmeal mixture add flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Mix until combined.  At this point you may add corn, jalepenos, cheese or whatever you wish.
Pour into prepared pan and bake in preheated oven for approximately 35 minutes or until done.  Check for doneness with a knife or toothpick.  It should
come out dry and clean.

Submitter: 
Stephany Brown
Description: 
This corbread is really moist, not too sweet and can handle the addition of some frozen or drained corn, jalepenos, cheese, chiles or whatever you like.
