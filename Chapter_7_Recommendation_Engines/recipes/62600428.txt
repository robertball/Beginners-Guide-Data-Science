Title:
Chicken and Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small eggplant (about 1#), cut into 1/4-in. rounds
4 T olive oil
1 t salt
Fresh-ground black pepper
1# boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 3)
2 C canned crushed tomatoes in thick puree
1/2# fresh mozzarella cheese, cut into thin slices
1/3 C grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 C lightly packed basil leaves

Directions:
Heat boiler. Arrange eggplant in single layer on a large baking sheet. Coat both sides of eggplant with 2 1/2T of oil and sprinkle with 1/2t salt and 1/4t pepper. Broil, turning once, until browned, about 5 min. per side. Turn off broiler and heat oven to 425 degrees.
In large nonstick frying pan, heat 1 T oil over mod-hight heat. Season chicken w/1/4t salt and 1/8t pepper and add to pan. Partially cook chicken for 2 min per side and remove from pan. When cool enough to handle, cut chicken crosswise into 1/4-in. slices.
Oil an 8-in square baking dish. Put 1/3 of eggplant in a single layer in dish. Top with 1/2 of chicken, 1/2 of tomatoes, 1/2 of mozzarella, 1/3 of Parmesan, 1/2 of basil, and remaining 1/4t salt. Repeat with another 1/3 eggplant, remaining chicken, tomatoes, and mozarella, another 1/3 Parmesan, and the remaining basil. Top w/remaining eggplant and sprinkle with remaining cheese. Drizzle with remaining 1/2T oil. Bake for 20 min. and let sit 5 min. before cutting.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Eggplant is broiled, chicken cooked on stovetop, then all is put in baking dish and oven cooked for 20 min.  Dish is good for using up those summer veggie and fresh basil. Uses fresh mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
