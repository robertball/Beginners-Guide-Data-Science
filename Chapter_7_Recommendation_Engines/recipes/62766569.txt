Title:
Cauliflower Pizza Crust (and 200 cal pizza slices (9)) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups ground raw cauliflower
4 eggs
1 cup part skim mozzarella
salt
oregano
garlic powder

Directions:
Beat eggs in large mixing bowl.  Add salt & spices, mix.  Add rest of ingredients.  Pat out thin on a parchment-lined jelly-roll pan and bake at 400 til lightly browned in spots on top.
Top with pizza toppings and bake. (For 9 pieces of 200 cal per slice, top with 4.7 oz turkey bacon; 3 large mushrooms, sliced; 1/2 onion, sliced; 1 green pepper, sliced; 1 cup stuffed green olives, sliced; 6 ounces tomato paste, diluted to sauce-like consistency; 2 tsp sriracha sauce; 1 cup grated mozarella, oregano and salt to taste.)
