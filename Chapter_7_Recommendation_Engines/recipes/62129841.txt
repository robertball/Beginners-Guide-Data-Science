Title:
easy herb bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 7.5 oz roll of buttermilk biscuits(10 biscits)
parmesan herb seasoning blend(such as McCormick, or any other seasoning blend)
1/4 cup melted butter

Directions:
preheat oven to 400 degrees.
spray bread pan with non-stick cooking spray.
unroll biscuits but do not seperate, add biscuits to loaf pan on their sides.
brush bread with butter, setting aside what is not used.
roll loaf to one side and sprinkle with seasoning to coat,do top, and then roll to the other side. making sure that all sides except bottom are coated.
drizzle with remaining butter.
bake in preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or untill golden brown on top and no longer doughy inside.

so simple its stupid and it comes out of the oven already sliced! i used the small can of buttermilk biscuits just because thats all i had. im sure a big one would work great and the loaf would be big enough for sandwiches.
