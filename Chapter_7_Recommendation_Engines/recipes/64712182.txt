Title:
Air Fryer Crispy Green Beans with Spicy Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5620034.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
2 teaspoons salt
1 cup beer
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
12 oz. fresh green beans, washed and trimmed
Parchment paper cut to the size of the air fryer basket
Dipping sauce:
1 cup of ranch dressing
2 teaspoons chile garlic sauce (such as Sriracha brand) or to taste
1 teaspoon horseradish or to taste

Directions:
In a bowl mix the flour, salt, pepper and beer. In small batches coat the beans with the batter and shake off excess.
Preheat the air  fryer to 400 degrees according to manufactures instructions.
Place the parchment paper in the bottom of the air fryer basket. Layer the battered beans in a single layer in the air fryer; cook in batches if necessary to avoid over crowding the air fryer basket. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
In a bowl mix the ranch dressing, chile garlic sauce and horseradish. Serve with green beans.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Beer battered green beans that are a healthier version of the deep fried ones. Serve with the spicy version of dipping sauce or your favorite dipping sauce.
