Title:
Green Enchilada Slow Cooker Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
28 oz can of Green Chile Enchilada Sauce (mild, med or hot depending on your preference)
16 oz sour cream
1 cup chicken broth
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Brown chicken breasts and season with salt and pepper. In a large bowl combine enchilada sauce, sour cream, chicken broth and spices. Pour enough sauce in bottom of pot to cover add chicken breasts and cover with remaining sauce mixture. Cook on high 4-6 hours or low 6-8 hours. Serve over rice or as a burrito with flour tortillas.

Submitter: 
Nonna Birdi
Description: 
This is so simple and so good. I serve it over rice, or with warm flour tortillas like a burrito. I put out the additions; beans, chopped onions, grated cheese, etc. and we all build our own. Works great with picky eaters.
