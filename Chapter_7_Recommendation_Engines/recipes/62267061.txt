Title:
Agave Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh pineapple, cut into bite size chucks
1 cup fresh kiwi, cut into quarters
1 cup fresh strawberries, cut into quarters
1 cup fresh blueberries, whole
3-4 tablespoons agave nectar - light

Directions:
Combine fruit in large bowl.  Drizzle agave nectar over fruit.  Toss to thoroughly coat fruit.  Let sit in refrigerator until chilled and it's ready to eat!

Submitter: 
Trish
Description: 
Quick, easy fruit salad!!  Experiment with whatever fruits you desire but I like this combination for the color and variety.  Not much else to say about this sweet, mouth-watering dish!  Enjoy!!
