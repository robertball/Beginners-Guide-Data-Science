Title:
Sugar Free Watergate Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can No Sugar Added Crushed Pineapple
1 tub Light Whipped Cream
1 package Sugar Free Pistachio Instant Pudding Mix
Â½ package Mini Marshmallows
Nuts **optional
Maraschino Cherries for Decoration **optional

Directions:
Mix together the pudding mix and the crushed pineapple.  Add the nuts.
Fold in the whipped cream.
SLOWLY stir in the marshmallows.
Top with maraschino cherries for decoration.
Cover and store in the refrigerator.
