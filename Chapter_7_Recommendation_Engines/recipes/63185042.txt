Title:
Mom's spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion
Garlic
1 can tomato soup
1 can water
Dash of ketsup
Dash Worcester
1-2 tsp of curry powder
Any veggies as desired ( I like mushrooms & peppers)

Directions:
Brown onion & garlic in olive oil. Add ground beef. Add remaining ingredients and veggies as desired. Simmer 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Chocolate
Description: 
A family favorite beefy spaghetti with a sneaky hint of curry powder as the secret weapon.
