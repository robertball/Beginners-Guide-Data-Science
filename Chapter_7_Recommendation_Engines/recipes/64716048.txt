Title:
Chia Seed Vanilla Breakfast Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5751767.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Vanilla Almond milk
3 T Organic Honey or Maple Syrup
1 T Vanilla Extract
1/2 C Chia Seeds
Fresh Fruit

Directions:
Combine all ingredients taking care to mix well.  Pour in container or invidivual bowls and place in the refrigerator for 24 hours.  Serve with fresh fruit like raspberries, strawberries, blueberries, bananas, peach's ect
