Title:
Tangy Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9 oz. bag of fresh spinach
2/3 cup Pepperidge Farm herb seasoned stuffing mix (blue bag)
1/3 cup crumbled bacon
3 hard boiled eggs, crumbled
Dressing:
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1/3 cup of sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp celery seed
1 1/2 T prepared yellow mustard
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup canola oil

Directions:
Combine dressing ingredients several hours ahead of time and chill in refridgerator. Mix the spinach, egg, bacon and bread crumbs in large bowl. Toss with salad dressing just before serving.
