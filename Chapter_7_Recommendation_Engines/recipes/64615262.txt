Title:
Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box strawberries (1 qt.)
1 small can crushed pineapple
2 or 3 bananas, mashed with fork
3 sm. pkg. strawberry jello
8 oz. sour cream

Directions:
Mix jello with 3 cups hot water.  Add pineapple and strawberries.  Mash bananas and add to mixture.
Pour 1/2 in dish and refrigerate.  Leave other 1/2 at room temp.
When jello is set, put sour cream on and pour on the rest of the jello.
Refrigerate until firm.
