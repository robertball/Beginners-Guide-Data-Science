Title:
Spicy White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/542797.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag of navy beans
6 cups of chicken broth
2 garlic cloves
2 chopped white onions
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
8oz can chopped green chilies
2 teaspoons Cumin
1 1/2 teaspoon Oregano
1/4 teaspoon ground clove
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 chopped Jalape√±o
5 boneless chicken breast
_______
Optional:
Grated Pepper Jack Cheese
Tortilla Chips
Sour Cream

Directions:
In a large pot boil water and cook chicken through.
Strain water, and shred chicken. Combine chicken broth, spices, peppers, chicken and beans.
Bring beans to a boil then reduce to medium-low heat and slow cook for five hours.
Serve with shredded cheese,tortilla chips, and sour cream if desired.

Submitter: 
Amaroa
Description: 
The trick to this chili is to slow cook it for at least 4-5 hours. Perfect for a cold day or as a dip when entertaining.
