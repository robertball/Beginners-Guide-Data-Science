Title:
5 Bean Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz Package Hot Sausage
5 15oz cans of beans, such as butter, kidney, black-eyed peas, black, white, etc.
2 15 oz Cans Italian Stewed Tomatoes

Directions:
In a large pot brown the sausage
Add the tomatoes and beans, do not drain them first! Bring to a simmer for 5 minutes or so stirring occasionally to heat through.
Serve with cornbread.

Submitter: 
Devin
Description: 
A hearty soup that is a favorite here in Virginia. The sausage and tomatoes give it an excellent flavor.
