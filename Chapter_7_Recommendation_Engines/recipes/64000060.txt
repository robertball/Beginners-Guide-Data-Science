Title:
The Next Best Thing to Robert Redford Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 cup all-purpose flour
* 1/4 cup butter, melted
* 1 cup pecans
* 1 cup sugar
* 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
* 16 oz. whipped topping
* 1 4-oz. package vanilla instant pudding mix
* 1 4-oz. package chocolate instant pudding mix
* 2 cups milk

Directions:
Mix flour, butter, and pecans in bowl. Press over the bottom of pan. Bake for 15 minutes. cool.
Cream sugar and cream cheese in mixer bowl until light and fluffy. Mix in half the whipped topping. Spread over crust.
Combine pudding mixes and milk in mixer bowl; beat until smooth. Spoon over cream cheese mixture. Top with remaining whipped topping.
Chill until serving time. Garnish with shaved chocolate.
