Title:
Kelly's Shepard's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs  ground beef
Sm. chopped onion
1 garlic clove ,chopped
Flour
2-3 packets of powered beef bouillon
1 bottle/can beer
1 can green beans and 1 can drained diced tomatoes(optional)
Prepared mashed potatoes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375* In a large skillet,brown ground beef, onion and garlic, season with S&P. Reduce heat and coat with flour and bouillon to absorb all oil.  Add beer slowly while stirring to make a "gravy" consistency. Optional- Add can of drained green beans, and can of drained tomatoes, mix evenly.
Place in 9 inch deep dish, and top with mashed potatoes, spread evenly.
Bake until bubbly and potatoes are browned, about 30 min.

Submitter: 
Kelly Sanchez
Description: 
A hearty meal, great to make ahead or for those cold autumn evenings when you are heading out to the football or soccer field.
