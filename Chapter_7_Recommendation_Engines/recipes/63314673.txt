Title:
Spiced Potatoes With Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/876936.jpg
Ingredients:
2 onions,peeled quartered
2 in ginger root,peeled and finely chopped
2 garlic clove,peeled
2-3 tbsp mild or medium curry paste
4 tbsp water
750 g new potatoes
vegetable oil,for deep frying
150 ml strained Greek or Natural yogurt
150 ml double(heavy) cream
3 tbsp chopped fresh mint
salt and pepper,for seasoning
3 tbsp vegetable ghee or oil
1/2 bunch spring onions,trimmed and chopped,to garnish

Directions:
Place the onions,ginger,garlic,curry paste and water in a blender or food processor and process until smooth,scraping down the sides of machine and blending,if needed.
Cut potatoes in to small quarters and pat dry with kitchen paper.Heat the oil in a deep-fat fryer to 180 C and fry the potatoes,in batches,for about 5 minutes or until golden brown.Remove from the pan and drain on paper towels.
Heat the ghee or oil in a large frying pan,add the curry and onion mixture and fry gently for 2 minutes,stirring all the time.Add the yogurt,cream and mint and mix well.
Add the fried potatoes and stir until coated in the sauce.Cook for further 5-7 minutes or until sauce has thickened,stirring frequently.Season with salt and pepper and garnish with spring onions.Serve immediately
