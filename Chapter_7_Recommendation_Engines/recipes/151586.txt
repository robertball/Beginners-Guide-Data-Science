Title:
Campbell's Kitchen Chicken Mozzarella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992486.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Healthy Request® Condensed Tomato Soup
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
4 cups hot cooked corkscrew-shaped pasta, cooked without salt

Directions:
Place chicken in 2-quart shallow baking dish. Mix soup, Italian seasoning and garlic powder. Spoon over chicken.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 20 minutes or until done.
Sprinkle cheese over chicken. Serve with pasta.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
This effortless meal from the oven features mozzarella-topped chicken breasts baked in an Italian-seasoned tomato sauce.
