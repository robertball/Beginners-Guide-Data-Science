Title:
Green Beans with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon
1/2 white onion chopped
1/2 cup beef broth
1 lb green beans, trimmed
salt and pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Fry bacon in pan.  Put bacon to the side and remove all but one tbsp of bacon fat.
In the bacon fat, fry the onion and garlic.
Stir in green beans and broth, covered to steam until the green beans are steamed and the broth has boiled away.  Remove from heat.
Crumble bacon slices and add back to beans.  Add pepper and salt to taste (optional).
