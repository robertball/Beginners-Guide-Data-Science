Title:
Buffalo Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs. boneless chicken breasts
4 large carrots
6 stalks celery
2 red peppers
10 cloves garlic
10 T chili powder
4 T cumin
2 T paprika
1 cup Frank's Red Hot sauce
2 large cans ground tomatoes (28-32 oz.)
2 cans black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can Bush's chili starter
1 can kidney beans, drained

Directions:
1. In food processor, finely chop carrots, celery, red peppers and garlic.
2. Pour mixture into 6 1/2 quart crock pot and stir in remaining ingredients except for chicken.
3. Once all ingredients are well mixed, put in boneless chicken and make sure it is covered with mixture.
4. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours.  Remove chicken, shred and return to crock pot.  Stir well.
5. Serve with blue cheese crumbles or blue cheese or ranch dressing.

Submitter: 
Karen Semmelrock
Description: 
One of my most requested recipes!  Some people like to add a little more hot sauce so I always keep the bottle out!
