Title:
Big Ray's Slow Cooker Chuck Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1984008.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tbl ketchup
4 tbl red wine vinegar
4 tsp vegetable oil
4 tsp soy sauce
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp Dijon mustard
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 small onion, minced
1/2 tsp salt salt
1 tsp pepper
3 Tbl cornstarch
3 lb boneless beef chuck roast

Directions:
In a resealable plastic bag, combine the ketchup, vinegar, oil, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, garlic, onion, salt and pepper; add meat. Seal bag and turn to coat; refrigerate overnight to 48 hours, turning bag over every 8 hours or so.
Place roast and marinade in slow cooker Cook on low for 8 hours.
Remove roast and let cool slightly, Mix cornstarch with 1/2 cup of juices from slow cooker. Pour remaining juice into saucepan and heat until starting to simmer.  Add cornstarch mixture and heat, stirring continuously until thickened.  Serve gravy on top or on side of roast.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This marinade is a great way to make this tough cut of meat tender and tasty.  The longer it marinades , the better.
