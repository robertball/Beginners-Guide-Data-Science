Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 c. ketchup
1/2 c. water
2 T. brown sugar
2 T. mustard
1 T. vinegar
2 t. Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Brown beef in skillet and drain; add other ingredients and simmer over low heat for 30 min.
