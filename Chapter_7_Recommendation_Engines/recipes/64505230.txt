Title:
July  - Lite Apron - Chicago Style Franks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 item(s) fat free beef and pork hot dog(s)
8 item(s) reduced calorie hot dog bun(s)
2 clove(s) (medium) garlic clove(s)
1/2 pound(s) kirby cucumber(s)
1 lb uncooked russet potato
1 medium fresh tomato(es)
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1/8 cup(s) sweet pickle relish
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1 serving(s) Subway Yellow Mustard
1/4 cup water
2 T olive oil
8 pieces Puckered Pickle Peppers, Sport
1 tsp Astor Celery Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475
wash and dry the produce
cut potatoes lengthwise into one inch wedges
thinly slice the cucumber
put cucumber in glass bowl.
Peel and smash garlic
Slice tomato thinly put potatoes on sheet pan and drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper and italian seasoning. toss to coat -
roast 26-28 minutes
in small pot combine garlic, vinegar, 1/4 of celery salt, 1/8 cup of water, season with salt and pepper and heat to boiling on high.
once boiling pour into bowl of cucumber.
set aside at least 10 minutes
fry frankfurters for 3-5 minutes in a drizzle of olive oil.
toast buns
assemble franks and buns and serve.
