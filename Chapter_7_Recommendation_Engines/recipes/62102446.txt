Title:
Caldillo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs cubed, trimmed round steak
2 potatoes, peeled and diced small
1 onion, peeled and diced very small
2 tomatoes, seeded and diced
2 small cans of chopped green chiles
1 can of Rotel (tomatoes & green chiles)
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 tsp ground cumin
2 cans beef broth
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp oil

Directions:
In large pot, saute beef cubes and onions in oil until meat is no longer pink on medium heat.  Add diced potatoes and tomatoes, mix in to meat.  Pour beef broth into pot and add enough water to cover meat by 2 inches.  Add all remaining ingredients, stir well and let simmer on low for 1-2 hours.  For extra spice, substitute 1 can of chopped jalapenos for 1 can of green chiles.  Serve hot w/warm flour tortillas.
