Title:
Gyoza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon sesame oil
2 cups chopped cabbage
1/4 cup chopped onion, fresh chives or spring onion
1 clove garlic, chopped
fresh ginger, finely chopped or grated
1/4 cup chopped carrot (opt)
1/2 pound ground pork
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (10 ounce) package wonton wrappers
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Sprinkle salt on the cabbage and let stand for 5 mins, than press the water out.
Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Mix in cabbage, onion, garlic, ginger and carrot. Cook and stir until cabbage is limp. Mix in ground pork. Cook until pork is evenly brown
Preheat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.  Place approximately 1 tablespoon of the cabbage and pork mixture in the center of each wrapper. Fold wrappers in half over filling, and seal edges with moistened fingers.
In the preheated vegetable oil, cook gyoza approximately 1 minute per side, until lightly browned. Place water into skillet and reduce heat. Cover and allow gyoza to steam until the water is gone.
