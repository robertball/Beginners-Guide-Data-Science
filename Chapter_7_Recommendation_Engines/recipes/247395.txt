Title:
Horsey Beef Pretzel Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4121176.jpg
Ingredients:
24 Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
8 ounces thinly sliced deli roast beef, cut or torn into bite-size pieces
8 ounces sliced smoked mozzarella cheese, cut into 24 pieces
1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives (optional)

Directions:
Arrange Pretzel Crisps(R) onto a large tray. Top with roast beef and a piece of mozzarella cheese. Add a slice or 2 of red onion. Put a dollop (about 1/4 teaspoon) of horseradish on the onion. Garnish with chives.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
A roast beef sandwich made into a delicious little bite. Seasoned roast beef, smoky mozzarella, and red onion are topped with a creamy horseradish sauce! You know you want some!
