Title:
POG Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6476824.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups Passion Fruit Juice (such as Welch's)
6 cups Guava Nectar (such as Goya)
3 cups fresh squeezed OJ

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a large glass pitcher and stir well. Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
If you've eaten breakfast at Walt Disney World (or visited Hawaii) then you've probably had P.O.G Juice. It's an amazingly refreshing blend of passion fruit, orange juice, and guava nectar. Enjoy!
