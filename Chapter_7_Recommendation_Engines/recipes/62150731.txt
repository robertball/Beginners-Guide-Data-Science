Title:
Plum Buckle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Batter:
2/3 c. butter
3 c. sifted flour
1 1/2 c. sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
3 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
2 c. milk
7 c. plums, pitted and quartered
Topping:
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. butter, softened
1/4 c. flour
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Place plums in bottom of greased 9x13 pan.
Make batter: cream together butter and sugar. Add in eggs. Mix in flour, baking powder, and salt. Add milk and extracts. Mix until smooth.
Pour batter over plums in pan.
Make topping: Combine all ingredients and crumble over batter and plums.
Bake at 350 for 45-50 min.

Submitter: 
prettykitten
Description: 
We used to make this as a great way to sweeten the tart plums from the tree in our yard, but any plums will do. Great when served warm with vanilla ice cream.
