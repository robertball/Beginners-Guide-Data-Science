Title:
French Onion with Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves of garlic, minced
2 red onions, halved then sliced
4 large white mushrooms, sliced
2 cans (10 oz fl) beef broth
1 can water
1/2 cup red wine
1 tbsp dried parsley
1 tbsp dried basil
2 slices of bread, cut into a circle and toasted
4 slices of mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat broiler at 350'F,
Heat olive oil on medium-high heat in a large pot.
Saute garlic, onions and mushrooms until onions are softened.  Add a little water to help soften onions.
Add broth, water, wine, parsley and basil  to onion mixture and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and let simmer for 10 minutes.
Pour soup into oven-safe bowls.
Place on toasted bread circle on top of soup.
Cover with 2 slices of cheese.
Place bowls of cookie sheet and place under the broiler until cheese has melted and browned, about 5 minutes.

Description: 
I decided to use ingredients I had around the house and see if I could make a French Onions Soup.  I found this soup to be very flavourful.
