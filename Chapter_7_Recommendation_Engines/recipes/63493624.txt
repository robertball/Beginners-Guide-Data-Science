Title:
Spaghetti Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one spaghetti squash
2 can chopped tomato's
1/2 cup fresh chopped basil
1/4 cup fresh oregano
2 cloves of garlic
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
poke holes in squash with fork and microwave until tender. Remove seeds and scrape out the squash strings with a fork into oiled casserole dish. Pour in tomato's, add basil, oregano, garlic, salt and 3/4 of the cheese. mix well  sprinkle rest of the cheese on the top. bake at 400 for 45-60 depending on the size of the squash.

A healthy low carb dish everyone one likes. I have taken it to potlucks and people love it.
