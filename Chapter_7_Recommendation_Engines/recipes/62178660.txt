Title:
Bavarina jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3oz jello
1/4C sugar
1C boiling water
3/4C cold water
1/2 pint whipped cream

Directions:
Dissolve jello and sugar in the boiling water, then add the cold water. chill slightly until thickened. blend in the cool whip. chill
