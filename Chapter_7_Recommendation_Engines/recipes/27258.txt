Title:
Crispy Pesto Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984891.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds baby red potatoes, halved, cooked and drained
1/4 cup BUITONI® Refrigerated Pesto with Basil

Directions:
PREHEAT broiler.  Line baking sheet with heavy-duty foil.
PLACE hot potatoes and pesto in large bowl; toss to coat.  Spread potatoes on prepared baking sheet.
BROIL about 5 inches from heat for 4 to 5 minutes or until potatoes are lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Buitoni®
Description: 
You're two ingredients away from this delicious, quick side dish - a perfect companion to any entree.
