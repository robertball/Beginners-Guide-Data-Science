Title:
Dill Mayonnaise Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 tsp dill weed

Directions:
Combine mayonnaise, garlic and dill weed in a small bowl, cover and refrigerate for one hour.

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
This dill mayo dip is scrumptious with my quick and easy chicken nuggets!
