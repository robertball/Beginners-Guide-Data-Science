Title:
Honey Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups warm water
1 pkg. dry yeast
1/4 c. honey
1/4 c.packed brown sugar
1 stick softened butter
3/4 t. salt
1 1/2 c wheat flour
1 1/2 c rolled oats
4 c. bread flour

Directions:
preheat oven to 375. combine all ingredients & knead about 7 minuts, until no wet but smooth. Let bread rise till doubled in size. Punch down & shape into loaves. Cover & let rise till double in a warm place. Shape and bake until lightly browned and crusty on top.
About 1 hour.
