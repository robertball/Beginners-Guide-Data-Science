Title:
tuna salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. macaroni, cooked
1/3 c. mayo
1/3 c. Ital. dressing
1 T. mustard
2 c. thin, pared cucumber slices
2 diced tomatoes
½ c. diced green pepper
¼ c. chopped green onion
1 tsp. salt, 1/8 tsp. pepper
2 cans (6 oz) tuna, drained
1 hard-cooked egg, chopped

Directions:
Combine mayo, dressing and mustard.  Add remaining ingredients.  Toss well.  Chill at least four hours.
