Title:
Cajun Mac-n-Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. roll sausage
1/2 stick butter
1/2 bunch of green onions, chopped
1/2 tbsp. diced garlic
2 cups heavy cream
1 tbsp Tony Cachere's (or less, to taste)
1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar
6 oz. large elbow noodles

Directions:
Brown sausage & set aside.
Cook elbow noodles, drain and set aside.
Meanwhile, saute green onions and garlic in butter till soft. Add cream & Tony's. Stir in cheeses until melted.
Mix all together and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Tammy Adkins Byerly
Description: 
A spicy, meaty macaroni and cheese with some cajun kick
