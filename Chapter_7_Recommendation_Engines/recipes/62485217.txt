Title:
Garlic Mashed Potatoes Secret Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound small unpeeled red potatoes
1 tablespoon light whipped butter
1/2 ounce Parmesan or Romano cheese, grated
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm,  about 30 minutes; drain.  Stir in butter, cheese, garlic, and salt.  Mash with a potato masher.
