Title:
Potions motion punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Strawberry Fanta soda. (This works best flavor wise)
Strawberry vodka
Cheap tequila
Fireball
Peaches
Black cherries
Dry ice.

Directions:
Prepare your container that will hold the punch.  Pour 2 liter bottle of strawberry Fanta into container
Add whole bottle of the vodka while mixing slowly add 1/3 bottle of the tequila less depending on how strong  it is. Spoon feed fireball til the slight cinnamon taste is there.
Slice the peaches into slices . Remove cherry pits and add to the punch.
Add in the dry ice for effect and let chill for a few hours 2-3
Drink responsibly

A fun boozy drink for adults .
