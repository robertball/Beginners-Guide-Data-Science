Title:
Hot Dog Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package hot dogs (any kind)
2 cans condensed vegetable soup
4-6 potatoes, mashed (or boxed mashed potatoes)
2-3 slices of american cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Use a large pan that can be used on a burner and in the oven.  Over medium heat, brown sliced hot dogs (use 8-10 hot dogs from package).  Add 2 cans of soup, do not add water.  Cook until warmed through.  Top with hot mashed potatoes and torn pieces of cheese.  Warm in oven until cheese is melted (3-8 minutes).

Submitter: 
Bonnie D
Description: 
Quick and easy casserole topped with mashed potatoes and cheese.  My kids love this, but it's a favorite with my husband.
