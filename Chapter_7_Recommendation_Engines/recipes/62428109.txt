Title:
Nicole's Finnish Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/449098.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup sugar
2 cups milk
1 1/4 cup flour
1/4 pound butter (1 small stick)
2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Beat eggs, salt and sugar until frothy, the add in other ingredients.
Melt half the butter in 9x12 pan in oven. Once butter melted pour in mixture and sprinkle cinnamon on top.
Bake for 20-25 minutes at 425 degrees.
Serve with *REAL* maple syrup...not the fake...

Description: 
A mix between flan and creamy texture, this receipe will be a hit with anyone you serve to!
