Title:
Oatmeal Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488522.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup large flake oats
2 cups buttermilk
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/4 cup ground flaxseed
1 tbsp Splenda or Stevia
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs or 1/2 cup of liquid egg
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp vanilla
Serve with
Maple syrup or peanut butter and choice of fruit

Directions:
In a bowl, soak oats in buttermilk for 20 min
While you are waiting for the oats to soak prepare other dry and wet ingredients.
In another bowl combine flour, flaxseed, Splenda, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt and stir.
Mix together eggs, olive oil, vanilla and blend in with oats and buttermilk mixture.
Pour wet ingredients over dry ingredients and mix well
Heat a non-stick griddle over medium heat and ladle 1/4 cup of batter on pan
Cook until pancake bubbles on top, approx 2 min on both sides or golden.
