Title:
Fried Noodles Singapore Style (Spicy) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 kg fresh yellow noodles
300g bean sprouts
3 bunches of Chinese Mustard leaves (cut into 1 inch lengths)
200g beef, mutton or chicken (cut into small pieces)
200g shrimp, fish cakes or squid (cut into small pieces if desired)
Ingredients to be pounded or finely chopped:
1 large onion
15 cloves of garlic
10 red chilis (fresh or dry)
2 teaspoons of a mixture of finely ground fennel seed, cumin seed and corriander)
1 tablespoon tomato paste or sauce
1 tablespoon black soya sauce (dark)
Salt to taste

Directions:
Saute the finely ground ingredients in a wok.
Add in meat and seafood after the aroma of the sauteed ingredients starts to become strong.
When the meat and seafood are cooked, add in tomato paste and soya sauce.
Mix thoroughly, then add in the noodles.
When noodles are half cooked, add in the STEMS of the mustard leaves.
When the STEMS are half-cooked, add in the bean sprouts.
When the bean sprouts are half-cooked add in the mustard leaves.
Stir until the vegetables are cooked... and its done!
