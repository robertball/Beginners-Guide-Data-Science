Title:
Chicken Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 whole shinned and boned chicken breast
salt and pepper
1/4 cup flour
4TBS butter
1 cup mushroom sliced
1 or 2 garlic cloves minced
1/4 cup of white wine or chicken broth
2 TBS lemon juice

Directions:
Pound chicken until thin. Salt and pepper then place in plastic bag with flour and coat. In a large skillet add 3 TBS butter until melted. Add chicken and brown on both sides. Remove and keep warm.
Add remaining butter and add mushrooms and garlic until tender. Return chicken to pan , add wine and lemon juice. Simmer for 7-10 minutes until done. Stirring occasionally till sauce slightly thickens. Serves four
