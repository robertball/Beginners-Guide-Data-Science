Title:
Friendship Tea - from Indiana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/719483.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Tang Instant Drink
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1/2 cup Instant Tea
1 .25oz pkg. unsweetened lemonade
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a container with a tight fitting lid or in a gallon zip-lock bag.  Stir or shake until well blended.
Put 2 heaping tablespoons in a 1-cup mug of hot water.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
A friend gave me this tea and the recipe about 20 years ago.  It is a nice blend of orange and lemon along with instant tea.  I enjoy it best on a chilly fall or winter night.
