Title:
Cold Mocha Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. sugar
1 1/2 qt. water
1/2 c. chocolate milk mix
1/2 c. instant coffee
1/2 gal. chocolate ice cream
1/2 gal. vanilla ice cream
1 c. whipping cream

Directions:
Bring water to a boil and stir in the sugar, chocolate milk mix, and instant coffee.
Cover and put in the refridgerator overnight.
The next day, 30 minutes before serving, put this mixture in a punch bowl and add the ice cream and whipped topping.
Stir until it starts to become frothy.
Emjoy!
