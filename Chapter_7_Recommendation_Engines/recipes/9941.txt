Title:
Congo Bars I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/803173.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2/3 cup shortening
2 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
3 eggs
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Melt shortening, stir in brown sugar.  Let cool.
Beat in the eggs.
Add dry ingredients.
Add chocolate chips, nuts and vanilla.
Pour in 10 x 15-inch pan and bake  for 30 minutes.  Cool and cut into squares.
