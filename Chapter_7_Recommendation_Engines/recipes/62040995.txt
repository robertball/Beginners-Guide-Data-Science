Title:
SLUSH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
7 cups boiling water
1 large can frozen lemonade
1 small can frozen orange juice
2 cups strong tea
1 cup vodka

Directions:
Boil 2 cups water with 4 tea bags to make 2 cups of strong tea, let cool.  Boil 7 cups of water with sugar in it, let cool.  Add all ingredients together and freeze.  To serve, fill a glass with slush and add 7-up or ginger-ale.  Keep slush in freezer.

Description: 
Refreshing summertime drink with low amount of alcohol.  Kept in freezer
