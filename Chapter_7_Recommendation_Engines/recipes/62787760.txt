Title:
Marinated Flank Steak with Horseradish Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
STEAK
1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce
1/2 cup dry sherry
1/2 an onion, finely chopped
1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, finely chopped
1 heaping tablespoon prepared minced garlic
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tsp. Black pepper
1 1/2 lb flank steak
SAUCE
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tablespponds prepared horseradish
3 green onions, finely chopped
Salt and pepper

Directions:
FOR STEAK:  Combine the marinade ingredieints in a bowl and mix well.  Pierce the steak with a large fork throughout, flip and repeat. Place the steak in a zip-lock bag and add marinade.  Marinate overnight refridgerated.
FOR SAUCE:  Combine all ingredients and season with S & P.  Cover and refridgerate.  (can be made 1 day ahead)
Prepare BBQ.  Drain steak, and pour marinade into a small saucepan and boil 1 minute.  Season steak with generous amounts of pepper.  Grill to desired doneness, basting occasionally with marinade.  Transfer steak to plate and let stand 15 minutes.  Cut steak across the grain into thin diagonal slices.  Serve hot or room temp with horseradish sauce.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This is a summer grilling masterpiece!  I like to serve slices on a pretty platter and top with blistered cherry tomatoes.  Garnish the platter with rosemary sprigs and be prepared to wow your guests.
