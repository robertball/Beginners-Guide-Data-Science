Title:
Seafood Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 large onion, chopped
2-3 peeled potatoes, chopped
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Potato Soup
1 1/2 (10.75 ounce) cans  milk
1/4 teaspoon dried dill weed, crushed
1/2 pound medium fresh or thawed frozen shrimp, shelled and deveined
1/2 pound fresh or thawed frozen firm white fish fillet, cut into 1-inch pieces (I used Sole)
Chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Heat oil in saucepot. Add onion, potatoes, and garlic and cook until tender.
Add soups, milk and dill. Heat to a boil.
Add shrimp and fish. Cook 5 minutes over low heat or until done (can also add fish to potato mixture and cook then). Garnish with parsley.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Shrimp and white fish simmer quickly in a creamy potato broth spiked with sauteed onion, garlic and dill.Modified from: Seafood Chowder
