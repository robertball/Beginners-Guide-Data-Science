Title:
Creamy Tomato Parmesan Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14.5 oz Italian Style Tomatoes
1 can Tomato Soup
1 14.5 can Chicken Broth
1/4 cup Butter
1 pint Heavy Cream
1 .5 oz container shredded Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
1.Puree the Italian Style Tomatoes in food processor.
2.In a large sauce pan combine the tomatoes, soup and chicken broth.
3. Bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes.
4. Add the butter, cream and Parmesan stirring until combined
5. Return just to a simmer then remove from heat and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Todi
Description: 
This is a creamy and extremely flavorful twist on a tried and true classic! I serve it with a pasta salad and crusty bread. It also goes great with grilled cheese sandwiches.
