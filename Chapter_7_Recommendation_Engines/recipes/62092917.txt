Title:
Taco Crescent Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of ground meat or diced chicken
1 can of seasoned black beans
1 can niblet corn
1 package taco seasoning
1 cup shredded cheddar or mexican blend cheese
1 can refrigerated crescent rolls

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray a large glass or stone baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
Arrange crescent rolls in dish (overlapping each roll) with the top point of roll hanging over outside of dish and set aside.
Brown ground meat or chicken and prepare with taco seasoning mix according to package directions.
Stir in beans, corn, & cheese and mix well.
Spoon meat mixture onto crescent rolls and fold the top point of each roll underneath the bottom of roll.
Bake for approximately 25 minutes or until golden brown.
Serve with your choice of toppings. We like sour cream, salsa, and a little extra cheese.  Enjoy!
