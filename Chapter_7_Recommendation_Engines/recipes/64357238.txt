Title:
Roast Beef Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 medium onion, chopped
1/4 lb fresh mushrooms, sliced
3 cups cooked roast beef, cubed
3 Tbsp flour
2-1/2 cups beef consomme (or broth)
1/2 cup red wine
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp marjoram
Salt & pepper to taste
Baking powder biscuits (or pastry)  not baked

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
In Dutch oven, heat oil and saute garlic, onion and mushrooms.
Stir in meat and saute lightly.  Sprinkle with flour and mix well.
Pour on consomme and wine, stirring constantly until sauce is smooth and thickened.  Season to taste with salt and pepper, and add marjoram and Worchestershire sauce.
Pour into greased dish and top with biscuits or pastry.  Slash to allow steam to escape.
Bake for 20 minutes, until top is golden brown.
