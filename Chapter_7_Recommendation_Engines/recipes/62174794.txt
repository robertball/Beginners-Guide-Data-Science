Title:
Baked Quinces with Cinammon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/287132.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large quinces unpeeled, washed, cut in halves and cored
18 cloves
6 tbs sugar
3 cinnamon sticks
½ cup sweet, red wine or any fruit liqueur
1 ½ cup water
6 tbs whipping cream or 6 scoops vanilla ice cream (optional)

Directions:
Poke each half quince with 3 cloves. Place the quinces in a roasting pan, cut surface down. Add water, wine and cinnamon. Sprinkle with sugar.
Bake 35-40 minutes at 375 degrees F or until lightly brown. Overturn and bake for 10 more minutes.
Let quinces cool and serve them topped with whipping cream or ice cream and 1 tbs of the juice of the roasting pan.
