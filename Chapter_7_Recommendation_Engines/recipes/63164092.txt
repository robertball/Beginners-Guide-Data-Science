Title:
Calico Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 whole onion, chopped
2 slices bacon
1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 tablespoon salt
1/2 tablespoon ground mustard
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 tablespoon vinegar
1 15 oz. cans butter beans, drained and rinsed
1 15 oz. cans kidney beans, drained and rinsed
8 ounces pork and beans, undrained

Directions:
Saute onion and bacon until onion is tender and bacon is browned; drain and pour into slow cooker.  Add all remaining ingredients to slow cooker.  Cook on low 4-6 hours.
