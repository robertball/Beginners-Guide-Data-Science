Title:
Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C sugar
1 1/3 C half and half or milk
1/4 C corn syrup (light is fine)
1/4 tsp salt
2/3 C cocoa
1/4 C butter or stick margarine
2 tsp vanilla
1 C chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Grease 9x9 pan
Butter sides of a 3 quart saucepan
Mix sugar, milk, corn syrup, cocoa, and salt in saucepan
Cook on medium heat (stir constantly!) until sugar dissolves
Cook to 234 degrees or to soft ball stage (about 25 minutes)
Remove from heat
Drop butter on top, but don't stir
Cool (without stirring) to 110 degrees (about 2 hours)
Add vanilla
Beat vigorously with wooden spoon until mixture thickens
Add nuts
Beat for another 5-10 minutes or until no longer glossy
Mixture should hold its shape when dropped from a spoon
Quickly spread or press into greased pan
Let stand for 1-2 hours, or until firm
Cut
