Title:
Spinach and Noodles Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 package (10 ounces) frozen chopped spinach
1 can condensed cream of celery soup
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
Dash of ground red cayenne pepper
2 cups cooked wide egg noodles (6 ounces uncooked)

Directions:
Place onion in 2-quart casserole dish.  Cover with lid and microwave on high for 3 minutes, stirring once during cooking.
Add spinach.  Cover and microwave on high for 5 minutes or until spinach is thawed, stirring twice during cooking.  Stir in soup, sour cream, cheese, black pepper, nutmeg and red pepper.
Cover and microwave at 50% power for 8 minutes, stirring once during cooking.  Add hot noodles and toss.
