Title:
Crystal's Beefy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
3 pounds ground chuck
3 cups chopped yellow onion
3 cloves garlic, minced
4 tbsp chilli powder
1 tbsp italian seasoning
2 tsp salt
2 tbsp cumin
1/4 tsp black pepper
28 oz. can whole peeled tomatoes, chopped and undrained
1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
1/4 cup sugar
2 cups water

Directions:
Heat oil in large heavy pot and cook ground chuck for 5 minutes.  Add onion, garlic, chilli powder, italian seasoning, salt, cumin, & pepper.  Combine well and cook for about 5 minutes.  Add the chopped tomatoes and juice, tomato paste, & water to the pot.  Stir well and bring to a boil.
Lower heat to med-low, and cook for 30-40 minutes, stirring often to prevent chili to sticking to bottom of pot.

Description: 
A hearty and beefy, no bean, chili recipe.  I have 2 small children & a picky husband - and they all rave about this wonderful dish!
