Title:
Nancy's Sensational Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lb Roma tomatoes, halved
1 red onion, cut into 1" pieces
6 garlic cloves
3 jalapenos
1 bunch cilantro
3 T sugar
2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat broiler in  the oven.  Place tomatoes, onions, garlic and jalapenos on sheet tray.  For a milder salsa, remove seeds and veins from jalapenos.  Sprinkle with salt.  Roast for 5 minutes, stir around and roast for another 5 minutes.  Remove from oven and let cool slightly.  Add the remaining ingredients and half the tomato mixture to food processor and pulse until texture is coarse.  Pour into bowl and pulse remaining tomato mixture.  Stir all together and chill.  Salsa keeps for up to 1 week in the refrigerator.
