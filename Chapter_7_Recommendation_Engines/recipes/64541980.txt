Title:
Dover Sole Meuniure Recipe

Ingredients:
4 Dover Sole filets (~1 pound)
3 Tablespoons butter
Salt to taste
All purpose flour
3 Tablespoons lemon juice
3 Tablespoons dry white wine

Directions:
Place 3 tablespoons butter in a large saut√© pan on medium heat
Season sole filets with salt then dust with flour
Cook fish 4 minutes per side, turning once
Remove from pan
Stir lemon juice into butter
Stir wine into lemon butter
Serve lemon butter over fish
