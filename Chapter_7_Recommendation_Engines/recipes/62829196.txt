Title:
Chicken Carbonara Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/669903.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Chopped Mushrooms
1/2 cups Chopped Onion
1 cup Bacon Pieces (cooked)
2 whole Boneless Chicken Breasts
1 whole Pizza Crust
2 cups Alfredo Sauce
Oregano
Garlic Powder
1/2 cups Grated Parmesan Cheese
8 ounces, weight Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ÂºF.
Chop mushrooms, onion and bacon and set aside.
Boil or bake the boneless chicken breast. When fully cooked, cut up into small pieces.
Roll out dough onto pizza pan and pour on 1 cup of alfredo sauce. Spread the sauce around and then sprinkle on the mushrooms, onion, bacon and chicken.
Add 1 more cup of alfredo sauce on top and oregano and garlic powder, along with parmesan cheese.
Finally, add the shredded mozzarella cheese. Lightly brush the edge of the pizza with olive oil.
Place in the oven for about 15 minutes. Depending on your oven you may need to leave it in longer. Once cheese has started to brown, it should be ready!
Cut and enjoy!
