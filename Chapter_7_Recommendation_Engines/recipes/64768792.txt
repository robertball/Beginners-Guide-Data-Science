Title:
Vegan Black Bean Burger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7420778.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, diced
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 celery stalks, chopped
2 medium sweet potatoes, small cubes
1- (15oz can) Low Sodium Black Beans
1- (32 oz) vegetable broth
3 Tbsp. tomato paste
1 tsp. ground mustard
3 Tbsp. nutritional yeast
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. white pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
4 oz fresh baby spinach
1 (10oz) bag Beyond Meat Beefy Crumbles

Directions:
1. In a medium stock pot combine olive oil, garlic, celery, and onion. Saute over medium high until soft, about 5 minutes.
2. Combine tomato paste, ground mustard,salt, and pepper into pot.
3. Add vegetable broth and sweet potatoes. Bring to a boil uncovered for approx. 6 minutes or until potatoes are tender, stirring occasionally.
4. Reduce heat to low.  Add in black beans, spinach, and Beyond Meat Beefy Crumbles. Heat soup approx. 10 minutes until spinach softens and soup is heated.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
A simple plant based soup containing sweet poatoes, black beans, and beyond meat beef crumbles. Ready in under 45 minutes to warm you up with great health benefits!
