Title:
Turmeric Sun Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2140518.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 avocado, peeled and pitted
1 lemons, zested and juiced
1 tablespoon ground turmeric
1 tablespoon raw honey
1 clove garlic, minced
Himalayan salt to taste

Directions:
Blend olive oil, avocado, lemon zest, lemon juice, turmeric, honey, garlic, and salt together on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
THOSJOYCE
Description: 
Quick. A bit different. Wonderful tang. Excellent for so many salads. Great as anti-inflammatory for what ails ya too.
Can use on basting chicken too. Can add more avocado to make a bit thicker.
