Title:
Sweet 'n' Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 eggs
9 medium onion, chopped
14 cups dry bread crumbs
2 #10 Lbs pounds ground beef
1 Cup vegetable oil
SAUCE:
1 #10 Cans tomato sauce
2 cups of water
10 cups packed brown sugar
10 finely crushed gingersnap or cooked off sugar cookies
1 Tablespoon of cinnamon or ginger
2 cups white vinegar
3 tablespoons onion powder

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the eggs, onion, bread crumbs. Crumble beef over mixture and mix well. Shape into 1-1/2-in. balls. In a large skillet, brown meatballs in batches in oil. transfer to a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. small hotel pan remember to batch cook.
In a saucepan, combine the sauce ingredients. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring until cookie crumbs are dissolved.ONLY COOK 1/2 FOR LINE!!! BATCH COOK!! Pour over meatballs. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 40-45 minutes or until meat is no longer pink.
