Title:
Royal Flush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980674.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 ounce Crown Royal® blended Canadian whisky
1/2 ounce peach schnapps
1/2 ounce raspberry liqueur
2 ounces cranberry juice
1 slice orange

Directions:
Add Crown Royal(R) blended Canadian whisky, peach schnapps, raspberry liqueur, and cranberry juice to a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
Shake with ice and strain into highball glass.
Garnish with orange slice.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Classic cocktails need the authentic taste of Crown Royal® blended Canadian whisky, with a delicately smooth, creamy taste and long-lasting finish.
