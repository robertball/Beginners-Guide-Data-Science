Title:
Herbed Chicken Tenderloins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977868.jpg
Ingredients:
16 Tyson® Individually Fresh Frozen® Boneless Skinless Chicken Tenderloins
1/4 cup margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary leaves, crushed
1/2 teaspoon dried sage
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper

Directions:
PREP: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. CLEAN: Wash hands. Remove protective ice glaze from frozen chicken by holding under cool running water for 1 to 2 minutes. CLEAN: Wash hands. In a small bowl, combine melted margarine, oregano, rosemary, sage, salt and pepper.
COOK: Place frozen tenderloins on foil-lined baking pan. Brush evenly with all of herb mixture. Bake at 375 degrees F for 18 to 22 minutes or until internal juices run clear. (Or insert instant-read meat thermometer in thickest part of chicken. Temperature should read 170 degrees F.)
SERVE: Serve chicken with steamed new potatoes and green beans, if desired.
CHILL: Refrigerate leftovers immediately.

Submitter: 
Tyson Foods
Description: 
Boneless skinless breast tenderloins basted with a rosemary herb marinate.  Great served with steamed new potatoes and green beans.
