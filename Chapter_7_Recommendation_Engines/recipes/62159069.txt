Title:
Chipotle Black Bean Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz can black beans drained
1/4 cup leftover canned black bean juice
1 1/2 tbsp tahini (sesame seed paste)
3 tbsp. freshly squeezed lime juice
2 cloves garlic
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
2 small chipotle peppers (canned in adobo sauce)

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in blender or food processor until smooth and creamy.

Submitter: 
Rachel
Description: 
Creamy black bean hummus with a smoky chipotle flavor and a hint of lime.
