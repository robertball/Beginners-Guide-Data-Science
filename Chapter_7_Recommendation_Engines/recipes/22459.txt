Title:
Mini Kisses Butter Pecan Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 (10 ounce) package HERSHEY®'S MINI KISSES™ Milk Chocolate Baking Pieces, divided
3/4 cup chopped pecans, divided

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 F.  Grease 8-inch square baking pan.
Beat butter, brown sugar, egg and vanilla in medium bowl; beat in flour.  Stir in 3/4 cup baking pieces and 1/4 cup pecans; spread evenly into prepared pan.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned; remove from oven. Immediately sprinkle remaining 1 cup baking pieces over top.  Let stand 5 minutes or until chocolate softens.  Spread evenly with knife. Immediately sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup pecans over top; press gently.  Cool completely in pan on wire rack.  Cut into squares.  16 squares.

Classic chocolate chip bars with a chocolate and pecan frosting melted onto the top.
