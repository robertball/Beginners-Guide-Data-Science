Title:
Baked Hot Dog Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 hot dogs, chopped
2/3 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
3 tablespoons pickle relish
3 tablespoons ketchup
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
3 tablespoons chopped onion
8 hot dog buns

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Stir the hot dogs, Cheddar cheese, relish, ketchup, mustard, and onion together in a bowl; spoon into the hot dog buns. Arrange the filled buns on a baking sheet. Wrap individually in aluminum foil.
Bake in the preheated oven until hot, about 20 minutes. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Renee Laufenberg
Description: 
Chopped hot dogs and traditional toppings are mixed together and stuffed into buns before going into the oven in this variation on the all-American tradition.Modified from: Baked Hot Dog Sandwiches
