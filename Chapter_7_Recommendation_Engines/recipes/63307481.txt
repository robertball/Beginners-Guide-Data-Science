Title:
Homemade Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 raw egg
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1 cup vinegar mixture (3/4 cup vinegar + 1/4 cup water)
1 tablespoon flour (lightly heaping)
salt & pepper to taste (I don't use salt I only use black pepper.)
3/4 cup sugar

Directions:
In a blender place the egg, dry mustard, vinegar mixture and flour into a blender.  Blend until well blended.
Pour into a mixture in a saucpan on  low-medium heat.  Add sugar, salt & pepper.  Stirring constantly cook on medium heat.  Mixture will come to a slow simmering boil as you stir.
Once it comes to the slow boil, remove from heat, set aside to let cool.
Pour dressing into a clean dry glass jar.  Cover with lid and store in refrigerator.
When you are ready to make cole slaw or potato salad use equal parts of this homemade mayonnaise with Hellman's Mayonnaise.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This is an old family recipe combining this homemade mayonnaise with equal parts of Hellman's mayonnaise (Best Foods mayonnaise) for making cole slaw or potato salad.
Make this mayonnaise ahead of time, store in a glass jar in your refrigerator to have on hand when you are ready to use it. 
It keeps for months in the refrigerator.
