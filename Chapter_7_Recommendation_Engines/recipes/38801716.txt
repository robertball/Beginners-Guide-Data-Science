Title:
Heidi's Shrimp Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds shrimp, peeled, deveined and cooked
1 pound linguine pasta
2 bell peppers, diced-any color
2 jars alfredo sauce (I prefer Ragu)
2 teaspoons black pepper
1 tablespoon crushed red pepper flakes, optional
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 14oz bag frozen broccoli, thawed and drained-this is optional

Directions:
In a large skillet, sautee shrimp, peppers, broccoli and seasonings until softened; add sauce and simmer.
In a large pot, bring water to a boil and cook linguine according to package instructions.
Drain pasta and return to pot, add sauce/shrimp mixture into pot and toss together.
Serve with garlic bread and a salad.
