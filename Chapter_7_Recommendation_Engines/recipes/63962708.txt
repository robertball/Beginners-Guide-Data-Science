Title:
Spinach Dip Monson Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Knorr Vegetable Soup Mix
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
1 package frozen chopped spinach, unfrozen, sqeezed
1 can water chestnuts, chopped
4 tablespoons fresh red onion, chopped fine

Directions:
Mix and let set overnight.  If too thick add mayo.
Serve with french bread, in round bread.
