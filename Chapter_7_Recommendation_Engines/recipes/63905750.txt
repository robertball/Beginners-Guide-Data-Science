Title:
Home-Fried Sweet Potato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Ibs Sweet Potatoes
1/4 cup Oil
1/4 cup Butter
1/8 tsp Cinnamon
1Tbsp Sugar
Dash Salt

Directions:
1. Peel and cut sweet potatoes. When peeling large amounts soak peeled potatoes in cold water to prevent turning dark.
2. Heat oil and butter on medium heat stovetop.
3. Fry sweet potatoes until center is soft, turning at least once. Keep lightly covered to cook faster and prevent popping oil.
4. Sprinkle salt, sugar, and cinnamon immediately after removing potatoes from oil and mix lightly.

Submitter: 
Alicia Taylor
Description: 
My grandfather grew up on a sweet potato farm, and I grew up watching him make this for breakfast or brunch.
