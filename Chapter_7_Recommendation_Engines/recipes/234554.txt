Title:
Parmesan Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1050446.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon Carlini 100% Pure Olive Oil
1 onion, thinly sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
4 zucchini, sliced
1/2 teaspoon Stonemill Essentials Iodized Salt
1/4 teaspoon Stonemill Essentials Ground Black Pepper
1/4 cup PrianoÂ® Shredded Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
In a large saute pan, heat the oil over high heat. Saute the onions until they begin to soften.
Add the garlic, saute one minute. Add the sliced zucchini. Cook, covered, until the zucchini is crisp and tender.
Season with salt and pepper. Toss with Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
ALDI
Description: 
Quickly cooked zucchini and onion slices are tossed with Parmesan cheese for an easy and delicious veggie side dish.
