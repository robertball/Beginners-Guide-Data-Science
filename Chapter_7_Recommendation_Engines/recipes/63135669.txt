Title:
Floor Wash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Distilled white Vinegar
1/2 capful of liquid castile soap
2 Gallons warm water.

Directions:
Add Vinegar and soap to the bucket, then add the water (stopping about 7" from the top).  Swish with the mop to mix.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
Vinegar is a safe, nontoxic antiseptic, that you can let dry on nearly any type of flooring including linoleum, vinyl, tile and wood.  The soap offers just enough detergent to dislodge stubborn dirt.  Makes approx 2 gallons at a cost of approx 25 Cents!!!
