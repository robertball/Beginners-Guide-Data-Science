Title:
Kay's Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup oil
1/4 cup half and half milk

Directions:
Put flour and salt in a large bowl.  Pour milk into the oil and then into the flour.  Mix until just blended.  Turn onto wax paper and, using rolling pie, roll out to pie crust.  Blanch in 400 degree oven for 8 minutes before filling.
