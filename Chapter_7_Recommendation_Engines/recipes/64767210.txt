Title:
Stuffed Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs small ripe Tomatoes
10 ounces of sharp Cheddar
4 Cloves of Garlic
1 tbsp each of fresh Parsley, Dill and Basil
3.5 ounces of Mayonnaise
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Wash, dry and remove the top 1/3 of each Tomato;  remove seed/Meat.
Stuff  the Tomatoes with Grated Cheese, Mayonnaise and mix of grated Garlic, Herbs, salt & Pepper.
arrange on a plate with a Bed of  Green Salad and garnish with Parsley and Basil Twig/Leaf.
This may be served Hot or Cold.!!   in Terms,  Bake at 360. until Cheese is melted and Tomato is Soft.

Submitter: 
Alois
Description: 
Tomatoes stuffed with  a picante Cheese filling.
