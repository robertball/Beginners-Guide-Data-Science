Title:
Strawberry pretzel salad (not layered) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup crushed pretzels
1/2 cup melted butter
1 cup sugar (divided)
1 8oz pkj cream cheese
2 cups fresh strawberries (I have used diced frozen)
2 6oz strawberry yoghurt
2 8oz cool whip

Directions:
Combine pretzels, butter and 1/2 cup sugar. Press into 9X13 pan. Bake 7min at 400. Set aside until cool and crumble. (make day ahead if needed.)
In bowl mix sugar, yogurt and cream cheese. Fold in strawberries and cool whip. Refrigerate. Add pretzels just before serving.
