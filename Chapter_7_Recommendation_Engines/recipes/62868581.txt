Title:
Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 chicken OR turkey thighs
1 can of cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c. milk
2 boxes of stuffing
1/2 stick butter/margarine
1 c. brother

Directions:
Cook the meat in a pot half-full of water. Let cool, then pull the meat off the bones, tossing it into a 9" x 13" pan. Reserve 1 c. of the broth made by cooking the thighs.
Mix soup and milk together and pour over the poultry.
Make the stuffing and cover the chicken with it. Melt the butter and broth together and pour over all.
Cook at 350 degrees, covered, for 30 minutes, then uncover and cook for another 30.
Let sit for 15 minutes before serving.
