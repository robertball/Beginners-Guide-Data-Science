Title:
Roasted Whole Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken
mustard
1 tablespoon salt
3 cups potatoes OR 1 can chick peas
2 sprigs of rosemary

Directions:
Chicken Prep Night Before:
With scissors, cut open the chicken along its front.
Rub whole chicken with mustard then sprinkle with salt.
Marinade in the fridge overnight.
The Day Of:
Preheat oven to 425F.
Cut up potatoes to bite size pieces.
Lay chicken like a butterfly in roasting pan with the skin facing up.
Put potatoes or chickpeas around chicken.
Put springs of rosemary in pan.
Roast at 425F for 35minutes. Chicken should be browned and crispy.

Submitter: 
Amy Lee
Description: 
Prepare chicken the night before and put in the fridge to marinade overnight. You could add either potatoes or chick peas in the roasting pan.
