Title:
My Favorite Thousand Island Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup ketchup
1/3 cup sweet pickle relish
2 teaspoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons sugar (or 1 packet sweetener)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl until well blended. Serve over your favorite salad greens.

Submitter: 
Francesca
Description: 
This is our all time favorite dressing. Uses simple ingredients that we always have on hand. It's great on simple green salads, taco salads, and even seafood salads.  Also great as a burger sauce. I found this recipe on a jar of mayo years ago.
