Title:
Zucchini Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 onion, sliced
salt and pepper to taste
4 zucchini, halved and sliced
1 tomato, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
Fresh basil and parsley

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  Saute onion and garlic with salt and pepper for 2 minutes.  Stir in zucchini.  When zucchini begins to soften, add tomatoes, basil, and parsley.  Cook until heated through.

Submitter: 
Gina Guerra
Description: 
Zucchini is sauteed with red onion, mushrooms, tomato and herbs.  You can use any veggies you like.  Serve over pasta, if desired.Modified from: Zucchini Saute
