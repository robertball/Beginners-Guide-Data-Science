Title:
Cowboy Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C. Flour
2 C. brown sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
2/3 C. shortening
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 C. sour milk (1 T. lemon juice + enough milk to make 1 cup)
2 well beaten eggs

Directions:
Combine flour, brown sugar, salt and shortening.  Reserve 1/2 Cup.  To remaining crumbs, add baking powder, baking soda, and spices.  Mix.  Add eggs and milk.  Mix well.  Pour into 2 greased and floured 8" round pans.  Sprinkle with reserved crumbs.
Bake 375 for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
jeanneferg
Description: 
This coffee cake makes the whole house smell wonderful.  It tastes wonderful warm or cold.  This recipe makes 2 cakes!!
