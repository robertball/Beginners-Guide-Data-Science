Title:
Apricot Crumb Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pastry for one-crust pie
FILLING:
1 cup white sugar
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon lemon juice
5 cups fresh apricots, pitted and quartered
CRUMB TOPPING:
½ cup flour
½ cup brown sugar
3 Tbsp. butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Prepare pie crust for one-crust pie and place in 9" pie plate.
FILLING: Mix the sugar, 1/4 cup flour, and cinnamon together in a large bowl until thoroughly combined; stir in the lemon juice and apricots. Spoon the apricot filling over the pie crust in the dish.
CRUMB TOPPING: Combine all ingredients with pastry cutter until crumbly. Sprinkle evenly over fruit.
Cover pie loosely with aluminum foil. Bake 25 minutes. Remove foil and continue baking 10 to 20 minutes, until lightly browned.
