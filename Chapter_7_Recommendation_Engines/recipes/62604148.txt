Title:
Wish I was in Mexico Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lean cut of Pork cut into 1/2 strips
1 cup chicken stock
Blob of lard
1/2 a big onion sliced finely
1 lime
20 dried apricots-cut up
1 cup mango juice (concentrate is fine)
Corn starch
Brown sugar
Salt
Pepper
Cayenne
Cumin

Directions:
Season pork with salt and pepper
Brown pork in hot shortening
Add chicken stock and turn down to simmer
Add dried apricots and allow to simmer for 40 minutes or so.
Add about 1/2 cup of mango juice, add onion and turn up heat a bit.
Squeeze juice of lime into mix
Mix about 1 tsp corn starch and 1 tsp br sugar into remaining mango juice.  Add.
Add cayenne and cumin to taste
(I personally would add thinly sliced red and yellow peppers at this point but other person didn't like them).
Heat until thickened and bubbly
Can serve alone, on rice or noodles.  Enjoy
