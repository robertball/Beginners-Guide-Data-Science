Title:
Garlic and pepper pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz.) package of spaghetti
1/4 cup olive oil
4 cloves garlic (minced)
1/2 red, orange, or yellow bell pepper (seeded and julienned)
1/2 tsp. dried basil or italian seasoning
1/4 cup parmesan cheese (grated)
Salt
Black Pepper

Directions:
In large pot cook spaghetti for about 10-12 minutes or until al dente.
Sautee chopped peppers and garlic in olive oil until tender and fragrant. Add basil, salt, and pepper.
Drain pasta and return to pot. Pour olive oil mixture over pasta. Plate and top with parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Emily H.
Description: 
I make this yummy pasta dish when the cupboards are getting a little bare before payday. Serve with a salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
