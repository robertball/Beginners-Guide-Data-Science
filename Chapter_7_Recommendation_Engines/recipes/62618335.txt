Title:
Left over Pork Roast ( and Cabbage ) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small head cabbage, coarsely chopped
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
3 pounds bone-in pork roast
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste

Directions:
Prep your cabbage as you like.  Shred leftover pork roast into cabbage, heat through and serve.  This recipe much better as a Left over usage.
