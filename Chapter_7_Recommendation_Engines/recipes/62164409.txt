Title:
stuffed shells with spinach,sundried tomatoes,and three cheeses Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 a box of jumbo shells pasta cooked according to the package directions
32oz.of ricotta cheese
3 cups of mozzerella cheesedivided
3/4 cup of parmesean cheese
1 package of frozen chopped spinachthawedand squeezed dry of all moisture
1/3 of a cup of chopped sundried tomatoes
1 teaspoon of seasoned salt
1 teaspoon of pepper
1 teaspoon of chopped garlic cloves
1 egg beaten
1 jar of spaghetti sauce
1 tablespoon of brown sugar

Directions:
preheat oven to 350'F
in a large mixing bowl mix ricotta cheese,1 1/2 cups of mozzerella cheese,parmesean cheese,spinach,tomatoes,season salt,pepper,garlic,and egg.Mix well
in a sauce pan go ahead and add your sauce and brown suger heat on medium and stir occasionally
once thats heated through put half of that sauce into a 9x13 baking dish
stuff your cooked shells with two tablespoons of cheese mixture
and set on top of the sauce into the 9x13 baking dish
cover the shells with the remaining sauce and the remaining mozzerella cheese
cover and bake for approxamatly 30 minutes or until the cheese on top is nice and bubbly in the center

this is an easy vegetarian meal that is simple to make and taste great!
