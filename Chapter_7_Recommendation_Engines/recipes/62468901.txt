Title:
Chicken Won Tons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-12 oz can of chunked chicken or 3 cooked chicken breasts, shredded
3 T finely chopped onion
1 C sour cream
3 oz. cream cheese
1/2 C real mayo
1 packet Hidden Valley ranch salad dressing (not the dip)
1/4 t. garlic powder
1 package won tons found with fresh veggies

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Shred chicken and mix with remaining ingredients.
Place one won ton wrapper into each cup of a cupcake pan.
Drop spoonful of chicken mixture into each cup.
Bake for 10 mins or until edges are brown.
