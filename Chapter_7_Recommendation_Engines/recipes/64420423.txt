Title:
Egg in a pepper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2300018.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pepper slice
1 large egg
salt
pepper

Directions:
Cut a slice of pepper. Remove seeds from middle.
Crack egg inside pepper as best as you can.
Flip when corners turn up or look brown.
Top with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Chef Lizzie
Description: 
This recipe is paleo and a super easy way to eat your eggs!
