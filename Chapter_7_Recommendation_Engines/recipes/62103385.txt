Title:
Healthy, low-fat Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all purpose flour
3/4 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup Splenda granulated sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1 can (15 oz) pure pumpkin
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 egg or 2 egg whites
1/3 buttermilk
2 tablespoons Canola oil

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Grease a 9x13 loaf pan.
Mix all dry ingrediants in a large bowl, set aside.  In a small bowl, combine egg and milk and whisk together.  Add pumpkin, buttermilk, oil and vanilla, stir.  Add to dry ingrediants and mix until combined.
Pour into prepared loaf pan.  Bake for 55-60 minutes or until toothpick inserted in middle of loaf comes out clean.

Submitter: 
PATTON0626
Description: 
I developed this tasty, healthy pumpkin bread recipe after discovering there are not many healthy ones out there!  Most are packed with sugar and oil.  I have incorporated whole wheat flour into the recipe for an added nutrition boost. Hope you enjoy.
