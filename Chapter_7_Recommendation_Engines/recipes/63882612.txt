Title:
Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup uncooked instant rice
3 eggs, slightly beaten
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsps vanilla
1/4 tsp salt
2 1/2 cups milk, scalded
1/2 cup raisins(Optional)
Cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F(180C)
Heat water to boiling. Remove from heat and stir in rice. Cover and let stand about 5 minutes.
Blend eggs, sugar, vanilla and salt. Gradually stir in milk. Mix in rice and, if used, raisins.
Pour this mixture into an ungreased 1 1/2 quart casserole dish and sprinkle with cinnamon.
Place casserole dish into square pan at least 2 inches deep and pour very hot water 1 1/4 inches deep into pan.
Bake about 60-70 minutes, or until knife inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean. Remove from water and serve warm or cool.
