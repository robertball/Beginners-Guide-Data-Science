Title:
Pigs in a blanket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb package coctail sausage or hot dogs (cut in fours)
2 cans crescent rolls

Directions:
Heat oven to 375
Unroll dough and seperate into 16 triangles.
Cut each triangle lengthwise into 3 narrow triangles.
Place sausage on shortest side of each triangle, and roll.
Place on a ungreased baking sheet, point side down.
Bake for 12 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Coctail sausage wrapped in crescent roll dough and baked.
