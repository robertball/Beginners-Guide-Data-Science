Title:
Southwestern Avocado and Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup(s) cilantro, chopped
2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
1 serving(s) Hot Sauce, 2 or 3 dashes
4 cup(s) romaine lettuce, chopped
1 cup(s) cherry tomato(es), chopped
1/8 cup(s) Bertolli Extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup(s) frozen corn kernels, fresh or frozen cornels, thawed and drained
1 medium avocado, pitted, peeled and diced
15 oz canned black beans, 1 15 oz cans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Whisk together cilantro, oil, lime juice and hot sauce.
Combine all other ingredeiants, add dressing, toss well.
