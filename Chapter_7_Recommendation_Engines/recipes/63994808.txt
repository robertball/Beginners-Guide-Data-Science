Title:
PB&J; Mini Mug Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PAM速 Original No-Stick Cooking Spray, 4 teaspoons Concord grape jelly ,1/2 cup dry yellow cake mix, 2 tablespoons Peter Pan速 Creamy Peanut Butter, Reddi-wip速 Original Dairy Whipped Topping (about 2 cups), 1/4 cup Egg Beaters速

Directions:
1.Spray insides of 4 microwave-safe mugs with cooking spray. Place 1 teaspoon jelly in bottom of each mug. Combine cake mix and peanut butter with fork in medium bowl; whisk in Reddi-wip and Egg Beaters. Divide batter evenly and place over jelly in mugs.2. Microwave each mug individually on HIGH 1 minute to 1 minute 15 seconds or until set. Invert each cake onto a plate. Serve immediately with additional Reddi-wip
