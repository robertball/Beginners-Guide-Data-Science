Title:
Easy Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 slices diced bacon
1/2 a medium diced onion
4 regular cans of Pork N Beans
1/2 cup of brown sugar
3 fat tablespoons of plain yellow mustard

Directions:
using an oven safe pan is easiest
brown the bacon & onion till dark gold
drain 3 of the cans of pork n beans,  I open the 3/4 and lay them in the sink
once drained, add all 4 cans to the bacon & onion, stir
then add brown sugar & mustard, mix well
either continue to heat stove top OR place in the oven @ 350 till hot

Submitter: 
Candy K Sheppard
Description: 
old family recipe with loads of flavor
I'm going to use the small version of this
I've made this for as few as 3 and for as many as 40
