Title:
Nora's No-Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/768572.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups sugar
1/2 Cup Milk
1 stick butter
1/2 Cup Cocoa
1/2 Cup coconut
1/2 Cup Peanut Butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. salt
3 Cups quick-cooking oatmeal

Directions:
Mix sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa.  Bring to boil and boil for 1 min.
Remove from heat, add coconut, peanut butter, vanilla, and salt.  mix well.  Add oatmeal.
Drop onto wax paper and chill in fridge.
