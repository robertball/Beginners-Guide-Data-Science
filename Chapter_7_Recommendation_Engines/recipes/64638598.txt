Title:
Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon evoo
1 cup chopped leeks
1 cup diced onons
8 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, celery, mushrooms, zucchini)
6 cups chicken stock
3 cups chopped kale
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
salt and pepper to taste
chicken
1 tablespoon hijiki or arame
1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger

Directions:
1) Heat oil in pot over medium heat.
2) Add leeks and onions and saute for 3-5 mins.  Add the mixed vegetables and stock plus any additions; cover and simmer for 10-15 mins until vegetables are tender.
3) Add kale and parsley; simmer for 4-5 mins; season with salt and pepper.
