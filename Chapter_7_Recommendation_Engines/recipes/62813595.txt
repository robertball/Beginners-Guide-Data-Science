Title:
BBQ Turkey Veggie Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4 lbs extra lean ground turkey breast
1 small onion, grated
1/4 cup plus 3 TBL of BBQ sauce
3 cups bagged dry broccoli slaw
1/2 cup liquid egg substitute
1/2 cup quick cooking oats
1 TBL Steak seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Add the grated onion into a large bowl.  Add 1/4 barbecue sauce and remaining ingredients.  Stir until everything is evenly distributed.
Mold into a loaf and bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes.  Pour remaining barbecue sauce on top.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This is my version of Hungry Girl's Turkey & Veggie Meatloaf Minis. I kicked up the spices and substituted barbecue sauce instead of ketchup but kept the lean turkey and veggies.
