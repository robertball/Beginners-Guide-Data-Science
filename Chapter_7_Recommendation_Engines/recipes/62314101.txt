Title:
Buffalo Style Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cooked and cubed
4-5 oz. hot wing sauce
1/2 c. blue cheese salad dressing
1 (16 inch) prepared pizza crust
1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Cook crust for 5 minutes.
In a medium bowl combine the cubed chicken, hot wing sauce. Mix well. Spread salad dressing over crust, then top with chicken mixture and sprinkle with shredded cheese.
Bake in preheated oven until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly, about 5 to 10 minutes. Let set a few minutes before slicing, and serve.
