Title:
Fresh Green Beans, Fennel, and Feta Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
1 fennel bulb, cut into thin slices
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil leaves
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Grill the Green beans and Fennel until soft and nice grill marks about 10 to 15 min.(medium heat on the grill) Pour  the olive oil and let it heat for a minute. Season with basil, salt, and pepper;  toss with feta cheese.
