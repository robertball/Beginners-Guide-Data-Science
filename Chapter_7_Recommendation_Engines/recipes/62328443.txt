Title:
Creamy Tomato-Basil Chicken and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup  KRAFT Sun-Dried Tomato Dressing, divided
4 small boneless skinless chicken breasts (1 lb.)
1 cup  fat-free reduced-sodium chicken broth
4 oz.  (1/2 of 8-oz. pkg.) neufchatel cheese, cubed
1/2 14.5-oz. can petite diced tomatoes, drained
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. black pepper
8 fresh basil leaves, cut into strips
3 cups penne pasta, uncooked
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat 2 Tbsp. dressing in large skillet on medium heat. Add chicken; cover. Cook 5 to 6 min. on each side or until brown.
Add remaining dressing, broth, neufchatel cheese, tomatoes, garlic powder, pepper and basil to skillet; cook, stirring frequently, until cheese is almost all melted.  Cover and cook until chicken is cooked through.
Meanwhile, cook pasta as directed on package, timing it to be ready to drain when the chicken is cooked through.
Drain pasta. Serve the pasta topped with chicken, sauce and parmesan.
