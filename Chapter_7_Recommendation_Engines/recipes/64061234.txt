Title:
Simple Mexican Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 2 cups of water
- 1 cup of Quinoa (see notes below for what brand i use)
- 1 package of Archer Farms Southwest-Inspired Corn & Black Bean Blend (from Target frozen food section)

Directions:
Cook Quinoa per packaging (as if you were making it plain).
When there is about 5 minutes left on the Quinoa cook time, put Black Bean and Corn blend in the microwave (according to your microwave settings, mine was 5 minutes)
Once Quinoa is done, mix in the entire package of the blend. Stir and Serve.
