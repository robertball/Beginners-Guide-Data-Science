Title:
Spicy Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. of hamburger
1 can of diced tomatoes
2 teaspoons of cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon of crushed hot pepper
1 lb of penne pasta
1/4 cup of red wine
1 small onion
1 jar of your favorite pasta sauce
2 cloves of garlic

Directions:
1. Cook pasta as directed on package.
2. Brown lean hamburger.  Drain hamburger and add onion and garlic. In the same pan, put cooked hamburger, onion and garlic and sautee for about two minutes.
3. Add tomatoes, wine, cayenne pepper, sauce and hot pepper.
4. Simmer for about 5 minutes or until hot.
5.  Pour sauce mixture over penne and enjoy with a glass of red wine.

Submitter: 
Melissa Rapoza Fragoza
Description: 
If you enjoy chop suey you will love this spicy version.  It is quick and easy and the family will love it!
