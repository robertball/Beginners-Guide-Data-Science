Title:
Ginger Chicken Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chicken stock
2 cups water
2 green onions, thinly sliced (including the green parts)
3 - 4 sticks of lemon grass, roughly chopped into 1 - 1.5" pieces
4" fresh ginger thinly sliced into sticks
1 clove garlic, diced
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp white pepper
salt to taste
3/4 - 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, diced
2 cups jasmine rice, cooked
1 cup frozen peas
Fresh cilantro leaves (to taste)

Directions:
Put chicken stock, water, onions, lemon grass, ginger, garlic, coriander, white pepper, and salt in stockpot. Cover, bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes.
Return to a boil. Add chicken. Cook 3-5 minutes  (until done)
Reduce heat. Add rice and peas. When peas are cooked but still firm, remove from heat. Add cilantro and serve.
