Title:
Chilled Mango & Pineapple Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666399.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups chopped pineapple
The fruit of 2 ripe champagne mangoes
2 tsp lime juice
2 tbsp sugar
1/2 water

Directions:
Put everything in a blender and puree it until smooth. Strain it through a fine mesh strainer. Chill in the refrigerator for two hours before serving. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Ivon
Description: 
A refreshing soup, perfect for a sweet side dish in the summer!
