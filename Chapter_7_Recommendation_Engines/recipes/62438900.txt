Title:
Zesty Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup or 20 to    30 oz.of tomato sauce
1/4 cup instant rice or reg rice, add baking time
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1 teaspoon onion salt
1/2 teas. garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 pound lean ground beef
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Turn oven on to 350 degrees.
Lightly beat egg with a fork,
Add a heaping tablespoon of the soup and mix lightly.
Mix in rice, onion, parsley, onion salt,garlic powder and pepper.
Stir in the ground beef and mix well with hands.
Flatten mixture into 3/4 inch rectangle and cut into the size meatballs you want.
Form into balls and lay out in casserole.
Add Worchestershire sauce to tomato soup or sauce.
Pour sauce over meatballs.
Place in oven and bake for 25-30 minutes.  Perhaps longer if using regular rice.
Serve over hot rice or mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Mary Alice
Description: 
Condensed tomato soup or tomato sauce and beaten egg are combined with ground beef, rice, chopped onion, parsley, and seasonings. The mixture is formed into meatballs,Modified from: Zesty Porcupine Meatballs
