Title:
Old fashioned banana cream pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
3 Tablespoons flour
2 1/2 or 3 cups milk
3 egg whites beaten fluffy
1 teaspoonful vanilla

Directions:
Add first 3 ingredients.
Cook until thick.
fold in beaten egg whites.
add vanilla
Let cool then pour over sliced bananas into a baked pie shell.
