Title:
Aunt Jemima Maple Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 cup granulated sugar
2 cups dark corn syrup
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon maple flavoring
3 tablespoons butter (optional)

Directions:
Combine the first four ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir occasionally, until the mixture comes to a full boil; let it boil for 7 minutes. Turn the heat off and let the syrup cool for 15 minutes.
Add the maple flavoring and stir. When completely cool, transfer the syrup to a covered plastic or glass container.
