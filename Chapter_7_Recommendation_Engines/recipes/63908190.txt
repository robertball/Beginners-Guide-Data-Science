Title:
Tomatoes & Bacon Egg Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084059.jpg
Ingredients:
6 strips of bacon
8 large eggs
1 cup of cherry tomatoes, cut in half
1 handful of chopped green onions
1 pinch of black pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat over at 350F.
Pan fry bacon until halfway cooked. blot excess oil with a paper towel. Cut into small pieces.
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
Spray muffin tray with cooking spray or butter. Pour mixture in pan and bake for 15-20 mins.

Submitter: 
Maggie
Description: 
I love making this for breakfast. Make 6 and bring them to work and my entire week of breakfast is covered! Heat one in the microwave every morning. Easy and healthy!
