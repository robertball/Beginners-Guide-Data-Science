Title:
Garlic Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup lime juice
1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 tsp. ground pepper

Directions:
Place chicken in slow cooker.
Combine the remaining ingredients in a bowl.  Pour over chicken.
Cover.  Cook on High 4-6 hours or low 6-8 hours, or until chicken is tender but not dry.
