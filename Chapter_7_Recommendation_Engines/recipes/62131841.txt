Title:
Randy's Coney Hot Dog Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound Lean Ground Chuck
1 Large Onion Chopped
3 Tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
1 teaspoon Granulated Garlic
1 Tablespoon Hot Chili Powder
1 Tablespoon Paprika
1 Tablespoon Dry Mustard
1 teaspoon Gound Cumin
1/2 Cup Ketchup
1 Tablespoon French's Salad Mustard
Salt and Pepper to taste
A few shakes of Cayenne Pepper
A few shakes of Durkey Hot Sauce

Directions:
Brown the ground chuck and onion together.
Add the spices, ketchup and French's mustard.
Add teh salt, pepper, Cayenne Pepper and Durkey Hot Sauce to taste.
Simmer slowly, stir frequently to prevent sticking.
Serve over hot dogs, top with additional mustard, chopped onion and cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
Randy
Description: 
Spicy rich sauce with a bit of a kick, served on dogs topped with mustard and chopped onion.
