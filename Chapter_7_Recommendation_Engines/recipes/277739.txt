Title:
Cold Brew Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 slices lemon
8 large mint leaves
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 fluid ounce cold brew coffee
4 cubes ice, or to taste
soda water

Directions:
Cut lemon slices in half and tear mint leaves into thirds. Crush lemon, mint, and sugar together in a cup until sugar is completely wet. Pour cold brew on top. Mix well until sugar is dissolved. Add ice cubes and top with soda water. Stir well.

Submitter: 
Sean Clark
Description: 
This iced coffee mojito is the best I have ever tasted without milk.
