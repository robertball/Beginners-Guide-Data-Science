Title:
Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cubed
1 Tbsp. oil
1 small onion, diced
1/2 bell pepper, diced
2 C. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 clove garlic, crushed
28 oz. can Italian style or Plain crushed tomatoes in puree
2 Tbsp. parsley
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1 tsp. basil leaves
8 oz. wide egg noodles, cooked
1/2 C. grated parmesan

Directions:
In a skillet, cook chicken, onion, pepper, mushrooms and garlic in oil over medium high heat until chicken is done.
Add remaining ingredients except noodles and parmesan cheese.  Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer 30 minutes.
Serve over hot egg noodles.  Top with parmesan cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
A great chicken dish full of veggies.  You can have it for dinner tonight or freeze it for later.
