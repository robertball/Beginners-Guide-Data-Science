Title:
Big Ray's Cheddar-Mashed Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045671.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb. potatoes (about 6), peeled, quartered
2 stick  butter
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 cup sour Cream
1 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
12 slices bacon, cooked, crumbled

Directions:
Cook potatoes in boiling water in large saucepan 20 min. or until tender; drain. Return to pan.
Heat oven to 350ÂºF. Add butter, onion powder, garlic powder,  and sour cream to potatoes; mash until smooth. Spoon half into 1-1/2-qt. casserole; cover with layers of cheese, remaining potato mixture and bacon.
Bake 30 to 35 min. or until heated through
