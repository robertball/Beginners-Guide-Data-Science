Title:
Skinny Mac & Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C (8 oz) uncooked whole grain pasta
1 jar Ragu light parmesan alfredo sauce
1 (9 oz) pckg frozen & thawed chopped spinach, well drained
1 C fat free cottage cheese
1/4 C fat free milk
1 tbs reduced fat butter or smart balance light melted
1 tsp minced garlic
1/4 tsp dried basil
1 egg
1/2 C shredded reduced fat mozzarell cheese

Directions:
1.  Coat 9x9 baking dish w nonstick spray
2. Cook pasta according to package.  Drain & rinse in cold water
3.  In lg bowl, whisk together Alfredo sauce, drained spinach, cottage cheese, milk, butter & garlic
Add pasta & mix weel
4.  Blend in egg, basil salt & pepper.  Be sure all is mixed well together
5.  Transfer pasta to the prepared baking dish
6.  Sprinkle top evenly w mozzarella cheese
7.  Bake casserole @ 450 for 25 min
8.  Let casserole rest for 10 min
