Title:
Cucumber Ice cream Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large cucumbers
3 bananas
0.5 cup icing sugar
0.5 cup lemon juice

Directions:
combine ingredients in food processor
puree until smooth
put in freezer for 3hrs+
OR
freeze cucumbers and bananas ahead for instant ice-cream

Submitter: 
PlaygroundHunt
Description: 
Super fast, healthy ice-cream /sorbet. This cucumber/lemon ice is very refreshing.
