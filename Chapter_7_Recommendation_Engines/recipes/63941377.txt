Title:
Nachos with Leftover Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bag On The Border Tortilla Chips
Shredded Mexican Cheese Blend
Leftover Salsa Chicken
1 Can Sliced Olives
Sour Cream
Holey Guacamole/Avocado
Salsa of choice

Directions:
Layer Tortilla Chips, warmed Chicken, then cheese. Bake in oven for 10 minutes in a 350 oven.
Serve with sour cream, olives, guac and salsa on the side.
After baking nachos - top with your favorites....YUM!
