Title:
Cheesy Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
•2 cups instant brown rice
•1-3/4 cups water
•1 can reduced-sodium condensed cream of chicken soup
•2 cups salsa
•1/2 cup chopped onion
•1-1/2 cups cheddar cheese

Directions:
1.Cook brown rice according to directions on box.
2.Meanwhile, place chicken breast halves into a large saucepan, and fill with water. Bring to a boil, and cook for 20 minutes, or until done. Remove chicken from water and cut into bite-size pieces.
3.Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease an 8×11 inch baking dish.
4.In a medium bowl, mix together cream of chicken soup, onion, and salsa.
5.Layer 1/2 of the rice, 1/2 of the chicken, 1/2 of the soup and salsa mixture, and 1/2 of the cheese in prepared dish. Repeat layers, ending with cheese.
6.Cover with foil and bake in preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until bubbly. Remove foil & cook 5 minutes longer.
