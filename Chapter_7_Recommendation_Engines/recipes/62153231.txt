Title:
Grandma Schell's Lacy Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1797616.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup Oatmeal
1/2 cup butter (or margarine)
pinch of salt
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 slightly beaten egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
***Line a baking sheet (or 2) with foil.***
Melt butter and pour over oats.
Add a pinch of salt, mix with fork.
Combine sugar, flour and baking powder.  Add to oat mixture.
Add egg, vanilla and walnuts; mix well.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto a FOILED cookie sheet about 2 inches apart (cookies are very thin).
Bake 8-10 minutes, cool before removing.
