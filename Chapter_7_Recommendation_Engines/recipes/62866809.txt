Title:
Barbequed Mexican Sweet Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ears of sweet corn
1 c mayonnaise
1 grated garlic clove
3 dashes hot sauce
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1/4 c grated parmesan cheese
1/4 c chopped fresh cilantro
2 limes

Directions:
Set the grill to medium heat.
To make the sauce combine the mayo, garlic, hot sauce, pepper, zest and juice of one of the limes.  Mix all in a bowl and refrigerate until the corn is done.
Cut the 2nd lime into wedges, chop the cilantro and set aside.
Peel the corn and wrap it in aluminum foil with a little water.
Grill the corn turning frequently until done.
Unwrap the corn and put on a platter, spread on the sauce, sprinkle with cheese, squeeze on some lime and top with the cilantro.
Serve immediately while hot!

Submitter: 
Barbara Kahian
Description: 
A very unique way to prepare sweet corn on the grill!
