Title:
Crock Pot Autumn Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 medium sized butternut squash
2 small turnips
4 or 5 carrots
1 1/4 lb baby red potatoes
4 or 5 stalks celery
1/2 medium sized sweet onion (or two small yellow onions)
3-4 cups water
1 Tbsp ground sea salt
2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1 Tbsp minced garlic
1/4 tsp white truffle oil
1 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp spaghetti sauce
1 Tbsp each of: dill, oregano, sweet basil, parsley, rosemary, thyme
1 Tbsp butter

Directions:
Cube/dice vegetables as desired (smaller sized veggies take less cooking time).
In the crock pot mix half of the herbs, sea salt, minced garlic, worcestershire sauce, spaghetti sauce, water, vinegar, and truffle oil.
Add vegetables to pot, stir, add remaining half of herbs and butter and stir again.
Grind sea salt and black pepper over the vegetables to taste.
Cook on "high" in crock pot for about four hours, or until the vegetables are tender.

Submitter: 
ally-gator
Description: 
A delicious, herb-covered cornucopia of feel-good cold weather vegetables that serve well as a main dish or a side to pot roast. This would be a great addition to any Thanksgiving or holiday meal!
