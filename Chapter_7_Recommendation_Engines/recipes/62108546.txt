Title:
Eat It Up Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole cooked chicken
2 celery stalks
1/2 roasted red bell pepper
1/2 small white onion
1 1/2 C Mayo
1 Tbl mustard
1 tsp garlic
seasoned salt
salt
pepper

Directions:
Shred up whole chicken.
Dice celery, roasted bell pepper, and onion.
Mix all together with mayo, mustard and garlic.
Add seasoned salt, salt & pepper to taste.
Refrigerate overnight for best flavor. Serve on sandwich rolls.

Submitter: 
Rachelle Shockey
Description: 
A simple yet tasteful recipe, even the kids will love! Refrigerate overnight for best flavors.
