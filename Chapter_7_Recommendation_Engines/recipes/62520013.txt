Title:
Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C all-purpose flour
2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1/2 C butter, softened
1 C sugar
2 eggs
1/2 C milk
1 t vanilla
2-2 1/2 C fresh or frozen blueberries
1 T sugar
1/4 t cinnamon

Directions:
Line sixteen standard muffin cups with paper cups. In med. bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt; set aside.
In large bowl, beat butter with electric mixer 30 sec. Add 1 C sugar, beat until well combined. Beat in eggs, milk and vanilla (mixture looks slightly curdied). Stir in flour mixture until just moistened (batter should be lumpy). Gently stir in blueberries.
Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups, filing each nearly full. In small bowl, combine 1 T. sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle sugar mixture over batter
Bake in 359 oven 25-30 min or until golden and wooden toothpick inserted in centers comes out clean. Cool on wire rack for 5 min. Remove from cups. Serve warm
