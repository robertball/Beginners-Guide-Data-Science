Title:
No Look Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Ground Beef
1 pound Ground Pork
2 Eggs
1/3 cup Mezzetta Zesty Bell Pepper Relish
1 cup bread crumbs
1 tablespoon Minced Garlic
1/2 teaspoon Paprika
1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper
1/4 teaspoon Salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients thoroughly and form into 1/2 inch balls using a cookie scoop.
Place meatballs on an oiled baking sheet.
Bake at 350* until reaching 165* internal temperature (about 20 minutes).
