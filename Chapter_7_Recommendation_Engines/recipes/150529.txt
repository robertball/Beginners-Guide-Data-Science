Title:
Minute速 Rice Chicken a la King Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979676.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Minute速 Rice, uncooked
1/3 cup salad dressing
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
2 (6 ounce) packages roasted chicken breast strips, diced
1 cup sliced mushrooms*
1 cup frozen peas*
1/2 medium red bell pepper, chopped*
Dash black pepper

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package directions.
Mix salad dressing, flour and milk in medium saucepan. Bring to boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat and cook until thickened, about 1 minute.
Stir in all remaining ingredients except rice; cook 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Serve over rice.

Submitter:

Description: 
This classic dish is easier to make than ever with oven roasted chicken, sliced mushrooms, frozen peas and bell pepper in a creamy sauce served over Minute速 Rice.
