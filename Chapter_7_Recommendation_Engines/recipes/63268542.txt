Title:
Lemon Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c. flour
1 T baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1 1/4 c. buttermilk
4 large egg whites
1 1/2 c. sugar
2 lemons, zested and juiced
1/2 c. butter, room temp
1 t. pure vanilla extract
1/2 t. pure lemon extract

Directions:
Stir lemon juice, zest, and sugar together until the sugar is fragrant. Add butter and cream well.
Beat in the extracts. Add remaining liquids, mixing well.
Slowly add dry ingredients, then whip well until the batter is light.
Bake at 350 degrees for 18-22 minutes
