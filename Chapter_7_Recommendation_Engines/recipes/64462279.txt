Title:
Blueberry Bottom Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2424495.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. white cake mix
4 cups fresh blueberries
1 cup sugar
1 pkg. (3 oz.) strawberry or raspberry gelatin
3 cups miniature marshmallows
2 cups Cool Whip

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ÂºF
Grease a 9" X 13" pan
Prepare cake batter as directed on package
Spread blueberries in pan
Sprinkle with sugar, dry gelatin mix and marshmallows, in that order
Pour cake mixture over marshmallows
Bake 50-55 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean
Cool on wire rack
When ready to serve top with Cool Whip.

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
This cake is light, airy and super refreshing with a lovely presentation. Not to mention super easy to make. Try using different fruits or berries or different flavored gelatin.
