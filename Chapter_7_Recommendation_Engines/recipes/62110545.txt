Title:
Italian  Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. fresh asparagus
2 to 3 tbsp. virgin olive oil
Kosher salt to taste
Coarse ground pepper to taste
1/2 cup of freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat grill or oven to a medium heat( around 350 f approximately). Wash and trim and dry asparagus. Place in a single layer in roasting pan.
Drizzle with oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Roast the asparagus in middle rack of oven or grill until still firm but darker in colour, approximately 10 to 12 minutes.
Coat with parmesan cheese in an even layer and cook for another 10 to 12 minutes until cheese is golden brown and spears are tender

Submitter: 
Kimmi
Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe that will compliment any steak  dinner. It can be done in the oven or grill and can be easily doubled  or tripled to feed a crowd.Add a slash of balsamic vinegar for some extra flavour
