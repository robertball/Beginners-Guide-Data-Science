Title:
Mrs. Field's $250,000 Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups butter
2 cups sugar
2 cups brown sugar
4 eggs
2 tsps vanilla
4 cups flour
5 cups oatmeal, ground
1 tsp salt
2 tsps baking powder
2 tsps baking soda
24 oz chocolate chips
1 8oz hershey bar, grated
3 cups chopped nuts

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugars. Add eggs, beating after each addition, and vanilla. In separate bowl, mix together flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder and baking soda. Combine dry ingredients with butter mixture. Add chocolate chips, grated Hershey bar, and nuts. Place golf ball sized cookies 2" apart on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 for 8 minutes.
