Title:
TOMORROW SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
2 TBSP butter
2 cups minature marshmallows
2 cups white cherries
2 cups pineapple
1 orange
1/4 cup candied cherries
1 cup whipping cream
1/2 cup diced celery

Directions:
Beat eggs. add sugar and vinegar. Cook in double broiler until thick and smooth, remove for heat and stir in butter, let cool.
Pit white cherries, cut pineapple into small pieces, section orange.
Whip cream: add egg mixture and fruits.
Pour into glass dish, cover and allow to stand 24 hours in the refrigerator.
