Title:
Mint French Silk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/396192.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oreo pie crust
1 cup heavy cream
8 oz. bittersweet chocolate
1 cup butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
4 tbsp. unsweetened cocoa
4 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
1 pckg. Andes chocolate mints
Whipped cream, (optional)

Directions:
Heat heavy cream in saucepan.
Meanwhile, chop bittersweet chocolate and put into a bowl.
When the cream is just to boiling, pour it over the chocolate and whisk until melted.
Let cool.
Cream butter and sugar together. Add in cocoa and vanilla.
Mix in eggs, one at a time until mixed well.
Unwrap all the mints and melt them in the microwave on 30 second intervals until melted.
Whip the chocolate into the chocolate mixture.
When the ganache is cool, spread it into the pie crust.
Spread the french silk mixture ontop and chill for 1 hour.
Garnish with whipped cream.
