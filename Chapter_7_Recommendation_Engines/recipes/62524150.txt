Title:
Pasta with Blue Cheese and Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
2 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
2 pats butter
1/4 pound uncooked spaghetti
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente.
Meanwhile, heat butter in heavy skillet.
Stir in walnuts, and saute for several minutes.
Add garlic, and saute for about two minutes, but do not let it get brown.
Drain pasta, and divide into 2 plates.
Top with saute and blue cheese.
