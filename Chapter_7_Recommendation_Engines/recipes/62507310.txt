Title:
Captain's Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 Mint leaves
3 lime wedges
Crushed Ice
1 teaspoon simple syrup (or 2 teaspoons of sugar)
1/2 teaspoon lime juice
2 oz Captain Morgan's spiced rum (or any spiced rum)
Cranberry juice
Soda water

Directions:
Muddle mint, limes, and half a glass of ice together.  If using sugar, muddle with the first three ingredients otherwise add simple syrup, lime juice and rum.  Fill glass to the brim with more ice.  Finish drink with eqaul parts cranberry juice and soda water. (amount will vary depending on the size of your glass.)  Stir and enjoy!

Description: 
A friend of mine and I were trying to make use of spiced rum and this is our new favorite drink!
