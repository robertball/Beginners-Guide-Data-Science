Title:
Blueberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 pint blueberries (wash and stem)
1 lb. fresh spinach
4 oz. crumbled bleu cheese
4 oz. candied pecans (the praline pecans from Kroger are great)
Dressing (shake all together in a cruet):
3 T. Cabernet Vinegar
1/2 Cup Olive Oil
1 teas. Dijon Mustard
1 teas. Sugar

Directions:
Combine salad ingredients in a large salad bowl.
Toss with salad dressing at the time of serving.

Submitter: 
Kathi Crawford
Description: 
A lovely salad with fresh blueberries, candied pecans and bleu cheese.  The homemade salad dressing is the best and brings the flavors together nicely!
