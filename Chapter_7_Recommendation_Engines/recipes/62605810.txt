Title:
spinach and artichoke quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 garlic cloves, or 2 tbl minced garlic
2 shallots, chopped
1/2 chopped onion - medium size
fresh spinach, about 7 ounces
olive oil
can of artichoke heats
1 can mushrooms stems and pieces
2 roma tomatoes, cored and diced
spices
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 black pepper
1/4 salt
3 eggs
1 1/2 cup milk
grated cheese.
use 3/4 cup parmesan
use 3/4 cup  mozzarella
1 pie shell  (do not pre-bake the shell)

Directions:
preheat oven 375
saute garlic and shallots and onion in olive oil for 1 minute.
add spinach and saute until wilted (about 1 or 2 minutes)
add chopped artichokes, seasoning and remove from heat
now add the diced tomatoes and mushrooms
set aside
in large bowl, add 3 eggs and milk
add cheeses, stir to blend, now add the spinach mixture and stir to blend.
pour all into the pie shell
bake 45 minutes, checking at 35 and 40, ovens varie.
bake til set.  you should set tinfoil around the pie crust edges, just set to keep them from burning, it won't effect the cooking of the quiche.

Submitter: 
sharron
Description: 
Easy to make, prep time 20 minutes, cook time 45 minutes. Stores for up to 1 week. cook more than one, freeze it, and enjoy another day as a an even quicker meal.
