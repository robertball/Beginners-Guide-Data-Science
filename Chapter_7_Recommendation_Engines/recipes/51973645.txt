Title:
Exploded Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds large brussel sprouts
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
3 gloves of garlic, minced
1/2 cup (or more) white wine
3 tablespoons toasted pinenuts
3 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
Optional crisp bacon crumbles

Directions:
Cut the bottom stems from the brussel sprouts.
Strip as many leaves as possible from the brussel sprouts then cut the core into quarters.
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Saut√© the brussel sprout leaves and cores for 5 minutes, turning frequently.
Add the minced garlic and continue cooking for 5 minutes
Add the white wine a splash at a time putting a lid over the skillet after each addition. Repeat until leaves reach desired tenderness, more or less white wine may be required.
Season with salt and pepper to taste, toss well.
Transfer to serving dish and top with pinenuts, Parmesan cheese and bacon crumbles (if using).
