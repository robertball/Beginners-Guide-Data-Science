Title:
Joe's Shamrock Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/701030.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large scoops vanilla ice cream
1 1/2 cups 2% milk
1/4 teaspoon green food coloring
1/4 teaspoon peppermint extract

Directions:
Place the ice cream, milk, food coloring, and peppermint extract into a blender. Puree until smooth; pour into glasses to serve.

Submitter: 
Joe
Description: 
A homemade version of the perennial McDonald's favorite!
