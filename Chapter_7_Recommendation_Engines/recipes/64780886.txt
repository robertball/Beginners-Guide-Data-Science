Title:
Mama Dina's Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C sour cream
2/3 C mayonnaise
10 oz frozen spinach, thawed & drained
2 slices lemon, peeled & seeded
1 lg clove fresh garlic, sliced
1 t salt
Freshly ground pepper to taste
8 oz frozen artichoke hearts, thawed & drained
1/2 C parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
In a blender, add first six ingredients.
Blend to combine ingredients, 10-15 seconds.
Add artichoke hearts and parmesan. Blend lighty, keeping artichokes in course pieces.
If serving warm, bake in an oven-safe dish at 350 for about 30 minutes, or until browned and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Mama Dina
Description: 
This dip is yummy on French bread and vegetables and can be served hot or cold.
