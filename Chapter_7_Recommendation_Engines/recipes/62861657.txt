Title:
Red Hot Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
½ tsp. baking powder
1 cup butter, softened
1 ½ cups sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
Red cinnamon candies

Directions:
Heat oven to 375F. In small bowl sift flour, baking soda and baking powder; set aside.
In large mixing bowl, beat butter with electric mixer on med-high speed for 30 seconds. Add sugar; beat until combined, scraping bowl occasionally. Beat in egg and vanilla. Beat in as much flour mixture as you can with mixer; stir in remaining flour mix.
Roll dough on lightly floured surface; cut out shapes. Place on ungreased cookie sheets. Lightly press in candies. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on cookie sheet 2 minutes. Cool on racks.
