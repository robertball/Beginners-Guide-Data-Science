Title:
Baked French Toast from the Bullberry B&B, Tropic, Utah Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 - 3 French Bread rolls cut into bite size pieces
12 oz Cream cheese, softened
12 eggs
1   cup milk
1  cup half and half
1/4 cup powdered sugar
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1/3 cup maple syrup
1 1/2 cup sugar
3 tbsp cornstarch
1 1/2 cup water
cinnamon
3 1/2 cups fruit (strawberries, or blueberries, peaches etc.)

Directions:
Grease a large cake pan
Place half of the bite size bread pieces on the bottom of the pan and sprinkle with cinnamon.
Mix softened cream cheese with 1/2 cup of half and half, 1/4 cup powdered sugar, and 1 1/2 tsp vanilla. Mix well and spread over bread.
Sprinkle with 2 cups of fruit and layer with same amount of bread.
In a bowl mix 12 eggs with 1 cup milk and 1 cup half and half, and 1/3 cup maple syrup. Pour over the bread and mash down.
Sit overnight in fridge. Bake covered 350 degrees for 30 min. Remove cover and bake 30-45 min more.
Sauce Bring to boil 1 1/2 cups water, 1 1/2 cup sugar and 3 tbsp of cornstarch. Add 1 1/2 cups fruit and simmer for 10 min.

Submitter: 
Jim K
Description: 
An easy breakfast for a group. Make it the evening before and bake in the morning. Use any fruit like strawberries, blue berries, or peaches.
