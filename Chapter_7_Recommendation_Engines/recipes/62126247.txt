Title:
Root Vegetable Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of red potatoes, cubed
1 large yam, peeled and chopped
4 long carrots, peeled and sliced
1 large parsnip, peeled and chopped
1 jicama, peeled and chopped
2 turnips, peeled and chopped
4 tablespoons fresh Rosemary, minced (depends on how much you like)
1 tablespoon of sea salt (or if you don't have that regular salt)
5 tablespoons of olive oil
6 cloves of garlic, minced

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Combine prepared vegetables into a large baking dish.
Put olive oil, sea salt, and rosemary into a dish and stir, pour over vegetables and toss gently.
Put baking dish into oven for 35 - 45 mins, serve warm.

Submitter: 
Megan
Description: 
Vegetable mix baked to perfection, I go through phases of wanting just vegetables for a main dish. My mom gave me this recipe and she got it from a friend. It's simple to do, peel and cut the vegetables and put them in a casserole dish.
