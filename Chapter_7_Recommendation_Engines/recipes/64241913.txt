Title:
Make-Ahead Roasted Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1748792.jpg
Ingredients:
4 oven roasted heads of garlic with olive oil
5 pounds each Gold and Red potatoes, quartered, cooked and mashed
1 8oz package cream cheese
16 ounces sour cream
2 8 ounce sticks salted butter
fresh ground black pepper to taste
1/2 bunch green onion (bias cut green portion only)

Directions:
Combine mashed potatoes, cream cheese, sour cream, roasted garlic, and pepper to taste. Mix well, garnish with green onion and place in a large slow cooker crock. Let cool to room temperature. Cover and refrigerate overnight or up to 2 nights.
Set slow cooker to low and re-heat 2 to 3 hours before serving, stirring periodically. Add water or low sodium chicken broth 1/2 cup at a time if additional moisture is needed.
