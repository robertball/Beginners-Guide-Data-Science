Title:
Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 bags of potatoes cubed
7 1/2 cups mayonnaise
1 cup yellow mustard
1 cup red onion (for color) optional
30 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
1 1/2 cups relish
freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 tsp. Paprika
1 tsp. Parsley

Directions:
Place the potatoes in the steamer for 10-15 minutes just enough to get soft not mushy. Let cool.
Stir the onion,and pepper into the potatoes.
Add the mayonnaise, mustard to the salad, and mix well. Gently stir in the eggs and relish add pepper to taste. Chill for about 30 minutes before serving.
Add paprika and parsley sprinkles on top for a garnish makes it look beautiful.
