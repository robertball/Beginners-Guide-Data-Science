Title:
King Ranch Chicken Mac and Cheese (Southern Living Jan 2012) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 (16-oz) pkg cellentani pasta
2 T butter
1 med onion diced
1 green pepper diced
1 (10 oz) can diced tomatoes and green chiles
1 (8 oz) package pasteurized prepared cheese product, cubed
3 cups chopped cooked chicken
1 (10 3/4 oz) can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup sour cream
1 t. chili powder
1/2 t. ground cumin
1 1/2 cups (6 oz) shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Prepare pasta according to package.
2.  Meanwhile, melt butter in a large dutch oven over medium high heat.  Add onion and bell pepper, and saute 5 minutes or until tender.  Stir in tomatoes and green chiles and prepared cheese product (I also added some shredded cheddar cheese).  Cook, stirring constantly, 2 minutes or until cheese melts.  Stir in chicken, next 4 ingredients, and hot cooked pasta until blended.  Spoon mixture into a lightly greased 10-inch cast iron skillet or 11 x 7 inch baking dish; sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese.
3.  Bake at 350 for 25 to 30 nminutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
Kim Nance
Description: 
This was cover recipe in Southern Living January 2012 - I made it and it was fantastic.  Only change I made was to add some cheddar cheese to the cheese/meat mixture also.
