Title:
Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/544565.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger meat
1 package of tater tots
Sliced Velveta cheese
Ketchup
1/2 cup onions, diced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Brown hamburger meat in skillet and drain. Finely chop onions in food processor. Mix onions in hamburger meat.
Place hamburger meat in 9x13 dish. Squirt ketchup all over meat.  Lay cheese slices over ketchup. Lay tater tots over cheese. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Enjoy!
