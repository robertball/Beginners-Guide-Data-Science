Title:
Hearty Beef & Butternut Squash Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2699797.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon olive oil
1.5 pounds lean stew meat, cut into 2-inch cubes
1 medium onion
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
4 cloves garlic, crushed
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1 cup low-sodium organic beef broth
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon sea or Himalayan salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cups cubed butternut squash
1/4 cup chopped fresh flat leaf parsley

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat.  Add beef and cook 4-5 minutes, stirring frequently.
Add onion and bell peppers and cook 4-5 minutes until onions are translucent, stirring frequently.
Add garlic and cook for 1 minute, stirring frequently.
Add tomatoes, broth, bay leave, salt, and pepper.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to medium-low.  Cover and simmer for 40 minutes.
Add squash and cook for 8-12 minutes or until sauce has thickened and beef is fork tender, stirring occasionally.
Remove bay leaf; serve topped with parsley.
