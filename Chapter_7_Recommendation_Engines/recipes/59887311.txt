Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/270737.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
2 (10 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough,  1 package torn into pieces
1 package frozen peas and carrots
1 package frozen corn

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soup, and frozen vegetables in a slow cooker, and fill with enough water to cover.
Cover, and cook for 5 to 6 hours on High. About 30 minutes before serving, place one package of the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker. Cook until the dough is no longer raw in the center.
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Place dumpling and chicken mixture in mini-tart pans. Take second container of biscuits and stretch each biscuit to cover mixture in pan. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until biscuit is golden brown.
