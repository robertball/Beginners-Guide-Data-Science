Title:
Pumpkin Gems Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE:
1 yellow cake mix
3 eggs
1/2 c. oil
1 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. cinnamon
20 oz. can pumpkin
CREAM CHEESE FROSTING:
3 oz. cream cheese
3/4 c. butter
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 tbsp. milk
2 c. powdered sugar

Directions:
CAKE: Blend together all ingredients. Fill paper-lined mini-muffin pans 2/3 full. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Cool, then frost with Cream Cheese Frosting. Makes about 100 miniature cupcakes.
CREAM CHEESE FROSTING: Cream together butter and cream cheese. Beat in vanilla and milk. Gradually add powdered sugar until frosting is desired consistency.
