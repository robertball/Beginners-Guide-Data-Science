Title:
Brats & Beans Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 links Bratwurst, removed from casings
1 small-medium onion, chopped
2 medium carrots, chopped
3 cups cooked navy beans, drained
1/4 cup pickled jalapeno slices, chopped
2 cups chicken broth
1-1/2 cups milk, divided
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
8 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded
2 cups torn spinach or Swiss chard
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown Bratwurst in a pot with carrots and onion.
Add broth, jalapenos, and beans. Bring to a boil.
Whisk together 1/4 cup all-purpose flour with 1/2 cup milk. Add to soup. Stir until thickened.
Reduce heat.  Add remaining 1 cup milk and shredded Cheddar. Stir until cheese is melted.
Stir in spinach or Swiss chard and cook until greens are wilted. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Heather Savage
Description: 
Browned and crumbled bratwurst and navy beans come together in a cheesy cream-based soup perfect for cold fall and winter days. Carrots and spinach add color and additional nutritional value. Pair it with corn muffins and a beer or cider!
