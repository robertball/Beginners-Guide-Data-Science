Title:
Swiss Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf french bread
1 pkg. sliced Swiss cheese
1/2 lb. butter (small tub)
2 Tbsp. chopped onion
2 Tbsp. poppy seed
1 Tbsp. dill weed

Directions:
Slice bread and place 1/2 slice of cheese between each slice of bread.  Melt together butter, onion, poppy seed, and dill weed.  Drip over bread.  Place on cookie sheet and cover with heavy-duty aluminum foil.  Bake at 375 degrees for 30 - 35 min.
