Title:
Publix BBQ Chicken Cobb Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head iceberg lettuce shredded
1 lg granny smith apple coarsely chopped
2 plum tomatoes coarsely chopped
12 oz breaded chicken tenderloins
1 cup shredded cheddar jack cheese
1/2 cup canned whole kernel corn
1/3 cup french-fried onions
1/4 cup + 2tbs bbq sauce
2tbs light ranch dressing

Directions:
Prepare chicken according to package instructions for microwave.  Let stand 1/2 minutes to cool; cut into bite size pieces.
Place chicken in 1/4 cup bbq sauce in large bowl; toss until evenly coated.  Remove chicken from bowl and set aside.
Place in same bowl, remaining 2 tbs bbq sauce and ranch dressing; stir until well blended.  Add lettuce  and toss until evenly coated.
Transfer lettuce to serving platter.  Arrange ingredients in rows on top of lettuce in this order: cheese, apples, chicken, tomatoes, corn and french onions.
Serve immediately.
