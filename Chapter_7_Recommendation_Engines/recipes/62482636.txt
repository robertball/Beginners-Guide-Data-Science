Title:
Breakfast Casserole for Meat Lovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/489232.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1 (16 ounce) package ground turkey breakfast sausage
•	½ lb turkey bacon cooked and chopped
•	½ lb breakfast ham, diced
•	12 eggs
•	1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
•	1 1/4 cups milk
•	1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
•	1 (32 ounce) package frozen potato rounds
•	1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
•	½ tsp sage
•	½ tsp Italian Spice
•	½ bell pepper
•	½ small onion

Directions:
Place sausage in a skillet over medium-high heat, and cook until evenly brown. Drain, add sage, onion, and bell pepper, cook until soft and set aside.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Arrange layer of potato rounds along the bottom of the baking dish.
In a large bowl, beat together the eggs, condensed cream of mushroom soup, and milk, cheese and Italian spice. Stir in the sausage mixture, bacon, ham and mushrooms, and pour over potato rounds.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes or until set.
Can be prepared the day before and left unbaked in the refrigerator.  Bake as listed in step 5 when ready.
