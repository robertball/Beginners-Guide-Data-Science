Title:
Roasted Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 20 Brussels sprouts – stems removed, outer leaves removed, cut in half.
- 1 red onion (half slices)
- 3 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tbsp white vinegar
- 1 tbsp water
- 1 tbsp melted butter
- salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
In a baking dish, mix all ingredients together.
Be sure the brown sugar has dissolved into the liquids.
With the lid on, bake for 30 minutes.
After 30 minutes – remove the lid and let the Brussels sprouts brown for an additional 15-20 minutes.
*Please don’t skip the lid on – lid off steps…it makes all the difference!
