Title:
Italian Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 jars of Ragu: tomato, garlic, and onion flavor
1 large bay leaf
1 tsp oregano and celery salt
1 TBS. basil
1 lb hamburger
1 medium yellow onion, diced
1 lb of elbow macaroni
2 1/4 cups sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1 10 oz can of cream of mushroom or cream of celery
1/2 cup of water or milk

Directions:
Cook hamburger in large saucepan, crumble as it cooks. Drain grease. Add diced onion and cook for 5 minutes. Add Ragu in saucepan on stove, adding spices. Let simmer for 1 hour. Remove bay leaf.
With 15 min left of sauce simmering, boil elbow noodles according to package directions. Drain water from noodles.
In small bowl, combine soup and water.
Spray 9x13 pan with Pam cooking spray.
Layer casserole as follows: 1/3 Spaghetti meat sauce, 1/2 of cooked noodles, 1/2 of soup mixture, one cup of shredded cheese, 1/3 meat sauce, rest of noodles, rest of soup, one cup of shredded cheese, rest of meat sauce and sprinkle 1/4 cup of cheese on top. Spray aluminum foil and cover. Bake in 375 degree oven for 30 min. Remove foil and bake 15 min. Serve with salad and crusty bread.

Submitter:

Description: 
To my family, this is known as Fannie's junk, named after my great aunt who gave this recipe to my mom over 30 years ago.  This is comfort food at it's best and an old family favorite.
