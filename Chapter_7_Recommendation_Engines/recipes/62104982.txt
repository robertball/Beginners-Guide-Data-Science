Title:
Leftover Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Leftover meat from a roast chicken
Pan juices from a roasted chicken
1 can of Cambells Chicken Herb soup
1/2 of a (16 oz) package of flat egg noodles
1 cup either peas or broccoli florets
1 small package of mushrooms, washed and sliced
1 onion

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Cut leftover chiken into bite-sized pieces.
Cook noodle according to package directions. Drain noodles. Into noodle pot put can of soup. Mix soup and noodles together.
Add pan juices from roast chicken into the soup and noodles; mix together.
Add broccoli/peas, mushrooms, diced onion, and pieces of chicken. Mix thoroughly.
Put in a casserole dish, and bake for 20 min.

Submitter: 
Emma J
Description: 
This has been adapted many times. Another good thing to add that isn't in this version is a large handful of croutons. They add much more texture to the dish.
