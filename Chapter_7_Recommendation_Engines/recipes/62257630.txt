Title:
New Orleans Shrimp 'n' Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Creamy Grits
2 c. Milk
1 1/2 c. Water
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 c. grits
1/2 c. cheddar cheese - shredded
Shrimp
1 T. oil
1 red pepper, chopped
1 cubanella or green frying pepper, chopped
1 sml. onion, chopped
1 t. minced garlic
1 lb. peeled large shrimp
3/4 t. Old Bay seasoning
3/4 t. Cajun seasoning
4 plum tomatoes, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Grits: bring milk, water, garlic, and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan.  Whisk in grits, cover, reduce heat to simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, 20 min. or until smooth and thickened.
Shrimp: meanwhile, heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.  Saute red and green peppers, the onion and garlic 5 min.  Add shrimp and seasonings; cook 2 min.  Add tomatoes; cook until shrimp are just cooked through, about 1 min.
Remove grits from heat; stir in cheese.  Serve shrimp and vegetables over grits.
