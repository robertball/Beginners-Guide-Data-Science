Title:
Neener's Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dry macaroni
2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
2 TBL finely chopped onion
1 6 oz can sliced black olives, drained
1/2 cup miracle whip
1/2 cup mayo

Directions:
Cook macaroni per package directions. Rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process. Mix all the ingredients together. Chill. Serve cold.
