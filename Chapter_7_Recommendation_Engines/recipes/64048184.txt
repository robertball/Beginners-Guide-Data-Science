Title:
Roasted Herb Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken
Olive oil
1 tsp basil
1 tsp parsley flakes
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp onion salt
1/2 tsp marjoram
1 Tbsp flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Wash chicken and pat dry.  Brush chicken with olive oil.  Combine basil, parsley flakes, oregano, onion salt and marjoram; crushing slightly.  Rub spices on chicken, outside and inside.
Put 1 Tbsp flour in medium cooking bag and shake to distribute flour.  Add chicken and loosely close bag.  Cut 3 one inch slots in top of bag.  Place bagged chicken in roasting pan.  Bake chicken for 1-1/4 hours.  Take out of oven and let stand for 15 minutes before carving.  Use juices to make gravy, if desired.
