Title:
Whole Wheat Date Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups wholewheat flour
1 1/4 tsp baking soda
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup buttermilk
1 egg, beaten
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup chopped dates

Directions:
Beat together the brown sugar and egg.  Beat in the buttermilk.  Sift the dry ingredients and stir them in just until blended (do not overbeat).  Stir in the walnuts and dates.  Pour in greased or sprayed loaf pan, and bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes, or until done.

Submitter: 
STARFLOWER
Description: 
This is my favorite date nut bread that I have been making for years, especially during the holidays. It contains no fat and uses buttermilk, whole wheat, nuts and dates.  It is moist and the dates and whole wheat give it a wonderful flavor.  May you enjoy it as much as I do!
