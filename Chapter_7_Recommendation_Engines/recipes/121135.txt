Title:
Tropical Tuna Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/61538.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 tablespoon finely chopped celery
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon lemon-pepper seasoning
2 English muffins, split and toasted
1 (8 ounce) can sliced pineapple, drained
4 slices processed American cheese

Directions:
Combine tuna, mayonnaise, celery, salt and lemon-pepper; spread over muffin halves. Top each with a slice of pineapple and cheese. Broil 6 in. from the heat for 2 minutes or until cheese is melted and lightly browned. Serve immediately.
