Title:
Spaetzle Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4221124.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup milk
4 eggs
2 teaspoons ground nutmeg
2 teaspoons black pepper
1 teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons butter
1 gallon hot water

Directions:
Mix together flour, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. In a separate bowl, beat eggs. Add milk to the eggs and mix.,
Add milk-egg mixture to the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth.
Press dough through spaetzle maker into simmering liquid. Cook 5 to 8 minutes. Drain well.
Saute cooked spaetzle in butter or margarine.
Meal cost: $2.30 (46 cents per person)
