Title:
Italiano Meat Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
1 green pepper, diced
1 small onion, chopped
1 medium jar spaghetti sauce (about 26.5 ounces)
1 frozen pie shell
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (divided)
2 cups mozzarella cheese (divided)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Brown hamburger.  Add green pepper and onion to the hamburger.  Drain liquid.
Add spaghetti sauce.  Simmer for ten minutes
Sprinkle 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese evenly over bottom of the pie shell
Layer 1/2 of the sauce/hamburger mixture over the Parmesan cheese
Sprinkle one cup of the mozzarella cheese over the sauce/hamburger mixture
Layer the rest of the sauce/hamburger mixture over the the mozzarella cheese
Sprinkle the rest of the Parmesan cheese over the sauce/hamburger mixture
Bake on cookie sheet for 15 minutes
Remove from oven and sprinkle the remaining 1 cup of mozzarella cheese over the top of the pie
Return to oven until the cheese melts and turns golden brown, to your liking (about 5 to 7 minutes)

Submitter: 
CURTISLEE
Description: 
My business partner's son gave me this recipe.  It's hearty, easy to prepare, and reminiscent of pizza!  I always make two of these because it goes so fast.
