Title:
Spicy Whiskey BBQ Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/882183.jpg
Ingredients:
1# ground beef
2 T butter
1/2 onion, diced
1/4 cup whiskey
1/2 cup BBQ sauce
1/4 cup jalopeno slices
buns

Directions:
Melt butter in hot iron skillet.  Form beef into either burgers (5oz) or slider size (2.5oz);  Sear on both sides until abpx 3/4th cooked.  Remove from pan and set aside.
Drain all but apx 2T oil from pan, then saute onions until golden brown.
Add whiskey (carefully) and let reduce 2 to 3 minutes.
Add BBQ sauce and jalapeno slices and stir to incorporate.
Return patties to pan, flip to coat in sauce, and let simmer 3 to 5 minutes or until done.
