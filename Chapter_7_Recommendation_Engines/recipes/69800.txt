Title:
Bill's Braisin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/340652.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups water
1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
1 tablespoon white sugar
3 cups bread flour
1 1/2 cups raisin bran cereal
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Med Crust cycle; press Start.

Submitter: 
WHATS HIS FACE
Description: 
Here's my own recipe for raisin bread made with raisin bran cereal. Enjoy.
