Title:
Cheddar Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985732.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 (12 fluid ounce) cans NESTLE速 CARNATION速 Evaporated Milk
1 cup beer
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dry hot mustard (optional)
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
Toppings: cooked crumbled bacon, sliced green onion, herbed croutons

Directions:
HEAT butter in large saucepan over medium heat. Add flour; cook, stirring constantly or until bubbly. Add evaporated milk; bring to a boil over high heat, stirring constantly. Reduce heat; stir in beer, Worcestershire sauce, salt, mustard and cayenne pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in cheddar cheese until melted.
LADLE into bowls. Serve with toppings.
