Title:
Fennel-Tangerine-Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion
2 bulbs of fennel
750 ml vegetable broth
2 pieces of star aniseed
1 small can tangerines
100 ml white wine
4 tblsp sour cream
olive oil
salt
pepper

Directions:
Peel and chop onion and fennel.
In a saucepan sautee the onion and fennel in the oil until tender.
Add the broth and the star anisseeds. Cook for about 10 minutes.
Add wine and continue to cook for another 5 minutes on low heat.
Turn off heat and remove star aniseeds from soup.
Add tangerine juice from can and puree the soup.
Add tangerines.
Pour in bowls and put one spoonful of sour cream in the middle of each bowl. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Nuttylicious
Description: 
This is a rather fancy soup that is good to surprise company with.
