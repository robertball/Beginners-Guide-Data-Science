Title:
Simple Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
pinch of garlic powder
pinch of onion powder
1 tsp of ground pepper
salt to taste
1 cup ketchup
1 tablespoon of mustard
1/3 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Pre-heat oven at 400
Mix ground beef, garlic powder, onion powder, ground pepper and salt in a bowl. Put in loaf pan.
Mix ketchup, mustard and brown sugar in a bowl, spread on top of loaf
bake for 45 mins or until center is done
