Title:
Macaroni and Cheese - Best Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. uncooked macaroni
3 Tablespoons unsalted butter
1/4 cup grated yellow onion
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
Dash nutmeg
Dash cayenne pepper
3 Tablespoons flour
1 3/4 cup milk (whole)
l cup heavy cream
1 teaspoon Worchestershire sauce
1/2 cup extra sharp Cheddar cheese, plus 2 oz diced
1/2 cup sharp white Cheddar, plus 2 oz diced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Melt butter and add next 6 ingredients
Stir for 30 seconds
Add flour; cook stirring until golden (2 minutes)
Gradually whick in milk and cream
Bring to a boil, whisking occasionally
Reduce heat to medium low, simmer and whisk until thickened
Stir in Worchestershire sauce
Remove from heat
Stir in half of cheese
Stir in macaroni and diced cheeses
pour into cassarole dish
Bake for 30 minutes; broil for 2 minutes
