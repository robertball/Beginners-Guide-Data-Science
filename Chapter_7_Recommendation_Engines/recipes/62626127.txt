Title:
Kudra's Five Cheese Baked Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz. Whole Wheat pasta shells
2 Tbsp Unsalted Butter
2 Tbsp while wheat flour
2 Cups 1% milk
2oz Apple Smoked Gouda
2oz Maple Smoked Cheddar
2oz Gold BellaVitano Cheese
2oz Monetery Jack
2oz low fat Mozzarella
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 cup crushed Salt & Vinegar potato chips

Directions:
Fill pot with water and add a pinch of salt and set on stove to medium heat. Add pasta shells and cook until done, drain and set aside.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In separate pot, melt butter on medium heat and whisk in flour until smooth.  Add milk, salt and pepper and whisk on medium heat for about 5 min.  Combine all 5 cheeses in a bowl and mix them around.  Take 1 cup of cheese blend and add to milk and butter.  Add pasta shells to milk, butter and cheese blend and stir.
Remove from heat and pour mixture into baking dish. Take remaining cheese blend and cover mixture in dish.  Take salt & vinegar chip crumbs and sprinkle on top of cheese.  Place into oven and bake for 20 min or until bubbling.
