Title:
Sesame Rotisserie Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981036.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup brown sugar
4 teaspoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons sesame oil
6 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 rotisserie-roasted chicken, meat removed and shredded
3/4 cup celery, thinly sliced
6 green onions, thinly sliced
1/2 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
1 cup chow mein noodles
1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, vinegar and oil.
Place chicken, celery, green onions, lettuce, noodles and cilantro in a medium bowl; add sesame oil dressing and toss to coat.
Divide salad among 4 individual serving plates.

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
A delicious combination of flavors and a variety of textures all on one plate. Using cooked chicken, this meal can be whipped up in under 20 minutes!
