Title:
Crockpot Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 Lbs chuck roast
4 cups water
4 cubes beef bullion
1/2 jar giardinara (heat to preference)
4 cloves jarred minced garlic
1 large green pepper
2-3 tsp dried minced onion
2-3 tsp dried basil
2-3 tsp fennel seeds
fresh ground pepper to taste
sea salt to taste
Italian rolls
cheese of choice

Directions:
heat water to boil and dissolve beef bullion cubes
add bullion liquid to crockpot and add uncooked chuck roasts
add giardinara, garlic, peppers, onion, basil, fennel, salt, and pepper between and on top of meat.
Cover and cook on low for 8 hours
Remove meat and giardinara/peppers from liquid and shred using forks
moisten rolls with liquid and add meat, cheese, whatever you desire.
Save meat for leftovers, but keep additional juices separate to avoid grease.
Enjoy!  Microwaved leftovers are excellent!
