Title:
lentils and rice with veggie crumbles. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4537610.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion
1 and 1/2 cups lentils- red or white, RINSED
1 cup brown rice
8 cups of water
1 package of veggie crumbles
1 package of spinach
1 package of mushrooms
olive oil
1 tablespoon sea salt
Pepper

Directions:
Saute onions on medium heat with olive oil and salt until soft, then add mushrooms and veggie crumbles.
Saute for another 15 minutes.
Add 8 cups of water, The lentils and brown rice. Increase to medium high heat until it boils.
When it reaches a boil, reduce heat to low and allow to simmer (covered)until all of the water is absorbed, approx. 30 minutes. Stir occasionally. Stir the bag of spinach into the cooked mixture.
Add additional salt (if desired) and pepper to taste.
