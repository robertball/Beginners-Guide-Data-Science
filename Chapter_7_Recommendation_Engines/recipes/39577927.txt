Title:
Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
1/2 C chopped onion
1/4 C grated sharp cheddar cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 C milk
6 saltine crackers
2T melted butter

Directions:
1. Steam or boil cauliflower and 1/4 C onion until tender.
2. Drain and place in 13 x 9-inch casserole dish
3. Mix grated cheese with remaining onion
4. Add soup mixed with milk
5. Pour this mixture over cauliflower
6. Roll crackers and mix with melted butter
7. Sprinkle on top of casserole
8. Bake 350 x 25-30 min
