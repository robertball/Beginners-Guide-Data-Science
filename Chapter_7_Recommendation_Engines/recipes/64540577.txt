Title:
Jan's Chocolate Stuffed Raspberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3160328.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 6 ounce package ripe Raspberries.
Approximately 42 berries.
1- 12 ounce package White Chocolate Chips. Approximately 42 chips.

Directions:
Gently push chocolate chip point first into raspberry.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Tangy fruit and creamy, sweet, chocolate..... yum! Delicious with white chocolate or milk chocolate chips. (Not so much a recipe - but what a fun idea!)
