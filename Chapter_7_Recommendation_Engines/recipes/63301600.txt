Title:
ZUCCHINI  CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups thin sliced zucchini
1 onion sliced thin
1 T. butter or oleo
1 can of cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1 cup carrots (grated)
2 -- 6oz. of stove top stuffing (chicken flavor.

Directions:
Cook zucchini and onion in 1 T. butter
Mix chicken soup, sour cream and carrots together.
Melt 1 stick butter and stir into stuffing.
Grease 13x9 casserole and layer stuffing, then zucchini mixture, then soup mixture. Repeat layer, ending with stuffing on top. Bake at 350 degrees.
