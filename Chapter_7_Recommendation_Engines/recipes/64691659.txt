Title:
Sausage Peppers and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of Italian Sausage (brand not important)
2 Large Green Bell Peppers (thinly sliced rings)
1 Large Yellow onion (thinly sliced Rings)
4 tbsp. EVOO (Extra Virgin Olive Oil)
1 Jar of Tomato, Garlic and Onion Ragu
1 package of Hoagie Rolls (preferably the fresh made ones from the bakery section of the store)

Directions:
Slice the onion and bell Peppers.  use the olive oil in a skillet to sautee the peppers and onions on a medium heat.  When they are almost translucent add the sausages and cover with a lid.
Checking every 5 to 10 minutes and turning several times.  Make sure that the onions and peppers do not burn.
Once Sausages are done drain excess liquid and add the jar of Ragu.  Heat thoroughly.  Remove from heat and set aside.
Open your bun, add the onions and peppers from the sauce mix, add a sausage and then add some of the sauce.  top with Mozzarella, Parmesan or even some grated provolone.
