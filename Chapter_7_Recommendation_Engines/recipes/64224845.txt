Title:
Chili Cheese Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. grated American ceese
3 oz. cream cheese
1/8 tesp. garlic powder
1/4 c. chopped peacns
2 tbsp. chili powder

Directions:
1. Let cheeses reach room temperature; mix both until well blended.
2. Stir in garlic powder and pecans.
3. Form into  a roll about 10 inches long.  Spread the chili powder on waxed paper and coat the outside of cheese with the chili powder by rolling over the waxed paper.
4. Chill.
5. Slice and serve with crackers or Mebla toast.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Johnson
Description: 
A delicious cheese ball that always gets compliments.
