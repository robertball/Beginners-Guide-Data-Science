Title:
Chicken Bundles (or Chicken Bon Bons) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6348890.jpg
Ingredients:
Dough
1 1/2 c shredded mozarella
2 T cream cheese
1 egg, beaten
3/4 c almond flour (bleached, fine)
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
Topping:
1 egg, beaten
shredded cheese (if desired), sprinkle of dill (if desired)
Ranch Filling (makes 4-6 bundles):
1  c shredded chicken
3-4 oz cream cheese
1 T sour cream
1/2 tsp dill weed
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 green onion
1-2 T parsley
Asian Peanut Chicken Filling (makes 4-6):
1 c chicken
2-3 T shredded carrot
2-3 T cilantro
Keto peanut sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F
Melt Mozzarella and cream cheese in microwave
Stir in almond flour, garlic powder, salt, egg
Mix well to combine (knead with hands)
Divide dough into 6 (or 12 if double) balls
Refrigerate if too sticky
Filling:
Combine all filling ingredients in bowl, mix well
Assembly
Roll dough balls into approx 6 inch circle
spoon approx 2 T filling in each bundle
fold up dough around filling to create bundle (or bonbon)
place on foil lined baking sheet
brush tops with beaten egg
sprinkle with cheese/dill (ranch) if desired
bake 400 for 15 min or until tops are golden brown
Serve Asian with extra peanut sauce
