Title:
Dena's Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 recipe for a double crust pie
6-7 large red apples
3/4 c. sugar
1/4 c. flour
2 tbs. cinnamon
1/2 stick butter, melted
2-3 tbs. orange juice

Directions:
Prepare crusts.
Place 1 crust in deep dish pie plate.
Peel and thinly slice apples.
Mix sugar, flour and cinnamon.
Add sugar mixture to apples and coat evenly.
Place apples in pie dish.
Mix melted butter and juice and pour evenly over apples.
Top with remaining crust, pinching edges to seal.
Cut slits in crust to allow steam to escape.
Brush crust with small amount of water and sprinkle with 1 tbs. of sugar.
Bake in preheated oven at 375* for about 1 hour.
Juices should be bubbly through the slits and crust will be golden.
Allow to cool before slicing.

Submitter: 
Dena Morris Locher
Description: 
This is a basic American apple pie.  Homemade crust is a must!
