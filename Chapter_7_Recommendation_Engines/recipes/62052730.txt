Title:
RaRa's Mexican Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cornmeal
3/4 cup self-rising flour
1 can diced tomatoes and chile's, drained well.
1 can whole kernel corn drained well
1 pound ground beef
2 jalapenos, peeled and seeded
2 cups shredded monterey jack cheese
1 egg
1-2 cups buttermilk

Directions:
Brown ground beef, and drain.  Grease a 9x13 baking pan, and preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix together all ingredients, except buttermilk.  Incorporate well.  Add buttermilk 1/4 cup at a time til desired thickness is reached.  Pour batter into greased pan, and bake for about 35-45 minutes or until golden brown.  Great served with Chilli.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I came up with this after scouring through recipes, and not quite coming up with what I wanted.  It was a big hit at a haunted house where we do charity work.
