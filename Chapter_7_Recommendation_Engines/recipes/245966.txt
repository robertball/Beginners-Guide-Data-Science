Title:
Cheeseburger Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3998248.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5.7 ounce) package Knorr® Rice Sides™ - Cheddar Broccoli
2 cups water
1 tablespoon I Can't Believe It's Not Butter!® Spread
1 large tomato, coarsely chopped
1 pound lean ground beef

Directions:
Prepare Knorr(R) Rice Sides(TM) - Cheddar Broccoli with water and Spread according to package directions, stirring in tomato with contents of package.
Meanwhile, brown ground beef in 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally; drain. Toss with hot Rice Sides.
Top, if desired, with tomato slices.

Submitter: 
Knorr
Description: 
Enjoy this family friendly recipe for a twist on your standard cheeseburger dinner.
