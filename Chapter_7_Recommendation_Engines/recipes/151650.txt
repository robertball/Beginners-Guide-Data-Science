Title:
Easy Chicken and Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F7082062.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Condensed Cream of Celery Soup or Campbell's(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Celery Soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Condensed Cream of Potato Soup
1 cup milk
¼ teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
4 cups cooked cut-up vegetables*
2 cups cubed cooked chicken
1 (7 ounce) can refrigerated buttermilk biscuits

Directions:
Mix soups, milk, thyme, black pepper, vegetables and chicken in 3-quart shallow baking dish.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes. Stir.
Cut each biscuit into quarters.
Arrange cut biscuits over chicken mixture. Bake 15 minutes or until biscuits are golden.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
A creamy chicken and vegetable casserole is topped with flaky, golden biscuits.
