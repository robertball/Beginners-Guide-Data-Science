Title:
Four Layer Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup walnuts
8 ounces  cream cheese
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1 Large Cool Whip
2 (3.9 ounce) packages instant chocolate pudding mix
2 1/2 cups  milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400  degrees F (190 degrees C).
Stir butter, flour, and nuts and pat into the bottom of a 9x13 pan.  Bake at 400 for 15 minutes.  Cool.  Beat sugar and cream cheese.  Add cool ship.  Spread about 3/4 on cooled first layer.  Prepare pudding according to directions on pkg but with only 2 1/2 cups milk.  Spread over 2nd layer.  Spread rest of cool whip on top of this layer.  Sprinkle with nuts.  Chill for several hours if possible before serving.
