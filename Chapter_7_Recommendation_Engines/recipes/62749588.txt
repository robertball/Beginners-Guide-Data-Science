Title:
Mango and Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/633425.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can black beans
1 mango, diced
1 green onion, cut in small rings
1 tsp dried parsley or cilantro
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp zesty pepper medley
1 Tbl olive oil
juice of 1 lime
dash of salt

Directions:
Stir all ingredients in a medium-sized bowl.
Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour or over night.
Serve
