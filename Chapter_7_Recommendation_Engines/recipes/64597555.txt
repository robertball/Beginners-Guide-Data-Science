Title:
Big Ray's Cheesy Mushroom-Fish Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3889915.jpg
Ingredients:
4 frozen tillapia fillets (1 lb.)
2 Tbsp. Italian Dressing
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 450Â°F.
Place frozen fish in 13x9-inch baking dish; drizzle with dressing.
Bake 25 min. Top with mushrooms and cheese; bake 5 min. or until cheese is melted and fish flakes easily with fork.
Sprinkle with parsley.
