Title:
Buffalo Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/2536399.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
½ cup ranch dressing
¼ cup hot buffalo wing sauce (such as Frank's(<sup>&reg;</sup>) <sup>&reg;</sup>EDHOT Buffalo Wing Sauce), or to taste
3 stalks celery, diced
2  green onions, chopped
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine the chicken, ranch dressing, buffalo wing sauce, celery, green onions, salt and pepper in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Nikki
Description: 
An awesome chicken salad without mayo! I serve it in a toasted hoagie roll with melted cheddar or jack cheese.
