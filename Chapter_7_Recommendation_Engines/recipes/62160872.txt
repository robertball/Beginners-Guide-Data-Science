Title:
Southern Homemade Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cold water
2 cups sugar
11 cups ice water
2 cups chilled lemon juice
lemon slices for garnish, optional

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, add 2 cups of the water, and add the sugar.
Over medium heat, disolve the sugar, stirring often, cool.
Place the 11 cups of ice water in a gallon container.
Add the 2 cups lemon juice to the gallon container.
Add the cooled sugar and water mixture.
Stir well.
Chill and serve over ice.
Garnish with lemon slices if you would like.

Submitter: 
JOYCE
Description: 
A drink that is good and can quench your thirst on the hottest of days. Not too sweet, not too tart... ...JUST right! Will please both the young and the young at heart!
