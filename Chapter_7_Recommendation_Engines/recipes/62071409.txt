Title:
Passover "unbelievable" brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz chocolate chips or semi-sweet chocolate
1 cup butter or margarine
1 3/4 sugar
4 eggs
1 tablespoon imitation vanilla flavoring
1 cup cake meal
pinch of salt
12 oz package chocolate  chips
2 cups marshmallows, cut into small pieces

Directions:
Melt 4 oz chocolate chips and butter/margarine in microwave together. Add sugar slowly and mix with mixer until smooth. Add eggs slowly, again using mixer until smooth, thick batter. Add vanilla flavoring, cake meal and salt. Blend well. fold in chocolate and marshmallows.
Bake in very greased pan for 30-35 minutes at 350 degree oven.

Submitter: 
SANDI
Description: 
I'll never buy another box mix again! My kids love these- no one thinks they are for Passover. They're good enough to make all year round! Unbelievable!
