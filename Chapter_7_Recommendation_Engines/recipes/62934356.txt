Title:
Easy Chilli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1 can tomato paste 5.5 oz 156 ml
1 can kidney beans 19 oz 540 ml
2 cans canned tomatoes 19 oz 540 ml
1 clove garlic
1 large onion
1 large green pepper
2 tbsp chilli powder
2 bay leaves

Directions:
Brown ground beef on medium heat
Meanwhile chop garlic, onion, pepper
When ground beef almost browned, drain excess fat
Add garlic and onion, cook until beef browned and onion translucent.
Add remaining ingredients.
Continue cooking until mixture comes to a boil, stirring every 5 minutes
Reduce heat to minimum and simmer 15 minute minimum, one hour is best
Serve with white corn tortilla chips
