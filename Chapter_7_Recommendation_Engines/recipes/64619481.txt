Title:
Terri's Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4397836.jpg
Ingredients:
2# split peas
1 ham bone
1# ham, cut or shredded
chicken stock, optional
2-3 onions
1 c. shredded carrots
2 c. chopped celery, optional
1 clove garlic
salt and pepper to taste
liquid smoke, optional

Directions:
Soak peas overnight or quick soak for an hour.
Drain liquid. Add enough water and/or stock, to make 4 qts.
Combine peas, water, ham and ham bone in a soup pot.
Simmer slowly, covered, 4 hours.
Add everything else and simmer, covered, another 2 hours.
