Title:
Stracciatella III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
q package (12 ounce) frzen cut spinach
4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 cup water
4 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
2 teaspoons dried parsley
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
8 eggs, lightly beaten

Directions:
Cook spinach per package directions. In a large saucepan over medium heat, bring broth and water to a boil.  Stir in cooked spinach.  Reduce heat and stir in Parmesan, dried parsley and nutmeg.  Slowly pour lightly beaten eggs while stirring simmering soup. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Joseph S.
Description: 
This quick Italian egg drop soup is flavored with Parmesan, nutmeg and parsley.Modified from: Stracciatella I
