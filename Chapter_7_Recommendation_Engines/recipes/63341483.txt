Title:
Gurkensalat - German Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887340.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large cucumbers, sliced thin
1/2 onion, sliced thin
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 teaspoon dill
1 teaspoon parsley
1 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Spread cucumbers and onion on a platter; season with salt and let rest for 30 minutes.
Squeeze excess moisture from cucumbers.
Stir sour cream, sugar, vinegar, dill, and parsley together in a large bowl.
Fold cucumber and onion slices into the sour cream mixture.
Refrigerate 8 hours to over night; garnish with paprika to serve.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Cucumber and onion slices marinate in a sour cream and vinegar dressing for maximum flavor in this German cucumber salad recipe.Modified from: Gurkensalat (German Cucumber Salad)
