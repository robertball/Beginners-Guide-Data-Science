Title:
Pumpkin Layer Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
25 NABISCO Ginger Snaps (finely crushed-about 1 1/3 cups)	¼ cup melted butter
2-8oz pkgs Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened			½ cup sugar
1 ½ cups canned pumpkin						½ tsp ground cinnamon
¼ tsp ground nutmeg							2 eggs
2 pkgs JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding (3.4oz each)		2 cups cold milk
1-8oz tub Cool Whip Whipped Topping				½ cup chopped Planters Toasted Pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F.  Mix cookie crumbs and butter; press onto bottom of 9”x13” pan.  Bake 10 minutes.  Beat cream cheese and sugar with mixer until well blended.  Add pumpkin and spices; mix well.  Add eggs, 1 and a time, beating on low speed after each just until blended; pour over crust.
Bake 30 minutes or until center is almost set.  Cool 1 hour.
Beat pudding mixes and milk in medium bowl with whisk 2 minutes.  Stir in 1 cup Cool Whip.  Spread over dessert; cover with remaining Cool Whip.
Refrigerate 3 hours or until firm.  Sprinkle with nuts before serving.
