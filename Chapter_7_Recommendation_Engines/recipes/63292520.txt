Title:
Sweet and Sour Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs Country Style Spare Ribs cut in 1 to 2 inch cubes
1/2 tsp salt
dash pepper
2 TBSP corn starch
1 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp ground ginger
2/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup onion chopped
2/3 cup brown sugar
1 tsp dry mustard
1 cup crushed pineapple
1/2 cup water
2 TBSP Soy Sauce

Directions:
Sprinkle ribs with salt and pepper
Bake ribs at 425 for 20 to 30 minutes. Drain off fat.
Combine all ingredients on stove until thick and glossy
Pour 1/2 of sauce over ribs and bake an additional 30 minutes at 325.  Cover with remaining sauce and back an additional 30 minutes at 325.
You can also combine the sauce and ribs and put in a slow cooker for 4 to 5 hours
