Title:
Shanda's Kartoffelkohl Gefüllte Brötchen, (Potato Cabbage Stuffed Rolls) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6100838.jpg
Ingredients:
DOUGH
1 T or 1 package dry yeast
1 1/2 C lukewarm water
1 T sugar
2 t salt
3-4 C unbleached flour
FILLING
1 large onion, finely diced
2 C cubed potatoes
16 ounces fresh mushrooms
2 carrots peeled and thinly sliced
3 T butter
2 C Finley chopped onions
1 cup cottage cheese
1 C grated sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 t salt
2 t chopped dried dill
2 T chopped fresh parsley
1/4 t ground caraway seeds
1/4 t white or black pepper
1 egg beaten with 1 tablespoon water
sesame seeds or poppy seeds (optional)

Directions:
DOUGH
In a large bowl dissolve the yeast in water with 1 tablespoon sugar
Add the salt and about 2 cups of the flour.
Work the dough with your hands add more flour until the dough can absorb no more.
Knead the dough on a floured board until it is no longer sticky but very smooth and elastic.
Cover with a damp cloth and let rise about 1 hour in the meantime assemble the filling
FILLING
In a large skillet sauté onions until translucent add all veggies and cook covered stirring occasionally for 20 minutes.
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Take off burner and stir in cheeses, herbs and seasonings.
Make bread dough into rounded circles and flatten heaping 2-3 tablespoons of vegetable mixture onto round and pull edges of dough together pushing together with fingers and using the egg wash (1 egg eaten with 1 T water)  to seal edges together.
Place on parchment paper on a baking sheet.
Allow stuffed dough to rise again for 40-45 minutes.
Brush tops with egg wash and sprinkle on sesame or poppy seeds as desired.
Bake in 400 degree F oven for 35-45 minutes and golden brown.
Allow to cool before serving.
