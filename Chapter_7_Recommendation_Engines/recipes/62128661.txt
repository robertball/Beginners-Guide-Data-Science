Title:
Southern Fried Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
1 tbsp lemon and pepper seasoning
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tsp Tony Chahere's creole seasoning (optional)
6 to 8 thin sliced pork chops
1 cup vegetable or olive oil

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet to medium/high
Rinse pork chops and place in glass bowl of ice water
Mix flour, lemon pepper and garlic powder in shallow dish
Shake off excess water and coat both sides of pork chops in flour mixture, shake off excess flour
cook in hot oil 5 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
Drain well on paper towels.
