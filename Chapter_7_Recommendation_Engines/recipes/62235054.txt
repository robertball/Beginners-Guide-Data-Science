Title:
Coffee Extract Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of ground coffee beans
1/2 cup water
6 oz vodka

Directions:
Mix together.
Let sit for 2 to 3 days, shaking once a day.
Strain.  Will last forever.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Deep strong rich flavor - fantastic when used in lieu of vanilla extract in chocolate recipes.
