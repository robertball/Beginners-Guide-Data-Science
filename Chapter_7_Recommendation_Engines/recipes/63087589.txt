Title:
Laura's Guacamole Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/781103.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Avocados
1/2 Lime, juiced
2 Tbsp chopped onion
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp olive oil
1 clove minced garlic
1 Tbsp chopped cilantro
1/4 Cup tomato, chopped

Directions:
Cut avocado in half, remove pulp
Mash the avocado using fork.
stir in remaining ingredients, cover and chill for an hour prior to serving
