Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/2 cup oil
1 cup sugar
1 cup zucchini, grated
1/2 tsp vanilla
1-1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp baking powder
1/3 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
In large bowl, beat eggs.  Ad oil, sugar, grated zucchini and vanilla.  Mix lightly.
Add flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon and baking powder and mix.  Stir in nuts.
Pour into a greased 9 x 5 inch loaf pan.  Bake for 1 hour.
