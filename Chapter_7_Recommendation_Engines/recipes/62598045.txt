Title:
Cheddar Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6c broccoli florets
1-1/2c shredded cheddar
1/3c chopped onion
1-1/2c mayonnaise
3/4c sugar
3T red wine vinegar
12 bacon strips, crumbled

Directions:
Combine broccoli, cheese, and onions.
Combine mayonnaise, sugar, and vinegar.
Toss these ingredients together.
Refrigerate for 4 hours.
Stir in bacon before serving.
