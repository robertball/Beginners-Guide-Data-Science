Title:
Lemon Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1037500.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
zest from 5 lemons
1/2 cups butter
1/4 cup Crisco
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup milk
2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
2. Cream the butter, Crisco, and sugar, and lemon zest mixture together.
3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well between each egg.
4. Then, add vanilla.
5. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda together in a separate bowl.
6.  Add half the dry ingredients into the butter/sugar mixture and mix.  Then, add half the milk and mix.  Then, half the remaining dry ingredients and mix.  Then, the rest of the milk and mix.  Then, the rest of the dry ingredients and mix.
7. Spoon batter into 24 paper-lined muffin cups filling each cup 1/3 full.
8. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 F.
9.  Top with favorite frosting.

Submitter: 
Bobbie Susan
Description: 
These cupcakes are for anyone who is tired of wimpy lemon cupcakes.  They have a serious lemony punch!
