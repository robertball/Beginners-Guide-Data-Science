Title:
Cajun Twice baked potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 Potatoes
1 cup Sour cream
3 Tbs. Creole
1 cup Shredded Cheese
1 cup Butter.

Directions:
First Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Put the potatoes in your pan. (any size. Its up to you.) Put the pan in the oven and bake for aproxamently one hour. When that is done put 3 Tbs. of creole in a mixing bowl. Cut you potatoes in half and scoop the insides into the bowl.(Keep the skins of the potatoes in the pan.) (Carefull potatoes will be hot. Let them cool a little before.)Now put 1 cup of Butter, 1 cup of Shredded Cheese and 1 cup of sour cream into the bowl. Mix well untill smooth. Now dip out the mixture back into the potatoe skins. Once done Sprinkle a little bit of creole on the tops and a little bit of cheese. Stick them in the oven and bake for another five minutes. Enough time to melt the cheese.
