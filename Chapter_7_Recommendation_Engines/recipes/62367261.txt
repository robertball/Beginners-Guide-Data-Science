Title:
Apricot / Peach / Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large cooking apples or 1 quart of apricots or 1 quart of peaches
1/4 c. sugar
3/4 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. butter
3/4 c. oatmeal flakes

Directions:
Arrange fruit in pan.  Sprinkle with 1/4 c. sugar.  Combine brown sugar and flour; cut butter into mixtures as for pastry. Toss in rolled oats.  Spoon mixture over fruit.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35 -40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Miriam
Description: 
Quick recipe that can use just about any fruit.
