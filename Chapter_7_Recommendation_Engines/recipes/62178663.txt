Title:
Broccoli salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bunches broccoli chopped
1C shredded cheddar cheese
1/2C onion(red)chopped
2/3C golden rasins
10 stips bacon
1/2C sunflower seeds
Dressing:
1C hellmains mayo
1/2C sugar
2Tbsp vinegar

Directions:
Fry the bacon until crispy, crumble and add to the other ingredients. Make the dressing and pour over the salad. Taste best made a day ahead!
