Title:
Popovers with Garlic and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Eggs
1 Cup of milk
1 Cup all-purpose Flour
1 Tbsp. Butter, softened
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese
1/8 Tsp. Garlic Powder
(For A different twist on the Classic recipe, try adding 1 1/2 Tbsp. Sugar to the batter. Yum!)

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 375'
Grease a 6 cup Popover Pan (or you may add a 1/4 tbsp of butter to each cup before adding batter to prevent sticking)
Beat eggs, Milk and butter in a medium bowl
Add flour and salt, garlic powder and parmesan cheese and beat until smooth (Do not over beat)
Pour batter into prepared cups, filling about 3/4 full, making sure they're filled evenly
Bake at 375' for 20 minutes
Lower oven temp to 325' and bake another 20 minutes or until brown, raised  and crispy.
Remove from pan and Serve Immediately
