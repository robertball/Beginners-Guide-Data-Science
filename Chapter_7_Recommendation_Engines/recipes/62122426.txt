Title:
Wonderful Wheat Waffels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cp Whole Wheat Flour
1 cp Unbleached GP flour
3 tbsp Splenda
1/4 tsp Sea Salt
1 tbsp Baking Powder
1/2 cp Egg Beaters' Egg Whites
3 tbsp Smart Balance Oil
2 cp Skim Milk
1 tsp Pure Vanilla

Directions:
Heat waffle iron until ready. (check with your iron's manual).
Combine all dry ingredients.
Add liquid ingredients.
Combine until smooth.
Add 1/3 cp of batter to iron.
Cook for about 2 - 3 min.

Submitter: 
Dave Niezabitowski Sr.
Description: 
A hearty, full-flavored waffle that is both tasty and healthy.
