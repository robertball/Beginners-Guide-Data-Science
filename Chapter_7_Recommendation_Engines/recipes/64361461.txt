Title:
Cholesterol-Free Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2147698.jpg
Ingredients:
Pie Crust:  - Made according to direction on shortening box
250 Ml whole wheat flour
75  ml Crisco all vegetable Shortening
60-75 ml cold water
Filling:
540 ml E.D Smith Pumpkin Pie Filling
60 ml unsweetened apple sauce (to replace the eggs)
75 ml evaporated Skim milk
2 ml ground nutmeg
2 ml ground cinnamon

Directions:
preheat oven to 400
in a bowl combine the pumpkin milk applesauce, nutmeg and cinnamon, and mix thoroughly
Line a pie plate with the crust, and pour the filling into crust,
bake for 40-50 minutes or until crust is golden brown, pie may be a bit thin when come out of oven, and  will thicken on standing  let cool and serve

Tony Carriere

a great Cholesterol-free variation of this great dessert, as well as fibre and wholesome
