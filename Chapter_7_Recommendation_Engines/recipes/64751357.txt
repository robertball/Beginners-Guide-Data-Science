Title:
Smashed Potatoes and brown onion  on the Backyard Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds new potatoes
2 TBS water
1 onion minced
2 TBS olive oil
Season salt
Paprika

Directions:
Place new potatoes and water in a microwave safe bowl. Cover and heat on high for 8 minutes.
Make sure the potatoes soft enough to smash.
Heat an out door skillet to high.
Drain the water out of the bowl and add onion and oil.  Mix well.
When oil is smoking , carefully spread potato mixture on the grill.
Smash each potato with the back of a spatula.
Grill for 5 minutes each side or until onions are browned and potatoes are crispy.
