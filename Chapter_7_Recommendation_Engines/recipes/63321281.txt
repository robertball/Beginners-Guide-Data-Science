Title:
Zucchini and Carrots (Rosh Hashanah) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 to 1 3/4 pounds zucchini, trimmed, coarsely grated
3 tablespoon olive oil
1 1/2 cups chopped green onions (about 6 large), divided
1 1/2 pounds carrots (about 5 large), peeled, coarsely grated
4 tablespoons chopped fresh dill, divided

Directions:
Roll up grated zucchini in large kitchen towel; press to dry.
Heat oil in large deep skillet over high heat. Add 1 cup green onions and sauté 30 seconds. Add zucchini, carrots, and 2 tablespoons dill. Sauté until vegetables are just tender, tossing often, about 8 minutes.
Mix in remaining 1/2 cup green onions and 2 tablespoons dill. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
