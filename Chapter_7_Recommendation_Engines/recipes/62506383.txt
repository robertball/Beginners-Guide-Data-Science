Title:
Mocha White Chocolate Chunk Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/506176.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
2/3 cup dark brown sugar
1 1/3 cup white sugar
2 large eggs
1 tsp vanilla
4 tbsp instant coffee granules
1/2 cup cocoa powder
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups white chocolate chunks

Directions:
Beat butter, sugars, eggs, vanilla and instant coffee until fluffy.
Mix together flour, baking soda and salt, add to wet ingredients and mix well.
Stir in white chocolate chunks.
Chill dough 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 325 F.
Drop by tbsp well apart onto greased cookie sheets and bake 12-15 minutes.
Allow to cool slightly before removing from cookie sheets.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
Traditional chocolate chip cookies get a makeover with cocoa powder and instant coffee in the batter.  White chocolate chips add contrast and a burst of extra sweetness to brighten the bitter cocoa and coffee flavors.
