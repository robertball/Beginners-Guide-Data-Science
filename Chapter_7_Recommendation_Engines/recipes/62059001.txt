Title:
Ultimate Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C butter, divided
1/2 sweet onion, diced
1 C diced ham
1/4 C flour
2 Cs milk
8 oz velveeta
12 oz shredded medium or sharp cheddar
16 oz. elbow macaroni or other shaped pasta, dry
1/2 C French's dried onions

Directions:
1) Boil pasta until cooked al dente, drain and rinse with cold water.
2) In large pan, melt 1/4 C butter and saute onions till translucent, add ham and saute till slightly browned.
3) Add flour and cook for 3-5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Add milk and stir until thickened.
4) Add velveeta and 8 oz. cheddar cheese, stir until melted.  Add macaroni to cheese mixture.
5) Pour mixture into large greased baking dish.  Top with remaining cheddar cheese and and French's dried onions.
6) Bake at 350F for 20 minutes or until heated through and top is browned.
