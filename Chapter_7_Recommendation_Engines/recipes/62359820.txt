Title:
Easy Italian Style Buttermilk Biscuits* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/450915.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups All Purpose flour
3 tsp baking powder
1 tsp Morton's Lite salt
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp dried chives
1 tsp parsley flakes
1 tsp grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup Benecol margerine
2/3 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder, Lite salt, garlic salt, chives, parsley, and parmesan cheese.  Cut butter into flour mixture.  Add buttermilk and mix.
Roll into 10 balls and place in a greased pie pan. Bake at 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) for 15 minutes.
