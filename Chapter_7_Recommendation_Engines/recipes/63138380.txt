Title:
Chef John's Chicken Fettuccinne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large chicken breasts
1 cups chicken broth
1 lb.fettuccinne
2 cups heavy cream
4 cloves finely chopped garlic
2 large egg yolks
1/4 cup fresh parsley
1/8 tsp.pepper
2 cups parmigiano-reggiano cheese

Directions:
Cook chicken in broth on medium high,cover,lower heat and simmer for 5 minutes,turn over and simmer another 5 minutes.Turn heat off and let sit for 15 minutes.Remove chicken and let cool.Slice into thick pieces.
Boil water for pasta,salt water well.
Boil broth down to 1 cup.Add garlic,pepper,parsley and cream,turn heat off.Slowly add a little at a time of the hot cream to the egg yolks,add eggs to cream.
Cook pasta for 1 minute less then directions state.Drain and return to pot.Add 1 cup of cheese then sauce to pasta and cover for 1 minute.Add chicken,cover and let sit another 2 minutes.Add remaining cheese, salt,pepper and a little parsley and serve.
