Title:
Chicken & Jicama Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, cubed
1/2 jicama, cubed
1 green pepper, diced
1 onion, diced
1 small can V8
1/2 tsp cumin (or to taste)
1/2 tsp turmeric (or to taste)
1/2 tsp garlic powder (or to taste)
1 tsp red wine vinegar
1 Tb soy sauce
2 tsp hoisin sauce
1 TB sesame oil

Directions:
Fry chicken and onion in sesame oil until cooked through.
Add other ingredients and boil.
Simmer for 20 minutes.
Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
It is a great way to use the jicama from our Bountiful Basket!
