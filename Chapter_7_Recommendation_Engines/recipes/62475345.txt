Title:
Oreo Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream, softened
1/2 large container Cool Whip
1 tray Oreo cookies
Chocolate Sauce:
1/4 cup cocoa
1/2 cup margarine
2 cups white sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1 can evaporated milk
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix ice cream and Cool Whip together.
Crush Oreos and add to ice cream mixture (save a few to sprinkle on top).
Spread cookie ice cream into a 9 x 13 pan, sprinkle Oreos on top and freeze.
Serve with chocolate sauce.
For chocolate sauce: melt margarine, add cocoa, sugar, and salt. Mix well. Add milk and cook, stirring constantly. When it boils, cook 4-5 minutes longer. Add vanilla when it has finished boiling.
