Title:
Alexander's Double Chocolate Chip Cookie Ice Cream Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 Tsp baking powder
1/2 Tsp baking soda
1/2 Tsp Kosher salt
1 stick plus 2 tbsp unsalted sweet cream butter, room temperature
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1 egg, plus one egg yolk
1 tsp McCormick’s pure vanilla extract
3/4 cup of mixed dark and milk chocolate chips
1 cup of dried cherries
Blue Bunny® Cherry Pickin’ Chocolate™ ice cream
Blue Bunny® Chunky Chocolate Chip
Blue Bunny® Double Strawberry
Smucker’s® Hot Fudge and Smuckers Caramel Ice Cream Toppings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, Kosher salt in a medium size bowl. Mix with a fork until all ingredients are incorporated. Set aside.
In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment add softened butter and whisk until just fluffy.
Add granulated and brown sugar and cream together and beat for 40 seconds.
