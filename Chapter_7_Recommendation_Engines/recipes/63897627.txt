Title:
Portobello Bolognese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz. dried mushrooms
2 cups water
2 tbsp. olive oil
3 tbsp. butter
4 portobello mushroom caps, diced
1 medium onion, chopped
1 small carrot, chopped
1 small stalk of celery, chopped
2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
1 bay leaf
2 tbsp. tomato paste
1 cup dry red wine
1 and 3/4 cups crushed tomatoes
1 cup milk
1 lb. fettuccine, cooked and drained
Chopped flat leaf parsley
Grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring dried mushrooms and water to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and simmer 10 minutes.
Cool then drain mushrooms, reserving liquid.
Chop mushrooms coarsely.
Heat oil and butter in a large pot over medium heat.
Add portobellos, carrot, onion, celery garlic and bay leaf.
Stir occasionally and cook about 15 miutes or until tender.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Stir in tomato paste and cook 1 minute.
Stir in wine and cook 3 minutes.
Stir in tomatoes.
Reduce heat and stir in shopped mushrooms, reserved liquid and milk.
Simmer over low heat until thick, about 30 minutes.
Toss sauce with pasta and garnish with parsley and Parmesan before serving.
