Title:
Teriyaki Chicken Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 Pounds Chicken Breasts - Cubed
5 Cups Frozen Vegetables for Stir Fry
Teriyaki Marinade:
1 T Cornstarch
1 T Cold Water
1 Cup Soy Sauce
1/2 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar
1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup White Sugar
3 Cloves Garlic, Minced
1 t Ginger

Directions:
Mix all ingredients for marinade in a small saucepan. Stirring Often, Bring to boil, reduce heat simmer until thick.
Mix 1 Cup Marinade with Cubed Chicken and marinate for 30 minutes. Keep remaining marinade and start to reheat when preparing to cook chicken so that it is warmed again when serving.
In a skillet with light oil, fry chicken for 7-8 minutes, add frozen vegetables and cook another 8-10 minutes covered, stiring occassionally, until vegies are heated through.
Serve over warm white rice and top off with as little or as much of the extra marinade you like.
