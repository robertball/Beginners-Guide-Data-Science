Title:
Ultimate 7 Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can 16oz Refried Beans
1 Tbsp. Taco Seasoning Mix
1 Cup Salsa
Sour Cream
1 Cup Lettuce
1 Cup Shredded Cheese
1/2 Cup Chopped Green Onions
2 Tbsp. Sliced Olives

Directions:
Mix Beans and Seasoning Mix. Spread into bottom of 9" pie pan. Layer remaining ingredients over bean mixture. Refrigerate. Serve with Tortilla Chips.
