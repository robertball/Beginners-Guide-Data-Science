Title:
red pepper soup with hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red peppers, chopped
2 T olive oil
1 large onion,chopped
3 T hummus with roasted red
peppers
2 cups chicken broth
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup half and half

Directions:
Saute peppers and onions in the oil. About 5 minutes. Add chicken broth and salt and pepper to taste. Cook for 20 minutes.
Puree in processor,add hummus and half and half. Allow flavors to meld.  Serves 2.  Best served about 2 days after cooking.

Submitter: 
simone stevin
Description: 
This is an easy yet unusual red pepper soup. Quick to make it incorporates store-bought hummus with roasted red pepper and (fat-free) half and half for a very tasty and satisfying hot soup.
