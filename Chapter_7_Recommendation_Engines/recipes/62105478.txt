Title:
Cheesy Crab Breads Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of crab meat
8oz velveeta cheese
1/4 cup of butter
1 baguette of bread

Directions:
Melt butter in a double boiler.
Break Velveeta into squares and add to the butter, stir until melted.
Stir in crab meat and remove from heat.
Slice up baguette into individual pieces.
Spread some crab and cheese mixture onto each bread.
Place breads on baking sheet and broil for about 5 minutes or until cheese is browned.
Serve immediately and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Emily Rumanek
Description: 
A delicious and easy appetizer that can be made ahead of time and frozen if need be. Just make them and put them in the fridge or freezer, broil them when you need them!
