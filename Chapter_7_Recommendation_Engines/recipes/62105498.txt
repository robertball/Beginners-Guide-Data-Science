Title:
Nacho Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound beef
pkg taco seasoning
3/4 cup of water
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 can sliced olives
pkg nacho dip
4 green onions
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
dash or two of tabasco
1 bag of tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown beef in skillet for 5 to 10 minutes then add the water, the package of taco seasoning,the dashes of tabasco,and the minced garlic. Simmer while you cut up the vegetables. After the beef and sauce has thickened put it in a 9"-13" casserole dish, put tomatoes and olives and put in the oven for 15 minutes on 400. Prepare the nacho dip according to the directions. Pull the casserole out and add the nacho dip. Eat and enjoy!
