Title:
Chipotle Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/917808.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. salt pork or bacon, cubed
1 16 oz. bag dried black beans
64 oz. water
4 large carrots, peeled and sliced or shredded
1 medium yellow onion, diced
4 large sticks celery, diced
3 dried chipotle peppers, julienned
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Over medium heat, brown the pork (about 5 minutes).
Add the onions and celery. Stir-fry until onions turn transparent.
Add water, beans and chipotles.
Simmer over low heat for 3 hours, adding more water as needed.
Mash beans for about 2 minutes or until desired consistency.
Add carrots and spices, return to simmer for another 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sharon
Description: 
Southwestern style, but without the cumin. Just the right amount of heat.
