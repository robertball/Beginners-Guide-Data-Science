Title:
Lisa's favorite mexican rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked rice
1/2 onion chopped fine
2 cloves of garlic minced
2lbs ground beef
taco seasoning one packet or make your own
1 package of southwestern frozen veggies
1/2c salsa to your taste
1/4 cup sliced kalamata olives
2c grated sharp cheddar
1 can of kidney beans(opt.)

Directions:
1. Saute onion and garlic in a little oil
2. add ground beef -brown beef and season
3.spray a casserole dish and mix together meat mixture frozen veggies,beans, salsa,black olives, and 1 cup of the grated cheese!
4.Top with other 1 cup of cheese
5.bake at 350 degrees for 30 mins.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Cole
Description: 
this is a welcome change from tacos! Serve with a salad, more salsa and sour cream.
