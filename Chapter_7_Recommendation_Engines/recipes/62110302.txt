Title:
Bean and Rice Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Long Grain Brown Rice (uncooked)
1 Can Pinto Beans
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp ground chipotle
pinch white pepper
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 Large Tomato Diced (seeds removed)
1/3 Red Onion Chopped
1/3 cup Chopped Fresh Cilantro divided
Lime
10" Flour Tortillas
Shredded Romaine Lettuce
Shredded White Cheddar Cheese
Sour Cream **optional**

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package, usually 1 1/2 cup rice to 3 cups water, bring to a boil cover and simmer 45-50 minutes.
While rice is cooking, in small sauce pan season beans with Onion Powder, Garlic Powder, Chipotle Seasoning, and White Pepper cook on low for 10-15 minutes stirring occasionally.
To prepare Salsa:
In small bowl combine tomato, onion, juice of 1/2 lime, and 3 TBSP of cilantro. Cover and let flavors meld.
When rice is finished fluff with fork.  Add juice of 1/2 lime, and remaining cilantro.
Heat Tortillas according to package
Lay each tortilla flat adding a scoop of beans, a scoop of rice, and fresh salsa, sprinkle with shredded white cheddar, lettuce, and add sour cream if desired.

Submitter: 
A.Davala
Description: 
Hearty and low fat, these burritos are great for cooks on a budget, vegitarians, or lenten meals.  They are full of flavor and will leave you satisfied but wishing you had room for more. (and you'd never know they didn't have meat in them.)
