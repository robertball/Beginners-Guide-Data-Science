Title:
Cinnamon Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 Tbs cinnamon (or to taste)
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 white sugar (reserve for topping)
1 tsp cinnamon (reserve for topping)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Mix dry ingredients
Mix cinnamon and sugar, then cream in butter
Add egg and vanilla
Gradually mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients
Roll dough into cookie sized balls
Mix 1/2 cup sugar and 1 tsp cinnamon (add more cinnamon to taste)
Coat cookie balls in cinnamon sugar mixture
Bake on ungreased cookie sheet, 8 - 10 minutes

Description: 
I made these for a holiday treat. Now my 4 year old asks for them year around.
