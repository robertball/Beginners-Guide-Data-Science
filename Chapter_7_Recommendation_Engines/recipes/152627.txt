Title:
Marshmallow Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977564.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chunks, melted
1 (10 ounce) package JET-PUFFED marshmallows
2/3 cup PLANTERS Pecans, chopped

Directions:
Melt chocolate as directed on package. Dip marshmallows, one at a time, in chocolate, turning to evenly coat each marshmallow. Gently shake off excess chocolate.
Place marshmallows, in single layer, on sheets of wax paper. Sprinkle each with about 1 tsp. of the pecans.
Let stand at room temperature 1-1/2 to 2 hours or until chocolate is set. Store in airtight container at room temperature.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
The kids can help with these easy marshmallow treats topped with chopped pecans.
