Title:
Grandma Fanelli's Coffee cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 1/3 sticks butter
1 c. white sugar
1 c. dark brown brown sugar
2 1/2 c. self-rising flour
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. baking soda
1 c. sour milk (add i tbsp. vinegar to 1 c. milk to sour it)
1/2 c. chopped walnuts
2 eggs, beaten beforehand

Directions:
mix first 6 ingredients together and reserve 1/2 cup for crumb topping. Add sour milk and eggs gradually, mixing together thoroughly. Add baking soda and walnuts. Grease 9x13 inch pan. Pour ingredients. Sprinkle crumbs on top. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 mins
