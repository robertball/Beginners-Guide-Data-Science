Title:
Lentil Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb lentils (dry, then soaked and rinsed)
1 can tomato soup
1 pkg onion soup mix
3T chili powder
6c water
1 can chili or kidney beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Combine soup, soup mix, chili powder, and water.
Boil, cover, and simmer 30 minutes.
Add lentils and kidney beans.
Boil, cover, and simmer 30 minutes.
