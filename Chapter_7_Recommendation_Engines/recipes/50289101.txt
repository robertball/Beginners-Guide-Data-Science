Title:
Cinnamon Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup splenda
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine softened butter, splenda and cinnamon. Mix well.

Submitter: 
Kay Prince Gionet
Description:
