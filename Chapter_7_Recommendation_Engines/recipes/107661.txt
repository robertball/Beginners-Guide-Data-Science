Title:
California Turkey Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989420.jpg
Ingredients:
4 burrito size MANNY'S™ flour tortillas
1/2 cup prepared ranch salad dressing
1 (9 ounce) package HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Honey Deli Turkey
1 tomato, thinly sliced
1 small cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
1 avocado, sliced and drizzled with
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 cup alfalfa sprouts
1 cup baby spinach leaves or arugula

Directions:
Lay tortillas on clean surface. Drizzle each tortilla with 1 tablespoon ranch dressing. Layer each tortilla with equal amounts of turkey, tomato, cucumber, avocado, alfalfa sprouts, and spinach or arugula. Drizzle with remaining ranch dressing. Roll up tortillas tightly, cut in half diagonally, and secure each half with a wooden pick.

Submitter: 
HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Deli Sandwich Meats
Description: 
The crisp crunch of fresh vegetables and the great flavor of deli turkey make these Ranch-dressed roll-ups a hit!
