Title:
Blue Lagoon Jr. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4482991.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup boiling water
2 cups vanilla ice cream
1 cup milk
1 pkg. Berry Blue Jello powder
1/2 cup blueberries
6 ice cubes

Directions:
Pour boiling water into blender.
Add Jello powder, cover and blend.
Add remaining ingredients, cover and blend until smooth.
