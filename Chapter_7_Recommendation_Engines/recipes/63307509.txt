Title:
Spicy Mini Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package dry oyster crackers
1 cup oil
1 tsp dill weed
1 package ranch style buttermilk dressing (dry)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large bowl.  Stir and mix well, every 30 minutes or so for 3-4 hours, until all oil is absorbed.
Keeps for several weeks in tightly covered container.
