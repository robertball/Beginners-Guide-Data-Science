Title:
Sesame Udon Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 servings Udon noodles
Sauce:
2 small cloves garlic, minced
1 T. fresh ginger, minced
4 T. soy sauce
3 T. rice vinegar
1/4 cup peanut oil
3 T. Asian sesame oil
Dash Tabasco
Vegetables:
1/2 of small red pepper, matchstick sliced
1/2 of small green pepper, matchstick sliced
1/2 of small yellow pepper, matchstick sliced
4 green onions, minced
1/2 bag snap peas, diagonaly sliced (optional)
2 T. sesame seeds, toasted

Directions:
Put all sauce ingredients in shaker jar.  Shake well to blend.
Chop vegetables and put in microwave safe bowl.  Just prior to serving heat the vegetables until warm but not cooked.
Cook Udon noodles in boiling water according to package directions.
Drain and toss with the dressing and the vegetables.  Top with the sesame seeds and serve.
These noodles are very good warm or at room temp.
