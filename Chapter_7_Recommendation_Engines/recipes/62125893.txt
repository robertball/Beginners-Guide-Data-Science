Title:
Rich Chocolate Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup light colored corn syrup
1 tbsp water
3/4 cup semisweet mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except chocolate
Simmer over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils.
Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted.
Cool to room temperature and pour over cake.
