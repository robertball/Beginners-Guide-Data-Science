Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2443823.jpg
Ingredients:
Pretzel Sticks, Mini Marshmallows, White Chocolate, Microwave, Fork, Wax/Parchment Paper

Directions:
1. Place a mini marshmallow on each side of the pretzel to form a bone shape. Get creative. Make like, 50 of them. 2. Melt the white chocolate chips in a bowl in your microwave. The package should tell you how long to microwave for. 3. Dip ya bones in the chocolate. Use a fork to fish 'em out. 4. Put dem bones on your wax or parchment paper. 5. Dip them again because YOU DON'T DO THINGS HALF-A**ED. 6. Eat them bones. 7. Pound your chest.
