Title:
Nichole's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 eggs
5 tablespoons Worchestor Sauce
1 1/2 package smashed ritz crackers
1 can basil garlic chopped tomatoes
1 small onion diced
1 tablespoon minced garlic
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degress. Get a 8x8 baking dish out and place the olive oil at the bottom of dish.  In a large bowl place eggs, worchestor sauce, crackers, tomatoes, onion, garlic, salt, pepper and beef mix all well with hands. Place your meet mixture in 8x8 dish and smash it out evenly.
Put in preheated oven bake for 1 hour.
