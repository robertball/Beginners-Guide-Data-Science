Title:
Grilled Pepperjack Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices Pepperjack cheese
4 slices of bread
1 1/2 tablespoons of butter

Directions:
Heat a non-stick skillet on medium heat for 5 minutes. In the meantime, butter ALL sides of each slice of bread. Arrange sandwhiches so that each one has 2 slices of cheese.
Place 2 sandwhiches on the hot skillet. Cook on each side for 3-5 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool for a few minutes and serve warm.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
This is a spicy twist to an old childhood favorite -- grilled cheese sandwiches.
