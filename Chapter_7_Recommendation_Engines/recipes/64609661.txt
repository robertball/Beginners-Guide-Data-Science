Title:
Sam's Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. oil
1 C. white rice
2 C. chicken broth
1 can diced tomatoes - do not drain
1 can corn (optional) - drained
2 bell peppers any color (optional) - sliced thin
1 large sweet onion - chopped
1 can or jar of chilies - chopped
2 packs of Goya seasoning

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet
Add white rice and cook until brown turning frequently
Add onion and bell peppers while browning rice
Once rice is brown, add all other ingredients
Bring to boil, then simmer 20 minutes with lid on
Serve with chicken or steak
