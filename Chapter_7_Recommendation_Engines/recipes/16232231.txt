Title:
Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1&1/2 lb. ground beef
1 egg lightly beaten
1/3 Cup Ketchup
1/3 Cup grated parmesan cheese
4 cloves of garlic minced or through a garlic press
1 med onion minced
3/4 Cup dry bread crumbs
1/4 Cup finely chopped fresh parsley
2 tsp. dried basil
2 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. yellow mustard
1 cup olive oil

Directions:
- combine all ingredients except olive oil in a medium bowl
- Roll mixture into 1& 1/2 inch balls
- Heat oil in saute pan over med/high heat
- Fry until golden brown but not cooked trough
- Remove balls to plate lined with paper towels
-  Heat balls in sauce & simmer for 40 min.
