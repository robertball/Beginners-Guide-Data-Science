Title:
Vegan Jalapeno Tofu Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz  (about 1 cup) silken tofu (such as Mori-nu Soft Silken Tofu)
1/2 cup soy milk (or your favorite non dairy milk)
2 green onions, green parts only or chive
2 Tablespoons pickled jalapenos (or canned or jarred or minced fresh jalapenos)or black pepper
2 Tablespoons chopped cilantro or parsley
1 clove garlic
1/2 teaspoon dry dill
1 teaspoon sugar (opt) (or a drop or two of liquid stevia!)
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Blend everything together until smooth. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
