Title:
Garden Tomato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 c. chopped fresh tomatoes (with seeds and juice)
1 c. finely chopped carrots
1 medium onion, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, crushed
Splash of extra virgin olive oil
2 c. chicken broth
¾ tsp. white sugar
2 tsp. salt (or less to taste)
¼ tsp. celery salt
¼ tsp. ground cloves
½ tsp. ground black pepper
¾ tsp. dried dill
1 c. skim milk
1 TBS cornstarch
½ TBS butter (optional)
5-6 large fresh basil leaves, plus more for garnish
Shredded parmesan cheese for garnish

Directions:
Add tomatoes, carrots, onion, and garlic to large pot along with a small splash of olive oil. Cook on medium heat. Cover and simmer slowly for about 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add chicken stock and all herbs except fresh basil. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes, again stirring occasionally.
Dissolve cornstarch in milk. Heat gently with butter on medium-low heat until melted.
When veggies and chicken stock have simmered together for 20 minutes, slowly add milk mixture.
Purée about ½ of mixture in small batches along with basil leaves. Return to pot and heat on low for 3-5 minutes.
Serve topped with parmesan cheese and shredded basil leaves.

Description: 
A delicious soup to use up the abundance of garden fresh tomatoes we have each year. Fresh and flavorful, and many opportunities to adjust this soup to your liking!
