Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 TBS sugar
2 TBS prepared mustard (Grey Poupon is good)
1 TBS vinegar
1 cup ketchup
Dash of pepper
1/8 tsp ground cloves
Few drops of Tobasco sauce
Few drops of Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Cook ground beef and onions until ground beef is no longer pink.  Drain fat from meat (if necessary). Mix all other ingredients together and then add to ground beef. Cover and simmer on low heat for 20 minutes before serving.
