Title:
Twisted Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1049512.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup MUSSELMAN'S® Apple Butter
2 (7.5 ounce) cans refrigerated biscuit dough
2/3 cup sugar
2 tablespoons cinnamon
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
Separate biscuits and cut in quarters. In a bowl, combine sugar and cinnamon. Roll biscuit pieces in cinnamon sugar to coat, and drop them in a greased Bundt pan. Make sure they are distributed evenly.
On the stove top, heat the butter, brown sugar, and sugar to a boil until sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and stir in apple butter.
Pour mixture over the dough, and bake in a pre-heated 350 degrees F oven for 30 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes, turn upside down onto serving plate, and remove pan.

Submitter: 
Musselman's® Apple Butter
Description: 
With refrigerated biscuit dough and delicious apple butter in the sweet sauce, this warm and sticky treat is on the brunch table in no time.
