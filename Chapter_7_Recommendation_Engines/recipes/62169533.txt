Title:
Jamaican rice and peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/285042.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups coconut milk
2 cups water
1 stalk of green onion
½ tsp. thyme flakes
1 can red kidney beans or pidgeon peas
½ tsp. salt
2 cups white rice
1 whole hot pepper (e.g. Scotch Bonnet)

Directions:
Combine milk, beans and water in a pot and bring to boil.
Add the crushed green onion, chopped onion and thyme flakes, salt and stir. Allow to simmer for 10 minutes.
Stir in rice and the whole hot pepper, cover and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and allow rice to cook until grains are tender.
Note: you can also cook the rice with ham stock or throw in a ham bone when you are cooking it for nice flavour.

Delicious Jamaican comfort food that can be paired with jerk chicken and cole slaw.
