Title:
Green Party Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages lemon-lime drink mix
3 cups granulated sugar
46 oz pineapple juice
46 oz orange juice
2 quarts water
1 quart lime sherbet
1 2 liter bottle of ginger ale

Directions:
Combine drink mix, sugar, pineapple and orange juices, and water.  Chill for 30 minutes.
When ready to serve, add sherbet and ginger ale.  Stir well.  Makes 2 gallons.

Submitter: 
Amy
Description: 
This sweet, citrus flavored punch is much beloved by kids and adults.  It's a favorite and is especially good at birthday parties and showers.
