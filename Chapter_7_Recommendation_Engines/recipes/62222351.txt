Title:
White Wedding Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (46 oz.) Pineapple Juice, chilled
1 C. White Grape Juice, chilled
1/2 Gal. Vanilla Ice Cream
3 qts. Ginger Ale, chilled

Directions:
Mix juices together. When ready to serve, put juices in punch bowl and add scoops of ice cream; pour in ginger ale. Stir slightly.
