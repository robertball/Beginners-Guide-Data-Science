Title:
Lem-Tea Whammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/851171.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups lemonade
4 cups cold tea
2 tablespoons white sugar

Directions:
In large pitcher, stir together lemonade and tea. Stir in sugar until dissolved. Chill in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Sarah Jefferson
Description: 
This also has been called several other names, this is just what I like to call it. Serve over ice.
