Title:
Groovy Grannies' Waffle-y Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983867.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups POST WAFFLE CRISP Cereal
1/2 cup PLANTERS Peanuts
1/2 cup raisins
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
Mix cereal, peanuts and raisins in large bowl. Pour melted butter over cereal mixture; toss well. Spread evenly in 15x10x1-inch baking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 10 minutes. Cool. Store in tightly covered container.
