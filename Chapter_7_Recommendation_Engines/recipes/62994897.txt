Title:
No Fail Simple Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar spaghetti sauce
1 lb hamburger
1 pint cottage cheese or ricotta cheese
2 C shredded mozzarella cheese
1 egg
10 uncooked lasagna noodles

Directions:
Brown hamburger then add spaghetti sauce and 1/2 jar of water.
In a small bowl, beat the egg and mix in the cottage or ricotta cheese.
Lay 4 lasagna noodle on the bottom of a rectangular pan. Layer 1/2 of the hamburger mixture over noodles. Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella cheese over hamburger mixture.
Layer 3 more noodles on top. Layer on the cheese mixture over noodles.
Layer last 3 noodles, remaining hamburger mixture a remaining mozzarella cheese.
Cover and bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
This recipe does not require pre-cooking the lasagna noodles. It is impossible to screw up. You can modify this recipe quite easily.
