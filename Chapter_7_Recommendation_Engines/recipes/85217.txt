Title:
Greek Avgolemono Soup with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
1 cup water, divided
1/2 cup orzo pasta
2 teaspoons McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Greek Seasoning
3 egg yolks
1 cup cooked, shredded chicken or turkey
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon cornstarch

Directions:
Combine chicken broth, 3/4 cup water, orzo, and Greek seasoning in 4-quart saucepan; bring to boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 9-10 minutes or until orzo is just tender.
Whisk together egg yolks, remaining 1/4 cup water and cornstarch in large bowl. Gradually whisk in about 2 cups of the hot broth mixture. Slowly whisk egg mixture back into the remaining broth mixture in pan.
Cook over medium-low heat 5-7 minutes to slightly thicken. Do not boil. Stir in chicken and lemon juice; heat. Garnish each serving with thin slice of lemon and chopped parsley, if desired.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
Avgolemono Soup is a creamy, white traditional Greek soup accented with McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Greek Seasoning, orzo and a touch of lemon.
