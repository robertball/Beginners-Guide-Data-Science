Title:
Sweet potato and black bean chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can light coconut milk
2 cans water
3 tablespoons minced garlic
3 tablespoons Sriracha hot sauce
2 teaspoons curry powder
2 teaspoons cumin
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
1/2 teaspoon garam masala
1/4 teaspoon coriander
dash of nutmeg
dash of cloves
dash of ginger
2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 green pepper, chopped
1/2 large red onion, chopped
1 can diced tomatoes
2 cans black beans, partially drained
1 can garbanzos, drained
3 links chorizo(optional)
fresh cilantro to garnish (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. In large stew pot, combine coconut milk, water, hot sauce, and spices. Bring to a boil. Add sweet potatoes, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
2. Meanwhile, fry chorizo links in skillet. Set aside to cool, then slice.
3. Add remaining ingredients. Allow to simmer approximately 1 hour, until chili has thickened and flavors are blended.
4. Garnish with fresh cilantro to serve.

Submitter: 
Laurie
Description: 
A hearty Mexican-Indian fusion dish I whipped up one chilly evening. Sop it up with some garlic naan or a crusty bread. Garam Masala is an Indian spice blend including coriander, cumin, cardamom, and cinnamon- I was able to find it at my local supermarket.
