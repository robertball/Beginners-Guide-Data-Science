Title:
Double fudge fancifiles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz pkg cream cheese-softened
2Tbsp butter
1/4C sugar
1Tbsp cornstarch
1 egg
2T milk
1/2tsp vanilla
Base :
1pkg devils food cake mix
3 eggs
1/3C oil
1C water

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Grease and flour 13x9pan. In small bowl blen all filling ing. Beat at high speed until smooth and creamy. Set aside. In large bowl blend cake mix, eggs, oil and water until moistened. Beat 2min at high speed. Pour half of the batter into the pan. Pour cream cheese mixture over the batter, spreading to cover. Pour remaining batter over the top. Bake at 350 45-55min Cool,
Frost with fudge frosting.
