Title:
Au Poivre Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4528703.jpg
Ingredients:
1 shallot, finely chopped
2 peppercorns, coarsely crushed
2 tbsp butter
3 oz cognac or brandy
8 oz heavy cream
1 large pinch sea salt

Directions:
Saut√© shallot & peppercorns in butter over low heat until shallots have just a tinge of brown. Add cognac carefully, as it may flame, and reduce by half. Add heavy cream and salt, and simmer 3-4 minutes.
