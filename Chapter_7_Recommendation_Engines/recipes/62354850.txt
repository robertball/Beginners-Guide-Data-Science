Title:
Rajma Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. kidney beans (1 can)
1 medium onion, chopped
garlic clove, minced
small piece of ginger, minced
2 T. tomato paste (or tomatoes)
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. chili powder
1/4 tsp. turmeric
1 tsp. coriander
1 tsp. garam masala
oil
1/2 tsp. mustard or cumin seeds

Directions:
Heat the oil, add seeds and fry the onions till they turn brown.  Add tomato paste, ginger, garlic, and turmeric.  Mix, then add coriander, salt, and chili powder.
Add beans and 1 1/2 c. water.  Simmer till correct consistency to serve over rice.
Finally, add the garam masala
