Title:
A Nearly Guiltless Strawberry Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large fresh strawberries
5 large ice cubes
2 tablespoons triple sec
1 tablespoon fresh squeezed lime juice
1/2 tablespoon splenda sugar blend
50-75 ml tequila

Directions:
In a blender add all ingredients and blend until smooth. Pour in your favorite margarita glass and enjoy!

Submitter: 
spinn710
Description: 
I decided to experiment on a hot summer day and came up with this delicious and nearly guiltless margarita!
