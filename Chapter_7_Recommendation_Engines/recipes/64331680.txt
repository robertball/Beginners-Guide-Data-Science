Title:
Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2050948.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup (225g) Plain Yogurt (full fat)
4 scoop Kal Pure Stevia Extract (equivalent to 4 tbsp Sugar)
Dash of Sea Salt
1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)
1 cup (140g) Frozen Sliced Strawberries

Directions:
Put Yogurt in blender
Add Stevia, Sea Salt, & Vanilla Extract
Add Frozen Sliced Strawberries
Blend

Submitter: 
Zan
Description: 
Tastes alot like a strawberry milkshake! Excellent for anyone watching their sugar levels or their waste line.
