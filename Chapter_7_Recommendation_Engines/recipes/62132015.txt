Title:
Santa Barbara Lime Chiffon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker pie shell
Lime Custard:
4 eggs yolks
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup lime juice (juice of 2 limes)
1 Tbs (1 envelope) gelatin
1/2 cup cold water
Grated rind of 2 limes (reserve some to garnish top of pie)
Merangue:
4 egg whites
Pinch of salt
1/2 cup sugar
green food coloring (optional)
Whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
To make the custard:
Beat the egg yolks lightly and stir in sugar and the lime juice.  Cook the mixture in double boiler until it coats the spoon. Remove from heat.
Meanwhile, soak the gelatin in the cold water for 5 minutes, then stir into the hot custard.  Add the grated rind and cool until thickened.
To make merangue:
Beat the egg whites with the salt until stiff and then gradually beat in the remaining sugar. Continue beating until the meringue is thick and glossy. Add a few drops of food coloring if desired.
Fold merangue into the lime custard and pour into the pie shell.  Garnish top of pie with reserved lime rind. Refrigerate until set.
Top with whipped cream if desired and serve.

Submitter: 
Momi
Description: 
A light and refreshing lime pie, a perfect dessert after a heavy meal or BBQ. Contains raw egg whites.
