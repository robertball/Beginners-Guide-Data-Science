Title:
Fluffy Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large eggs
1 1/2 tsp. cornstarch
1/4 cup plus 2 T sour cream
2 T butter or margarine

Directions:
Place eggs, cornstarch and sour cream in a large mixing bowl.  Using a mixer, blend on medium speed until all ingredients are incorporated.
Melt butter in a large non-stick skillet.  Add egg mixture and cook until eggs no longer appear wet, stirring occasionally.  Season to taste and serve.

Submitter: 
CSANDST1
Description: 
These are the best scrambled eggs--fluffy and flavorful.  Use 1/4 tsp. cornstarch per egg and 1 T sour cream per egg if you want to increase the servings.
