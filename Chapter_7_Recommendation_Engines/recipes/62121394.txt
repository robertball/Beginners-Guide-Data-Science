Title:
Grandma Bergman's Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup vegetable shortening
1 Cup brown sugar
1/2 Cup sugar
1 egg
1/4 Cup water
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 Cups oats
1 Cup flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
With electric mixer, beat together shortening, sugars, egg, water, and vanilla until creamy.
Add remaining ingredients.
Mix well.
Add any additional ingredients.  (raisins, dried cranberries, walnuts, coconut, etc.)
Dropped 2 inch balls onto greased cookie sheet.
Bake 12- 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Hank's Mom
Description: 
Traditional and healthy favorite! Can be modified by adding raisins, dries cranberries, walnuts, or coconut.  Whatever you like!
