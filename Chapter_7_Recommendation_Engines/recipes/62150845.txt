Title:
Italian Meat and Spinach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Makes 8 servings
1 Unbaked 9-inch pastry shell
1/2 lb. ground beef
1/2 lb. Italian turkey sausage, casing removed
3/4 cup chopped red pepper
1 chopped onion
10 oz. sliced fresh mushrooms
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/4 cup water
1 can tomato paste ( 6 oz)
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dried basil
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1 10 oz. package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained
1 cup light ricotta cheese
1 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup chopped plum tomatoes, seeds removed
1 small can sliced black olives, drained

Directions:
In a medium skillet cook beef, sausage and 1 clove of garlic until beef is browned.  Add onion, red pepper and mushroom until tender.  Drain well.  Stir in tomato paste, water, remaining garlic, salt, basil, oregano and bring to a boil.  Cover and simmer for 10 minutes on low.
Line pie plate with pie crust and cover with a double thickness of foil.  Bake at 450 degrees for 9 minutes.  Remove foil and continue baking for 7 minutes or until crust is dry, set aside.
In a medium bowl combine spinach, ricotta and 1/2 cup mozzorella cheese.  Spoon the filling into the bottom of the prepared crust.  Top with meat mixture.  Cover the edges of the crust with foil and bake in a 350 degree oven for 45 minutes.
Top with remaining mozzarella, chopped tomatoes and olives.  Bake 10 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.  Makes 8 servings.
