Title:
Chicken Tettrazini - Jeanie Briscoe's recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz spaghetti
1/2 stick butter
3-4 lb chicken
1 lg can mushrooms
1 T parsley flakes
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 c sour cream
salt
pepper
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook chicken in seasoned water. Prepare water for spaghetti and cook to package directions.
Saute mushrooms and season with parsley.
Drain cooked spaghetti and mix with chicken mixture.
Top with parmesan cheese.
Bake nat 350 until bubbly.
