Title:
Sweet & Sour Chicken Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1-8 oz. can pineapple chunks
1 small bell pepper
1 small zuccinni, halved lenthwise
SAUCE:
1/2 C. Pineapple juice
3 Tbls. Brown Sugar

Directions:
Cut chicken and vegetables into 1-inch chunks and thread onto skewers: Veggie, chicken, pineapple, chicken, veggie, and so forth until 3/4 of skewer is covered. Place on hot grill, turning frequently until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
Meanwhile, pour pineapple juice into small saucepan; add brown sugar and stir well to dissolve. Bring to a boil and allow to cook down to a thin syrup.
Serve kabobs over a bed of rice with the sauce drizzled liberally over the top.

Submitter: 
Lisa Garnier
Description: 
Chicken, pineapple, squash, and green peppers are threaded on skewers and slow grilled over charcoal, drizzled with sauce and served over a bed of rice.
