Title:
Chicken Paprikash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, sliced
1 1/2 tablespoons butter
1 cup chopped onion
1 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
1 1/2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons paprika
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon ground red pepper
1 cup chicken stock
1 cup heavy cream
1 teaspoon all-purpose flour
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
Cooked egg noodles

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat butter over medium-high heat.
Add chicken and pan-fry until cooked through and juices run clear, about 10 minutes.
Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.
Add remaining butter to the skillet.
Saute onion and bell pepper in butter until onion is translucent but not brown, about 5 to 8 minutes.
Add paprika, salt, garlic powder and red pepper.
Pour chicken stock and cream into the pan, and bring all to a boil.
Reduce heat to simmer; mix flour with sour cream until smooth, then whisk into chicken stock.
Place chicken in skillet; simmer until chicken is heated through and sauce has thickened.
Serve over egg noodles.
