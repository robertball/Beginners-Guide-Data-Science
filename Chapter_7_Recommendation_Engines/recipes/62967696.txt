Title:
Orange Sparkle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 unbeaten egg
1 tsp lemon extract
1 tsp orange extract
2 Tblsp orange zest
1/4 cup orange juice
Sugar to coat cookie dough with

Directions:
1. Sift flour, baking soda and salt together and set aside.
2. Cream butter and slowly add sugar. Add to cream mixture: egg, orange extract and lemon extract, orange zest and orange juice.
3. Blend in sifted dry ingredients.  Chill for 1 to 2 hours
4. Drop by teaspoonfuls into sugar and roll to coat.  Place on lightly greased cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees F for 12 to 15 minutes or until golden.
