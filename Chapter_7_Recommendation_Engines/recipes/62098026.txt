Title:
Easy Japanese Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3-5 pound beef roast
1 (14.5 oz.) can of onion flavored beef broth
1 1/3 c. rice wine
2/3 c. soy sauce
2 tablespoons sugar
5 cloves garlic
4 thin slices of fresh ginger
2 medium onions
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Finely chop onions and garlic, and spread them on the bottom of a 5 quart slow cooker.
Place roast on top of onion and garlic layer.
Mix broth, rice wine, and soy sauce in a separate bowl, and pour the mixture into the slow cooker.
Drop ginger slices into broth mixture around roast.
Sprinkle the top of the roast with sugar and pepper.
Cover and cook on low for 12 to 18 hours, until roast easily pulls apart with fork.  During cooking, flip entire roast over two or three times.
