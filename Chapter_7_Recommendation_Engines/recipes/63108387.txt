Title:
Peanut Butter and Honey Cereal Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Multigrain Cheerios
3 cups Kix cereal
½ cup natural peanut butter
½ cup honey
Cooking spray

Directions:
Combine cereal in large mixing bowl; set aside.
Spray 8x8 baking pan with cooking spray.
Over low heat, combine peanut butter and honey, stirring constantly in saucepan until smooth.
Pour peanut butter-honey mixture over cereal and mix until coated. Press into baking pan.  Place in freezer for 10-15 minutes before cutting.
