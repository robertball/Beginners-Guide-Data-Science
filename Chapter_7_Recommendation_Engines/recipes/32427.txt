Title:
Holiday Punch II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4756316.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup white sugar
1 quart cranberry juice
2 cups unsweetened pineapple juice
3 tablespoons almond extract
1 (2 liter) bottle ginger ale

Directions:
In a large container, mix together sugar, cranberry juice, pineapple juice and almond extract. Refrigerate for 1 day.
To serve, pour juice mixture into a punch bowl. Stir in ginger ale.

Submitter: 
Aleta
Description: 
Great non-alcoholic punch! Make a day in advance to allow the flavors to meld.
