Title:
Sloppy Does Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. chopped onion
1 1/2 c. chopped celery
6 lb. ground venison
1/3 c. prepared mustard
3 tsp. salt
1 tsp. chili powder
1 1/4 c. catsup
2 sm. cans tomato sauce
1/4 c. Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. pepper
1 c. brown sugar
1/4 c. white vinegar

Directions:
Brown venison, celery and onion.
Add remaining ingredients, simmer 1 hour.
Serve on buns.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
A Sloppy Joe recipe for ground venison.
