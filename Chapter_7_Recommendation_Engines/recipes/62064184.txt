Title:
Cheeseburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 small diced onion
1 can tomato sauce (8 oz.)
1/4 c. ketchup
3/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
12 slices American cheese
9 can biscuits ("butter-me-not")
3 Tbsp. sugar, to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.  Brown beef and onion in skillet.  Drain fat and add salt and pepper.  Stir in ketchup, tomatoe sauce and sugar.  Heat well and pour mixture into 8" x 8" x 2" baking dish.  Place cheese slices evenly over top.  Place uncooked biscuits on top of cheese.  Bake uncovered at 425 degrees for 20 mins. or until biscuits are golden brown.  Let sit about 3-5 mins. before serving.  Great served with au'gratin potatoes and corn.
