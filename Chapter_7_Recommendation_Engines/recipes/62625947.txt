Title:
What the ELLE...Poppy Seed Lemon Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon vinegar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 egg
2 tablespoon apple sauce
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour or 1/2C wwflour + 1/2C flour
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tbl. poppy seed

Directions:
In small bowl...stir the milk, vinegar, and lemon juice together; let stand 10 minutes to curdle. Whisk in egg, apple sauce, and vanilla extract.
In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and poppy seeds until well combined. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture and whisk a few times until the batter is mostly free of lumps.
Heat a large non-stick pan *treated with oil over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of  batter per pancake into the skillet  and cook until bubbles appear on the surface, about 2 minutes. Flip the pancakes over with a spatula and brown the other side, about 2 more minutes.
Final touch...sprinkle with powdered sugar

Description: 
Apple Sauce instead of Butter...Full tsp of Vanilla Extract. The flavors of vanilla, lemon, poppy seed and apple sauce combine to make lighter, fluffier pancake for any time...any place.Modified from: Sunday Morning Lemon Poppy Seed Pancakes
