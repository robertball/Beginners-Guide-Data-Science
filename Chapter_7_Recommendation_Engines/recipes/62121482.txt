Title:
Mike's Mexican Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages refrigerated crescent rolls
1 can Kuners refried beans with chiles
Fat free Ranch dressing
1 pound ground beef
1 packet taco seasoning
6 ounces shredded Mexican cheese
1/4 cup black olives, sliced
cilantro leaves, chopped
1/4 an avocado, cut into approx. 1/2 inch cubes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Roll crescent roll dough flat onto 14x16 inch pan.
Press seams to seal together.
Bake dough until it begins to brown.
While crescent rolls are baking, cook ground beef with taco seasoning. Cook over medium heat until browned, stirring occasionally to mix in seasoning.
Remove dough from oven once it starts to brown.
Spread refried beans on dough in an even layer.
Drizzle ranch dressing over beans.
Spread seasoned ground beef on top of beans and dressing.
Sprinkle cheese on top of beef.
Spread olives, chopped cilantro, and avocado chunks on top of cheese.
Return pan to oven and reduce heat to 350. Bake for 15 minutes.
