Title:
Delicious Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, bone-in chicken breast halves
2 stalks celery, very thinly sliced
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 cups cooked rice (cooked in chicken broth)
6 ounces sour cream
2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
1/8 teaspoon onion powder or 1/2 onion diced
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
2 cups shredded chedder
2 cups crushed buttery round crackers
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted (optional)
Broccoli or other veggies may be added if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
May use cooked breast or canned chicken.
Saute celery & onion in small amount of olive oil until transparent.
In a large bowl, stir together sour cream, soup,  onion powder or onion, garlic powder, celery and salt and pepper to taste. Mix in cooked rice and chicken and 1 cup shredded cheese. Spoon mixture into a 9x13 inch casserole dish. In a resealable bag, shake together crushed crackers and melted butter. Sprinkle crackers and 1 cup grated cheese over the top.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Debra Ferrara
Description: 
Chicken and rice combine with cream of chicken soup and sour cream in this crowd-pleasing casserole topped with a buttery cracker crust.Modified from: Delicious Chicken Casserole
