Title:
Caramel Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of can pumpkin
1 cup milk
1/2 teasp nutmeg
1 carton Cool Whip thawed and divided
1/4 cup caramel ice cream topping (save 3 Tblsp for topping)
2 packs vanilla jello instant pudding
1 graham cracker pie crust
1/2 cup pecan pieces (save a few for topping)
1 teasp cinnamon

Directions:
Mix pumpkin, pudding mixes, spices and milk with whisk until well blended.To this, add 1 1/2 cup Cool whip.Pour 1/4 caramel topping into pie crust and sprinkle with the 1/2 cup pecan pieces.Pour pumpkin mixture over this and put in fridge for an hour. Top with the rest of the cool whip. Just before serving add remaining pecans and remaining caramel topping on top.Enjoy with a great cup of coffee.
