Title:
Killer Tosada Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can refried beans
1 cup mild salsa, your favorite brand and type
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
1 pkg taco seasoning
2 avocados mashed well with 1/2 cup mayo
4 cups shredded cheddar cheese or colby jack
4 diced tomatoes
1 bunch thinly sliced green onions
1 medium can sliced black olives

Directions:
Mash beans with salsa until well mixed; spread on a large platter or in a 13x9 pan.
In a small bowl mix mayo, sour cream, and taco seasoning well; spread over beans.
Spread avocado mixture over sour cream, making sure not to blend it in.
Cover with shredded cheese, sprinkle with onions, olives, and tomatoes.
Cover and chill at least 2 hours.
Serve with corn chips or crackers.
