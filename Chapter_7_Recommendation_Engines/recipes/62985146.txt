Title:
Cranberry Salad III.0 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 approx 16 oz package of fresh cranberries (2 plus cups)
2 large unpeeled oranges, quartered, seeds removed
1 cup finely chopped carrots
1 cup finely chopped celery
1 cup chopped pecans
3/4 cup white sugar
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
2 (3 ounce) packages raspberry flavored gelatin mix
1 cup  water
1 cup cold water

Directions:
In a food processor, combine the cranberries and oranges. Process well.
Place orange, cranberries, sugar and 1 cup water in a 2 quart saucepan and bring to a boil and then simmer for 10-15 minutes.
Add pineapple and gelatin and continue to cook till gelatin is dissolved.
Remove from heat and add rest of the ingredients, i.e. the carrots, celery and pecans and one cup cold water
Pour into a large bowl or mold and refrigerate till gelled.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a modification of Cranberry salad III by  Carri Hyche. It's great as is but I think you need to cook up the cranberries and oranges together and also added celery, Oh, and this serves 10 so use a big containerModified from: Best Cranberry Salad
