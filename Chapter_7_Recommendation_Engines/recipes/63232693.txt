Title:
Banana Split Cake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups crushed graham crackers
1 cup margarine, melted
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
4 large bananas
sliced strawberries to cover bananas
2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
drizzle with chocolate syrup before serving
8 maraschino cherries

Directions:
Combine the crushed graham crackers and melted margarine. Pat into the bottom of one 9x13 inch pan.
Beat the cream cheese and confectioners' sugar together until light and fluffy. Spread over the top of the crust. Spoon crushed pineapple over the cream cheese layer. Then layer sliced bananas, then layer sliced strawberries over the bananas. Cover with the non-dairy whipped topping and sprinkle top with chopped walnuts and maraschino cherries. drizzle with chocolate syrup befoe serving

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
An easy no-bake sweet treat. Perfect for those hot summer months when turning the oven on is unbearable.Modified from: Banana Split Cake II
