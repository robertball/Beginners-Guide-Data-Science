Title:
Chicken stew w/ couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 skinless, bonless chicken thighs
3 T oil
1 onion diced
4 garlic, minced
1 T gingerroot
2 t cumin
2 t tumeric
1 t coriander
1/2 t nutmeg
2 bay leaves
pinch saffron
1/8 t cloves
3 c chicken broth
6 artichoke hearts
2 c carrot, diced
2 c turnip
3 c zucchini
1 c bell pepper
15 oz garbanzo beans
4 plum tomatoes, peeled, cut into wedges
1 c lima beans
3 c cooked couscous

Directions:
Heat oil and add onion and spices. Cook 2 min. Add chicken and broth, simmer 40 min. Add carrots and turnips 15 min. Add artichoke hearts.
Uncover add zucchini and pepper, simmer 4-5 min. Add rest and simmer 4-5 min.
Serve over couscous.
