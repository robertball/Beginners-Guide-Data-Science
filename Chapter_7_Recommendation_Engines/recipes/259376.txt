Title:
Cucumber Blueberry Feta Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4555287.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers - halved, seeded, and cut into 1/8-inch slices
2 cups blueberries
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup chopped scallions
1/4 cup finely chopped flat-leaf parsley
3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
1 teaspoon honey
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine cucumbers, blueberries, feta cheese, scallions, and parsley in a bowl.
Mix oil, vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Pour dressing over salad; toss to combine.

Submitter: 
iliketocake
Description: 
This light refreshing side salad uses fresh herbs with just the right balance of flavors to brighten up any summer party!
