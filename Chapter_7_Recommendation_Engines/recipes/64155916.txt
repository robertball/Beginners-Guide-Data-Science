Title:
Tangy Sauce for Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup creamy blue cheese dressing
2 tsp horseradish
1 tsp lime juice
1/2 tsp honey
1 tsp brown mustard
pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and serve with salmon patties
