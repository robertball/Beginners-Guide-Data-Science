Title:
Sea Bass Veracruz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Roma tomato, seeded, diced
1/2 cup pimiento stuffed olives, chopped
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
1/4 cup jalapeno, seeded, sliced
3 Tablespoons lime juice
2 Tablespoons capers
4 fillets sea bass, 6 oz
salt
pepper
1/4 cup all purpose flour
6 Tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 cups  onion, sliced
1 tablespoon garlic, minced
2 bay leaves
1 teaspoon thyme, dried
1/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Combine tomatoes, olives, cilantro, jalapenos, lime juice and capers.  Season fillets with salt and peppers and dredge one side with flour.  Heat oil in a large oven proof skillet over medium high heat.  Add fish, flour side down and saute 3 minutes.  Stir in broth to deglaze then add half of the tomato mixture.  Add onion, garlic, bay leaves, and thyme to pan; saute 3 minutes.  Stir in broth to deglaze, then add half of the tomato mixture.  Nestle fillets into tomato mixture, roast in oven 5-7 minutes, or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.  Remove and discard bay leaves.  Add remaining tomato mixture, salt, and pepper.  Serve over fillets.
