Title:
Super Baby Food - Mixed meat dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fresh meat, ground beef, ground turkey or a combination
1/2 cup raw grated vegetable
1/2 cup whole grain bread crumbs, wheat germ, or oatmeal or other dry grain pulverized to a powder in the blender, or a combination
1-2 tablespoons of soy milk, cow's milk or tomato sauce

Directions:
Press into a loaf pan and cook throughly in a preheated 350 degree oven or in the microwave until meat is all done Puree for a smaller baby or break into bite sized finger-food pieces. Freeze for up to one month.
Freeze for up to one month
