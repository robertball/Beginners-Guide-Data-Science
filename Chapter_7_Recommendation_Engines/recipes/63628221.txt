Title:
Chocolate graham cracker pie crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups chocolate cookie crumbs (or use 4oz chocolate graham crackers, crumbled)
2 tbsp confectioner's sugar
2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F. In a bowl, combine all ingredients. Press crumb mixture evenly into bottom of a 9" glass pie pan (with a flat bottomed measuring cup or an empty pie pan). Bake 12 mins. Let cool.  Add pie filling.
