Title:
Brisket Mopping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz of ale or lager (nothing too hoppy)
2 Tbsp cider vinegar
1 Tbsp paprika
1 Tbsp Cholula hot sauce
1 tsp cayenne powder (to taste)
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp kosher salt
2 cloves garlic (crushed)
1 medium onion (chopped)
1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Combine the beer, cider vinegar, paprika, hot sauce, cayenne powder, black pepper, salt, garlic and onion in a small bowl and refrigerate overnight to allow the flavors to meld
Just before using add the vegetable oil and mix well. Allow the meat to smoke for an hour before applying the mopping sauce. Mop generously onto the meat every hour until done.

Description: 
Beer based mopping sauce used to keep the brisket moist while smoking
