Title:
Laura Herzig's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
1 cup of sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 cup flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup of sour cream
3 ripe/mashed bananas
1 tsp of vanilla
optional: 1/2 cup chocolate chips and/or walnuts

Directions:
Heat oven 375
~Cream 1 stick of butter with 1 cup of sugar. Add 2 eggs and mix. In sep. bowl, combine 1 1/2 cup flour, 1 tsp. salt, and 1 tsp baking soda. Slowly add dry ingredients to butter mixture. Add 1/2 cup of sour cream, 3 ripe/mashed bananas, and 1 tsp of vanilla and mix. I like to add 1/2 cup chocolate chips and/or walnuts. Pour into greased loaf pan and cover with tin foil. Bake 1 hour (remove foil halfway through cooking).
