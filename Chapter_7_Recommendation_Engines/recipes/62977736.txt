Title:
Navaho Indian Fry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unbleached flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. powdered milk
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 cup water
Vegetable or Peanut oil for frying

Directions:
Sift together all the dry ingredients into a large bowl.  Pour all of the water over the sifted ingredients.  Stir until a sticky mixture forms.
Flour your hands and begin to work all of the flour to form into a ball. Do not knead the dough.
Fry bread in a heavy skillet in 350 degree F. oil. Fry unil bown then fry otherside. 3-4 mins. each side. Drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
maddy03
Description: 
A fried bread that I learned to make while wroking on the Navaho reservation.  Can be used as a main dish bread or as a dessert with honey or jam.
