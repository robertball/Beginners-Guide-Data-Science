Title:
Baked Eggplant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp olive oil
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 onion, chopped
1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 tsp oregano, or 1-1/2 tbsp fresh, chopped
1/4 cup dry white wine
1 cup canned crushed tomatoes
1 can tomato paste or sauce
1 lb eggplant, cut into 2‚Äù pieces
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 clove garlic, crushed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Heat oil in a sauce pan over medium heat.
Saute onion and garlic 3-4 minutes or until onion is tender.
Add tomatoes, wine, oregano, and salt and pepper to taste.
Increase heat to medium high and simmer 15 minutes.  Stir in eggplant and half the mozzarella cheese.
Spoon into a 9 by 13 inch baking pan. Bake 35-40 minutes.
Remove from oven and top with remaining cheeses.  Bake another 3-4 minutes or until cheese is melted.
