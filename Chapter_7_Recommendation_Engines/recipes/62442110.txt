Title:
Black Magic Cake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/591977.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups white sugar
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon Morton's LiteÂ® salt
3 eggs
1 cup strong brewed coffee
1 cup low-fat buttermilk
1/2 cup canola oil
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a bundt pan.
In large bowl combine flour, sugar, cocoa,  baking soda, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center.
Add eggs, coffee, buttermilk, oil and vanilla. Beat for 2 minutes on medium speed. Batter will be thin. Pour into prepared pans.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 to 55 minutes, or until toothpick inserted into center of cake comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and finish cooling on a wire rack. Fill and frost as desired.
