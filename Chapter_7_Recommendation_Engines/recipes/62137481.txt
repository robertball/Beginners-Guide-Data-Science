Title:
Portuguese Coconut Cakes- Bolos de coco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
200 gr shredded coconut
150 gr sugar
1 tablesppon of lemon zest
Place ingredients in a bowl and mix together. Place cupcake liners in 3 muffin tins and distribute batter.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 180.
Place ingredients in a bowl and mix together.
Place cupcake liners in 3 muffin tins and distribute batter evenly.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
