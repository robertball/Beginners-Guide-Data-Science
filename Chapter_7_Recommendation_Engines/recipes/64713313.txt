Ingredients:
1 medium acorn squash, halved and seeded
1 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 tablespoons maple syrup (optional)

Directions:
Divide butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, and maple syrup amongst both halves.
Add one cup of water to Pressure Cooker cooking pot and place Trivet inside.  Carefully place Acorn Squash on top of Trivet.
Lock lid and close pressure valve. According to manufactures instructions set to high pressure for 5 minutes. Allow 10-15 minutes for pressure to build. When the time is up allow for natural pressure relief for 7 minutes. Release remaining pressure (if any) according to manufactures instructions. Unlock lid and carefully remove squash and serve.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
A perfect way to make a sweetened acorn squash to serve up as a side dish to any meal. This Sweet Acorn Squash cooks up in less than 25 minutes using an electric pressure cooker.
