Title:
Thyme Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes, cubed (about 9 cups
3 cups sliced sweet onions (about 1 1/2 large)
3 medium carrots sliced
1/2 pound medium fresh mushrooms
1 large sweet red pepper cut into 1 1/2 in. pieces
1 large sweet yellow pepper cut into 1 1/2 in. pieces
2 Tablespoons butter, melted
2 Tablespoon olive oil
1 Tablespoon minced fresh thyme or 1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  In a large bowl, combine vegetables.  Add remaining ingredients, toss to coat.
2.  Transfer to a 15x10x1-inch baking pan.  Roast 45- 50 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
