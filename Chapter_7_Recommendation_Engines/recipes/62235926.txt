Title:
Spinach and Leek Pinto Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 leeks, bulb only, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 (16 ounce) cans fat-free chicken broth
2 (16 ounce) cans pinto beans, rinsed and drained
2 bay leaves
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1/2 cup quinoa
2 cups packed fresh spinach
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Steam the leeks and garlic; saute until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in the chicken broth, pinto beans, bay leaves and cumin. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, and stir in the quinoa. Cover, and simmer for 5 minutes. Stir in spinach and season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Bonnie May
Description: 
I changed the white beans to pinto and took out the oil to save the calories.  I also decided to change the couscous to quinoa for the added protein.Modified from: Spinach and Leek White Bean Soup
