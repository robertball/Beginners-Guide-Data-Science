Title:
** Coconut Chews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter, softened
1 1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
2 1/3 c. AP flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 lb. sweetened coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees.
Line 2 cookie sheets w/parchment paper or silicone liners.
Cream butter, sugar & vanilla with electric mixer on med. speed until light & fluffy. Beat in eggs, one at a time, & scrape bowl. Add flour & baking soda; mix until combined.
Fold in coconut by hand.
Drop dough by heaping tablespoons about 2 inches apart onto prepared cookie sheets.
Bake 15 min, rotating pans halfway through. ookies are done when they spread out & turn golden around the edges.
Let cool completely before serving.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
From the Denver Post.  Recipe says to bake 15 min. I got good results at 16-17 min., rotating halfway through. Crispy edges & chewy center. Yum!
