Title:
Marinated Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/615789.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef tips
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 ounces butter
1/2 yellow onion, sliced
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablesppons cornstarch
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed beef broth
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
1 cup fresh sliced mushrooms
1/3 cup sour cream
1/3 cup cream cheese
1/3 cup red wine
1 tablespoon garlic (minced or dry)
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Marinate and season beef tips with 1/2 teaspoon of both salt and pepper and red wine.
In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter and brown the beef tips quickly, then remove the beef from th pan and set aside. Add the onions and cook slowly for 3 to 5 minutes, then push to the side.
Stir the flour, cornstarch, and garlic into the juices in the pan. Pour in beef broth and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Lower the heat and stir in mustard. Add the beef tips back to the pan and stir in the sliced mushrooms.  Cover and simmer for 1 hour or until the meat is tender.
Five minutes before serving, stir in the sour cream and cream cheese. Heat briefly then salt and pepper to taste.
Serve over Egg noodles

Description: 
This version of Beef Stroganoff is marinated in a red wine and then simmered on the stove for an hour to create a tasty dinner.  You can serve this over rice or egg noodles.Modified from: Beef Stroganoff III
