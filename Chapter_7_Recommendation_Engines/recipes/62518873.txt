Title:
Possum Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz cream cheese
3/4 cup powdered sugar
1 3.9 oz package instant chocolate pudding mix
1 T instant vanilla pudding mix (optional)
1 3/4 cup milk
1 prepared graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Whip cream cheese with powdered sugar until smooth.
Spread into base of graham cracker crust.
Combine milk and instant chocolate pudding mix (adding a tablespoon or so of instant vanilla pudding mix (if you have) intensifies flavor, but is not necessary) and whisk for 2 minutes.
Pour over cream cheese mixture.
Chill 20 to 30 minutes before serving (will keep covered for up to 3 days)

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
For those spur of the moment Oh my gosh I need a dessert times in your life.  Cream cheese, instant pudding mix, and a graham cracker pie shell make one quick and easy decadent fix.  If you have it, fold a little Cool Whip into the cream cheese mixture for added smoothness.
