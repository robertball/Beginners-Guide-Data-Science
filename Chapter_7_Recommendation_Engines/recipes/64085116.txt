Title:
Jim's Zesty Slow Cooker Chicken Barbecue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 frozen skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (12 ounce) bottle barbecue sauce
1/2 cup Italian salad dressing
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons Hot sauce (jalipeno)

Directions:
Place chicken in a slow cooker. In a bowl, mix the barbecue sauce, Italian salad dressing, brown sugar,hot sauce and Worcestershire sauce. Pour over the chicken.
Cover, and cook 3 to 4 hours on High or 6 to 8 hours on Low.

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
Use your slow cooker to prepare this great twist on basic barbecue chicken. It is easy to prepare and the sauce also makes a great topping for baked potatoes.Modified from: Zesty Slow Cooker Chicken Barbecue
