Title:
Corn Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
dash of salt
2 cans cream corn (I prefer every brand EXCEPT del monte)
6 T. flour
1 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Beat eggs together in a large bowl.  Add sugar and salt.  Add corn.
In small bowl, pour milk and slowly mix in flour.  Add milk and flour mixture to corn.
Top with butter.  (I typically cut 6 pieces of butter off and lay them on top of the mixture) and bake in a covered dish for 1 hour.
