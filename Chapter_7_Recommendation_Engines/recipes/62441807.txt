Title:
Italian Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 Tbsp. melted butter
½ cup grated parmesan cheese
¼ cup dry bread crumbs
1 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. dried parsley
¼ tsp. paprika
¼ tsp. salt
¼ tsp. pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 and grease cookie sheet.
Mix cheese, crumbs, and seasoning. Then dip chicken in butter and coat with crumb mixture.
Place on baking sheet and bake 20-25 minutes until thoroughly cooked.
