Title:
Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2255923.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch fresh asparagus, ends trimmed
Canola oil (or olive oil)
Fresh ground pepper
Kosher salt or sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.  Place parchment paper on baking tray.  Spread out asparagus on tray.  Drizzle with canola oil, pepper, and salt.  Mix around the asparagus to coat well.  Add more oil, salt, and pepper if needed.  Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until asparagus starts to brown.
