Title:
Pat's Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ozs. cups dry, small elbow macaroni, cooked in salted water and drained
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cheddar cheese soup
3/4 cup green pepper, diced
3/4 cup onion, diced
1 T. butter & 1 T. olive oil
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 c. + 2 T. mayonnaise
1 small can diced pimiento
1/2 t. dry mustard (optional)
1/4 t. ground black pepper
1/2 cup milk
1 lb. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 2-3 quart casserole dish with Pam.
Cook green pepper and onion in oil in non-stick frying pan until soft.  Add garlic powder and pepper.
Combine soups, green pepper and onion mixture, milk, mayonnaise, mil, and 3/4 lb of cheese.
Add cooked macaroni...mix well. Pour into prepared casserole dish. Top with remaining cheese.  Sprinkle with sweet paprika.
Bake for 35-45 minutes or until cheese is melted and edges are bubbling and light brown.
