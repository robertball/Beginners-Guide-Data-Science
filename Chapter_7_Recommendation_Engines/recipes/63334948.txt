Title:
Vegan Creamy Southwest Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Veganaise
2 tsp lime juice
2 tsp white apple cider vinegar
2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp ground cumin
dash of cayenne
1-3 tbsp agave nectar depending on sweetness
2-4 tbsp water - add more as needed to create the consistency you enjoy in your salad dressings

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a blender.
Keep refrigerated in an air tight container for up to 5-7 days.

Submitter: 
Jamie Lang
Description: 
I adore Newman's Own Southwest salad dressing from McDonald's. Sadly it's not available at any stores (that I can find).  

Play around to make it taste as you remember it. Enjoy!
