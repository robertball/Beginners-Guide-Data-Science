Title:
Antipasto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. canned tuna in oil
2 oz. anchovies, minced
1 clove garlic, grated or pressed
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup white wine
1 can small pitted black olives, drained
1/2 cup pickled pearl onions, drained
1 cup sweet gherkin pickles, sliced
1 2 oz. can of sliced mushrooms, drained
1/2 cup sliced carrots, cooked
1/2 cup celery, sliced

Directions:
Bring the ketchup, olive oil and wine with garlic and anchovies to a boil.
Add other ingredients to boiling liquid.
Allow to cool and refrigerate.
