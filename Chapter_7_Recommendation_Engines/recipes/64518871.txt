Title:
Coconut cream pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2919051.jpg
Ingredients:
I baked 9-inch shallow pie crust
5 tablespoons cornstarch
1 cup sugar
1/4 tsp salt
13.6 oz coconut milk unsweetened (in can)
1 1/2 cups milk
3 egg yolks
2 tsp salted butter
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup coconut, toasted
1 cup whipping cream

Directions:
Mix cornstarch, sugar, and salt in a 3 quart saucepan. Add milk and half and half; cook over medium heat until smooth and thick, stirring  constantly.
Place egg yolks on a bowl. Pour a small amount of the hot mixture into the egg. Yolks; blend thoroughly; then pour egg yolk mixture back into saucepan. Cook another 2-3 minutes.
Remove from heat and add butter and vanilla. Stir in 1/2 cup coconut. Pour into baked pie crust. Chill 3-4 hours.
When ready to serve, whip and sweeten the cream, then spread over the pie. Top with the remaining 1/2 cup of coconut.
