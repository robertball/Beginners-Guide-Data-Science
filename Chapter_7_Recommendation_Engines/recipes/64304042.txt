Title:
Pumpkin Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground meat (turkey or beef)
1/2 tsp Liquid smoke
Chili powder to taste
1 tsp smoked paprika
2 tsp ground cumin
1 can pumpkin pur√©e
1 bag frozen corn
1 large onion chopped
1 large bell pepper chopped
2 cloves garlic crushed
2 cans chili beans with sauce
Hot sauce (Tobacco chipolte) to taste
Cream cheese (optional)

Directions:
Brown ground meat in a large skillet over medium heat.  Add onions, peppers, chili powder, paprika, and liquid smoke cook 5 minutes.   Turn heat to low and add remaining ingredients.  Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or longer as desired.  Taste and adjust spice accordingly with hot sauce.  Serve with a tablespoon cream cheese mixed in.

Submitter: 
Dustin Jay
Description: 
Best chili I've ever eaten!  Came up with this on a whim when I needed something to thicken the chili and only had a can of pumpkin.  Everyone raves about this dish!  The cream cheese cuts the heat and adds creaminess.
