Title:
Alaska Salmon Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14.75 oz. canned traditions pack Alaska salmon
OR 8 to 10 oz. skinless, boneless salmon (canned or pouched), drained and chunked
1/3 cup low-fat mayonnaise
1 Tablespoon fresh chopped parsley or 1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
3 to 4 English muffins, split

Directions:
Mix salmon with mayonnaise, parsley, lemon juice, seasoned salt, and celery seed.  Stir in 1/2 cup cheese.
Set oven to broil.  Lightly toast cut side of muffins on a baking sheet set 5 inches from heat. Remove from oven.
Spoon 1/4 cup salmon mixture (for 8 servings) or 1/3 cup (for 6 servings) onto each toasted muffin half.  Top with remaining cheese.  Broil 3 to 4 minutes until cheese melts and is lightly browned.
