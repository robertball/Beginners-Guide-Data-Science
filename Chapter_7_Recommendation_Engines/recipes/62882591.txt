Title:
Baked Chicken in a sweet bbq sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, thickly sliced
1/2 cup of bbq sauce
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup red wine
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tbsp mustard powder
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tbsp cornstarch
1 cup of water

Directions:
Whisk well:  BBQ sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, maple syrup, chili, mustard, garlic, onion powder and cumin.
In large Pyrex dish, arrange chicken in a single layer and pour sauce over all.  Turn pieces to make sure all is well covered.  Bake for 40 minutes at 400F.
Remove fully cooked chicken from dish and set aside.  Pour sauce into small pan.  Add one cup of water and bring to boil.  Stir in cornstarch to thicken.  Place chicken back  dish and cover with sauce and blend well.
Enjoy.
Simple.
