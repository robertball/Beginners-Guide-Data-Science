Title:
Sweet & Salty Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793687.jpg
Ingredients:
1-3/4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 Cup butter, softened
1 Cup brown sugar
3 Tablepoons white sugar
1 egg
2 Teaspoons vanilla
1/3 Cup mini semisweet chocolate chips
1 Cup Pretzel M&Ms;

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees
Whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.
Beat in egg and vanilla.
Mix flour mixture a cup at a time until fully incorporated.
Fold in the mini chocolate chips and Pretzel M&Ms.
Form dough into 2" balls and place 3" apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten just slightly.
Bake in preheated oven until edges just begin to turn golden brown, 17-20 minutes. Do not overbake.
