Title:
Snicker's Candy Bar Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup light brown sugar
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup milk
3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
30 each caramel candies, unwrapped
1 tablespoon water
2 cups salted, shelled peanuts
1 cup milk chocolate chips (or you may try semi-sweet)

Directions:
Cover an 8" square pan with foil--grease foil with oleo or butter.
Combine the sugar, cocoa, butter and milk in a microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave on high for 3 minutes or until bubbling.
Stir in the powdered sugar and vanilla extract.
Pour mixture into the foil lined pan.
Place caramels and water in a second microwave safe bowl, and heat on high for 2 minutes or until melted.
Stir in peanuts.
Spread mixture carefully over chocolate layer in pan.
In a small microwavable bowl, melt chocolate chips on high in the microwave for 1 minute.
Carefully spread over caramel nut layer.
Put pan in refrigerator and chill until firm
Cut into squares and eat!

Description: 
Easy to make in the microwave!  Tastes just like a Snicker's candy bar.
