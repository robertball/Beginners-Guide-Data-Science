Title:
Seafood Salsa dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Mayonaise
1/2 Cup Ketchup
1 TBSP Worcheshire Sauce
1 TBSP Mustard
1 Teaspoon evaporated milk

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a bowl and blend. May be used immediatly or placed in fridge.

Submitter: 
BajaValle
Description: 
A tangy and delicious dipping sauce for fried seafood.
