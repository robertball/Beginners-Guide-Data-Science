Title:
Sauteed Baby Squash with Basil and Feta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
4 c baby mix squash (about 18 oz)
2 c sliced leek (about 2)
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp freshly ground pepper
3 tbsp  crumbled reduced-fat feta cheese
2 tbsp finely chopped fresh basil

Directions:
Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
Add oil to pan, swirling to coat; heat 20 seconds.
Add squash and leek to pan; saute 5 minutes or until tender, stirring frequently.
Transfer squash mixture to a serving platter.
Sprinkle with cheese and basil.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Quick stir fry that woeks with a variety of fresh vegetables you have on hand.  If you are planning an oven dinner this recipe can be adapted to a 'roasted' vegetable side dish.
