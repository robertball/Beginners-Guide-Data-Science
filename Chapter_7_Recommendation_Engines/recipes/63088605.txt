Title:
Mama's Italian Wedding Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound extra-lean ground beef
1 pound cooked skinless chicken breast, diced
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup dried bread crumbs
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon dried basil
3 tablespoons minced onion
2 1/2 quarts chicken broth
2 cups spinach - packed, rinsed and thinly sliced
6 oz angel hair pasta, broken
3/4 cup diced carrots

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine the beef, egg, bread crumbs, cheese, basil and onion. Shape mixture into 3/4-inch balls and put on cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until just cooked through.
In a large stockpot heat chicken broth to boiling; stir in the spinach, pasta, carrot, chicken and meatballs. Return to boil; reduce heat to medium. Cook, stirring frequently, at a slow boil for 10 minutes or until pasta is al dente. Serve hot with Parmesan cheese sprinkled on top.

Submitter: 
WILKBROWN
Description: 
Tiny meatballs made of ground beef, beaten egg, bread crumbs, and parmesan are combined with spinach, carrot, and pasta for this delicious Italian soup.  No wedding required!Modified from: Mama's Italian Wedding Soup
