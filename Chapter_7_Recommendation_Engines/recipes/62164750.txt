Title:
Mexican Rice with Sour Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, diced
2 small tomatoes, diced
1 TB olive oil
1 1/2 cups dry brown rice
3 1/2 cups water
1 packet taco seasoning
1 cup salsa
1 container Cabot Salsa Grande sour cream dip
2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Saute onions in olive oil for two minutes.  Add tomatoes and cook until onions are tender.
Add rice, water, taco seasoning, and salsa to a pot and bring to boil.  Cook until rice is tender; times vary based on the brand of rice.
Add the onions and tomato, along with Cabot Salsa Grande sour cream dip and two cans black beans, to the rice.  Mix together.
Serve topped with shredded Monterey Jack cheese.

Submitter: 
Brooke Elizabeth
Description: 
A Mexican restaurant in my area serves a great sour cream and rice dish.  After eating enough, I decided to try to replicate it, and I ended up loving the result more than the original!
