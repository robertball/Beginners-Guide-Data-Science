Title:
Cornflake Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/497609.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. crushed cornflakes cereal
2 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp. dried parsley flakes
1 tsp of thyme
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
2 chicken breast halves, skinned and boned
1/4 c. milk
1 egg

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients in a shallow dish.
Dip each chicken breast in milk, then egg
Roll in crumb mixture.
Place chicken in a lightly greased 12 x 8 x 2 inch baking dish.
Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes. Yield: 2 servings.

Submitter: 
Michelle Lecompte
Description: 
I got this recipe from cooks.com. Its was so tasty and juicy. I added Diane's original BBQ sauce after wards for some sweetness but it is not needed.
