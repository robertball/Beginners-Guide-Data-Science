Title:
Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free "Norwegian Pancakes" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/361204.jpg
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1/4 cup sugar
1-1/2 cups brown or sweet rice flour
1/3 cup tapioca starch
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 cups almond milk, soy milk, rice milk or apple juice
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, or vegetable oil
Butter, oil or vegetable spray for cooking
2 tablespoons confectioner’s sugar, for garnish

Directions:
Beat eggs with sugar until foamy.
Add rice flour, tapioca starch/flour, and baking powder.
Beat until smooth.
Add milk and butter (or oil).
In medium skillet, heat butter or oil or coat with vegetable spray.
Ladle ¼ cup batter into pan and spread thin,
Cook until top is set, about 1 to 3 minutes.
Flip with spatula and cook another 1 to 3 minutes.
Turn pancakes onto a warm serving plate.
Repeat with remaining batter until all is used.
Lightly dust pancakes with powdered sugar, if desired.
Serve warm.
To eat:
Spread jam or confectioners' sugar and lemon-juice length-wise and roll tightly.
Enjoy!
