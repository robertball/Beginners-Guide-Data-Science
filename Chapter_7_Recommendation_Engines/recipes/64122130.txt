Title:
Chocolate Sponge Cake Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1307277.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. All purpose flour
1 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
4 Eggs
1 C. Granulated sugar
1 C. Chocolate milk
1 C. Coconut, fine shred
1 C. Quick oats
Chocolate chips for sprinkling on top of muffins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan. Set aside
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl. Set aside
In a large bowl beat eggs until light and frothy
Add sugar, chocolate milk, coconut and quick oats, beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until thoroughly combined
Slowly add dry ingredients to mixture and blend all ingredients together until no lumps remain in the batter
Pour batter into muffin cups filling 2/3 full and sprinkle a few chocolate chips on top of each muffin before baking in the oven.
Bake for 20 minutes in preheated oven until lightly browned
Remove from oven and allow to cool before putting out on serving plate

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A chocolate muffin with sponge cake texture that goes well for brunch or dessert with a nice chocolatey flavour.
