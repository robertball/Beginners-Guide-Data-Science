Title:
Cheesy Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs Yukon Gold or new red potatoes
8 oz sour cream
8 oz cheddar cheese
1/2 c. milk or heavy cream
4 T butter
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Wash and chop potatoes and place in a large pot of boiling water.  Boil until fork tender
Drain off water, and return to pan.  Over low heat, add in butter until melted.  Stir in milk while mashing to a rough, lumpy texture.  Add salt and pepper.  A hand mixer can be used as well.
Fold in grated cheddar and sour cream.  Serve immediately.
