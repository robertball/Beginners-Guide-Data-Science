Title:
Red Velvet Cupcakes with Marshmallow creme frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/702253.jpg
Ingredients:
Red Velvet Cupcakes:
2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
1/4 cup good cocoa powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups graduated sugar
2 eggs
1 bottle red food coloring
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 tablespoon white vinegar
Frosting:
1 jar (7oz) marshmallow creme/fluff
1 cup butter, softened
5 cups powdered sugar, or more if necessary

Directions:
For Cupcakes:
Heat oven 350 F.
Mix flour, cocoa, and salt in small bowl, set aside.
Beat butter and sugar in large bowl until smooth. Add eggs to butter mixture; one at a time and stir.
Next add food coloring and vanilla and mix on low speed until combined.
Then add the flour mixture alternately with the buttermilk. Blend well.
Lastly, beat in baking soda and the vinegar. Batter should be thick and smooth.
Line muffin pan with baking cups. Divide batter evenly into muffin cups.
Bake for 20-22 minutes. Cool completely for about 15 minutes.
For Frosting:
Warm jar of marshmallow creme for 15 seconds in microwave, without lid.
Beat marshmallow and butter together in bowl until smooth. Then add one cup at a time of the powered sugar, mix this on low speed or it will get everywhere! Add more powdered sugar, depending on how thick you want it. Spread desired amount on baked, cooled cupcakes.
