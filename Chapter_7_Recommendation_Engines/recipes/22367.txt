Title:
Hershey's ® Chocolate Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983509.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon unflavored gelatin
1 tablespoon cold water
2 tablespoons boiling water
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup HERSHEY®'S Cocoa Powder
1 cup heavy cream, chilled
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In small cup, sprinkle gelatin over cold water; let stand 1 minute to soften.  Add boiling water; stir until gelatin is completely dissolved and mixture is clear.  Cool slightly.
Stir together sugar and cocoa in medium bowl; add whipping cream and vanilla.  Beat on medium speed of electric mixer, scraping bottom of bowl occasionally, until mixture is stiff.  Pour in gelatin mixture; beat until well blended.  Spoon into serving dishes.
Refrigerate about 30 minutes before serving.  Garnish as desired.  Cover; refrigerate leftover desserts.

Submitter: 
HersheysKitchens.com
Description: 
Sweetened heavy cream is flavored with cocoa, whipped with gelatin and chilled for a cool chocolate mousse.
