Title:
Asparagus Bundles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/293472.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound of fresh asparagus, trimmed
2 thin slices of proscuitto, cut into 1" wide strips
1 tablespoon of olive oil
2 tablespoons of chopped roasted red peppers
1/4 teaspoon of kosher salt
1/8 teaspoon of cracked black pepper

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 450Â°F. Line a 10 1/2x15 1/2x1-inch baking pan with aluminum foil and set aside.
Wrap asparagus spears in bundles of 3 or 4 with a strip of proscuitto around middle. Place bundles in a single layer in foil-lined pan. Drizzle olive oil over asparagus bundles. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Top with roasted red peppers.
Bake 13 to 14 minutes.
Variation:  They are also yummy sprinkled with some crumbled goat cheese halfway through cooking.

Submitter:

Description: 
Bundles of asparagus spears wrapped in proscuitto make a tasty and pretty presentation.
