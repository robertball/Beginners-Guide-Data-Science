Title:
Jumpstart Chocolate Workout Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2317011.jpg
Ingredients:
1.  12 icecubes
2.  1 whole banana
3.  1/3 cup chocolate whey protein
4.  1 tablespoon of honey or agave
5.  1 and 1/4 cups almond milk, sweetened or unsweetened
5.  1 heaping tablespoon of unsalted organic peanut butter
5.  1/2 cup of refridgerated strongly brewed coffee

Directions:
1.  Combine all ingredients in a blender and use the ice crushing mode to break up the ice.  2.  Work up in speed to highest setting then blend for 20-30 seconds.  3.  Get to the gym.

Submitter: 
Jake Atherly
Description: 
This smoothie is a great meal replacement, pre workout or even just a bomb snack.
