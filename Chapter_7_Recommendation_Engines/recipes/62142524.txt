Title:
Thanksgiving Day Frosted Pumpkin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ C butter, softened, no substitutes
1 C brown sugar
1 C granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 t vanilla
2 ½ C flour
¾ t baking soda
½ t salt
¾ t ginger
1 t cinnamon
2 t baking powder
1 ½ C solid-pack pumpkin
Frosting:
2 ½ T butter, softened
1 ½ C powdered sugar
¾ t vanilla
1 ½ T milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350º.
Cream together butter and sugars; beat in eggs and vanilla.
Combine dry ingredients and add alternately with pumpkin to creamed mixture.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto well greased cookie sheets.  Bake for 15 minutes.
Cream together frosting ingredients until smooth.  Frost cookies.

Submitter: 
FoodFan
Description: 
This recipe has been in my family longer than I have and it's been a yearly tradition to make these every Thanksgiving. They are everyone's favorite!
