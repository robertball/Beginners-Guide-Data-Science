Title:
Smoked spiced eggplant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large eggplant
3 T. oil
1-inch piece ginger, finely chopped
1 large red onion, finely chopped
1 tsp. salt
2 garlic cloves, ground to a paste
1 T. shredded coconut
1 T. ground coriander
1/2 tsp. cumin
1/2 tsp. garam masala
1/4 tsp. cayenne
2 tomatoes, chopped
1/2 fresh hot green chile, chopped
2 T. chopped fresh cilantro
juice of 1/2 lemon

Directions:
Roast the eggplant on a cookie sheet in 500 degree oven till the skin is blackened, about 20 minutes, or on grill.  When cool, peel and mash to a puree with a potato masher.
Meanwhile, heat oil and add ginger.  Cook, stirring, 30 seconds.  Add onion and salt and cook, stirring often, until the onion begins to brown, about 10 minutes.
Add the garlic, cook about 30 seconds.  Add coconut and cook for 1 minute, stirring.  Add coriander, cumin, garam, cayenne, and cook, stirring about 1 minute. Add a T. of water and cook, stirring, until the onion begins to stick, about 1 minute more.
Add the tomatoes and stir.  Add the mashed eggplant and cook, stirring often, 5 minutes.
Stir in the fresh chile and 1 T. of the the cilantro.  Add lemon juice.
Spoon into a serving bowl, sprinkle with remaining cilantro.
