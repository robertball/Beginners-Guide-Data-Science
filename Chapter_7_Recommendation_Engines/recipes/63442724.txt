Title:
Quiche with Kale, Tomato, and Leek Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/934247.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 cup milk
4-6 oz shredded cheese (Italian mix preferable)
1 small leek, sliced (white and light green parts only)
1 cup chopped kale
4 oz halved heirloom cherry tomatoes
1 sprig fresh rosemary (leaves finely chopped or ground with a mortar and pestle, stem discarded)
1 pinch sea salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 tbs grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
2. Steam the kale for about 1-5 minutes in a steamer. Cook until it has turned bright green, and then remove from heat.
3. Place the sliced leek, kale, and heirloom tomatoes into the bottom of an 8 inch pie dish.
4. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs, then stir in the milk and shredded cheese. Stir in the rosemary, pinch of sea salt, and pepper.
5. Pour the milk and cheese mixture over the vegetables into the pie dish. Stir together gently until evenly combined.
6. Place quiche into the preheated oven and bake for 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and sprinkle parmesan cheese over the top of the quiche. Place back in the oven for an additional 20 minutes. (Approximately).
7. Quiche is ready when top appears lightly browned. Remove from oven and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Katherine
Description: 
A simple and healthy breakfast! I don't use pie crust, although I'm sure it would be delicious with crust also. I make this on Sunday night, and I'm a little less grumpy getting out of bed on Monday morning knowing I can microwave this for breakfast!
