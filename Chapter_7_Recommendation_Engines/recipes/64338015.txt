Title:
Sparkling Cranberry Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2073752.jpg
Ingredients:
1 14 can 0z Whole berry Cranberry Sauce
1/3 c. Club Soda
1/4 c. Sugar
1 T Cinnamon

Directions:
Whisk together all ingredients thoroughly. Serve with ID Sparks Speedy 30 - Second Spinach Salad.
Store in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
