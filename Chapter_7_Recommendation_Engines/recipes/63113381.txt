Title:
Goan Fish Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
300g firm white fish such as Haddock, Pollack, Tilapia, Rosada or Cod - cut into chunks
2 cloves Garlic - crushed
1 Onion - thinly sliced
Small piece (to taste) fresh Ginger - finely grated
2tsp ground Coriander
½tsp ground Cumin
1 dried Red Chilli - crushed
1-2 fresh Green Chillies - finely sliced
½ tsp Turmeric
1 x 400ml tin Coconut Milk
1tsp Tamarind Paste

Directions:
Mix the coriander, cumin and crushed red chilli together;
Heat the oil in a frying pan and gently cook the onion, stirring occasionally, until soft and golden;
Add the ginger, garlic, turmeric and coriander mixture and cook, stirring continuously to combine well, for 1 minute;
Add the coconut, tamarind paste and green chillies and simmer, stirring occasionally, for some 5 minutes or so;
Add the fish and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the fish is just cooked through.

Submitter:

Description: 
A simple, mild spicy delight from the shores of Goa in India.
