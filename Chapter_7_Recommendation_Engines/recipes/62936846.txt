Title:
Snapper Veracruz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves crushed garlic
1 roasted red bell pepper, sliced
1/4 cup green olives, chopped
2 tablespoons minced capers
1/2 teaspoon dried mexican oregano
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 (10.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes and chilies, mexican style with lime and cilantro
1 jalapeno, seeded and chopped  (optional)
1/2 cup dry white wine
salt and pepper to taste
4 (6 ounce) fillets red snapper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
In a large skillet, heat olive oil.  Add onion, garlic, roasted red pepper, capers and jalapeno. Saute over medium heat until onion is soft.  Add the tomatoes and chilies with juice and add the wine.  Stir in oregano.  Reduce heat to low and simmer.
Sprinkle snapper fillets with salt and pepper and place in a baking dish sprayed with cooking spray.  Pour sauce over filets and bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
