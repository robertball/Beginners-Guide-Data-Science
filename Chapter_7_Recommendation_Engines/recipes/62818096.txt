Title:
Rhubarb Wild Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup cooked wild rice
1 Cup cooked long grain white rice
2 Tbs olive oil
1 Cup sweet onion, chopped
2 Cloves garlic, minced
2 Cups rhubarb, chopped
1/2 Cup white wine or apple juice
1/2 Cup golden raisins
1 Tsp cinnamon
1/4 Tsp cayenne
2 Tbs honey
1 Tbs low sodium soy sauce
1/4 Cup toasted almonds

Directions:
Cook the rices according to package directions and set aside.
In a large skillet heat the olive oil.
Saute the onion until just translucent, do not brown.
Add the garlic and stir to release the aroma.
Add the rhubarb and continue to saute for about 2 minutes.
Stir in the white wine (or juice) raisins, cinnamon and cayenne.
Cover the pan, reduce heat to medium low and cook until rhubarb is tender crisp.
Stir in the honey and soy sauce.
Mix in the rices and cook until heated through.
Add the almonds as a garnish.

Submitter:

Description: 
A new way to use rhubarb, it plays off the nutty flavor of wild rice.  This makes an attractive, unexpected side for fish or poultry.  It has become a requested holiday dish in our family.
