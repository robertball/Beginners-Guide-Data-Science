Title:
Red Velvet Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T ground flax seed
3 T warm water
1 cup GF multi-blend flour
1 cup blanched almond flour
1/2 cups cane sugar
1/3 cup cocoa powder
1½  teaspoons baking powder
½ teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon instant coffee granules (opt)
½ cup milk (unsweetened almond, hemp or other non-dairy alternative)
1 cups beet/applesauce puree*
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon cider vinegar
½ cup chocolate chips (or chopped dark chocolate)

Directions:
Set the oven to 375 degrees. Prepare the muffin pan with liners.
Make a faux egg (flax gel) by whisking together 1 T flax seeds and 3 T warm water
In a large mixing bowl whisk together dry ingredients
In a 2-cup measuring cup whisk together the rest of the ingredients, except chips
Pour about half the wet mixture into the mixed dry ingredients and give it a few turns with a spoon or silicone spatula. Add the rest of the liquid and stir until combined.
Add to the batter and mix in chips
Spoon into the paper liners filling them about ¾ full. Bake for 20-22 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool and enjoy.
