Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
2 carrots, diced
1 can evaporated milk
2 cups chicken broth
1 can corn, drained

Directions:
Add broth, milk, carrots and potatoes to a slow-cooker and cook for 8 hours on low heat.
With 30 minutes left in cooking time, add corn.
Use a potato masher to gently mash some potatoes and thicken the soup (to preference).
Serve.
