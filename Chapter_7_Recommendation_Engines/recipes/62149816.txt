Title:
Kielbasa Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14oz package beef kielbasa (cut into pieces)
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
1/2 onion (diced)
1 medium potatoes (diced)
2 carrots (diced)
1 15 oz can whole kernel corn (drained)
1 14 oz can Cut green beans (drained)
1-2 tbsp. soy sauce

Directions:
In a large non-stick skillet or wok stir fry onion and kielbasa until heated through. Remove from pan and keep warm. In the same pan stir fry carrots and potatoes until tender. Return onion and kielbasa in pan.
Add corn, green beans and soy sauce. Heat through and serve.

Submitter: 
ait0shi
Description: 
This is a whole new way of making kielbasa! Its tasty and got lots of veggies. Even my picky 2 yr old had seconds. And as with any stir fry this is best served with rice.  Enjoy!
