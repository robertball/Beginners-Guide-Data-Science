Title:
Mamaw's BBQ Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1011366.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup paprika
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 teaspoon meat tenderizer
1/2 teaspoon onion salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
Mix paprika, chili powder, mustard, meat tenderizer, onion salt, garlic salt, and basil in a bowl.
