Title:
Anna's Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2# pork tenderloin (not seasoned)
1 C soy sauce
4 cloves, or 1 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp ginger
1/2 C sesame oil
1/4 C brown sugar
1 C honey

Directions:
Rinse tenderloin and pat dry.  Mix together soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil.  Marinade tenderloin for 2 hours or more.
Remove tenderloin, pat dry, place in baking pan and poke several holes with a fork.
Mix together br. sugar and honey.  Pour half over tenderloin, turn over and pour other half.  Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
Bake @375 for 30 minutes.  Meat thermometer should read 160 degrees.
