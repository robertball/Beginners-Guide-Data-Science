Title:
Cinnamon 'N Spice Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup plus 1 TBL sugar, divided
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 to 1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
2 to 2 1/2 lb thinly sliced (about 7 cups) tart apples, peeled and cored
2 refrigerated ready-to-bake pie crusts for 9" pie

Directions:
In large bowl, combine 1/2 cup sugar, flour, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg. Add apples; toss to coat apples. Place one crust in 9" pie pan. Layer apples in pan. Moisten outside edges of crust with water. Top with second crust, fold edges under edges of lower curst; flatten with fork or pinch together tightly to seal. Cut air vents in top. Lightly brush top with water and sprinkle with 1 TBL sugar. Bake at 450 degrees for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 375 degrees; bake 30 to 40 minutes longer or until golden and bubbly. If top begins to get too brown, loosely cover with foil.
Note: Tears in the dough can be repaired by moistening edges with water and pressing tegether to seal.
