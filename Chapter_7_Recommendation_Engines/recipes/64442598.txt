Title:
Fazzoli's Chicken Broccoli Penne Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Penne Pasta Cooked
4 oz Alfredo Sauce
3 oz Steamed Broccoli
1 oz Diced Tomato
2.5 oz Grilled Chicken Breast Peppered
3 oz Mozzarella Cheese
Pinch of Shredded Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Take the cooked penne pasta, place in individual oven baking dish with the penne pasta hot. You can heat this by placing it in boiling water for 15 seconds.
Pour alfredo sauce evenly over the hot pasta
Place steamed broccoli evenly on top of the alfredo sauce
Place the peppery chicken evenly on top of the broccoli
Sprinkle diced tomatoes evenly on top of the chicken
Cover the entire dish with mozzarella cheese
Sprinkle shredded parmesan cheese over the entire entree
Bake at 400 degrees until cheese is completely melted and light brown.
