Title:
Sweet & Spicy Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup rice or white wine vinegar
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 2 tsp salt
- 1-2 tblsp chili garlic sauce (Lee Kum Kee recommended)

Directions:
Heat vinegar and sugar in a small pot for several minutes (until sugar dissolves).
Add the salt, turn the heat down and simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened slightly.
Stir in chili garlic sauce and let cool.

Submitter: 
Sean Gough
Description: 
Simple  sauce that combines sweet, sour, salty and spicy flavours into a wonderful dipping sauce for chips, grilled chicken and spring rolls.  It can be kept in the fridge for about a week.
