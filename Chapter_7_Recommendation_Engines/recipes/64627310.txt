Title:
Ripen Avocados quickly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
avocados
foil

Directions:
preheat oven to 200 degrees
wrap the avocados in foil ; put on a baking sheet
put in the oven and let them warm for 10 minutes
take avocados off baking sheet and put them into the refrigerator.  let them sit until completely cool.
