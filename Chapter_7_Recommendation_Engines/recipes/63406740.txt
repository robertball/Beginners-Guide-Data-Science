Title:
Lemon Garlic Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. olive oil
2 cloves of garlic - minced
12 oz. fresh baby spinach
1 tsp. lemon zest
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Heat oil in large non-stick skillet.
Add garlic to pan and cook 1 minute.
Add half of spinach and cook for 1 minute.
Add remaining spinach and cook about 4 minutes or until wilted, stirring frequently.
Sprinkle spinach with salt and zest.
Stir in lemon juice and serve.
