Title:
ONE DISH:Southwestern Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground turkey
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, chopped
2 teaspoons taco seasoning powder
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1 (8.75 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 cup light sour cream
1 cup fresh salsa
1 (10 oz) can of rotel
3 cups crushed plain tortilla chips
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 large fresh tomato, finely chopped
3 green onions, chopped (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
In an oven-proof medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground turkey with the garlic and onion; drain. Reduce heat to simmer.
Add to the meat mixture, taco powder, garbanzo beans, corn, rotel and salsa. Stir until well blended. Fold in sour cream.
Arrange all of the crushed tortilla chips on top of the meat mixture and and top with Cheddar cheese.  Top with diced tomato and green onions.
Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
