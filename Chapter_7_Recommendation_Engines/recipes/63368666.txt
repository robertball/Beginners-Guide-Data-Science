Title:
Chinese Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked white rice, chilled
3 Tbsp sesame oil
1 cup frozen peas and carrots, thawed
1 small onion, diced
2 tsp minced garlic
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 cup soy sauce

Directions:
Heat oil over medium high heat in large skillet. Add peas, carrots, onion and garlic; saute until tender. Lower heat to medium low. Push mixture off to one side; pour eggs in empty side and scramble. Add rice and soy sauce; blend together well. Heat through and serve
