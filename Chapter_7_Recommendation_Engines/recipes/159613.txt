Title:
Sour Cream-Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979308.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon CALUMET Baking Powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped PLANTERS Pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Beat butter and sugars in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy. Add sour cream, egg and vanilla; beat until well blended. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; mix well. Stir in chocolate chips and pecans.
Drop tablespoonfuls of dough, 2 inches apart, onto greased baking sheets.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. Cool completely on wire racks.

Submitter: 
Breakstone's and Knudsen Family of Products
Description: 
The addition of sour cream makes these chocolate chip cookies light and fluffy.
