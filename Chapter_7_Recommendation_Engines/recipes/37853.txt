Title:
Italian Pork Chops Mozzarella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982877.jpg
Ingredients:
8 boneless pork chops, 1/2 inch thick
1 packet SHAKE 'N BAKE Original Pork Seasoned Coating Mix
1 1/2 cups spaghetti sauce
1 cup KRAFT Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Coat chops with coating mix as directed on package. Discard any remaining coating mix.  Place chops in 15x10x1-inch baking pan.
Bake at 425 degrees F for 15 minutes or until cooked through. Top chops with spaghetti sauce and cheese. Bake an additional 10 minutes or until sauce is warm and cheese is melted.
