Title:
Bleu Cheese Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. butter or margarine
2 oz. blue cheese
1 can refrigerator biscuits

Directions:
Melt butter and cheese in microwave, stirring once or twice, about 1.5
minutes. Cut each biscuit into 4 pieces and arrange in greased 8 x 8 "
pan. Pour cheese mixture over top and bake in 450 degree oven for 10
minutes or until puffed and brown.

Submitter: 
kelcampbell
Description: 
These tasty little bites are a very quicky and tasty appetizer.  The bleu cheese, once heated has a very mild flavour
