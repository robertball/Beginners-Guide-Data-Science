Title:
Kale & Mushroom Side Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1085266.jpg
Ingredients:
Fresh Milled Pepper (to taste)
Fresh Milled Salt or Kosher Salt (to taste)
Fresh Ground Nutmeg (to taste, aprox 1/2 - 1 tsp)
1 tsp Oil (Grape Seed or Macadamia Nut)
1 Bunch Kale (removed from stem and torn to bite size pieces)
1 medium Onion (yellow or red, diced)
1 tbsp Wine Vinegar (red, white, or cherry)
3 cloves Garlic (diced)
1 tbsp Butter
½ cup Shiitake Mushrooms (sliced)

Directions:
In wok sauté on medium - high in oil the mushrooms, onions, and garlic until the mushrooms are cooked (aprox. 5 min)
Add butter, salt/pepper to taste and Wine Vinegar; stir until butter melted (aprox. 1 min)
Turn heat off, add kale and stir until kale is bright green (aprox. 5 min)
Grate moderate amount of Nutmeg (aprox 1/2 - 1 tsp) over finished dish and stir
Serve Hot

Submitter: 
alluvcooking
Description: 
I make this at least twice a week for my family. It is a super easy and yummy way for us to get a good does of our dark leafy greens.
