Title:
Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz. package frozen spinach- defrosted and water squeezed out
1 cup sour cream
1 cup mayo
1 packet Knorr Leek soup mix
6 oz. marinated artichoke hearts, drained
Dash of garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
1 cup shredded parmesean cheese
1 cup shredded mozzerella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray.
Place spinach and artichokes in a medium bowl and snip with scissors to make small pieces.
Add the rest of the ingredients except the mozzerella cheese; mix well.
Spread dip in baking dish and top with mozzerella.
Bake 25 minutes.
