Title:
Amazing Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 (3.5 ounce) package vanilla pudding mix
1 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 3/4 cup quick oats
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Lightly grease a 9 x 13 inch baking pan
Cream together butter, brown sugar and white sugar.  Beat in eggs.  Stir in pudding mix and then vanilla extract.Combine flour, baking soda and salt.  Then stir in the oats and chocolate chips.
Press dough into prepared pan.  Bake for 25 minutes.  Cool and cut into squares.
