Title:
Tyler's Favorite Eel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup mirin (Japanese sweet wine)
1/4 teaspoon crushed bonito flakes

Directions:
Heat soy sauce, sugar, and mirin into a small saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until liquid is reduced to about 3/4 cup.
Pour into serving dish and stir in bonito flakes.
If sauce gets too thick when it cools, just thin with a touch of water.

Submitter: 
Cari
Description: 
A sweet and salty sauce that goes great with grilled fish or chicken.Modified from: Eel Sauce
