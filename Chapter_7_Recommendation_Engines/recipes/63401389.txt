Title:
Peanut Butter Frosted Brownie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BROWNIE BITES
1-box (1 lb 2.3 oz) brownie mix (any brand)
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 tablespoons water
2 eggs
PEANUT BUTTER FROSTING
2 cups powdered sugar
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup butter or margarine-softened
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
FOR BROWNIE BITES:
Spray (2) mini muffin tins with vegetable spray (you will only need 36 of them sprayed)
Fill each muffin cup about 3/4 of the way full
Bake for approximately 15-18 minutes, till toothpick inserted in center comes out clean
Allow to cool in pans for for about 30 minutes
Run a knife around the edges to remove
Cool completely before frosting
PEANUT BUTTER FROSTING
Combine all ingredients
Mix with electric mixer till smooth
Can be put on with knife or piped on

Description: 
Brownie Bites made in Mini Muffin Tins...frosted with smooth creamy Peanut Butter Frosting......a perfect combo!
