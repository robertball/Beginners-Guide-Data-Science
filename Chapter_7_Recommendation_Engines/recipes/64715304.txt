Ingredients:
3 pounds sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 2" chunks
2 cloves garlic, peeled
2 ounces honey-flavored goat cheese
1 tablespoon butter
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Place 1 cup of water into a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot(R)). Place potatoes and whole garlic cloves into a steamer basket inside the pot, lock the lid and turn venting knob to sealing. Select High pressure, and set timer for 10 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build. Release pressure manually when timer is up.
In pressure cooker pot or medium bowl, mix together potatoes, goat cheese, butter, salt and pepper using a hand mixer or potato masher. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
France C
Description: 
These smooth and creamy mashed sweet potatoes, mixed with honey goat cheese, make the perfect side dish any time of year, especially during the holidays!  Excellent with pork tenderloin or roasted turkey.
