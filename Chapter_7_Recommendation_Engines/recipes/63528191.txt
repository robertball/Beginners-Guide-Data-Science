Title:
Lean and Green Chicken and Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups broccoli florets, cooked (6 Greens)
8 to 9 oz raw chicken breasts - yield 6 oz cooked (1 Lean)
1/2 tsp McCormick Perfect Pinch Rotisserie Chicken Seasoning or seasoning of choice (1 Condiment)
1 packet cream of chicken or cream of broccoli soup (1 Meal)
3/4 cup water
1/4 tsp Mrs. Dash Garlic and herb Seasoning (1 Condiment)
1 cup or 4 oz 2% reduced fat Sargento Italian Blend Cheese 3-6 grams fat/oz (1 Lean)
1 bag Parmesan Cheese Puffs (1 Meal)
10 sprays I Can't Believe It's not Butter Spray (1 Healthy Fat)

Directions:
Season chicken with rotisserie chicken seasoning and rub it into the meat (1/4 tsp on each side) or your seasoning of choice. Grill, bake or broil chicken until cooked. Cube into 1 inch chunks and set aside.
Whisk cream soup of choice, water, and garlic and herb seasoning until blended in a medium bowl. Add broccoli, chicken and 1/2 cup cheese until stir until blended. Spread into a casserole dish. You don't have to spray with cooking spray. Spread remaining cheese over the top. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
Crush Parmesan cheese puffs until it resembles bread crumbs. Spread the crumbs over the top. Bake an additional 10 minute.
