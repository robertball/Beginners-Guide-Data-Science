Title:
THE ORIGINAL CHEX MIX by General Mills Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c Corn Chex
3 c Rice Chex
3 c Wheat Chex
1 c mixed nuts
1 c bite-size pretzels
1 c bite-size bagel chips, garlic flavored
6 tbsp butter
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tsp seasoned salt
3/4 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder

Directions:
In large microwavable bowl, mix ceareals, nuts, pretzels, and bagel chips; set aside.
In small microwavable bowl, microwave butter uncovered on high about 40 seconds or until melted.
Stir in seasonings.
Pour over cereal mixture; stir uNtil evenly coated.
Microwave uncovered on high 5-6 minutes.
Spread on paper towels to cool.
Store in airtighT container.
