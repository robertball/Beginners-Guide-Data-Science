Title:
Late Summer Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ears corn, husks and silks removed
2 tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
coarse salt & ground pepper
2 cans (14.5 oz each) chicken broth
1 large zucchini & 1 large yellow squash, halved length-wise & thinly sliced
8 oz green beams (stem ends removed), cut into thirds
1 can (14.5oz) diced tomatoes in juice
1/2 cup orzo

Directions:
Cut off tip of each ear of corn and use knife to release kernels. Set kernels aside.
In a Dutch oven or 5 quart pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion; season with salt & pepper.  Cook, stirring frequently, until onion is translucent, 3 to 5 mins.
Add broth and 2 cups water; bring to a boil.  Add zucchini & squash, green beans, corn, tomatoes (with juice) & orzo.  Cook uncovered 8 to 11 minutes.  Season with & pepper
TO FREEZE: Ladle 1.5 cups into each of 6 ziploc bags. Store flat in freezer, up to 3 months. Thaw in refrigerator before reheating.

Submitter: 
Cole Furfaro-Strode
Description: 
This veggie-packed soup is a great late summer dish. It's easy to make and you can add veggies from your garden.  A crowd pleaser.
