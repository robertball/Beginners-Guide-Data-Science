Title:
Shortbread Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871638.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups butter, softened
1 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
3-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup cornstarch

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream butter and confectioners' sugar until light and fluffy. Combine flour and cornstarch; gradually add to creamed mixture.
Pat into an ungreased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan. Pierce several times with a fork. Bake at 325Â° for 40-45 minutes or until lightly browned. Cut while warm.
