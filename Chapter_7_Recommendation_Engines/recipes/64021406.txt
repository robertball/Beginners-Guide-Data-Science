Title:
Easy Swedish Meatball Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup beef stock
1 cup fat-free Half and Half
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
Ground black pepper to taste
1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
1/2 (20 ounce) package frozen cooked meatballs, thawed

Directions:
Thaw meatballs in microwave.
Whisk together the beef stock, Half and Half, flour, worcestershire sauce, black pepper, and rosemary in a large saucepan until smooth. Cook and stir over low heat until thickened, about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in the meatballs, and continue cooking until meatballs are heated through, about 5 more minutes.

Description: 
This quick and easy savory sauce turns heat-and-eat meatballs into tonight's main attraction. Serve on a bed of pasta or rice and a side salad, and dinner is ready in no time.Modified from: Easy Swedish Meatball Sauce
