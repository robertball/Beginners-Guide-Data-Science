Title:
Chocolate Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (19.8 ounce) package brownie mix
1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1/2 cup water
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup whipping cream whipped
1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping french vanilla flavored, thawed
toffee bits
or shaved chocolate

Directions:
Prepare brownie mix according to package directions and cool completely. Cut into small squares or crumble.
In a large bowl, combine pudding mix, water and sweetened condensed milk. Mix until smooth, then fold in whipped cream until no streaks remain.
In a trifle bowl or individual pretty glasses, place half of the brownies, half of the pudding mixture and half of the 12 ounce container of whipped topping. Repeat layers.  Shave chocolate onto top layer for garnish or sprinkle on toffee bits. Refrigerate 8 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Shirley
Description: 
Trifle with layers of brownies, chocolate pudding and whipped topping chilled to perfection. Put in individual pretty glasses to serve at a special function.Modified from: Chocolate Trifle
