Title:
Tender Pan-Fried Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tablespoon dried tarragon
2 tablespoons garlic powder
1 tablespoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 tablespoon dry mustard powder
1 tablespoon dried thyme leaves
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 egg
1/4 cup milk
8 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Stir together flour, tarragon, salt, ginger, garlic powder, pepper, mustard powder, thyme, garlic powder, and oregano in a shallow bowl until well blended. Beat the egg together with the milk until smooth in a bowl. Dredge the chicken breasts in the flour mixture, shake off excess.  Use tongs to dip in egg.  Use different tongs to dip in flour. Set breasts aside to rest for 10 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Dredge the chicken breasts in flour again, and shake off excess. Brown the chicken in the hot oil until golden brown on both sides. Place onto a cookie rack on to of a baking sheet, and bake in preheated oven until the chicken is no longer pink in the center, 20 to 30 minutes.
