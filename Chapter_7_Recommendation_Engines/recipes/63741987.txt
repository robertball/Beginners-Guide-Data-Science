Title:
Peachy Sweet and Spicy BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Stick butter
1/2chopped onion
2 stalks celery chopped
2 peaches chopped small
1 or 2 jalapenos seeded and chopped(to taste)
2 cloves garlic chopped
4 cups ketchup
1/2 cup mustard
2cups water
1 TB worcestershire sauce
1/2 teas chili powder
1/2 teas black pepper
1 teas hot sauce(or to taste)
1 teas lemon juice
1 teas salt
1 TB Grand Marnier

Directions:
In a large pot melt the butter and add the chopped vegetables, garlic, and peaches.  Cook down.  When the veggies are cooked down add the remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.  Lower and simmer for about 90 min.  Taste to see if additional spicing is needed.
Remove from heat and let cool.  When cooled put th sauce thru a strainer to remove the vegetables so you are left with a smooth sauce.
Cover and refridgerate for a couple hours or overnight.
Put on your favorite meat or poultry on the grill

Description: 
This has a sweet and spicy tang to it.  You can make a larger batch and freeze the extra for whenver you want sauce for the grill.
