Title:
Baked Parmesan and Golden Onion Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3082955.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large onion, sliced thin
2 large cloves fresh garlic, minced
2 Tbsp olive oil
4 Tbsp. butter
6 medium red or yellow Yukon gold potatoes, sliced thin, skins on
1 c. half and half
3/4 c. crumbled fresh parmesan
dash EACH, salt and pepper
2 c. Durkee French Fried Onions

Directions:
Spray a 2 quart casserole dish.
Place sliced potatoes into casserole dish.
Heat skillet to a medium/low heat and add oil and butter.
Add onions, cooking and stirring until transparent.
Add garlic and cook for 2 more minutes.
Add onion mixture to potatoes and toss gently.
Add crumbled cheese and toss gently again.
Pour half and half over all.
COVER and bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
UNCOVER and bake an additional 30 minutes.
Top with french fried onions and bake an additional 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
A smooth, rich side dish that tastes like a main dish.
