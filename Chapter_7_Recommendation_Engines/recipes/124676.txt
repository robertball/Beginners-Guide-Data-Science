Title:
Broccoli Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990669.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups fresh broccoli florets
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 1/2 teaspoons lemon-pepper seasoning

Directions:
In a skillet over medium-high heat, stir-fry broccoli in butter and lemon pepper until crisp-tender, about 2-3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Susan  Davis
Description:
