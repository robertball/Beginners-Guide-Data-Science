Title:
Melt-In-Your-Mouth Short Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992679.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds beef short ribs, cut into serving-sized pieces
2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 (10.5 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed French Onion Soup
1 (12 fluid ounce) bottle dark ale or beer
Hot mashed potatoes or egg noodles

Directions:
Place the beef into a 3 1/2- to 6-quart slow cooker.  Add the brown sugar, garlic, thyme and flour. Toss to coat.  Stir the soup and ale in a small bowl. Pour over the beef.
Cover and cook on LOW for 8 to 9 hours* or until the beef is fork-tender. Serve with the mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Slow-cooked in a mouthwatering sauce seasoned with thyme and garlic, these tender short ribs are full of flavor.
