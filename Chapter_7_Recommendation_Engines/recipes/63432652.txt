Title:
White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp oil
1 c chopped onion
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into pieces
5-6 c chicken broth (3 14 oz cans)
2 15 oz cans cannelini beans, drained
2 4 oz cans chopped green chiles, drained
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp cumin
Dash cayenne pepper
6 oz shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
In large stock pot or Dutch over, heat oil over medium-high heat until hot.
Add onions, garlic, and chicken; cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink.
Stir in remaining ingredients except cheese.
Bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer 10-15 minutes.
Garnish with cheese.
