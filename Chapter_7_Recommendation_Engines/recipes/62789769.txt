Title:
Strawberry Lemon Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 purchased Angel Food cake, cubed
2 qts strawberries, sliced
1 small instant lemon pudding
2 8oz cream cheese, softened
1 c. powdered sugar
1 1/2 c. milk
1 8oz Cool Whip (2 tubs needed total)

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and sugar with electric mixer.
Add lemon pudding and milk and mix well.
Fold in one tub of Cool Whip.
Layer cake cubes, then strawberries, then pudding mixture in trifle bowl.
End with pudding mixture on top.
Refrigerate for a few hours (or overnight, if possible).
Serve in bowls - add additional Cool Whip on each serving.
