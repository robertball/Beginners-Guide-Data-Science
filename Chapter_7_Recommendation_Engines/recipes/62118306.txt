Title:
Cheesy  Pronto Pups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. refrigerator biscuits
8 frankfurters
8 strips Cheddar or American Cheese
1/2 C. corn flake crumbs
3 Tbsp. barbeque sauce

Directions:
Preheat the oven 400 degrees F.
Lightly grease a cookie sheet.
Crush the corn flakes in a plastic bag with rolling pin
Separate the doughinto 8 rolls.
Cut a narrow slit lengthwise in each frankfurter and insert a cheese strip.
Shape 1 dough roll evenly around the frankfurter pinching to seal seams.
Brush with barbeque sauce and roll in crushed corn flakes to coat completely.
place on cookie sheet and bake 400 degrees for 15 - 20 minutes or until golden brown.
Serve with ketchup and mustards.
Note: For snack size, cut frankfurters in half before making a slit and insert cheese. Then follow the remaining directions.
