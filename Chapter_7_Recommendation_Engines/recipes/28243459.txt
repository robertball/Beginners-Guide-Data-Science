Title:
Scallops and Marinara Sauce on Spinach Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tsp. olive oil, divided
1 cup chopped onion
1 can (14-1/2 oz.) Italian style stewed tomatoes, undrained
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 tsp. Thai chili paste
9 oz. fresh spinach fettuccine, uncooked
12 oz. scallops, rinsed, drained
2 TBS. freshly grated Parmesan cheese
2 tsp. chopped chives

Directions:
For Marinara sauce, heat 1 tsp. oil in medium saucepan over medium heat.  Add onion; cook and stir 3 minutes or until soft.  Add tomatoes, mushrooms, bell pepper and chili paste, if desired.  Bring to boil over high heat.  Reduce heat to low.  Cover; simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Cook fettuccine according to package directions, omitting salt.  Drain; keep warm.
Meanwhile, heat remaining 2 tsps. oil in large nonstick skillet over medium heat.  Add scallops; cook and stir 4 minutes or until scallops are opaque.
Divide fettuccine amoung 4 serving plates.  Top with marinara sauce and scallops.  Sprinkle with cheese and chives.
