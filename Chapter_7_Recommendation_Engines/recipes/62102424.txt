Title:
Sausage and Sundried Tomato Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs sweet Italian Sausage links
1/2 cup sliced sundried tomatoes, chopped
5 cloves sliced garlic
1 14 1/2 oz can diced tomatoes
1 2lb 13 oz oz jar pasta sauce
1/2 Cup chopped flat-leaf Italian parsley
1 sprig rosemary, removed from stem
1 bay leaf
1 Tablespoon fresh oregano, chopped
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
salt and pepper to taste
grated fresh parmesan, optional
1 lb Linguine, cooked al dente

Directions:
In large sauce pan or dutch oven on medium heat, brown Italian sausage all over. Remove from pan when no pink is longer visible and slice into 1/2 inch slices. Add back to the pan along with sundried tomatoes. Stir gently for approx 5 min and then add garlic. Saute for 5 more min until sausage is cooked through. Add remaining ingredients and cook at least 1/2 hour on low heat. Remove bay leaf and serve over linguine with lots of parmesan cheese(optional).
