Title:
Caramel Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2oz. semi-sweet Chocolate
1 Tsp. vanilla extract
1/4 cup  sugar
1/4 cup Caramel syrup
4 cups milk
whipped topping (optional)

Directions:
In a large sauce pan combine chocolate, vanilla, sugar, caramel, and 1/2 cup of milk
bring ingredients to a boil over medium heat
Reduce heat to low after ingredients boil
Add remaining 3 1/2 cups milk
Warm to desired temperature
Pour into mugs and top with a dollop of whipped topping and serve.

Submitter: 
cocoa Queen
Description: 
A rich Hot chocolate with a hint of Caramel to warm your taste buds on a cold winters day.
