Title:
Broccoli-Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cs. fresh broccoli florets
1/2 c. grape tomatoes
1/2 c, chopped red onion
1/2 c. sliced black olives
1/4 c. plus 2 tbsp light olive oil vinaigrette or Italian dressing
1/2 c. crumbled reduced-fat feta cheese

Directions:
Combine the broccoli, tomatoes, onion and olives in a large bowl.  Pour the dressing over the salad, and toss to mix.
Add the feta cheese, and toss again.
Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
AJ
Description: 
One of my favorite salads. Just chill for 30 to 60 minutes.
