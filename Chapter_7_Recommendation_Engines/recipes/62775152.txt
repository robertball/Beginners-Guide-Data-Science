Title:
Maggie's Muffin In a Minute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup ground flaxseed
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 teaspoon sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon butter

Directions:
Add all dry ingredients to a coffe cup and mix well.
Cut butter into cup.
Add egg and mix well.
Microwave 1 minute.
Emty onto plate.
Butter and enjoy!
