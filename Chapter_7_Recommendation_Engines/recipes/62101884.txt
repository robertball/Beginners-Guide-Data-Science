Title:
Super Healthy Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup white flour
1/2 cup oat bran
1/2 cup wheat germ
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon cloves
1 teaspoon allspice
1 teaspoon nutmeg
2 cups mashed pumpkin
1 cup applesauce
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup walnuts
1/2 cup pecans
1/2 cup raisins
3 eggs
1/2 cup plain yogurt

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F
Mix the dry ingredients(flours, oat bran, wheat germ, baking soda, baking powder, brown sugar, salt, and spices) in a bowl and set aside.
Mix the eggs, pumpkin, applesauce, honey, and yogurt in a second bowl.
Add the dry ingredients and mix thoroughly.
Mix in the nuts and raisins.
Pour the batter in 4 greased mini loaf pans or two greased small loaf pans and bake for 45-55 minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Sara
Description: 
Lots of spices and a powerful pumpkin flavor give this bread its unique and delicious taste.  It is very moist and packed with nuts and raisins, perfect for breakfast, snacks, or anytime at all.
