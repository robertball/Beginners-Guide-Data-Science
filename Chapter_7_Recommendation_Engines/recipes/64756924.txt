Title:
Tomato-Oregano Preserves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6961235.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Roma tomatoes, cut into quarters
1 c onion, chopped
6 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 c fresh oregano (snipped leaves or whole)
1 T fresh parsley (snipped leaves or whole)
1 T black peppercorns
2 cups distilled vinegar
1/8 c sugar
1/3 c olive oil
1 tsp salt

Directions:
In a non-reactive microwave bowl, add all spices and liquids except for tomatoes, onions and garlic.
Microwave on high for 3 minutes. Stir.   Add tomatoes, onions and garlic.  Stir well, ensuring all are coated with mixture and microwave a further 4 minutes.  Stir, pressing tomatoes under the liquids.  Tomato juices will mix into the liquid.  Microwave another 4 minutes, and carefully remove.  It will be bubbling hot.
Carefully transfer to a sterilized jar or jars.
Store in the refrigerator until ready to use.

Submitter: 
BeachMom65
Description: 
Roma tomatoes and fresh oregano kick this one off.  Black peppercorns and plenty of onion and garlic back-drop this gorgeous side dish for bruschetta, antipasto platters and fish.  I keep fresh parmigiano reggiano on hand a lot, and it pairs beautifully.
