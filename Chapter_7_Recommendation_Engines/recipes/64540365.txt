Title:
Chocolate Chickpea Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3157064.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can chickpeas rinsed & drained very well
1/2 cup almond butter
1/3 cup maple syrup
2 tsp almond extract
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven 350F degrees
Prepare a 8x8 baking pan by spraying it with nonstick cocking spray
Add all ingredients except chocolate chips to a blender or food processor
Process to combine until smooth
Add chocolate chips and fold into the batter by hand
Bake 20-25 minutes
Cool in the pan 20 minutes before eating or cutting
Using a plastic knife, cut brownies into 16 squares & enjoy!

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Need a chocolate fix? Sink your teeth into these decadent, gluten free and healthy Chocolate Chickpea Brownies guilt free!
