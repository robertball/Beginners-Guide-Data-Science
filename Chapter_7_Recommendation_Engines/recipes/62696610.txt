Title:
Elt's Greek Chicken and Spinach Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz boxes of frozen chopped spinach, thawed
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp parsley
1 tsp basil
1 egg, beaten
2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 Tbsp lemon juice
8 oz feta cheese, crumbled
4 oz mozzerella, shredded
3 chicken breasts, grilled and chopped
6 large flour (burrito size) tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Drain the spinach thoroughly.
In a medium mixing bowl, stir together all ingredients but the tortillas.
Fill each of 6 tortillas with the spinach mixture and roll.
Spray each tortilla with nonstick cooking spray or brush with melted butter and place seam-side down on a foil lined baking sheet.
Bake for 10 minutes, turn and bake for 10 minutes more.
Serve with salsa.

Submitter: 
elt
Description: 
These delicious, filling wraps made with grilled chicken, cooked spinach, mozzarella and feta cheeses have a distinctively Greek flavor.  For a twist, add in sun-dried tomatoes and fresh mushrooms.
