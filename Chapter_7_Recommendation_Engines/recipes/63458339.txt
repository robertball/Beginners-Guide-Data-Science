Title:
Bridget's Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box frozen chopped spinach, defrosted and squeezed dry
4 small jars marinated artichokes, drained & finely chopped
2 T. minced garlic
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 package full fat or 1/3 fat cream cheese
4 oz. shredded mozzarella
4 oz. shredded Monerey Jack
1/4 cup shredded Romano (shredded from a fresh block)
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan (shredded from a fresh block)
pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Mix all ingredients in a large bowl or ovenproof container.  Bake approx 45 min.  Top with diced tomatoes if desired.  Serve warm with tortilla chips.
Can be made several days ahead.

Karen Martin

Super cheesy spinach artichoke dip!
