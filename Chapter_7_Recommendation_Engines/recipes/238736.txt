Title:
Jolly Rancher®-Infused Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4554273.jpg
Ingredients:
20 pieces fruit-flavored hard candy (such as Jolly Rancher®)
1 1/4 cups vodka
1 tablespoon white sugar

Directions:
Place candies in a container with a lid and add vodka. Seal container and place in a cool, dark place, shaking vigorously 1 to 2 times per hour, until candy has dissolved, 3 to 4 hours.
Stir sugar into vodka mixture until dissolved.

Submitter: 
JonahLuck
Description: 
These colorful vodkas are a fun addition to any bar and are an approachable alcoholic drink for anyone of age. Cherry, sour apple, and watermelon are divine. Serve chilled.
