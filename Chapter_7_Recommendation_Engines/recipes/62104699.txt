Title:
Mary's Special Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large pieces of chicken, bone in (any combination of legs, thighs, breasts)
onion salt, paprika and pepper to taste
4 tbs. butter or margerine
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 4-oz. jar of chopped pimentos, drained
1 4-oz jar of cut-up mushrooms, drained
3/4 cup dry white wine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Generously spray roasting pan with nonstick cooking spray, or line pan with foil and spray generously.  Place chicken, single layer, in roasting pan.  Sprinkle chicken pieces with onion salt, paprika and pepper.  Dot generously with butter or margerine.  Bake uncovered at 350 for 1 hour to 1 hour and 15 minutes, basting with juices 4 times while baking.
In medium size bowl mix together the mushroom soup, pimentos, mushrooms and white wine.
Spray large casserole dish with non-stick cooking spray.  Transfer chicken from roasting pan to casserole dish.
Pour mixture over chicken, cover casserole dish and bake at 350 for an additional 45 minutes.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Jackie
Description: 
A co-worker served this recipe several years ago at a small dinner party and every person at the dinner requested the recipe.  It is easy, delicious, and presents very nicely for guests or makes a fabulous family meal.
