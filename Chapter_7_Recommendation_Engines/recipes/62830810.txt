Title:
Fruits of the Earth Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe peaches, peeled and sliced
2 apples, peeled and sliced
1 cup blueberries
1 cup raspberries OR blackberries
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup chopped walnuts OR pecans (optional)
1/2 teaspoon vanilla OR almond extract
TOPPING:
3/4 cup flour
3/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup rolled oats (regular or quick)
1/2 cup COLD butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375°
Grease an 8" x 8" baking dish.
In a large bowl, combine the fruits and toss with the extract.  Set aside.
In a separate bowl, mix together the dry ingredients.
Cut the butter into chunks.
Using a pastry blender or two knives, cut the butter into the dry mixture until coarse chunks form.
Pour fruit into the prepared baking dish and top with the crumble mixture.
Bake at 375° for 40-45 minutes or until fgolden brown and bubbly.
Serve warm with ice cream, if desired.
