Title:
Honey Soy Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tilapia Filets
3 tbsp Honey
3 tbsp Soy Sauce
3 tbsp Balsamic vinegar
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 tsp fresh cracked black pepper

Directions:
1.  Mix honey, soy sauce, balsamic vinegar, garlic
2.  Place fish in with liquid mix and marinate for 30 minutes (at least)
3.  Preheat oven to 350
4.  Sprinkle fresh pepper on fish
5.  Bake for 15 to 20 minutes
