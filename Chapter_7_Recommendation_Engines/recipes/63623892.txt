Title:
Maple Chocolate Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box brownie mix
1 cup of maple syrup

Directions:
Prepare the brownies according to instructions on the box but leave out the oil.
Add the syrup.
Cook for as long as the box says and enjoy!
