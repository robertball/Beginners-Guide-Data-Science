Title:
Skinny Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3051155.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
3 over ripe bananas
1/2 cup applesauce
1 Stevia envelope
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups Gluten Free Oat Flour
1/4 teaspoon Gluten Free Baking Powder
Pinch of Pink Himalayan salt
1/4 cup dark chocolate chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk eggs. Add bananas and mash mixture with a potato masher.
Add applesauce, Stevia, pinch of salt, and vanilla extract. Hand whisk until everything looks well incorporated.
Add oat flour and baking powder, and whisk. Once batter blends well, add dark chocolate chips.
Pour mixture into a greased 6×6 squared pan. If desired, add extra chocolate chips on top as a garnish.
Bake for 35-40 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
ModishHouseWife
Description: 
Forgot to finish your bananas and they over ripped on you? Don’t toss them, treat yourself to some guilt free, refined sugar free, gluten free, low fat Blondies!
