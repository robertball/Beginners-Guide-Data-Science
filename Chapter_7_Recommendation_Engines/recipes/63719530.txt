Title:
Savory Sweet Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium sweet potatoes
1 chopped onion (save ¼ for the seasoning)
4 celery stalks
2 garlic cloves
¼ cup coconut oil
1 Tbsp. fresh sage
Sea salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Prep your veggies.
Add all veggies and garlic and coconut oil to large pot.
Cover the ingredients with water.
Cook until tender, about 15 – 20 minutes.
While cooking, sauté ¼ of your chopped onion in pan with fresh sage.
When soup is done, use Immersion Blender to combine your soup ingredients, or use a regular blender.
Season with onion and sage and salt and pepper and serve!

Submitter: 
J Alex Alexander
Description: 
Incredibly healthy and delicious soup.  Sweet potatoes are considered one of the world's healthiest foods.
