Title:
Chicken Cheeseburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/751457.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground chicken
3 Tbs water
3/4 cup garlic croutons, finely crushed
1 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 tsp chopped parsley

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Use moistened hands to form mix into patties. Grill until internal temp is 165.
Serve on buns with your choice of toppings. My families favorite is barbeque sauce and cheddar cheese.
