Title:
Toni's Cherry Flips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/2 cup icing sugar
2 egg yolks
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp almond extract
2 cup flour
glazed cherries (red & green)

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 325 degrees F
2. Mix first 6 ingredients to form the dough
3. Cover the cherry with dough making sure it isa well covered with dough
4. After cooled, dab the top of the cookie with butter icing and 1/2 cherry
