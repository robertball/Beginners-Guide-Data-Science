Title:
Pineapple Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) boneless pork roast
1 teaspoons seasoned salt or garlic salt
1 Pinch of East Seasoning
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup water mixed with 2 tsps. chicken base
1 Tbsp. minced ginger
5 or 6 whole garlic cloves
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple tidbits, undrained
1 cups chopped dried cranberries

Directions:
Rub the pork roast on all sides with salt, pepper, and East seasoning.  Brown on all sides in 1 Tbsp. of oil in Dutch oven. Add sliced onion, garlic, and ginger and garlic.  Place in a slow cooker or if cooking right away, pour in the pineapple chunks and juice, and sprinkle in the cranberries.
Cover, and cook 7 hours on Low in crock pot, or 1 hour in Oven at 350.  Uncover the last 15 minutes to brown and help reduce juices.
