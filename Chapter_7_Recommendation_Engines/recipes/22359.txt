Title:
Slow-Cooked Tangy Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups vinegar
1/2 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 dash ground black pepper
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 pounds pork spareribs

Directions:
Combine vinegar, ketchup, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt, pepper and oil in a slow cooker; mix well. Heat a small amount of oil in a large skillet and brown ribs.   You can cut the ribs into serving sizes before  browning, if you would like.
Transfer ribs to slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 4 to 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
Easy to prepare and absolutely delicious!  My husband isn't fond of ribs, but asks for this recipe.  Cooking a little longer doesn't hurt them, so this is good for a work day !
