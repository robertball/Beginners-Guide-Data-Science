Title:
Coleslaw with Cranberries ~ No Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5969220.jpg
Ingredients:
6-8  cups shredded red cabbage
1/4 large red onion thinly sliced
1 cup dried cranberries
2 stalks of celery thinly sliced.
(all can be sliced in food processor.
DRESSING:
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1/3 cup canola oil, I added some olive oil too.
A few teaspoons of sugar to taste

Directions:
combine all.  Whisk together the dressing ingredients, pour over the slaw, cover and refrigerate a few hours or overnight. Drain off the dressing before serving after it has marinaded in the dressing.  I stirred as much as possible to combine.
