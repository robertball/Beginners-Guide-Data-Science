Title:
Nectarine Bananna Fiber Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 nectarine pitted and cubed
1/2 banana
1/2 tsp steviastevia (optional)
2tbsp flaxseed powderpowder
1 heaping tbsp Greek yougert
Ice cubes for preferred smoothness

Directions:
Add each ingredient 1 at a time into blender.  Mix until it reaches desired consistency.  May need to shake up between blendings if using a personal sized blender.

Very filling smoothie ideal for meal replacement.
