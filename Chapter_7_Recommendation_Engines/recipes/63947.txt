Title:
Festive Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986494.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 dash salt
1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
In heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt chocolate chips with sweetened condensed milk and salt.  Remove from heat; stir in nuts (optional) and vanilla.  Spread evenly into wax paper lined 8- or 9-inch square pan.
Chill 2 hours or until firm.  Turn fudge onto cutting board; peel off paper and cut into squares.  Store leftovers covered in refrigerator

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
This sweet chocolaty fudge is easy to make and makes a great addition to party trays.
