Title:
Chicken Skewers with Radicchio and Bean salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c plain lowfat yogurt
2 T pine nuts
1 clove garlic
2 1/4 c fresh parsley leaves
1/4 c plus 2 T olive oil
kosher salt and pepper
1 15 oz can cannellini beans, rinsed
1/2 small head radiccio sliced (1 c)
1 T white wine vinegar
1 1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 2 inch pieces

Directions:
puree yogurt, pine nuts, garlic, 2 c parsley, 1/4 c oil, 1/4 t salt and pepper in food processor
chop remaining parsley.  toss with beans, radiccio, vinegar, 1 T oil, 1/4 t salt and pepper
heat grill to medium high
thread chx onto 8 skewers, coat with remaiing oil, 1/4 tsp salt and pepper
grill chx, turning occassionally, until cooked, 7-10 min
serve with pesto and salad
