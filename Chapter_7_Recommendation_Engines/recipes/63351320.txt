Title:
Low Fat Hot Wing Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
3 stalks of celery
3 green onions
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/4 cups plain fat-free yogurt
Frank's Red Hot Pepper Sauce to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Spray baking dish with cooking spray and place chicken in dish.
Cover chicken with hot sauce and bake for about 45 minutes or until cooked through.
After chicken has cooled, chop into small pieces.
Chop celery and green onions into small pieces.
Place all ingredients except hot sauce in a bowl and mix thoroughly.
Add hot sauce to taste (usually about 3 tbsp.).
Serve with crackers or even on a sandwich.
