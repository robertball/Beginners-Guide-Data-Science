Title:
Fried Buttered Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
seasoned bread crumbs
8, 12, or 16 oz. egg noodles
salt

Directions:
Boil water for noodles add noodles to boilig water. Lower flame. boil till tender. Drain. Meanwhile, melt butter in skillet or large pot. Add bread crumbs over low flame and mix. Add noodles; stir and mix. Lower flame. Simmer approximately 1/2 hour, stirring occasionally- you want a crust on the bottom. serve with roast beef or meat of your choice
