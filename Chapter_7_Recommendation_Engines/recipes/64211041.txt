Title:
Hillshire Farm Sausage and Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg  Hillshire Farm Turkey Smoked Sausage
6 cups red new potatoes, cut into 1" chunks
1 green bell pepper, cut into 1" chunks
1 red bell pepper, cut into 1" chunks
1 large onion, sliced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 cup reduced sodium chicken broth
1 TBL olive oil
1 tsp dried rosemary leaves, crushed
1 tsp dried thyme leaves, crushed
1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cut sausage in 1/4" slices, set aside.
2. Place potatoes in medium sauce pan; cover with water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Simmer 10 minutes or until crisp-tender. Drain potatoes
3. Combine sausage, potatoes, bell peppers, onion, garlic, chicken broth, olive oil and seasonings in a 12x8" baking dish; mix lightly.
4. bake for 30 minutes or until potatoes are lightly browned and vegetables are tender.
