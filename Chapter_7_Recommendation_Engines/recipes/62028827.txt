Title:
No bake chocolate mousse pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package semisweet chocolate chips
6 eggs - seperated
2 T sugar
16 oz container of cool whip
2 graham cracker crusts

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips over a double boiler until smooth.  Place in large bowl and beat in egg yolks one at a time.  Set aside.
Make meringue with egg whites & 2 T of sugar.
Beat 1/4 of meringue into chocolate mixture.  Fold the remainder of the meringue into chocolate mixture, then fold in 3/4 of the cool whip.
Pour equal amounts into the 2 pie crusts (or into 1 pie crust and individual serving bowls).  Chill in the refridgerator a few hours, then top each pie with remaining cool whip.
