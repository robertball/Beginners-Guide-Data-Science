Title:
Cream Cheese Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter softened
1 8oz pkg cream cheese softened
2 cup sugar
2 large eggs
3 c. all purpose flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 c mashed bananas (about 4 med.)
1 cup roughly chopped pecans, toasted
1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Beat butter & cream cheese at a med speed with an electric mixer until creamy.  Gradually add sugar, beating until light & fluffy. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating just until blended after each addition.
Combine four and the next three ingredients; gradually add to butter mixter, beating at low speed just until blended.  Stir in bananas, pecans & vanilla.  Spoon batter into 2 greased & floured 8 x 4 inch loaf pans.
bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until wooden pick comes out clean.  Shield with aluminum foil last 15 minutes to prevent browning if necessary.
Cool bread in pans on wire racks 10 minutes.  Remove from pans and cool 30 minutes before slicing.
