Title:
Italian Sausage Spagetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb. good quality Italian Sausage
4 c. chopped yellow onions
1 c. chopped parsley
3 c. chopped pepper, mix red and green
1-1/2 lb mushrooms, sliced
8 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbl. Rosemary
1 Tbl. Turkish Oregano
1 Tbl. Thyme
1 Tbl. Basil
1-1/2 tsp Marjorum
3 bay leaves
1/4 tsp creshed red pepper
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp cracked black pepper
1 Tbl sugar
1 28 oz can tomatoe sauce
4 1 lb cans ready cut tomatoes
2 sm cans tomatoe paste
3 c. burgundy wine

Directions:
In a large (11 quart) stock pot, brown Italian sausage, breaking it up as it cooks.
When almost cooked, add onions, garlic, peppers, parsley and mushrooms, cook a few minutes more until sausage is completely cooked.
Add spices, stir, then add tomato sauce, tomatoes, tomato paste and burgundy.
Stir throughly, bring to a simmer, cover and simmer a minimum 3 hours - longer is better.  Serve over your favorite pasta.
Freeze remaining sauce.

Submitter: 
Debi Blair McGinness
Description: 
I've had this recipe for years and it's the absolute best spaghetti sauce I've  found.  I often serve for company and am always asked for the recipe.  Burgundy wine is the key.
