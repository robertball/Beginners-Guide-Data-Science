Title:
Corn, Potato, Bacon, Poblano Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
1 whole yellow onion, chopped
1 large poblano pepper, chopped
2  15 oz cans corn, drained
5 slices bacon, crisp
5 large yukon gold potatoes, chopped into medium size pieces
salt and ground pepper to taste
garlic powder to taste
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, or margarine

Directions:
in a pan, cook bacon until desired crispness.
in a dutch oven, sautee onions and poblano until shiny (i used a little oil so they didn't stick)
put 2 cans of drained corn in the pot
add spices to desired taste
allow to simmer for a few minutes
add chicken broth
add potatoes and make sure they are JUST covered by the broth
bring to a boil, then let simmer for 30 minutes
when the potatoes are the desired tenderness, add crumpled bacon.
use an immersion blender to make some of the soup creamy
