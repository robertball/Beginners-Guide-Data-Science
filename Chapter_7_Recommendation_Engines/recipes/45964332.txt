Title:
Tomato Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup canned kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 large tomato, diced
1 celery rib, sliced
4 green onions, sliced
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup ranch salad dressing
1 teaspoon dill weed
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
Lettuce leaves or your favorite Salad Mix

Directions:
In a bowl combine the first four ingredients.
In another bowl, combine the Mayo, ranch dressing, dill weed and garlic powder.
Pour over vegtables and toss to coat.
Place lettuce in a serving bowl, add vegtable mixture.
Toss just before serving

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
A creamy dilly flavored salad with protein and crunchy veggies. I serve over bagged salad greens.
