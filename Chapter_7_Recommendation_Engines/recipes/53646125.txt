Title:
Strawberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup wheat germ
1/2 cup wheat bran
2 cups quick oats
1 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
4 eggs
1 Tablespoon lime juice
1 1/3 cup smashed very ripe strawberries (if not very ripe then add 1/4 cup sugar)
1 cup sliced very ripe strawberries, more if needed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line 20 muffin tins with papers.
Stir together all dry ingredients, flour - salt, in large mixing bowl. In another bowl, whisk together the vegetable oil, eggs, and smashed strawberries. Mix dry and wet ingredients until just moistened. Fold in sliced strawberries. Fill muffin tins until almost full using a 1/4 cup measure . Try to get some sliced strawberries into each muffin, add a few if necessary.
Bake in the preheated oven for 22-25 minutes. Cool the in tin for 5 minutes before cooling completely on a wire rack.
