Title:
Keto Avocado Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Ripe Avocado (the riper the better)
2 oz Heavy Cream
1/2 Tsp Stevia
1/4 Tsp Vanilla Extract
1/4 Tsp Cinnamon

Directions:
Halve and Seed Avocado, and remove from skin. Section meat and place in mixing bowl. Mash up avocado.
Add Heavy Cream, Vanilla Extract, Cinnamon, and Stevia. Mix thoroughly.
Chill in fridge at least one hour, or place in freezer for one hour for a more ice cream like treat.

Submitter: 
John Larkin
Description: 
This is something I made up on the fly one day for breakfast to try and use up an overly ripe avocado.
