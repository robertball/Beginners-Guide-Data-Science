Title:
Broccoli Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag fresh -or frozen- broccoli
1 bag fresh -or frozen- cauliflower
2 c. ranch
2 c. cheddar cheese
1/2 c. REAL bacon bits OR minced cooked bacon

Directions:
Thaw vegetables OR rinse and rough chop fresh.
Stir together with remaining ingredients.
Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Super easy way to get kids to eat their cruciferous vegetables.
