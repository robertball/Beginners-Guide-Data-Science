Title:
Wallaby-Darned Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid oz. Vodka
1 1/2 fluid oz. peach schnapps
1 Tblsp. sugar
1/2 cup champagne
8 oz. frozen peaches
1/2 cup Bacardi Fuzzy Navel mix
1 cup ice

Directions:
Add the ingredients into a blender in the order listed so that the cold ingredients get added last. Temperature is the key to the correct consistency. Blend for about 30 seconds or until smooth but thick. Pour into glass and enjoy!

Submitter: 
PRELUDELJS
Description: 
A close replica of my favorite Outback Steakhouse drink. Peachy and frozen, made from peach schnapps, vodka, champagne, and Bacardi Fuzzy Navel mixer.
