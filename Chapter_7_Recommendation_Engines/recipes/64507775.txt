Title:
Boiled Chocolate Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SAUCE:
3 1/2 cups water
2 cups sugar
DOUGH:
2 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup cocoa
2 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 cup milk
4 tbsp. shortening
1/2 tsp soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
Cream for serving

Directions:
Boil the water and sugar together for 5 minutes.
Sift the remaining dry ingredients together for the dough.   Add wet ingredients and mix well.
Add dough to sauce by spoonfuls.   Cover and cook 20-25 minutes on medium heat.   DO NOT REMOVE LID.
Server warm with cream poured over the top.

One of my mom's favorite recipes.  Kind of an old-time version of molten lava cake.
