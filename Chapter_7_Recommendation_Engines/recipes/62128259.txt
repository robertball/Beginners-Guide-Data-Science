Title:
Classic English Shepards Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs potato
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 large sweet onion
3 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
2 fresh garlic cloves, peeled and minced
1 lb cooked and chopped tender pork
1/4 cup of butter, chopped
1/4 pint heavy cream
3/4 cup finely grated mozzerella cheese
1 tablespoon fresh minced oregano
fresh mint leaves
1 cup thick gravy

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Peel, and boil potatoes
chop onion into very small peices
heat olive oil in a frying pan
sautee onion and garlic in the pan
add cooked pork to pan (setting gravy aside) and flash brown
remove pan from heat, add sauteed onion and garlic and cover
Mash potatoes with half the butter, cream and salt
In a casserole dish, coat the bottom with half the mashed potatoes
Add the pork
add the sauteed onion and garlic
add pepper
add the rest of the potatoes
sprinkle the top with minced oregano, cheese
add the rest of the butter
cook in the oven for 15 minutes, or until the top is lightly browned
Serve in neat squares with a fresh mint leaf and drizzle with a bit of gravy

Submitter: 
Jackie B.
Description: 
This is a classic recipe, like you will find in London. You can change it around a little, but this is 'THE classic shepard's pie'
