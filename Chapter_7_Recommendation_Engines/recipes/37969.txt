Title:
Honey Bee Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup hot milk
1/2 cup hot water
2 1/2 teaspoons Gourmet Roast TASTER'S CHOICE® 100% Pure Instant Coffee
1 tablespoon honey
2 tablespoons milk foam (optional)

Directions:
Blend milk, water, Taster's Choice and honey in tall glass; top with milk foam. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Taster's Choice®
Description: 
A sweet, creamy cup to kick back with.
