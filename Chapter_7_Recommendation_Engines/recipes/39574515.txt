Title:
Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4C flour
1/2t salt
1/4t pepper
4T butter
4T olive oil
1C mushrooms, sliced
1/2C Marsala wine
1/4C cooking sherry
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
In shallow bowl mix together the first 4 ingredients. Coat chicken pieces in mixture. In large skillet melt butter and olive oil over medium heat. Place chicken in pan and lightly brown, turn over chicken, add mushrooms, and pour in wine and sherry. Cover skillet, simmer chicken for 10 min., and cook until juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
