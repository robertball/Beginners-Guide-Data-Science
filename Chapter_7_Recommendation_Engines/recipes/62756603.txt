Title:
Cheesy Broccoli and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices white sandwich bread, torn into pieces
3/4 C grated parm
4 Tbsp butter,melted
plus 2 Tbsp, chilled
1 garlic clove, minced
2 lb broccoli; florets cut into 1" pieces; stems peeled and chopped
1 onion, chopped
1-1/4 c rice
4 C chicken broth
1-1/4 c half-and-half
1 tsp salt
2 C shredded extra sharp cheddar
1/8 tsp cayenne

Directions:
Prepare Topping- Adjust oven rack to middle position and heat to 400 degrees. Grease 13 x 8 baking dish. Pulse bread, 1/4 C parm, and melted butter in processor until coarsely ground. Add garlic.
Make Filling- Microwave broccoli florets, covered, in lg bowl until bright green and tender, about 2 to 4 min; set aside. Melt remaining butter in Dutch oven over md heat. Cook onion and broccoli stems  until softened, 8 to 10 min. Add rice and cook, stirring constantly, until rice is translucent, about 1 min. Stir in broth, half-and-half, and salt and bring to boil. Reduce heat to md-low and cook, stirring often until rice is tender, 20 to 25 min. Off heat, stir in cheddar, cayenne, remaining parm and broccoli florets.
Top and Bake- Pour mixture into prepared baking dish and top with bread crumb mixture. Bake until sauce is bubbling around edges and top is golden brown, about 15 min. Cool 5 min. Serve.

Submitter: 
Lisa Harmon
Description: 
Lively casserole with tender rice, fresh broccoli flavor and sharp cheddar bite
