Title:
Spinach Salad with Warm Bacon Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg baby spinach
6 pcs thin sliced bacon (chopped then cooked/save fat)
2 tblsp minced/dehydrated onion (then rehydrate in water)
1 tblsp minced garlic or 1 clove fresh
2-3 tblsp apple cider vinegar/equal amounts sugar
dash salt and pepper more to taste

Directions:
1.  Put spinach in large salad bowl
2.  Chop and fry bacon until crisp saving fat.  Place fried bacon on paper towel.
3.  Cook onion in bacon fat for 1 minute stirring constantly add garlic cook until fragrant. (Don't burn!!)
4.  Add rest of the ingredients cook until very hot!!
5.  Add veggies to pan to soften, add crouton to salad if desired and pour hot dressing over salad stir carefully until salad is wilted.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Schepper
Description: 
This is a classic warm version of a spinach salad you can add chopped eggs or fresh mushrooms or experiment on your own!!
