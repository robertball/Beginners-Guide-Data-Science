Title:
Slow Cooker Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 (16 ounce) can chili beans, with liquid
1 (15 ounce) can white beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 can refried beans
1 can Rotel tomatoes
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 cups beef broth
1 (14.5 ounce) cans peeled and diced tomatoes
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chile peppers
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix

Directions:
In a medium skillet, cook the ground beef and onion until browned over medium heat, add taco seasoning and set aside.
Place the ground beef, onion, chili beans, white beans, corn, Rotel tomatoes, tomato sauce, beef broth,diced tomatoes, and green chile peppers in a slow cooker. Mix to blend, and cook on Low setting for 8 hours. It's can also be done on stove top.

Submitter: 
LemLem
Description: 
Browned ground beef seasoned with a package of taco seasoning is combined in a slow cooker with canned beans, tomatoes, and green chilies.  Serve with tortilla chips and a dollop of sour cream.Modified from: Slow Cooker Taco Soup
