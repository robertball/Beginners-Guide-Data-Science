Title:
Thanksgiving Molded Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351962.jpg
Ingredients:
0.6 oz Orange Jello
16 oz Cranberry sauce
8 oz. crushed Pineapple
1/2 cup finely diced celery
2 Tablespoon Orange Zest
1 1/2 cups Boiling Water

Directions:
Dissolve Jello mix into boiling water
Add zest and cranberry
Chill 1 hour until lightly thickened
Stir in pineable and celery
Pour into 1 1/2 quart mold
Chill till completely done
