Title:
Coca Mocha Milk Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4461303.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup prepared coffee, cooled to room temperature
1/4 cup Vanilla DREYER'S or EDY'S SLOW CHURNED Light Ice Cream
1 packet Rich Milk Chocolate Flavor CARNATION BREAKFAST ESSENTIALS® LIGHT START™ Complete Nutritional Drink

Directions:
Place coffee, ice cream and Carnation Breakfast Essentials Drink in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Carnation Breakfast Essentials
Description: 
Chocolate and coffee lovers unite under one shake. Try the Coca Mocha Milk Shake, the quintessential milk shake with an extra pick-me-up!
