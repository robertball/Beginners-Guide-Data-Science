Title:
Updated Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3T dry breadcrumbs
1t EVOO
1/4t paprika
1 16-oz or 10 oz pkg. frozen spinach
1 3/4C 1% milk, divided
3T flour
2C grated extra-sharp Cheddar cheese (6oz)
1C low-fat cottage cheese
1/8t ground nutmeg
1/2t salt, or to taste
Freshly ground pepper to taste
8oz (2C) whole-wheat elbow macaroni or penne

Directions:
Put lg. pot of lightly salted water on to boil. Preheat over to 450 degrees. Coat 8-in baking dish (2-qt) with cooking spray
Mix breadcrumbs, oil and paprika in sm. bowl. Cook spinach according to pkg. directions. Drain and refresh under cold water, press out excess moisture.
Heat 1 1/2C milk in lg. heavy saucepan over med-high heat until steaming. Whisk remaining 1/4C milk and flour in sm. bowl until smooth; add to hot milk and cook, whisking constantly, until sauce simmers and thickens, 2-3 min. Remove from heat and stir in Cheddar until melted. Stir in cottage cheese, nutmeg, salt and pepper.
Cook pasta for 4 min, or until not quite tender. (It will continue to cook during baking). Drain and add to the cheese sauce; mix well. Spread 1/2 the pasta mixture in prepared baking dish. Spoon spinach on top. Top with remaining pasta; sprinkle w/breadcrumb mixture.
Bake until bubbly and golden, 25-30 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Has all the comfort and great taste of its predecessors with substantially more healthful profile. Uses whole-wheat pasta and includes a colorful layer of spinach.
