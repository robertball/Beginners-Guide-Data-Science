Title:
Dorito Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 2 pnds. hamburger meat
1 taco seasoning 2 packs if 2 pnds. of meat
1 can or Ro-Tel
1 can of Ranch Style Beans
Shredded Cheese - I use mexican blend.
1 Bag of Doritos ( I use Nacho.)

Directions:
Brown your hamburger meat and add taco seasoning. Drain.
Add Ro-Tel (you can use 2 cans depending on your taste)
Add Ranch Style Beans (you can do the same with this)
Simmer for about 10 min.
Then put a handful of doritos on a plate then put meat mixture on top and add cheese.
Enjoy!
