Title:
Pumpkin Dip Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/486434.jpg
Ingredients:
1 --8 oz bar of cream cheese
1 --can of pumpkin puree 15-16oz
1/3 cup of maple syrup or 1/2 cup of brown sugar
pumpkin pie spice to taste

Directions:
mix ingrediants well
soften cream cheese alone first beat well in mixer
add your syrup mix well
add pumpkin and spice
finish mixing
Enjoy :)
