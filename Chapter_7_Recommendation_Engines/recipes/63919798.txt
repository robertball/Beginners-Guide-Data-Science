Title:
Sweet and Savory Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp honey
2 tbsp vinegar
1 kiwi
1/4 onion
2 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 butter aprox.
1 stick of celery
1 egg
1 handful of spinach
1/2 cup of cake or bread crumbs
1 tbsp slivered almonds

Directions:
In a small pot, combine honey and vinegar; heat on stove. Cut the kiwi into 4 chunks. Squeeze the juice and flesh of kiwi by hand into the honey/vinegar mixture. Leave the vinaigrette simmering on low. Drop about 1 tsp of butter into a small frying pan, and saute celery with egg. Toast cake crumbs on a griddle on the stove. In another small frying pan, combine brown sugar, onion, and a little butter. Saute on low. Wash a handful of spinach and arrange it on a small plate. Top spinach with celery and egg, sprinkle with cake crumbs, caramelized onion, slivered almonds, and the kiwi vinaigrette. Serve.
