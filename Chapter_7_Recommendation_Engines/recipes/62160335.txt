Title:
southern style fried chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
chicken thighs about 6
2 cups flour
3 eggs beaten
1 tablespoon of seasoning salt
1 teaspoon of onion powder
1 teaspoon of garlic powder
1 teaspoon of paprika
1 tablespoon of pepper
1 cup of oil for frying

Directions:
preheat oven to 325..
mix the flour and seasonings all in one big baggy
beat eggs in a large enough bowl to dip chicken in
rinse chicken thighs well in cold water
heat oil in large fry pan on medium
dip in baggy of flour
dip in egg
and dip in flour once more
add to heated oil in pan and brown on both sides
put in oven safe dish
cook in oven for approx.4 hours

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
this chicken literally falls off the bone the trick is slow cooking it in the oven after frying in a pan....
