Title:
** Shrimp & Grits w/Poached Egg & Spicy Hollandaise Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 servings instant grits.
1 andouille sausage, split lengthwise, then sliced.
12 peeled/cooked frozen shrimp.
1 pkg hollandaise sauce mix
1/2 chipotle pepper w/adobo sauce, finely chopped.
2 poached eggs.

Directions:
Cook grits per pkg instructions; stir in 1 tbl. butter.
Prepare hollandaise sauce per pkg instructions; stir in chopped chipotle.
Saute sliced andouille sausage in a little oil; add frozen shrimp & cook until hot.
Poach eggs.
Layer hot grits, sausage & shrimp, and poached egg in individual bowls. Top w/chipotle/hollandaise sauce.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Quick breakfast; semi-homemade.  Made this one up when craving a different shrimp 'n grits breakfast. It has become a regular for us  western yankees.
