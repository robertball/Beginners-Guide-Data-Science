Title:
Pumpkin Streusel Coffeecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
STREUSEL:
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
3 TB cold butter
1/2 cup coarsely chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)
CAKE:
2 cups flour
2 tsps. baking powder
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup (2 sticks) butter, room temp.
1 cup sugar
2 large eggs
1 cup canned pumpkin
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Grease and flour a 9 inch round cake pan.
For STREUSEL: Mix flour, brown sugar, cinnamon in a bowl.  Cut in butter with pastry blender or two knives until crumbly.  Stir in nuts.
FOR CAKE:  Mix flour, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda and salt in bowl.  Set aside.  In another bowl, beat butter and sugar till creamy.  Add eggs one at a time.  Beat well after each addition.  Beat in pumpkin and vanilla.  Gradually beat in flour mixture.  Spoon half of batter into prepared pan.  Sprinkle 3/4 cup streusel over batter.  Spoon remaining batter over streusel, sprinkle with the rest of the streusel.
Bake for 40 minutes.  Cool in pan 10 minutes, then finish cooling on wire rack.
