Title:
Oriental Style Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one head coursely shredded green cabbage
1/2 head shredded purple cabbage
4 sliced green onions
1 cup slivered almonds
2 tsp. sesame seeds
1 tsp. butter
1 pk. ramen noodles (beef flavored)
4 tsp. sugar
4 tsp. red wine vinegar
1/2 c. virgin olive oil
pinch of salt

Directions:
1. lightly toast almonds and sesame seeds in butter and pinch of salt until brown.
2.  combine both cabbages, green onions
3. to make dressing, thoroughly mix together the vinegar, oil, sugar, and the seasoning packet that comes with the ramen noodles.
4. mix the dressing, cabbage, and almond/sesame seeds together- the salad may be chilled or served room temperature
5.  Mix in crushed ramen noodles right before serving to make it crunchy and fresh!

Submitter: 
TNCOLLEGEGIRL
Description: 
This light and flavorful salad is cabbage-based.  Crushed ramen noodles and almonds add crunch, and the ramen seasoning packet is used to make the vinigarette dressing!  This is enexpensive and perfect to impress guests. Tastes good with grilled fish.
