Title:
Delicious Spaghetti w/ meatsause Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/491342.jpg
Ingredients:
Noodles:
Fill pot with water, let it come to a boil, then add noodles.
Meatsauce:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 13- to 15-ounce jar spaghetti sauce
2 14-1/2-ounce cans diced tomatoes, undrained
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Once noodles have come to a boil pour out water with strainer.
Meatsuace:
Cook ground beef until it is fully cooked, then add sauce, undrained can of diced tomatoes, then add saltand pepper.Once all ingredients are incorporated into sauce pour over noodles.
Boil noodles on high then half way through lower to medium heat.
Cook ground beef on high until fully cooked, then lower heat to medium while you mix in the rest of the sauce ingredients.
