Title:
Tenders Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989084.jpg
Ingredients:
8 servings Gorton's® Original Batter Tenders
4 hard taco shells
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup salsa

Directions:
Cook Gorton's Tenders per instructions on package. Place 2 Gorton's Tenders on bottom of taco shell. Layer lettuce, cheese, and salsa on top as desired.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Gorton's®
Description: 
Who can resist tacos? Gorton's Tenders Tacos are a crunchy, delicious way to enjoy an authentic Mexican meal in minutes. And, with just 5 ingredients - it's easy to throw together any night of the week!
