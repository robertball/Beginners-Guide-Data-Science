Title:
Jacksonville Crab & Arti-'don't choke' Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986960.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dip Mix
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, rinsed, drained and chopped
3/4 cup cooked crabmeat, rinsed and drained
2 tablespoons chopped red or green bell pepper
Sliced French bread, crackers or fresh vegetables, for dipping

Directions:
Combine dips mix and sour cream in large bowl. Stir in artichoke hearts, crabmeat, and bell peppers. Chill 30 minutes.
Serve with sliced French bread, crackers or fresh vegetables

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Serve this creamy crab, artichoke, and Ranch spread with bread, crackers, or fresh vegetables.
