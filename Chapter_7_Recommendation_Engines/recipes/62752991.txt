Title:
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
2 T. butter
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 1/2 cups rolled oats
2 1/4 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
In a large bowl cream shortening, brown sugar and granulated sugar.  Add eggs, vanilla, cinnamon and mix thoroughly.
Combine the baking soda, salt and flour and stir into creamed mixture
Add oatmeal and chocolate chips and stir until well blended.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet.  Bake for 10 minutes.
