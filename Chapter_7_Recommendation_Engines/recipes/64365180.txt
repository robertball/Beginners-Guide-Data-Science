Title:
Chocolate Guinness Cupcakes with Irish Creme Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2160286.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup of butter
3/4 cup of cocoa powder
1 cup Guinness Extra Stout
1 tbsp. vanilla extract
1 1/4 cup all purpose flour
1 2/3 cup sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
2 eggs
Frosting
1 1/2 cups butter softened
3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/4 cup Baileys Irish Cr√®me

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Melt butter in saucepan on low, add cocoa powder and whisk until smoot.  Stir in Guinness and vanilla.  Remove from heat to cool. Sift dry ingredients.  Add liquid mixture and stir until thoroughly combined.
Add eggs, stir until well incorporated.  Fill mini-muffin lined tins to 3/4 full.  Bake for 10-11 minutes until toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Cool completely then frost.  Refrigerate leftovers.
Frosting: Beat butter and sugar until combined.  Add Baileys a tablespoon at a time.  Then beat until light and fluffy, white in color.  About 10 minutes.

Description: 
Good St. Patricks Day dessert.  Very light, moist.  The frosting is the showstopper though!
