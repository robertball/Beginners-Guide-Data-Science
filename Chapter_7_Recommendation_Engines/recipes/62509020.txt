Title:
Double Layer Pumpkin Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/2 C. sour cream
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
3/4 cup pumpkin pie filling
1/2 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (165 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Blend in eggs one at a time.  Fold in 1/2 C. sour cream. Remove 1 cup of batter and spread into bottom of crust; set aside.
Add pumpkin to the remaining batter and  gently stir until well blended. Carefully spread over the batter in the crust.
Bake in water bath for one hour or until center is almost set. Turn off oven.  Prop door open and let sit for 1-2 hours.  Will not crack.  Top with whipped topping before serving.
