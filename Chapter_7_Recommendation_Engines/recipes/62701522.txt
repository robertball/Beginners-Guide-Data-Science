Title:
Beefy Baked Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef or frozen meatballs heated and ground in food processor
1/2 (25 ounce) package frozen cheese ravioli
1 (14 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Brown ground beef. Put ground beef or heated frozen meatballs in food processor with spaghetti sauce and chop. Heat chopped sauce in saucepan.  Boil ravioli according to package.
Spread 1/3 of the sauce in the bottom of casserole dish. Arrange 1/2 of the ravioli over the sauce. Sprinkle 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese and 1/2 of the Monterey Jack cheese over the ravioli. Repeat layers, ending with the last of the sauce on top. Cover with aluminum foil.
Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top before serving.
