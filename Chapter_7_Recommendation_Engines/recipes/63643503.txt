Title:
Spring Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
can of cannellini beans
can of kidney beans
vegetable broth
garlic (diced)
onion (diced)
carrots (diced)
celery (diced)
potatoes (diced)
chard (diced)
kale (diced)
tomatoes (diced)
sage
bay leaves
4 pieces of bacon (diced) (optional)

Directions:
Mix ingredients in the crockpot.
Cook on low for 6-10 hours.
Serve with corn bread.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
This is a great way to use kale and chard in the springtime.
