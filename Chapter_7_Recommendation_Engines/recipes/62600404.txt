Title:
Parmesan Baked Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 C bread crumbs, Italian-style
1/4 C Parmesan cheese
1/4 t black pepper
2 cloves garlic
2 T olive oil
24 oz chicken breast halves
cooking spray
1/2 C spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Heat large baking sheet in oven for 5 min
Combine first 3 ingred. in shallow dish
Place garlic and oil in small glass bowl, and microwave at high 30 sec. or until warm and fragrant
Dip chicken in garlic oil; dredge in breadcrumb mixture. Coat preheated baking sheet with cooking spray, and place chicken on pan. Coat chicken with cooking spray
Bake at 425 for 25 min. or until done and golden. Serve with pasta sauce.
