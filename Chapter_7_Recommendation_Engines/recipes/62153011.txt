Title:
Yummy Pomegranate Vinegarette Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Olive Oil
1 Cup Pomegranate Juice
4 T white sugar
1 Cup Rice Vinegar
1 1/4 tsp chopped garlic
2 T chopped shallots
1 tsp white pepper
Kosher Salt to taste

Directions:
Mix Ingredients in blender until smooth and enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
This tangy dressing goes great on any salad.  Its addictive!!
