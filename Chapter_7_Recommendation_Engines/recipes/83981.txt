Title:
Chipotle Shrimp & Pineapple Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976676.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Lawry's® Baja Chipotle Marinade With Lime Juice, divided
1 1/2 pounds uncooked large shrimp (21 - 25 per pound), peeled and deveined
1 (16 ounce) can pineapple chunks in natural juice, drained or fresh pineapple chunks
1 medium red onion, cut into chunks
2 bell peppers, cut into chunks

Directions:
In Ziploc(R) bag, combine 3/4 cup Marinade and shrimp.  Marinate in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
Remove shrimp; discard used marinade.  On skewers, alternate shrimp with pineapple, onions and peppers.  Grill kabobs over medium heat, brushing with remaining Marinade, until shrimp just turn pink, about 4 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Juicy marinated shrimp are skewered with onion, pineapple, and bell pepper, and grilled to delicious perfection.
