Title:
Reyes Rice Especial Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
half a medium onion, chopped
2 cups rice
4 cups water
16 oz tomato sauce
1/2 cup fresh, chopped cilantro
1 tablespoon garlic powder
2 teaspoons chili powder
2 tablespoons chicken bouillon powder
salt to taste

Directions:
Soften the chopped onions in the butter in a pan over medium heat.
Add rice and brown.
Pour rice and onion mixture into a large pot, stir in all remaining ingredients.
Bring to a boil, cover and simmer on low for 30 minutes.
