Title:
Oh My Gosh Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413463.jpg
Ingredients:
1 skinless boneless chicken breast per serving
Italian dressing (for marinating)
2 tbsp barbecue sauce
2 slices smoked or pepper bacon
Equal parts Jack, Cheddar and Swiss cheeses shredded
1 small chopped Roma tomato
2 tbsp chopped red onion
Chopped scallions for color and taste

Directions:
Marinate chicken in italian dressing, ideally overnight.
Preheat oven to 400. In shallow baking dish, cover chicken breasts liberally with barbecue sauce and bake for 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, cook bacon to crisp, and set aside. Chop tomatoes and scallions and set these aside.
Remove chicken from oven, place 2 pieces of bacon on each breast. Top liberally with cheese, tomato, then onions. Place under broiler until cheese melts thoroughly. Sprinkle with scallions.
Serve and enjoy!
