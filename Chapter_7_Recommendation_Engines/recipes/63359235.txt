Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. apples sliced but not peeled
3/4 c. packed light brown sugar
1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. old fashioned oats
1/3 c. butter softened
3/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 c. water or apple juice

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Spread apples in an 8 inch square pan
In a medium bowl combine sugar, flour, oats cinnamon.
Using your fingers blend in butter.  Leave lumpy.
Sprinkle over apples.
Add water or juice
Bake 39 minutes or until topping is golden and liquid is bubbly.  Apples should be tender when pierced with a fork.
