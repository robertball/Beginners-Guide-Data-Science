Title:
Double Chocolate Mint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4c. butter, softened
1/2c. white sugar
1/2c. brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. mint extract
1 1/2c. flour
1/4c. baking cocoa
1tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1c. chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cream together the butter and sugars in a large mixing bowl. Add the rest of ingredients and mix until combined. Drop by spoonfuls onto cookie sheets and bake 10-12 minutes
