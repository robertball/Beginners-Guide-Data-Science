Title:
Raw Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 organic zucchinis, ends cut off
1 organic red bell pepper, thinly sliced
2 organic carrots
1/4 organic red cabbage, thinly sliced
1/2 cup bean sprouts
Sauce
3/4 cup raw almond butter
2 organic oranges, juiced
1 Tbs fresh ginger, minced
1 Tbs Nama Shoyu (Braggs Aminos work too)
1 Tbs unpasturized Miso
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tbs raw organic honey
1/4 tsp organic cayenne

Directions:
1. Peel the zucchinis lengthwise with a carrot peeler.These will create long, thin strips that can serve as your "noodles". Place on individual plates and set aside.
2. Peel the carrots lengthwise as well.
3. Place the carrot, peppers, bean sprouts and cabbage in a large bowl.
4. In a seperate bowl, wisk 'sauce' ingredients together until blended.
5. Pour 1/2 of the sauce mix in the carrot and veggie bowl and mix well.
6. Place this mixture atop each plate of zucchini and top with remaining sauce.
