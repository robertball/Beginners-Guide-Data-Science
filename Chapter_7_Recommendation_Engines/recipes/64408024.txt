Title:
Zuccini Boats with Ricotta Basil Mousse (2 points plus each) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2268987.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large zucchini
1 Cup of light ricotta cheese
1/4 Cup of light grated Parmesan cheese
1 Cup of loosely packed basil leaves, chopped
1/2 Cup of loosely packed flat leaf parsley, chopped
1 Tbsp lemon juice
2 Tbsp hot water
Salt and Pepper to taste
2Tbsp light grated Parmesan

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450
Cut zucchini lengthwise and scoop out seeds
Mix all other ingredients together
Split cheese mixture between 6 zucchini halves
Sprinkle with 2Tbsp light grated Parmesan
Bake for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
This recipe is 11 WW points plus for the whole thing.  2 points per zucchini boat.
