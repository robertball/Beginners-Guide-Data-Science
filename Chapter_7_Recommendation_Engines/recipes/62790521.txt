Title:
Hot Turkey Salad Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced cooked turkey
2 celery ribs, diced
1 small onion, diced
3/4 cup Kraft Cracked pepper mayonnaise
6 hamburger buns, split
1/4 cup grated cheese

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the turkey, celery, onion, mayonnaise. Spoon into buns, top with cheese. Wrap each in foil. Bake at 400 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until heated through.
