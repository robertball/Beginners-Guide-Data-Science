Title:
Slammin' Smoked Salmon 'n Bacon B-fast Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 light English muffin
1 oz. smoked salmon, roughly chopped
1 slice extra-lean turkey bacon
2 T fat-free cream cheese
1 T chopped red onion
1 T chopped cucumber
1 slice tomato

Directions:
Cook bacon slice according to package directions, either in the microwave or in a pan sprayed with nonstick spray. Once cool, chop the bacon into small pieces.
In a small dish, combine cream cheese with smoked salmon, chopped bacon, onion, and cucumber until mixed well.
Heat or toast muffin halves.
Spread cream cheese mixture over half of the English muffin. Top with the tomato slice.
Finish it with the other muffin half, pressing down firmly. Your sandwich is ready!
