Title:
Coquilles St. Jacques Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. scallops
1/4 C. dry white wine
1/2 lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 bay leaf
2 TBS. butter
1 heaping TBS. flour
1/2 C. real mayonnaise
1/2 C. milk
1 tsp. dried parsley
1/2 tsp. fresh ground pepper
2 TBS. dry sherry
1 C. parmesan/garlic croutons
1/2 C. shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Combine scallops and mushrooms with wine and bay leaf in a large frying pan.  Bring to a boil.  Cover and simmer for 2-3 minutes.  Remove pan from heat.
Melt butter in a saucepan.  Blend in flour, stirring until smooth.  Add milk and mayonnaise, cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens.  Add parsley, pepper and sherry.
Stir scallop mixture into cream sauce.  Spoon mixture into 4 oven safe ramekins or an 11x7 glass baking dish.
Top with croutons and shredded cheese.
Cover with foil and bake for 15 min. Remove foil and broil approx 5 min. or until the top browns.

Scallops and mushrooms in a delicious white sauce topped with croutons and cheese.
