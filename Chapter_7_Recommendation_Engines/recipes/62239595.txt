Title:
Peanut Butter & Banana Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/326439.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup butter, softened
2 large eggs
2 ripe bananas, mashed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F and grease some muffin tins.
In a small bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder and salt.
In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until fluffy.  Beat in the peanut butter, then add in the eggs one at a time, followed by the bananas.
With the mixer on low speed, add in the flour mixture until just combined.
Evenly distribute the batter into 16 muffin cups.
Bake for 16-18 minutes, until the edges are lightly browned.
