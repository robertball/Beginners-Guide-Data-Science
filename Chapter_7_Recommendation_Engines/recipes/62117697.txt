Title:
Honey/Ginger Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 pounds fresh green beans, rinsed and trimmed
1 C. honey
3/4 C. soy sauce (reduced sodium is best)
1 Tablespoon garlic-chopped or minced
1 Teaspoon ginger-chopped or minced

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil.  Add green beans and cook for five minutes, or until crisp tender.  Drain and rinse in cold water.
In a large saute pan on medium heat, add soy sauce.  Stir in garlic and ginger.  Add honey and stir until honey is dissolved. Add green beans and toss to coat, simmering on lower heat until desired tenderness.

Submitter: 
JuliaC
Description: 
Fresh green beans flavored with honey, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.
