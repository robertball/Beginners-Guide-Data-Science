Title:
Honey Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. chicken - cut up
1/4 cup melted butter
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup mustard
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. curry powder

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees.
Wash and dry chicken - set aside
Mix remaining ingredients in medium bowl.
Roll chicken in mixture and place meaty side up in casserole pan.
Bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 1 hour, basting occasionally.
