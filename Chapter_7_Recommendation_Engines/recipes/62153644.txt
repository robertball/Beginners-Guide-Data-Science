Title:
Penne A La Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 thin slices prosuitto ham (cut into strips)
1 can diced tomatoes
1 pint 10% cream
1/2 cup vodka
2 cloves garlic
1/2 teaspoon oregano
Pepper to taste
1/4 teaspoon crushed chili peppers (more or less depending on the heat you want)
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup fresh romano cheese, grated.
5 1/2 cup penne

Directions:
Combine olive oil, prossuito, garlic, pepper, oregano, and peppers in a large saucepan, and stir fry for 5 minutes
Add tomatoes, vodka and simmer for 5 minutes. Stir in cream and cheese.
Cook penne until al dente, and pour into a casserole dish. Add sauce and serve!
