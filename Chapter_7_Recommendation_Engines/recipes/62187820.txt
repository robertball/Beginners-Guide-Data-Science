Title:
Cheesey Zucchini -Veggie Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups zucchini, diced
1 cup yellow squash
1 onion, chopped
1/2 cup red bell pepper,diced
4 eggs, beaten
1 cup buttermilk baking mix(bisquik)
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 10x6 inch pan or a 12 inch pie plate.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine zucchini,squash,bell pepper onion, eggs, buttermilk baking mix, vegetable oil,sour cream, Parmesan cheese, marjoram, parsley,salt and pepper; mix well. Spread into the prepared baking dish.
Bake for 30 minutes, or until lightly brown.
