Title:
Morning Power Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour (we use whole wheat, but you can also use AP)
3/4 c ground flax seed
3/4 c oat bran
3/4 c honey
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 tsp ground cinnamon
3/4 c milk
2 eggs, slightly beaten
2 tbsp applesauce (replaces oil)
2 cups shredded carrots (use your food processor or do a fine chop by hand)
2 apples, peeled and shredded
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup other dried fruits as desired

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together first. Then add milk, eggs and applesauce. Finally, shred and fold in all fruit and dried fruit.
Grease tins and bake at 350 for 20 mins.

Submitter:

Description: 
Power muffins are so good they will replace your morning pastry and actually be good for you!
