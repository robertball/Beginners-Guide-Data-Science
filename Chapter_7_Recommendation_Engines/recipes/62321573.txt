Title:
Smoked Salmon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Cream Cheese
1/4 lb. Hot Smoked Salmon, finely diced or flaked
1 T. Fresh chives,finely chopped.
4 Scallions, thinly sliced
1 T. Worcestershire Sauce
1 t. finely minced garlic
2 T. Roughly chopped parsley

Directions:
Here comes the hard part: mix it all together!  Don't be afraid to over mix it. Serve with crackers or crudite

Submitter: 
wsf
Description: 
Fish such as trout, salmon, mackerel, or even eel, is transformed by the smoking process.  In this case, we take the wonder of smoked salmon and turn it into a luscious dip for crudite, crackers, etc.  Feel free to substitute the smoked salmon for your favourite smoked fish products!
