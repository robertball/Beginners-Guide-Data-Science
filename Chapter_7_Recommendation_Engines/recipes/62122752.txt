Title:
Chocolate Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C Smoothe Peanut Butter
1 T plus 1 t honey
2 C Rice Cereal
1 Pkg Sugar & Fat Free Chocolate Pudding (not instant)
2 C Skim Milk

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients and press into a 9in pie plate.  Freeze 1/2 hour.  Prepare 1 envelope (serves 4) sugar free chocolate pudding mix according to directions.  Allow to cool.  Pour into pie plate.  Refrigerate for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Lisa
Description: 
This is a low calorie, low fat but big on taste recipe.
