Title:
Beth's no-bake rice pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt. milk--1% works well
1/2 c. white rice,uncooked
3-5 T. sugar
2 T. butter
1/4+ raisins (optional)
Pinch of salt
2 eggs
1 tsp. Vanilla
Cinnamon

Directions:
Mix milk, rice, sugar, butter, raisins (if using) and salt in the top of a double boiler. Set over boiling water and cook for 1 hour.  After an hour, beat eggs in a small bowl and add a small amount of the hot, cooked pudding and mix well. Then add the gg mixture to the rest of the pudding and mix well. Add vanilla. Sprinkle top with cinnamon to taste and chill several hours.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
A family favorite, this creamy pudding  reminds me of some wonderful rich rice pudding that I enjoyed at a Greek restaurant in Ann Arbor, MI back in the 60s.
