Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can tuna
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can vegetables (can be mixed, corn, peas, whatever. Doesn't matter, your choice!)
2 cups egg noodles
1 can of milk (use the soup can)
crushed rippled chips
shredded cheese

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2) Boil noodles until cooked, drain.
3) In a bowl, mix all ingredients except chips and cheese. Pour into a casserole dish. Sprinkle with chips and cheese.
4) Bake for 30 minutes.
