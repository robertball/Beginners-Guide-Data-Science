Title:
pot roast with sour cream gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. chuck roast
1 tbs. vegetable oil
1 can beef broth
1 cup water
1 package Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing and Seasoning Mix
1 tbs. minced onion (dry is okay)
1/2 tbs. minced garlic
1 tbs. Worcestershire sauce
2 potatoes, peeled and quartered
1/2 cup baby carrots
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped celery
1 cup sour cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325. In a skillet, heat the oil and brown the chuck roast on both sides.
In an deep baking dish, stir together the broth, water, Hidden Valley Mix, onion, garlic and Worcestershire sauce. Place the chuck roast in the baking dish. Cover and cook for 2 hours. Add the vegetables and cook for or until the meat is tender. Transfer the meat and vegetables to a serving platter, remove excess fat from the meat. In a saucepan, pour in the liquid from the meat and veggies. Reduce the liquid by 75%. Add the sour cream. Stir and adjust the seasonings. Pour the sauce over the meat and vegetables and serve.
