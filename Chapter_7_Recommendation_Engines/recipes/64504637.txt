Title:
U.S 31  Bar B Que Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lbs Cabbage - chopped
1 onion chopped
1 pint sweet pickle relish
3/4 cup catsup
1/2 cup yellow mustard
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
3/4 cup white sugar
1 oz pimentos
2 oz horseradish

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a large pot.
Bring slowly to a boil, stirring frequently
Cool
Refrigerate  at least 48 hours.
Put ~ 2oz on pulled pork, bratwurst, hot doges, etc.

Description: 
A wonderful relish for pork, bratwurst, hot dogs, etc.
