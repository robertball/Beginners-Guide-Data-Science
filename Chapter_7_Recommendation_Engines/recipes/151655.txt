Title:
Italian Chicken and Peppers for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977829.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
4 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
4 medium green peppers, cut into strips
4 medium onions, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
7 1/2 cups PregoÂ® Fresh Mushroom Italian Sauce
Hot cooked spaghetti

Directions:
Heat oil in saucepot. Add chicken and cook until browned and done, stirring often.
Add peppers, onions and garlic and cook until tender. Add pasta sauce and heat through. Serve over spaghetti.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Sauteed chicken, pepper, onion and garlic are added to perfectly seasoned pasta sauce and served over tender spaghetti.
