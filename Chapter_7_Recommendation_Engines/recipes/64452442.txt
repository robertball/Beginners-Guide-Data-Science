Title:
Black Binder Mostaccioli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16-oz package mostaccioli
6 drops yellow food coloring
1/2 C neutral-flavored oil (canola/vegetable, etc.)
1 cucumber, finely diced
1 small onion, chopped
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp monosodium glutamate
1 Tbsp dried parsley flakes
1/2 Tbsp yellow mustard
1 C sugar
1 1/2 C vinegar

Directions:
Cook pasta according to box instructions, adding food coloring to the cooking water.
Rinse and drain pasta.
In a large bowl, add pasta and oil, toss to coat.
Add remaining ingredients to a blender, and blend until smooth.
Toss pasta with the cucumber/onion dressing, and refrigerate overnight.
Stir before serving.
