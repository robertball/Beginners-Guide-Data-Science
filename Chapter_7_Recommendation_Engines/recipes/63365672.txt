Title:
Polynesian Chicken Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade:
1/4 C soy sauce
2 Tbl lemon juice
2 cloves minced garlic
1 tsp fresh (1/8 tsp ground) ginger
1/8 tsp dry mustard (1/8 Tbl wet)
Skewers:
1 lb chicken
red and green sweet peppers
pineapple chunks
bamboo skewers

Directions:
Marinate the chicken overnight. String chicken, bell peppers, and pineapple onto skewers. Grill or broil (about 7 min) and serve hot.
