Title:
Apple and Pear Compote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp honey
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
1/8 tsp ground ginger
3 apples peeled, pared, chopped
3l water
3 pears pared, chopped

Directions:
in large saucepan, combine water, honey, vanilla, and spices.
cook over high heat until mixture boils.
add apples.
reduce heat and cook about ten minutes
add pears
cook until fruit is tender.
serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Pots
Description: 
Russian style drink that can be served hot or cold.
