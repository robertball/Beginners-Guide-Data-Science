Title:
Tortellini Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871666.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large package refrigerated tortellini
2 pints grape or cherry tomatoes
1/2 cup pesto

Directions:
Cook the tortellini according to package directions. Drain immediately and rinse with cold water. Coat the tortellini and tomatoes with the pesto in a bowl. Skewer one tortellini and one tomato on a toothpick. Set on tray. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
