Title:
Romaine Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3  cup	Spinach and Romaine lettuce leaves 40/60 blend
2  oz	Portabello Mushroom Sliced
5  ea	Kalamata OlivesCarrots Diced
1  oz	Red Onion
1  oz	Sun Dried Tomato / 24 hr
2  ea	Croutons points
10 ea	Croutons
2  floz	Balsamic Herb Vinaigrette
.5 oz	Parmesan Grated

Directions:
Gently toss Spinach, Romaine, Onion, Tomato, Mushroom, Olive and Croutons together with Balsamic Dressing
Place salad mixture in large pasta bowl top with Parmesan and Crouton points
