Title:
Aunt Mary's Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
3 tablespoons cocoa
1 cup evaporated milk
6 tablespoons butter
1 jar marshmallow creme
3 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs

Directions:
In a large, non-stick saucepan, combine the sugar, cocoa and evaporated milk. While stirring constantly, bring mixture to a boil. Boil, stirring constantly for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the butter and marshmallow creme. Working quickly, stir in the graham cracker crumbs. Drop by spoonful onto wax paper. Allow to sit until firm. Store in airtight containers.

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
This was my great-grandma's sister's recipe. It's an oldie, but a goodie!
