Title:
Mamaw Bea's Cornbread Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz pkg yellow cornbread mix
1/3 c butter or margarine
1/2 c chopped celery
1/2 c copped onion
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 - 1 t poultry seasoning
1/2 t pepper
1-1/4 c chicken or turkey broth

Directions:
1. Prepare cornbread as directed.  Cool 15 minutes and crumble to make 3 cups
2. Heat oven to 350.  Grease 8" square pan.  Melt butter in med skillet over med heat.  Add Celery and onion; cook until veggies are tender, stirring occasionally.
3. In lge bowl, combine veggies, crumbled cornbread and all remaining ingredients.  Spoon into greased dish.
4. Bake for 30-35 min until golden brown.
