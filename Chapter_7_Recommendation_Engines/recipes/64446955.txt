Title:
All Beef Sausage Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp sage
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1 tbsp frozen lard, grated (optional, only for very lean ground beef)

Directions:
Mix all together in a bowl with a fork, using a fluffing motion that will not compress the meat.
Form into patties and fry on each side until browned and cooked through.

Submitter: 
Bestfoodist
Description: 
This is our standard breakfast sausage. we have issues with pork so it makes a great stand-in.
