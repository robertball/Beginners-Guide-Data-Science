Title:
Picante Black Bean Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979368.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup Pace® Picante Sauce
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 teaspoon grated lime zest
Pepperidge Farm® Baked Naturals™ Toasted Wheat Wheat Crisps

Directions:
Place the beans, picante sauce, lime juice and lime zest into a food processor or blender.  Cover and process until the mixture is smooth and creamy.  Serve the bean mixture with the crisps for dipping.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Unexpected guests stop in? Why not whip up a batch of this flavorful ‘hummus' that has only four ingredients but loads of great flavor.
