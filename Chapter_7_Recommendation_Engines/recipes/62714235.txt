Title:
Oriental Chicken Salad Like Applebee's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/617005.jpg
Ingredients:
4 breaded chicken breast fillets
1 romaine heart
1 1/2 c. coleslaw (finely shredded cabbage)
1/3. c. toasted sliced almonds
1/2 c. chow mein noodles
Oriental Vinaigrette (elsewhere on a/r site)

Directions:
Cook chicken fillets as directed (these come pre-breaded in the frozen food section).
Layer romaine, then cabbage, almonds, chicken and dressing.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Crispy chunks of chicken breast on a bed of romaine topped with finely shredded cabbage, chow mein noodles and toasted sliced almonds.  Serve with Oriental Vinaigrette Dressing found elsewhere on this a/r site.
