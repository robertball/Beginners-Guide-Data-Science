Title:
SUPER FAST DIY Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3685136.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Large Pasture Raised Egg (if  you can't find one, or don't have one, coddle your egg for one minute)
Dash Cayenne Pepper
1/2 tsp. Dry Mustard
1/4 tsp. Celtic Sea Salt
1 Tbsp. Fresh Squeezed Lemon Juice or 1-2 drops Lemon Essential Oil
1 Tbsp. White Vinegar
1 c. of either: Avocado oil, Mac nut oil, or EVOO

Directions:
In a narrow, tall sided container add all your ingredients.
With your stick blender set flatly on the bottom of the container, blend on high until all ingredients are incorporated and it has become thick.
Transfer mayonnaise to a small jar with cover.
Store refrigerated for up to 14 days.

Submitter: 
Lori Rowley-Sipple
Description: 
I've been making my own mayo for years now, and I won't go back to store bought. If you are allergic to eggs, try this with a flax egg replacement.
