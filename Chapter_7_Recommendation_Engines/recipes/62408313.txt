Title:
Peanutbutter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter
2 c brown sugar
5 oz can evaporated milk
5 oz marshmallow creme
3 1/2 c powdered sugar
1 1/2 c peanutbutter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Heat butter, brown sugar, & evaporated milk. Bring to boil for about 5-7 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add peanutbutter & vanilla. Stir.
In a large mixing bowl, beat powered sugar with marshmallow creme & hot mixture. Beat on low speed until even & creamy. Plop into a nonstick 9x13 pan & smooth out. Let cool (my daughter like to eat this when it's still soft & warm.) Cut into sqaures.

Quick & easy. Yummy & creamy. My daughter's favorit.
