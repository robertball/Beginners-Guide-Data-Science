Title:
Dijon Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons dijon mustard
1 1/2  teaspoons dried dillweed
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2 pounds new red potatoes cooked with skin on and quartered
1 cup sliced radishes (after slicing quarter them)
1/2 cup sliced green onoins

Directions:
Cook potatoes 10  minutes, drain and cool.Combine mayonnaise, mustard, dillweed, salt and pepper. Stir in remaining ingredients cover and chill, at least a few hours or overnight. *You may want to add more mayonnaise to your taste
