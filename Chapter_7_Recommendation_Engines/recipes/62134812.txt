Title:
TexMex Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can refried beans
1 pint sour cream
1 pkg. taco seasoning
2 avocatos (Hass) 1/4 diced
1 lime (fresh squeezed)
3 tomatoes 1/4 diced
1 red onion 1/4 diced
1 Jalp. 1/4 diced
1 cup cilantro chopped
1 cup chedder/Jack chz (50/50 blend)

Directions:
First take the refried beans spread on the bottem of a pizza pan; then mix the sour cream and taco seasoning together and spread on top of beans. Then cut and dice avocotos and toss with squeezed lime juice and place on top of sour cream mixture. thenm dice tomatos,onions, jalp., and place on top of avocotos. then chp cilantro and sprinkle on top. then add chedder chz. blend. serve with your favorite tortilla chips, enjoy..

Chef Scott

A Savory blend of fresh vegitables, spice and crisp tortillia chips.
