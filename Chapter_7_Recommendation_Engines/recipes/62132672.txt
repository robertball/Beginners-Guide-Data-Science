Title:
Spicy Turkey Salad Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg flour tortillas
8 oz. shredded cheese (I use a blend made for tex/mex)
4 oz shredded or chopped lettuce or salad mix
Spicy turkey salad
1.5 # chopped/shredded turkey
1 10oz can diced tomatoes w/green chiles
2 T minced onion
2 med jalapenos, chopped
2/3 C mayonnaise (Miracle Whip)
1/2 t pepper
1/2 t chili powder

Directions:
Make turkey salad by combining all ingredients in bowl, mix thoroughly.
warm tortillas to soften
spread 2 T turkey salad on one side of tortilla
top with lettuce and cheese
roll up like a burrito
enjoy!

Submitter: 
PRCOOK
Description: 
I was looking for an easy turkey salad recipe to go with the ingredients I had on hand, but couldn't find one I liked.  So quite naturally, I came up with my own.  These have just a touch of spice to them.  I found them both, easy and good.
