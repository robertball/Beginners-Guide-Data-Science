Title:
Tzatziki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6710431.jpg
Ingredients:
2 or 3 cups (as in containers sold at store) of Griek yogurt (or bulgarian, as long as it's thick yogurt).
2 minced toes of Garlic.
1 tbls. Mayo
1 tbls. Olive Oil
1/2 grated cucumber
1/4 tsp. Mint
1/8 tsp. Dill
1/8 tsp. Parsley
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Pepper and salt to taste

Directions:
In bowl:
Yogurt, oil, mayo, oil and spices.
Grate the cucumber and drain completely.
Add cucumber to yogurt mix.
Chill for at least 1 hr. to thicken.
Take out of fridge 20 minutes before to return to room temp.
