Title:
Grilled Pork Tenderloin with Fried Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs pork tenderloin
Garlic salt
pepper
3 T butter
3-4 Granny Smith apples, sliced
1/3 c sugar

Directions:
Heat grill. Season pork generously with garlic salt & pepper. Cook pork 6-7 min; turning twice on each side.
Remove; cover with foil. Let sit 5 min; then slice.
Apples: Melt butter in med skillet; med heat. Add apples; cover. Cook 5 min.
Turn and sprinkle with 1/3 c sugar. Reduce heat to low. Cover; cook 5 min.
Uncover; cook 2 to 3 min, or until apples are lightly browned on bottom.
Serve pork topped with fried apples.
