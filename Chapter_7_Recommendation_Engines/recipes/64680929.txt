Title:
Bacon Balsamic Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4915954.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bags of frozen brussel sprouts
1 lb of bacon
1 T butter
salt
pepper
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup balsamic
1 T butter
pinch of cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a microwave safe dish add brussel sprouts and 1 tablespoon of butter. Cook until butter is melted and sprouts are mostly thawed. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, toss together.
Pre-heat oven to 450*
Cut the larger sprouts in 1/2 to help with even cooking.
Cut the bacon into 3rds, and then cut those pieces in 1/2 the long way.
Wrap the sprouts with bacon, put the seam side down on a cookie sheet lined with parchment.
Bake 450 for 30 min.
While that is baking place the balsamic, brown sugar, and 1 T of butter in sauce pan and reduce by 1/2.
Once the bacon is crispy and sprouts are almost burnt take out of the oven and pour sauce over and serve.
