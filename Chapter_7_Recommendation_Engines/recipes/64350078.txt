Title:
Fabulous Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1/4 cups sugar
1/4 cup butter
1 cup marshmallow creme (or 16 large marshmallows)
3/4 cup evaporated milk
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Mix sugar, butter, marshmallows, evaporated milk and salt  in a heavy 2 qt saucepan.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, to a boil.  Boil and stir 5 minutes.
Take off heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted.  Add vanilla and walnuts.
Spread in a buttered 8 x 8 pan and cool.
