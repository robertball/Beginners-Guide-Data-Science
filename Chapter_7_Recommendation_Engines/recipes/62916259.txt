Title:
Brilliant Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups wheat flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. cinnamon
1 t. vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1/8 cup butter, melted
1/8 cup of applesauce
3 bananas, mashed
handful of pecans

Directions:
Grease and flour two 7x3 inch loaf pans. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
THENâ€¦In one bowl, whisk together flour, soda, baking powder, cinnamon, salt, and sugar. Mix in slightly beaten eggs, melted butter, applesauce and mashed bananas. Stir in some pecans. Pour into prepared pans. Sprinkle the rest of pecans on top of batter.
THEN...Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes, or until a wooden toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
