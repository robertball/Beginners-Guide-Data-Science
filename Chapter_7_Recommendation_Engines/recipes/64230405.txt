Title:
artichoke and spinanch dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cloves of Garlic Chopped
•    1/2 Large White Onion Chopped
•    A bundle of Organic Spinach
•    A large Jar of Organic Artichokes
•    1 cup of Raw Cashews Soaked
•    Purified Water
•    Himalayan Salt
•    Ground Pepper
•    Organic Garlic Powder
•    Organic Onion Powder

Directions:
Directions:
Sauté about 3 cloves of garlic and ½ of the onion with salt.  Add the artichokes and spinach and sauté until wilted.  Place the soaked cashews in a Vitamix or Food Processor with about 1/3 cup of water (add more if needed), a dash of all the spices and 2 cloves of garlic.  Blend until smooth, you want the texture to be similar to peanut butter.  Place the sauté and cashew cream in a bake dish and place in the oven for 25 minutes on 375 degrees.
