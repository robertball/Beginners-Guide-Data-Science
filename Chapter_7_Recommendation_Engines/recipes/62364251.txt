Title:
Slow cooker Thai Pork with Red Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c chicken broth
1 14 oz can coconut milk
1 onion, thinly sliced
1 red bell pepper, cored and sliced lengthwise
1 pkg of white mushrooms (quartered)
2 TB red curry paste
3 TB veg oil
2.5 lbs of boneless pork butt/shoulder cubed
1 TB kosher salt
1/2 t black pepper
1 c chopped cilantro

Directions:
stir 3/4 c broth, coconut milk, onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, and curry paste into slow cooker.
Heat oil in skillet and brown cubed pork for 6 minutes.  Transfer pork to slow cooker.  Pour remaining broth in skillet and incorporate browned bits and put all into slow cooker.  cover and cook for 5 hours on high heat.  Skim grease off.  Serve over rice. Sprinkle with cilantro
