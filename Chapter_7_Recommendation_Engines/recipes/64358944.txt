Title:
Chicken Caesar Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup  creamy Caesar salad dressing
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3 cups cubed cooked chicken breast (may sub canned chicken)
2 cups torn romaine
6 whole wheat tortillas (8 inches), room temperature

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the dressing and cheese.
Add the chicken, romaine and croutons.
Spoon 2/3 cup chicken mixture down the center of each tortilla; roll up.

Submitter: 
SST
Description: 
This is a quick and easy lunch. If it is more economical, use bags of caesar salad mix that comes with dressing included. If using raw chicken, add to your prep time. You can serve with chips, carrot sticks, or what you think is appropriate.
