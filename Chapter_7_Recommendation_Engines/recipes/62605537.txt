Title:
Hermits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Tab unsalted butter, room temp
2/3 C white sugar
3/4 C dark-brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
3 C all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp baking powder, cinnamon, cloves
1/2 tsp ginger, all-spice
1/4 C molasses mixed with 2 Tab warm water
1 C raisins
1 C chopped walnuts

Directions:
Cream butter with the two sugars, then beat in the eggs.  Toss flour, salt, baking powder, and spices.  Add to the butter-sugar mixture with the molasses.  When well mixed stir in nuts and raisins.  Bake at 350 for 15 min.
