Title:
Rosemary White Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. white beans, soaked and rinsed
1 Tbsp minced fresh rosemary
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 large or 2 small onions, chopped
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 15 oz can petite dice tomatoes
unsalted vegetable stock (enough to cover by an inch or so)
1 Tbsp kosher salt
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Place first five ingredients in slow cooker.  Add vegetable stock.  Cook on low 6-8 hours, until beans are softened, adding salt about half way through cooking time.  Stir in tomatoes and cook about 30 more minutes.  Season with pepper.
