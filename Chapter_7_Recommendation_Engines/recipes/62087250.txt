Title:
Hearty Tuscan Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 large carrot, peeled & chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
Salt to taste
Freshly ground black pepper
1 16 oz can diced tomatoes
4 cups chicken broth
1 cup beef broth
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried parsley
1/4 lb fresh green beans, stemmed and chopped into 1 inch pieces
1 16oz can lentils, rinsed and drained
3 cups raw whole wheat rotini
1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large soup pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
Add the onion, carrot and celery and cook for five minutes, or until vegetables begin to soften.
Add the garlic, season with salt and pepper and cook for two more minutes.
Add the tomatoes, chicken broth, beef broth and herbs, bring to a boil and turn down to a simmer.
Add the green beans and lentils and simmer for two minutes.
Add the pasta and simmer for 10 minutes or until pasta is cooked al dente.
Season with salt and pepper.
Serve in large bowls with shaved parmesan cheese on top.

Description: 
Delicious and healthy minestrone recipe - my husband cannot get enough of it!  Serve with crusty whole grain buns and a glass of red wine on a cold winter night!
