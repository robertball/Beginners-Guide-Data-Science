Title:
Robin's Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 1/2 cups salted peanuts
1 teaspoon butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking soda

Directions:
Butter a cookie sheet.
Combine sugar and corn syrup in a 2 quart glass bowl and microwave on high 2 minutes.
Stir in peanuts and microwave on high 2 minutes.
Stir in butter and vanilla and microwave for 2 minutes.
Stir in baking soda until light and foamy.
Pour onto cookie sheet and spread thin.
Cool completely and break into pieces and serve.
