Title:
Seafood Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1035387.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Butter
1 tsp Olive Oil
1 medium Onion (minced)
1/3 cup Flour
1 liter 18% Table Cream
1 cup sliced Tiny Tomatoes
2 cups Cream Corn
Kernels from 1 large freshly cooked Corn
2 medium potatoes cubed
1.5 cups Water (as needed)
1/3 cup Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
1 fillet fresh Haddock cut up in 1 inch pieces
2 cups uncooked de-veined large Shrimp
2 cups precooked Bar Clams* (with juice)
2 cups precooked Lobster* (with juice)
Sea Salt to taste
Corse fresh ground Pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter and oil add onion saut√©, add flour stirring constantly then add cream and mix well, add clam juice to thin sauce. Cook on low while stirring for about 10 minutes.
Add cheese stir until melted (a few minutes only), add cream corn, corn, tomatoes and potatoes. Salt and Pepper to taste
Add Clam, Haddock and cook about 5 minutes then add Shrimp, taste and add more pepper and salt to taste.
Add lobster and its juice.
