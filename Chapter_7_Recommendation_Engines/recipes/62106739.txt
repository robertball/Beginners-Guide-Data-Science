Title:
Almond Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 packages of Chicken Ramen noodles, crushed (save and set flavor packets aside)
3 green onions, chopped
1 carrot, grated
2/3 C rice vinegar
3/4 C vegetable oil
1/4 C sugar
1/2 C sliced almonds
10 oz can chicken

Directions:
Cook Ramen noodles in boiling water for three minutes.
Drain.
Run noodles under cool water.
Set aside.
Mix flavor packets, vinegar, vegetable oil, and sugar in large serving bowl.
Add green onion and carrot.
Fold in Ramen noodles, chicken and almonds.
Mix well.

A simple, tasty ramen noodle salad.
