Title:
Mom's Scalloped Potatoes & Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can	Cream of mushroom soup
½ Cup	Milk
4 Cups	Thinly sliced potatoes
½ Cup 	Chopped onion
1 Cup	Cheddar cheese, shredded
1-2 Cups Ham, diced

Directions:
Blend soup and milk, and a dash of pepper in a bowl.  In a 2 quart casserole dish layer potatoes, onion, ham and cheese.  Pour half of soup mix over top.  Repeat layers.  Add cheese to top layer after soup.  Dot with butter, sprinkle with paprika.  Cover and bake and 375 for 2 hours.  Uncover and bake for 15 minutes more.

Description:
