Title:
Cream of Coconut Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3752500.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sugar
2 tbs flour
1/4 tsp salt
6 tbs butter, melted
1 tsp coconut extract
3 eggs
8 oz cream of coconut (Coco Lopez or Coco Real)
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 cups shredded sweetened coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Grease and flour a 9" pie plate.
Combine sugar, flour and salt.
Add butter and coconut extract.
Add eggs 1 at a time. Mixing well after each addition.
Add cream of coconut and milk.
Add shredded coconut.
Pour into prepared pie plate.
Bake 45-50 minutes.
Chill before serving.
Note: Be sure to use Cream of Coconut not Coconut Cream. They are 2 different products.

Description: 
If you are a coconut lover like I am, you are going to love this super easy to make crustless pie.
