Title:
Breakfast/Lunch Barley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups cooked barley (various methods for cooking are in the footnotes)
1/4 cup vanilla or plain yogurt
1 large apple, chopped into 1/2 inch pieces
cinnamon to taste

Directions:
If the Barley has been in the refridgerator, microwave on half-power for 30 seconds, to take the chill off.
Mix yogurt into barley to coat (use more if needed)
Mix in the apple.
Sprinkle cinnamon on top and mix through.

Submitter: 
MK!
Description: 
Delicious, nutritious meal - great for breakfast or lunch. Any fruit can be used (you will  need 1/2-3/4c per person) and amounts can be adjusted for personal tastes.
