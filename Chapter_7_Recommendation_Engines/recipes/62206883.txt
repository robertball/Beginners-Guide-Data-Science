Title:
Howdy's Guacamole in 5 minutes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium sized avacados, peeled and pitted
1 4 oz can of diced green chiles
1 Tablespoon reconstituted lemon or lime juice
1 Teaspoon Garlic salt
1/2 Teaspoon salt
Optional:  add chopped onions and/or tomatoes to taste

Directions:
Mash the avocados in a small mixing bowl. Add the chiles, lemon or lime juice, garlic salt and salt and continue to blend the mixture.  When you have the texture you desire add the onions and/or tomatoes and blend again.

Submitter: 
Suzanne W
Description: 
This recipe was given to us about 35 years ago and is one we always go back to for 100% satisfaction of our guests.  It goes great with enchiladas or just as an appetizer with cocktails.
