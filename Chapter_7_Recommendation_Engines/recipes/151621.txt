Title:
Golden Mushroom Pork & Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981898.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Golden Mushroom Soup
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
8 boneless pork chops, 3/4-inch thick
4 large Granny Smith apples, sliced
2 large onions, sliced

Directions:
Mix soup, water, sugar, Worcestershire and thyme in slow cooker.  Add pork, apples and onions.
Cover and cook on LOW 8 to 9 hours or until done.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Savory pork chops simmer in the slow cooker with tart apples and onion in a sweet 'n' sour sauce.
