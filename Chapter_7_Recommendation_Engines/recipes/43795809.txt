Title:
Wanda's Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup sugar
3/4 cup milk
2 eggs, beaten

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
Add milk and eggs.
Mix well and cook over medium heat.

Submitter: 
CLField
Description: 
A light crepe-like pancake. a family favourite.
