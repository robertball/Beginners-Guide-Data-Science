Title:
Lemony Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
3 garlic cloves
3 anchovies
1/2 fresh lemon juice (2 lemons)
1 tsp. dijon mustard
1 tsp. dry mustard
6 Tbsp. parmesan cheese
1- 1-1/4 C. EXTRA LIGHT extra virgin olive oil
1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
Salad:
1 head Romaine lettuce
Croutons

Directions:
Blend all dressing ingredients in a blender/cuisinart until creamy. Toss amount to taste into bowl of torn romaine.  Top with croutons.  There will be left over dressing.

Submitter: 
Nicole73
Description: 
This is a great caesar dressing from a bed & breakfast in St. Simon's Is., Georgia.  It is the best I've had. Great w/ a grilled chicken breast on top.  Make sure to use the EXTRA LIGHT, extra virgin olive oil.  Enjoy!
