Title:
The Best Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
1 c. sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
2 eggs
1/2 c. milk
2 c. flour
2 c. chocolate chips
Coarse sugar or brown sugar for topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.  Lightly grease or line muffin pan.  In a large bowl, beat the butter, sugar, baking powder, salt, vanilla and cinnamon until fluffy.  Beat in eggs, then stir in milk.  Mix in the flour and then add chocolate chips.  Divide batter evenly among the muffin cups.  Sprinkle with coarse sugar.  Bake for 30 minutes or until tester comes out clean.
