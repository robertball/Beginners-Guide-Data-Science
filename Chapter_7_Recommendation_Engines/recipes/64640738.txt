Title:
George's Hawaiian Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5500263.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups		Pineapple Juice
3 oz		Cider Vinegar
2/3 cup		Sugar
7 oz		Soy Sauce
½ tsp		Ginger Powder
½ tsp		Garlic Powder

Directions:
Combine vinegar, garlic powder and ginger powder in small sauce pan and heat to 120 degrees.  Do not boil.
Slowly stir in sugar and dissolve thoroughly.
Remove from heat and mix in pineapple juice and soy sauce. Blend on low setting for 2 minutes.
Makes approximately 1 quart.
Marinate steaks in ziploc storage bags and refrigerate for 4-12 hours flipping steaks ½ way through (can be marinated for as long as 2-3 days). Skirt or ribeye steaks are preferred. Dispose of used marinade once ready to grill steaks.
