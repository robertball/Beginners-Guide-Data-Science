Title:
Tastes-Like-Ice-Cream Kale Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups filtered water
1/2 cup raw, unsalted cashews, soaked
1 cup torn-up curly green kale leaves (1 or 2 large leaves with stalk removed), plus more to taste
2 sliced medium ripe bananas
1/4 cup chopped pitted dates, soaked, or 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
1/2 teaspoon natural vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon minced ginger, plus more to taste
1 tablespoon flax oil (optional)
1 cup ice cubes, plus more as needed (you want this shake really cold)

Directions:
Throw all of the ingredients in your Vitamix and blast on high for 30 to 60 seconds, until smooth and creamy.

Description: 
This is the famous tastes like ice cream kale smoothie from The Blender Girl cookbook and The Blender Girl Smoothies app.
