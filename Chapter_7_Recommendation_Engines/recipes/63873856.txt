Title:
Roasted Pork Loin with Balsamic, Apricot, Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
salt and pepper
2 lb pork loin roast boneless
5-8 garlic cloves
1 onion
1/2 c dried apricots
2 c mushrooms
1 c red wine
3/4 c balsamic
3/4 c orange juice
3/4 c apple cider vinegar
3/4 c apple cider
(approx 4 c total liquid)
1 T cumin plus more
1/2 T curry
1/2 T cinnamon plus more
1/2 T sage
1/2 T thyme
1/2 T rosemary
1/2 T oregano
1 sprig rosemary
2-4 T brown sugar/agave nectar, to taste

Directions:
season pork loin with salt/pepper
sear pork loin (would not do this next time -- or use pork shoulder/butt, see below) and put in crock pot on low
put all other ingredients into slow cooker
cook on high 3 hours, low for another 1-2 hours
remove pork, let rest
put sauce in sauce pan and cook until reduced to taste/consistency desired
NOTES:  next time, use pork shoulder/butt; use less liquid and/or cook on LOW for longer period of time; adjust the liquid -- might be too acidic.
