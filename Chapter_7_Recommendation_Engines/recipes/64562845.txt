Title:
Turtles Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 ½ cups flour
3 tbsp. icing sugar
¾ cup butter or margarine
Caramel layer:
½ cup butter or margarine
½ can Eagle Brand condensed milk
224g Mackintosh toffee candies (4 Mack toffee bars)
100g pkg. chopped pecans (more is even better!!)
Cheesecake:
250 g cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 tbsp. lemon juice
500 ml whipping cream, whipped with some sugar & vanilla
Butterscotch sundae topping, optional

Directions:
Crust:
Mix flour, sugar and butter together. Press lightly into a greased 9” x 13” pan.
Bake @ 350°F x 15 - 20 minutes, until edges are browning a bit. Cool.
Caramel layer:
Melt together butter, Eagle Brand and toffee slowly (either on stove or in microwave).
Pour over cooled crust.
Sprinkle pecans evenly over caramel layer.
Cheesecake:
Beat cream cheese, sugar and lemon juice together to dissolve sugar.
Prepare whipped cream and fold into cream cheese mixture.
Spread over caramel layer.
Drizzle with butterscotch sundae topping (optional).
Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A large, very sweet & highly addictive dessert that easily feeds a crowd. Toffee & pecans, cream cheese & whipped cream star in this refrigerated winner.
