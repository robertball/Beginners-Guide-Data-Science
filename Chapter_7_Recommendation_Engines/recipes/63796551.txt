Title:
Awesome Killer Chili (Canning recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1053319.jpg
Ingredients:
5-6 lb ground beef
2 cup chopped onion
2-3 cloves garlic
6 cups canned tomatoes w/juice
1/2 cup chili powder
2 red chili peppers, finely chopped (I use jalapenos)
1 tsp cumin
2 qt canned pinto or kidney beans

Directions:
Brown beef, drain off grease
Add garlic and onion til soft
Add rest of ingredients. Bring to boil over med-hi heat.
Simmer 20 min if canning, 90 min if not canning.
Ladle hot chili into hot jars leaving 1 inch headspace. Remove air bubbles and adjust headspace if necessary.
Clean rims with paper towel moistened with vinegar. Center lids on jars and screw down bands.
Pressure can 75 min for pints 90 for qts at appropriate pounds pressure for your altitude.
Turn off heat, allow to return to 0 naturally.
Remove vent, wait 2 min. Remove lid, wait 10 min.
Remove jars to towel covered counter, cool and store. (remove bands before storing)

Kim Snyder
