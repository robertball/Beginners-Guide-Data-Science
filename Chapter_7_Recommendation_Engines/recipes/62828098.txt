Title:
Grandma's Apple Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/669443.jpg
Ingredients:
1 guart freshly brewed black tea, unscented
1 quart freshly boiled water
1 (8 ounce) apple of your choice, quartered, seeded
4-8 tbsp caster sugar or demerara

Directions:
Pour tea into large wide-rimmed cups or 1 pt soup bouls, filling them about half full. Dilute with freshly boiled water to a golden color, add 1 tbsp sugar per cup (2 tbsp if your apples are not very sweet).
Slice a quarter of an apple into each cup, stir and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Petrovna
Description: 
In Russia, the apple season begins on St. Ilia's day, August 2. Here is the sweet hot drink my grandma still makes for chilly autumn evenings.
