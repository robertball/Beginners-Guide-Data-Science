Title:
Spaghetti with Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 8 ounce cans tomato sauce,
2 15 ounce cans chopped tomatoes,
1 small can tomato paste,
1 1/2 lbs ground beef,
1/2 cup chopped onion,
1 clove garlic, crushed,
1 tsp. oregano,
1 Tbs. basil,
2 bay leaves,
2 tsp. sugar,
1 can chopped ripe olives,
Salt and pepper to taste,
16 ounces dry spaghetti noodles,

Directions:
Brown the hamburger in a large skillet with the onion until hamburger is no longer pink and onion is transluscent.
Add tomato products and spices.
Simmer for ten minutes.
Add chopped olives and simmer an additional ten minutes.
While sauce is simmering, bring 6 quarts of water to boil in a large pot.
Add a tsp of salt and spaghetti noodles.
Boil the noodles until soft when squeezed, but not mushy (about 9 minutes).
Drain noodles in a colander and rinse with warm water.
Serve spaghetti sauce over noodles and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
