Title:
Chinese Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head cabbage, finely shredded
3 green onions, chopped
1/2 c sliced almonds, toasted
1 pkg. ramen noodles, broken into pieces
Dressing
2 tbsp sugar
1/2 c veg. oil
3 tbsp rice vinegar

Directions:
1.In a large bowl, mix all salad ingredients.
2.In a small bowl, mix dressing ingredients.
3.Add dressing to salad and refrigerate at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
7woolfords
Description: 
I love this salad in the summer.  It is very light and refreshing, a nice change from the common coleslaw.
