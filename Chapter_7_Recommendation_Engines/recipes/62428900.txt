Title:
Raspberry Rush Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/449660.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana, peeled, chunked and frozen
1/2 cup mango, roughly chopped
1 cup fresh raspberries
1 cup plain yogurt
1 cup orange juice
1 tbsp honey (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
This tart raspberry smoothie is made different with the addition of mangos.  Add the optional honey if you'd prefer a sweeter smoothie.
