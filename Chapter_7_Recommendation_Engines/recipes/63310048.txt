Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875000.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 cups old fashion oats
1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup golden raisins

Directions:
1. In a large bowl, cream the shortening and sugars until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs and vanilla. Combine oats, flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture and mix well. Stir in walnuts and raisins.
2. Drop by tablespoons 2-in apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 375* for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Remove to wire racks.
