Title:
Golden Corral Seafood Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds imitation crab meat
1 cup chopped green onion
1 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup ranch salad dressing
1/2 cup heavy mayonnaise

Directions:
Separate (by hand) the pieces of crab.
Put all ingredients together and mix well
If it sets over night in the refrigerator the ingredients will blend.
Recipe Source: This is the original recipe from Golden Corral.  Salt, and pepper; toss well.
