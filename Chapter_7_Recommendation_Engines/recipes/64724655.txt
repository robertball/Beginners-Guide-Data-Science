Title:
Peanut Butter  Kandy Kake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6543766.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 c. milk
2 c. flour
2 c. sugar
1 heaping tsp. baking powder
1 dash salt
4 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
1  to 1 1/4 c. peanut butter
1 12 oz. bag of semi sweet or milk chocolate morsels
1 T. solid vegetable shortening

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour jelly roll pan.
Heat milk and butter in saucepan over medium heat until milk is steaming but not boiling and butter is melted.
In stand mixer with paddle attachment, mix dry ingredients. Add eggs and vanilla and mix thoroughly.
While mixer is in motion, SLOWLY, to avoid spatter,  drizzle milk and butter mixture into batter until completely incorporated.
Pour mixture into jelly roll pan and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Remove from oven and while still warm, spread with desired amount of peanut butter until completely covered. After it has cooled a bit, place in refrigerator in order to cool more quickly so peanut butter will set.
Heat chips and shortening in microwave until melted.  Spread over peanut butter layer, and return to refrigerator until completely set.
