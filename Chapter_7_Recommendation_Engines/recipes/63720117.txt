Title:
Bulk Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7  tablespoons olive oil
1 (105 ounce) can tomato puree
4 teaspoons dried onion, minced or chopped
1 tablespoon dried basil
1 tablespoon dried oregano
2 teaspoons dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 tablespoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon onion powder
11 1/4 cloves (1/3 cup, or 66 grams) fresh minced garlic
1/3 cup honey
1/3 cup + 2 Tablespoons (not packed) brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
In a large pot, saute garlic in olive oil. Combine rest of the ingredients, simmer on low for 1 hour.
I use 1 cup of sauce per large pizza. I freeze in 2 cup containers.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
This uses a warehouse size container of tomato puree, making measurement and cleanup easy with a single can. The sauce freezes perfectly!
