Title:
Chicken Parm Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Egg beaten
1/2 cups Parmesan Cheese
5 ounces Panko Japanesse Bread Crumbs
4 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts pounded flat
1 tablespoon Vegetable Oil
12 ounces Marinara Sauce (see Best Marinara Sauce recipe)
4 Slices of Mozzarella Cheese
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasonings
1 pinch Garlic & Onion Powders

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Pour beaten egg in shallow dish. In another dish mix together the grated parmesan cheese and panko and spices. Dip chicken into beaten egg, then into panko mixture to coat.
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium high heat. Add coated chicken and saute for about 8 to 10 minutes each side, or until chicken is cooked through.
Pour a cup of marinara ssuce into a lightly greased 9x13 baking dish. Add chicken and bake for 20 minutes. Add cheese during last 5 minutes.
Serve with side of thin spagetti noodles with extra marinara sauce on top.
