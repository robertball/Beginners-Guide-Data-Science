Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoon of sweet butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2 cups of heavy cream
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
3/4 cup of mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium saucepan with olive oil on low heat. Add garlic, cream, white pepper and bring to a simmer. Stir often. Add the parmesean cheese and simmer sauce for 8-10 minutes or until sauce has thickened and is smooth. When sauce has thicken add mozzarella cheese and stir until smooth. STIR FREQUENTLY!
