Title:
Oat Applesauce Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups flour
2/3 cups oatmeal
1 1/3 cups milk
1 large egg
2 tablespoons applesauce
1 tablespoon cinnamon

Directions:
Combine all ingredients a large bowl.
Pour into waffle grids and cook for about 2-3 minutes or until browned on top.
Taste great covered with applesauce or syrup.

Submitter:

Description: 
A new twist on waffles without fatty vegetable oil.
