Title:
Easy Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dry chicken stove top stuffing
2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups cooked sausage (Italian)
6 eggs
1 cup cheddar cheese shredded
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly grease 9 x 12 pan.  Mix all ingredients together.  Pour into baking dish and bake for 45 to 50 minutes.
