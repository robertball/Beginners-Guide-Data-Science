Title:
Crock pot garlic brown sugar chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large chicken, cut in pieces
1 c brown sugar
2/3 c vinegar
1/4 c sprite or 7 up
2 tbs minced garlic
2 tbs soy sauce
1 teas pepper

Directions:
Put pieces in crock pot.  Mix ingredients and pour over chicken.   Slow cook for 6 to 8 hours.  Serve over rice or noodles.  Can thicken sauce with cornstarch.
