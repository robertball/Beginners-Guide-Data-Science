Title:
Almond Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup non-fat plain yogurt
1/2 cup chocolate soy milk
1 tbsp. almond butter
1/2 cup frozen blueberries
1 cup frozen strawberries (sliced or halved works best, but you can use whole)
2 tsp. ground flaxmeal
1 tsp honey or sweetener of your choice (optional)

Directions:
Place all ingredients (except honey) in blender and blend until smooth.  Sweeten to taste with honey if necessary.  Pour into glasses.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Almond butter adds protein and rich flavor to the strawberry, blueberry smoothies made with chocolate soy milk.
