Title:
Lemon Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985479.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sliced green onions
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
1/4 cup butter or stick margarine
4 cups cold cooked rice
1 (10 ounce) package frozen peas, thawed
2 tablespoons reduced-sodium soy sauce
2 teaspoons grated lemon peel
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce

Directions:
In a large nonstick skillet or wok, stir-fry onions and parsley in butter for 1 minute. Add the remaining ingredients; stir-fry for 4-6 minutes or until peas are tender and rice is heated through.

Submitter: 
Janice  Mitchell
Description: 
This appealing of rice and veggies complements most meats. The lemon peel and hot sauce transform regular fried rice into a distinctive side dish.
