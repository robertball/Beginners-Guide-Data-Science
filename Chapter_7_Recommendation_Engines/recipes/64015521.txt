Title:
Pork Chops with Walnut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chops;¼ cup chopped onion;½tsp.garlic powder;1/8 tsp. ground cloves;1/8 tsp. cinnamon;2 Tbsp. cornstarch;1 10 oz. can beef consomme´;1 bay leaf;½ cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350°.  Place chops in oblong baking dish. Sprinkle chops with all ingredients except consomme and cornstarch. In a small bowl mix consomme and cornstarch until smooth. Pour over top of the chops. Bake for 30-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
ChezSue
Description: 
Pork that tastes like you spent hours but doesn't. The seasonings blend together in a richness that makes you want more.
