Title:
Mama's Quick & Easy Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Large Eggs *(See note blelow)
¼ cup Milk
1 small can diced green chili peppers
½ can Spam, diced
--OR--
1 small can Spam, diced
--OR--
6 oz. diced ham
1 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese
2 Frozen Pie Shells, uncooked

Directions:
Prehest oven to 375°.  Do not precook the pie shells before filling them.
Whisk together Eggs and Milk until eggs are slightly beaten. Add green chilis, Spam/ham, cheese.  Mix together well.
Pour ½ of the egg mixture into each uncooked pie shell.  Bake at 375° for 30-40 minutes, until eggs are set.  Test by sticking toothpick in the middle.  If it comes out clean, the quiche are ready.
