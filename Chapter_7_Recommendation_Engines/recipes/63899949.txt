Title:
Spinach & Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1082052.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (4 to 5 ounces) boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1 cup baby spinach leaves
4 slices of mozzarella cheese
4 slices of provolone cheese
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
1 tablespoon Crystal Farms® Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 each of salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 400° F.
Line baking sheet with aluminum foil; spray with nonstick cooking spray; set aside.
Place one chicken breast between sheets of waxed paper. Pound until chicken breast is ¼-inch thick.
Repeat with remaining chicken.
Place chicken breasts flat on work surface.
Lay spinach leaves on top of each chicken breast.
Cut mozzarella & provolone cheese into ½” strips.
Place in center of chicken.
Gently roll up.
Brush outside of chicken with olive oil.
Stir together breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.
Gently roll outside of chicken in crumb mixture coating outside completely.
Place tightly rolled chicken, seam side down, on prepared baking sheet.
Bake 22 to 26 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink. (Cheese will melt outside chicken slightly.)
