Title:
Salsa at it's best Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-15oz can diced tomatoes
1 - cup cilantro - chopped fine-divided
2 - cloves garlic
1- cup diced green onions - chopped fine-divided
1 - teaspoon salt
1-  teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup tomato sauce
2 to 4 jalapeno peppers - roasted and stems removed

Directions:
To roast jalapeno peppers put the raw peppers on a baking sheet and put in broiler for about 5 minutes.  Take out of oven and turn peppers over.  Put back into broiler and broil another 5 minutes.  Remove from boiler and cool.
In a blender put in 2 jalapeno peppers, 1 can 15oz. diced tomatoes, garlic, 1/2 cup cilantro, 1/2 cup green onions, water, salt & pepper.  Blend until smooth about 1 minute. Check to see if salsa is hot to your likening, if not add another pepper and run through the blender.  Check to see if it need more salt and pepper.  Pour into a bowl, add remaining cilantro and green onions chopped fine and stir in tomato sauce.

Submitter: 
Tina Andre' Fox
Description: 
This is such a flavorful salsa that I created years ago.  It's easy to make and just so good.
