Title:
Vanilla Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/818402.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 tablespoon milk

Directions:
In a bowl, combine confectioners' sugar, vanilla and enough milk to achieve desired consistency.

Submitter:

Description: 
This classic glaze adds a special sweet finishing touch to a variety of breads, rolls and muffins.
