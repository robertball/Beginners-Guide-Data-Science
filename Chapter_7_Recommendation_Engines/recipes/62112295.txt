Title:
Seafood Factory Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 medium Yukon or red skin potatoes, cooked peeled and cubed.
1/2 C. Celery, strings removed and diced
1 large white or yellow onion, diced
3/4 C Mayonnaise, (Hellman's regular or canola)
1 Tbsp white vinegar
2 tsp. celery seeds
1 tbsp. minced parsley
Pepper
Salt
3 hard cooked eggs, chopped Optional

Directions:
Put all the diced and cubed vegetables in a large bowl.
In a small bowl blend the mayonnaise, vinegar, salt, pepper
Mix the mayonnaise mixture into the large bowl with the vegetables and blend to coat all ingredients.
Sprinkle with celery seeds and minced parsley and toss lightly.
Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A side dish for fish or chicken. I had this potato salad at a restaurant on Sanibel Island and went home a made up my own version. The celery seeds compliment the other ingredients and creates a gourmet potato salad that all will enjoy.
