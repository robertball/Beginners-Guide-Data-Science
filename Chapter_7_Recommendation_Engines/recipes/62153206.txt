Title:
Low fat, low sugar green beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of french green beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup splenda
3/4 cup water
3 TBLS white vinegar
4 TBLS chopped onion
3 strips turkey bacon, cooked and crumbled

Directions:
In medium saucepan combine green beans, turkey bacon onion,vinegar,splenda and water.  Bring to a boil and reduce heat.  Simmer until the liquid has evaporated.
