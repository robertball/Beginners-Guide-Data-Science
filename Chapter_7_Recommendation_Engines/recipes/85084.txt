Title:
Grilled Scallops Au Vin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds fresh sea scallops
1/4 cup white wine
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Tarragon Leaves
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Onion Powder
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Garlic Powder
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Season-All® Seasoned Salt

Directions:
Preheat grill or broiler. Thread scallops onto skewers. Combine remaining ingredients in a small bowl and brush over both sides of scallops.
Grill or broil scallops, 10-15 minutes, brushing occasionally with remaining wine mixture.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
Tender scallop morsels, seasoned just right with white wine, tarragon, and garlic are grilled to perfection in this quick and easy recipe.
