Title:
Quick Beef Sroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. round steak
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 can golden mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
2 TBSP flour
8 ounces wide egg noodles, cooked

Directions:
Cut meat diagonally across grain in 1/4 wide strips. Brown meat and onions in a pressure cooker. Add soup and 1 can of water. Pressure for 10 minutes.
Blend sour cream and flour, stir into meat mixture. Cook and stir until mixture thickens. Season to taste. Serve over noodles.
