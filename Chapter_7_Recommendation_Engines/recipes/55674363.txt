Title:
Lemon Curd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs  yolks
1 cup sugar
4 fresh lemon juiced and zested
1 stick of butter

Directions:
In the top of a double boiler, beat eggs and sugar add juice and zest. Cook for 8 min. remove and stir in butter one T at a time. Place in glass container and press plastic wrap onto suraface, refridgerate up to 2 weeks.
