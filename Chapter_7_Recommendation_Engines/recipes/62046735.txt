Title:
Beef and Olive Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) block cream cheese, softened
1 can black olives, chopped
2 packages wafer beef, chopped
1 tablespoon garlic
2 tablespoons green onion, chopped, optional

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and refriferate.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This dip is best if made a day in advance to let the flavors blend.
