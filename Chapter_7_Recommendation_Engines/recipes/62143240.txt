Title:
Cheesy Kale Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz package kale, thawed and drained
4 eggs
8 oz block sharp cheddar cheese
4 oz cream cheese
1/2 cup milk
4 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled
thawed pre-made pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Beat the four eggs. Warm cream cheese and combine with milk. Fold milk/cream cheese mixture into beaten eggs.
Shred all 8 ounces of cheese. Combine with previous ingredients. Add kale and bacon.
Pour all ingredients into pie crust and bake in the oven for about 45 minutes, or until top has golden brown spots and a "toothpick test" comes out clean.

Submitter: 
ally-gator
Description: 
Created when I had used all of my spinach for spinach dip, this creamy, simple quiche is great for family breakfasts!
