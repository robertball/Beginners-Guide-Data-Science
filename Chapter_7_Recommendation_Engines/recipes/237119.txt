Title:
Peanut Butter and Jelly Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1146120.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons peanut butter
3 tablespoons grape jelly
1 cup milk
5 cubes ice
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Stir peanut butter and jelly together in a bowl until smooth.
Blend milk, ice, and vanilla extract in a blender for 30 seconds; add the peanut butter mixture and continue blending until smooth, about 30 seconds more.
