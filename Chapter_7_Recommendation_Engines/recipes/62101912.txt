Title:
Easiest Chicken & Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 cup good white wine, any kind you like
1-1/2 cups raw white rice
6 chicken breasts with bone, skinned/or not
2 Tbsps. melted butter
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 deg.
In medium sauce pan, heat soup, wine and rice just till it is warmed through. Pour into a greased 9X13 casserole dish. Place chicken breasts on top. Cover with foil and put in preheated oven. for 1 hour.
After 1 hour, remove foil, brush chicken breasts with melted butter and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Return to oven uncovered for 1/2 hour. Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
A fool-proof chicken and rice casserole using items you usually find in your cabinet, and white wine. So easy your hubby can throw this together..great for busy Mom's..a quick salad or side dish and your done.
