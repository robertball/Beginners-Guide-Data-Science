Title:
Chicken Rotel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken Rotel
2-3 lbs boned chicken
1/2 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
1/4 cup butter
1 can Cream of Chicken soup
1 can Cream of Celery soup
1 can of chicken broth (use liquid of boiled chicken)
Doritos
1 lb grated cheddar cheese
1 can Rotel tomatoes

Directions:
Cook chicken, cool & debone. Saute onion & bell pepper in butter. Add soups, broth & Rotel tomatoes and mix. Layer Doritos, chicken, cheese & sauce. Repeat until all used. Bake 20-30 minutes in 350 degree oven.
