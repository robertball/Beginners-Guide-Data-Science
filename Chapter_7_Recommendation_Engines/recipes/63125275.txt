Title:
EASY TURKEY DIVAN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz pkg frozen broccoli florets or chopped broccoli
1 8 oz pkg deli smoked turkey
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1 can french fried onions
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 tsp curry powder
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook the brocolli according to package directions or until tender.  Chop the turkey into bite size peices.  Mix together all the remaining ingredients except the curry, reserving one cup of cheese for top.  Layer in a 2 qt casserole: 1/2 turkey, 1/2 broccoli & 1/2 soup/sour cream/onion mixture.  Sprinkle 1/2 tsp of curry all over, repeat layers & top with remaining cup of cheese.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes .

Submitter: 
Sydney Sadler Walker
Description: 
This is one of my favorite recipes.  The curry is a must...it's what gives the dish that little something!
