Title:
Shell's Quick Cherry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Refrigerated pie crusts
5 cups frozen pitted dark sweet cherries, thawed, drained
1 cup sugar + 1 T
3 tablespoons cornstarch + 1 T
1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
1 tablespoon butter cut into small pieces
1 egg, slightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat over to 425 degrees F. Add 1 tablespoon cornstarch and 1 tablespoon sugar into crust lined plate.
Mix cherries, sugar, cornstarch, almond extract and lemon peel together.  Spoon into crust lined plate.  Dot with butter.
Cover with lattice designed pie crust.  Trim ends, seal and flute. Brush crust with egg.
Cover edges with foil. Bake for 10 mins.  Reduce to 350 degrees and bake another 20 mins. Remove foil and bake 30 mins.
Cool on wire rack.
