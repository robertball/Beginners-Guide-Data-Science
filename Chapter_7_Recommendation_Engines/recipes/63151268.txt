Title:
Ongol Ongol Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/807206.jpg
Ingredients:
Bahan I:
1.5 cup of tapioca(corn) Starch
1.5 cup air
Bahan II:
1.5 cup of Brown Sugar
1.5 Cup of Water
2 Pandan leave
Bahan III:
Steam frozen Coconut with 1/4 tsp of salt

Directions:
1. Cook ingredient II until resolved.  Add Ingredient I until thicken.
2. Pour in pyrex, cool and refregirate.
3. Cut in pieces and coat with coconut

Submitter: 
JFKR
Description: 
Recipe from  Justina
This is a recipe of a popular Indonesian sweet made with vanilla and cornstarch boiled with palm sugar.
