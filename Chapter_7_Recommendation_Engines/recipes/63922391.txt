Title:
SUGAR-FREE BEAN SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (1 lb.) cans kitchen sliced green beans, drained
1 (1 lb.) can wax beans, drained
1 (1 lb.) can dark red kidney beans, rinsed & drained
1 (1 lb.) can garbanzo bean, rinsed & drained
1 small red onion, quartered
1 red bell pepper, stemmed & seeded
3 ribs celery, quartered
1 cup white wine vinegar
3/4 cup Splenda granulated sweetener
2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. black pepper
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Place the drained cans of beans on a clean kitchen towel and gently pat dry. Set aside.
In the bowl of a food processor, place the red onion, bell pepper and celery. Pulse to a fine chop.
In a large bowl, whisk together the white wine vinegar, Splenda, salt and pepper until desolved. Whisk in the vegetable oil. Mix in the vegetables from the food processor. Fold in the canned beans.
Cover and refrigerate at least overnight, the longer it marinates the better it gets. Stir again just before serving.
