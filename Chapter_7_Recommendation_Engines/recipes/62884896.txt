Title:
Coconut Fish Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695969.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cloves garlic
1 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 teaspoon ginger
1 onion chopped
1/4 teaspoon turmeric
125ml tin coconut milk
100g cod or other white fish
1 large tomato
1/4 teaspoon olive oil

Directions:
Add the ginger, turmeric and curry powder to a wok or frying pan and toast until browned.
Add the olive oil and garlic and mix to form a paste.
Add the onions and fry until soft.
Add half of the coconut milk and about the same amount of water. Bring to the boil.
Cut the fish into bite size pieces and add to the boiling mixture. Simmer for 5 minutes.
Add the tomato and the rest of the coconut milk and simmer for a further 5 minutes, adding more water if needed.
Serve with brown rice.

Submitter: 
Victoria Teasdale
Description: 
Lightly spicy, this is a wonderful creamy fish curry.
