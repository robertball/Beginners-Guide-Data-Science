Title:
Slow Cooker Turkey Chili with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1001843.jpg
Ingredients:
2- pounds Ground turkey
1- Lg onion sliced julianne
2- 12 oz cans light red kidney beans
2- 12 oz cans dark red kidney beans
2- 10.5 oz cans diced tomatos w/ jalapenos
2- 10.5 oz cans diced tomatoes
1- 12 ox can tomato sauce
2- 3 oz cans diced peppers
2- T chili powder
1- T ground cumin
1- T minced garlic
2- T hot sauce such as El Yucateeco
1- T ground red pepper
Salt to taste

Directions:
Brown the ground turkey and drain the excess fat. Add the turkey and all the ingedients to the slow cooker on low for 4 hours

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
This turkey chili has a nice kick but not over powering. It does pack some heat though
