Title:
Sweet and Sour Chicken III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast meat - cubed
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup sliced green bell pepper
1/2 cup sliced red bell pepper
1 cup carrot strips
2 clove garlic, minced
1-1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple (or chunks) with juice
2 tablespoon vinegar
2 tablespoon brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground or fresh ginger

Directions:
Brown chicken in oil with 1/2 of garlic and 1/2 of ginger in a large skillet over medium high heat. Set aside.  Add green bell pepper, red bell pepper, carrot,remaining garlic and remaining ginger and stir fry for 1 to 2 minutes.
In a small bowl, combine cornstarch and soy sauce and mix together; pour mixture into the skillet, along with the pineapple and liquid, vinegar, and sugar. Stir together and bring to a full boil.  Return chicken to pan and heat through.  Serve over rice.

Description: 
Carrots and red and green bell peppers add lots of color to this classic sweet pineapple and sour soy-vinegar chicken stir fry with just a whiff of ginger.Modified from: Sweet and Sour Chicken III
