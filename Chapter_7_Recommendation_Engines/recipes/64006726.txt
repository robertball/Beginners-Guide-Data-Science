Title:
7-UP Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups bisquik
1 cup sour cream
1 cup 7-up
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Melt butter in 9x13 pan, cool slightly.
Combine sour cream and 7-up mix slightly. Add Bisquik until is all incorporated. Roll out dough on slightly floured surface to 1/2 inch thick. Cut and place on top of the melted butter, turn once to  butter top and bake 15-20 minutes.
