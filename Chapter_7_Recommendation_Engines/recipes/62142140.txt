Title:
Happy Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken (4-5 pounds)
½ Tbsp butter
2 lemons
1 whole garlic
2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper
1/2 cup white wine or chicken broth

Directions:
Wash and dry one whole (organic) chicken (4-5 pounds)
Slide ½ Tbsp butter underneath the skin of each breast
Cut lemons in half and put in cavity, along with 6-8 large cloves of garlic
Rub entire skin with Dijon mustard, then salt and pepper
Place in white wine or chicken broth (or combination) in a cast iron skillet
Roast in a preheated 425 degree oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees and cook for an hour to 75 minutes or more (until internal temperature is 160 degrees and thighs pull away easily)
Let it rest for min 15 minutes (be patient - this is important!)
Enjoy happy chicken
