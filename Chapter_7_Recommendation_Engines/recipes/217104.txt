Title:
La Vida Mezcal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4829546.jpg
Ingredients:
ice
1 1/2 fluid ounces tequila
1/2 fluid ounce mezcal
3/4 fluid ounce sweet vermouth
3/4 fluid ounce bitter orange aperitif (such as Campari® or Aperol®)
1 dash orange bitters
1 thin slice orange

Directions:
Place ice into a cocktail shaker. Pour in tequila, mezcal, vermouth, and Campari. Add a dash of bitters, then stir or shake. Strain into a ice-filled rocks glass and garnish with a slice of orange.

Submitter: 
Lorem Ipsum
Description: 
There's only a touch of mezcal in this drink, but it adds a delicious smoky quality. Be sure to use good quality mescal and tequila here. You can substitute Aperol for the Campari -- and play with the ratios of sweet vermouth to Campari and tequila to mezcal. Enjoy!
