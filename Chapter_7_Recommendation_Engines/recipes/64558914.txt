Title:
Creamy Potato Leek Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4575604.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
3 leeks - halved & thinly sliced
2 russet potatoes - peeled & diced
Kosher salt to taste
Fresh ground white pepper to taste
4 cups vegetable stock
1/4 cup heavy cream
Chives  - chopped

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, melt the butter
Sweat the leeks until tender (~10 minutes)
Add the potatoes
Season with salt & pepper
Cover & cook until potatoes start to soften, stirring frequently (~10 minutes)
Add the stock and bring to a boil
Reduce heat & simmer until the vegetables are tender (25-30 minutes)
Using an immersion blender, purses until smooth
Add heavy cream & check seasoning
Serve garnished with chives or crumbled bacon.
