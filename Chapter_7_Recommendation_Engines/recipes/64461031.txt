Title:
Scottsdale Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb pkg frozen hash browns, thawed
2 1/2 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese
2 cups sour cream
1 10 1/2 oz. can cream o chicken soup
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/4 cup butter, melted
salt & pepper to taste
2 cups cornflake cereal, crushed
1/2 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Lightly grease 7x10 inch baking dish. Combine hash browns, cheese, sour cream, soup, onions and 1/4 cup butter in large bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to baking dish. Mix cornflakes and 1/2 cup butter and sprinkle over potato mixture. Bake until browned, about 45 minutes. Serve immediately.
