Title:
Steak N Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz Steaks
1/2 tsp oil
1 cup Soy sauce
1 cup Lemon juice fresh or from a bottle
pot of rice, as much as your family will eat
1 cup veggie of your choice

Directions:
start your pot of rice, in seperate pan heat up the oil and place the steak to cook turning to evenly cook until the steak is as rare as you like. Then place on cutting board and cut into thin slices. Place slices into bowl, mix enough soy sauce and lemon to cover the steak. Let sit 5 min then serve over cooked rice. Add veggie of your choice, we usually use corn.

a quick easy filling meal the whole family will love.
