Title:
Scalloped Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Melted Butter
4 Cups Bread Cubes
1 Cup Sugar
3 Eggs
1/2 Cup Milk
1 20oz Can crushed pineapple

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together and place in an oven safe casserole dish.  Bake at 375 for 35 minutes until brown on top.

Description:
