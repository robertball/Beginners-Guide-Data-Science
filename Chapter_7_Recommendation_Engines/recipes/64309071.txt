Title:
Mediteranean Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
3/4 teaspoon white sugar
juice of 1/2 a lemon
2 1/2 cups cooked tricolor spiral pasta
15 cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup sliced red bell peppers
3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 (4 ounce) can whole klamata olives
1/2 cup provolone cheese
1/2 cup genoa salami

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, garlic powder, oregano, black pepper, and sugar lemon onion powder. Add cooked pasta, tomatoes, red peppers, feta cheese, olives, and pepperoni. Toss until evenly coated. Cover, and chill 2 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Toni
Description: 
Taste the earthy flavors of the Mediterranean in every bite of this colorful summer salad. Tangy feta cheese,  tomatoes, bell peppers and olives are tossed with salami and pasta in an herb-infused vinaigrette.Modified from: Greek Pasta Salad I
