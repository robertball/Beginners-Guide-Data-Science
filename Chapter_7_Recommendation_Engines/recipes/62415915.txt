Title:
BEEF STEW from RIVAL Crock-Pot book Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-lbs. Beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch cubes
1/4 cup Flour
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
1 1/2 Cups Beef Broth
1 Teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
1 Clove Garlic
1 Bay Leaf
1 Teaspoon Paprika
4 Carrots, sliced
3 Potatoes, diced
1 Onion, chopped
1 Stalk Celery, sliced

Directions:
Put meat in stoneware.  Mix flour, salt and pepper, pour over meat; stir to coad meat with flour.  Add remaining ingredients and stir to mix well.  Cover and cook on LOW 10 hours (HIGH 6 hours).  Stir thoroughly before serving.
