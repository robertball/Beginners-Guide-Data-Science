Title:
Hermit Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. butter
1/4 C. shortening
1 1/2 C. firmly packed brown sugar
2 Tbl. milk++
3 eggs
2 1/2 C. all purpose flour
1 tsp. salt
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/8 tsp. ground cloves
1 C. raisins
3/4 C. chopped walnuts
Confectioners sugar

Directions:
Preheat over to 400 degrees
Cream butter, shortening, sugar and milk in a large bowl at medium speed until well blended.
Add the eggs, one at a time.  Beat well after each addition
In a separate bowl, combine flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves
Mix flour into butter mixture
Stir in raisins and nuts
Drop dough by even tablespoons, 2 inches apart, onto ungreased cookie sheet
Bake @ 400 degrees for 7 to 8 minutes
Allow the cookies to rest one minute, remove to cooling rack
Sift powdered sugar over the cooled cookies
