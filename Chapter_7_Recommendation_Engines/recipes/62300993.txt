Title:
Avacodo Salsa & Sardine Frenchy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364702.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can sardines in water
1/4 green pepper chopped fine
1 ovacoda pitted and mashed
1 teaspoon lemon juice
chopped lettuce tops
french bread sliced (4)
e.v.o.o.
1 can tomatoe & oregeno

Directions:
mix ovacodo, chopped lettuce tops, green pepper and lemon juice in a small bowl. open sardines and drain water. slice bread yielding 4 slices. brush e.v.o.o. on bread and bake 5 mins each side @ 350 deg.
apply ovacodo to bread, top with sardines & add tomatoe from can to top of sardines.
