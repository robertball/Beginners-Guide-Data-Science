Title:
Easy Southern Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535330.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups bread flour
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 cup water
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. salt
1 tbs. baking powder
4 tbs. powdered butter milk powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Spray aluminum cupcake pan.
In a large bowl, combine bread flour,sugar, baking powder, butter milk powder, and salt. Stir in milk and mayonnaise until a smooth dough is formed. Spoon batter into sprayed muffin pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 17-20 minutes, until golden brown and doubled in size.
