Title:
Best Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 tsp.baking powder
2 tsp.cinnamon
1 tsp.baking soda
3/4 tsp.salt
1/2 tsp.nutmeg
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
3 eggs
3/4 cup oil
1 tsp.vanilla
2 cups grated carrots
1 cup drained canned crushed pineapple
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
ICING:
1 pkg.cream cheese
1/4 cup softened marg.
1 cup icing sugar
1 tsp.vanilla

Directions:
Grease and flour 9x13 pan.In large bowl whisk together flour, baking powder,cinnamon,baking soda,salt and nutmeg.In separate bowl beat together both sugars,eggs,oil and vanilla until smooth,pour over flour mixture and stir till moistened.Stir in carrots,pineapple and walnuts.Spread into prepared pan.Bake at 350 for 40 min. or till toothpick comes out clean.When down let cool on wire rack.
Icing:In bowl,beat cream cheese with marg.till smooth,add vanilla.Beat in icing sugar gradually till well mixed.Spread over cake once cooled.
