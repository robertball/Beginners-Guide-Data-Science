Title:
Kimberly’s Apple Roast Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb roast?
1 cup applesauce?
1 orange?
1 Gala apple?
1/2 lb baby carrots?
8 red potatoes?
¾ medium onion?
2 cloves garlic?
1 1/2 tbsp rosemary?
1 tbsp garlic salt
?3/4 tbsp pepper
?3/4 tbsp salt

Directions:
Place roast in a slow cooker. Pour applesauce over roast. Squeeze juice from 1/2 orange over roast. Slice the other half of orange and place over roast. Slice potatoes into large chunks and place in crock pot.
Slice onion and place in crock pot. Add baby carrots. Mince fresh garlic and place in crock pot. Slice apple and place throughout crock pot. Sprinkle rosemary, garlic salt, salt, and pepper over mixture. Cook on high for 5-6 hours, or until roast is falling apart, and vegetables are cooked through.
