Title:
Fried Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
8 medium sized russet potatoes, peeled and chopped
2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 medium onion, diced
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1/2 cup bacon bits
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1 (16 ounce) tub sour cream
1 (6 ounce) can Fried onions

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large skillet on medium high until potatoes will sizzle.  Add potatoes to skillet and sprinkle with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.  Stir in onion and minced garlic and continue to cook/fry potatoes until lightly browned.  Meanwhile, mix sour cream, cheese, and bacon bits in a bowl.
When potatoes are done, layer half of them in the bottom of a 13x9 baking dish and spread half of the sour cream mixture on top, then layer the other half of the potatoes on top of that and spread the other half of the sour cream mixture.  Top with the fried onions and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
