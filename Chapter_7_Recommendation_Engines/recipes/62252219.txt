Title:
Kale and black bean tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small red onion, halved, thinly sliced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp. cumin
1 small head kale (about 6 oz), tough stems removed, cut in small pieces
1 15-oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 c. frozen corn kernel
2-4 oz queso cotija (or jack or feta), chopped
8 6-inch corn tortillas
salsa
avocado, cubed

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat 1 T. olive oil on medium heat.  Add onion and garlic; sprinkle with cumin, stir, saute for 2 minutes.  Add kale, stir to coat with oil.  Cover and cook for 2 minutes till just tender and bright green.  Add beans and corn, and cook for 2 minutes longer to warm through.  Add queso and sir; season with salt and pepper.
Warm tortillas and divide mixture among them.  Top with salsa and avocado.
