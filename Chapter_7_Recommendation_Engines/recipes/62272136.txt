Title:
Cherry Pecan Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup chopped pecans
1 jar (10 oz) maraschino cherries, drained and chopped
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; add to the milk. Stir in Pecans, cherries and vanilla. Pour into a greased and floured 8-in.x 4-in x 2-in. loaf pan.
Bake at 350* for 65-75 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.  Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack.
