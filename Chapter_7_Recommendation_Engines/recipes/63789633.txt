Title:
Beet and Avocado Stacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1051392.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small or medium size beets
2 avocados, peeled and cut
2 tbsp. plain yogurt
1/2 small lemon or 1 lime juice
Sea salt and crushed red pepper
Feta or goat cheese

Directions:
Cut roasted beets into round slices. Puree avocados with lemon or lime juice, salt, pepper and plain yogurt until very smooth. On a plate, lay a circle of the beet and tablespoon of the avocado spread, then the slice of beet and avocado spread.  Build your stacks as high as you like. Top with some feta or goat cheese and serve.
