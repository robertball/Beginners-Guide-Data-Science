Title:
One dish Turkey and Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless turkey breasts or cutlets
1 box stove top stuffing mix
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 can milk
1/2 cup sour cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
spray a medium sized baking dish with nonstick.
Place turkey in a single layer in  dish.
Season with salt and pepper.
On the stove prepare the stuffing according
to the directions.
Combine soup, milk and sour cream.
Pour the soup mixture over the turkey.
Next top with stuffing mix.
Bake for 35 minutes or until turkey is cooked.
