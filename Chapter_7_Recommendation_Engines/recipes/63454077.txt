Title:
Best Ever Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c flour
1 1/2 c sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs, beaten
2 bananas, mashed
1/2 c canola oil
1/4 c + 1 tbsp buttermilk
2 tsp lemon juice
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp almond

Directions:
In large bowl, combine dry ingredients.
In another bowl, combine wet ingredients.
Mix wet with dry ingredients until just moistened.
Pour into 2 8x4 baking loaf pans.
Bake at 325 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
