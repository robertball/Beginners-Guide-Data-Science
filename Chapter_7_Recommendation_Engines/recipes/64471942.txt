Title:
Black Bean and Feta Stuffed Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown rice
1 cup Greek yogurt
1 1/4 cup chopped cilantro leaves
2 T lime juice
1/4 tsp salt
2 sliced avocados
1 cup crumbled feta (4 oz)
1 cup shredded red cabbage
1 15.5-oz can black beans (rinsed)
1/2 halved and thinly sliced cucumber
2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken (optional)
1 T hot sauce (optional)
4 warmed burrito-size tortillas

Directions:
Cook rice.
Combine yogurt, 1/4 cup chopped cilantro leaves, lime juice, and salt.
Divide yogurt mixture, avocados, Feta, cabbage, 1 cup cilantro leaves, beans, cucumber, chicken (optional), and hot sauce (optional) among tortillas.
Roll into 4 burritos.

This burrito offers a great mix of healthy foods from four of the food groups!
