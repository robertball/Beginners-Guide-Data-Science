Title:
Ron's Baking Powder Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups yellow cornmeal
1/2 cup flour
1 tbsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/4 cup oil
1 1/4 cups buttermilk

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl. Mix wet ingredients separately. Make a well in center of dry ingredients and add wet mixture. Combine thoroughly, but do not overmix.
Pour into a round baking dish. Tap out air bubbles. Bake 20 to 25 minutes at 400 or till golden brown.
Variation: If buttermilk is not available, use 1/3 cup sour cream and 1 cup of milk in its place.

Submitter: 
colettenoelle
Description: 
This is my Dad's simple cornbread recipe. Tastes delicious with jelly over it. Enjoy!
