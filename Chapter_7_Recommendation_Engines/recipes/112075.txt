Title:
Garlic Bread Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992016.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) loaf Archer Farms™ frozen garlic hearth-baked bread
1/2 cup Contadina squeeze pizza sauce
20 slices Market Pantry™ pizza-size pepperoni
1 cup Market Pantry shredded Mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Remove bread from wrapping; place on cookie sheet, cut side up.
Squeeze 3 long rows (about 1/4 cup) of pizza sauce on each half.  Top with pepperoni and cheese.
Bake for 10 minutes or until center is hot and cheese is melted.
