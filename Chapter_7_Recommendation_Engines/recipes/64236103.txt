Title:
Home Style Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1711679.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean hamburger, browned and drained
1/2 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup diced celery
1/2 cup chopped red or green bell pepper
1 can tomatoes with green chili
1 can tomato soup
1 can tomato puree
1 bottle beer
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 can dark red kidney beans, drained

Directions:
Brown hamburger and drain most of the fat.  Add onions, celery and bell peppers and saute until vegetables are tender.  Add tomato ingredients, beer, spices and kidney beans.  Bring to a boil and cook for about 30 minutes.  Transfer mixture to a crock pot and cook for at least 2 or 3 hours on low.  Test for flavor and add seasoning to taste.  Top with your favorite toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, green onions.  Serve with saltines or macaroni.
