Title:
Jeannie's Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. boiled and mashed yellow squash
1 stick of butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1 stalk celery, diced
1 package mexican cornbread, prepared and crumbled
1 can cream of chicken soup
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
If not already boiled, slice and boil yellow squash until soft. Drain and mash. This can be lumpy, mash to your texture preference.
Saut√© onion, peppers, and celery in butter until somewhat softened. Add boiled squash and soup mix.
Put crumbled cornbread into a casserole dish. Pour vegetable mix over and combine.
Bake 30 minutes or until golden brown around the edges.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
My next-door neighbor's husband was a wonderful gardener and he always had lots of yellow squash is his garden. Jeannie would boil and freeze what they couldn't eat or give away to make this yummy casserole year-round.
