Title:
Celeriac mash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb celeriac (celery root)
1/4 - 1/2 c sour cream
dried dill weed to taste
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut off top and bottom of root
Scrub
Peel with sharp knife
chop into 1/2" cubes
boil until tender and mash
mix in sour cream, dill weed, salt and pepper.
Delicious!

Submitter: 
LizardLover2009
Description: 
This is a delicious and quick way to prepare a root veggie that isn't often talked about. Celeriac has a slightly tangy taste that is reminiscent of celery, but much subtler and has a fabulous flavor.
