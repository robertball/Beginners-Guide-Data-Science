Title:
Jake's Potatos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Potatos:
5 large potatos
3 T. Butter, melted
2 C. grated cheese
1 can Cr. Of Chicken soup
1 C. Sour Cream
3 C. Milk
3 T. Green onion, finely chopped
Topping:
1 1/2 C. Corn flakes, crushed
6 T. Butter, melted
6 T. Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325Â°
Peel and cut potatos (bite sized) Boil.
Put into 9" x 13" pan
Cover w/ 2 C. milk, melted butter and seasoning to taste
Mix soup, sour cream, 1 C. milk, onions, and cheese
Pour over potatos.
Mix topping ingredients and spinkle over top.
Bake 30 min. uncovered

Marianne Jones Islas

Great to combine with left-over holiday ham!
