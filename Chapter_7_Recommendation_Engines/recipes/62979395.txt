Title:
Pumpkin Layered Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/743965.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/3 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup butter, melted
2 pkgs (8 oz each)cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1-1/2 cups canned pumpkin
1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
2 eggs
2 pkgs (3.4 oz each) vanilla flavor instant pudding
2 cups cold milk
1 tub (8 oz.) whipped topping, thawed, divided
1/2 cup chopped pecans, toasted *

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Mix cookie crumbs and butter; press onto bottom of 13x9-inch pan. Bake 10 min.
Beat cream cheese and sugar with mixer until well blended. Add pumpkin and spices; mix well. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating on low speed after each just until blended; pour over crust.
Bake 30 min. or until center is almost set. Cool 1 hour.
Beat pudding mixes and milk in medium bowl with whisk 2 min. Stir in 1 cup whipped topping. Spread over dessert; cover with remaining topping.
Refrigerate 3 hours or until firm. Sprinkle with nuts before serving.

A delicious layered pumpkin treat. If you want something a little different this year for your thanksgiving table and your holiday get-togethers, here's a great dessert to try.
