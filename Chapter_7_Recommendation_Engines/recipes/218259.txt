Title:
Wacky Mac® California Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993353.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package Wacky Mac®
1 (15 ounce) can Mandarin oranges, well drained
3 large scallions, trimmed and finely chopped
3 ribs celery, trimmed and chopped
2 cups cooked chopped chicken breast
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/3 cup orange juice
1/3 cup white wine vinegar
1 teaspoon curry powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 small ripe avocado, diced

Directions:
Prepare Wacky Mac according to package directions. Drain and rinse under cold water. Place in very large salad bowl. Add oranges, scallions, celery and chicken.
Stir together oil, orange juice, vinegar, curry powder, salt, paprika and pepper in a small bowl. Pour over salad. Toss gently but well. Top with avocado.

Submitter: 
Wacky Mac®
Description: 
A west-coast take on the traditional pasta salad blending Wacky Mac, chicken, mandarin oranges and avocado.
