Title:
Weeknight Turkey Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Store prepared pico de gallo
1 lb ground turkey
pearled cauliflower (frozen is fine)
Taco seasoning
greek yogurt or tatziki, low fat sour cream
flour tortillas
romaine lettuce
low fat shredded cheese

Directions:
Saute ground turkey until cooked thoroughly
Add 1.5 cups of cauliflower and season mixture with taco seasoning to make "meat"
serve with favorite toppings in flour tortillas or in salad
