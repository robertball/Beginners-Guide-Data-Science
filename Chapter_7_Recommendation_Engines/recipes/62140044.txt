Title:
Simple Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 3  Onions
2 Cups Buttermilk
1 1/2 cups of Flour
2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
oil

Directions:
Slice onions to desired thickness thin to medium.
Soak onion rings in buttermilk for 30 minutes
Mix flour with cayenne and salt.
Heat oil fo frying
drudge onions through the flour mixture
Put in oil and fry to golden brown.

Submitter: 
Cheesecakemama
Description: 
I like a lightly seasoned onion ring and not battered. This way is the answer, I have tried batter mixtures with eggs, but this is so much easier.
