Title:
Fall Festival Cranberry-Apple-Nut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/481471.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 TBSP baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg, lightly beaten
3/4 cup apple cider
1/3 cup canola oil
3/4 cup apples (about one medium)- peeled, cored and finely diced
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup sweetened, dried cranberries, (such as CraisinsÂ®)
Topping:
2 TBSP finely chopped walnuts
1 TBSP light brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C). Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners.
In a medium bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, 1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt; mix well. In a small bowl, combine apple cider, oil, and egg; blend well.
Add dry ingredients all at once; stir just until dry ingredients are moistened.
Stir in chopped apples, 1/2 cup walnuts, and cranberries. Divide batter between 12 muffin cups.
In a small bowl, mix together the 2 TBSP walnuts, brown sugar, and 1/2 tsp cinnamon. Sprinkle toping over muffins.
Bake for 16 to 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 5 minutes before removing from pan. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
Cranberries, apples, and walnuts combine with cinnamon, nutmeg and apple cider to capture the flavors of fall in a delicious muffin!
