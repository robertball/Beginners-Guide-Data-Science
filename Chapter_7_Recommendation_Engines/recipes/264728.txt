Title:
Pineapple Mango Coconut Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5401913.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Almond Breeze Vanilla Coconut Almond Blend Unsweetened almondmilk
1 cup fresh or frozen mango cubes
1 cup fresh, frozen, or juice-packed pineapple chunks
1 tablespoon toasted slivered or sliced almonds (optional)
1 teaspoon ground flax seeds (optional)

Directions:
Puree all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
