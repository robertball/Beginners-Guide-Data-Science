Title:
Southern Coffee Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/591005.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 oz.) jar NesCafe instant coffee
2 c. boiling water
1 c. sugar
1 gal. 2% milk
1/2 gal. vanilla ice cream
1/2 gal. chocolate ice cream

Directions:
Stir coffee and sugar into boiling water until it dissolves. Let coffee cool. You may chill coffee in fridge overnight.  When ready to serve pour coffee into a large punch bowl and add milk and ice cream.  Stir well.  When ice cream starts to melt down the punch is ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Esther Loveall Saunders
Description: 
Smoothe and delicious.  This is a coffee lovers dream come true.
