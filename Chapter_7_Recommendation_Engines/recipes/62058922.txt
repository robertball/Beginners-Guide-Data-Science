Title:
Southern Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. sausage
8 slices Texas Toast, crusts removed, cubed
6 eggs, beaten
2 cups milk
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 cups mild cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Spray a 9 x 13 pan with non stick cooking spray. Place cubed bread into bottom of pan. Cook sausage until crumbly. Place sausage onto bread. Mix remaining ingredients except cheese and pour over sausage. Top with cheese.
Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight. Bake at 350 for approximately 45 minutes or until set. Place foil over top the last 15 minutes to prevent the cheese from hardening.

Submitter: 
Butterfly Flutterby
Description: 
This casserole is prepared ahead and allows you to enjoy family and friends while it bakes. It's wide appeal allows for use during holidays, showers or just lazy weekends.
