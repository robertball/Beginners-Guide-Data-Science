Title:
Surprise Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Sour Cream
4 TB Brown Sugar

Directions:
Just mix together sour cream and brown sugar until the sugar is dissolved. Taste as you go so that you reach your desired sweetness. You may want to add more or less brown sugar.
Keep refrigerated until serve time.
