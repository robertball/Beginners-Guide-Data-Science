Title:
Gayle's Pink Poppyseed Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120525.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups vinegar
3 cups sugar
4 tsp. dry mustard
4 tsp. salt
6 tbsp. red onion
2 cups canola oil
1 1/2 tbsp. poppy seeds

Directions:
Blend vinegar and sugar. Add dry mustard, salt, red onion and blend again.
Slowly add canola oil and poppy seeds.
