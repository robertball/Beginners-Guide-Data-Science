Title:
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies Treats® Drumsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979783.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal
Peanut butter
Kellogg's® Cocoa Krispies™ cereal

Directions:
In a large microwave safe bowl, heat margarine and marshmallows at HIGH for 3 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes. Stir until smooth.
Add Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal. Stir until well coated.
Using buttered spatula or waxed paper, press mixture into 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan coated with cooking spray. Allow to cool slightly.
Cut cereal mixture with knife into drumstick shapes. Spread peanut butter over top of each drumstick, then dip in Kellogg's(R) Cocoa Krispies(TM) cereal. Chill until firm. Best if served the same day. Microwave cooking times may vary.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
Treat yourself to this Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal creation. Kids are sure to love the fun 'drumstick' shape.
