Title:
Hamburger Hot Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef or ground turkey
1 1/2 Tsp. minced onions
1 garlic clove, pressed
2 cans tomato soup
1 can corn, drained
italian seasoning, to taste
season salt, to taste
3 slices fat-free american cheese slices
1/2 lb. elbow macaroni

Directions:
Cook elbow macaroni according to directions on box.
While macaroni is cooking, brown ground beef or ground turkey and minced onion in a large skillet or pan until almost brown.
Add pressed garlic to meat and cook until meat is done.
Drain meat, if needed, return to skillet or pan.
Add tomato soup, corn, italian seasoning and season salt, mix well.
Drain macaroni when done cooking (do not rinse).
Add macaroni to hamburger mixture and mix well.
Top with cheese slices and cover for 2-3 minutes or until melted. Stir again after cheese has melted.
Serve with a side salad if desired. Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Kristi Goulette Lozensky
Description: 
Hot dish or Goulash, call it what you want. I grew up on this quick and easy meal. I have tweaked the ingredients a bit, but the results are just as good. Serve with a side salad for a great weekday dinner.
