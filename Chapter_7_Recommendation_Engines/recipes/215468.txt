Title:
Three Ingredient Carrot Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4557209.jpg
Ingredients:
5 carrots, coarsely grated
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
1/4 cup oil and vinegar salad dressing (such as Newman's Own)

Directions:
Toss carrots and sunflower seeds in a bowl with the oil and vinegar salad dressing.

Submitter: 
Sylvia Moestl Vasilik
Description: 
This recipe will put some fresh vegetables on your table in almost no time at all!  My kids love it too. All the ingredients keep very well, so it's pantry-friendly.  I keep my sunflower seeds in the freezer, and they stay fresh a long time.
