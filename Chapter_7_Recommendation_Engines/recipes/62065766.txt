Title:
Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb confectioners sugar
1 C graham cracker crumbs
1 C crunchy peanut butter
6 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips, melted

Directions:
Mix sugar, graham cracker crumbs, and peanut butter until combined.  Roll into 1 inch balls and dip into melted chocolate.  Cool on waxed paper.

Submitter: 
Rochelle Brooks Thompson
Description: 
If you like peanut butter and chocolate, your going to love these!  These candies go great on a cookie platter at Christmas time.
