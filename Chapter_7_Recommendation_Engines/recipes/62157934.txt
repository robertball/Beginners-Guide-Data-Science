Title:
Summer Kidney Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (15 oz.) dark kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 can (15 oz.) whole kernal corn, drained and rinsed
1 can (10 oz.) diced tomatos with green chili (like Ro*Tel)
1 T. vinegar
1 T. brown sugar
1/8 tsp. cumin
1/2 T. dried onion flakes
dash salt & pepper

Directions:
Put all canned items in a medium bowl.
Mix vinegar and spices (spoon some liquid from main bowl if needed).  Combine.
Refrigerate at least half an hour?

Submitter: 
Lisa_P
Description: 
Quick and easy to throw together, I created this salad for a summer picnic.
