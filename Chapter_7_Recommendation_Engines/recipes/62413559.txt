Title:
Orange Chicken and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1 1/2 cups water
2 tablespoons orange juice
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup rice vinegar
2 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon grated orange zest
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/8 tsp ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
3 tablespoons cornstarch
2 tablespoons water
Chicken:
2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
3 tablespoons olive oil
Sauteed Vegetables:
1 head of broccoli
2-3 whole carrots, sliced thin (julienne)
Optional: bell peppers, onions or snow peas

Directions:
Steam broccoli and carrots, set aside.
Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, sautee the chicken in olive oil until lightly browned.
Meanwhile, start sauce. Pour 1 1/2 cups water, orange juice, lemon juice, rice vinegar, and soy sauce into a saucepan and set over medium-high heat. Stir in the orange zest, brown sugar, ginger and garlic. Bring to a boil. Mix cornstarch and water in separate container until smooth, add to sauce mixture and stir until thick and bubbly. Remove from heat.
Add veggies and sauce to chicken and simmer over low heat 7-10 mins while rice is cooking.
Serve chicken mixture over rice.

Submitter: 
Kristin McKee
Description: 
Enjoy delicious sauteed chicken and steamed vegetables in an zesty lemon-orange sauce. It's terrific served with rice.Modified from: Asian Orange Chicken
