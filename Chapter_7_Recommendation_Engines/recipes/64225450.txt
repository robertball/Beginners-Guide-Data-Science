Title:
individual beef pot pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
prepared pie dough
3 tbl olive oil
2 lb beef stew meat, chuck roast, or sirloin cut into small cubes
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
2 C chopped mushrooms
2 tbl tomato paste
2 garlic cloves minced (2 tsp)
1/4 tsp thyme
1/8 tsp rosemary
1/2 C dry sherry
5.5 C low sodium beef broth
12 sm red potatoes cubed
2 C fresh or frozen pearl onions
1/2 C cornstarch
1 C cold water
1 1lb bag frozen peas & carrots medley
1/2 C fresh parsley
1 egg & 1 tbl water

Directions:
Heat 1 tbl olive oil in dutch oven over med high heat
pat meat dry, sprinkle with salt & pepper,add 1/2 to pan
Cook, stir occasionally until browned and liquid is gone
Remove to plate and repeat w remaining meat.  Remove to plate
Add 1 tbl olive oil to pann and add mushroom, cook until liquid is gone
stir in tomato paste, garlic, thyme & rosemary.  cook for approx 30 sec.
stir in dry sherry, scraping up any browned bits.
stir in potatoes, broth & browned meat.  Bring to a boil, reduce to simmer, cover & cook until potatoes are cooked and beef is tender - about 45 min
Add onions,
whisk together cornstarch & cold water, stir into soup and cook until thickened - about 2 min
remove from heat, stir in parsley, frozen peas & carrots
