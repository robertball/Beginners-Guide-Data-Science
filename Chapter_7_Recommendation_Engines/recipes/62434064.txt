Title:
Lobster Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chicken broth
2 tbs. finely diced shallot or onion
4 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups heavy cream
2 cups half n half or milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pound cooked, shredded lobster meat
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
3 oz. tomato paste
sherry to taste (1/2 cup to cup)

Directions:
In a large soup pot over medium heat melt the butter and sautee the onions and garlic. Slowly whisk in flour. Whisk until a creamy mixture is created.
Gradually pour in stock, whisking constantly. Whisk in milk, salt, onion, Worcestershire sauce, tomato paste, sherry and cayenne pepper. Add lobster meat. Heat through, but DO NOT boil. Reduce heat and simmer on low for one hour, stirring occassionally to keep milk from sticking.

Submitter: 
Carol
Description: 
Simple and elegant lobster bisque.  Can be used as base recipe for any seafood bisque. The sherry is a must but use sparingly so as not to overpower the lobster.Modified from: Lobster Bisque
