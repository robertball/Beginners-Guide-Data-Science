Title:
Grandma Z's Baileys Irish Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1863303.jpg
Ingredients:
1c Whiskey or Brandy (I use Seagrams Whiskey)
1 1/2 TBSP Hershey's syrup
1/4 tsp coconut extract
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2c heavy whipping cream
3 eggs
1 can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Mix in a blender on medium high for 2 minutes. Refrigerate for 2 days in dark glass bottle before serving. Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
Tracie Schanen
Description: 
My husband's Grandma Z made this every year at Christmas for as long as I can remember. Now, we make it every year and make a delicious toast to her. Cheers, Grandma Z!
