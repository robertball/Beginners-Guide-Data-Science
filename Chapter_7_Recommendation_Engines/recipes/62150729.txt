Title:
Aunt Sonja's Cherry Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. butter, softened
2 c. sugar
4 eggs
3 c. flour
1.2 tsp almond extract
1 tsp. vanilla
1 large can cherry pie filling

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar together.
Add eggs one at a time.
Beat in flour and extracts.
Our 3/4 of the batter in greased 9x13 pan.
Spread cherry pie filling over top of batter.
Drop remaining batter by spoonfuls over top of the cherries.
Bake at 350 for 45 min.

Submitter: 
prettykitten
Description: 
These bars are soft and sweet with a touch of almond flavor that complements the cherry nicely.
