Title:
Teriyaki Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs bone-in chicken pieces or ~ 1.5 lb chicken breasts**
1 egg, beaten
1 cup flour
Oil or butter to fry chicken
Sauce:
3 tbsp. soy sauce
3 tbsp. water
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup vinegar
1 tsp. Mrs. Dash or other seasoning mix
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Dip chicken in egg and flour
Fry in oil or butter till browned
Put in casserole dish
Meanwhile, mix sauce ingredients, heat in micro., stirring till sugar dissolved
Pour sauce over chicken
Bake @ 350 x 1 - 1.5 hrs till tender and baked. Cover for last half of cooking time.
**Reduce cooking time if using chicken breasts

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A tasty teriyaki sauce baked over chicken.
