Title:
Flavorful Pumpkin Turkey Chili with Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
3/4 cup chopped yellow or red bell pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 pound (1 package) ground turkey
2 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
2 cans kidney beans, undrained
29 ounce can pumpkin puree
4 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat, and saute the onion, green bell pepper, red or yellow bell pepper, and garlic until tender. Stir in the turkey, and cook until evenly brown. Mix in tomatoes and pumpkin. Season with chili powder, pepper, nutmeg and salt. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 20 minutes. Serve topped with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.
