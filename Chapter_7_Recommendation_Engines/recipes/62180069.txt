Title:
Easy & Delicious Crockpot Cassoulet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 large onion, finely chopped
4 boneless skinless chicken thighs, chopped
1/4 pound precooked smoked sausage, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
4 tablespoons tomato paste
3 cans (about 15 ounces each) great northern OR white beans, rinsed and drained
6 pieces cooked turkey or pork bacon, crumbled
14 oz diced tomatoes, drained
2 bay leaves
3 tablespoons minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Fry up the turkey or pork bacon. Drain, set aside.
Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add onion to hot oil; cook and stir until onion is tender, about 4 minutes.
Stir in chicken, sausage, garlic, thyme, bay leaves, and pepper.
Cook 5 to 8 minutes, or until chicken and sausage are browned.
Stir in tomato paste; transfer to slow cooker.
In slow cooker, stir great northern beans, tomatoes, and bacon into the chicken mixture.
Cover and cook on LOW 4 to 5 hours.
Before serving, sprinkle parsley over cassoulet.
