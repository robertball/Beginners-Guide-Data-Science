Title:
Mexican Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c prepared quinoa
1 large red bell pepper
1 medium onion, diced
1 16oz can black beans, drained
18oz frozen yellow corn
3 cloves garlic, chopped
1 medium jalapeno, finely chopped
canola oil
olive oil
salt
paprika
cilantro
basil
turmeric (sparingly)
ground garlic

Directions:
Place on high heat pepper and onion in a large frying pan with enough canola oil to coat them and the pan but not leave excess.  Cover.
Place on high heat jalapeno and chopped garlic with enough canola oil to pool.  Cover and allow to partially blacken.
Then add the corn, and black beans to the peppers & onions.  Pour the jalapeno-garlic mixture with its oil into the large pan as well.
When the corn is thawed & warmed, remove from heat and stir in quinoa.  Season to taste with all spices and salt, as well as with olive oil.
Allow to cool if desired.  This can also be served hot.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
Great as a dinner, despite being a salad.  Can be served hot or cold.  To increase spiciness, use a larger jalapeno and/or blacken it less.
