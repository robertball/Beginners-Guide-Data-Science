Title:
Green Curry Paste- Nam Prik Gaeng Kheow Wan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T coriander seeds
1 t cumin seeds
5-10 bird's-eye chilies
3 T sliced green onions
3 cloves garlic, sliced
1 T lemongrass
1/2 t lime zest (kaffir)
1 t chopped cilantro root
5 black peppercorns
1 t salt
1 t dried shrimp paste, roasted (fry dried)

Directions:
Dry coriander and cumin for 5 min over low heat in pan with oil.
Grind to a powder, add remaing (not shrimp paste) and grind.
Add shrimp paste and grind.
