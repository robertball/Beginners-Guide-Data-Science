Title:
Southwestern Beef Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988130.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound extra-lean ground beef
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 cup medium chunky salsa
1 cup water
1 cup frozen corn
1 cup frozen cut green beans
1 1/2 cups instant white rice, uncooked
1 cup KRAFT Tex Mex Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Brown meat with chili powder in large deep skillet.
Add salsa, water, corn and green beans. Bring to boil. Stir in rice; cover. Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 minutes.
Remove from heat. Top with cheese; cover. Let stand 5 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
An easy one skillet meal made with ground beef, rice, and mixed vegetables seasoned with chili powder and salsa.
