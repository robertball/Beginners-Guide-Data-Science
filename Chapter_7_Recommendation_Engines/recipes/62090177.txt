Title:
Mexican Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz package frozen chopped spinach, thawed & drained
1 8oz package cream cheese, room temperature
1/2 white onion, chopped
2 cans diced Rotel tomatoesâ€”1 drained & 1 undrained
12 oz pkg. grated Mexican style (four cheese) blend
1/3 c. sour cream
1/4 t. ground cumin (can use seeds as well)
1/2 t. chili powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, mixing thoroughly.
Turn into lightly greased baking or casserole dish.
Bake 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
Serve hot, and dip with Wheat Thins or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
KAC2
Description: 
This Mexican Spinach dip is served warm, and it is almost like a spinach queso, but even tastier.  It is super easy and I have brought it to many potlucks and always seem to have someone requesting this recipe!!
