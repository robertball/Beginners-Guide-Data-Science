Title:
Tomato Mozzarella and Basil  Salad; Wilton Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1 c oilive oil
2 tsp sugar
3 clove garlic, crushed
8 cChopped leaf lettuce
10 tomatoes, sliced; 8 slices each
20 balls of fresh mozzarella, sliced
1 # fresh basil leaves cleaned and trimmed
salt and black pepper to taste
2

Directions:
Mix balsamic vinegar, olive oil and crushed garlic; set aside
Divide the lettuce leaves among 40 salad plates.
Layer tomato slices then fresh mozzarella and fresh basil
drizzle with the olive oil and balsamic vinegar mixture.

Submitter: 
Gretchen
Description: 
This elegant green salad garnished made with fresh mozzarella, ripe slicing tomatoes, raspberries, and olives is sure to impress your guests.Modified from: Tomato Mozzarella Salad with Balsamic Reduction
