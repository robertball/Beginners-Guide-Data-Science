Title:
Thanksgiving Turkey gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Turkey pan juice and drippings
flour
potato water saved from the mashed potatoes
water or milk as needed
salt and pepper to taste
Cooked giblets, chopped small

Directions:
Remove your turkey from the pan and put it on a platter to rest.  If there is a lot of juice/grease in the bottom, remove some until you have desired amount Take the roasting pan and heat it on the stove...add a TB of flour at a time  stirring until you have a rue that is lightly browned.  Add the potato water a little at a time stirring until the pan drippings are loosened.  If there is not enough water add additional plain water and or milk/half and half.  When desired consistancy is acheived add salt and pepper to taste and the cooked chopped giblets.
Serve immediately

Submitter: 
jadedgurl
Description: 
Basic gravy for Thanksgiving but with my Aunt's secret trick that makes it seem like no other gravy would be as good with your potatoes.  So simple you will wonder why you did not think of it!
