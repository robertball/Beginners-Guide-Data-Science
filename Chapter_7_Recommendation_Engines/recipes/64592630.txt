Title:
Jam Diagonals Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3808684.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. lemon flavoring (I usually use almond)
2 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/3 cup jam (your choice - a red jam is festive)

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar in bowl.
Beat in egg, vanilla and flavoring.
Stir flour, baking powder and salt together; add and mix.
Divide dough into 8 portions. Make each into a rope about 10" long and 3/4" wide.
Place on baking sheets (makes about 2 sheets full).
With side of your hand, press to indent along center lengthwise of each strip.
Spread jam in indentations.
Bake @ 350 x 15-17 min.
Cut while warm into 1" diagonal slices.
Makes about 5 doz.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A tasty cookie baked as a log with jam down the center and then cut into diagonal slices. A pretty addition to baking trays.
