Title:
Frothy Orange Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (6 oz) frozen orange juice concentrate, unthawed
1 cup water
1 cup milk
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
8-10 cubes of ice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender; cover and process drink until thick ans slushy.
