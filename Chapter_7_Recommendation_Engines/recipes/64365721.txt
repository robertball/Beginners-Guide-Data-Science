Title:
Oven-baked Pork Chops with Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooking spay
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
4 (6 oz) center-cut pok chops
2 large crisp apples. cored and each cut into 12 wedges
1 red onion, cut into 12 wedges
1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
1/3 cup unsalted chicken stock
3 tablespoons cider vinegar
1 tablespoon  extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
3 cloves grlic, minced

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 425.  Coat a baking dish with cooking spray
2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.  Add butter, swirl until it melts.
3.  Add garlic and pork chops to pan
4.  Cook ~3 minutes each side or until browned
5.  Transfer pork chops to center of baking dish, scatter appe, onion, and thyme around the pork chops
6.  Combine stock, vinegar, and oil. Drizzle over te pork chops and vegetables.
7.  Roast at 425 for 20 minutes or until apples are golden brown, and pork chops are desired degress of doneness
