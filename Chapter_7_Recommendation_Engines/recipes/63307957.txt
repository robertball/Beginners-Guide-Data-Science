Title:
Broiled Halibut with Lemon-Thyme Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 6oz. halibut filets
LEMON-THYME BUTTER ingredients
1 Tbsp. butter, melted
1/4c. water
Juice squeezed from one large lemon
2 Tbsp. dried thyme
1 Tbsp. salt
1 Tbsp. pepper

Directions:
Combine Lemon-thyme ingredients in a small bowl.
Preheat broiler.
Place filets on broiling pan.
Brush filets with butter.
Broil 10 - 15 mins. or until halibut flakes easily with fork. Surface of halibut should be 3-4 in. from broiler.
Continue brushing halibut with butter, often.
