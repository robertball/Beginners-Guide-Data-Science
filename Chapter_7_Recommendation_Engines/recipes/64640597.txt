Title:
Chicken Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4513213.jpg
Ingredients:
48 ounces chicken stock or broth
2 cups chicken, diced and cooked
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 clove garlic, crushed
2 stalks of celery, chopped
2 carrots (Optional), chopped and blanch
½ teaspoon sage (Optional)
½ teaspoon poultry seasoning
½ (12 ounce) package frozen corn kernels
2 cups rice, cooked

Directions:
Throw everything into a pot and simmer until celery and carrots are tender.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
After cooking a whole chicken, make chicken stock from it and reserve some chicken.  Add some cooked rice and vegetables and you have a simple but tasty soup. I've been known to drop an egg into a hot bowl of this soup, dust with some white pepper, and drizzle with some sesame oil.
