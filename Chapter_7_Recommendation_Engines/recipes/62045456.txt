Title:
Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups broccoli
2 cups cauliflower
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup mayo
2 eggs
1 cup cheddar cheese
1/2 cup butter melted
1 package seasoned croutons

Directions:
1.  Cook broccoli and cauliflower separately for 3-4 minutes in boiling water.  Drain and spread together in the bottom of 7x11 baking dish.
2.  Beat eggs until creamy then mix together with onion, mayo, and 1/2 cup of the cheddar cheese.  Pour over the vegetables.
3.  Melt the butter and pour over.  Spread the remaining cheese on top and try to press lightyly over the whole dish.
4.  Crush croutons and spread over the top.  You need just enough to cover the top!
5.  Bake at 350 until browned and bubbling.
