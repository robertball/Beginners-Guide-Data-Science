Description: 
This is a take-off of Brad's favorite salad from Little Apple Brewery in Manhattan. It has Black Angus steak, crumbled Bleu cheese, grape tomatoes, croutons, honey-roasted almonds, and red onion slices topped with a balsamic vinaigrette. I sometimes add sliced mushrooms.
