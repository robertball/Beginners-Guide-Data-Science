Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb of ground beef
1 packet of taco seasoning
1 can of beans (pinto, kidney, or black)
1 can of white hominey
1 can of whole kernel corn
1 can of diced tomatoes
1 can of tomato sauce
salt and pepper
Garnish
sour cream
cheddar cheese
green onions
crushed tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown ground beef over medium-high heat, follow directions on taco packet, combine beans, hominey, corn, tomatoes, and tomato sauce and let simmer for 15 to 20 minutes. Place crushed tortilla chips in bowl, spoon soup over chips, garnish with sour cream, cheese, and onions.

Submitter: 
Lindsey Watson
Description: 
Wonderful combination of tacos and chili all mashed together.
