Title:
Authentic Napoli Margherita pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1055396.jpg
Ingredients:
Pizza dough(homemade or store bought
1 can of San Marzano tomatoes (whole, peeled)
1 ball of buffalo di mozzarella (or any other quality mozzarella cheese)
Olive oil
Salt
4 fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 500 degrees F, and place pizza stone in the oven while it's pre heating.
Open a can of peeled whole San Marzano tomatoes and place into a sauce pan over medium high heat, crush tomatoes with the back of a spoon stirring occasionally,  and simmer for 15 minutes.
Add salt, pepper and a basil leave to the sauce.
Roll out the dough and place on  the preheated pizza stone.
Put a tablespoonful of sauce in the middle of the dough and roll it out in a circular motion.
Generously salt the pizza
Tear pieces of the mozzarella and place around the sauce. (Try to put 1-2 pieces of cheese for every slice)
Drizzle olive oil in a circular motion around the pizza.
Place pizza in the oven and bake for  15-20 minutes  or until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted.
Before serving tear 3 basil leaves and sprinkle on top of the pizza.
