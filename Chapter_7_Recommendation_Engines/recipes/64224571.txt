Title:
Crock Pot Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1666788.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon butter
1 cup of Basmati rice
2 cups water
1 pinch Kosher salt

Directions:
Use a 4-quart slow cooker.  Rub the butter on the inside of the stoneware.  Pour in the rice. Stir in the water and salt.  Cover and cook on high for 2 to 3 hours, checking ever 45 minutes or so.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
Trying to find creative uses for the crock pot and discovered it makes a fine rice cooker!
