Title:
Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2# beef stew meat
1/4 C flour
2 Tbsp oil
4 C hot water
4 cubes beef bouillon
tsp Rosemary
tsp Parsley
1/2 tsp Pepper
tsp Garlic powder
1/2 tsp Paprika
Tbsp Worcestershire
bay leaf
3 potatoes cubed
4 carrots chunked
4 stalks celery sliced
1 onion, chopped

Directions:
Coat meat in flour. Brown in oil in large pot. Dissolve bouillon in water and add to pot. Add herbs, spices, and Worcestershire, cover, and simmer for an hour. Add rest of ingredients. Simmer for another hour or until vegetables are tender. If not thick enough, add a corn starch slurry or some masa in and cook till thick enough.
