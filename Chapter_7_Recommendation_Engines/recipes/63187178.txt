Title:
Joyce's Fantastic Lemon Easter Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4cup + 2 tablespoons water 100 degrees
2 tablespoons softened butter
2 eggs, at room temperature
2 tablespoons (yes, tablespoons!) pure lemon extract
3 1/2 cups bread flour
1/2 cup sugar
3 teaspoons yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients into bread machine per manufacturer
Place on dough setting
When finished, remove and divide into 3 sections
place each section into  a well greased 8" round cake pan or pie plate. Cover and  let rise for 1 hour.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes, do not overbake.

Submitter: 
parrish
Description: 
This recipe was developed by my good friend, Joyce. It was written for bread machine, but I'm sure it could be adapted.
Light bread with a wonderful lemon flavor!  Try toasted...Yum!!
