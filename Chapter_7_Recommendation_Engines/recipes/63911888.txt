Title:
Big Ray's Vino Italian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084941.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp paprika
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
3 pounds bone-less, skin-less chicken breast
1/2 pound mushrooms,sliced
1 medium sweet red pepper, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1-1/2 cups chardonnay
1 can (14 oz) diced tomato, drained
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 Tbl minced fresh thyme or 2 tsp dried thyme
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
Hot cooked pasta
Shredded Romano cheese

Directions:
Combine the paprika, salt, pepper and cayenne; sprinkle over chicken. Place the chicken, mushrooms, red pepper, and onion in a 5-qt. slow cooker.
In a small bowl, combine the chardonnay, diced tomato , garlic and thyme; pour over vegetables.
Cover and cook on low for 5-6 hours or until chicken is tender. Remove chicken and shred, replace into sauce.  Stir in parsley. Serve with pasta; sprinkle with cheese. Yield: 6 servings.

What more could you ask for? A skillet dish that has been altered  into this delicious slow cooker meal, and it's ready and waiting when you get home. It's perfect for a weeknight meal but is nice enough for company, too.
