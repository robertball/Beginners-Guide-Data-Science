Title:
Caesar Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981686.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/4 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese, divided
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 KRAFT DELI DELUXE Low-Moisture Part-Skim Mozzarella Cheese Slices
2 cups torn romaine lettuce
6 tablespoons KRAFT Classic Caesar Dressing, divided
4 hamburger buns, split

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium heat. Mix meat, 3 tablespoons of the Parmesan cheese and the garlic. Shape into four patties.
Grill 6 minutes on each side or until burgers are cooked through (160 degrees F). Top with cheese slices. Continue grilling 1 minute or until cheese begins to melt.
Toss lettuce with 2 tablespoons of the dressing and the remaining 1 tablespoon  Parmesan cheese. Spoon evenly onto bottom halves of buns; top with patties and remaining 4 tablespoons dressing. Cover with tops of buns.

Submitter: 
Kraft
Description: 
Fit for an emperor, these grilled burgers are livened up by the flavors of Parmesan cheese, garlic, and Caesar dressing.
