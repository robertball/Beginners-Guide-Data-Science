Title:
Camping Sweet Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bags Boil-in-bag white rice
2/3 cup. dry milk
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. ground cloves
1 tsp. nutmeg
3 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup shredded sweetend coconut
1 cut chopped/dried fruit (apricots, raisins, cranberries, mango-you decide)

Directions:
At home: combine dried milk, coconut, and spices in a ziplock bag.
In Camp: cook rice as directed on the package. As the water begins to boil add dried fruit to pot. Remove bag of rice and drain, leaving fruit and about 1 cup of liquid in pot. Add the spice bag. Slit open the rice and add to the pot. Stir to combine for a yummy breakfast or dessert. Serves 8

Submitter: 
Andrea Luhman
Description: 
Learned how to make this as a Girl Scout back packing at Isle Royal National Park.
