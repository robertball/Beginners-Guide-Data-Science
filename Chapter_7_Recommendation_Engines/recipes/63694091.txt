Title:
Roasted Potato Salad with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds red or Yukon gold potatoes, washed and larger ones cut in half to create even pieces
Olive oil
6 slices of thick cut bacon, cooked crisp and broken into small pieces
Salt and Pepper to taste (for roasting the potatoes)
1 C mayonnaise
1/4 C sour cream or plain Greek yogurt
1/2 tsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. cider vinegar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Arrange the potatoes on a baking sheet, sprayed with PAM nonstick spray.  Season the potatoes with salt and pepper and drizzle with olive oil.  Bake @ 400 degrees, turning the potatoes half way through cooking.  When browned and soft, remove the potatoes and allow to cool completely.
Place the potatoes, vegetables and the bacon in a large mixing bowl.  Set aside.
In a medium bowl, mix the mayo, the sour cream, the sugar, the cider vinegar and the spices.  Stir 1/2 of the dressing into the potatoes and coat completely  Cover and store the potatoes several hours or overnight in the refrigerator and store the rest of the dressing in the refrigerator as well.  One hour before serving mix in the remaining dressing and return to the refrigerator until serving.  Store leftovers in the refrigerator.
