Title:
Big Ray's One-Dish Chicken Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1080486.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups  chicken stock, heated
1/4 cup  butter, melted
1 pkg. Stuffing Mix for Chicken (6 oz.)
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts (1-1/2 lb.)
1 can(10-3/4 oz.)condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/3 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
salt
pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 375°F. Prepare stuffing as directed. Place chicken in 13x9-inch baking dish or 2-qt. casserole. Mix soup and sour cream; pour over chicken. Top with seasonings and stuffing. Bake 35 min. or until chicken is done.chicken is done (165ºF).
