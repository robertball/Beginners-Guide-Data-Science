Title:
Killer Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground pork sausage
2 cloves garlic minced
1 Tbsp dried basil
1 tsp salt
1/28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
2/6 oz cans tomato paste
10 oz lasagna noodles
3 cups fresh Ricotta cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan or Romano cheese
4 Tbsp fresh parsley minced
2 eggs beaten
2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 lb Mozzarella cheese thinly sliced

Directions:
Brown sausage in a skillet.
Add next 5 ingredients and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.
Boil noodles till al dente, drain and set aside.
Combine remaining ingredients (except Mozzarella cheese) in a large mixing bowl.
Place 1/2 of the noodles into a 9" x 13" x 2" baking dish.
Spread 1/2 of the egg/ricotta mixture over the noodles.
Spread 1/2 of the meat/marinara sauce over the ricotta.
Repeat for second layer.
Bake for about 30 minutes in a preheated oven at 375* or until lasagne is bubbling.
Remove from oven and allow to stand for 12 minutes before serving.
You can also assemble early, cover and refrigerate. Be sure to add at least 15 minutes to cook time.
