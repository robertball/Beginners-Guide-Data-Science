Title:
Fresh Lemon Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1   Cup butter softened
2   Cups white sugar
2   Cups All Purpose flour
5   Large eggs slighty beaten
1/4 Cup Fresh Lemon juice(A little extra is OK)
Zest of one large lemon

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 325 degrees.                     In a high speed mixer bowl or large bowl cream butter and sugar until fluffy.Add 1 egg at at time alternating with the flour. Beat in lemon juice and zest until combined.
Pour into greased and floured bundt pan.

Submitter: 
Handy Andy
Description: 
This is a beautiful golden colored moist cake that stays fresh that you can bake two or three days ahead. I used a Nordic 10 cup bundt pan that provided eye catching presentation.
