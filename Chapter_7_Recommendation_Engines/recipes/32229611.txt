Title:
Sweet & Salty cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 C coarsely chopped dry roasted peanuts
1 C flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 C granulated sugar
1/2 C brown sugar
1/4 C softened butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 egg
1/3 C choc chips
1/2 tsp sea salt

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350
2.  Place nuts in baking pan and bake for 8 min.
3.  Combine flour, baking powder & soda.
4.  Place sugars and butter in large bowl and beat w/mixer at md speed until well blended.  Add vanilla and egg; beat until combined.  Add flour to sugar mixture; beat at low speed until well blended.  Stir in peanuts, choc chips, and salt.
5.  Drop by tsp onto cookie sheet 2" apart.  Bake for 10 min.
