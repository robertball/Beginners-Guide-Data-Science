Title:
Iced Green Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Green Tea Bags
4 cups boiling water
4 cups cold water
2 tablespoons fine-grated orange zest
1 cup honey
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Ice (for serving)
Lemon wedges (for serving)

Directions:
Steep tea bags in the boiling water for 20 minutes
Add the honey to the tea-infused water
In pitcher, combine zest, lemon juice, cold water, and tea mixture. Chill.
Serve over ice with lemon wedge, as desired.

Submitter: 
Hugatunity
Description: 
This light, slightly tart, citrus-flavored tea is perfect for summer sipping!
