Title:
Hot and Spicy Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 egg white
3 cups mixed nuts (1 cup each: pecans, almonds, cashews)
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon McCormick® Season-All® Seasoned Salt
1 teaspoon McCormick® Curry Powder
1 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Cinnamon
1 teaspoon McCormick® Cumin Seed
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Red Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees F.  Beat egg white until foamy.  Add nuts, tossing to coat.  Combine remaining ingredients in a medium bowl; toss with nuts to coat.
Spread nuts on a greased baking sheet in a single layer.  Bake 1 hour, stirring once.  Cool slightly and break apart.  Cool completely and store in airtight container up to two weeks.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Hot and spicy nuts are a great 'munchie' to snack on during any game. They also make a nice addition to sports themed gift baskets.
