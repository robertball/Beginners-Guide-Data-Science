Title:
Slow cooker Turkey Cheeseburger Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Turkey burger
2 packets of your favorite Chili seasonings
1 29 oz can tomato sauce
1 14.5 oz can of fire roasted diced tomatoes
1/2 cup of Ketchup
1/4 cup of Mustard
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 cans dark red kidney beans
1 package shredded colby jack cheese or favorite shredded cheese.
1 onion of your choosing

Directions:
In medium pan brown the turkey burger. Drain
In medium sauce pan bring to a low boil,
Tomato sauce,fire roasted tomatoes,chili seasoning, Ketchup,mustard, and brown sugar. Sprinkle brown sugar to taste.
Drain kidney beans just a bit and pour them into a slow cooker.
dice your onion into small bits stir into kidney beans.
Pour in Sauce Mixture mix well.
Add drained turkey burger mix well.
Let simmer on high for 20 min turn to low
Serve topped with cheese
