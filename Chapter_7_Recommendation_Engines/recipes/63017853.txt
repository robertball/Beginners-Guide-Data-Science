Title:
Oatmeal Craisin Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755515.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. butter, softened
1 c. white sugar
1 c. packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla extract
2 c. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
3 c. quick oats
1/2 c. mini chocolate chips
2 handfuls Craisins, finely chopped
1/4 c. slivered almonds

Directions:
In a large bowl,cream butter and both sugars together.
Beat in the eggs, one at a time.
Add vanilla and mix.
In a medium bowl,combine all the dry ingredients minus the oats.
Stir the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients to combine.
Mix in the quick oats, then the chocolate chips, craisins and almonds.
Cover  dough and chill for 1 hr.
Preheat oven to 225 degrees when ready to bake cookies.
Roll dough into walnut size balls and place 2 inches apart on cookie sheet.
Flatten cookies just slightly with the back of a large spoon.
Bake for 15-17 minutes.
Let cool 5 minutes on cookie sheet before removing to a wire rack.
