Title:
Gingersnaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 butter or margarine, softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup molasses (any variety seems to work)
2 1/2 cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tbsp. ginger
1 heaping tsp. cinnamon
1/2 + tsp. cloves
1/2 tsp. salt
Sugar

Directions:
Mix butter and 1st amount of sugar together well.
Beat in egg.
Mix in molasses.
Stir flour, baking soda, salt and spices together and add.
Mix well.
Shape into 1" balls and roll in sugar.
Bake on cookie sheet @ 350 x 12 - 13 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Soft, chewy ginger goodness and, after amping up the spices, extra delicious! I didn't care for the results when using a convection oven, so wouldn't recommend it. From my favorite cookbook series.
