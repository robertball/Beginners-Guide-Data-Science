Title:
Poppy Seed Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Butter Cake Mix
4 Eggs
1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
1/2 Cup Cream Sherry
1/3 Cup Poppy Seeds
1 sm package instante French vanilla pudding
1 Cup Sour Cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Stir all ingredients slowly until mixed
Beat 5 min at medium speed
Bake 1 hour in greased and floured bundt pan
Cool 15 min
Sprinkle with powdered sugar
