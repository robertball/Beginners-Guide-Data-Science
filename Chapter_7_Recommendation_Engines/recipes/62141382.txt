Title:
Awesome Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lb. Ground Beef(80/20)
3 Tbs. Ketchup
3 Tbs. Mustard
1 Medium Onion
1 Tbs. Minced Garlic
1 Tbs. Worcestershire Sauce
(Desired Amount of Bread Crumbs)
1 Jar of Meat Sauce

Directions:
Preheat Oven To 375 Degrees
1.) In a large bowl combine all ingredients except meat sauce.
2.) Add enough Bread crumbs to make the mix not to wet.
3.) Form the mixture into 1 large meatloaf or a few single servings,you can also make it into meatballs.
4.)Pour half of the jar of meatsauce into bottom of baking pan, place the meatloaf into the pan and pour the remaining sauce over the top of the loaf.
5.)Place in oven for 1-2 hours.
6.)Enjoy!!
