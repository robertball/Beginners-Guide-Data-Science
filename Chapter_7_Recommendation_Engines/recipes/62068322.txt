Title:
Shrimp Salad Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  pound trimmed asparagus, sliced diagonally into 2 inch pieces
1  pound large shrimp, cooked and peeled
2 cups cubed and seeded English cucumbers
2 cups chopped tomatoes
1/2 cup diced bell pepper
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1/4 cup thinly sliced fresh basil
1/4 cup tomato juice
3 T. red wine vinegar
1 T. olive oil
2 t. sugar
1/8 t. hot sauce
2 garlic cloves, minced
salt and pepper to taste
8 cups salad greens

Directions:
Steam asparagus for 3 minutes or until crisp-tender.  Drain and rinse in ice cold water, drain well.  In a large bowl, combine asparagus, shrimp, cucumber, tomato, pepper, onion and basil.
In a small bowl, combine juice, vinegar, oil, sugar, hot sauce, garlic and salt and pepper. Stir gressing with a whisk until well blended.  Pour over shrimp mixture and toss well.  Serve over greens. (2 cups salad and 2 cups greens)

Submitter: 
Amy Brolsma
Description: 
I serve this as a main-dish salad either cold or at room temperature.  It's especially great on a warm summer day.
