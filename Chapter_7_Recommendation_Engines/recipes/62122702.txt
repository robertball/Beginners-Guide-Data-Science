Title:
Green Beans A Step Above Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds green beans, trimmed, rinsed, and drained
6 small red potatoes, halved or quartered
1 package dry onion soup mix
6 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled
2 cloves minced garlic
pepper to taste
1 beef boulion cube
water enough to cover

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan add all ingredients, making sure enough water is added to cover by 1 inch above the green beans.
Stir well.
Bring to a boil, then turn down to a simmer for about 20-25 minutes, until green beans and potatoes are fork tender.

Submitter:

Description: 
If you are tired of the same ol' same ol' way that green beans can taste? Then take them a step above the usual.
