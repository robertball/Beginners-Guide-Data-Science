Title:
Grilled Brats and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. mayonnaise
2 T. stone ground mustard
3/4 lb. petite new potatoes (about 10 - 12)
1/4 c. water
2 tsp. olive oil
2 garlic cloves, pressed
1 1/2 tsp. dried dill
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
6 large green onions
1 lb. bratwurst (about 6 sausages), cooked
12 cherry tomatoes

Directions:
Combine mustard and mayonnaise in small dish; refrigerate.
Cut potatoes in half (or quarters depending on size).
Place cut potatoes and water in a covered microwave safe dish.
Microwave on high 5 - 7 minutes or until fork tender.
Drain potatoes and place in a medium mixing bowl.
Toss warm potatoes with oil, garlic, dill, salt, & pepper.
Trim green onions to 2 inch lengths (white and light green parts only, no tops) and toss with potatoes and oil.
Cut cooked bratwurst into 1 inch pieces and toss with potatoes and oil.
Alternately thread bratwurst, potatoes, onions, and cherry tomatoes on skewers.
Grill 6 - 8 min or until bratwurst is just browned.
Serve with mustard sauce.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
This is a super simple recipe.  The garlic dill oil can be increased to cover more veggies.  This can be done indoors under the broiler.  The mustard sauce makes the dish, double it!
