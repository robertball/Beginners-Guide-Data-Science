Title:
Baked Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground turkey
1 Tbsp. oil
1 c. finely chopped onion
2 (15oz.) cans tomato sauce
28 oz. can whole tomatoes, undrained
15 oz. can kidney beans, drained
3-4 Tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 - 1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 325Â°F. In 4-Quart Dutch oven, brown ground turkey in oil; add onion & cook until crisptender. Add remaing ingredients; mix well. Cover; bake for 50 - 60 minutes or until hot & bubbly.

Description: 
Ground turkey has much less fat than ground beef. Chili is an excellent way to take advantage of this. Serve with a salad and Cornmeal Muffins.
