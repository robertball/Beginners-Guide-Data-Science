Title:
Pampered chef potato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large baking potatoes (about 2.5 lbs)
3 1/2 cups milk divided
4 oz cream cheese, softened
2 Tbsp butter
2-3 green onion with tops
8 oz sharp cheddar cheese grated
1.5 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
Toppings, bacon, sour cream, green onion.....

Directions:
Slice potatoes in half lengthwise . Plac in deep covered baker. Pour 1/2 cup of milk over potatoes. Microwave covered on high for 11 min or until fork tender. Remove from the microwave and coarsely mash with the mix and chop.
Meanwhile wisk cream chhese until smooth. Begin slowly adding 3 cups of milk. Add cream cheese mixture and butter to the baker. Microwave covered on high 3-5 min. till hot. Slice green onions.
Remove from microwave. Add grated cheddar, green onions and s&p. Serve wth toppings

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This was a delicious loaded bake potato soup that we had at a party. Very easy too!
