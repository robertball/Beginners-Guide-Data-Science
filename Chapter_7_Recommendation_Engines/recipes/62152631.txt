Title:
Peanut Butter Swirl Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsweetened peanut butter
1/3 cup butter, softened
3/4-1 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
6 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease 9 X 13 X 2 baking pan
Mix: peanut butter, butter, sugar, eggs, vanilla
Add: flour, baking powder, salt
Pour into baking pan
Sprinkle chocolate chips over all
Bake 5 minutes only
Remove pan from oven and swirl chocolate with a knife
Return pan to oven and bake 20 minutes or until soft set on top
