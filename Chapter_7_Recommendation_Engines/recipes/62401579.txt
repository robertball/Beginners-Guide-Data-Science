Title:
yummy chicken casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
meat of 1 rotisserie chicken, skinned and diced
(or 6-8 chicken breasts, cooked and diced)
1 can cream of chicken soup
8 oz. sour cream
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/2 tsp. celery salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 sleeve Ritz crackers
1/2 stick margarine or butter, melted

Directions:
stir chopped chicken, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, salts, and pepper until well-combined. crush Ritz crackers and sprinkle on top.  pour melted butter evenly over the crumbs.  place in 2 qt. baking dish.  bake at 375 for approx. 30 mins or until brown and bubbly on top.  serve over hot, buttered rice.

Submitter: 
Jenifer Myers Crane
Description: 
my family's favorite dish! delicious served over hot, buttered rice with some garlic green beans and hot, fresh bread :) nummy!
