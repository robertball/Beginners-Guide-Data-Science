Title:
My chicken n dumplins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bone in chicken breasts
1 small yellow onion (chopped)
2 carrots (chopped)
1 can condensed cream of celery soup
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
5 cups water
2 cups Bisquick mix
2/3 cup of milk
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Bring water to a boil in a large pot.
Boil chicken for 20  mins.
Saute onions and carrots while chicken is boiling.
Add garlic powder,salt & pepper to veggies.
Once chicken is cooked pull meat apart.
Bite size pieces are great!
Using the water left from the large pot-
stir in both soups and veggies & add chicken.
Bring to a boil.
In a bowl mix together the bisquick & milk.
Using a spoon or an scoop make small balls.
Gently drop the balls into the liquid.
Make sure the dough cooks for 20 minutes.
