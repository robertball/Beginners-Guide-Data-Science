Title:
Easy Meatballs with Sauces Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 egg
2 tablespoons water
1/2 cup bread crumbs
3 tablespoons minced onion
Sauce;
Use favorite BBQ sauce or use
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup Dijon mustard (any prepared brown mustard will do)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, egg, water, bread crumbs, and minced onion. Roll into small meatballs.Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, turning once.
In a slow cooker or large saucepan over low heat add sauce. Then add meatballs, and cook on low for 1 hour before serving.
