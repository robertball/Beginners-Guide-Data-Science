Title:
Shalae's Sour Cream Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 cup sugar
2 large eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/4 cups mashed bananas
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Sift dry ingredients.
Cream butter with sugar, add eggs, vanilla, bananas, nuts and sour cream. Gradually add dry ingredients and mix at low speed 30 seconds until blended.
Pour into a greased 9x5x3 loaf pan and bake at 350 for one hour or until bread tests done.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description:
