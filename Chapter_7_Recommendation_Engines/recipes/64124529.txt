Title:
Easy Crockpot Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 boneless chicken breasts
3/4 c orange marmalade
3/4 c BBQ sauce
2 tbsp soy sauce

Directions:
Cook chicken in crock pot on medium for 3 hours, then drain.
Mix sauce together and pour over chicken.
Cook on high for an additional 30 minutes

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
Easy and quick. Can convert to stovetop cooking easily.
