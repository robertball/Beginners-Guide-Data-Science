Title:
Mr. Maggie's Egg Stuff! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
6 unpeeled hard-cooked eggs, mashed
1 (4.5 ounce) jar baby food, fruit and vegetable mix
1 large carrot, grated
1 apple, grated
1/4 cup rolled oats
1/4 cup fruit pellets for cockatiels (from pet store)
2 tablespoons bird seed mix
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
1/4 cup apple juice, or as needed

Directions:
Mix eggs with their shells, baby food, carrot, apple, oats, fruit pellets, bird seed mix, and cinnamon in a large bowl until combined. Add apple juice as needed to make a thick paste.
Shape egg mixture into 24 individual balls and place on a baking sheet. Freeze for 1 hour, transfer to zip-top plastic bags, and return to the freezer.
Defrost balls in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours before feeding.

Submitter: 
Danielle
Description: 
Mr. Maggie loves this recipe and will often get right into it, needing a bath afterwards. No one ever said cockatiels were neat eaters!
