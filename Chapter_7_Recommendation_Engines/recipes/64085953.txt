Title:
Slow Cooker Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rub
------
1   4 or 5 lb Brisket
1/2 cup dark brown sugar, packed
1   Tbls Cumin
little bit Paprika
1   tspn salt
2   tspn black pepper
2   Tbls Chipotle Chile Peppers in Adobo Sauce
Slow Cooker
-----------
1   medium white or yellow onion finely chopped
2   Tbls tomato paste
1   Tbls chile powder
1   Tbls Chipotle Chili Peppers in Adobo Sauce
2   Cloves garlic minced
1/2 cup water or beef broth/bouillon
BBQ Sauce
---------
1/4 cup Ketchsup
1   Tbls cider vinegar
1/4 tsp liquid smoke

Directions:
1.  Score both top and bottom of brisket in cross hatch about 1/2" apart.
2.  Mix Rub ingredients together and rub into all parts of Brisket.
3.  Wrap Brisket in cling wrap for 1 hour.
4.  Use 3 tbsp oil in skillet and saute onions until soft (5 min), then add tomato paste (for 1 min).  Add cjo;e [pwder. chiotle chile, minced garlic for another min.
5.  Put onion mixture into slow cooker under aluminum loaf pan.  Add 1/2 cup water/broth/bouillon. Put Brisket on top of loaf pan.
6   Cook for 7 to 8 hours on High (10 to 12 hours on Low)
7.  Remove from slow cooker, cover in aluminum foil for 30 minutes.  Then slice across the grain.
8.  Pour juices from slow cooker into fat separator and let sit for 5 minutes.  Pour 1/2 of the juice over meat.
9.  Pour 1 cup of juice into bowl and add 1/4 ketchup, 1 Tbls cider vinegar and 1/4 tsp liquid smoke; stir and serve with brisket, with coleslaw

Submitter: 
John Adrian
Description: 
Dry, slow cooked, beef brisket served as dinner or shredded for sandwiches.
