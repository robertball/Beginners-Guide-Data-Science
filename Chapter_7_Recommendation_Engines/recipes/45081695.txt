Title:
Oma's Cheater Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups fine egg noodles
(cooked seperately)
12 cups water
4 tablespoons chicken oxo (more or less to taste)
4 tablespoons vegetable seasoning (more or less to taste)
1 tablespoon parsley
1 grated carrot
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
1 whole cooked deli chicken

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add egg noodles and oil, and boil for 8 minutes, or until tender. Drain, and rinse under cool running water.
In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, combine water, oxo, parsley and vegetable seasoning. Stir in carrot, celery and onion. Tear up whole chicken into small pieces, add to vegetables. Bring to boil.Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15-25 minutes.
Add noodles to bowl, ladle out broth over noodles and serve.
Throw out carcass or use to make next batch of chicken soup base.

Submitter: 
Michele
Description: 
This is a recipe that was given to me by my grandmother.  It is a very savory and tasty soup and I believe that all will like it.  If you would like to add even more flavor, try using smoked chicken!!Modified from: Grandma's Chicken Noodle Soup
