Title:
French Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup semisweet chocolate pieces
1/4 cup light corn syrup
3 tablespoons water
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup chilled whipping cream
4 cups milk

Directions:
Heat chocolate pieces, corn syrup and water over low heat, stirring until chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth. Add in vanilla and chill.
In chilled bowl, beat cream until stiff, adding chilled chocolate gradually. Continue beating until mixture mounds when dropped from a spoon. Refrigerate
Just before serving, heat milk through but do not boil.
Fill cups 1/2 full with whipped cream mixture.
Fill cups with milk and blend.
