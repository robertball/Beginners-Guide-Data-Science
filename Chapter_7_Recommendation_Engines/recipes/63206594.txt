Title:
Beef Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bag of baby carrots sliced
1 can beef consomme
1-2 Tabs butter
1-2 Tabs Brown sugar

Directions:
Heat the beef consomme over medium high head until it comes to a boiled adding the carrots and turning down to a simmer. once the carrots have gotten to your liking of  doneness,  add the butter and brown sugar and reduce to a syrup keeping a close eye to make sure they do not burn.

Submitter: 
Christina S. Wall
Description: 
These are yummy and full of flavor, my mom made them one time and I fell in love with the flavor. you can speed up or slow down the cooking of these depending on how long the rest of your meal will take.
