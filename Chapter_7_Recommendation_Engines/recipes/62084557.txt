Title:
Chicken Diane Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken breat halves
1 cup green onions
1/2 cup white wine
1 Tbls. dijon mustard
1 tsp. lime juice
1/2 chicken broth
8 ozs fresh mushrooms
2 Tbls butter
4 Tbls Olive Oil

Directions:
Pound the chicken breats until they are 1/8 inch thick.  Melt butter in pan with the Olive oil over medium high heat.  Place the chicken in the pan and cook for about 1 - 1 1/2 minutes on each side.  Add the Rest of the ingredients and simmer for  5 minutes.  Serve over rice.
