Title:
Watergate Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (3.4 ounce) package instant pistachio pudding mix
2 (20 ounce) cans crushed pineapple, drained
1/4 cup reserved pineapple juice
1 cup miniature marshmallows
1/2 cup maraschino cherries (optional)
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together pudding mix, pineapple with juice, marshmallows, cherries, and nuts.
Fold in whipped topping.
Chill for at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Rick Janssen
Description: 
Sweet and fluffy. This dish makes a perfect potluck salad.
