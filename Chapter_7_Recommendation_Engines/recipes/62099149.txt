Title:
Nana's Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/551560.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Carrot, washed, unpeeled, cut in chunks
1 Small onion, quartered
4 Large garlic cloves
1/4 C Extra virgin olive oil
1/2 C Good, dry red wine
2 Large cans tomato sauce
1 Large can crushed tomatoes
2 tsp Dried oregano
1/3 C Fresh chopped basil
1 Large bay leaf

Directions:
Place first 5 ingredients in food processor and puree well, stopping occasionaly to scrape down sides. Pour mixture into a large, heavy pot and cook on medium heat, stirring frequently, for 5 minutes. Add tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, oregano and bay leaf. Simmer, slowly, for an hour. Stir in the fresh basil at the very end. Serve over your favorite cooked past.

Submitter: 
EAKE
Description: 
A wonderful, basic, pasta sauce that I have been refining for years. The addition of a carrot adds just the right amount of natural sweetness to offset the acidity of the tomatoes...Photo by SD, TY!
