Title:
Don's Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 slices bacon
2 heads fresh chopped broccoli
1 cup shredded extra sharp Cheddar cheese. Or 1 1/2 cup sharp cheese
1/3 cup white sugar
1/2 large red onion , chopped.
3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup mayonnaise (or a 50-50 mix mayo and sour cream)
1 small can pineapple juice
1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional)

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, and crumble or chop.
In a large bowl, combine broccoli, cheese, bacon and onion.
Combine remaining ingredients, whisk throughly and pour over salad.
Cover, and refrigerate 2 hours, serve
