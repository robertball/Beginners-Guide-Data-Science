Title:
monterey chicken tenderloins on a stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds of chicken tenderloins
1 pound of bacon
dash of pepper
1/2 cup of shredded cheddar
wooden skewers
sweet BBQ sauce

Directions:
precook your bacon a little bit not until crispy but just until it starts to shrink a little bit
meanwhile lay your tenderloins on a platter and sprinkle pepper on them
take the bacon and wrap it around the tenderloins
then skewer that through your wooden skewers longways
preheat your outside grill to medium
and grill for about 20 minutes barbequing during the last 10 minutes of cooking
lay the finished pieces on a cookie sheet and sprinkle with shredded cheddar
put the cookie sheet on the grill and close the lid until the cheese is melted about 3 minutes

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
these are mouthwatering,tender and juicy pieces of tastyness.Chicken tenderloins wrapped up in bacon, barbequed and grilled then topped with shredded cheddar cheese.These would be great while watching football ENJOY!
