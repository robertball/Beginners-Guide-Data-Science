Title:
Peach-Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
3/4 cup peeled and chopped fresh peaches
1/2 cup chopped yellow bell pepper
1/2 cup peeled and chopped mango
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 teaspoons lime juice
1/2 teaspoon minced fresh cilantro

Directions:
Combine tomatoes, peaches, bell pepper, mango, garlic, lime juice, and cilantro in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate until serving, at least 30 minutes for flavors to blend.
