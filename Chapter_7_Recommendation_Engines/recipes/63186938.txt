Title:
Cabbage Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1631927.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 medium onions, chopped
8 ribs celery, sliced
1 green pepper, seeded and chopped
2-3 jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped.
2 t. garlic
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1 quart beef broth
1 (15 1/2 ounce) can kidney beans, optional
1 medium cabbage , chopped
2 t. cumin
salt and pepper
1 quart water, if needed

Directions:
Brown the beef with the onions, celery and peppers.
Add garlic, cumin, salt and pepper.
Drain the fat.
Add the beef broth, beans and tomatoes.
Add the cabbage, and simmer for 1-2 hours or until tender.
Taste and adjust seasoning.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
Delicious cabbage beef soup. Leave out the beans for low carb.
