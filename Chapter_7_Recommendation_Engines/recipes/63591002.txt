Title:
Spinach and Strawberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bunches spinach, rinsed and torn into bite-size pieces
4 cups sliced strawberries
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup balsamic strawberry vinegar
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 teaspoon paprika
2 tablespoons chopped pecans
1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together the spinach and strawberries.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the oil, vinegar, sugar, paprika, pecans, and poppy seeds. Pour over the spinach and strawberries, and toss to coat.
