Title:
Hamia's Seaweed Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685711.jpg
Ingredients:
4-5 oxtails and/or 1 lb Beef Chuck Round
1 pkg. dried brown seaweed (Miyeok)
2 cloves minced garlic
3 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp Hondashi (Japanese Soup Stock)
4-6 cups water

Directions:
Add oxtails (and/or chuck round)to large stock pot, pour water in to cover by 2 inches.
Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer, covered for 2 hours.
Soak, rinse, drain and cut seaweed.
Let cool for 4-6 hours or until fat forms at top.  Skim fat, remove meat.
Add Oxtail Jelly/Marrow back to pot and add water to fill pot halfway to top. Shred meat.
Bring to a boil, add seaweed, shredded beef, minced garlic and soy sauce and reduce to a simmer for 30 minutes.  Add Hondashi and simmer 30 minutes more.
Serve with a scoop of white rice and red chili sauce!

Submitter: 
DadDuzTheCooking
Description: 
Seaweed Soup with Oxtail and Beef Chuck, served with a delicious red chili sauce and a handful of white rice.  This is Hamia's long and slow recipe.  Many imitators do this quickly, but not Hamia. She'd pamper this bad boy for a whole day...and man, was it worth it.
