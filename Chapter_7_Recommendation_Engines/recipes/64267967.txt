Ingredients:
3 medium onions, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 tbsp. oregano
1 tbsp. parsley
1 tsp. basil
1 tsp. marjoram
28 oz. can tomato puree
12 oz. can tomato paste
15 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 dash cayenne pepper
salt and pepper, to taste
1 tbsp. sugar
3 tbsp. olive oil

Directions:
Saute onion, garlic, and green pepper in olive oil.
Add the oregano, parsley, basil, marjoram, salt, cayenne and black pepper, and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
Add the tomato puree, tomato paste, diced tomatoes and sugar.
Bring sauce to a simmer, then cover and reduce heat to low.
Cook for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
Add pasta when still al dente to marry to the pasta.(see notes)
Serve over your favorite pasta.
