Title:
Capresse Couscous Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986055.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups water
2 teaspoons HERB-OX® Chicken Flavored Bouillon Granules
1 cup couscous
1/4 cup prepared pesto
4 medium tomatoes, sliced
2 large balls of fresh mozzarella, sliced
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup shredded fresh basil

Directions:
Bring water and chicken granules to a boil in a small saucepan. Take pan off heat, add couscous and cover. Let sit for 5 minutes, add the pesto and toss to mix.  Place a mound of couscous on each of 4 plates and surround with alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with basil.

Submitter: 
HERB-OX® Bouillon
Description: 
Add a spark of interest to quick cooking couscous by adding HERB-OX® Bouillon to your water and tossing in pesto.
