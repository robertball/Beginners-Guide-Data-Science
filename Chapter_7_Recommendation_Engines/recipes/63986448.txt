Title:
Tzaziki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Greek yogurt
1 Tbsp. olive oil
3 - 4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 English cucumber
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel some of the rind off the cucumber lengthwise and grate.
Place in a sieve over a large bowl and allow to stand at least 1 hour, to remove excess moisture.
Squeeze in a cloth and place in a bowl.
Add yogurt, garlic, olive oil, salt and pepper and stir to blend.
Cover and let stand in fridge for at least 2 hours, to allow flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
cdagirl
Description: 
I got this recipe from a cooking class I took at a local Greek restuarant. Using Greek yogurt and removing excess moisture from the cucumber makes this sauce extra thick and creamy. Delicious!
