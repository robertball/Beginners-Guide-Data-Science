Title:
Grandma Bowers'  Favorite Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. sifted all purpose flour
1 T baking powder
1 T sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1 egg, beaten
1 c. milk or more if thinner pancakes are desired
2 T canola oil

Directions:
Sift together dry ingredients.  Combine egg, milk and oil;  add to dry ingredients, sitrring just till moisted.  Bake on hot griddle  Makes about 12 dollar size or 8 - 4" pancakes.
