Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff - Alonda's way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/337994.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound cubed beef stew meat
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 (10.75 oz) cans  condensed golden mushroom soup
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup water
1/2 teaspoon beef boullion granules
3 ounces cream cheese softened
1 (8 oz) package of sliced mushrooms
1 tablespoon minced garlic
Black pepper and garlic powder to taste
1 pound bag of wide egg noodles

Directions:
Add beef, onion, mushrooms, garlic, soup, Worcestershire sauce, water, boullion and seasoning to slow cooker.
Cook on high for 5-6 hours.
Stir in cream cheese 20 minutes before serving.
Cook egg noodles and enjoy.

Creamy classic comfort food. Serve over egg noodles with a dollop of sour cream for a hearty meal.Modified from: Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I
