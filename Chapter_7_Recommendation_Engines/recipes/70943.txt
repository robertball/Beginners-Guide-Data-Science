Title:
Italian Vermicelli Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound mild Italian sausage links
1 (4.6 ounce) package PASTA RONI® Garlic & Olive Oil with Vermicelli
1 3/4 cups water
1 medium zucchini
2 plum tomatoes
Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Remove casings from sausage links; discard casings. Cut sausage crosswise into 1/2 -inch pieces. In large skillet, cook sausage over medium heat until well browned; drain. Add water to skillet with sausage; bring to a boil. Slowly stir in pasta and seasonings. Reduce heat to medium. Boil uncovered, 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
Meanwhile, cut zucchini and tomatoes lengthwise in half; cut crosswise into slices. Add zucchini to skillet and continue boiling 3 to 5 minutes or until pasta is tender, stirring frequently.
Stir in tomatoes. Remove skillet from heat. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Submitter: 
RICE-A-RONI®
Description: 
This zesty dish is an excellent solution to a busy evening. Everyone will love the Italian sausage, zucchini and tomatoes in a garlic and olive oil pasta toss.
