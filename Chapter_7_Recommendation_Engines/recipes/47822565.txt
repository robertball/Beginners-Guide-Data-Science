Title:
Dipping Sauce for Asian Vegetable Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Juice of 2 limes or 3 Tbsp
3 Tbsp rice vinegar
3 Tbsp. sesame oil
1 Tbsp liquid honey
1 Tbsp grated gingerroot
2 garlic cloves, minced
3/4 cup hoisin sauce
1 Tbsp chopped cilantro

Directions:
Combine in blender and put into serving bowl. Stir in cilantro.  Refrigerate until needed.
