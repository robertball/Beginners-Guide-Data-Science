Title:
Raspberry Mousse Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/786345.jpg
Ingredients:
1 0.60 package Sugar-Free Raspberry gelatin
1 cup Boiling Water
1 cup fat free Greek Yogurt
1 1/2 cups Fiber One Cereal
1/4 teaspoon ground Cinnamon
2 Tablespoons Sugar
2 Tablespoons Butter, melted
3 cups frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 pint fresh Raspberries

Directions:
Spray 8" spring form pan with a nonstick spray. Set aside.
In a small bowl, add boiling water to gelatin and stir till dissolved.
Pour into blender. Add yogurt; and blend until smooth.
Pour mixture into a large bowl and refrigerate for 15 to 20 minutes.
Put cereal in a food processor and pulse to a light crumb. Add sugar, cinnamon and melted butter and pulse till combined.
Pour crumb mixture into an 8" springform pan and press into the bottom evenly.
Whisk two cups whipped topping into chilled gelatin mixture until well blended.
Spread gelatin mixture over crust.
Chill for at least 1 hour.
Serve with remaining whipped topping and garnish with fresh berries.
