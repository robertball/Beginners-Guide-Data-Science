Title:
Layered Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive Oil for greasing
2 cups sliced carrots
1 small onion chopped
2 cups sliced celery
1/2 green pepper chopped
1 can cut green beans drained
3 tablespoons tapioca
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 32 oz can of tomatoes diced
seasoned bread crumbs

Directions:
Arrange vegetables in layers in olive oiled deep casserole dish, just as listed in recipe, all the carrots first, onions, celery, etc.
Combine tomatoes, tapioca, sugar and seasonings. Heat and pour over vegetables
Top with bread crumbs
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours or until vegetables are tender.
Let stand 5 min before serving
