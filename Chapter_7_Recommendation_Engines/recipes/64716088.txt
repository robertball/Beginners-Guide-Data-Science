Title:
Pumpkin Macaroni & Cheese (Mommy's Secret Macaroni & Cheese) By Marie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5749727.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pound Pasta cooked
4 Tablespoons Butter
3 Tablespoons Flour
2 Cups Whole Milk
1 Cup Canned Pumpkin
1 ½ Cups Cheddar Cheese shredded
¼ Cup Parmesan grated
1 Cup Sautéed Chopped Spinach
½ Cup Yellow Onion diced fine
3 Green Onions sliced
1 Garlic Clove minced
2 Teaspoons Dried Sage
¼ Teaspoon Black Pepper
½ Teaspoon Salt or to taste

Directions:
Directions: Cook pasta in salted water. Drain into metal colander and set aside.
In a large, deep frying pan, melt butter on medium heat.
With a wooden spoon add onions and garlic and fry till lightly golden.
Add flour and continue to fry stirring continuously about 2 minutes.
Whisk in milk and continue whisking until thick and bubbling.
Whisk in cheese until smooth. Then whisk in pumpkin and spices.
Turn off heat and fold in green onions and spinach. Serve immediately.
To Reheat Leftovers: Warm up on medium low heat a little whole milk and stir in leftovers. Cover with a lid for a few minutes until hot again.
