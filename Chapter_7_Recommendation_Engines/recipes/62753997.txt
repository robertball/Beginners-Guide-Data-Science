Title:
Amish Friendship Bread Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635436.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Amish Friendship Bread Starter
1 cup butter
1 cup shortening
2 1/2 cups brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla
3 eggs
2 teaspoons baking soda
5 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
16 ounce bag chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350° F (176° C).
Cream butter, shortening and sugars.
Stir in rest of ingredients.
Drop tablespoon size dough onto ungreased baking sheet.
Bake 12 to 14 minutes.
ENJOY!
