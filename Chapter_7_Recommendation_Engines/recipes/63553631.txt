Title:
Sausage Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/970908.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
3/4 cup onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup celery, diced
1 cup chopped carrots
2 or 3 cooked chicken sausage (I use sun-dried tomato, basil, asiago chicken sausages), sliced*
5 cups chicken stock (homemade or no salt added)
2 tsp Italian seasoning blend
1/2 tspdried thyme
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1 15-oz can diced tomatoes
Salt & pepper to taste
1 cup ditalini or other small pasta
1 15-oz cannelini beans, drained and rinsed
1 1/2 cups chopped spinach or kale
Shaved parmesan

Directions:
Heat oil in large pot over medium heat.
Add onion, garlic, and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
Add carrots and cook an additional 3-4 minutes.
Add sliced sausage and cook until slightly brown, about 5 minutes.
Add stock, tomatoes and seasonings; bring to boil.  Add pasta and reduce temperature to a simmer.  Cover and cook until pasta is cooked, about 20 minutes. Add beans.
Add kale or spinach, and cook a minute or two.
Garnish with parmesan when serving.
