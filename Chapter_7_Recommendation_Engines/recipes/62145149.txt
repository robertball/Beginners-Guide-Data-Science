Title:
Joan's Whipped Canadian Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Butter - not margerine (Room Temperature)
1 C. Powdered Sugar
1 C. Cake Flour (Silk)
1 C. Cornstarch
1 C. Flour

Directions:
Whip butter until light and fluffy.
Add remaining ingredients, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Divide dough into 3 lumps.
On wax paper, form into 3 rolls.
Chill 2 hours or overnights.
Allow to warm slightly to slice thinly (1/4 of an inch) and decorate. You can use colored sugar.
Bake at 300 F for 10-15 minutes. DO NOT BROWN, they should be just a pale color.
Makes 90-100 Heavenly Cookies.
