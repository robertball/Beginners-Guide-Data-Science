Title:
Creamy Chicken & Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796184.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 ~ 1/2 cup of chopped onions and celery
1/3 cup of fresh mushrooms, sliced
2 large chicken breasts cooked and chopped into bite size pieces
Half a stick of butter
1/3 Cup of flour
1/8 teaspoon of black pepper
1 can (14 1/2 ounces) of reduced sodium chicken broth
16 oz carton of half & half.
Salt, Pepper and seasoned salt, Use to your taste
1 Box (6.0 oz) Uncle Ben's Long Grain and Wild Rice Original Recipe, already prepaired as per direction on box, or you can use the Wild Rice in the 90 second pouch.

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute' onions and celery in butter until almost tender and then add your mushrooms. Stir in flour and pepper until blended. Stir well and cook for just a minute.
Gradually stir in chicken broth. Bring to a boil, cook and stir for 1 - 2 minutes or until thickened. Stir in rice and chicken and heat. Pour in your halh & half stir well. heat through, but do not boil. Serve HOT
** If you don't have half & half on hand I have used regular milk or evaporated milk **

Submitter: 
Robin S.
Description: 
I created this recipe after looking at several other recipes in a book. I added a few ingredients and took out a few. My son will ask for this soup all year around. All my family & friend go WILD over it.
