Title:
Perfect Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon cream of tarter
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter- softened a little
2 cups white sugar
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
4 tablespoons white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon

Directions:
Grease cookie sheets.
Stir together flour, soda, cream of tartar, and salt.
Beat butter for a minute, add the 2 cups of sugar and beat till fluffy in a separate bowl.
Add eggs, milk and vanilla, beat well to the wet mixture.
Add dry ingredients slowly to beaten mixture, beating till well combined.
Form dough into 1 inch balls, roll in a mixture of the 4 T sugar and cinnamon.
Place 2 inches apart on cookie sheet, flatten slightly.
Bake at 375 for 8-10 minutes or until light golden brown.
Makes about 66 cookies.
