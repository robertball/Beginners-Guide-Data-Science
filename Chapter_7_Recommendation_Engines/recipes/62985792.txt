Title:
My Pasta Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Italian Sausage
1/2 lb. ground beef
4 strips of bacon, well done and crumbled
1/2 cup mini pepperoni
1/2 onion, chopped
1/2 cup carrots, chopped or sliced
1 1/2 stalks, celery, chopped
3-4 garlic cloves
1 can Fire-Roasted tomatoes
3 cups zesty spaghetti or Pasta sauce
2 tsp chicken base
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp parsley
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 basil
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
48 oz. water
1 cup ditalini or salad-roni
1 can Navy Beans, drained and rinsed
Parmesan Cheese for garnish.

Directions:
Brown Italian sausage and ground beef together. Drain; set aside.
Fry Bacon until well done. Stir in pepperoni near the end of cooking time. Drain; set aside.
Saute carrots, celery, onions, and whole garlic cloves. Drain; set aside.
Pour Fire-Roasted tomatoes into skillet scraping bottom of pan to get drippings incorporated.
Add chicken base, herbs and Spaghetti or Pasta Sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.
Stir in water and bring to a boil.
Add pasta and cover.  Turn down to simmer until pasta is cooked.
Stir in beans and heat through.
Top with shredded Parmesan.
