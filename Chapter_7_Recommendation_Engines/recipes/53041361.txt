Title:
Jaymi's Mac 'N' Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Box of elbow mac
4 cups small curd cottage cheese (28 oz. tub)
2 cups sour cream (16 oz. tub)
1 egg
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
16 0z cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook mac according to directions. Drain and reserve two cups for another recipe.
Combine cottage cheese, sour cream, egg, salt and pepper. Stir.
Add cheese, mix well. Reserve some cheese to sprinkle on the top.
Stir in cooked mac.
Turn into a 13x9x2 greased pan.
Cover with foil and cook for 35 minutes.
Take foil off and cook for another 10-15 minutes.
