Title:
BBQ Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/496474.jpg
Ingredients:
5 strips Bacon (I prefer Applewood)
1/2 Sweet Onion diced
1/2 Green Bell Pepper diced
1/2 Red     Bell Pepper diced
1 lb of Ground Pork
1/4 Tsp Salt
1/2 Tsp blk Pepper
1 -28oz can of BBQ Baked beans (I like Bushes Original)
1 -15oz can of "Ranch" Style Beans
Optional add in's: 1 Jalapeno seeded/minced & 1 Tblsp Chile Powder

Directions:
Fry Bacon strips in a very large pan, medium heat for 4 mins. Leaving bacon oil in pan, remove bacon, keeping them straight and set aside. (they'll be soft)
Add ground meat, salt/pepper and saute until just cooked through adding Onions, Jalape√±os, Green and Red Bell Peppers cook until veggies are soft (Chile Powder now, if desired)
Add both cans of beans to meat mixture, warming as you thoroughly mix.
Pour into 13 x 9 casserole/baking dish, top with the reserved Bacon Strips
Bake uncovered 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Let cool 10 minutes, serve.

Submitter: 
Lizette
Description: 
These IN-A-HURRY BBQ Baked Beans always get GREAT raves! People think I make them from scratch. Easy to throw together and 'bake n take' to a potluck or Holiday gathering. Add the Chile Powder and Jalapeno for a spicier version!!
