Title:
Eclair Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
1 box honey graham crackers
2 3.4 oz. boxes instant vanilla pudding
3 c. milk
1 8 oz. frozen whipped topping
Topping
2 ounces bakers chocolate, melted
1 1/2 c. powdered sugar
3 T milk
2 T corn syrup
2 T soft butter
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix pudding milk and whipped topping until smooth.
Layer bottom of 13x9 pan with crackers.  Spread half of pudding mixture over it.
Layer with crackers again.  Spread remaining pudding mixture over it.
Mix ingredient for topping until smooth and very easy to spread, adding a splash more milk if it is too thick. Place a last layer of crackers on top of the cake.  Spread topping smoothly over that.  Refrigerate at least 12 hours for best results, 24 hours...even better!
