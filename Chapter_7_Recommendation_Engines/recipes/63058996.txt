Title:
Creamy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/769845.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups potatoes peeled and cubed
1 lb. bacon diced or 1 1/2 cups cooked, diced ham
1 cup sliced celery
1 cup diced onion
2 cloves minced garlic
6 tablespoons butter (or bacon grease)
1/4 cup snipped fresh parsley
4 tablespoons chicken boullion granules (1/4 cup)
2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
3 cups water
8 cups milk
1/2 cup flour

Directions:
Fry diced bacon in dutch oven or stock pot, remove bacon.
In same pot add butter (or bacon grease), celery, onion and garlic. Saute for 5-10 minutes until onion is transparent. Add water, potatoes, bacon or ham, parsley, chicken boullion, salt and pepper.
Cover and simmer until potatoes are tender (about 15-20 minutes).
Add 7 cups milk, heat till hot.
Blend flour with 1 cup milk stirring till smooth, then stir in soup.
Cook stirring constantly until smooth and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Nanny's Kitchen
Description: 
Creamy potato soup made with bacon or ham, whichever you prefer. It goes fast at our house so this recipe makes a big batch. Serve with warm crusty bread.
