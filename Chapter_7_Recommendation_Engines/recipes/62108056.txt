Title:
Bacon Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Cheddar Cheese
1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese
1/2 cup Heavy Mayonnaise
1 cup Cream Cheese, softened
1/2 cup chopped Bacon
1/2 cup Green Onions, Chopped
16oz. Strawberry Preserves
1/4 cup Pecan Pieces

Directions:
In bowl, mix shredded cheddar and shredded mozzarella cheeses. Add chopped green onions and bacon pieces. Mix in softened cream cheese and mayonnaise. Be sure not to make mixture to creamy or it may not hold its form.  In a pie dish, either coat with non-stick spray or lay a sheet of saran wrap, sprinkle edges with pecan pieces and pat cheese mixture into pan.  Flip onto serving dish and top with strawberry preserves.  Serve with butter crackers.

Submitter: 
Chickentarian
Description: 
This AWESOME recipe is  perfect for showers, receptions and the holidays. The strawberry preserves that tops  this spread is what makes it so delicious.
