Title:
Infused Olive Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup olive oil
zest of one lemon or lime
2 cloves garlic
1 tablespoon oregano
sea salt to taste
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil until hot. [sizzles water drop]
Remove from heat and immediately add all ingredients.
Allow to cook 30 seconds.
Put in another container to cool until ready to use.
