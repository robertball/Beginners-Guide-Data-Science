rustic chicken rub Recipe

Ingredients:
to make enough so that you wilt an ail have it. i suggest you get an air tight container. and keep out of light and damp spaces.
the recipe is as following:
1/2 cup cilantro (dried)
1/2 cup tarragon (dried)
1/3 cup basil
1/4 cup parsley (dried)
1/3 cup garlic powder
1/3 cup onion powder
4tblesp lemon zest
2tblsp course black pepper
1/2 cup course salt
1 tblesp Hungarian paprika
1-1/2 cups packed brown sugar

Directions:
place all ingredients in a food processor and let mix for about a minute seal in container and use as much as you like  ( also great on potatoes )
