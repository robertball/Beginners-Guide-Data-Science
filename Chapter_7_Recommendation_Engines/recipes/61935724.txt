Title:
Baked Garlicky Butternut Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large(3 1/2 lb.) butternut squash
1/3 cup fruity olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 Tbsp. minced fresh garlic
salt to taste
fresh ground pepper
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In a large bowl, toss together oil, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper.
Spoon the vegetables into a 12x7x2-inch Pyrex dish or other shallow 2 1/2-quart baking dish. Sprinkle on the Parmesan cheese. Bake for 1 hour, or until the squash is tender, not mushy.
