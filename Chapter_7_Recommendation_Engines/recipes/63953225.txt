Title:
Roasted Chick Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs package dried chick peas
8 cups water
2 Tbls olive oil
kosher salt to taste

Directions:
soak chick peas in water for 8 hours or overnight or per package directions.  preheat oven to 400 degrees.  pat dry the chick peas.  toss or stir in olive oil until evenly coated.  add a pinch of salt.  spread on a cookie sheet in a single even layer.  roast in 400 degree oven for 40 minutes stirring every 8 minutes.  allow to cool (very important). toss in more salt to taste.  should be very crunchy and delicious.

Submitter:

Description: 
These are a nice healthy snack to have for munching.
