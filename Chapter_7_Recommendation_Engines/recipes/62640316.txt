Title:
Pomegranatetini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/581985.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 oz. Stirrings Pomegranate Liqueur
0.5 oz. Crown Royal Deluxe (25 oz. per bottle)
1.5 oz. grapefruit juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake well. Strain into an ice filled rocks glass or serve up in a martini glass. Garnish with a maraschino cherry

Submitter: 
Ally
Description: 
A delicious combination of flavors, perfect for a girls' night.
