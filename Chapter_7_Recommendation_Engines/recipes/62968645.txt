Title:
Simple Tasty Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (13 oz) Multi-Color Spiral Pasta
1 bottle zesty Lite Italian dressing (chilled)
4 Roma tomatoes (quartered
1 Cucumber (sliced and quartered)
1/2 Bell pepper (finely chopped)
1/4 of a Red onion or as desired (finely chopped)
1 small can of sliced black olives
1 package of bacon bits (or imitation bits for vegans)
1/2 package of dried cranberries or as desired

Directions:
Prepare pasta as directed on package; let cool.
Combine all prepared (sliced & chopped) veggies in a bowl with cooked pasta. Pour salad dressing over contents to desired taste. Toss in bacon bits and cranberries. Can serve immediately.

Submitter: 
agirlygirl (OClancy)
Description: 
I wanted to try something different from the average pasta salads I've had. So I decided to experiment and thankfully everyone loved it! Feel free use more or less of any of the ingredients according to your palet.
