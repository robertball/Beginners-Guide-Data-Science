Title:
My Grandma's Veggie Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups whole wheat wide egg noodles
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
12 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 tablespoons salt
1 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup water
1 package frozen green beans
chopped fresh tomatoes, different colors
1 poblano pepper
garlic powder, ground black pepper, and cayenne pepper to taste.
2 cans beans, cannelini and pinto

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add egg noodles and oil, and boil for 8 minutes, or until tender. Drain, and rinse under cool running water.
In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, combine all other ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes. stir in noodles and serve hot
