Title:
Peanut Butter Fudge Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter (creamy)
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 large eggs
21.5 ounces brownie mix (fudge)
1/2 cup water
6 ounces chocholate chips (semi-sweet)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl, beat peanut butter, oil and eggs.
Add brownie mix and water, stir until moistened.
Stir in chips.
Drop tablespoonfuls of dough onto an ungreased baking sheet.
Bake 12 to 14 minutes or until fingertips leaves a slight indentation when center of cookie is touched.
