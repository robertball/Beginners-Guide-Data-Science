Title:
Tracy Ann's Salmon Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044150.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans red salmon (spines removed)
4 eggs, beaten
1 small onion, diced
1 cup plain bread crumbs
1 1/2 teaspoons dill weed
1 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon celery salt
1 teaspoon salt
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
2 Tablespoons Panko crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix everything together in a large bowl until well blended. Put in a loaf pan.  Drizzle the top with lemon juice and sprinkle with Panko crumbs. Bake for 1 hour.  Slice and serve with cheese sauce.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
This is an updated version of my Aunt Joanne's recipe for salmon loaf. Easy and delicious!
