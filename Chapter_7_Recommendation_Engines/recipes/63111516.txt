Title:
Heloise Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk
3 (6 oz) pkgs chocolate chips
1 (10 oz) pkg mini-marshmellows
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Mix the sugar and milk in a large, heavy saucepan. Slowly bring the mixture to a rolling boil, and let boil for 7-8 minutes while stirring constantly.
Remove the pan from the heat, then add the chocolate chips, marshmellows and butter. Mix only until the chips and the marshmellows are melted (don't overmix). Next, add the vanilla extract and the nuts (if using).
Pread the mixture in a large, 13x9" ungreased pan. Let cool. Cut into squares. Enjoy!
