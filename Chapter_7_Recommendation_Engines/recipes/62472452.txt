Title:
San Juan Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 individual sized salmon filets
1/3 cup butter
1/2-1 tsp. grated fresh ginger
1 tsp. dry mustard
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbsp. soy sauce
3 Tbsp. ketchup
1/2 tsp. worcheshire sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lightly grease 9x13 baking dish.
Place salmon filets in dish.
Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
Whisk in the rest of the ingredients.
Pour over salmon filets.
Bake for 15-20 minutes or until salmon is just cooked through.
