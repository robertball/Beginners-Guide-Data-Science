Title:
Pickled Cauliflower and Baby Carrots Recipe

Ingredients:
Â½ head  or 1 small bag of cauliflower, cut into small pieces
2 handfuls baby carrots, cut in half
1 tsp mustard seeds
2 tsp Truvia
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1 1/2 cup water
4 Tbsp salt
1 tsp dill weed
1 tsp black peppercorns

Directions:
1.	Microwave veggies for 2 minutes.
2.	Add carrots and cauliflower to fill the entire jar.
3.	Add mustard seeds into jar.
4.	Place water and vinegar into a saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce to medium heat.
5.	Add sugar, salt, coriander, peppercorns and red pepper flakes. Heat until salt and sugar are dissolved.
6.	Turn off heat and cool to room temperature.
7.	Place mixture into jar.
8.	Place jar in refrigerator and let sit for 2 weeks. Shake once a day to ensure seasonings are distributed evenly.
9.	DO NOT OPEN UNTIL READY. Open after two weeks to taste.
