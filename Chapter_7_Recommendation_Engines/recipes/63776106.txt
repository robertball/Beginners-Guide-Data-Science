Title:
Sublime Shrimp Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1046465.jpg
Ingredients:
Prawns:
2 cups hot water
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup of course kosher salt
2 cups of ice
3 Tbs olive oil
3/4 Tbs old bay seasoning
2 lbs U12 Shrimp (12 shrimp per pound): raw, shell on, deveined & cleaned
Cocktail Sauce:
3/4 cup chili sauce
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1 tsp. grated onion (or finely minced)
2 tsp. worcestershire
1/2 tsp tobasco
salt to taste

Directions:
Cocktail sauce:
In a small glass bowl mix together chili sauce, lemon juice, onion, worcestershire, tobacco and salt. Cover and refrigerate for at least one hour.
Prawns:
In a large bowl add hot water, sugar and kosher salt. Mix and dissolve the salt and sugar until water is clear.
Add the ice to cool down the water.
Once cooled add your prawns and fully cover in (brine) water.
Preheat oven on broil and place broiler pan in oven to heat (10 minutes from prawns being done brining).
Let the shrimp sit in brine for 30 minutes (no longer).
Remove prawns from bowl and rinse and dry your bowl.
Add olive oil and old bay seasoning. Mix together (should be a dark brown color).
Add prawns to bowl and coat evenly.
Remove the hot broiler pan from oven (wear gloves!)
Place Prawns in single layer on the tray (they will sizzle).
Broil for 4 minutes.
Remove broil pan and flip all prawns over.
Broil prawns for another 3 minutes.
Remove from oven (prawns will be pink and firm)
Put prawns on a plate, cover and refrigerate.
Allow to cool completely (2 hours)
Once cooled, peel shells and leave tail on.
Serve with cocktail sauce.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is the best shrimp cocktail I have ever tasted! The secret is in the brine. This is a family favorite for holiday appetizers and BBQ get togethers.
