Title:
Roasted Fennel and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups thinly slice fennel bulbs
3 tbsp. olive oil
3/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
2 cloves garlic - minced
3 tbsp. Parmesan cheese
2 (15.8 oz.)cans Great Northern beans, drained and rinsed
4 cups fresh baby spinach

Directions:
Heat oven to 450.
Combine fennel, 1 tbsp. olive oil, 1/2 tsp. pepper, 1/4 tsp. salt and cayenne pepper in a bowl and toss to coat.
Arrange in a single layer on a baking sheet coated with cooking spray.
Bake for 15 minutes, or until fennel begins to bron.
Stir fennel then sprinkle Parmesan evenly over fennel and bake an additional 5 minutes or until golden brown.
Add 2 tbsp. oil to a large skillet.
Add beans and cook 2 minutes or until heated.
Add fennel mixture, spinach and remaining salt and pepper, and cook about 2 minutes or until spinach is just wilted.
Serve immediately.
