Title:
Tex Mex Beef STew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs beef chuck
1 onion, sliced
2 cans diced tomatoes with jalapenos
1 pkt taco seasoning
1 can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 pkg frozen corn
sour cream, scallions, and tortilla chips for garnish

Directions:
*Coat slow cooker bowl with nonstick cooking spray.  Place beef and onion in bottom of slow cooker. Combine tomatoes and taco seasoning; pour over beef
*Cover and cook on HIGH for 6 hrs or LOW for 8 hrs.  One hour before end of cooking time, stir in beans and corn
*Serve with garnish if desired
