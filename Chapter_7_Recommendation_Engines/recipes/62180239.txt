Title:
Holiday Mincemeat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 apple, peeled and chopped
1 large egg
1 c apple cider
1/3 vegetable oil
1 jar mincemeat
3 c flour
1 tbsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
3/4 c sugar
GLAZE:
3/4 powder sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon
3-4 tsp apple cider

Directions:
Whisk together egg, juice and oil; stir in mincemeat and apple.
Sift together flour baking powder and cinnamon; stir in sugar.
Add mincemeat mixture and stir just untill combined.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Fill paper lined muffin tins 3/4 full and bake fro 20 mins, or untill toothpick test comes out clean.
Cool on wire rack.
GLAZE:
Mix together powdered sugar, cinnamon, and 3 tsp of cider until smooth, add a little more if it is to thick to spread.
Spread on warm muffins.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Mincmeat muffins a traditional thanksgiving and christmas morning breakfast for our family. They can be made several days ahead and kept in the freezer. Bring them out and warm them up for a festive morning treat.
