Title:
Honey Garlic Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp canola oil / ¼ cup canola oil
1 small onion, finely chopped
6 cloves garlic, chopped
2 tsp fresh ginger, chopped
¼ tsp chili flakes (optional)
¾ cup honey
¼ cup lemon juice
¼ cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp ketchup
1 cup all purpose flour
12 chicken wings, tips removed and cut in half

Directions:
Sauce: Heat oil (1 Tbsp) in small sauce pan. Add onion, garlic and chili flakes. Cook for 2-3 minutes until soft. Add ginger, honey, lemon juice, soy sauce and ketchup. Bring to boil, lower heat and cook for 5 minutes.
Chicken: Preheat oven to 375F.
Place flour in a large bowl or plastic bag. Toss wings in flour.
Heat oil over medium heat in a large skillet. Add chicken and cook until golden brown. Do not worry about fully cooking chicken as it will bake in the oven.
Place chicken and sauce in 9x13-inch baking disk. Bake, uncovered, in oven for 30 minutes or until sauce is bubbling and chicken is cooked through.
