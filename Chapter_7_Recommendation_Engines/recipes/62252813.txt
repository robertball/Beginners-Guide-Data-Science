Title:
Tasty Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/338201.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups bran or bran flakes
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 cup warm water
2 large eggs
1 3/4 cups milk
2 tablespoons white distilled vinegar
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
raisins, dried fruit, grated coconut, chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
1.	Combine bran flakes, flour, baking soda, and sugar. Cut in 1/2 cup butter.
2.	In another bowl, combine warm water, eggs, milk, vinegar, and 1/2 cup melted butter; mixing well. Add all at once to dry ingredients; stirring only until dry ingredients are moistened. Add raisins, dried fruit, grated coconut, nuts if desired. Pour batter into prepared muffin cups, filling each cup 2/3 full (Or pour into loaf pans instead).
3.	Bake in a 375*F oven for 20 minutes, or until golden brown (a little longer if using loaf pans). Remove from oven.
Completely cool on wire rack.
Makes 2-3 dozen muffins.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
These healthy muffins are even better when you add nuts and dried fruit.
