Title:
Party Cheese Ball with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 packages of cream cheese (8 oz.)
1 can deviled ham (4.5 oz)
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 tbsp prepared mustard
1 green onion, chopped
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp celery seeds
1/8 tsp salt
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except pecans; mix well.  Chill for 1/2 hour.  Shape into a ball or log and roll in the chopped pecans.  Chill well.  Serve with assorted crackers.

Submitter: 
TABLESPOON
Description: 
For a delicious cheese ball with a twist, try this one made with deviled ham.  If you like zing, add a few drops of picante sauce.  Best if made a couple days ahead for full flavour.
