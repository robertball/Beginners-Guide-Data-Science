Title:
RANCH BREAD BITES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 PKG OF DRY RANCH DIP MIX, 1 green pepper chopped finely, 1 bunch green onions chopped finely( about 4) 1 container 16 0z sour cream, 1 loaf of italian bread, sliced serving size, 1 pkg of shredded cheddar cheese.

Directions:
mix ranch mix with sour cream, spread on bread,sprinkle on green onion and green pepper, add cheese, pop into a 450 degree oven till cheese melts , then turn on broiler till lightly brown, watch carefully it cooks fast,
