Title:
Chocolate Raspberry Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/309167.jpg
Ingredients:
Base:
1 1/4 C. flour
1/2 C. sugar
1/2 C. Soft butter
1/2 C. Seedless rasberry jam
1 10 oz. package milk chocolate chips
Crumb topping:
2/3 C. flour
6 T. soft butter
6 T. sugar
1/2 C. chopped pecans
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine and mix dry ingredients for the base.
Cut in butter with a pastry blender until the mix is in coarse crumbs.
Press into a 8" square pan.
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until edges are golden brown (do not over bake).
While base bakes mix the crumb topping.
Mix flour, sugar together and cut in butter until it resembles coarse crumbs.
Stir in chopped pecans.
Add vanilla and mix lightly with a fork.
Remove base from oven and cool slightly.
Spread rasberry jam onto base to within 1/2" of edges.
Sprinkle chocolate chips evenly over jam and base.
Sprinke crumb topping over chips and return to oven.
Bake an additional 15-20 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
Cool completely and cut into small squares.
