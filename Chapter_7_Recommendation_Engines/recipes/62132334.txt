Title:
Salisbury Steak With Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs of ground beef
1/2 c chopped onion
1/2 c chopped green pepper
1/2 c chopped celery
1/2 c chopped carrot
1 egg
1/2 c bread crumbs
milk-enough to add a bit of moisture
1 t salt
1/2 t pepper
Topping:
1 cup beef broth
1 cup tomato sauce
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 tsp Worshshire Sauce
Dash of hot pepper sauce if you choose

Directions:
Mix and shape into small patties, place in greased flat pan. In bowl combine topping and pour over patties. Bake 350 1 hour

Submitter: 
Julia
Description: 
Evidently when my mom made this she snuck lots of veggies in! I don't see any other recipe like it on this site, but it's the only way I know to make it.
