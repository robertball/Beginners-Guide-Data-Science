Title:
Air Fryer Keto Garlic Cheese 'Bread' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6552999.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 large egg
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Line the air fryer basket with a piece of parchment paper.
Combine mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, and garlic powder in a bowl; mix until well combined. Press into a round circle on the parchment in the air fryer basket.
Heat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Fry bread for 10 minutes. Remove. Serve garlic cheese bread warm, but not hot.

Submitter: 
Elle
Description: 
Only 4 ingredients, ready in 10 minutes, and tastes delicious - what more can you ask for? Once you let the bread cool it develops a 'bread'-like texture.
