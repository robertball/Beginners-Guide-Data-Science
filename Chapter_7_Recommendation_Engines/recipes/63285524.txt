Title:
Chili Nacho Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds ground beef
3 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
2 (16 ounce) cans pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1 (10 ounce) can tomatos and green chiles, undrained
2 envelopes of chili seasoning mix
2 pounds processed American cheese, cubed
1 cup whipping cream
Corn chips
Sour cream

Directions:
Cook the beef until no longer pink, drain. Add tomato sauce, beans, tomatoes and chili mix. Heat thru. Add cheese and cream. Cook over low heat until cheese is melted. Serve over corn chips, garnish with sour cream.
