Title:
Patsy's Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
1 binch green onions
1 bunch radishes
1/2 Cup mayonnaise
1/2 Cup sour cream
5 Tbs grated parmasan cheese
1 Tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Thinly slice cauliflower into bite-sized pieces.  Thinly slice green onions and radishes.  Combine in a large bowl.
In a smaller bowl, combine mayonnaise with sour cream.  Add cheese and garlic powder.
Fold into vegetables.  Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Scott Simmons
Description: 
A very delicious, mayonnaise-based cauliflower salad.  Mom's been making it for over 30 years.  It wows everyone who tastes it.  Don't bring it to your next postluck unless you plan on bringing it every time.
