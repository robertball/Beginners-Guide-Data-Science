Title:
Apple Cider Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Flour- 2 cups
Apple Cider-  1/2 cups
Whip cream- 2 cups
Cinnamon- 1 tablespoon
Butter- 1 stick (melted)
Eggs- 2
Baking soda- 1 tablespoon

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350
Mix all ingredients into a big bowl
Bake uncovered for 35 minutes into cupcake pan
Fill pan 2/3 of the way full
7 servings 1/2 cup

Submitter: 
Abby
Description: 
Apple Cider flavored cupcakes with whip cream and cinnamon on top.
