Title:
Easy Apple Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg yellow cake mix
1 pkg instant vanilla pudding mix
4 eggs
1 c sour cream
1/2 c oil
topping and filling
1/2 c chopped nuts
1/2 c sugar
2 tes cinnsmon
2 apples peeled and sliced

Directions:
combine cake mix eggs oil pudding mix  sour cream and mix for 5 min...pour 1/2 into greased 10" tube pan...arrange 1/2 slices on top and 1/2 sugar cinnamon nuts mix add rest of batter and repeat with apples and top with cinnamon mixture...bake 350* for 1 hr

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Lisa  Hansons mother made this for Bible study back in the 1970's fast and delicious
