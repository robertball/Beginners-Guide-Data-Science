Title:
Hit of the Party Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3698444.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cans assorted beans, drained (may include any of the following: red, kidney, black, garbanzo, navy or pinto)
1 can tomatoes
1 pound bacon cut into quarter inch strips
1 pound spicy breakfast sausage
¼ cup molasses
½ packed brown sugar
3 tblsp mustard
1 onion, diced

Directions:
Directions: In a frying pan, cook bacon strips until fat is rendered. Remove bacon with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Cook diced onion in bacon fat until translucent (approximately three minutes).  Add sausage to onion and cook until brown. Transfer sausage mixture to a large pot. Add drained beans and tomatoes to pot. Add molasses, brown sugar and mustard to pot. Stir to combine. Cook beans on medium low heat until heated through and flavors develop (approximately 15 minutes). Serve! You will be the hit of the party with this beans.

Submitter: 
Rosalind Brazel
Description: 
This modern-style version of baked beans is a great side-dish for any summer barbecue and a good main dish served with a chunk of bread or some cornbread for those chilly winter evenings.
