Title:
Peanut Butter No Bake Cereal Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1/4 + half of a 1/4 cup honey
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 cups old-fashioned oats
2 1/4 cups crisp rice cereal
1/4 + 1/2 of a 1/4 cup miniature chocolate chips

Directions:
In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the brown sugar, peanut butter and corn syrup; cover and microwave on high for 2 minutes or until mixture comes to a boil, stirring once. Stir in the vanilla; add oats and cereal. Fold in chocolate chips. Press into a 9-in. square pan coated with nonstick cooking spray. Cool and cut into bars.
