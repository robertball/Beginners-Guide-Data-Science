Title:
Gun Lake Sandbar White Chili Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Old El Paso Burrito meat seasoning
6 - 15oz Great Northern White Beans
2 1/4 lbs. chicken breast
4 oz. butter
1/2 lb. Spanish onion, diced
2 T. garlic, minced
3 T. jalapenos, diced
2 T. chili powder
2 T. ground cumin
1/4 c. all-purpose flour
6 c. chicken stock
5 c. half and half
2 T. kosher salt
2 T. Franks Red Hot cayenne pepper sauce
3 T. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/4 lbs. shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Soak beans overnight in a gallon of water. Preheat oven to 375. Season chicken breast with burrito meat seasoning. Bake until chicken tests 165, about 30 minutes. Dice and reserve.
Melt butter and sauté onion, garlic and jalapenos until the onions are translucent. Drain beans and add beans to sautéed mixture, cooking for five minutes. Add chili powder, cumin and flour, stirring five minutes. Add chicken stock, half and half, kosher salt, red hot sauce and Worcestershire, cooking until mixture is thickened and beans are soft. Add diced chicken and cheese, stirring until cheese is melted. Makes one gallon.

Submitter: 
Scott Getter
Description: 
This chili recipe is originally from Reflections in Traverse City, Mi.  I had very similar chili at the Sandbar near Gun Lake and wanted to make some myself.  I found the recipe online at the record-eagle blog.
