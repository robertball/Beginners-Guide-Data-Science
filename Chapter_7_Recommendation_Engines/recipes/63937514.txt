Title:
Mesquite Grilled Shrimp Pasta with Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Mesquite marinade
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup water
1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 tbsp olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
1-1/2 tbsp minced garlic
5 baby bella mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup butter
1 tbsp all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
1 pinch salt
1 pinch pepper
3/4 cups grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 (16 ounce) package egg noodles

Directions:
Combine Mesquite marinade seasoning packet with 1/4 olive oil & water in metal or glass bowl, or zip loc bag. Mix well and add shrimp and marinade for up to 30 minutes. Preheat grill.
Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute onion until tender and translucent. Stir in garlic, mushrooms and butter, and saute until garlic is soft and fragrant, about 1 minute. Dissolve flour in milk, then stir in. Season with salt and pepper, and simmer 4 minutes, stirring constantly. Add cheese, and stir until melted.
Meanwhile, bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 3 to 4 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Grill shrimp 5 minutes.
Take drippings from drip pan and add to cream sauce. Stir to mix well. Add shrimp and toss to cover.
Divide pasta evenly and top with sauce.
