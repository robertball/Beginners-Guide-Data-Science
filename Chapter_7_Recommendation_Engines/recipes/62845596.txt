Title:
Kue Pepe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/677904.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag Tapioka Starch (14 ounces) (1-1/2 for 8x8x3 pan)
2 Cans Coconut Milk (Md Size)(3 cans)
2 - 2.5 cups Sugar (3 1/4 cups)
1/2 tsp salt (1 Tsp)
Pandan Flovor Coloring (1/2 TSP), Chocolate 3 1/2 TSP
Rosen Red Food Coloring (5 drops of red, 2 drop of Yellow

Directions:
1. Pour Coconut milk into a pan and rinse the can with a little warm water 75 ml.
2. Boil the coconut milk to lukewarm
3. Turn off the stove
4. Add sugar, after the sugar dissolves add flour little by little and stir.
5. Strain the mixture.
6. Take out 1/2 cup for red.
7. Divide the rest between white and green (1/2 cup each)
8. Warm 8x8 pan.
9. Put 1st green layer, steam for  minutes.
10 4 minutes for the rest of the layers and 15 on the last layer.
