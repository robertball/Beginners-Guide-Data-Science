Title:
Cream of Mushroom Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/753960.jpg
Ingredients:
- Chicken Breast (No Skin)
- Mushrooms (Sliced)
- Scallions
- Heavy Cream (Half Pint)
- Port Wine or White Wine

Directions:
1) In a skillet cook the chicken breast thoroughly, set aside. 2) In another skillet cook the sliced mushrooms (Make sure not to crowd the mushrooms or else they won't cook properly) Once mushrooms are cooked add Port or White Wine (Let the mushrooms soak in the wine (Medium Heat). 3) Add the Heavy Cream into the mushroom, bring to a simmer, then add the chopped scallions. 4) Add the cooked chicken to the mushroom cream sauce and let it simmer, chicken will soak in the flavors (medium/low heat) for 15 to 20 minutes! Serve with preferred side and Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mrs Sarah
Description: 
This is a delicious dish, not to heavy and fairly healthy! You can make any preferred side, either rice, pasta or vegetables will go well with this dish.
