Title:
Easiest Pizza Sauce Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) can Tomato sauce with garlic, oregano and basil.
1/4 to 1/2 tsp. white sugar
1/4 tsp. olive oil, if desired

Directions:
Pour tomato sauce into a small bowl and add 1/4 tsp. sugar and olive oil if desired. Whisk together.  If a sweeter sauce is desired, add another 1/4 tsp. sugar.  Pour over pizza crust and top as usual.

Submitter: 
Tanya Belt
Description: 
Friday night has traditionally been Pizza Night in my family and we're always looking for new ways to perfect our sauce and crust.  This sauce is one of my favorites-I love the taste and it's so easy and quick!
