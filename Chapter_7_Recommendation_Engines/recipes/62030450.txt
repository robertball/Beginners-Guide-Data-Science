Title:
Potato Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced potatoes
3 carrots, peeled and diced
3 stalks celery, diced
1 large onion, diced
1 bay leaf
1/8 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 16oz bag of frozen corn
1 12oz can evaporated skim milk
1 cup shredded 2% cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large pot, combine potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, bay leaf, salt and garlic powder.  Bring to a boil, add corn.  Simmer for 20 minutes.
Add evaporated milk and cheddar cheese.  Stir until cheese is melted.
