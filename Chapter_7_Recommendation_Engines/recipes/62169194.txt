Title:
white chocolate bugles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-7oz bag of Bugels
1=18 ounce jar of peanut butter
1-24 ounce package white  or almond chocolate

Directions:
Put peanut butter in a bowl & microwave about 15-20 seconds to soften.Place peanut butter in a cake icing tube and gently fill bugles and set aside.
Break chocolate into piece and melt(if you are doing 15 -20 only use 1/2 of chocolate) dip or roll Bugles in chocolate and place on wax paper to cool.Store in air tight container.
