Title:
Balsamic-Glazed Green Beans and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744644.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lbs green beans
1 TBS butter *divided in two*
4 oz red onion, chopped
2 oz yellow onion, chopped
1/4 cup chicken broth
3 TBS balsamic vinegar
1 TBS sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Place beans into a large saucepan of boiling water; cook 3 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water; drain well. Place beans in a large bowl; set aside.
Heat 1 1/2 tsp (1/2 TBS) butter in a large nonstick skillet over *medium-high* heat. Add onions; saute 3 minutes or until lightly browned, stirring frequently.
Add broth, vinegar, and sugar to onions; bring to a boil and simmer 3 minutes or until syrupy.
Add beans, remaining 1 1/2 tsp (1/2 TBS) butter, salt, and pepper to onion mixture
Cook 2 minutes or until thoroughly heated
