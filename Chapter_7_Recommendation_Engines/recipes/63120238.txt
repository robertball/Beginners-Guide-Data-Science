Title:
Healthy Pumpkin Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pumpkin puree
1/2 very ripe medium sized banana
3/4 cup fat-free vanilla yogurt (greek)
1 TBSP honey
1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup crushed ice

Directions:
Place all ingredients ni a blender and puree until smooth, making sure the ice is completely crused.  Makes 2 servings.

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
Like chilled pumpkin pie in a glass; but without the fat & calories.  This thick & creamy smoothie makes a delicious and filling liquid breakfast or an energy-boosting snack.  Be sure to use a very ripe banana, preferably frozen, and sweeten your smootie according to your preference.
