Title:
Tapioca Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sugar
3 Tbsp minute tapioca
2-3/4 cup milk (2% or whole)
1 egg, beaten
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix sugar, tapioca, milk and egg in medium pan; let stand 5 minutes.
Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to full boil.  Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Cool 20 minutes; stir.
