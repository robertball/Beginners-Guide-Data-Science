Title:
Zucchini Mess Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large Vidalia onion (softball sized) or any other sweet onion, cut in half, sliced into 1/4" thick slices
1 large zucchini squash, cut into 1 inch cubes, (approx. 8 cups)
1 T olive oil
1 (14 oz) can petite diced tomatoes
4 oz (2% or regular) Velveeta, cubed

Directions:
In large fry pan or small Dutch oven, saute onion slices in olive oil over med heat about 5 minutes.  Add zucchini cubes and continue sauteing over med heat about 15 min, stirring occasionally.  Add tomatoes and increase heat to high until boiling, then reduce to simmer.  Simmer mixture about 20 minutes until tomato liquid has reduced and zucchini is tender.  Prior to serving, add Velveeta and stir until melted.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
It's A New Day
Description: 
This is a recipe from my husband's childhood.  Simple comfort food.  Please note that I'm only including measurements for reference.  In general I use one large zucchini, one onion, one jar of home canned tomatoes and whatever Velveeta I feel like.  Enjoy!
