Title:
Maple Caramel Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 - 8 ounces day-old bread....1/4 cup maple syrup
2 eggs........................1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 egg white...................1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 cup white sugar...........1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Cut bread into 3/4 inch cubes.  Arrange in baking dish. Whisk together eggs, egg white and sugar in a bowl. Stir in the milk, syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Pour over bread. Bake in preheated oven until lightly browned on top and middle is set, about 40 minutes.
