Title:
"Who Needs Buffalo?" Sauce Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 C ValentinaÂ® Extra Hot Sauce
1/4 C butter
1/4 C sugar
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp celery seed
1 lb prepared chicken wings

Directions:
In a small sauce pan combine all ingredients, heating over low heat until butter is melted and ingredients are well combined.
Place wings of choice in a large bowl, top with desired amount of sauce, and toss gently to coat.
