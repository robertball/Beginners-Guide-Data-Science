Title:
Sunken Peach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1st layer:
3/4 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 small box instant vanilla pudding
3 T softened butter
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
2nd layer:
1 large can peaches (Save juice)
3rd layer:
1/2 cup sugar
3-4 T peach juice
8 oz softened cream cheese
Topping:
1 T sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
1st layer:
Mix ingredients 2 min on medium speed
Pour into greased 9" pie plate
2nd layer:
Drain peaches (saving juice)
Arrange peaches in a single layer on top of first layer
3rd layer:
Mix ingredients 2 min on medium speed
Spread evenly over 2nd layer
Topping:
Mix sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle over 3rd layer
Bake 30-35 minutes at 350 degrees.
Cool before serving!

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
A true family favorite.  It has become a tradition to make this for Thanksgiving and Christmas as an alternative for those that don't like pumpkin pie.
