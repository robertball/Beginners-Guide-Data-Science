Title:
Best Ever Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg frozen chopped spinach
2 8 oz pkgs cream cheese
1 cup mayonaise
2 Tbsp dill weed
3 green onions, chopped
1 cup cheese, grated
1 round loaf of bread (ie pumpernickle or sourdough)

Directions:
1. Cook spinach according to package and drain well.
2. Combine spinach, cream cheese, mayo, dill, green oninons, & cheese in a large bowl and beat at a low speed until thouroughly mixed.
3. Hollow out loaf of bread, reserving the inside bread pieces for dipping. Fill the inside of the loaf with the dip. Chill until served. For best taste, refrigerate overnight! Serve with bread, crackers, veggies, or anything your heart desires!
