Title:
Peanut Butter Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups butter, melted (2 1/4 sticks)
1 3/4 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 2/3 cups all purpose flour
2/3 cups cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoons salt
1 2/3 Reece's peanut butter chips (10 oz pkg)
GLAZE-
1/2 cup PB chips (reserved from brownies)
2 Tablespoons butter
2 Tablespoons milk
1/4 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x13x2 inch baking pan or line with foil overhanging and grease to remove easier later.
In a large bowl, stir together butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla; beat with spoon or whisk until well blended.  Stir Stir together flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt. Add to butter mixture, stirring until well blended. Reserve 1/2 cup peanut butter chips for glaze. Stir remaining chips into batter. Spread into prepared pan.
Bake 30-35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted comes out clean. Cool completely in pan on wire rack.  Meanwhile, prepare peanut butter chip glaze. Drizzle over brownies. Let glaze stand (do not cut brownies yet) until glaze had set. Cut into squares.
Glaze- in small microwaveable bowl, place 1/2 cup of pb chips (reserved from brownies), 2T. Butter and 2 T milk. Microwave on high 45 seconds. Stir. If needed, microwave an additional 15 seconds at a time, stirring after each heating- just until chips are melted when stirred. Gradually add 1/4 cup powdered sugar, beating with whisk until smooth.
