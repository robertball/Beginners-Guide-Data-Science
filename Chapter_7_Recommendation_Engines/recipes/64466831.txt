Title:
Newbraska's Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2450810.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Thigh of a T-rex
6 Packages of Hotdogs
3 Jars of Marshmallow Fluff
2 Cans of Condensed Milk
5 Jars of Raspberry Jam
1 Jar of Dried Mites
2 Boxes of Pillsbury Pie Crust

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 600 degrees.  Place thigh of a T-rex onto on un-greased cookie sheet.  Cook for 8 hours.  Once cooked cool thigh of a t-rex for 4 hours.  Peel skin off of the thigh and shred.  Set a side. Reduce oven heat to 150 degrees. Take the hot dogs and the marshmallow fluff poor them into a bowl mixing them together. Microwave for 10 minutes.  Add condense milk, raspberry jam, and dried mites.  Mix for 30 minutes.  Roll out pie crust and cut out 3 inch stripes.  Smear the marshmallow and hotdog mixture onto pie crust top with thigh of a T-rex. Roll-up pie crust and place on greased cookie sheet.  Bake for 1 hour.  When finished you may top bites with more dried mites or pasta with jam sauce (recipe to follow). Once you try this recipe you will be telling your friends that they are “Dinomite!!”
