Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. cooked chicken or turkey, chopped
1 c. green bell pepper, chopped
1 package (8oz) cream cheese, cubed
1 c. salsa, divided
3/4 lb. (12oz) VELVEETA® cheese spread, cubed
1/4 c. milk
8 6-in. flour tortillas
2 Tbsp. fresh snipped cilantro

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F. Stir chicken, bell pepper, cream cheese, & 1/2 c. salsa in saucepan on low heat until cream cheese is melted.
Spoon 1/3 c. chicken mixture down center of each tortilla; roll up. Place, seam-side down, in lightly greased 12x8-in. baking dish.
Stir cheese spread and milk in saucepan on low heat until smooth. Pour sauce over tortillas; cover with foil.
Bake for 20 mins. or until thoroughly heated. Pour remaining salsa over tortillas. Sprinkle with cilantro.
