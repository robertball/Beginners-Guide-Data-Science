Title:
Corny, Sweet and Moist Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups yellow corn meal
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
8 oz. sour cream
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
15 oz. can whole kernel corn, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly grease an 8 x 8 cake pan.
In a bowl, whisk together the corn meal, flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar until combined.
Add the egg, sour cream, milk and vanilla and whisk until mixed.  Do not overmix.
Add drained corn and combine.
Pour into prepared pan and bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Judy in Delaware
Description: 
This is a recipe made up from trial and error.  The added vanilla gives it an extra burst of sweetness.  It's very moist, but holds up well as slices, and it's great with sweet butter.
