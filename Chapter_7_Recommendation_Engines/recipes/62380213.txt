Title:
Creamed Eggs or Eggs Goldenrod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBS butter
3 TBS flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
2 1/4 cup milk
6 hard boiled eggs, chopped

Directions:
Melt butter over low heat. Blend in flour salt and pepper.
Cook over low heat stirring constantly until smooth and bubbly.
Remove from heat. Stir in milk. Reheat to boiling stirring constantly.
Boil and stir for 1 min. Stir in chopped eggs.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Creamy, warm eggs. This is like warm egg salad.
