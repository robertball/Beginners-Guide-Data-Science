Title:
Stuffed Blueberry Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
3 Tbsp chopped almonds
2 Tbsp light brown sugar
1 tsp lemon juice
8 slices bread
3/4 cup blueberries
2 eggs
3/4 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
butter flavored cooking spray

Directions:
In a bowl stir together cream cheese, nuts, brown sugar, lemon juice; mix well.  Spread 1 side of each bread slice with cream cheese mix.
Spread 2 Tbsp of blueberries on 4 slices of bread, press down to keep them from rolling off.  Place remaining bread sliced cheese side down.  Now you have a sandwich.
Mix egg, milk, vanilla, cinnamon.
Turn on the heat.  Spray pan with butter flavored cooking spray.  Dip sandwiches in egg mix, cook each side until golden brown, about 3 minutes.
Serves 4

Submitter:

Description: 
I made this for the first time with my daughter on Mother's Day, I think it is a great recipe for any breakfest where you wish to indulge.  Very easy and quick and versatile to you taste.  Berries, nuts, bread can be changed to your favorites.  Have fun and enjoy!
