Title:
portuguese steak eggs (Jens Bife Casa) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
portugese olive oil
1 large russet potatoe cut 1/6th of inch
2 very thin rib eye steaks
2 hot finger peppers (STAR BRAND)
1/4 cup portugese red wine or good red wine
3 garlic cloves or to taste
1/4 t spoon red pepper relish
salt and pepper to taste
2 eggs over easy or how u like them(not scrambled)

Directions:
Coat bottom of frying pan with oil,fry potatoes until light brown,season with salt & pepper,and reserve.Next add steaks,wine,both kinds of peppers,garlic,and reduce five minutes,our until your steak is cooked how you like it,steaks will cook fast.Next add back your potatoes heat them through,Remove everything from pan set a side to rest.Cook two eggs over easy,place them on top off steak.Eat steak, egg,finger peppers, and potatoes at the same time.enjoy!!!
