Title:
Chicken Parmigiana II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Chicken breast halves, boneless, skinless
1/2 c. Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1/4 c. Parmesan cheese
3 to 4 c. spaghetti sauce (homemade or commercial)
8 oz. mozzarella cheese, slices or grated
3 to 4 c. hot cooked pasta
2 beaten eggs

Directions:
Dip each chicken breast in beaten eggs.
Mix together bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese, roll chicken in the mixture.
Brown chicken over medium heat in a large skillet with oil.
Pour spaghetti sauce in 9 x 13-inch pan and arrange chicken over top.
Cover with foil and bake at 275 for 1 1/2 hours.
Remove foil and top chicken with mozzarella cheese.
Sprinkle with more Parmesan, if desired and return to oven for about 10 minutes until cheese is melted.
Serve with hot pasta.
