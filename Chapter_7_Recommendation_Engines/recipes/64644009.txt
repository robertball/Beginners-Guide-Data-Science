Title:
Cornflake Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7820015.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 eggs, beaten in 2 separate bowls
1/4 cup diced onion
salt & pepper to taste
2 tbs flour
1 1/2 cups crushed cornflakes or more as needed
1/4 cup oil

Directions:
Mix ground beef, onions, 1 egg, salt and pepper
Form into 3 equal sized patties
Dip each patty in flour then in beaten egg and then coat completely with crushed cornflakes
Heat oil and fry patties until crispy and golden brown

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
A beef patty coated in cornflakes and fried to a crispy golden brown. I like to eat mine with a little steak sauce on the side but my daughter eats hers like a regular burger, sandwiched in between a bun with all the fixings.
