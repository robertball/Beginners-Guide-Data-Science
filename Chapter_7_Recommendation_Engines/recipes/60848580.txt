Title:
Frozen Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gal of sangria
1 (12oz) can frozen limade thawed
1 (2 liter) of lemon-lime soda
2 cups sliced oranges, lemons and limes

Directions:
Place 1 (2gal) zip top plastic freezer bag inside another 2gal, zip top freezer bag. Place bags in a large bowl. Combine sangria, limeage, lemon-lime soda all inside the bag. Seal both bags and freeze 24 hours.
Remove mixture from freexer 1 hour before serving, squeezing occasionaly until slushy. Transfer mixture to a 2 gal container. Stir in fruit.
