Title:
Layered Vegetable Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T oil
1 lg. onion, chopped
1 lg. green pepper, cut into 1-in squares
1 sm. eggplant, chopped
1/2 # fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 lg. tomato, chopped
1 t salt
3/4 t thyme
1/8 t pepper
1 C herb seasoned stuffing mix
2 C shredded Swiss cheese

Directions:
Saute onion and pepper 3 min in hot oil. Add eggplant and mushrooms. Saute 3 more min. Add tomato and spices. Cook 1 min.
Spread stuffing on bottom of buttered 2 qt. pan.
Layer 1/2 vegetables and 1/2 cheese, top with rest of vegetables.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 min.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake 10 min. longer.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar

Made with veggies from the garden, herb seasoned stuffing mix and Swiss cheese
