Title:
Tomato Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/476031.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Macaroni
½ stick Butter
1 pkg Sharp Cheddar Cheese (shredded)
1 can Tomato Juice
1 t Salt
½ t Pepper
=======
GARNISH
=======
Hot Sauce

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350°.
BOIL macaroni until el dente, 6 minutes.
RINSE, drain, repeat and return to pan.
MIX in butter until melted.
STIR in tomato juice, salt, pepper and reheat.
ADD half the cheese.
POUR into greased 9"x13" casserole.
TOP with remaining half of the cheese.
COVER and bake for 30 minutes.
ADD a little hot sauce, if you like it a bit spicier.

Submitter: 
Jody
Description: 
A tangy, tomato-ey twist on macaroni and cheese.  We like it as a nice side dish.
