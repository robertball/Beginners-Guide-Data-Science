Title:
Kurush ~ Assyrian Green Bean Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/496321.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2lbs  stew beef cut up into bite size pieces
2 teaspoons salt
1 small onion, finely diced
1 28-oz. can crushed tomatoes
1 bell pepper, diced
16-oz. sliced mushrooms
3 stalks celery, sliced
2 cans whole green beans, drained
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 T butter
1 T paprika
1 T sugar
1/4 tsp. of cayenne
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Place beef in 6 quart stock pot and add enough water to cover meat by 2" above, add 2 teaspoons salt.  Bring to a boil then lower heat; cover with a vented lid, simmer for 45 minutes. Water should have decreased by about half.
Meanwhile place the butter in a saute pan and brown the mushrooms, add celery, onion and bell pepper and cook until soft, add garlic and cook 1 more minute.  Stir in Paprika, salt, pepper and cayenne pepper.
When meat has cooked add crushed tomatoes, sugar and sauted vegetables to the meat and continue to simmer for 50-60 minutes more or until meat is tender.
Drain green beans and add during last 10 minutes.
Taste for seasoning, serve over long-grain buttered rice.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
This stew goes back many generations in our family.  Tender chunks of beef simmered with green beans, mushrooms, onions, celery and tomatoes to make this delicious stew.  Serve on top of long-grain buttered rice*.
