Title:
Sweet and Spicy Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
2 teaspoons salt
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon Louisiana gold  hot sauce (or another of your choice)
1 teaspoon honey
2 teaspoons olive oil

Directions:
Fry the green onions and garlic in 2 teaspoons olive oil until the onions are wilted. Add the mushrooms and green beans and fry 4 to 5 minutes.
In a bowl, mix the hot sauce, honey, and salt.
Pour in the hot sauce mixture. Continue cooking and stirring 2 minutes, or until the liquid is nearly evaporated. Serve immediately.
