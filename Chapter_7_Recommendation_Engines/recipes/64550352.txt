Title:
Coconut Oil Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PEANUT BUTTER PORTION:
½ cup natural peanut butter
½ cup natural almond butter
1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
2 tbsp organic coconut oil (melted)
CHOCOLATE PORTION:
½ cup cocoa powder
1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
1/4 cup coconut oil (melted)
¼ cup chocolate chips

Directions:
PEANUT BUTTER PORTION:
Spray muffin tin or line with muffin paper liners.
Combine the nut butters, coconut oil and syrup (or honey) in a bowl.
Stir until the ingredients are well mixed.
Place a spoonful of the mixture into each muffin cup until you have used it all.
Place in the freezer until hardened.
CHOCOLATE PORTION:
Combine the cocoa powder, coconut oil, maple syrup (or honey) and chocolate   chips in a bowl.
Stir until well mixed.
Place a spoonful of the chocolate mixture over the hardened peanut butter mixture.
Pop them back into the freezer and harden.
Remove frozen treats from muffin tin; store in the freezer in airtight bag or container.
