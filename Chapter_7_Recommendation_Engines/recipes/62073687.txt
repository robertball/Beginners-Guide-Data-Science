Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2lb bag of salad mix
2 c. cheddar cheese
1 lb of lean hamburger
2 cans of sliced olives
1 bag of Cheese Deritos (Crushed)
1 16oz sour cream
1 24oz 1000 Island
1 8oz fresh made salsa(hot)

Directions:
Begin with cooking the hamburger, while the hamburger is cooking, put the salad in a large bowl, then grate the cheese, place the cheese in the salad. When the hamburger is cool put it in the salad. Then toss those first three ingredients together. In another bowl pour out the 1000 island dressing, mix the salsa in.
Place salad in individual bowls, pour dressing salad, granish with sliced olives, sour cream, and Deritos
