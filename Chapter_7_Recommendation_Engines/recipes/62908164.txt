Title:
Chef Jeff's Premium Spice Rub (2012) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/706547.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup paprika
1/3 cup black pepper
¼ cup sea salt
¼ cup chili powder
¼ cup ground cumin
¼ cup brown sugar
1 TBL plain sugar
1 TBL cayenne pepper (Optional)
¼ cup granulated garlic
¼ cup onion powder

Directions:
Blend all ingredients and mix thourghly.  Keep stored in a glass air tight jar. Shelf Life of 1 year in pantry

Submitter: 
Jeffrey Morris
Description: 
This delicious blend of seasonings will enhance any pork shoulder, pork chops, baby back ribs, whole chicken, brisket, taco meat and shredded chicken.  I had this recipe for years and made some recent adjustments to the blend and it really turned out great.   This seasoning blend has been tested with oven roasting and slow smoking.
