Title:
Sour Cream Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup butter - softened
3 cups sugar
3/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. baking soda
6 eggs
1 cup sour cream - room temperature
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
3/4 tsp. almond extract
Powdered sugar for dusting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Coat a 12 cup Bundt pan with cooking spray then dust with a little flour.
Beat butter and sugar until fluffy.
Add eggs, one at a time then add sour cream and extracts.
Add dry ingredients in increments then pour batter into pan.
Bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool before removing from  pan.
Dust top of cake with powdered sugar before serving.
