Title:
Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts water
3 T chicken broth powder
handful of spaghetti noodles

Directions:
Bring 2 quarts water to boil.  Add 3 T chicken broth powder.  Break up spaghetti into broth and cook about 8 minutes.
