Title:
Sparkling Gingersnaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup margarine
1 cup shortening
2 eggs
1/2 cup molasses, dark
4 cups flour
1 Tbsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1 tsp allspice
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix together sugar, margarine, shortening, eggs and molasses.
Mix dry ingredients and add to sugar mixture, mixing well.
Roll into balls and roll in sugar.  Place on ungreased baking sheet.  Bake for 12 minutes or until tops crack.
