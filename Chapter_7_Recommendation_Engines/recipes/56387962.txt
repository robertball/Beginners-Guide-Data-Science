Title:
Smothered Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 thick slices bacon, chopped
1/2 cup onions, minced (optional)
1 tbls minced garlic
1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
1-2 cups chicken broth (add 1/2 cup at a time)
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground black pepper

Directions:
Place bacon, garlic and onion in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until the fat begins to brown and garlic and onions are tender. Stir in beans and 1/2 cup of broth. Let the beans cook until the broth has evaporated, then add another 1/2 cup of broth. Cook til the beans are tender. If the beans are not tender once the broth has evaporated, add a small amount more broth and let them cook until tender. Season with salt and pepper (to taste) and serve.
