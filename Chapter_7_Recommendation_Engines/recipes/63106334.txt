Title:
Fiber One Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/792826.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Pork Chops
1 packages (half box) of Fiber One Cereal, ground
1 cup parsley
2 tsp paprika
1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
Put Fiber One in a bag and use rolling pin to make crumbs or use your food processor. You need 1 1/2 cups of crumbs. Add in Parsley and Paprika.  Add in olive oil.
Broil pork chops on high for 2-3 minutes each side. Lower oven temperature to 350 degrees and bake for 20 minutes, or until pork chops are tender.
