Title:
Kids Favorite Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1124949.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1 tablespoon milk
1/2 cup chili sauce or ketchup
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 red bell pepper, finely chopped
1 onion, finely chopped
1 zucchini, finely chopped
1 carrot, finely chopped
4 cloves garlic
1 pound ground beef
1 pound lean ground pork
1 cup toasted Italian bread crumbs

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the ketchup, brown sugar and mustard and mix well. Saute the veggies in olive oil until soft and then cool.  Mix with bread crumbs. Mix Worcestershire sauce, salt, egg, milk, chili sauce with ground meat.  Stir in the veggies,  mixing well. Shape the mixture into a mini loaf on parchment paper covered jelly roll pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 65 minutes. Brush on reserved ketchup mixture and bake for 15 more minutes.
