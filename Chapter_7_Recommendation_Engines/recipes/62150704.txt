Title:
Grammy's Easy Blackberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 cups fresh blackberries, washed
1 stick butter, softened
1 package yellow cake mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Add washed berries to the bottom of a glass baking dish.
Mix softened butter into yellow cake mix to make a "crumb" topping.
Sprinkle yellow cake mix topping over berries.
Cook uncovered in preheated oven for about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jellybean
Description: 
In West Virginia, blackberries used to grow wild near our neighborhood. My Grammy would make me this recipe after we would go berry-picking. This recipe is also good with blueberries and raspberries.
