Title:
Fettucine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. broccoli florets
2 T. butter
3 T. flour
2 c. lowfat milk (or cream for a rich dish)
2/3 c. ricotta
1/2 c. grated parmesan
salt and pepper
8 oz. hot cooked fettucine

Directions:
Bring water to boil; add fettucine.
Meanwhile, mellt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.  Add flour and cook for a minute, stirring constantly.  Gradually add milk, stirring with a whisk till blended.  Cook till thick, stirring constantly.
Stir in cheeses, salt and pepper, and cook till cheese melts.
Add broccoli during last 3 minutes of boiling the fettucine.  Drain and stir into cheese.
