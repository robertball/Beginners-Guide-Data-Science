Title:
Hot dog relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C ground onion
4 C ground cabbage (about 1 med head)
4 C ground green tomato (about 10)
6 red peppers
1/2 C pickling salt
6 C sugar
1 1/2 t tumeric
4 C cider vinegar
1 T celery seed
2 T mustard seed
2 C water

Directions:
Grind vegetables.  Sprinkle with salt.  Let stand overnight.  Rinse and drain vegetables.  Take cheese cloth put in strainer rinse and then squeeze.  Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl.  Pour over vegetables in pot, heat to boiling.  Simmer 3 minutes.  Seal in sterilized jars.
