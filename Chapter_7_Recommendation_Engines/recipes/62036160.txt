Title:
Open-Faced Cucumber and Cream Cheese Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf rye bread
8 oz. cream cheese, softened - low fat okay
1 large cucumber with edible skins - sliced thin
dried dill weed

Directions:
Cut slices of bread into quarters (triangles). Spread cream cheese on bread. Lay a cucumber slice on each piece of bread. Sprinkle dill weed over top.

Submitter: 
Robbie Rice
Description: 
I'm a caterer and serve this at brunches and teas. Rather popular with the ladies.
