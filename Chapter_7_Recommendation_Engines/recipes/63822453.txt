Title:
Popcorn Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart vegetable oil for frying
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg
1 cup dry bread crumbs
1 1/4 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
24 uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Used the deep fryer.
Place flour in a bowl. Beat egg with spices and added cut pieces of shrimp let sit in fridge for 1 hour.  Used my seasoned bread crumbs.
Tossed shrimp with some flour to coat and shake off excess. Drain egg off shrimp.  Roll in bread crumbs.  Toss with a little more flour to prevent sticking. Deep fry breaded shrimp in preheated oil until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes. Transfer shrimp to a paper towel-lined platter to drain.

Submitter: 
Cat Hill
Description: 
Crunchy, golden fried shrimp make an easy appetizer your guests will love. Serve it with your favorite dipping sauce.  Really came out good and it was simple to whip together.Modified from: Popcorn Shrimp
