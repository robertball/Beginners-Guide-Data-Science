Title:
Julie's Roast Taco's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/725543.jpg
Ingredients:
1 chuck roast
1 pork roast (equal amount)
1 pkg beefy onion soup
1 can beef broth(optional:you can substitute with water if necessary)
tortillas
shredded lettuce
pico de gallo
diced tomatoes
shreded chedder cheese
chopped green onion
sour cream
picante sauce

Directions:
preheat oven to 250 degrees
place both roast in same pan
add beefy onion soup
and beef broth
bake 6-8 hrs.
when done let cool.
next shred (pull apart) and reheat
heat tortillias and serve
