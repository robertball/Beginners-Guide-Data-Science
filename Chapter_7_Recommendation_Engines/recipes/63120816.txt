Title:
Guacamole pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb whole wheat spaghetti
1 avocado
2 garlic cloves, minced
Juice of 1 lime
1/2 cup cilantro
Dash of salt and pepper

Directions:
Put a large pot of salted water over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil and cook pasta according to package instructions.
Mash the avocado with a fork or wooden spoon.
Mix the mashed avocado with the minced garlic until the “sauce” reaches desired tenderness.
Squeeze the lime over the pasta.
Toss the pasta with the cilantro and season with a dash of salt. Divide into 6 portions and enjoy!
