Title:
Mandy's Applesauce Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup unsweetened applesauce
2 eggs
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix.
Pour in a greased bundt pan.
Bake for 25 to 40 minutes at 350 degrees.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
ChefMandy
Description: 
This applecause cake is moist and delicious. The nutmeg makes you think of the holidays when you bite into this cake.
