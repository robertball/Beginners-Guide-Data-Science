Title:
Homemade Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dry, quick oats
1/4 c. sugar or sweetener
2 TB. brown sugar
1/2 Tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup each raisins and walnuts
1/2 tsp Orange Zest (Grated orange peel)

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients thoroughly.
Put in large microwaveable dish.  Add enough water to cover (about 2-3 cups).  Stir till well blended.
Cook 3-4 minutes, or until well cooked

Submitter: 
Donna Lee Harris Boddy
Description: 
Cinnamon walnut raisin Oatmeal, a warm, tasty and hardy breakfast
