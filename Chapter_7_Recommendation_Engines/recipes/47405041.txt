Title:
Chili Rea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 can green chiles
1 lb. hamburger
1/2 cup onion
1.5 cups cheddar cheese
1.5 cups milk
3-4 eggs
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. tabasco

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Saute chiles, hamburger and onion until meat is no longer pink.
In a larger casserole, layer 1/2 meat mixture and 1/2 cheese, then layer remaining.
Beat together remaining ingredients and pour over meat and cheese layers.
Bake for 45 minutes, cool at least 5 minutes before serving.
