Title:
Homemakers Ground Meat Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp oil
2 onions, diced
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1 tsp minced ginger root
1 tbsp chili powder
2 - 1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground cloves
5 cardamom pods, cracked
1 lb lean ground beef, pork or lamb
1 tbsp fish sauce
1 tsp packed brown sugar
3/4 tsp ground pepper
1-398 ml can of coconut milk
3 tbsp lime juice
1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves (optional)

Directions:
In a large pan saut√© the onion in the oil over medium high heat. Cook until onions have begun to soften and look translucent.
Add garlic and spices (not including fresh coriander) and fry 2 minutes or until fragrant.
Add the meat. Cook, breaking up the meat and fry until almost cooked through and just a hit of pink remains.
Stir in fish sauce, sugar and coconut milk.
Reduce heat to medium low.
Simmer, uncovered for 20 minutes.
Stir in lime juice and chopped coriander just before serving.

Submitter: 
CarolynCooks
Description: 
This recipe came to me from the now defunct Homemakers magazine. It is an economical and tasty recipe that I have enjoyed making for years. It tastes great with rice.
