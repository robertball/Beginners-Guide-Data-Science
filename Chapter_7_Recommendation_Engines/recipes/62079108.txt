Title:
Prawn Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 handfuls prawns
1 large onion
2 cloves garlic
1 tomato
1 tsp minced ginger
1 tsp salt
Â½ tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp (adjust to taste) chilli powder or 4 green chillies
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp cumin powder
1 cinnamon stick
2 bay leaves
oil
4 tbsp plain yogurt
fresh coriander (optional, to decorate)

Directions:
Heat some oil in a pan and fry onion till golden.
Add garlic, ginger, tomato and all the spices and fry until dried up.
Add prawns and 1 cup of water, cover and cook for 15-20 minutes till thickened.
Switch off and stir in yogurt.
Serve with plain or pilau rice.
