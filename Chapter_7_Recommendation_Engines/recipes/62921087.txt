Title:
Pumpkin Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
2-3 T butter, separated
1 large yellow onion, chopped
5 garlic cloves, smashed/diced (to taste)
2.5 c arborio rice
1 c water
4-5 c vegetable broth
1 16 oz can pumpkin puree
parsley to taste/garnish (optional)
parmesan or feta cheese to taste/garnish (optional)

Directions:
heat small amount olive oil (and/or butter if desired) in pan;
add garlic, onions and saute to soften
add 2.5 c arborio rice, coat rice with onion/garlic/oil mixture to release flavors
add 1 c water; stir to mix and warm
add rice/garlic/onion mixture to rice cooker
add pumpkin and vegetable broth
turn to "on"
stir and add liquid as needed
add parmesan cheese or feta cheese if desired
garnish with parsley if desired

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
made in rice cooker; can make on stovetop if adjust liquids and add as needed, stirring regularly
