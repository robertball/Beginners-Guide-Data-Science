Title:
Kraft Graham Cracker Eclair Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/524695.jpg
Ingredients:
15 HONEY MAID Honey Grahams, broken in half (30 squares), divided
1 pkg.  (3.4 oz.) JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding
1-1/2 cups cold milk
1 tub  (8 oz.) COOL WHIP Whipped Topping, thawed
1 cup BAKER'S ONE BOWL Chocolate Frosting

Directions:
ARRANGE 10 graham squares, slightly overlapping, on bottom of 8-inch square pan; set aside. Beat pudding mix and milk in large bowl with whisk 2 min. Let stand 5 min. Stir in COOL WHIP.
SPREAD half the pudding mixture over graham squares in pan; top with layers of 10 of the remaining graham squares, remaining pudding mixture and remaining graham squares.
REFRIGERATE 3 hours. Spread with frosting just before serving.
