Title:
Ultimate Twice Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 mediums russet potatoes, scrubbed, dried, rubbed lightly with oil and poked several times with a fork
4 slices bacon, minced
1 medium onion, minced
1.5 cups shard cheddar, shredded
1 cup sour cream
4 tbsp. unsalted butter, softened
salt & pepper

Directions:
1.  Bake potatoes as is on middle oven rack at 400 degrees about 1 hour, flipping them over halfway through baking time.  Transfer to wire rack and let cool about 10 minutes.
2.  Meanwhile, fry bacon until crisp and transfer to paper lined plate.  Leave fat in skillet and add onion for about 10 minutes until softened and lightly browned.
3.  Cut each potato in half lengthwise.  Scoop out flesh into blow leaving 1/4 inch thickness of flesh around in each shell.  Transfer potatoes to large baking dish.
4.  Mash potato flesh and stir in cheese, sour cream, butter and sauteed onions.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.   Spoon mixture into shells and sprinkle with remaining cheese and bacon bits.
5.  You can store these covered with plastic wrap or even freeze if you like.
6.  Heat in oven until filling his hot.
