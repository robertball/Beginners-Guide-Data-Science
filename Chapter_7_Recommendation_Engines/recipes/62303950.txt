Title:
Chicken Wings ala Sweet and Sour Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/414959.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. chicken wings, cut to separate; discard tips
Salt and pepper to taste
1 cup cornstarch
2 eggs, well beaten
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
Oil for frying
4 Tablespoons ketchup
3/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups cider vinegar
1 Tablespoon soy sauce
Hot sauce as desired

Directions:
In a large bowl, add salt, pepper and garlic powder to eggs and beat well.  Add chicken to egg bath.  Roll/dredge chicken pieces into cornstarch until well coated.  Fry until lightly golden in color. Place wing pieces into a large baking pan. Combine all other ingredients in a small saucepan.  Heat until sugar is completely dissolved.  Pour over cooked wings.  Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.  Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Gail Cobile
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by a Southern Gentleman, long since passed. It has become our favorite wing recipe, hands down. Never a Super Bowl Sunday without these wings to carry on a much loved tradition.  They are delicious served hot or cold.
